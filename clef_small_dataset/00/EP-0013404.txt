<ABSTRACT>
Method and apparatus for grinding, polishing or the like of curved surfaces of non-metallic solid body, in particular glass body, with the aid of the surface to be machined at least partially adapted tools (7), which moves relative to the kör to be machined per (5) under contact pressure.This tool (7) follows the solid body (5) elastic to be machined, and the working space between the tool and solid body is continuously fed to flowable obliquity and / or polishing agent.In this case, the device has a turntable (1) for receiving and attaching the solid body (5), and the elastically mounted tool (7) presses at least at a part of the surface of the solid body (5).Preferably, this is rotated, while the tool (7) is elastically moved released in space and is held in places against the solid body (5).
