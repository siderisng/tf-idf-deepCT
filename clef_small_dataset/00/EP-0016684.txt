<ABSTRACT>
The device of the invention comprises a sighting helmet (1) provided with a viewfinder (2), and on which are fixed a magnetometer (3) and an accelerometer (4).Calculation means (6) make it possible to process the information delivered by the magnetometer (3) and the meter accelero (4).The method of the invention makes it possible to determine the orientation of the trihedron associated with the headset (1) in a reference trial.The invention is particularly well adapted to the automatic clarine of weapons or tools.
