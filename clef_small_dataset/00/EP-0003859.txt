<ABSTRACT>
a device (3) for treating waste water wherein, by gravitational  segregation, the waste water is separated from the  water-insoluble substances contained therein, for example  oil, and consisting of a pre-separator (31), and intermediate  separator (32) comprising a first and a second filter element  (1, 2) to which the outlet of the pre-separator can be alternately  connected, and a post-separator (33), treating the waste water  in stages.  the post-separator and the intermediate separator  are arranged in an overlying position in order to manufacture  a compact device.
