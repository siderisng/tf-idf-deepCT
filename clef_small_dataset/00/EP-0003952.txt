<ABSTRACT>
In the compression of synthetic threads in stuce chambers, special emphasis is to be placed on the thermal treatment, in particular cooling of the treated threads.The threads can be wrapped in the configuration of the thread plug, which is formed in the compression chamber on a rotating drum in several turns and resolve the thread plugs at the end of the drum and remove the finished crimped thread.The dissolution of the thread plug and peeling the thread is facilitated by applying axially parallel and / or radial forces to the last thread plug winding in the region of the dissolution point (32).Suitable mechanical and pneumatic devices are provided (31, 33, 48).
</ABSTRACT>
