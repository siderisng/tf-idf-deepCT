<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a method for obtaining isobutene from isobutene-containing C₄ hydrocarbon mixtures by reacting the Gemi shell with a primary alcohol in the presence of a sow condensation agent to form the tertiary ether, separation of the unreacted hydrocarbons and decomposition of the tertiary ether formedPresence an acidic catalyst at elevated Tempera Tour, where a primary C₃ or C₄ alcohol is used and the outlet temperature of the reaction mixture is deeper from the etherification stage for the formation of the tertiary ether than the average temperature in the etherest stage.
