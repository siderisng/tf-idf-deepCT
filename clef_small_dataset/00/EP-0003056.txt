<ABSTRACT>
n-substituted ω-aminoalkanoyl ω-aminoalkanic acids of the general formula wherein R¹ is an aliphatic or alicyclic hydrocarbylcarbonyl radical, an optionally substituted benzoyl group, a furoyl group, a thenoyl group or a nicotinoyl group, R² represents a hydrogen atom, a substituted low alkyl radical or an optionally substituted phenyl group, R³ is a hydrogen atom, optionally substituted low alkyl radical or optionally substituted phenyl group, wherein R² and R³ are not at the same time a hydrogen atom and R 2 and R³ do not simultaneously mean a straight-chain low alkyl radical, a one - (CH₂) M group or a -CH (R⁴) group means B one - (CH₂) N group or a -CH (R⁵) group, M and N are equal or different and a positive integer of 1 to 5, R⁴ and R⁵ are the same or different and a methyl group, a benzyl group, a hydroxymethyl group, one 2-hydroxyethyl group, a methylmercaptomethyl group or a 2-methylmercaptoethyl group represent or R³ and R⁵ together mean a trimethylene group, as well as their salts with inorganic and organic bases, are new compounds. They develop a protective effect for stomach and liver at warm bladers; In addition, they increase an increase in secretion of pancreas and liver (bile). Method for producing the new, pharmacologically active compounds as well as the intermediates required for their preparation are given.
