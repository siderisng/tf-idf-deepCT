<ABSTRACT>
1.  modular device designed for fitting out premises and placed inside an already existing building, the device being constituted of a half-structure or penthouse comprising a series of assembled elements which constitute a vertical wall (1, 1a, 1b), rigidly connected by a series of intermediate elements (2, 2a, 2b) to a series of horizontal elements (3, 3a, 3b) assembled together to form a roof, characterized in that the penthouse is held by cables (6, 6a, 7, 7a) secured at one end on the roof (3, 3a, 3b) and at the other end on a supporting member integral with the framework (5) of the building in which the penthouse is installed.
