<ABSTRACT>
In an infusion device operating after the gravity principle, a solvent container (1) raised compared to the infusion site is connected via a drop chamber (2) and an infusion hose (3) with an infusion needle (4) and a hose clamp (7) for changing the cross-sectionof the hose (3) for controlling the infusion speed.For special applications in which the infusion pressure caused by gravity is insufficient, the flow from the hose terminal (7) is a pressure increase device (9) angeord Net which is selectively switched on and an increase of the pressure in the hose section between the hose terminal (7)and infusion needle (4) causes, without the risk of air infusion with emptied solvent-based solvent (7).
