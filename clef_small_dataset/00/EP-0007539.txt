<ABSTRACT>
the laminated rectangular blank made of cardboard or card for producing a detergent drum has a beaded edge (3) at the top and/or bottom.  the drum consists of a single - or multi-layered, bearing inner wall (1) and a single - or multi-layered outer coating (2).  the outer coating (2) projects in the region of the beaded edge (3) over the bearing inner wall (1).  the beaded edge (3) is then formed from the projecting strip of the outer coating (2).  by this means, the formation of the beaded edge (3) is simplified in relation to the previous beading of all the coatings of the drum wall lying above one another without the stacking stability of the drum produced from the blank being weakened by the beaded edge (3).  <image>
