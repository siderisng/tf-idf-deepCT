<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the compounds of formula wherein: - the substituents, R₁, R₂ and R₃ independently represent a hydrogen atom, a halogen atom, a trifluoromethyl radical, an alkyl radical C₁ to C₆ saturated or unsaturated, linear or branched, a C₁ to C₆ alkoxy radical whose alkyl portion can be saturated or unsaturated, linear or branched, a benzyloxy radical, or a hydroxy radical, - the substituents R₅, R₆ and R₇ independently represent one or the other a hydrogen atom, a halogen atom, a trifluoromethyl radical, a saturated or unsaturated, linear or branched C₁-C₆ alkyl radical, a C₁ to C₆ alkoxy radical whose part Alcoyle can be saturated or unsaturated, linear or branched, or a hydroxy radical, the R₄ substituent represents a hydrogen atom, a C₁ to C₆ alkyl radical, or a hydroxy, - M and N radical represents independently one of the other a number equal to 0, 1 or 2, - X represents an oxygen, sulfur or a single bond atom, as well as their addition salts with pharmaceutically acceptable acids. These compounds are useful in therapeutics for the regulation of the cardiovascular system.
