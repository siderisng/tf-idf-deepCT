<ABSTRACT>
Dental handpiece arrangement with a handpiece housing for receiving at least two drive wave sections (8.9) connected to each other by enginal gears (19, 20) and a headset detachably arranged on the handpiece housing (4).The handpiece housing is divided into two sections (16. 17) in the area of the drive wave connection, which are articulated by a pivot bearing arranged in the region of the engagement point (19, 20).The bearing is made so that the housing portions (16,17) from a first position in at least one further pivoting position with a deviating angle (α) of its axes (12,13) are adjustable, wherein the tooth engagement is retained.The head part (4) is provided with a sleeve (15) corresponding in the contour of the provided pivoting position, which engages both housing portions (16,17) in the provided swing position.
