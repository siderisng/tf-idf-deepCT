<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a high frequency sputtering device for the precipitate of materials on substrates.By insulation (5) of the anode shield (45) of the high-frequency sputtering device against earth potential (21), the grounded surfaces exposed to the plasma are reduced, so that the impedance between the plasma and the grounded surfaces is increased.As a result, the reconsideration speed is significantly increased without the system becoming unstable.
