<ABSTRACT>
an improved backing for carpet or carpet tiles is provided in  which a bitumen is filled with a limestone-type filler.  carpet and  carpet tiles incorporating the filled bitumen (7) are cheaper to  produce, less brittle and more fire resistant than conventional  carpet or carpet tiles.  because of the nature of the filled bitumen  (7) when in a fluid state, the process for manufacturing carpet or  carpet tiles of the invention differs from conventional processes.
