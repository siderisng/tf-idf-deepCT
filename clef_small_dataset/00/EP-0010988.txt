<ABSTRACT>
a quasi-static electrical friction sleeve cone penetro­ meter probe which includes a main body (10), portion of the  body (10) being a cone load cell (11) connectible to a cone  (16) for measurement of cone resistance.  a friction sleeve  load cell (14) is connectible to the main body (10), and a fric­ tion sleeve load cell (14) is connectible to the main body (10),  and a friction sleeve (18) is connectible to the friction sleeve  load cell (14) for separate measurement of side friction.  each  load cell (11), (14) has strain gauges attached around an  outer surface, and balancing circuitry located within the  cell.  the friction sleeve (18) is supported against lateral  forces by three annular sleeves (30, 68, 48) located on the  main body (10), friction sleeve load cell (14) and the cone  (16).
