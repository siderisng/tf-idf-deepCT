<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to novel oximino-triazolyl ethane, a method for their preparation and their use as fungicides.The compounds of the general formula in which R, R 'and N have the meaning given in the description are obtained by reacting the salts of oximes with a halide in the presence of a diluent.The active compounds according to the invention have a strong fungithoxic effect.As plant protection products, the same can be used with particularly good success to combat venturia species, podosphaera species and grain diseases.
