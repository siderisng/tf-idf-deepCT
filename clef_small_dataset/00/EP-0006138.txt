<ABSTRACT>
A continuous pressure change adsorption method with at least eight phase-shifted adsorption units is described, in which each adsorption unit is operated periodically alternately in the working states «adsorption» and «countercurrent flushing» with interconnected pressure equalization phases by switching a plurality of valves according to a predetermined first clock schema.The method allows continuous operation without removing the system in the case of valve defects.A defective valve is determined during continuous operation by comparing characteristic physical measurements with predetermined setpoints in multiple consecutive clocking steps.Thereafter, a second predetermined clock scheme is switched under omission of the adsorption unit to which the defective valve belongs.
