<ABSTRACT>
imidazolylvinyl ether of the formula and its acid adhesion salts, wherein in the formula in the substituted or unsubstituted imidazole-1-yl group, is a one or two-core aromatic or heterocyclic radical, R hydrogen, a lower alkyl, aryl or aralkyl and Y is substituted or unsubstituted alkyl with up to 12 carbon atoms, aryl or aralkyl, whereby an alkyl or alkylene group bonded directly to the ether oxygen may be interrupted once or twice through the bridges -O-, -S-, -SO or -SO₂- and wherein R can only be hydrogen when Y is not bound with one of the groups -CH₂O, -CH₂SSS, -CH₂SO or -CH₂SO₂- on the vinyl ether sauer fabric. The new imidazolylvinyl ether are bactericide and fungicide and find use as active ingredients in medicinal products and pesticides. The production of the ethers is carried out by reacting a 1-aryl acylimidazole of the formula AR-Co-Chr-in in the presence of an alkaline. Sub punching and in solution with a compound Z-Y, where Z is an alkaline cleavable group.
