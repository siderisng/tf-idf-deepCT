<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to new polyisocyanates having new biuret and / or polyuret groups, a method for their preparation by reacting a primary and a secondary amino group having excess amounts of organic diisocyanates obtained from the above-mentioned starting materials, a urea and oneBiuretgruppe-containing transplexes with a second diisocyanate, as well as the use of the new compounds, optionally in with blocking agent for polyisocyanates, as the isocyanate component in the production of polyurethane plastics.
