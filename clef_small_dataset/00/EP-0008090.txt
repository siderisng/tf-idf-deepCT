<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to aqueous silicone-poly ester resin systems having a silicone content of over 30 wt.-%, based on the binder solid, containing a dimming of a polyester resin having an acid number of 50 to 170 and a silicone precocal decadensate with SiOH or SIIOR functionality or containing a silicone polyester combination resin having an acid number of 25-110,0 to 80 wt.% of an organic solvent and 0 to 10 wt.-%, each based on the binder solid, an emulsifying or dispersing aid, a nitrogen base and optionally pigments and fillers.The invention further relates to methods for preparing such silicone polyester resin systems and their use in aqueous paint systems.
