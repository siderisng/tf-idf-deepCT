11-amino-undecanic acids and 11-amino-andeca-4,8-monsteric acids and the esters of these acids.according to Formula I wherein R¹ and R³ independently of each other is hydrogen or an alkyl radical, R³ also a phenyl radical, R² and R⁴ alkyl radical or R¹ and R² and / or R³ and R⁴ together with the binding C atom form a cyclo aliphatic ring and whereinE each one of the radicals and means R⁵ and R⁶ independently of each other for hydrogen or alkyl and R⁷ represents hydrogen or alkyl radical.The preparation is carried out by known methods by reacting a 1-aza cyclododecene or a 1-AZA-1,5.9 cyclododecria in the presence of an inorganic acid to amine aldehydes and oxidized them to the acids.The esters are suitable for the production of in organic solvents very well-soluated salts of anionic dyes.
