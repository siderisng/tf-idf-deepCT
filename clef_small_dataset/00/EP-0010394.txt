<ABSTRACT>
uranium is extracted from wet process phosphoric acid  by extraction with a mixture of a diorganophosphate and a  neutral phosphorus compound, which is preferably triorgano  phosphine oxide, in the presence of nitrate to form an organic  extract layer containing uranium and an aqueous acid layer,  which are separated.
