<ABSTRACT>
A method of dechloring nitrogen or amino aromatic compounds, which consists of reacting anilines or polychlorinated nitrobenzenes with hydrogen, hot, in the presence of noble metal and cations (BI, PB, SN, TL, HG, AG), in the liquid phase at low pressure, and in acid medium.The method makes it possible to selectively obtain the metachal anilines.
