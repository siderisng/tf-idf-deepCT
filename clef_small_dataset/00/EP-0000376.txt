<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to new urea and thiourea compounds of the general formula in which R¹, R², X and Y have the meaning specified in the description.The compounds are obtained when reacting an N-benzyl-N-cycloalkylamine having an isocyanate or isothiocyanate, or when a N-benzyl-n-cycloalkylcarbamoyl (or thiocarbamoyl) halide of the general formula is reacted with an amine.The compounds have fungicidal effect.
