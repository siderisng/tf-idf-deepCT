<ABSTRACT>
Device supporting the image reinforcing screens needed by X-rays, whose structure is such that these screens and supports can be very easily removed for radioscopies.The exposure device according to the invention comprises a chassis (7) attached to the radiodiagnostic apparatus in its exposure zone and a removable grade (8) supporting the sliding reinforcing screens in this chassis.Application to radiographic devices that can be used alternately in radiography and radioscopy.
