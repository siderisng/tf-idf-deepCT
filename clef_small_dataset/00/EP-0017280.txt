<ABSTRACT>
in a blended lamp, an incandescent filament and a  high-pressure discharge tube are arranged electrically in  series.  according to the invention the filament (8) forms part of  a halogen incandescent lamp (2) and the discharge tube  contains a sodium amalgam which is very rich in mercury.  a  relatively high lumen per watt-value is thereby combined  with an acceptable colour of the light.
