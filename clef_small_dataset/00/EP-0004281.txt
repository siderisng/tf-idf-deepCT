<ABSTRACT>
In a method for controlling the speed of the screw of an extruder, the actual degree of filling of the memory is queried for each working cycle at a certain time and, depending on it, the speed of the snail is regulated. The screw promotes a certain volume of a material into a memory of the extruder as a function of the rotational speed. From this memory then a predetermined ejection volume is ejected. In order to turn off disadvantageous vibration phenomena, in addition to the actual degree of filling, the filling volume of the memory to measure and the speed control device for the worm to supply a signal given by the following relationship: S = actual filling degree - target filling degree + Fill volume - emissions volume. An apparatus for carrying out this method has, in addition to a setpoint transmitter for the desired degree of filling and a position of the position of the memory piston for providing the actual degree of filling a transducer, which outputs a filling volume signal. A signal generator outputs a signal representative for a particular ejector volume. The outputs of these devices are connected according to the above equation with the inputs of a summing device (17).
