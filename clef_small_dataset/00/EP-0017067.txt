<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a process for the continuous production of trioxane, optionally together with cyclic formal formal, aqueous formaldehyde solutions in a circulating reactor with evaporator, wherein the vapor / liquid mixture leaving the evaporator is initiated under the liquid mirror of the reaction mixture.According to the method according to the invention, high room time yields can be achieved in particular.
