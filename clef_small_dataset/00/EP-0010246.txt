<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to composite layers of a filler-provided polyolefin layer and a thermopla stable decorative layer, a method for producing these composite materials and the use of composite composite materials.The new composite liches contain between the outer layer of polyolefin and the decorative layer an adhesion-protected layer of a mixed fiber fleece or fabric of at least two enamel fiber types, each of which is well adhesive with one of the outer layers.If the outer layer of the composite body is a polyolefin, then a fiber type of mixed fiber fleecium may be made of polypropylene, the other outer layer is polyvinyl chloride, then a fiber type may consist of PVC.Preferably, a polypropylene and PVC fabric is preferably, wherein the mixed fiber reserved or fabric consists of polypropylene and PVC fibers.
