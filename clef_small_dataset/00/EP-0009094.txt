<ABSTRACT>
Condensing (1) from arylamines, in particular aniline, and formaldehyde in the presence of acid catalysts in which at least one COM PONENTE is a perfluorocarboxylic acid is prepared as condensation products ortho- and para isomers of methylendianilin (MDA) and higher polyamines.From this mixture of the condensation products, the particularly valuable 4,4'-MDA (p-isomer) particularly valuable as a product is obtained (2) with cooling of the resulting homogeneous, aqueous solution in high purity;The invention is based on the new realization that the 4,4'DDA salts of the perfluorocarboxylic acids are heavier in water than the corresponding salts of the O-isomers and the higher polyamines.
