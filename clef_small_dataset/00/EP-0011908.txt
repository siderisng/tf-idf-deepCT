<ABSTRACT>
the invention relates to an apparatus for controlling the  fluid pressure in an operating space or cylinder (11) of a  vehicle clutch.  a control valve (18) controls the fluid supply to said  operating space dependent on the input speed of rotation of  the clutch because the valve body (18) is subjected to cen­ trifugal forces.  on the other hand the valve body is loaded by  the fluid pressure in the operating space (11) to make the fluid  pressure in the operating space (11) dependent on the input  speed of rotation.
