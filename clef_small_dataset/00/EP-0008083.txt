<ABSTRACT>
Method for producing granules of hard-fusible additives for plastics, in particular pigments, characterized by thermal rolling granulation, characterized in that one mixes the additive in powder form with a granulating aid that softens between 30-200 ° C and a particle size of 0.1-2.0 mm has and subjected to this mixture of a gentle rotational movement, whereby at the same time by means of a heating source to minimum for the starting of the granulating aid.
