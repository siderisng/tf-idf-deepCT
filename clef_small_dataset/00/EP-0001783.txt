<ABSTRACT>
1.  a coupling field in an exchange system, for example in a video or telephone exchange system, which is assembled on a base plate (10) comprising a matrix consisting of rows and columns and has the shape of a rectangular trapezium, in which the base side (13) for the contact exchange of c subscribers has a length of (c-1) coupling points (11, 12) and the side (14) parallel to the base side and the side (15) extending perpendicularly to the parallel side has a length of c/2 coupling points, characterised in that, jack strips are provided on the base plate (10) at the coupling points (11, 12) for the reception of plug-in uni-directional coupling elements, which jack strips permit plug-in portions of the coupling elements rotated through 180 degrees with respect to each other. 
</ABSTRACT>
