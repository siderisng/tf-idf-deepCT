<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a wind signich, whose rotor (2) with star-shaped externally extending dispersed material taskbanes (7, 7a, 7b) and with suction openings (19) arranged between the sighted material feed channels is provided.The space between adjacent sighted material feed channels located between the outer end of the disposable material feed channels and the suction openings is limited to at least one side axially through a stationary element (12).This reduces wear problems in the outer region of the rotor.It also eliminates the need to accurately adjust a sealing gap between the rotor and suction housing.Finally, the free flow cross section for the visual air is enlarged at consistent overall dimensions of the vistern.
