<ABSTRACT>
a wobble plate drive mechanism (11, 13, 20, 30) for  machines having axial pistons (4) or the like clustered about  a shaft (11).  the moving parts are dynamically balanced over  a range of wobble plate tilt angles (γ°) by a balance ring (25)  arranged outboard of the wobble plate (30).  the wobble  plate restraint has balanced gimbal rings (60, 70).
