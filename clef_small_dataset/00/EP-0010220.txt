<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a sliding door compartment constructed of disk elements (10), in which the plate elements are provided along their edges with perforated rows (15).It is created for this sliding door circuit running rails (20), which have a T-shaped groove (26) to the reciprocating nuts (31) on the back.These rails (20) can then be secured independently of the construction of the sliding door cupboard with the known connecting angles (35) directly to the plate phenomes (10) provided with pitted rows (15).
