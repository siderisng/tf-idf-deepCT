<ABSTRACT>
Grinding grain (1) made of Al₂O₃ which has a proportion of titanium oxide, preferably TiO₃, is provided with a sheath of a ceramic mass (3).The abrasive grain (1) is subjected to a heat treatment, wherein a conversion of the Ti oxides takes place from the 3-value in the 4-valuable oxidation stage.The melting of the sheathing and the conversion of the grain structure takes place simultaneously.
