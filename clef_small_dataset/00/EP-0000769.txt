<ABSTRACT>
polymers containing polyalkylpiperidines and use  thereof as stabilizers for synthetic polymer compositions are  described.  in said polymers, sterically hindered polyalkylpiperidines  are linked in the main chain via bridging members  containing 2-hydroxy-1,3- trimethylene groups, or derivatives  thereof.  the polymers are distinguished by an improved  stabilizing effect in various synthetic polymer compositions,  such as polyolefins, against light- and/or heat-induced  deterioration thereof.  the polyalkylpiperidine-containing  polymers of the invention are hardly volatile upon heat-processing  or during storage of shaped articles containing  said polymers, and are resistant to extraction with solvents.  
</ABSTRACT>
