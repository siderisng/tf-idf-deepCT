<ABSTRACT>
the invention concerns a sheet feed for rapidly and  accurately placing a sheet onto a processing station (2).   a carriage (3) is movably mounted above this processing  station (2) and reciprocated for feeding.  two belts (4, 5)  extend between the sheet feed frame (10, 11, 20, 21) and  opposite ends of the movable carriage (3), leaving an opening  in the carriage (3).  when the carriage (3) is reciprocated,  the belts (4, 5) transport the sheet to register it, feed it  through the carriage opening onto the processing station  (2), hold it down on the processing station (2), and remove  it from there.
