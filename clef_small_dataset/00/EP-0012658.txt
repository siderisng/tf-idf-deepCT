<ABSTRACT>
The method comprises circulating the residage water on an adsorbent material (activated carbon) placed in the tanks (2, 4, 6, 8) and seeded beforehand in microbial flora (B₁, B₂, B₃, B₄) and onwhich organic materials are divided according to their ease of adsorp tion by defining successive trays.The adsorbent material is in the form of as many beds depending on it has trays, these beds being disposed in series.The beds can operate as well in aerobiosis and anaerobiosis.
