<ABSTRACT>
The rotary tower for the transport of casting pans (12) has two opposite arranged, in plan view of fork-shaped lever.The fork-shaped levers (7) are dual-armed and mounted on the rotatable tower head tiltable.Each lever is pivotable with the help of a hub tower engaged in the hub tower and otherwise hoisted in the vertical level.For the storage of the levers are open bearings in plan view, z.b.Spherical axial joints (14) provided.The levers are lifted off from the rotary tower head without disassembly of the storage.
