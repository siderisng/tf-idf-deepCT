<ABSTRACT>
1.  a process for the manufacture of hydroxylammonium sulfate by catalytically reducing nitric oxide with hydrogen in a dilute aqueous solution of sulfuric acid or ammonium bisulfate in the presence of a suspended supported platinium catalyst at an elevated temperature, wherein from 40 to 700 g of supported platinum catalyst are used per liter of reaction solution.
