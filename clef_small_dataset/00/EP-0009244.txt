<ABSTRACT>
The invention is within the scope of a method for producing homo- and copolymers of Î±-mono olefins by polymerization of the monomers at relatively low temperatures under relatively lower presses by means of a Ziegler natta catalyst from (i) a tanchlorideComponent and (ii) an aluminum alkyl-combente.Characteristic of the method according to the invention is that the titanium chloride component (I) used is such that is obtained by obtaining a catalyst component consisting of titantrial or titanium tetrachloride or substantially consisting of titantrial or titanium tetrachloridestraps under certain conditions of a carbon dioxide atmosphere.Such treated titanium chloride components (I) are characterized by having a relatively long induction period.
