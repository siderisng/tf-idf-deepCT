<ABSTRACT>
a novel protease product suitable for admixture to  washing compositions and exhibiting substantially attenuated  allergenic properties is prepared by cultivating strains  of bacillus licheniformis which have been mutated to block  their synthesis of proteases other than subtilopeptidase a  (subtilisin).  the commercial bacillus licheniformis derived  protease products are mixtures of subtilisin and a nonserine  protease of lower stability and greater allergenicity  than subtilisin.
