<ABSTRACT>
in an electric vacuum cleaner, whose fan, arranged in a housing, is driven by an electric motor (7), a device (11) is provided for limiting the current at make.  in order that the device (11) for limiting the current at make does not adversely affect the subsequent operation of the vacuum cleaner, it is controlled by a control device (10), which responds to the vacuum in the vacuum cleaner, in such a way that the limiting effect is reduced with increasing vacuum.  <image>
