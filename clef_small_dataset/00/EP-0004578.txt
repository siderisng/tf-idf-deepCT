<ABSTRACT>
The flame-retardant styrene polymerite beam according to the invention is obtained by melting a granules of styrene polymer and aluminum hydroxide of a grain size of 20-100 μm and a specific surface area below 1 m² / g in an extruder, incorporation of a physical propellant, good mixing and extrusion.
