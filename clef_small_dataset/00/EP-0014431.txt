<ABSTRACT>
Fluorinated emulsifier acids, in particular perfluorocar bonic acids, can be eluted from basic anion exchangers with the aid of mixtures of dilute mineral acids and polar organic solvents, wherein the solvent should preferably be mixed at least 40% with water.By this method, the amount requirement on eluent and the time requirement for elution can be reduced and the yield of recovered emulsifier acid can be increased.The method is particularly suitable for recovering fidelated emulsifiers in coagulation or concentration of fluoropolymer latices.
