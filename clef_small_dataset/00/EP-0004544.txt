<ABSTRACT>
With each switching element, the electrical contacts (A-C) always have to be closed abruptly and disconnected, otherwise they welcome themselves through the arc resulting in switching. For suchable switching is therefore between a push button (4), which is usually manually operated and a contact carrier (7) a biasing spring (5) is clamped. This counteracts a return spring (6), which is arranged between the contact carrier (7) and the bottom (1a) of the main body (1) of the switching element. Between two opposing contact pairs (A-B) and (C-D) contact springs (8) are arranged, which press the contacts against the contact terminals (A - B) and (C - D) if they are not from the contact carrier (7) in which the contacts are guided, held in a separation position. The mentioned springs together with latch springs (9) and detection bodies (10) which lie in locking cams (7g) of the contact carrier (7), result in a sudden switching of the contacts (A - C).
