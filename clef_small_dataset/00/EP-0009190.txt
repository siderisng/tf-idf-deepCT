<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a composition processed in screen printing with a content of an epoxidized novolak, a phenoxypolymer, a liquid anhydride, colloidal silica and a dilution agent.The composition is used to coating documents, for example, printed circuit cards, and serves as a solder mask and protective coating.
