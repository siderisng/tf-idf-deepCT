<ABSTRACT>
Crystal clear polyamides based on aromatic dicar bonic acids and dialicyclic diamines are prepared by polycondensation of isophthalic acid having a mixture of 3,3'-dimethyl-4,4'-diamino diclyclohexylmethane and hexamethylenediamine.The polyamides are suitable for the production of moldings piers, especially in the injection molding process.
