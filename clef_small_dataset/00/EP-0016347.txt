<ABSTRACT>
low density, closed cell, soft foam products having  improved dimensional stability are made from blends of  ethylenic resins having low stiffness by extrusion foaming of  the heat plastified polymer blend and a volatile blowing  agent.  the blends especially comprise (1) a copolymer of  ethylene and monoethylenically unsaturated carboxylic acid  and (2) a copolymer of ethylene and at least one monoethylen­ ically unsaturated non-ionic comonomer, preferably blends  of ethylene/acrylic acid copolymers and ethylene/vinyl ace­ tate copolymers, having stiffness less than about 20,000 psi  (1400 kg/cm²).  exemplary volatile blowing agents are 1,2-­ dichlorotetrafluoroethane and 1-chloro-1,2,2,2­ -tetrafluoroethane.
