<ABSTRACT>
For testing the channels for the two transmission richs of a data transmission path test signals are sent out of the testing station, which transforms in the loop formed against station and then compared with the originally stitched test signals. For data transmission, where the two channels are designed for very different speeds and is transmitted asynchronously on the fast channel asynchronously and on the slow channel asynchronously, the test signal can only be looped through when it is adapted to the low transformer speed of the slow channel . A safe test of the fast channel is not possible with such a signal. Therefore, a test signal having the maximum transmission speed is emitted on the fast channel and in the reverse station is provided in the loop a selection shell device, which selects only a portion of the incoming steps of the rapid signal and transfers over the long samer channel.
