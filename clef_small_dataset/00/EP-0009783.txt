a system for driving an alternate current motor includ­ ing a pulse width modulation inverter having the two-range  driving characteristics, wherein the motor is driven by the  pulse width modulation output having a simulated sine wave  form (pwm (1), pwm (2)) at the speed lower than the base  speed of the motor and by the pulse width modulation out­ put having a rectangular wave form (pwm (3), pwm (4)) at  the speed higher than the base speed of the motor.
