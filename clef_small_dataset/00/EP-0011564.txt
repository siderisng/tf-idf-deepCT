<ABSTRACT>
The subject of the invention relates to a method for correcting the properties of fibers consisting of a moplastic thermal material, in particular of mineral fibers, during their manufacture by stretching with a gaseous current emitted by a generator having a generator havingAt least two burners, the process in which the burners of the temperature and pressure measurements of the gases were used in the inter valles separating the flames of these burners.It also relates to a device for carrying out this process wherein the bonding channels (8.9) receiving pressure and temperature measurement (10) are provided between the burners.It applies in particular to the manufacture of glass fibers using centrifuges from which it accelerates performance and quality.
