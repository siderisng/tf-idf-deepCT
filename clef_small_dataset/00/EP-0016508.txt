<ABSTRACT>
the invention pertains to a double fibre magazine for  brush manufacturing machines, characterized in that it mainly  consists of a combination of two fibre magazines (4-5) which  are immovably located opposite to the bundle plucker (2),  each fibre magazine (4)5) being provided with a separating  device (11-12) and a pressing component (9-10) for pressing  the fibres (v1-v2) towards the bundle plucker (2); and means  which alternately put the fibres (v1-v2) beyond the reach of the  bundle plucker (2) in one and the other fibre magazine (4-5).
