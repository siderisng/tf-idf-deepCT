<ABSTRACT>
the keyboard comprises, one beneath the other: a support plate comprising a metal plate (1) onto which is deposited a layer (2) of dielectric material covered with electroluminescent conductive depositions (3) and with conductive metallised zones, an insulating interpolated sheet (12), drilled with holes (14) in a vertical line with the metallised zones of the support plate, an upper plate (11) of which at least the parts facing the holes (14) of the inter-polated plate (12) are carriers of metallised zones.  this keyboard is used in control boxes or remote control boxes.  <image>
</ABSTRACT>
