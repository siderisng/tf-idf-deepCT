<ABSTRACT>
Mixtures of a) copolymers of ethylene and tetrafluoroethylene, which may optionally contain up to 10 mol% of units of at least one simply ethylenically unsaturated monomers, with tetrafluoroethylene copolymerizable monomers, with b) acids of the phosphorus formed in one of their tautomeric forms 2or 3 oxygen atoms and 1 or 2 hydrogen atoms are contained directly at each phosphor atom or described with salts of such acids.These mixtures have improved thermostability and may optionally be used with interconnection of a melt granulating process, in particular for coating metals.Component b) is added to the copolymer a) in a dry or preferably dissolved form during the work-up, either the powdery copolymer or in particular a moist product obtained from dispersions after the last washing process.
</ABSTRACT>
