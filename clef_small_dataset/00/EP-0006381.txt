<ABSTRACT>
During this process, an amount of an expandable mixture is cast in a mold (1), after which, the mold is closed with a plug (16) through which a depression is created in the chamber(4) of the mold (1) to allow the foaming of the mixture.Depression is maintained a while after the foaming mixture has filled the entire volume of the mold.At the end of the polymerization period of the foaming lange, the depression can be re-applied to the mold, preferably during several cycles to prove the bursting of the cells of the foam obtained.
