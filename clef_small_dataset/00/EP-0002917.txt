<ABSTRACT>
the present invention provides a detergent composition  which comprises an anionic and/or nonionic surfactant  and, as builders, an imido-bis-sulfate and an aluminosilicate.   these detergent compositions are of low toxicity,  and in particular they avoid the use of phosphate builders,  and yet possess satisfactory washing properties.
