<ABSTRACT>
the upper fire pot 1 of the barbecue shown is mounted so as to pivot by means of a shaft 20 carried by the upper cross piece of a frame 4 in the form of a u.  in the horizontal position of use as a grill, shown in solid lines, the fire pot 1 is held by means of gravity in contact with the end 28 of a movable rod 27 locked at 34a.  in order to change to the position of use as a rotisserie, shown in broken lines, the rod 27 is unlocked and, sliding downwards, it releases the fire pot 1 which then tilts until it reaches its other position.  <image>
