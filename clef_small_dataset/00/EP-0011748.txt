<ABSTRACT>
Method for producing crosslinked foams based on polyolefins due to peroxidic viruses zion and foaming by a chemical foaming agent, preferably azodicarbonamide, under normal pressure bed in the presence of certain magnesium compounds, preferably MGO, in particular in an amount of 0.5 to 3.3 wt.-% based on the foaming agent used.These magnesium compounds result in a reduction in the density of the foams in the appropriate quantity range.
