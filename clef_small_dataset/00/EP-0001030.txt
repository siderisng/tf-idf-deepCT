<ABSTRACT>
The mask support (20) is first coated with a layer (21) of a first actinic ray-sensitive material (from the X-ray spectrum) and then formed on this layer, a thin metal film (24)delimited according to a configuration determined by a lithograph step using a second layer of a second electrons sensitive material, not shown.The first material is then exposed to actinic rays through the openings (25) of said metal film thus delimited and said first material is then developed.This process that combines the electronithography (calculator exposure faculty) and the photolithography (excellent nition of lines) finds its application in the lithographic steps of the manufacture of integrated semiconductor devices in particular.
