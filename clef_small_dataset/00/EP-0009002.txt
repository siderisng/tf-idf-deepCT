<ABSTRACT>
an assembly comprising longitudinal members (1) connected by cross pieces (2) comprises joining elements (3) with a view to permitting the tool-free mounting of this assembly.  the joining elements (3) are intended to link the ends of the cross pieces (2) to the longitudinal members (1).  these joining elements (3) each have guide (4, 6, 10) and locking notch (8, 12) assembly means, interacting in two directions which are perpendicular respectively with the end of a cross piece (2) and a longitudinal member (1).  <image>
