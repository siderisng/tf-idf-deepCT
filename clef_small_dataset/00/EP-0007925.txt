plant for the thermal treatment at elevated temperature of objects to be treated as they pass.

<ABSTRACT>
installation for the heat treatment at high temperature of materials to be treated in succession, comprising pairs of rollers (2, 3) in which, in order to obtain efficient cooling of the rollers, the latter support rings (5, 13) each defining an open container fed by a device (7, 8) for dispensing a cooling liquid.  moreover, the rollers are pierced with holes passing from the base of the container to the upper face of the roller (2, 3) towards the lower face of the latter.    <image>
