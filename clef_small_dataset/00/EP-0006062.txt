<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a new process for the preparation of chloro-2-pyrimidine from amino-2-pyrimidine, hydrochloric acid and alkali nitrite, the reagents being substantially in the same proportions as in the methods previouslyKnown, consisting of preparing a mixture of hydrochloric acid and a low-boiling non-polar solvent, and then introduced the amino-2-pyrimidine, characterized in that slowly adds to room temperature,a metal chloride selected from those of the transition metals, tin, antimony, thallium and zinc, and then introduced, under the circulation of inert gas, the alkaline nitrite, at a temperature not exceedingNot 10 ° C and finally the reaction mixture is poured into ice water.
