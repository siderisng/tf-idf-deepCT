<ABSTRACT>
Power control circuit, intended to provide, in direct connection, a sufficient intensity current for controlling the base of a power transistor (21) in a cutting feeding device.The invention provides for acquiring different analog information representative of the electrical and magnetic states of the controlled device, to treat them, and to group them into a central monitoring processor (47), whose output controls two power elements (48, 49), a providing, the other absorbing the basic current of the switching power transistor (21).Application to cutting power supplies and television line sweeping devices.
