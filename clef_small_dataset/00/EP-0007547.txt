filter assembly for intravenous liquid administration apparatus.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
a filter for intravenous liquid administration is provided  having a filter housing (1); a filter chamber (13) in the housing;  an inlet member (17) and an outlet member (22) in the housing  (1) in fluid flow communication with the filter chamber (13); a  filter (11) in the filter chamber (13) from the inlet member (17)  to the outlet member (22) so that all through flow must pass  through the filter; and dividing the chamber (13) into two  portions (15, 16), one upstream (15) and one downstream (16)  of the filter; a vent (26) in the housing (1) in flow communica­ tion with the upstream portion (15) of the filter chamber (13);  and a liquid-impermeable gas-permeable porous member  (11) disposed across the line of flow through the vent (26), so  that all vent flow must pass through the member (11), the  member (11) restricting such flow to gas to which it is perme­ able; the inlet member (17) being shaped for attachment to a  supply of liquid (21) for intravenous administration; and the  outlet member (22) being shaped for attachment to an  intravenous liquid administration apparatus (25).  
</ABSTRACT>
