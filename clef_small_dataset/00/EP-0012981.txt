<ABSTRACT>
2,4-dinitrophenyl urea is technically in a simple manner and good yield, if 2,4-dinitro-1 chlorobenzene with cyanamide, advantageously in excess, at a temperature of about 55 to 75 ° C to the 2,4-dinitroPhenyl-cyanamide reacts and then hydrolyzed with no intermediate insulation in a wet acidic medium at a temperature of about 40 to 75 ° C.2.4 dinitrophenyl urea serves as a starting compound for the production of 5-amino-benzimidazolones, which can be used example in the production of azo compounds.
