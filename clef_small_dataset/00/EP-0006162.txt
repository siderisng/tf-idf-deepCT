<ABSTRACT>
The invention describes a method for obtaining xylitis from final syrups of xylitolis crystallization by chro matographic separation to weakly crosslinked strongly acidic cation exchangers.In this case, the separation of the final syrup takes place in two or more fractions of cat ion exchangers in the calcium form.The first fraction containing predominantly oligosaccharides and polysaccharide alcohols is subjected to acid hydrolysis and hydrogenated after removal of the added acid.From the hydrogenated solution, the xylitolis is crystallized.From the predominantly xylitol-containing subsequent fraction, the xylitol is obtained after evaporation of the water by cooling crystallization or precipitation crystallization.Subsequent subsequent mixed fractions in which xylitol and other sugar alcohols and polysaccharides occur in different amount ratio to each other, further chromatographic separation are subjected.
