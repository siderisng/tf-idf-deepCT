<ABSTRACT>
a vehicle such as a track-laying vehicle includes first  and second drive transmission assemblies, each having a  control element (32, 34) and associated control valve (28)  for selectively establishing forward and reverse operation  thereof.  a control system includes a plurality of master (52,  54, 97, 99) and slave (110, 111, 112, 113) cylinders for  individually moving the control elements, a first manual  control (36) for operating the transmission assemblies at  substantially the same speed and direction, and second  foot operated controls (38) for controllably overriding the  first manual control for steering purposes.
