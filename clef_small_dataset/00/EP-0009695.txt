<ABSTRACT>
Graft copolymers from a 2 to 30 wt.-% of a poly saccharide ester as graft base and B 70 to 98 wt.-% of a grafted monomer mixture from 1.) 8 to 90 wt.-% ethylene and 2.) 10 to 92 wt.-% vinyl acetate.The polymers are suitable for the production of molding compositions, films, packaging films and coatings.
