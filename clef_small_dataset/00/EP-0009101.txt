<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a new fire protection coating composition of an alkali silicate solution as binders containing a water glass-soluble nonionic surfactant in dissolved form and an alkalitrisilicate powder.This mass has excellent fire-retardant properties and is processed within a wider trisilicate powder area as known masses.
