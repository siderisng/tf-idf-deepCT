<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an implantable pacemaker intended to be associated with a cardiac probe.It is characterized by the fact that it comprises inside its housing, means ensuring a control function, a posteriori, state and degree of dependence of the patient relative to the cardiac stimulator ensuring its therapeutic function,and by the fact that its internal means ensuring a control function are controlled by removable external control means.
