<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a device for the transverse flow filtration of liquids or gases, wherein this device has disc-shaped filter elements (4), which are concentrically arranged by a filtrate collecting tube (5) and within a tubular shell (1), and a parallel to the filtrate collecting tubeextending feed and drain pipe (6, 7) each with one opening (8, 9) per filter element ensure that the liquid to be filtered is fed tangentially, so that the liquid on the filter element takes a spiral-shaped course.The filter elements are provided on both sides with a microporous membrane (12) and have a support layer (11) provided with drainage channels (13) to supply the filtrate to the central filtrate collection tube.
