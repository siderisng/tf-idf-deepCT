<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to display devices, in particular liquid crystal cells, which should be both variable and fixed information (tightening).In such a display, it is provided according to the invention that the fixed characters are formed by body (spacer 14, 16), which at the same time distant the two substrates (carrier plates 2, 3) against each other.In a preferred embodiment, the display is a so-called rotary cell with spacers consisting of a glass solder enriched with spacer elements (17).The invention is used in all types of displays which have a relatively tightly-tolerated plate spacing and contain to solids;It is particularly suitable for liquid crystal displays in measuring devices such as tachometers or clocks.
