<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a method for producing vinyl chloride polymers by polymerizing the monomeric or monomer mixture in aqueous dispersion with radical-forming catalysts optionally suspension stabilizers, emulsifiers and other polymerization aids, which is characterized in that the polymerization is carried out in a reactor whose inner walls and its remaining partsOn which polymer deposits can form, in whole or partially provided with a coating containing certain compounds of the general formula.According to the invention, in particular in vinyl chloride copolymers, extensive suppression of the formation of polymeric coverings can be achieved.
