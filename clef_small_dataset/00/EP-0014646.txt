<ABSTRACT>
in order to improve the mechanical properties of the surfaces, a quantity of between 0. 3 and 3%, and preferably between 0. 5 and 1. 2%, of the total weight of the composition, of a plastic in powder form of the type of the polyethylenes, the polypropylenes and their copolymers is incorporated, in a mixer to the hot and dry mixture of mineral fillers, before the introduction of the hydrocarbon binder.  prior to the addition of the binder, the hot mineral fillers and the plastic powder are mixed sufficiently in order to obtain a homogeneous distribution of the plastic over the surface of the mineral components.  the maximum size of the plastic grains is 2. 5 mm, the quantity of particles less than 0. 2 mm not exceeding 10% of the total weight of the plastic. 
</ABSTRACT>
