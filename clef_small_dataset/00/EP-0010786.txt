<ABSTRACT>
In electrolytic recovery or galvanic deposition of ne-metals from solutions with generally small ne-metal ion concentration, it is expedient to place the electrolyte in particular by fumigation in circulation and thus avoid the added hung of a concentration gap.In order to dispense with complicated cell enclosures or special cell constructions, the invention provides for use with support rods provided with support rods of UN soluble metal which on the lower edge of a via the width extending with gas outlet openings (7), detachably mounted pipe(6) and an anode connected via their longitudinal side, to the tube (6) leading gas supply (9).By using quick couplings (8) and plug-in sleeves (10), a fast pipe exchange or faster connection to a gas supply line (14) can be achieved.
