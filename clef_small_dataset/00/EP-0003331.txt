<ABSTRACT>
An X-ray film cassette has a bottom part (1), a lid portion (2) and connecting elements for closing the two cassette parts.At a cassette part, a magnetic plate (8) for the film size is arranged approximately equal to the same magnetic plate (8) and in the other cassette part, a counter-plate (10) of a material sufficiently large magnetic moment receiving material.With closed cassette, amplifier foil (9) and film between magnetic disk (8) and counter-plate (10) are amplifier foil (9) and film.In such a cartridge, an absolutely uniform pressure between the amplifier foil and X-ray film is ensured, whereby the absorption ratios are improved.
