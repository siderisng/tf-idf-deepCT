<ABSTRACT>
microcapsular electroscopic marking particles for use  in toning latent images comprise a pressure rupturable  shell and a pressure fixable core.  the pressure rupturable  shell is formed by causing first and second reactive substances  in separate phases to undergo a polycondensation  reaction at the interface between the phases.  this enables  the thickness and robustness of the shall to be controlled  so that the particles have suitable characteristics for normal  handling without requiring unnecessarily high fixing pressures.
