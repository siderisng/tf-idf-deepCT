<ABSTRACT>
To spacer the control hole plate (5) from the front panel (1) of a gas discharge display device (plasma display), a stack of several perforated glassfo releases (2,3,4) is proposed which carry on its surfaces of metal layers (7.8).The metal layers (7,8) have floating electrical potentials and distribute the electrical field in the accelerometer filled from the spacer holder.
