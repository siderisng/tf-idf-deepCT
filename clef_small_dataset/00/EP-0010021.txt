<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to serial-parallel print devices for impressing an electrosensitive paper.The device comprises translation control means, at a constant speed, of an electrode holder (1) supporting n electrodes E₁ to can print each in consecutive points.The print order is supplied by an electrical signal resulting from a photodetector (23) capturing the light emitted by a light source (6) through one of the k holes of a movable plate (5) whose displacement.is neck to that of the electrode holder (1).Application to fax machines.
