<ABSTRACT>
a method for producing thermoplastic resin-molded  articles is described which comprises heating a sheet com­ prising a crystalline thermoplastic resin, which is crosslink­ ed to a degree of crosslinking of more than 5%, to a tem­ perature higher than the melting point of the crosslinked  thermoplastic resin and below the decomposition tempe­ rature of the same, and press-molding the preheated sheet  between a pair of press molds maintained at a temperature  below the melting point of the crosslinked crystalline ther­ moplastic resin.
