<ABSTRACT>
Method for treating strand-shaped textile material with gaseous and / or liquid treating agents in jet dyeing systems, wherein the feed of the jet with the strand of goods and its locomotion in the system is accomplished with tels of a nozzle under pressure on the fiber material gas flow, in particular steam,.Optionally, gas flow can alternate or combine for the drive.The drive is particularly suitable for the isothermal dyeing from stranded textile material with hot-fixable dyes after pull-out technique.
