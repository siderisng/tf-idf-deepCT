<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the field of refractory materials.It relates to refractory compositions comprising the following essential constituents: (1) 30 to 60% by weight of a coarse fraction made of grains, more than 50% of which are greater than 0.5 mm;(2) 10 to 30% by weight of a fine fraction consisting of particles, at least 50% of which are less than 75 microns;(3) 10 to 30% by weight of a very fine fraction consisting of particles, at least 50% of which are less than 5 microns;(4) 10 to 30% by weight of an ultra-fine fraction consisting of particles, at least 80% of which are less than 0.1 micron;The fractions (1) to (4) being formed of mineral materials of natural or synthetic origin, inert vis-à-vis water.
