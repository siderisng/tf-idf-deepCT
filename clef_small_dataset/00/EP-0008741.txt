<ABSTRACT>
Preparation of carbonyl compounds R¹-CHR²-CHR³CR⁴O (I, R¹ = H or organic residue; R² - R⁴ = H or C₁-C₄-alkyl) by selective hydrogenation of the β-unsaturated carbonyl compounds R¹-CR² = CR³-CR⁴O (II) in the liquid phase with hydrogen in the presence of a palladium catalysa gate and 15 to 50 wt.%, based on (ii), a tertiary amine.
