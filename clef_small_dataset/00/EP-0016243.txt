<ABSTRACT>
paint for the spectrally selective coating of metal surfaces containing a metal oxide pigment which transmits heat radiation and a curable binder which, after curing, retains the pigment on a metal surface and at the same time transmits heat radiation, the transmissivity not only of the pigment but also of the cured binder being such that the paint film, applied to the metal surface and cured thereon, confers on the said surface an average selectivity of at least 2. 5.  preferred pigments are in particular cr2o3, co3o4, fe3o4 and cuo, and the preferred binder is glycerol.  these paints are used for the spectrally selective coating of the absorption element of a radiative energy collector. 
</ABSTRACT>
