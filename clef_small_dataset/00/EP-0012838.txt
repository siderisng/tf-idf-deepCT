<ABSTRACT>
A rod-shaped DC heated annealing cathode in a discharge chamber of an ion source forming the anode is shown in the region of its electrically negative end relative to its residual part in its cross section.As a result, the electric resistance of the Glühka Thode is determined during nearly the entire lifetime of the cathode of the negative end, which is not under erosion, and not by the rapidly eroding positive end and remains almost constant.
