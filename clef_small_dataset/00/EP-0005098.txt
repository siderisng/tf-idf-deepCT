<ABSTRACT>
The compositions according to the invention are solid and dry mixtures of a mono or polyalkylarylsulfonate of an alkali metal (I), of the corresponding hydroperoxide (II) and possibly alcohol (III) from the decomposition at least partial hydroperoxide. M: alkali metal such as NA RH: lower alkyl radical, preferably isopropyl R ': lower alkyl radical N: integer less than the peroxidation of sulfonates I is carried out using oxygen in aqueous solution, at a temperature not greater than 85 ° C, at a pH of between 9 and 10.5, in the presence of 1 to 50 ppm cobalt, by weight relative to I, in the form of cobalt naphthenate. Obtaining dry compositions containing I, II and possibly III is performed by partial precipitation of the reaction medium, filtration and drying of the filtrate enriched in hydroperoxide II. Hydroperoxide II finds an application as a whittleing agent in textile washing. The invention also relates to dry detergent compositions containing the dry mixtures of I and II and possibly III.
