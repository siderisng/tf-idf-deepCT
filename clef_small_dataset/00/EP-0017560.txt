<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a method and installation of manufacturing of nitropraffins.The gas phase nitration reaction is carried out under closed loop pressure with recycling of unreacted products, continuous purge of deconcentration and continuous pressure in hydrocarbon and nitrant agent.The method is applicable to the nitration of saturated hydrocarbus below C₅, only or mixture.
