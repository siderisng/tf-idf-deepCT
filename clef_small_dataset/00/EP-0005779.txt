<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a novel process for the preparation of aryl glyoxyl acids (e.g., phenylglyoxylic acid) by catalytic oxidation of α-hydroxyarylessic acids (e.g. b. of mandelic acid) of the general formula (with the meanings specified for the symbols R, M and N in the description).The method is characterized by the oxidation of oxygen or molecular oxygen-containing gases in aqueous-alkaline medium in the presence of platinum metal catalysts with simultaneous presence of lead and / or bismuth and / or dooren compounds and optionally in the presence of an inert organic solventperforms at temperatures up to the boiling point of the reaction mixture.The aryl glyoxyl acids serve as organic intermediates, e.g.b.for the production of pesticides.
