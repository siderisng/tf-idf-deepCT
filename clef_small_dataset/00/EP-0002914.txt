<ABSTRACT>
a xerographic copier includes a pre-clean corona (13)  and a transfer corona (12).  a developing station (14)  employs carrier beads coated with tetrafluoroethylene,  which material can film on to the drum.  in order to reduce  such filming, the pre-clean and transfer corona currents  are adjusted to the same polarity and to levels, under a  condition of operation of the machine in which no copy  sheet is fed through the transfer station, which produce  charge densities on the photoconductor element within  0. 025 microcoulombs of each other.
