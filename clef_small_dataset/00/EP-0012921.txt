<ABSTRACT>
To increase the luminous efficiency in a light screen for flat image display devices is proposed to place a perforated plate (5) on the inside of the luminous shield glass (1).The walls of the platen ten holes (6) lying over the luminous events carry a metallization (8) and / or an extension of the luminous layer (7) of the shield glass (1) and are preferably open to the screen glass (1) at least one inches parabolic.
