<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a tubular packaging cover based on cellulose hydrate, which in its wall has a fibrous insert, wherein at least a part of the insert-forming fibers comprises at least partially a coating of a mixed chemical substance, which is based on a predominant proportion of chemical binders based onfrom synthetic hydrophilic heat-hardened resin precondensate and to a smaller proportion of optically active chemical material;The invention further includes a method for producing the tubular packaging sheaths as well as their use as artificial sausage skin.
