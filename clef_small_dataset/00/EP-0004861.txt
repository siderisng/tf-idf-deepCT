<ABSTRACT>
a movable work station apparatus includes travelling-hoist  supported tools and a vertically and rotary-wise  movable frame-carried radiation-shielded enclosures (40) for  working personnel involved in tube-bundle-replacement  tasks performed within the shell of a steam generator in a  nuclear plant.  the enclosures (40) are spaced from each  other and accommodate four upright workers in mutally  facing pairs at opposite sides of a work-accomodating  clearanceway.  this makes work and tools in the clearance-way  observable and accessible from the enclosures interior  via lead glass windows and hand holes.
