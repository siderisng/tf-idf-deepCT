<ABSTRACT>
1.  a soldering iron comprising a soldering tip (3) detachably fixed on the free end of its tubular shaft by means of a sliding fit and an elastically resilient element (4) which holds the soldering tip (3) on the tubular shaft (2), characterised in that the soldering tip (3) is fixed to the tubular shaft (2) by means of a tension spring (4) serving as an elastically resilient element, that one part of the tension spring (4) is secured on the tubular shaft (2) and the other part is provided with a hook (11) which engages with the soldering tip (3), and that the free end of the tubular shaft (2) abuts against the bottom of the internal bore (16) of the soldering tip (3) in forcelocking fashion. 
</ABSTRACT>
