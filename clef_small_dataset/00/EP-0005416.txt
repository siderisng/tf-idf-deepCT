<ABSTRACT>
the present invention regards a method for determining  the surface weight of a mineral wool mat (26), in which the  mineral wool is transported from the spinner aggregate (13) to a  collector band (64, 25) worn by a gasformed transportmedium.   some property of the gas formed medium at the time of separating  same from the mineral wool shaped is measured and used  for controlling the speed of the collector band (25) in order of  eliminating any variation of the surface weight of the mineral  wool mat (26) produced.
