<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for removing silica reactances from a semiconductor surface.Semiconductor materials are treated after polishing with a silica-containing polishing agent with an aqueous phosphoric acid solution and optionally a sulfuric acid solution and rinsed with an ammonium hydroxide solution and deionized water to release colloidal silica and remove from the semiconductor surface.Process according to claims 1 to 5, characterized in that colloidal or amorphous silica can be removed from the semiconductor surface.
