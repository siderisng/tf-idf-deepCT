<ABSTRACT>
In a method for producing plastic, in particular foam moldings, on the basis of reaction mixtures, at least two liquid reaction components are introduced into a mixing zone (5) and mixed together.The reaction mixture is then carried out by an adjustable outflow cross-section (69) and filled into a cavity (21).In this case, the mixing zone is closed before the beginning of the introduction of the reaction components and only by the pressure-based pressure at a predetermined pressure value is automatically opened.In addition, in the filling of the reaction mixture, the outflow cross-section is automatically regulated.
