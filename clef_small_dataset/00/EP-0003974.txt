<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to novel trifluoromethyliinothiazolidine derivatives, a method for their preparation and their use as fungicides.The compounds of the general formula in which R¹, R² and R³ have the meaning given in the description are obtained when reacting thioamides with bis-trifluoromethylminooxal acetary fluoride in the presence of a hydrogen fluoride-acceptor.The active compounds according to the invention have a strong fungithoxic effect.They have a good effectiveness against Fusicladium Dendriticum, Phytophthora Infestans and against Pyricularia Oryzae.
