<ABSTRACT>
gelatinization and dispersion of starch with a starch  solvent such as sodium hydroxide and using mechanical  shear is described.  the process is operated very rapidly by applying a  shearing force to a mixture of an aqueous starch slurry  and the solvent, for example with a centrifugal pump oper­ ated under a back pressure.  no external heat need be ap­ plied.  the viscosity of the starch paste produced can range  from one to two times the shear stable viscosity.
