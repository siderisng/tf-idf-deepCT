<ABSTRACT>
The liquid absorber has an ultrasonic vibration generating vibration generator (9) whose vibrations are transmitted to a atomizer plate (13).The invention relates to the transport of the liquid (25) of a liquid container (17) to the atomizer plate, wherein this liquid transport should be carried out with the lowest possible cost of mechanical means.For this liquid transport, a wick (20) is used according to the invention, which protrudes on the one hand into the liquid container and elastically abuts the atomizing plate on the atomizer plate, while in a damped shape, the vibrations produced in the vibration generator attenuate, whereby the liquid is atomized directly from the wick the liquid.The liquid absorber is particularly used in inhalation appliances and in room spray devices.
