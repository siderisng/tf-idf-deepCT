<ABSTRACT>
1.  method of producing deep-drawn articles with an even mat surface by heat-deep-drawing a sheet consisting of a vinyl chloride polymer, lubricants, stabilizers and fillers, characterised in that a sheet is deep-drawn which contains as filler chalk or aluminum oxide having an average particle diameter of from 1. 5 to 10 mu m in an amount of 10 to 35 percent by weight, relative to the weight of the vinyl chloride polymer.
