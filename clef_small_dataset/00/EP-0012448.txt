<ABSTRACT>
Method and apparatus for separating a material isotopene mixture of the remaining constituents of the hydrogen-containing gas, which is aimed for example for the production of ultra-a hydrogen. The method is intended to allow continuous separation of hydrogen even when it is contained in the gas mixture helium and / or the water material is chemically bound to other substances. A ver seal of the hydrogen should be possible without the follow-up of compressors. For this purpose, the hydrogen present in molecular and / or in bound form is first converted into atomic hydrogen or from the fabric with which the hydrogen has received a che mixing compound is separated into atomic form. The atomic hydrogen thus formed is then passed through a preferably for atomic hydrogen via meabling wall and then collected in the form of moles of culaneous hydrogen. The device consists essentially of two, a common permeable wall (3) and provided with to- and outflow openings chambers (2, 5), wherein in one of the two chambers means (4) for converting molecular and / or bonded Hydrogen is provided in atomic hydrogen.
