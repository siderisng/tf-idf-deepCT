<ABSTRACT>
In a device for monitoring the threadvores of the double stage stitch gripper (6) of a sewing machine, a light source (33) is provided, which emits an approximately to the gripper axis parallel lich beam which by openings (25, 26, 27, 35, and 36) in the gripper body(7), in the coil housing (9, 10) and in the coil (11) is passed through to a light receiver (37).In order to be able to attach the light source and receiver as close as possible to the gripper (6), a part of the gripper is formed as a deflection surface (30) for the light beam.
