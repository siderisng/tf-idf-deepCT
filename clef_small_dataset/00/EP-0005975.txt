<ABSTRACT>
zinc-modified phenol-aldehyde novolak resins for use as  a color-developing agent in pressure-sensitive record sheet  material are made by reacting together, preferably by  heating, particulate zinc oxide or carbonate, ammonium  benzoate and a phenol-aldehyde novolak resin.  the resulting  modified resin has good color developing properties and is  resistant to desensitization when coated on to paper and  dried in contact with a heated drum.
