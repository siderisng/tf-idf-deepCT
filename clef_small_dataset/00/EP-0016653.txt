<ABSTRACT>
a method and apparatus for transversely stretching a  longitudinally continuous fibrous web or film-like material of  organic synthetic thermoplastic polymeric material in which  the opposite selvages of the web or film (m) are gripped and  are stretched transversely during heating by gripping means  (11, 12, 17, 18), the stretched film being thereafter released.  in order to avoid drawbacks in existing equipment such  as cost and rupture of the material being stretched, the  stretching of the film or web (m) by the gripping means (11,  12, 17, 18) takes place by moving the selvages along two  divergent arcuate paths lying in the same plane.  
</ABSTRACT>
