<ABSTRACT>
low pass filter for low frequency signals, with respect to an average value, upper reasters and lower parts.The filter comprises at least two integrators (101, 102) each comprising a capacitor (110,120) connected between the input and the output of a voltage reversing amplifier (111, 121) and a current source (104, 106) connectedAt said output and three threshold comparators, one (107) at the filter inlet, the second (108) after the first integrator, the century (109) at the filter outlet.
