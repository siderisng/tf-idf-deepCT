<ABSTRACT>
In a cylinder head gasket (1) of optionally a metallically reinforced soft material plate made of fibrous and / or porous material, the legs (6, 7) of the metallic enclosures (5) are preferably in the region around the combustion chamber passages (2) on or on both sides at their endof annular depressions or beads (10) in the soft material (1) angled.This creates a fixed adhesion of the bördle legs (6, 7) on the soft substance (1), so that in the subsequent impregnation by dipping, rolling or spraying the residual surface areas of the soft material are impregnated while the areas below the mount (5) remain free of impregnating agents.
