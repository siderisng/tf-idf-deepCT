<ABSTRACT>
A drive device (10) having a low voltage circuit breaker (1) having a lining lever (6) comprising of this outstanding rocker arm (6) and a housing (10) has a housing (12) and a deflection mechanism mounted therein with a fork-shaped, drivable by a rotational movement lever (32) for converting the rotational movement into a rectilinear movement required to actuate the rocker lever (6). On the fork arms (30, 31) of the lever (32) radially to the axis of rotation (33) a slider (21) is slidably guided, which has a circular cylindrical opening (22) for a substantially spherical head piece (16), which with a Recording opening (20) for the end of the rocker lever (6) is provided. On the housing (12) of the drive device (10) a slide (40) is attached as a lead surface for the headstuck (16). On a shaft stump (34) of the fork-shaped lever (32), for example, an actuating handle (36) may be attached.
