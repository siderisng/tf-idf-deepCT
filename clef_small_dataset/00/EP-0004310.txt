<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a method for producing silanes of the general formula RasiHB (OR ') 4- (A + B) wherein R for an optionally substituted alkyl, alkenyl or aryl radical of up to 7 C atoms, R' for oneAlkyl group with 1-4 C atoms and A and B for 1, 2 or 3 and the sum of A + B is at most 4, by reacting Si-H-group-containing organopolysiloxanes with compounds of general formula Rasi (OR ') 4-A, R, R 'and A have the above meaning, in the presence of known per se, the exchange reaction favorable redox-stable siloxane polymerization catalysts, especially in the presence of Al (III) chloride.
