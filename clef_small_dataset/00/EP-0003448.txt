<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to temporal telephone caps that use MIC modulation.It consists in using to construct the connection network of such a switch of standard circuits of a single type called symmetric temporal matrix (MTS).In a particular embodiment a MIC telephone junction is multiplied on two inputs of two MTS respectively (404-405), and these are combined with two bilateral junctions (402-403).It allows to build simple and easily stretchable capacity capabilities.
