device for biological treatment with circularly polarized electromagnetic waves.

<ABSTRACT>
the device for biological treatment by means of electromagnetic radiation comprises an oscillator 1 which produces a frequency of 27. 12 mhz which is applied to a variable tuned amplifier 2 receiving from a modulation generator 3 an all or nothing modulation signal of recurrence frequency and of adjustable form factor.  the output of the amplifier 2 is applied directly to the power amplifier 4 controlling a first frame 5.  a second frame 8, situated in a plane perpendicular to that of the first one, receives the output of another power amplifier 7, which comes from the tuned amplifier 2 by way of a phase shifter 6 producing a phase shift of 90 deg  to the carrier frequency of 27. 12 mhz.  application to biological treatments.  <image>
