<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for purifying epoxides or epoxides containing mixtures which up to 2 wt.-% of a carbonyl compound containing 1 to 5 carbon atoms, characterized in that the epoxide is initiated in the central region of a distillation unit, above the inlet of the epoxy-containing product stream, a compound having one or more unsubstituted NH₂ groups initiates and above the introductory site of thisAt least one NH₂ group containing compound into the distillation column, the purified epoxide as head product or as a side stream.
