<ABSTRACT>
1.   a device for mounting on a scaffold a single-piece floor-element forming a complete floor-section extending between two lateral vertical scaffolding frames, by resting at its ends by means of hanging lugs (12) on horizontal cross-members (11) of the said frames, the said hanging lugs (12) comprising rigid pins (18) inserted in the said horizontal cross-members (11), characterized in that the said pin (18) of each lug (12) fastened to the ends of the floor element (10) is greater in length than the diameter of the horizontal cross-members (11) and is inserted in a corresponding vertical diametrical hole (25) of the said cross-members (11) to form a rigid semi-embedment of the floor element (10) in the cross-members (11).
