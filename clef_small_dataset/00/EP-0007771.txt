<TITLE>
wire stripping tool.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

<ABSTRACT>
a wire stripping tool is described in which the gripping  jaw (10) is pivoted mid-way along its length to a frame part  and at one extreme end to a lever handle (12).  the lever  handle is connected to a drawbar (32) by a pair of links (16,34)  so that in closing movement of the lever handle one of the  links serves to aid jaw closing, and subsequently the second  of the links displaces the drawbar so that a cutting jaw (26)  mounted inside the gripping jaw is displaced relative to the  gripping jaw.  
</ABSTRACT>
