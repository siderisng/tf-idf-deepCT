<ABSTRACT>
- Improvement in semiconductor positive drug manufacturing processes.-The process avoids the appearance of the phenomenon of the "bird spout" and allows, however, the direct deposition of the mask in Si₃n₄ on the silicon substrate.It includes the following steps;(a) development of a semiconductor substrate typically silicon (10);(b) implantation of neutral ions (argon) in the surface layer (20) of the substrate to damage it, and make it almost amorphous, this step is followed by an annealing;(c) Training on said layer of a silicon nitride mask (Si₃n₄) according to a desired configuration;(d) Oxidation of expo portions of the substrate to form regions (16) of built-in silicon dioxide.-Application for the manufacture of insulated semiconductor dispositions by regions made of recessed dielectric material with high integration density.
