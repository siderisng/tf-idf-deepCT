<ABSTRACT>
an ink composition includes phtalein indicator color-developed  by the combination of inorganic weakly basic materials  and organic amines.  this ink composition provides  bright color, is good in stability and in long storage property  free of color fading.  the image of this ink composition can be  erased by drying or wiping up with moistened fibrous materials  such as cloth and paper.
