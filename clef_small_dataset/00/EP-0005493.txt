<ABSTRACT>
Dry steam and liquid flow straightening element, and separation between steam and liquid, comprising an external vertical tube 4 of the vapor-liquid mixture to be separated in swirling descending flow, and a coaxial internal tube 3 ofDry steam collection, equipped with steam flow straightening means 2, the lower edge of the outer tube being at a lower level than the upper edge of the inner tube.It comprises orifices on the perimeter of the lower edge of the outer tube, descending width upwards, and straightening members of the flow of gravity descending liquid along the wall of the outer tube.Application to wet water vapor separators.
