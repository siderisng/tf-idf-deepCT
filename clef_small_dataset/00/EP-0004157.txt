<ABSTRACT>
in an oil-filled electric cable installation, when the oil  flows outwardly from the cable to a pressure-maintaining oil  tank it passes through a percolating filter (35), preferably of  an active material, to remove particulate and/or dissolved  impurities.  a one-way return passage (9) bypasses the filter  so that maintenance of pressure is not impaired, and if  required a high-pressure bypass (10) may be used to protect  the filter from unusually high pressures.
