These polyethers can be prepared (1) by reaction of alcohol or undecylenic acid with thioacetic acid, (2) saponification with an alkaline base, (3) reaction of the pro duit obtained in (2) with the Tetramer of epichlorohydrin or epibromhydrin, (4) reaction of the mesylate or tosylate of the compound obtained in (3) (a) with dimethylamine or methylothanolamine, the resulting compound that can be dirty or alkylated, ie (B ) with the mercapto ethanol or the mercapto glycerol; The compounds thus obtained can then be subjected to polyadditional ethylene and / or glycidol oxide reactions.
