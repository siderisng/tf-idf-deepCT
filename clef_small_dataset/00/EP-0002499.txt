<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to paint binders and their use. The substantially epoxy-free paint binders are based on a reaction product of a) Mannich bases from at least one ether group-free, polyhydric phenol containing two or more aromatic rings, and / or at least one ether group containing single or polyhydric phenol, two or more Containing aromatic rings, at least one secondary amine containing one or two hydroxyalkyl groups, optionally in the mixture with secondary dialkyl or dialkoxyalkylamines, and formaldehyde or formaldehyde forming compounds and b) epoxy resin, wherein the reaction product of (a) and (b) additionally Olefinically unsaturated alkylphenol of the general formula (I) incorporated, with the proviso that R is a radical of 11 to 19 carbon atoms containing one to three CC double bonds. These paint binders are used in particular in their protonated mold for cathodic electrodeposition painting of metal parts.
