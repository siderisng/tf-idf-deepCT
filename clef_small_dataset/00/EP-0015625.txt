<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for the treatment of heavy products resulting from the manufacture of hydro chlorinated lightweight carbides.Water vapor intercourse is distilled off in the presence of a non-soluble surfactant in water.The invention makes it possible to recover the valuable light products accompanying the heavy products resulting from the manufacture of 1,2-dichlororethane.
