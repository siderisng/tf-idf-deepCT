<ABSTRACT>
a two-component compressed, shaped, carbonated  gum confection is made of a gum portion and a finely di­ vided candy portion, each of them having a controlled  water activity between 0. 10 and 0. 30 which is capable of  surviving long periods of storage when packaged in a mois­ ture-resistant material.  the candy portion is preferably a  bubble gum with a high gum base content of at least 28%;  the candy portion comprises an amorphous candy glass  containing . 05 to 15 milliliter of carbon dioxide in each  gram of candy.
