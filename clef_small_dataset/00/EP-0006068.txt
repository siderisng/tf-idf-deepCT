<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to new water-soluble immunological adjuvants.These agents respond to a type formula in which R is an alkyl group of 1 to 10 carbons.These compounds are useful as immunological adjuvants, in particular to promote immune responses vis-à-vis antigens whose immunogenicity is low.
