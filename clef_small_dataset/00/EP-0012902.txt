<ABSTRACT>
assigned to each of the driving wheels (10l, 11l and 10r, 11r respectively) (l and r) of the vehicle are dedicated hydraulic propulsion systems (a and b respectively) which comprise a hydraulic pump (14l and 14r respectively) and hydraulic motors (12l, 13l and 12r, 13r respectively) and which are connected by a blockable compensating line (25).  during cornering with all-wheel drive, the compensating line (25) permits a compensation of the difference in the quantities and pressures of fluid to be processed by the hydraulic motors of the two sides of the vehicle while, when travelling straight ahead, the full driving power is transmitted evenly to all the driving wheels.  the differential action can also be cancelled by closing the shut-off valve (26) (differential lock).  <image>
</ABSTRACT>
