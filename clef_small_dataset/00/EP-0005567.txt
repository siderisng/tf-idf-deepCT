<ABSTRACT>
1.  a method of manufacturing a colour television display tube having a colour selection electrode, in which in at least one step of the method a supporting frame of a colour selection electrode to be formed, or a finished colour selection electrode is laid in the face plate, in which for the mutual positioning at least three mandrels locked in the face plate cooperate with holders on the supporting frame and on the colour selection electrode, respectively, characterized in that as an adjusting means for inserting the said magnetic parts a periodically varying magnetic field is applied to said parts so as to jolt them in a reproducible mutually correct position.
