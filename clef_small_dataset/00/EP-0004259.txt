<ABSTRACT>
An arrangement for magneto-optical current measurement with a linearly polarized light beam is described.The measuring section contains an analyzer (8) with two mutually perpendicular passing directions, and in one of the partial beams (P 2), a light deflector (16) is provided which directs the partial beam (P 2) alternately with high frequency to a detector (10)which is arranged in the second partial beam (P 1).The detector (10) alternately receives the measurement signal with the noise proportion and only the noise content.
