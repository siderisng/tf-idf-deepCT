<ABSTRACT>
The present invention treats the hydrogenation of chlorine tube rabs to the corresponding hydrazobenzo len in the presence of precious metal catalysts and Cokatalysts.The reduction is carried out according to acquaintances per se, which, however, which leads to insufficient yields and impure products can lead to unreasonable measures without the invention.The new measures contribute to compliance with a defined catalyst concentration, working in strongly basic milieu and the use of certain solvents, which make it possible to filter the catalyst in the heat after completion of the reaction and then from the filtrate the chlorohydrazobenzenzen in great puritycan win.
