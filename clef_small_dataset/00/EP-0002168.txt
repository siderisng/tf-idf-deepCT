<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an auxiliary device for eß fork for winding Spaghetti and for the purpose of making the winding of spaghetti with the eß fork significantly simplify and facilitate.It has a holder to be fastened to the plate (1) with a round recess (3) for receiving the fork tip and a recess partially enclosing, vertically upwardly extending guide member (7).In the recess, a rotatable sleeve (5) with driver pins (8) may be arranged, which is optionally rotatable by means of a drive (10, 11, 12).
