<ABSTRACT>
Phenoxyalkylcarboxylic acid derivatives of the general formula I in which R is hydrogen or a lower alkyl group, R₁ an alkyl group 0 of an aryl, aralkyl or R aralkenyl group whose aryl radicals, which are optionally one-or more times by hydroxyl, halogen, trifluoromethyl or lower alkyl , alkexy or acyl groups may be substituted, R₂ and R₃ may be the same or different, hydrogen or a wedding alkyl group, and n the numbers 0 to 3, and their physiologically harmless salts, esters and amides with thrombocyte aggregation inhibitor and / or lipid lowering Effect are described. The preparation of the substances according to the invention is carried out either by reacting an aminoalkylphenois with a sulfonic acid and a reactive alkanecarboxylic acid derivative or by reaction of a sulfonamide with a suitably substituted reactive phenoxyalkylcarboxylic acid derivative. In a conventional manner, medicines are prepared with these compounds.
