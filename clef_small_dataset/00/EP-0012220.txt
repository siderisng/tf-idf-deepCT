<ABSTRACT>
A Schottky contact (24) with a protective ring zone (23) surrounding it is made space-saving by a self-adjusting masking method. In this case, the surface (12) of a semiconductor body (10) is applied a first (13) and a second masking layer (14). The first masking layer (13) has a larger etch rate than the second masking layer (14), so that when etching the mask window for applying the Schottky contact (24), the second masking layer (14) is etched in the first masking layer (13). When applying the Schottky contact (24), it is limited to the expansion of the smaller mask window in the second masking layer (14). In the exposed, annular, outer edge of the SCHOTTKY contact (24) and the inner edge of the larger mask window in the first masking layer (13) limited surface area (12b) of the semiconductor body (10), the protective ring zone (23) is diffused. In this case, the first masking layer (13) and the Schottky contact itself form the diffusion mask.
