<ABSTRACT>
The seat and lying furniture, in particular bedside, has a lying in the head, medium and foot partitioning lying area and at least over the length of the middle part extending enclosing frame (10) on which the head part is pivotally mounted about a horizontal axis (14) andcan be locked by means of a locking device.The head part (16) is extended over its pivot axis and there by means of horizontal bolts (22) in longitudinal slots (30) of the middle part (28), so that in the case of swing the head part, the middle part also changes its inclination.The head part is partially balanced by means of a spring device.The locking device is arranged space-saving between the head part and the removal frame.
