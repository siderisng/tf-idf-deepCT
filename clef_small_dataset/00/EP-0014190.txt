<ABSTRACT>
1.  means for guiding a drawer, consisting of profiled guiding members (3) and rollers (4, 5), characterized in that one pair of profiled guiding members (3) and two pairs of rollers (4, 5) are provided, the profiled guiding member (3) is designed to receive the rollers (4, 5) on the side facing the earas to receive a guide bar (9) on the side facing the drawer and rolls in steps in cooperation with corresponding stops (6, 12, 13, 14) and the drawer (2) or its guide bar (9) is sliding in the profiled guiding member at the same time or with a delay so that the extent to which the drawer (2) can be pulled out is increased by the distance which corresponds to the distance of the rolling movement.
