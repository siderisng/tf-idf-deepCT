device for filing and selecting suspended drawings.

<ABSTRACT>
1.  device for filing and selecting plans provided with suspending holes along their upper edge, which device comprises two rows of cylindrical pins (2a, 2b) on which the plans are suspended, the pins (2a, 2b) of each row being secured by one of their ends on longitudinal members (1a, 1b) respectively, said pins (2a, 2b) extending horizontally in the same plane and being arranged in pairs one facing or substantially facing the other, in such a way that, when the plans are suspended, said planes are always supported by at least one of the two pins of one pair of opposite pins, the pins (2a) of one of the said rows being fixed, and the pins (2b) of the other row permitting to create, by moving their free end with respect to the free end of the corresponding fixed pins, a space "d" making a passage for the plans, to suspend them, remove them after selecting them, or to replace them on the pins, characterized in that the pins (2a, 2b), are provided at their free end with a conical transfer surface (2a2 , 2b4 ) and in that the pins of at least one of the said rows are extended by a cylindrical portion of smaller diameter (2a1 , 2b2 ) the total length of which is at least equal to space "d", permitting when removing or positioning a plan on the device, to position and support the said plan, in the vicinity of space "d" reserved for the passage of the plans. 
</ABSTRACT>
