<ABSTRACT>
a roller fixture for travel luggage or the like, in which the roller (33) is foldably attached to a roller support (27).  in order that the roller is clearly protected in the folded-in position and can be similarly clearly fixed in the folded-out position, the roller support is designed as a box closed on three sides and the roller is articulated opposite the open side.  a striplike arresting member (40) covers the roller in the folded-in position and at the same time serves to secure the folded-out roller.  in this arrangement, the novel roller fixture constitutes a compact unit which can be fastened to all travel luggage in the manner of a floor stud.  <image>
