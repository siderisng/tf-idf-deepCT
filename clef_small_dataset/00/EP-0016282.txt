<ABSTRACT>
an injection molding apparatus capable of producing  disposable wax patterns or the like and which apparatus  includes a logic control and a servo-control especially  designed to provide a substantially automatic molding pro­ cess and to continuously monitor and to dynamically control  certain variable parameters during the molding process.  the servo-control is a "closed loop" servo type of control  which continuously provides signals relating to the fluid (wax)  flow and fluid pressure which is then occurring in the molding  cycle and which signals are then compared with preselected  input or reference signals whereupon the difference there-­ between is used to adjust or drive the fluid flow and pressure  control systems of the apparatus toward a dynamic or bal­ anced state, thus providing a substantially automatic molding  process.  
</ABSTRACT>
