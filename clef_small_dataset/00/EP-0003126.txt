<ABSTRACT>
in the impact-modification of polycarbonamides, the  incompatibility of acrylic core//shell impact modifier with low  molecular weight nylon is overcome by forming an impact  modifier concentrate of impact modifier and high molecular  weight nylon which concentrate is then mixed with a larger  amount of low molecular weight nylon so as to modify its  impact resistance properties.
