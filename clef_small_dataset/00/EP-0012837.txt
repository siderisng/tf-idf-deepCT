<ABSTRACT>
Method for producing ammonium sulfamate from ammonia and sulfur trioxide.Ammonia and sulfur trioxide are introduced under pressure into a vessel containing a melt consisting essentially of ammonium sulfamate and ammoniumi Midodisulfonate.In this case, a molar ratio NH₃ / SO₃ is adhered to 1.5: 1 to 2: 1.The resulting reaction product has the composition of the melt submitted and is withdrawn.It is essential that you initiate sulfur trioxide into the gas phase above the melt.
