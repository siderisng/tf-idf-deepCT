<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a shaft-shaped countercurrent heat exchanger in which the uppermost part of the shaft is formed by a vertebral chamber (5), which with the accidents anchored countercurrent chamber (4) of the shaft through a central compensation discharge (5c) and a plurality of gas pipes (9-12) connected is.This achieves a significant improvement of the separation degree in this stage and thereby improving the thermal efficiency of the heat exchanger.
