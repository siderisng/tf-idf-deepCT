<ABSTRACT>
neutral copper complexes, particularly copper-salicylate  complexes having the formula cu[c₆h₄(oh)coo]₂ roh in  which roh represents an alkanol, have been found to have  therapeutic activity in the treatment of inflammatory diseases.   the complexes have been found particularly suitable  for the treatment of arthritis.
