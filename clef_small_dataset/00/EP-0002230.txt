<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a heat-pressure melt fixing device (1) for fixing toner images.For this purpose, the device has a heatable fixing roller (56) with a surface of polytetrafluoroethylene, a pressure roller (50), a pressure and cleaning device (40), a wetting device (22) and a heating chamber (60).The fixing roller (56) and the pressure roller (50) each consist of a plug-in tube (54), a coating (53) and a sleeve (52), which are pushed over each other.the plug-in tube (54) of the fixing roller (56) is seated on a drive shaft (55) which carries friction wheels (67) which are engaged with corresponding friction wheels (69) on the shaft (74) of the provided pressure roller (50)to take them with the peripheral speed of the fixing roller (56).The friction wheels (67, 69) are connected to overhole couplings (72) which seat on the drive shaft (55) and the shaft (74).
