<ABSTRACT>
Method for producing low molecular weight polyhydroxyl compounds by self-condensation of formaldehyde hydrate in the presence of soluble or insoluble compounds of metals of the second to fourth main group or the second to eighth subgroups of the periodic system of the elements as a catalyst and, where appropriate, in the presence of endiolufed compounds as a cocatalyst and /or of low molecular weight and / or higher molecular weight polyhydroxyl compounds, characterized in that the pH is controlled during the condensation reaction by the addition of 5 to 200 millie equivalents of a tertiary amine, based on 1 mol formaldehyde.
