<ABSTRACT>
With a hand protection for karate athletes made of a lumpy, soft-elastic protective pad, this consists of a polyurethane integral foam molding, wherein the pipe-shaped forearm region on the arm inside is provided over its entire length with a narrow slot and the hand portion molded after a closed fist balloon-shapedwhich is bent back in the region of the fingertips by about 180 ° versus the hand back of the hand and as a finger holder has an internal integrally formed web, and which is provided with a lateral, lowly angular th and at the front end shelter shell.
