<ABSTRACT>
a self-adhesive tape particularly suitable for use in the  building industry for joining and sealing pvc sheets, comprising  100 parts by weight of a vinyl chloride/vinylisobutylether  copolymer from 100 to 400 parts by weight of a  mixture of nitrile rubber and a polyvinylchloride homopolymer,  from 60 to 230 parts by weight of a plasticiser consisting  of one or more esters of phthalic acid, and from 4 to 70 parts  by weight of a resinous plasticiser which is an aromatic  polyether, an epoxy resin or a mixture thereof, and which  imparts adhesive properties to the composition.
