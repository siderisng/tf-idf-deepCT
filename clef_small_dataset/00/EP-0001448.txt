<ABSTRACT>
a vapor-liquid contacting tray having downcomer  means (10) arranged to discharge liquid onto liquid impingement  area portions (30) of the horizontal part (12)  main top surface of an underlying tray in a contacting  column.  a plurality of slot means (3) is distributed across  the liquid impingement area portions (30) of the tray horizontal  part (12) main top surface to provide vapor flow for  prevention of weeping of impinging liquid, discharged for  downcomer means (10) in an overlying tray, through fixed  size openings in the liquid impingement area portions of  the tray horizontal part (12).
