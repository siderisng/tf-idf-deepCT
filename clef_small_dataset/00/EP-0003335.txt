<ABSTRACT>
Method for producing aminomalonic acid dinitrile from a malonic acid initrile compound, for example oximinomalonic acid nitrile or phenylazomalonic acidinedinitrile.The reaction is carried out at elevated hydrogen pressure in the presence of Raney catalysts in tetrahydrofuran as a solvent and the reaction product is precipitated with p-toluenesulfonic acid or acetanhydride is applied as a solvent and the reaction product is precipitated as acetylaminomalonic acidinedinitrile.
