<TITLE>
apparatus for blowing glass articles.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>
reinhausen maschf scheubeckde<sep>maschinenfabrik reinhausen gebrueder scheubeck gmbh & co. kg.<sep>maschinenfabrik reinhausen gmbhpostfach 12 03 60d-93025 regensburgde<sep>
</APPLICANT>
<INVENTOR>

</INVENTOR>
<ABSTRACT>
apparatus (1) for blowing glass articles (11) with the aid of a pipe (7).  the blowing inlet of the pipe (7) can be connected by means of a coupler (6) to a compressed-air source (18), a control device (17) for the compressed air which responds to a control pressure being provided between the compressed-air source (18) and coupler (6).  the inlet of this control device (17), which can be charged with the control pressure, is connected via a connecting line (16,28) to a changeable volume of an actuation device (29).  the actuation device (29) here may comprise either a bellows or a cylinder with externally actuatable pistons.  <image>
