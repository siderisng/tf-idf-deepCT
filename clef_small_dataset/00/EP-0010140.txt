<ABSTRACT>
a data entry system comprises a plurality of data entry  and display devices (10, 24, 34, 36, 38, 40) each coupled to a  common communications link (22).  each device includes a  data processor and local data storage for processing entered  data.  at least two of the devices (10, 40) also include a further  data processor and storage for control and data collection for  the system.  either one of these devices may be designated as  the controlling device and switched in a control mode by  entering suitable information into its keyboard.  devices 10  and 40 also include a disk file for program storage and data  collection for the system.  control can be switched from one of  these devices to the other without physical reconfiguration of  the devices in the system.  by transferring a file disc from one  to the other, all of the program and update collected data are  also transferred.
