<ABSTRACT>
In the method of obtaining pure acetyl chloride with a phosphorus content below 1 ppm, the crude, phosphorus compounds containing acetyl chloride are introduced in intimate contact with 1 to 20 liters of gaseous chlorine per liter of liquid, raw acetyl chloride and then fractionated.
