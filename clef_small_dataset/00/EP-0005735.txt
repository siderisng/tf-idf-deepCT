<ABSTRACT>
a circuit arrangement for phase modulation with generation of a synchronously phase-modulated carrier frequency is specified which is used for data transmission without dc component by means of double- sideband modulation of an auxiliary carrier.  the circuit arrangement essentially comprises a rectangular-wave oscillator, a divider, exclusive-or logic elements and an integrator circuit, which are connected in such a manner that a triangular-wave output signal is supplied at the output of the integrator circuit, which signal is band-limited for maximum utilisation of a limited frequency band and which essentially corresponds to the band-limited output signal of a pure sinusoidal-wave phase modulator.  <image>
