<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the surface elastic wave filters made on a piezoelectric wafer.The filter according to the invention comprises, at the surface (s) of the wafer (1), a transmitter transducer (2-3), a surface wave coupler (4) and the receiving means of the radiated waves byThe coupler comprising two transducers (51-51 and 61-62) having a first common terminal (m) serving as a phase reference, the voltages of the second terminals (B₁ and B₂) of each transducer with respect to the common terminal being in opposition.phase in the filter transmission band.The invention applies in particular to the reception of television signals.
