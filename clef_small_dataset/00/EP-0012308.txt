<ABSTRACT>
A ceramic burner head has for separate to drove from fuel gas and oxidizing agent for combustion space several, in the combustion head parallel to each other led flow channels for fuel gas and oxidizing agents. These flow channels (2, 3) are equivalent to each other. In order to provide good turbulence when the fuel gas and the oxidizing agent in the combustion chamber as well as a near the burner mouth to see rich preheat zone, the flow channels (2, 3) have an extended cross-section and are arranged in such a way that the one heat exchange between oxidation means and fuel gas enabling partitions (6) extend in cross section meaning. The flow channels (2, 3) extending in the longitudinal surface of the focal head are covered by cover walls (4, 5), wherein for the formation of inflow openings (7) to the flow channels (2, 3), a part of at least one of the cover walls (4, 5) is broken. The soaped Strö mung channels are closed on their front of the combustion chamber front side of the burner head.
