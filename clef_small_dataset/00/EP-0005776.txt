<ABSTRACT>
In a device with a welding station 21 for producing plastic bags made of double-layered foil web 13, on which facing surfaces are secured to closing strips 11 and 12, which extend longs to the edges of the two layers and thereby form a closure, a milling device 16 is provided which is providedBefore growing the film web 13 into the welding station 21 at the places provided for welding, the closing strips 11 and 12 mills and switchable from an ineffective in an effective state for this purpose.
