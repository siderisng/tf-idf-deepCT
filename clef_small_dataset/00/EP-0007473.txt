<ABSTRACT>
For the SZ tilting of non-predralled power wires (2) with sector-shaped conductor cross-section, two litter heads (6.8)) are used (6.8)), which are each arranged behind or before a vertex nipple (5.9) and circulate synchronously with changing rotational movement.Before the first point point for each cable, a forced guide (4) is arranged.The forced guides (4) and the square heads are arranged with the shortest possible distance (A, B) to the sequence points (5.9).In this way, a precise squeezing geometry of the swing assembly (7) is obtained in the area of reversal points of the rotary direction.
