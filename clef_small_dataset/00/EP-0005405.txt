<ABSTRACT>
Method and apparatus for the destruction of microfiches and the like by heat treatment.These microficles are enclosed in a tube 25 with sealed closure and cooked at a temperature sufficient for the melt after which the tube 25 and thrown.The apparatus comprises a tubular envelope 1 provided with a heating electrical resistance 7 and which receives the tube 25 whose plug 26 can be pressed thanks to a manually screwed cap.Application to the destruction of microfiches and similar documents with photosensitive coating.
