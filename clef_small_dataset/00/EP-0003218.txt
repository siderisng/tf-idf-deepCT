knob for a crank lever, especially for window winders of vehicles.

<ABSTRACT>
1.  a winder knob, particularly for window-winding handles of vehicles, the knob being rotatably mounted on a pivot of a winder arm and including a withdrawal-inhibiting mechanism (2) which is insertable in click-in fashion and fast against rotation into an opening of the pivot and which is provided at its one end with latches (8) and at its other end with spring arms (7) formed integrally with a flange (9), characterized in that winder knob comprises a sleeve (1) for slipping on to the pivot, the sleeve being surmounted at one end face by the flange (9) of the withdrawal-inhibiting mechanism (2), a cover (3) surmounting the flange (9) and concentrically enclosing a portion of the sleeve (1), and a winder knob jacket (4) anchored to the sleeve (1) and embedding the cover (3), the winder nob jacket (4) being formed of a material substantially softer than the other components of the winder knob and having a hardness of maximum 50 shore a. 
</ABSTRACT>
