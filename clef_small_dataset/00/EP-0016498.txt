<ABSTRACT>
The invention describes a starting mixture for a dielectric position, which can be used in particular for the manufacture of insulating screen printing ink, compatible with a copper-conductive semicrained layer, ink comprising essentially dispersed in an organic vehicle, a phase mixtureCeramic (s) consisting of zinc oxide or zinc oxides and cobalt, a glassy phase comprising at least one devitable glass as a result of the dissolution of zinc oxide.Application: Thick layer (s) circuit (s).
