self-sticking bread crumb composition and process of preparing a comestible.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
a bread crumb composition is provided which adheres  to a moistened comestible during coating and cooking  without the necessity of batter coating the comestible.  the  composition comprises bread crumbs having a particle size  wherein at least a majority of the crumbs are retained on a 20  mesh u. s.  standard screen after passing through a 5 mesh  u. s.  standard screen, and an adhesive, the adhesive having  been applied to and adhering to the surface of the crumbs.   the adhesive contains a protein at a level of at least about 1%  by weight of the crumbs and optionally may also contain a  starch and/or a gum.
