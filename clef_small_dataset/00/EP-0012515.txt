<ABSTRACT>
instruments are disclosed for testing the susceptibility  of pcm regenerators to rear end and far end crosstalk noise.   the instruments of the invention are more compatible with  pcm system engineering rules since certain important pcm  system parameters are synthesized in the circuitry.  the in­ strument for measuring susceptibility to rear end crosstalk  includes a filter (20) having a voltage transfer function   1 / r₃|f/fo|3/4 and the instrument for measuring suscepti­ bility to far end crosstalk includes a filter (50) having a  voltage transfer function  1 / r₃|f/fo|.
