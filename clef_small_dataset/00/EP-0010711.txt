<ABSTRACT>
Metallized, textile fabrics made of synthetic polymers or native fibers on which a metal layer has been wound up nasystemlessly de-energized, are particularly suitable as reflectors for electroma beam waves in the range of 10 MHz to 1000 GHz.In stretched, metallized tissues, the reflection DE radiation is partially polarized, which facilitate or increase the recognizability of an object with radar rays.Due to periodic stretching and relieving the tissue even a modulation of the reflected Mi crowells can be achieved.
