<TITLE>
welding method for joining a branch pipe to the wall of a tube or a vessel.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>
geffen tech adviesbureau bvnl<sep>drabus b.v.<sep>drabus b.v.prins willem alexanderlaan 1491nl-7312 gc apeldoornnl<sep>
</APPLICANT>
<INVENTOR>
stroo christiaan albertusnl<sep>stroo, christiaan albertus<sep>stroo, christiaan albertusno. 40, mezenlaandierennl<sep>
</INVENTOR>
<ABSTRACT>
the method of using the frictional heat and pressure  produced by a rapidly rotating non-cutting piercing tool to  weld two or more parts, such as a piece of tubing on one  hand and a plate, a tube or another body having locally a  relatively thin wall on the other hand, together, to which  end said piercing tool is forced through the two or more  parts after the latter have been placed in the exact relative  positions.  
</ABSTRACT>
