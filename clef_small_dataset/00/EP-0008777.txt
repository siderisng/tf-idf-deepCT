<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to magnetic recording media, consisting of a non-magnetic carrier material and a niche-mounted matisable layer containing fine-distributed magnetic particles in a polymeric binder and additives, wherein the surface of the magnetizable layer is provided with a coating of a perfluoropolyether oil mixture as a lubricant.
