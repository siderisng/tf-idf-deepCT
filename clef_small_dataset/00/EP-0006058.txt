drilling unit for steel products.

<ABSTRACT>
a drilling unit for steel products, and in particular for beams, profiles, sheets and wide flats.  this drilling unit is characterized in that it comprises means (32) for the translational movement of the drilling assembly (22) in order to form a series of holes arranged transversely with respect to the advance of the steel products (18).  the invention concerns the machining, in particular the drilling, of steel products.  <image>
