<ABSTRACT>
Process for the preparation of self-crosslinking bin models for cathodically depositable electrodeposition coatings which are obtained by reacting hydroxyl groups, optionally with mono and / or dicarboxylic acids modifi-cited epoxy resin amine adducts with unsaturated and basic monoisocyanates in the presence of isocyanitary solvent, wherein isocyanates with theGeneral Formula OCN-R- (NCO) N with a tertiary dial kylalkanolamine and then with a connection with a free hydroxyl group and 1 to 3 CH₂ = Cr₁ ₋ groups, where R₁ = H or CH₃ is reacted.The final product has at least 0.5, preferably 0.5 to 2.5 and / or intended double bonds and at least 0.15, preferably from 0.5 to 2.5 basic nitrogen atoms per 1000 molecular weight units.
