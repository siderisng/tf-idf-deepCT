<ABSTRACT>
The precision spot welding head consists of a feed carriage (2) and a electrode carriage mounted therein (4), by means of which an always accurate reproducible resistive fan point welding groove is possible.The electrode carriage (4) is designed as a clamping device (5) for the welding electrode, wherein a pressure spring (21) serving for applying the electrode force is mounted within the electrode carriage.Both the electrode carriage as well as that of the latter feed carriage (2) are each guided in ball guide rails (32 and 33).
