<ABSTRACT>
a method and apparatus for detecting longitudinal faults  in a conveyor belt (1) while the belt (1) is in motion.  pairs of  transducers (t₁, d₁) (t₂, d₂) etc. , are spaced across the belt  (1) and transmit respective acoustic signals across partially  overlapping regions.  each transducer is a piezoelectric disc  which has a synthetic plastic tyre and an aluminium rim.
