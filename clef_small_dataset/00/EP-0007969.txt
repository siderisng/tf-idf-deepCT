<TITLE>
process and apparatus for burrless cutting of castings.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

</APPLICANT>

<ABSTRACT>
a method and a device for burr-free cutting, a cover part through which the cut is simultaneously made being attached in the cutting area to the underside of the continuous casting to be cut.  the cover part cut along with the continuous casting is then removed.  the cover part can consist of individual pieces or a synchronously guided strip of material.  <image>
