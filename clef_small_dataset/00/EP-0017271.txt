<ABSTRACT>
Clamping device (1) wherein the knives (8) for grinding in a knife head (7) remain inserted.This is rotatable in the clamping device (1) about a knife head axis (12), as well as a pivot axis (15) pivotally arranged.In order to provide a clamping device (1) which has compacker outer mass and when adjusting the blades (8) has little moving freedom, as well as a good adjustment accuracy, the pivot axis (15) for knife head axis (12) is inclined by a pointing angle δ suchand arranged that the smallest distance between the pivot axis (15) and knife head axis (12) is outside the clamping device.
