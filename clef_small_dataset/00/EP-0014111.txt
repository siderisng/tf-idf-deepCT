<ABSTRACT>
A method of manufacturing the oxydrylated compounds of neither ckel from a solution of nickelleous ions, comprising the following steps: a) The said nickelous ion solution is subjected to electrolysis, the catholyte being maintained at a pH between 1 and6 and at a temperature below 50 ° C;b) The precipitate obtained obtained in step a) is recovered.The process may further comprise the conversion of the pure nickel pure pure hydroxide by contacting the oxhydryl complied with a base.
