<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for stabilizing an image layer of an organic polymer material on a pad.For this purpose, the pad is treated with the resist image of an electrodilless glow discharge in an atmosphere with a content of fluorine, for example with a content of CF₄, under low pressure to cure the exposed areas of the resist layer, and then the layer is heated.With the method according to the invention resist images are obtained, which have temperature stabilities up to temperatoses of over 300 ° C.
