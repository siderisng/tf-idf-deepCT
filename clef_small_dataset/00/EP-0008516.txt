<ABSTRACT>
a steering arrangement 10 for a bicycle having a steering  shaft 14, said arrangement 10 comprising a steering wheel 11,  a generally "u" shaped member 12 extending from said  wheel 11 for the securing of the wheel to said shaft 14 and a  clamp 15 to adjustably clamp said "u" shaped member 12 to  said shaft 14 so that said steering wheel 11 may be adjusted  relative to a rider of the cycle.
