<ABSTRACT>
the treatment of gas streams such as exhaust gases from  internal combustion engines to remove carbon monoxide and  hydrocarbons by oxidative combustion and nox constituents  by reduction to nitrogen and water is accomplished by contacting  the gas streams with a catalyst composition comprising  a group viii metal supported on the surface of the pores  of a hydrothermally stable hydrophobic form of zeolite y.
