<ABSTRACT>
Catalyst comprising a magnesium compound and a transition metal halide, method for preparing said catalyst, catalytic system comprising said catalyst and method for polymerization using said catalytic system. The catalyst comprises the product obtained by contacting (a) a atelium compound containing at least one species selected from the monohalides mgx (x being a halogen), the HMGX hydrohalides and the hydride MGH₂, said species being formed. when constituted by HMGX or MGX, by thermal decomposition of an organomagnetular halide R₁Mgx (R₁ being an organic radical) and (b) at least one halide of a transition metal selected from titanium and vanadium , the valence of said metal in said halide being less than or equal to 3, the respective amounts of (A) and (b) in said product being such that the atomic ratio of the magnesium to said transition metal is between 1 and 25. Catalyst is used, together with an activator, to polymerize α-olefins.
