<ABSTRACT>
Process for the preparation of alkyl esters of saturated aliphatic carboxylic acids by reacting inherent double bonds containing aliphatic monoolefins containing carbon monoxide and alkanol in the presence of a catalyst consisting of a cobalt compound and a promoter from the group pyridine and / or non-ortho substituted alkylpyridines, where necessary, possibly pa raffine containingMonoolefins with 6 to 20 C atoms in a promoter: cobalt ratio of 3 to 25: 1, a cobalt concentration of 0.02 to 0.2 gram atom cobalt per mole of monoolefin, a temperature of 165 to 195 ° C, a pressure of150 to 300 bar, a molar alkanol: monoolefin ratio of 1 to 10: 1 and a residence time of more than 15 minutes.
</ABSTRACT>
