<ABSTRACT>
derivatives of trypsin kallikrein inhibitor from animal organs, preferably from the organs of cattle are prepared by reacting bpti, or bpti derivatives with free carboxyl groups, with carbodiimides followed, where appropriate, by deamidation or azo coupling with diazonium salts, and these bpti derivatives are used as medicines, in particular medicines whose action is based on protease inhibition.
