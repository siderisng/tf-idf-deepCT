<ABSTRACT>
a method and apparatus for electromagnetic hydrocar­ bon exploration which is accomplished by implanting at least  two pairs (10,15) of electrodes into the ground.  one pair (10  and 6) transmits a signal into the earth, and the other pair (15  and 6) receives the signal.  with proper processing the polar­ ization and resistivity of the ground can be calculated.  the  components (11, 16) are arranged in a manner to insure that  the electromagnetic coupling can be determined and can be  removed from the measurements.  electrode spacing can be  varied to focus on the anomalous stratum.  if an anomalous  ratum is discovered, it is cored to determine the nature of  the material creating the anomaly.  
</ABSTRACT>
