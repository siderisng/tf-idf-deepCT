<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for transforming thermal images, particularly in visible images, and a device for its implementation.This device comprises a visible lighting system giving a beam of parallel light rays, a dioptre reflecting plane or transmitting the beam, an optical device O₁ adapted especially to infrared radiation, and an observation system E, the reflective diopter or transmitter beingcovered with at least one layer of liquid material absorbing the infrared radiation on which said optical device O₁ forms a thermal image of an object emitting infrared radiation, and being constituted by a liquid-liquid interface IA-B of low surface voltage.obtained by mixing two liquids partially misgible at room temperature and under the pressure of use.Transformation of infrared images into visi images.
