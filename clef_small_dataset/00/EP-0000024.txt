<ABSTRACT>
Mixes of oligomeric phosphorus compounds obtained by reaction in the first stage of 1 to 6 valuable alcohol with phosphine phosphonal and phosphoric acid derivatives containing at least an anhydride oxygen and may contain still terminal acid p-OH radicals and subsequent implementation in the2. Level with alkylene oxides or alkylene carbonates.These phosphorus compounds containing hydroxyalkyl phosphone ester groups are suitable directly as a flame retardant for textiles or as precursors.
