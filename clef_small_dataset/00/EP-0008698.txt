<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an electrical capacitor containing a wrap of dielectric films of paper and / or plastic and metallic coverings.At the condenser sator, at least one winding (1) is housed in a gas-tight enclosed housing (2).The housing further includes an overpressure control system and is filled with an impregnating liquid (9), in particular with mineral oil, except for a buffer volume.The impregnating liquid (9) is saturated with an electronegative gas (11), and the PUF fervolume in the housing (2) is also filled with this gas (11) in an amount that ensures pressure higher than the normal pressure, but loweras the pricing pressure for the security system.The invention is particularly suitable for regenerable DC and AC capacitors.
