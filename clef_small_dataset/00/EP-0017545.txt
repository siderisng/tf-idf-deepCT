<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a removable function block or drawer 32, 34 which is introduced into a medium voltage cell 38, open to the front, said block or drawer providing at least the sectioning-isolation function and the disconnect function of aAdjacent cutoff 33. The block is characterized by the combination: a) a sliding chassis 42 in slides 39, 41, B) of a movable crew I compound: - a central cylinder 2 rotating, - ofPoles 4, 5 of disconnector composed each of a conductive core 6 cooperating with the fixed contacts 37 of the cell in their vertical position, and with other contacts 15 of the chassis, in their horizontal position, c) of screens 11,12 attached to the chassis, offset on either side of the horizontal diametrical plane of the cylinder 2. Application to medium voltage cells.
