<TITLE>
method and apparatus for indicating and adjusting the angular position of a sail.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

<ABSTRACT>
apparatus and method for indicating and adjusting the  angle between the horizontal tangent to the luff of a sail and  the direction of the apparent wind.  the apparatus comprises a  bracket (10, 24, 40 or 61) carried by the effective luff of the sail  (13, 26 or 67) and a wind vane (14, 27, 44 or 63) responsive to  the direction of the apparent wind which is pivotally mounted  on said bracket (10, 24, 40 or 61).
