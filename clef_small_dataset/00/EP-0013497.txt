<ABSTRACT>
process for preparing particulate, porous, amorphous  water-insoluble poly(alumino-silicate) having a si/al ratio of  2:1 to 100:1, of high surfa-e area, and in high yield, by mixing  an aqueous solution of an alkalimetal aluminate and an  aqueous solution of silicic acid, allowing the aluminate and  silicic acid in the resul-ant reaction mixture to polymerize to  poly(alumin-silicate), and thereafter removing sufficient  water from the reaction mixture to permit recovery of the  particulate poly(alumino-silicate) therefrom; and particulate  poly(alumino-silicate) prepared thereby.
