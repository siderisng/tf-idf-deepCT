<TITLE>
method for improving the processing qualities of powdery polyolefins
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The processing properties of powdery polyolefins should be improved.A polyolefin powder is desired, which can be processed on screw machines to almost bubble-free product.The polyolefin powder is heated to a temperature between 40 ° C and its melting point and brought into the gap of two cleavage-forming elements.In the gap, the powder is pressed with contact forces of 0.1 to 10 t / cm cleavage length and then crushes the compressed powder.
