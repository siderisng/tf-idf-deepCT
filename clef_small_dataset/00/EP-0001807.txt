<ABSTRACT>
Closed-pored foamed polyolefin film from a mixture of low density polyethylene and at least one polar group containing ethylene polymer and optionally a crystalline olefin polymer, the mixture containing 0.025 to 1 weight percent of these polar groups.The low density polyethylene is preferably a homopolymer, the polar group-containing ethylene polymer is a copolymer of ethylene and vinyl acetate.The foamed film serves to make wall and ceiling clothing.
