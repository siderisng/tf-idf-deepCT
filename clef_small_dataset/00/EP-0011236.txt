<TITLE>
method of producing foundry mould or core and composition for use in the method
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
a foundary mould or core is prepared by mixing with a foundry aggregate a modified polyisocyanate and a polyhydroxy composition which react together to bind the aggregate.  the polyisocyanate is modified by reaction, prior to mixing with the foundary aggregate, with a drying oil.
