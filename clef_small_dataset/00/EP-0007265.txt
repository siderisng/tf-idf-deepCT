<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to novel products of the poly (carbonatethane) acrylic or methacrylic terminations of the formula: wherein: X is hydrogen, or the methyl, R is an alkylene, cycloalkylene or aylene, substitutedor unsubstituted, representing a lower alkylene remaining, Z represents a polycarbonate chain, - C, H, N, O respectively represent the carbon, hydro gene, nitrogen and oxygen.Insaturated poly (carbonates) according to the invention are obtained by condensing a diisocyanate with a diol polycarbonate and a lower hydroxyalkyl acrylate or methacry latter.They constitute a base for ticcable photore compositions resistant to hydrolysis and having good adhesion.
