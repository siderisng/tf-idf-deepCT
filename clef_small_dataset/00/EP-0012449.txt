<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the use of an apparatus for separating and enriching a top mixture of a gaseous hydrogeniso. Except that the separation and antiches of the hydrogen isotopes should be possible in an economical manner, the invention is also based on the object of enabling the compaction of the separated isotopes or the enriched isotopic mixture without additional directions. The device used according to the invention consists essentially of two, respectively closable and provided with attributable and discharge chambers (2, 5), which have a common, preferably for atomic hydrogen permeable wall (3). In one of the two chambers, a direction (4) for converting molecular water substance into atomic hydrogen is arranged. In the second chamber, the hydrogen obtained by the wall is collected in molecular form. The invention further relates to a device for the aforementioned useful purpose, wherein the device consists of a plurality of KASKA densched series-connected devices of the aforementioned type.
