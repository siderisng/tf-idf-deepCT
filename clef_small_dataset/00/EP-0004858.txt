<ABSTRACT>
A device for bending and / or curving pipes or rods includes supports (2, 2 ') for the tube to be bending, which are arranged on a support member (1) and from which a handle (4) emanates the one axially displaceable shaft (6) include.The handle (4) is continuously flowed through a fluid in the closed circuit, the flow rate of which is controllable by a throttle body (12).The pressure increase of the fluid resulting from the throttle body serves to exert a feed pressure on the shaft (6) with the resulting bending and / or curvature work.
