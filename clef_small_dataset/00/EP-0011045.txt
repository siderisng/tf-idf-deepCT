<ABSTRACT>
The object of the invention relates to a safety device for tank vehicles.It is constituted by the combination on the one hand of a detector assembly of the vehicle constituted by an oscillating arm with telescopic shaft (10) of contact with the ground or with an organ used to move the vehicle, mounted on a fixed bearing.on a static portion of the vehicle, such as a spar and resulting in its oscillation shaft a roller and on the other hand, by a transmission assembly set by the displacement of the roller (5) on a piston (2) receiver, connected toThe control of the tanker valve.It is intended to automatically ensure the closure of the tank valves in the case where during the filling or drain operations, the vehicle would make an accidental displacement.
