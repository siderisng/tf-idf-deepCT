<TITLE>
method of welding metal parts.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>
grootcon uk ltdgb<sep>grootcon (u.k.) limited<sep>grootcon (u.k.) limited14th floor church house grange roadmiddlesbrough ts1 2lrgb<sep>grootcon (u.k.) limited<sep>
</APPLICANT>
<INVENTOR>
porter ronald<sep>porter, ronald<sep>porter, ronald17 fairways drive little suttonellesmere port wirral cheshiregb<sep>porter, ronald  <sep>porter, ronald17 fairways drive little suttonellesmere port wirral cheshiregb<sep>
</INVENTOR>
<ABSTRACT>
a process for the arc-welding of cupro-nickel (asme  p. 34) parts particularly pipe ends using a flux-coated stick  electrode, characterised in that an "open v" butt weld is  employed with a root face having a mean width greater than  1/16 inch (1. 5 mm) and not greater than 1/8 inch (3. 2 mm) and  a root gap from 2 to 3 mm the process being further  characterised in that the welding is carried out solely from  the open side of the v without employing additional inert gas  shielding, backing material or subsequent welding at the  reverse face.  
</ABSTRACT>
