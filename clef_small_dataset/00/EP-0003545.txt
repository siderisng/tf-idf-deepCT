<ABSTRACT>
In the field of food and in particular the pharmaceutical preparations, the combined use of lactate dehydrogenase and sucrose-inverting enzyme is proposed to avoid the cariogenic attack.Particularly toothed products result in homogeneous distribution of enzymes and simultaneous presence of a hydrogen acceptor.The food or pharmaceutical preparations with addition of lactatedehydrogenase and sucrose-inverting enzyme are advantageous in the water-poor or anhydrous condition.At the same time, methods for producing tattershake, water-poor, sugary-containing, specially succharose-containing food and pharmaceutical preparations are proposed.
