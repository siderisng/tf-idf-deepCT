<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for regulating the water level in boilers or steam generators.It is active on the flow of food water intake by means of a difference proportional to the difference ε between the measured real water level and a reference level.The proportionality factor is a linear function of the boiler power level with respect to the nominal power, modified by a function of ε.This non-linear ε function of very low values for ε neighboring zero and increases very rapidly for stronger values of ε.In this way we increase the total gain for low power levels and the high values of ε inside a bounded domain.The invention applies in particular to the case of vapor generators of the pressurized water nuclear reactors.
