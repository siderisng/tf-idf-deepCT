<ABSTRACT>
1.  a receiving device for data signals having no direct current component, comprising a transformer having its primary (5) connected to a transmission line and its secondary winding terminals (8, 9) connected via connecting paths to the input terminals of a differential operational amplifier (7), characterized in that a first capacitor (11) is provided in the connecting path between the second secondary winding terminal (9) and the inverting input of the operational amplifier (7), a second capacitor (12) is connected between the second secondary winding terminal (9) and an earth potential point of a voltage supply unit (13), and compensation of the offset voltage is provided by a feedback resistor (15) from the output of the operational amplifier (7) to the inverting input.
