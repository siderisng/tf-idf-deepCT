<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an articulation for manipulator arm.This joint has two crowns 5 and 6, connected with three rods, 9a to 9c, whose ends, preferably bent, are coupled with 5 and 6 by six Cardan joints, 10aa to 10ca and 10ab at 10CB.The invention is applicable to constitute the wrist, elbow or shoulder of a manipulator arm, for example in a paint controller.
