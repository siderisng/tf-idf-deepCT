<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to spiropyrannic compounds. They respond to the general formula (I) wherein: X represents a CH₂ group or a sulfur atom; R₁ and R₂ represent donor groups and / or electron acceptors chosen for example in the NO₂, COC₆H₅, CN, halogen, acyl substituents, alkoxy, hydrogen, alkyl, captoalkyl, groupings, ethers of formula CH₂OR, thioethers of Formula CH₂SR and amines of Formula CH₂NR₂ in which R represents an alkyl group; R₃ is an alkyl, linear or branched group, comprising 1 to 18 car bore atoms, an arylalkyl, alkenyl group. -R₄, R₅, R₆, R₇, R₈ and R₉ have one of the meanings indicated for R₃, or represent two to two, with the plant in ortho, a group - (CH₂) N- in which N is a number included between 5 and 10. R₁₀ represents if x = s a hydrogen atom and if x = CH₂ a hydrogen atom, an alkyl group compror for both 1 to 18 carbon atoms, an arylalkyl group in which the alkyl group comprises 1 to 5 carbon atoms, an alkenyl group comprising 1 to 10 carbon atoms. The compounds according to the invention are endowed with photochromed property and are suitable for recording informations on transparent or opaque media.
