<ABSTRACT>
The invention is a method for the electrophoretic refraction of electrically conductive emulsions, which is characterized in emulsions with an electrical conductivity of greater than 2000 μ 1 / ohm, the conductivity by increasing the degree of dispersion to values in the range of approx. 0,1 to about 2000 μ 1 / ohms and then the emulsion breaks when passing a DC voltage field from about 100 to about 1000 volts.
