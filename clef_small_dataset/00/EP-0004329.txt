<ABSTRACT>
an additional mirror for a motor vehicle having a bracket which  is releasably mounted on the vehicle has an arm bearing the mirror housing (27).  the upper end of the bracket engages with a clamping device (16) around the upper edge (12) of a vehicle door (10).  the lower end is supported with a supporting device (18), for example on the metal of the body (11) of the vehicle or on the lower edge of the window (13).  <image>
