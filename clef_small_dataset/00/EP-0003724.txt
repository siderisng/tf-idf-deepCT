<ABSTRACT>
Mechanical opening and emptying machine of bags that can contain a toxic product.comprising a carrier (1) for bringing each bag (7) to a hopper with produced (3), cutting means (2, 2 ') capable of cutting three faces from the bag during the progress of the latter and a setgripping (4) capable of grasping the cut bag to move it, empty it in the hopper (3) and to bring it to an exhaust location (6), the machine that can be equipped with proper means for automatically controllingThe carrier, the cutting means and the gripping assembly following a predetermined cycle of operations.The machine according to the invention makes it possible to open and empty bags that may contain powdery or fibrous products which can be toxic or corrosive, such as the asbestast, without affecting the health of the workforce, opening up and videring theBags mechanically near the product usage item.
