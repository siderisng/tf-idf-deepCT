<ABSTRACT>
Aluminosilicate granules of the particle size 0.1-0.5 mm with aluminosilicate as a binder is prepared by mixing aluminosilicate powder with aqueous silicate solution or aqueous silica sol as SiO₂ source and alkali aluminate or acidic hydrolyzing aluminum salt as powder and / or asAqueous solution in mixed granus Lators under granulate formation and subsequent, 0.5-30 hours of drying at 80-150 ° C.The SiO₂ / Al₂O₃ ratio in the binder is between 1: 1 and 5: 1.Technically particularly valuable products with a particle size of 0.2-0.4 mm are obtained by mixing alumosilicate powders simultaneously with metaalumine powder and an aqueous sodium silicate solution under Granu lied formation.The process products are used to soften up water during washing and cleaning processes.
