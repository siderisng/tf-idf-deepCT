<ABSTRACT>
A holder is proposed, which serves to releasably attach a housing to a wall.The holding tion consists of a holding partially connected to the wall holding part (10), which has a horizontally arranged tongue (13) and a vertically arranged elongate latching element (16), as well as a suitable on the tongue slot and an elongated latching element on oneWall of the housing.In the case of the tongue of the holding part and pressed against the holding part housing, the locking elements come into force and positively engaged.
