<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a process for the preparation of Bis-2-pyridyl disulfides or their bis-n oxides by oxidation of 2-mercapto pyridines or 2 mercaptopyridine-n-oxides using alkali or alkaline earth alkalines in acidic solutions at temperatures of 0 to70 ° C.
