<ABSTRACT>
an electrochromic display, preferably employing an  organic electrochromic material such as heptyl viologen, has  display electrodes with a rough surface in contact with the  electrochromic material, and is operated at a current density  such that the diffusion depth of the material conforms to the  contours of the electrodes.
