<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method and a Vorrich device for eliminating the accumulating in a reactor or a downstream waste heat boiler in the gasification of coal and in a water bath collect the slag.According to the invention, they collect the slag particles by the addition of additives, in particular heavy heating oil on the water bath agglomerated.As a result, the buoyant particles are moved from the falling slag particles.
