<ABSTRACT>
In the context of a method for producing isolated conductive portions of polycrystalline silicon equipped semiconductor assemblies with field effect elements, a composite insulating layer is provided for the insulation in particular of the side surfaces (31) of the polycrystalline silicon regions (30).The composite insulating layer preferably consists of a thermal silica layer (24) formed directly on the polycrystalline silicon regions (30) with a layer (26) arranged thereon (26) of phosphorosilicate glass or silica formed by chemical precipitate of the vapor phase.Also treated are correspondingly constructed semiconductor assemblies in the form of so-called charge coupling devices (CCD) and an element memory cell structures.
