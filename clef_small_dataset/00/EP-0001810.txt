<ABSTRACT>
Copolymers of vinyl esters and crotonic acid are usually prepared by polymerizing the monomers in bulk or in solution with the aid of a radical-forming initiator in the presence of a controller substance.The polymers should be as free as possible of residual monomers and impurities.This can be achieved in that after completion of the polymerization, the unreacted monomers and other volatile impurities are removed from the reaction mixture.The use of a towing means is advantageous.The copolymers are suitable as raw materials for adhesives and hair coatings, hairstyles and hair sprays.
</ABSTRACT>
