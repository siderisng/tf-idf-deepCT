<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an identification card (10) of a plurality of layers (1,2,3,4), of which the cover layers (1,2) of strong transparent films and the layers (3.4) of the card core of films of medium to weaker Transparency. The layers (3,4) are colored by Pig Matt Matt. The layer (3) directly carries lesible information, optionally over a safety pressure, while the layer (4) an additional securing feature (6), such as a guilloche pressure, a visually only with the aid of a lamp can be read out feature or a piece of one Magnetic tape has. All layers consist of fused polymeric polymeric, for example polyamides, polyvinyl chloride or the like, which form after laminating under a pressure of 9. 8 to 20 bar at temperatures of 353 ° to 452 ° k fused to a block fused to a block which a mechanical, thermal or chemical separation favorable intermediate surfaces.
