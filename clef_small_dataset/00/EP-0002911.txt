<ABSTRACT>
a method of constructing arrays of collimators and/or  detectors for penetrating radiation is described.  a tape or  foil is wound alternatively over and under rods provided in  upper and lower sets to define a plurality of substantially  parallel or slightly inclined channels for the radiation.  the  channels can be filled with suitable plastics material.
