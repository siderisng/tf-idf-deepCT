<ABSTRACT>
a method and apparatus for electron-beam welding in  which the electron beam is directed from an electron gun onto  the material being welded through a magnetic shielding tube,  the lower end of which is water cooled.  in order to clear the end  of the shielding tube of deposits of metal vapour and sputterings  emitted from the weld cleaning devices are provided which are  driven by a motor through a reduction gear, to open and close  across the lowermost surface of the tube thereby cutting off or  dislodging the deposits during the course of welding, that is,  without interrupting the welding operation.  
</ABSTRACT>
