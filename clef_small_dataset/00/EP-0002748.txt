<ABSTRACT>
the invention relates to a magnetic trip device for fault current protection switches having a trip coil, two pole pieces (3, 4), a moving armature (1), having a spring (2) acting thereon and a permanent magnet, the armature (1) being guided and held with respect to the pole pieces (3, 4), according to the invention, for precise guidance of the armature (1) with respect to the pole pieces (3, 4), by means of the spring (2) configured as a leaf spring.
