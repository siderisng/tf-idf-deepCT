<ABSTRACT>
Oven with a stove channel (1) provided with laterally and geben circumferentially arranged supports on which support plates for the focal material (3) are lifted off laterally protected, and withdrawing from below the support plates from the eye bearings, in a transport direction TRA gendenand after reaching a stride length again on the support lifting device.The lifting device consists of stamping (13), on the upper side support plates (16) are placed.These form a longitudinally substantially closed coating.The support plates are on the support plates and touch on the sides.However, they are not firmly connected.
