<ABSTRACT>
Continuous process for producing micro capsules with polymeric capsule walls and solid or left capsule content in which a polyisocyanate is mixed with the substance to be slammed, this mixture, a polyamine and a dispersing aid-containing aqueous phase are reacted, the reaction in a mixing zoneWith high turbulence initiated to form an emulsion and the reaction is then transferred to a multi-stage boiler cascade and the temperature of step to stage is increased and kept constant within a step.
