<ABSTRACT>
Known facilities of this type are dissected in CCD technology. They consist of a prefilter (HV) and the owned scan filter [H (2πf)]. In order to connect the formation and utilization of folding products to the output signal, one has tried to target complicated pre-filters. However, such filters can not be easily integrated with the rest of the system monolithically. Referring to the invention between the pre-filter (HV) and the actual scanning system [H (2πf)], a first scanning system [Ha (2πf)] which has the consequence of the structure and can be produced monolithically in CCD technology without difficulty, Thus, a compli-oriented pre-filter (HV) becomes expendable and can be replaced by a simple RC filter for tape limitation. The first scanning system consists of M parallel branches whose inputs at the output of the prefilter (HV) and their outputs are connected together to the signal input of the second scanning system [H (2πf)]. At the entrance of each parallel branch a clock-controlled sampling (A) is prerequisite, to which a delay and finally one sampling filter (Ha) follows. The bars to control the scanner have the same frequency. But they are at one of the phase shifts. The task of the delay is to ensure that the output signals accruing per sampling period (t) of the parallel branches simultaneously appear. The sampling filters (HA) are matched to the desired filter function and realized in CCD technology.
