<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the connection of the opti fibers, into unit or multiple connectors.The connection of the optical fibers must imperatively be done with a high centering accuracy of the fiber (1, 1 ') in a tip (10, 10'), in order to have a good light transmission through the connection.The invention provides: on the one hand to use the tip as a mold for the polymer injection or fastening glue (11) of the fiber and on the other hand a simple geometric device of the fiber in itsTip, the accuracy of the centering being due to the accuracy of this device and independent of the precision of the tip.Use in donation systems born by optical fibers.
