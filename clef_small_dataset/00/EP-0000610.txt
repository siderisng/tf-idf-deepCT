<ABSTRACT>
spray dispensing of a metered quantity of a preferably  semi-liquid product from a filled container having a fixed wall  (28) provided with at least one and preferably a plurality of  generally open and unobstructed discharge passageways  (30).  a moveable wall (32) spaced apart from the fixed wall  (28) is impacted with a moving mass to induce a pressure  pulse in the product sufficient to surge a metered quantity of  product less than the entire content of the container through  the generally open and unobstructed discharge passageways  (30) with sufficient velocity as to break away from the passageways  (30) and the remaining product within the container  for ballistic travel generally normal the fixed wall (28) outwardly  of the container.  a plurality of uniform discharge passageways  (30) and a proper match of product formulation and  energy input produces a generally uniformly moving cluster  of generally uniformly defined and moving particles.  
</ABSTRACT>
