<ABSTRACT>
The device comprises, in series, a continuous voltage regulator (1), an alternating voltage voltage voltage converter (4), an isolation circuit (voltage transformer 21) and a recovery circuit and filtering (5).A conventional voltage counter-reaction, obtained by comparing a continuous voltage produced downstream (16) of the controller (1) with a reference voltage (VR) allows to control (link 15) the regulator.To this counter-reaction is added a current reaction obtained by means of a current transformer (22) whose primary is in series with the primary of the voltage transformer (21) and whose output current, after recovery and filtering (3), is also used to control the controller.Application to the control devices of a DC voltage requiring isolation between regulation and use.
