<ABSTRACT>
For finishing textile yarns of organic solution, soluble block mixing polymers of at least two different unsaturated polymerizable hydrocarbons having two separate softening ranges, one of which is below 20 ° C and one above 20 ° C, and wherein the blocks with the higher softening range form the disperse phase, used.The preferred simplicity solutions contain block mixing polymers of ethylene and propylene or hydrogenated block mixing polymers of styrene and butadiene or isoprene.For the simple and selective, halogenated hydrocarbons, in particular perchlorethylene are preferably used.
