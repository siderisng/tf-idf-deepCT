<ABSTRACT>
Hydraulic power steering device for a motor vehicle, comprising a direction control member extending by a shirt (20), a rotor (26) and a torsion bar (34) connecting the rotor and the direction control member..The shirt (20) has two pairs of orifices such as 62, offsets axially and angularly, connected respectively to a source of pressure and a drain, and the rotor (26) has two pairs of grooves (28,29),Each groove that can communicate with orifices belonging to different pairs, the edges of the orifices and / or grooves parallel to the axis of the shirt, such as 64, have a non-rectilinear profile adapted to the desired valve characteristic.
