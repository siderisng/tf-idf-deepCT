<TITLE>
mass and force meter.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

<ABSTRACT>
Mass and force meter with a frame, a load carrier and a two dynamometer, a reference size and a force input having measuring system, in which between the load carrier and the frame a load spring (16) and between the load carrier and the force input of the measuring system a weaker measuring spring (15) are arranged so that these springs are partially or completely replaced in mass meter and, if desired, for linearization of non-linear characteristic curves can also serve a wide range of functions.
</ABSTRACT>
