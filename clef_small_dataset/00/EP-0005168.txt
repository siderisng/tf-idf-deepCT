<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for producing PVP-iodine by reacting powdered polyvinylpyrrolidone with elemental iodine at temperatures of 70 to 100 Â° C, in which the reaction in the presence of formic acid, oxalic acid or an ammonium salt or amide of the coal, ants- orOxalic acid is performed.The inventive method avoids the disadvantage of long tempering times, which can result in degradation of the polyvinylpyrrolidone, and is particularly suitable for the preparation of a stable PVP iodine having a low molecular weight polyvinylpyrrolidone.
