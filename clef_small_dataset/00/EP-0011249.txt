<ABSTRACT>
For the preparation of mineral fibers resistant in the alkaline medium, a mixture of cast rock with an addition of 4 to 6% by mass of zirconia is used. A composition of the starting materials is chosen, which is melted between 1350 and 1500 ° C and to further process to 10 to 20 μm thick fibers of high mechanical train strength and resistance in the alkaline medium. These mineral fibers serve to micro-flavoring of cement, lime or plastered or bituminous binders or building materials. For the purely oxidic components of the starting material or the mineral fibers, the following composition is selected: SiO₂ intermediate 48 and 51 mass% Al₂O₃ between 12 and 14 mass% Fe₂O₃ between 8 and 10 mass% Cao between 6 and 8 mass% Mgo between 10 and 12 mass% TiO₂ intermediate2 and 6 mass% ZrO₂ intermediate 4 and 6 mass% K₂O + Na₂O 4 and 6 mass%. To increase the adhesive strength of the mineral fibers in the matrix material, the fibers are pollinated with hydrated or unhydrated cement dust or with calcium silicate or calcium silicate reactive dust.
