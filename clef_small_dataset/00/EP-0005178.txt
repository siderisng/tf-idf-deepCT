<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a circuit arrangement for displaying characters on screens, from the sighting devices operating according to the scanning principle in which the drive signals for the characters of the representable character supply are stored in a character generator in the form of a point matrix and composing from character points of defined point width, and in the First character generator (ZG1) for storing a first subset and a second character generator (ZG2) for storing a second subset of the character points of the point matrix is ​​provided. In an assumed consecutive numbering of the point matrix columns (p1.,, P16), the first subset correspond to the set of all even-numbered columns and the second subset the set of all odd-numbered columns. In addition, a mixing circuitry is provided which shifts the subsets of the character points relative to each other in the row direction around the fraction of a point width and forms the driving signals for the signs from the shifted subsets.
