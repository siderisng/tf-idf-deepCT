<ABSTRACT>
A housing (1), preferably for a sewing machine consisting of a stuff carrier (2) a stand (3) and an upper arm (4) with head part (5) and can be clamped to the spa nabbing processing in a clamping device (6).The housing (1) has bearing surfaces (7) which form reference surfaces for the machining machining.The contact surfaces (7) are opposed to clamping surfaces (8) on which the clamping elements (9/19) of the clamping device (6) are applied.The position of the clamping and support surfaces on the housing is chosen so that they are covered by components of the finished assembled machine.The housing can therefore be machined in lacquered state.
