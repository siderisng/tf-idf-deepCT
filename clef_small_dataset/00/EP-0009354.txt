<ABSTRACT>
a waveform shaping circuit which has large hysteresis  loop characteristics and which is operated as a schmitt  trigger circuit comprises several impedance elements (14a,  . . .  14n) and several reference voltage source units (15a,  . . .  15n).  by appropriately selecting the number n of the  mos transistors (15a, . . .  15n) the hysteresis loop voltage  n-vtha can be determined (vtha = mos transistor  threshold voltage).  the changes in the state of a third mos  transistor (13) consequent upon changes in the states of  a first and a second mos transistor (11, 12) result in a  hysteresis effect while permitting a high input impedance  and, a low output impedance to be obtained.  the circuit  which shapes an input signal into an output signal having  a rectangular waveform is suited for being manufactured  as a large scale integrated circuit.  
</ABSTRACT>
