<ABSTRACT>
A method is given, which allows for spoken words a representation as a colored images on a picture screen.Any sound frequency, that is, every acoustic tone, is associated with a hue.Since the eye can accommodate thirty times as much information as the human ear, it is possible to convey the acoustic information converted into an optical information.For this, the sound color image transform process has been created.An apparatus for carrying out the specified method of a sound color image transformation conversion has a three-channel triangle film ter as the centerpiece.Nine parameters are specified on this filter, three center frequencies and six flank angles that are optimal.
