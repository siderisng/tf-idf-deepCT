<ABSTRACT>
In a method for retrieving a heavy cable (1) into a cable channel (3), cable holder (5) are attached to the cable at regular intervals, the configuration of which fits the cross section of the cable channel (3).With the aid of a tensioning rope (6), which is attached to the cable holder and / or on the cable, the cable 1) is retracted into the cable channel (3).Subsequently, the clamping cable is anchored at both ends under tension.In this way, no tensile stress itself is exerted on the cable even when pulling in and the cable is held by the cable holder at a distance from the inner wall of the cable channel.
