<ABSTRACT>
Preparation of highly concentrated aqueous solutions of salts from dicarboxylic acids and diamines as well as polyamide precalculations by reacting alkandicarboxylic acids with 6 to 12 carbon atoms and diamines of the formula NH₂NNH₂, in which R denotes an alkylene radical having 6 to 12 carbon atoms or the radicals, whereby aqueous salt solutions of dicarboxylic acids andDiamines of less concentration containing a corresponding amount of the respective dicarboxylic acid dissolved in excess, with the respective molten diamine in equivalent amounts;Relative to the dissolved dicarboxylic acid, reacting with the proviso that the reaction is carried out under elevated pressure and a final temperature is maintained from 140 to 210 ° C in the implementation.The prepared solutions are used to produce polyamides.
</ABSTRACT>
