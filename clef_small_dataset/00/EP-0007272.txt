<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method of automatic reading of the irradiation dose of a portable dosimeter with ionization chamber, as well as a device implementing this pro cede.The method consists of measuring for each reading, the value of the charge of the ionization chamber and it is characterized in that it further consists in memorizing for each reading, the value of the charge of the chamber ofIonization, then to calculate at any moment the dose of irradiation from one of the previously memorized values and the value of the charge of the chamber at that time.Application including the measurement of the radia tions borne by staff in nuclear facilities.
