<ABSTRACT>
For the production of molecular sieve-containing granules of good mechanical strength, the constituents molecular sieve, metal oxide and silicate are immersed, mechanically or deformed by dropwise in a faight solution and dried after base exchange and tempered.By selecting the oxide and / or molecular sieve, granules are obtained which are used as adsorbents, catalyst or catalyst support.
