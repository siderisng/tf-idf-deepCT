<ABSTRACT>
heavy-duty wire resistor in which the connecting wire is fed back through an off-centre bore hole of the ceramic housing and acts as a fuse.  in this way, the fuse acts as a temperature limiter, i. e.  the current flow is interrupted if the temperature of the resistor exceeds a limit value.  <image>
