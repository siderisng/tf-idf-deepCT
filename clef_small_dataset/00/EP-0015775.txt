<ABSTRACT>
a thermoplastic bag particularly adapted for the con­ tainment and storage of foods comprising a laminar bag  structure having an interior layer of a high density polyethy­ lene and an exterior layer of polycaprolactam.  the layers are  lightly bonded together at their interface.  such bag struc­ tures are suitable for the containment of foodstuffs while  they are being heated or reheated following storage.
