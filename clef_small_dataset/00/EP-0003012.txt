<ABSTRACT>
this invention is directed to cycloaliphatic acrylate  compositions and to a method of making them.  the compositions  comprise (a) dicyclopentadiene acrylate, dicyclopentadiene  methacrylate or mixtures thereof; (b) a  mixture of polycyclopentadienyl acrylates; (c) a mixture  of the copolymers of acrylic acid with isoprene, piperylene,  methylcyclopentadiene or mixtures thereof and (d) a mixture  of polyacrylates.  the compositions of this invention  are useful to make crosslinkable blends with ethylenically  unsaturated monomers and/or unsaturated resins.
