<ABSTRACT>
In a device for welding metallic hollow body (1, 3) with a remote magnetic field-moving arc acting on a longitudinal weld edge seam path, this magnetic field is generated by a magnetic coil (11, 12) arranged in the interior of the hollow body (1, 3).The device is specially applicable to hollow bodies and extends the scope of the manner known per se with magnetically moving arc by simplifying the workpieces easier when welding.
