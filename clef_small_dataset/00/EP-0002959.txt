corrugated pipe sections (10 and 11) are coupled end  to end by shrinking a heat shrinkable sleeve (12) into close  gripping frictional engagement with the end portions of  the pipe sections (10 and 11).  the sleeve (12) conforms  to the corrugated surfaces of the pipe sections (10 and  11) thereof providing a sound mechanical interlock, and  an auxiliary sleeve (13) of relatively hard thermoplastic  material is used to resist shearing forces.  a sealing ring 17  is provided at the join.
