<TITLE>
means for holding tumbler-like objects in a glass treating machine.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>
ilk emil<sep>ilk, emil<sep>ilk, emilahornweg 4d-8372 zwieselde<sep>
</APPLICANT>
<INVENTOR>
ilk emil<sep>ilk, emil<sep>ilk, emilahornweg 4d-8372 zwieselde<sep>
</INVENTOR>
<ABSTRACT>
a device for holding tumbler-shaped glass articles (tumblers) against a glass-treatment machine, in particular against an automatic blasting or grinding machine, into which device the tumbler (8) is inserted before commencement of the treatment operation and in which it is held during the treatment and possibly during further transport and from which it is then removed again using a cup-shaped holder element (4) into whose cavity the tumbler (8) is inserted and whose base (5) is held by an optionally rotating holder (3) of the glass- treatment machine, the insides of the edge of the holder element (4) carrying sealants, adhesives or clamps (6).  <image>
</ABSTRACT>
