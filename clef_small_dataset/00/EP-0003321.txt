<ABSTRACT>
By successive application of a chromate ester and a titanium ester on a silica-containing carrier in the absence of an unsaturated polymerizable hydrocarbon and activation with an aluminum organic compound one obtains a catalyst for the polymerization of 1-olefins.For the manufacturing method for the catalyst, no washing liquids are incurred, from which metal compounds must be separated before preparation.The catalyst gives in the presence of hydrogen a polymer with wide molecular weight distribution in high yield, which is arm on high molecular weight ingredients and provides virtually tapping films.
