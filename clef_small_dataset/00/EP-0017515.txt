<ABSTRACT>
Maneuvering and automatic locking device remotely from a chest door or hatchback (1) of a motor vehicle, articulated at the top of the body around a transverse axis (2) and offset by a balancier.The tailgate (1) can be maneuvered by means of a pulling (12) articulated, on the one hand, on the tailgate and, on the other hand, at a point of a first toothed sector (17) entraped into rotation,On a predetermined stroke, by an electric motor (11) controlled remotely.The tailgate can, furthermore, be locked automatically after adjusting the box of the trunk (3) by mechanical means (13, 18, 31) actuated by the electric motor as far as the means of maneuver (12, 17).
