<ABSTRACT>
New herbicide and plant growth regulating phenoxy alkanarboxylic acid derivatives are described.They correspond to the formula wherein A is the cyano group, a carboxylic acid, a salt, ester, thloester or amide thereof, q a one to triple-substituted phenyl or pyrid-2-yl residue, R₃ hydrogen, halogen, cyan, nitro, alkylor carbomoyl, R₄ alkyl, alkoxyalkyl, cycloalkyl, alkenyl, alkynyl, phenyl or benzyl as well as x, y and z each oxygen or sulfur.They can be considered selective herbicides in cereals and rice cultures, for inhibiting vegetative growth, z.b.In sojacultures, as well as for defoliation and desicination in cotton or potato cultures, use shortly before their harvest.
