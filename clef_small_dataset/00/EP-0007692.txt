<TITLE>
apparatus and method for filling a valve bag with particulate material
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
an apparatus and method for filling with particulate material valve bags having an elongated valve lined with a water activated sealing means including water spray supply means having at least one spray nozzle (36, 36 min ) for delivering an air/water spray to a location, preferably a cavity (34) in the outside of an elongated filling nozzle (24), adjacent the valve lining to activate the sealing means.  an air supply (32,32 min ) supplies air under pressure to the cavity (34) during filling to maintain the cavity free from contamination by the particulate material.  the spray nozzle may be separate from the filling nozzle.
