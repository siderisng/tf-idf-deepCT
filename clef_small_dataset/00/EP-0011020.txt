<TITLE>
device for aligning an element between two fixed points.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
a suction nozzle 13 engages with an element 12 housed in the cavity 14 of a support 11, in order to displace this element with it, short of sliding over the element 12 when the latter comes into contact with one of the stops 16 and 17.  there is imparted to the nozzle 13 a motion whose component along the axis 20 passing through the stops is an alternating translation.  the total amplitude of this translation decreases so as to substantially cover the interval (l-amax, l-amin), where l is the distance between the stops 16 and 17, whilst amax and amin are the maximum and minimum values of the dimension of the element 12 in the direction 20.  application to the centring of an electronic component in a cavity.  <image>
</ABSTRACT>
