Boron-containing phenol-formaldehyde resins can be isolated from their reaction media by evaporating the hard solution in a coiled evaporation tube with a ratio of throughput to pipe cross-section of 0.1 to 1.5 and with a ratio of throughput to evaporation surface from 100 to 400.It is thus obtained, even in approaches of technical order of magnitude, largely solvent-free and un = networked, meltable and storage-stable resins, the z.b.Can be used as amplifier resins for rubber.
