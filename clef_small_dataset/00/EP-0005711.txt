<TITLE>
device for securing a plinth under a piece of furniture and foot provided with such a device.

<ABSTRACT>
1.  a device for securing a plinth (23) under a furniture unit on feet (1), characterized in that it comprises a slide (19) which is guided inside a groove (12) provided in the external lateral surface of at least one foot (1) of the furniture unit, substantially parallely projecting from the said groove (12) towards the plinth (23) being secured, and a means (26) for moving and holding in place the said slide (19) in the said groove (12) so that the said finger (21) acts on the plinth (23) so as to apply this plinth against a fixe surface substantially perpendicular to the direction of movement of the slide (19).
