<ABSTRACT>
Transfer and packaging device in a sheath (2) waterproof of contaminating products such as radioactive products, as well as a method for implementing this device.In order to allow the transfer of contaminant products (16) into a sealed sleeve (2) without contaminating the environment, a COM device taking a sleeve (1) with a central recess is disposed between a SAS (12) and the sheath., and a double bottom plug (8) is disposed in the recess.The sleeve (1) and the cap (8) are cut simultaneously so as to seal the SAS and the sheath separately.Application to transfer to a reprocessing factory of the Bustibles used in nuclear reactors.
