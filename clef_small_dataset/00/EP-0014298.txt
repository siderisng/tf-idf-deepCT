<ABSTRACT>
Endless carrier at locally different speeds.The carrier uses an expansion-contraction device, comprises a sequence of forks 13 guided on a closed loop path of which a stretch 16 coincides with a section of the path of the axes 2 of an endless carpet, each fork 13 being adapted to grasp.An axis 2 and being connected to one of the adjacent forks, by a regulating binding device, according to a pre-established program, the spacing between these two oven depending on the position of the first fork, so thatThis spacing decreases gravelly in a first zone 17 of the coincidence section and gradually increases in a second zone 34 of said section, located downstream of the first zone.The invention applies in particular to the rolling sidewalks.
