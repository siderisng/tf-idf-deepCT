<ABSTRACT>
1.  process for the one step preparation of 2,2,6,6-tetramethylpiperidone-(4) by reaction of acetone and ammonia in a molar ratio of 2-25:1 in the presence of solid acid catalysts at elevated temperatures, at the self-pressure of the system, which is increased by addition of inert gas, if necessary, characterized in that acetone and ammonia are reacted at temperatures in the range of 80-130 degrees c in the presence of solid acid catalyst, which are as well pratically insoluble in the feed stocks as also in the reaction mixture.
