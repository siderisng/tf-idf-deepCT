<TITLE>
machine for filling a pre-cut slot in the ground.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
an assembly for filling a pre-cut slot in the ground with  granular material, such as sand comprises one or more  compressing members, which may be in the form of fingers  (11,12) or discs, which extend into the slot (7) to different  depths to provide more even placement of sand than if the  sand is simply rolled after being placed in the slot (7).  two  fingers or discs are preferably used to compress the sand to  1 / 3  and  2 / 3 depth of the slot, a ground roller (18) following to  compress the sand in the remaining  1 / 3 depth.
