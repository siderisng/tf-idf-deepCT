<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for producing a solid support based on anhydrous magnet sium compounds and electron donor compounds onto brought catalyst component made of TiCl₄ subsequent reduction of TiCl₄ with a reducing agent of the formula AR RN x3-N, in which R is a C₁-C₁₈ alkyl radical, X is a halogen, particularly Cl, and N an integer, so that 0 <N ≦ 3 is, characterized in that the TiCl₄ is converted into a solution of an inert solvent with the carrier from the magnetic compound and the electron donor compound.The catalysts obtained by the process are suitable for stereospecific polymerization of α-olefins without further measures to increase the stereospecificity of the catalyst.
