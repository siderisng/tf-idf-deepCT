<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for hydrophilizing metal surfaces and / or metal oxide surfaces.According to the invention, a hydroxide of the relevant metal is generated on these surfaces.In particular, one generates a hydroxide of the lowest value level of the metal on the surface.Such according to the invention hydrophilized metal louber surfaces can not only be deep-drawn or stripped without the use of lubricants.They also allow a much more effective and economical paint.
