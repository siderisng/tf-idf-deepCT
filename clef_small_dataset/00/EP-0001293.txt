<ABSTRACT>
a disconnecting switch for high tension having a first (5) and a second (4) tubular switch arm, supported in a swivelling manner, has at least two contact fingers (10, 11) on the first switch arm which engage with an opposing contact piece (15) on the second switch arm in the switched-on position.  in this arrangement, the first switch arm (5) is provided with openings (21, 22) corresponding to the number of contact fingers (10, 11) into which openings the contact fingers (10, 11) are inserted and in which they are braced.  <image>
