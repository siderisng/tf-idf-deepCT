<ABSTRACT>
Electronic subscriber equipment, two-wire-four-wire conversion circuit, for telephone exchange, which is connected to the subscriber by the terminals (1, 2) and the central office by the terminals (3, 4, 5, 5,6).It comprises a DC voltage source (8), four transistors (10, 12, 14, 16) and three pairs of equal resistors (9-11, 13-15 and 17-18).The elimination of the local effect is obtained by the circuit (18-16-17).Stabilization of the duty power supply of the subscriber line.Application to a multiplex type telephone exchange by sharing time.
