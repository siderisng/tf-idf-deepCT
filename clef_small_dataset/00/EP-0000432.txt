<ABSTRACT>
a process for vapour-phase catalytic isomerization of  monocyclic methyl-substituted aromatic hydrocarbon  compounds of from 8 to 10 carbon atoms contained in a  feedstock also containing ethylbenzene.  the catalyst for the  process comprises a zeolite having a constraint index of 1  to 12.  the improvement in the process comprises treating  the catalyst with one or more basic nitrogen compounds or  precursors thereof.
