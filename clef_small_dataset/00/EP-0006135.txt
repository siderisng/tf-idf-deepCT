<ABSTRACT>
Additive for inorganic binders such as cementes, calcium sulfate, magnesiazement, etc. containing as active ingredient a water-soluble salt of a mixing resin of an aromatic aminosulfonic acid, urea, melamine and formaldehyde, wherein in the resin the molar ratio of aromatic aminosulfonic acid to the sum of melamine and urea (0,2 to 1.0): 1.0 and melamine to urea of 0.75: 0.25 to 0.30: 0.70, as well as building materials containing this means with increased flowability and early resistance.
