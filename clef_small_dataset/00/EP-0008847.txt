<ABSTRACT>
a removable annular hearth member (40), shaped to fit  over the slag outlet member (20) of a slagging gasifier, com­ prises a cast body of high thermal conductivity having integral  liquid coolant passageways, the central openings of the annu­ lar hearth member and slag outlet member being arranged in  vertical alignment for the discharge of slag, and the lower  portion of the hearth member opening having a lip or beak  (70) extending downwardly so as to form a sealed joint with  the slag outlet opening whereby in operation of the gasifier to  prevent penetration of molten slag therebetween.
