<ABSTRACT>
a fuse for transfer of a detonation from an explosive  charge to another or for initiating or igniting a detonation in an  explosive charge.  the fuse has the shape of a hose (1) forming  a channel (2) having a thin layer (1a) of a reactive substance on  its inner surface.  in order to prevent any throttling of the channel a  pyrotechnic charge (3) is inserted into each end of the hose.
