<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a weld system for both assembling a GaAs (200) diode and a copper base (100).This system comprises an indium layer (3) inserted between two layers of tungsten (1 and 5), which serve as a barrier to the diffusion of the Indium.The invention applies to optical fiber telecommunications systems.
