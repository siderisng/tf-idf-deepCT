<ABSTRACT>
A crankshaft milling machine is described with at least one milling tool (16, 17) with a plurality of disc mills, in which the milling tool (16, 17) itself is mounted with an axial bearing (35, 36) and the clutch (18) between milling tools (16, 17) and drive spindle (14, 15) no axial forces transmits.
