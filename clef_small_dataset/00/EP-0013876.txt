<ABSTRACT>
The non-contact potential measurement on integrated circuits is done by measuring the energy of the secondary electrons triggered at a measuring point.At a predetermined distance from the measuring point (MP), a suction electrode (16) and a counterfeil electrode (18) are arranged successively, which are arranged as a lattice electrode which are arranged and parallel to the flat side of the circuit (4).This device allows the touch-silent potential measurement on tracks of integrated circuits.
