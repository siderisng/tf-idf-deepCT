<ABSTRACT>
anodized aluminum (10) having a porous, unsealed  anodic oxide layer (12) is provided with a polymeric coating  (14) over the oxide layer that is substantive to a sublimatable  dye (16).  a sublimated dye forms a design or image in the  oxide layer and the polymeric coating.  single and multi-color  designs and images can be used for nameplates, dials and  signs.
