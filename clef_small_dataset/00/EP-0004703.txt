<TITLE>
universal joint and method for manufacturing it.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
an outer member for a universal joint with torque  transmission by balls engaged in grooves (15) in the outer  member, comprising a first component (10) of a lightweight  material for attachment to a transmission shaft (13), a second  component (14) fitting closely within the first component (10)  and defining the grooves (15), the first and second components  (10, 14) being keyed (18) against relative rotation and  retained together by deformation (17) of the first component  (10).  
</ABSTRACT>
