<ABSTRACT>
Method for producing aldehyde of the formula: R-CH (CH₃)) - CHO.(R: straight chain alkyl radical having 7 to 12 carbon atoms) by hydroformylation of olefins of the formula R-CH = CH₂ at 80 to 170 ° C and 200 to 350 bar in the presence of complex rhoduim compounds as a catalyst, followed by thermal treatment at 100 to 190 °C of the reaction product, optionally after separation of the catalyst.
