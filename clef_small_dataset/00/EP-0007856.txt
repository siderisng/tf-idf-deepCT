<ABSTRACT>
Method and installation to recover bare tale metals in plummy oxidized compounds and zincifers.According to this method, these products which have been dried, in a cast iron bath are injected and condensed, leading, lead and zinc being retrieved in metallic form.The installation includes an electric oven (1) with means (7) for injecting these products and a condenser (3) which communicate in their upper part.It may also include a gas purification assembly (4) which is said after the condenser group (3).
