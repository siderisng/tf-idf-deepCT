<ABSTRACT>
For purposes of guiding and regulation of electrical high performance networks, in particular the associated generators, a network identification with determination of the longitudinal replacement reactance (XE) of each with a measuring location (XM) or with a network portion coupled with a measurator (G) connected there is required.The latter is represented by this longitudinal reactance between the measuring site and a network site V₂.This longitudinal replacement reactance (XE) can be continuously from the local measurement sizes (P, Q, V₁) with the aid of a plurality of predetermined values (XE1 to XE3) of the desired reactance by similarity comparison of the time curves here made of formed phase angle differences (D₁ to D₃) and the localPhase angle (DM) can be determined.
</ABSTRACT>
