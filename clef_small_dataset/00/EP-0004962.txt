<ABSTRACT>
the invention relates to a glow discharge lamp for qualitative and quantitative spectral analysis having, on the cathode side, a discharge cavity 3, which can be connected to an electrically conducting disc 8 which is at the cathode potential.  for increasing the light intensity and the accuracy of analysis, means are provided, according to the invention, for generating a magnetic field in the discharge cavity 3 running essentially in the axial direction.  these means can consist, in particular, of a ring magnet 32, magnetised in the axial direction, of permanent-magnet material.  furthermore, a controllable electromagnet 33 can be provided coaxially with respect to this ring magnet.  the glow discharge lamp is suitable for the analysis of solid, liquid and gaseous substances.  <image>
</ABSTRACT>
