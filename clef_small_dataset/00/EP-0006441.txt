<ABSTRACT>
Optical device for a reflected gate bidirectional position detecting apparatus for precisely determining the position and direction of the movement of a part by reporting to another.Two sets (32) and (33) of opaque lines and transparent lines with light, offset 90 Â° relative to each other, are illuminated by two light sources (S1) and (S2).The images, reflected on a concave spherical mirror, are formed in the plane of the two sets.The light signals generate thanks to the detectors (D1) and (D2) of the electrical signals whose phase difference is indicative of the direction of movement.This optical device can be used for the detection of the two-piece position relative to each other, for example tool turret tool, printer, inkjet printer.
