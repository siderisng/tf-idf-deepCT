<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a computer terminal condired by an input device 1 and by an output device 2, the device 1 being separated from the device 2. according to the invention, the extension comprises a support for the device 2Constituted by a column 4 mounted along its axis in a base 5 and rotatably around its axis relative to said base, the part 4a of the column being provided with a console 8 articulated around an axis 7.recliner for device 2 while, between column 4 and base 5, are provided for means for their axial immobilization relatifically adjustable and means 9 for their relative rotation fixture and that, between column 4 and theConsole 8, are provided with the adjustment means 10 of the inclination of the console.
