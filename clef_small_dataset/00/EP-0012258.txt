<ABSTRACT>
Device for supporting or attaching piping (1) low thickness relative to their diameter and subjected to significant temperature variations, and / or to be protected against seismic jolts.It comprises a collar (2, 3) surrounding the piping (1) and spaced from it, pierced with holes (12) in at least two points symmetrically arranged, tiles (11) welded on the piping (1) in the centerof these holes (12), connecting parts (15) engaged in each of the tabs (11), and flexible blades (17) welded on one side on the liae pieces (15) and on the other sideOn parts (18) integral with the necklace (2, 3).
