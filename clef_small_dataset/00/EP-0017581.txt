<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a load receiver for weighing, of the type comprising a fixed amount 11 integral with a frame 20, a charged amount 12 integral with a loader tray 10, and two arms 21 and 22 articulated on these amounts forTrain with them a mable mable parallelogram.The set of two amounts 11 and 12 and both arms 21 and 22, are carried out in one piece, the joints being defined by strangles 211,212,221,222 of the constitutive frame.A condensed charge transducer is formed by a first electrode 320 mounted integral with the fixed amount 11 and by a second electrode 343 mounted on the parallelogram elsewhere than on the fixed amount.Application including sharing scales.
