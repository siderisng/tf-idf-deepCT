<ABSTRACT>
an automatic centering and cramping condition for  rolled material (w) adjusting mechanism for adjusting the  clearance between sub-guide (41, 51) rollers, for use in a  material guiding apparatus of a rolling mill for rolling a  steel blank into wires, bars etc. , the material guiding ap­ paratus having a pair of roller holders (2, 3) carrying a  pair of main guide rollers (4, 5) and a pair of sub-guide  rollers (41, 51).  for even contact with the rolled material  (w) and for optional centering the subguide rollers are  (41, 51) rotatably carried by eccentric shaft pins unitary  with support shafts (25) supported by the roller holders  (2, 3).  left and right hand screw gears (27, 28) respec­ tively are attached to the support shafts (25).  manually  operable left and right hand screw worm gears (29, 30)  respectively engage with the gears (27, 28) attached to the  support shafts (25) respectively.  the worm gears (29, 30)  are carried by respective shafts (13, 14) which may be  brought into engagement for unitary rotation with each  other as necessitated.  
</ABSTRACT>
