<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a process for the preparation of 2-oximinophenylacetonitrile by reacting benzyl cyanide with alkyl nitrite in the presence of a base by dosing an alkyl nitrite in a mixture of an alcohol, an aqueous solution of an inorganic base and benzylcyanide, after the reaction was terminated2-Oxi minophenylacetonitrile base salts acidifiers and then the 2-oximinophenylacetonitrile is isolated.
