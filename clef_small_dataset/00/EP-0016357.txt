<ABSTRACT>
Method for producing alkaline earth alkyl benzenesulfuric acids by neutralization of alkylbenzenulosul fosamers, characterized in that a solution of the alkylbenzenesulfonic acid in an organic solvent having 1 to 10 wt.-% of an oxalkylate, relative to the weight of the alkylbenzenesulfonic acid, neutralized by adding the required amount of a basic alkaline earth metal compound and the resulting salt of the alkyl benzene sulfonic acid is isolated.
