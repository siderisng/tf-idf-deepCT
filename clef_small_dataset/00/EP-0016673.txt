<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for solubilizing an organic or mineral salt in an organic solvent. According to this method, the organic or mineral salt of formula A⁻M⁺ is involved in which a mineral or organic anion and M⁺ represents a cation selected from the group including the NH₄⁺ cation and its derivatives and the cations derived from Metals of groups IA, IIA, IIIA, IVA, VA, VIA, VIIA, VIII, IB, IIB, IIIB, IVB and VB of the periodic classification of the elements and at least one sequestering agent, soluble in said organic solvent of formula: n [CHR₁-CHR₂-O-O- (CHR₃-CHR₄-O) N-R₅] ₃ or N is superior or equal to 0 and ₁ R₂ R₃ and R₄ identical or different are an atom H or a front alkyl radical 1 to 4 carbon atoms and R₅ represents an alkyl or cycloalkyl radical having 1 to 12 carbon atoms, a phenyl radical, or a radical -CM H2M-O or CM H2M + 1-O-, (1 ≦ M ≦ 12). Application to the implementation of the salt A⁻ M⁺ in an organic synthesis reaction and the extraction of the salt A⁻ M⁺ of a solution the container.
