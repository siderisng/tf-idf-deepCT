<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a process for the preparation of heavy-flammable polyurethane soft foams by reacting a mixture of diphenylmethane diisocyanates and polyphenyl polymethylene polyisocyanates having a content of diphenylmethane diisocyanates from 40 to 90 wt.% based on the total weight, polyols, cyanuric acid derivatives as a flame retardant, such as cyanamide, dicyanamide, guanidine, biguanide, melamine, cyanur acid alkyl ethydrazides and amides, and blowing agents and optionally chain extensions and additives.The polyurethane soft foams are suitable for the scaffoldings known per se, but in particular as a sound insulation material for the construction sector.
