<ABSTRACT>
New derivatives of 5H-benzocyclohepten-7-amine and their salts, their process for the preparation, their application as drugs and the pharmaceutical compositions containing them.The invention relates to new derivatives of 5H-benzocyclohepten-7-amine and their salts, their process for preparation and pharmaceutical compositions containing them.Said derivatives respond to the formula wherein R₁ represents hydrogen or methyl, R₂ and R₃ represent hydrogen, fluorine, chlorine or bromine, a nitro, amino, trifluoromethyl or methoxy and X radical represents hydrogen or halogen.The derivatives according to the invention are endowed with inhibitory properties on the capture of serotonin in vitro and in vivo and some, of anorexigenic properties.
