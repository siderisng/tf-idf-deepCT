<ABSTRACT>
In a step stage and a method for their preparation, the step surface is formed from a lost wooden shawl (2), in the interior of which a reinforcement (7) is arranged, which is enclosed in a filling composition of cement-bound concrete (8). For the purpose of using cement-bonded concrete (8) in conjunction with the lost wooden shawl (2), which consists of miter cut plates (3), and for producing a permanent connection between the concrete (8) and the wooden shawl (2) is between the the interior (6) limiting surface of the wooden shawl (2) and the concrete (8) provided a separating layer (4) of synthetic resin, which glues and stiffen the plates (3) of the wooden formwork (2) in the area of ​​miter and stiffened and, moreover Lost wooden shaping protects against water exposure through the cement-bonded concrete. Between the concrete (8) and the wooden shawl (2), adhesive bridges (5, 9) are provided which ensure a solid and permanent connection between concrete (8) and wooden shawl (2).
