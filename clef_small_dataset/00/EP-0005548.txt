<ABSTRACT>
In a contactless conductivity potentiometer with rotation setting, it is proposed according to the invention to take a photoconductor layer (4.5.14, 15) as a conductivity element.This layer is circular and diametrically divided.A solid circular tap electrode (3, 13) connects both parts (4,5,14,15).Outer electrodes are semi-circular with the two layer parts (4,5,14,15) contacted.The photoconductor layer (4,5,14,15) is axially exposed in a coaxial half.The partial guiding values (G₁, G₂) are linearly dependent on the rotation angle (α).
