<ABSTRACT>
For a magnifying apparatus with a rotatable in the original plane image stage (7), a lighting device (5) is provided with at least one light line (17), which is rotatable about a normal to the original plane axis (A).Between the image platform (7) and the light line (17) there may be an active compound, so that when the image stage is rotated the lighting bay is rotated simultaneously.
