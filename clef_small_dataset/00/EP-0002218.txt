<ABSTRACT>
High-molecular polyester with good processability, high glass transition temperature, mechanical strength and clear transparency are obtained reproducibly by transesterification and polycondensation of bisphenols with diaryl teeththalates and / or diarylisophthalates in the presence of aromatic polycarbonates having a molecular weight of 600 to 15. 000.
