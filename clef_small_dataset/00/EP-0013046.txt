<ABSTRACT>
In a device for biaxial simultaneous stretching of a flat film web from particular polypropylene, the edges of the film web (1) are detected by clip (3), which are driven in the stretch zone (9) by means of divergently arranged threaded spindles (5), and it becomes at leastA part of the stretching in the longitudinal direction by means of a progressive slope of the threaded spindles (5) at the same time with a part of the resource.At the region of the progressive slope (7), a thread with a region degressively slope (8) preferably at the end of the threaded spindles (5), which causes shrinkage of the previously stretched film web in the longitudinal direction, while the film preferably continues in the transverse directionis stretched.The shrinkage of the film web takes place at about 5 to 10% of the spindle length by up to 10% of the previously generated longitudinal direction on the desired grade in the finished film stretching degree in the longitudinal direction.
