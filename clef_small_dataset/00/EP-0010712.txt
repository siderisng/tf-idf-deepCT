<ABSTRACT>
Metallized, textile fabrics made of synthetic polymers or native fibers on which a metal layer is nasally-shakemically depressed, are particularly suitable for shielding electromagnic tables in the range of 10 MHz to 1000 GHz.Since the textile properties are not disturbed by the metallization, the fabrics are particularly suitable for use in the medical and message-related be rich.A multi-layered structure with isolation the intermediate layers and superficial laminations can be expedient, for example, in the shielding relatively low frequencies.
