<ABSTRACT>
this invention is in the field of plastics films for packaging.   it is concerned with the problem of causing surfaces of the  packaging film to self-adhere (self-weld) so as to provide a  second seal and close off the parts of the package not in contact  with the packaged article.  the solution of the invention involves a packaging film of a  blend of at least two ethylene polymers, one of the polymers  having a melt flow substantially greater than that of the other.   the film may be a single film or a surface layer (101) of a multiply  film (laminate) (100â€²).  preferred ethylene polymers for the  blend are the ethylene-vinyl acetate copolymers wherein one  copolymer has a melt flow of less than 5. 0 and the other has a  melt flow greater than 28. 0.  preferably the multi-ply film has 4  layers (104, 103, 102, 101)
