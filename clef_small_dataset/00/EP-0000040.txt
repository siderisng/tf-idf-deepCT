<ABSTRACT>
A holding device, which as a multi-purpose clamp in the household, in workshops u. Like. For releasable holders of gene levels on walls, furniture pieces and the like, serves as well as to hold open or closed doors.The holding device should be made of plastic, by a snap operation from the closing man to bring the offset state and vice versa and be designed so that no fatigue phenomena of the plastic material can occur, but nevertheless towards removal of the supported objects towards significant forces.For this purpose, the holding device has a curved or angled bending spring (10) and gripping parts (22, 26) which are connected to each other by film hinges (12, 13, 18) in such a way that portions of the gripping parts in the locking state in the bending springArright.
