<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for producing H₂ and co-containing gases, in which fine-grained carbon or coke with steam in a fluidized bed under heating by means of in the fluidized bed immersed by a gaseous medium, heat diver in a paved zone partially advantage and the residue cokeIn a rolled-up combustion zone in a troweled in a fluid layer is burned, whereby the cooled medium flowing the gasification zone heats again, during which the medium cooled in the gasification zone is guided in the circular run over the heat diver.The gasification zone may be preceded by a vapor-operated fluidized bed as a gasing zone.The vortex layers are operated at a gas pressure of 5 - 60 bar, in the degassing zone at 700 - 870 ° C and in the combustion zone at 900 - 1050 ° C.
