<ABSTRACT>
According to the invention, cyclic dimethyl polysil oxane are prepared by heating a mixture of linear or branched organopolysiloxane, which consists of at least 50 mol percent of dimethylsiloxane units, and 50 to 85 weight-percent aqueous sulfuric acid and simultaneous distilling off the cyclic dlmethylpolysiloxanes formed in this heating.
