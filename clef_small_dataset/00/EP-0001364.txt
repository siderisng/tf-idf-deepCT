<TITLE>
device for lowering or lifting the upper platform of a car-carrying transporter.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

<ABSTRACT>
device for lowering or lifting partially or fully the upper platform of a car-carrying trailer of the type comprising a lower platform arranged on a chassis and an upper platform held at a distance from said lower platform by at least four vertical support posts (2), characterised in that it comprises at least one motor (1) turning at least two vertical screws (3) each housed in one of the posts (2) of a given pair, the rotation of which screws brings about the vertical displacement of means integral with the upper platform.  application: to the loading and unloading of the upper platform of a car-carrying transporter.  <image>
