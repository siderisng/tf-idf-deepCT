<ABSTRACT>
1.  biaxially stretched polyester film having an antistatic coating applied to one side thereof and including stearamidopropyldimethyl-beta-hydroxyethyl-ammonium nitrate and a polymer, wherein a latex coating formed of a coating agent is applied, which comprises a) from 2. 75 % to 3. 20 % by weight of stearamidopropyldimethyl-beta-hydroxyethyl-ammonium nitrate and b) from 0. 85 % to 1. 16 % by weight of a crosslinkable methylmethacrylateethylacrylate-methacrylamide terpolymer, having a glass transition temperature in the range from 40 degrees to 50 degrees c, the ratios of a:b being in the range between 2. 75:1 and 3. 20:1.
