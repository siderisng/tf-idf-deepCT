<ABSTRACT>
1.  polyamide moulding compositions which contain 0. 5 to 25% by weight, relative to the total moulding composition, of a flameproofing mixture of red phosphorus having an average particle size of less than 200 mu and phenol/aldehyde resins and which also optionally contain reinforcing materials and/or fillers, characterised in that they contain a mixture of a) 55 to 95% by weight of phenol/aldehyde resins and b) 5 to 45% by weight of red phosphorus.
