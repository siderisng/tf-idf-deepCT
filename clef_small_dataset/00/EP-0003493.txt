<ABSTRACT>
Digital data transmission system by frames between a plurality of series connected stations according to a unidirectional loop configuration. The system consists of a plurality of indentic stations (1, 2.. 16) connected by a main bus (17) and by two alternating buses (18) one of the buses connecting the peer order stations and the Other odd stations, so as to allow the avoidance of a defective station. Data from or for processors (21, 22.. 36) connected to the stations are transmitted on the loop in the form of frames transiting by all stations between the original station and the destination station, allowing at any station to eliminate erroneous frames. When a station received a frame that was intended for it, it returns a response frame and zero frames circulate on the loop in the absence of data frames.
