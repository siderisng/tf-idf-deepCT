<ABSTRACT>
1.  compositions which are heat-curable to give elastomers with improved resistance to hydrolytic degradation and are based on organopolysiloxanes, characterised in that they contain the following constituents a) an organopolysiloxane polymer having a viscosity of 1 000 000 to 200 000 000 mpas at 25 degrees c and comprising the structural unit (r)a sio4-a /2 wherein r represents a monovalent hydrocarbon radical which can optionally be cyano- or halogen-substituted, and a is between 1. 95 and 2. 01, b) polycarbodiimide-polysiloxane copolymers or polycarbodiimidepolysiloxane mixtures, it being possible for the polycarbodiimide content t to be from 0. 1 to 70% by weight - relative to total polymer, c) curing catalysts and d) fillers.
