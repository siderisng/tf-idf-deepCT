<ABSTRACT>
At a race, each participant is equipped with a sen.At the destination, two receiving antennas (10, 11) are installed, which react to the field of the relevant transmitter when passing the cell line and enable a precise assessment of the target passage.The two Camping antennas (10, 11) are arranged with directional guidelines extending at right angles to each other, which reacts to horizontal and the other on vertical fields.A target passage signal (25) is generated when the signal of a receiving antenna (10) above a high first threshold value (20) and the signal of the other receiving antenna (11) is below a low two th threshold (22).
