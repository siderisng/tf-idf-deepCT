<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an etchant for etching sili cium oxides on a base and an etching process.The etchant according to the invention contains a solution of hydrogen fluoride in water, a polyhydric alcohol, for example glycerol and optionally a wetting agent.It is essential that the etchant contains no unbound water and no ammonium fluoride.It is particularly suitable for removing silicon oxides which are exposed on a Metallsi Licide layer under the or in the same silicon environment.
