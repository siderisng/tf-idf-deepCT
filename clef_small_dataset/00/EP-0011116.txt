<ABSTRACT>
Tertiary aminoalkyl ester or tertiary aminoalkylamides of acrylic or methacrylic acid are quaternized in admixture with 30 to 80% acrylamide, based on the weight of the mixture with an alkylating agent in the absence of water.It creates in a short conversion time with high yield, the corresponding quaternization product in the form of a liquid mixture which can be used directly for the preparation of mixing polymers of the acrylamide.
