<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to addition products and their use.The addition products are reaction products from an epoxide compound with guanidine or a guani dinder derivative, whereby at least 1 mol of guanidine or guani dinder derivative per epoxide equivalent of the epoxy compound and the addition product has optionally been partially or completely neutralized by acid additives.Addition products may additionally contain capped ISO cyanate groups.The addition products according to the invention can be used in combination with a full-capped polyisocyanate, AMI noplastic resin and / or phenoplastic resin as a binder in coating means as well as for the cathodic electrotauchlac cation of metal parts.
