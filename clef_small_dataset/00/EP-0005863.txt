<ABSTRACT>
An apparatus for supplying and aligning a sheet of pressurizing machines has a transverse carriage (10) slidable transversely to the arch running direction, on which a sucker (16) is mounted movably.The sucker (16) is mounted on the carriage (10) in such a way that during the transverse movement of the carriage (10) of the sucker (16) is movable in the arch running direction.The sucker (16) is formed as a pipe piece (16. 2) which is suppliable by a valve housing (8) suction air.The control of the carriage (10) and the suction air occurs in the clock of the sheet by a curve (3) or by a rotating valve disc (5).
