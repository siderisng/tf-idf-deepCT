<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for the manufacture of stable filler polyol dispersions by tuming and simultaneous dispersing of filler fen, preferably inorganic fillers, with high verses - energy densities, z.B of 10 to 3000 kWh / m³, on particle sizes of less than 7 μm inhibitors of polyols with functionaries of 2 to 8 and molecular weights from 200 to 8000. The filler polyol dispersions have filler strain holding from 3 to 50 wt.%, based on the total weight, and viscosities less than 2 500 cp at 25 ° C and dye held of 10 wt.% or less than 5 000 cp in dye held of 20 wt.%.
