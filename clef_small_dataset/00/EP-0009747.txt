<ABSTRACT>
The present invention is a procedure for the preparation of soluble thermoplastic graft polycarbonates with molecular weights (weight medium MW, measured by light scattering) of 30 000 to 400 000, which is characterized in that they are aromatic polycarbonates with at least one unsaturated at least one unsaturated per polycarbobicPolymerized end group in the presence of radically polymerizable monomers by means of solution polymerization, wherein the weight ratio of the output polycarbonate to the radically polymerizable monomers of 70:30 to 10:90, preferably from 70:30 to 40:60 and more preferably from 65:35 to 45: 55should be, and wherein the concentration of the reaction solution 30 wt.-%, based on total weight of polycarbonate with unsaturated end groups, radically polymerizable monomers and reaction medium should not exceed.
