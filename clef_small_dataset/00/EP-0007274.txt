<ABSTRACT>
Positioning device of a plug on a bottle.According to the invention, a thickness (6) provided on the lerete neck (3) of the vial (1) has a stop face (6a) that can cooperate with the face (7a) of a stoppiece (7) formed inside the plug (2), so as to block the rotational movement of the cap on the vial neck.Application: Improvement of the presentation in particular in perfumery.
