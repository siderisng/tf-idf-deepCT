<ABSTRACT>
the invention provides a method of combating weeds which comprises applying to the locus which is infested or liable to be infested with them an effective amount of: (a) bromoxynil, (b) ioxynil, (c) cmpp and (d) linuron and/or bentazone.  the invention also provides a herbicidal composition comprising from 0. 05 to 99% by weight of (a), (b), (c) and (d) in association with a suitable carrier and/or surface active agent.  also provided are novel salts of bentazone with an alkylamine of 8 to 18 carbon atoms.
