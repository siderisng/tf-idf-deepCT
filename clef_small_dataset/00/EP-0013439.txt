<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to reaction products from glycidyl groups containing acrylate resins and hydroxyl groups bearing, secondary monoamines. An acrylate resin of copolymerized, statistically distributed units of a) 3 - 30 wt. % of the glycidyl ester of the (meth) acrylic acid or vinyl glycidyl ether; b) 0 - 20 wt. % of a hydroxyalkyl ester of (meth) acrylic acid; c) 5 - 80 wt. -% of an optionally substituted styrene, (meth) acrylic acid methyl ester, (meth) acrylonitrile or mixtures thereof; d) 5 - 70 wt. -% of a C₁ -C₁₂-acrylic acid ester or a C₂ -C₁₂ - (meth) acrylic acid esters or mixing them; e) 0 - 10 wt. -% of an α, β-monoolefinically unsaturated mono- or dicarboxylic acid or its half-surfactants having at least one secondary hydroxyl group-carrying secondary monoamine at 50-200 ° C has been reacted in such a ratio that 1 epoxy is equivalent of the acrylate resin with 0.7 to 1 NH equivalent of the hydroxyl group supporting amine under extensive preservation of the OH functions responded. The implementation products serve as crosslinkers for polyi soocyanates.
