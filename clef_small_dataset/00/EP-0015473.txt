<ABSTRACT>
the method for immobilising cells on a surface entails binding the cells with the aid of a coupling agent to the surface of a plastic material, e. g.  polyvinyl chloride or polystyrene.  the plastic material is preferably used in the form of a microtitre plate.  preferably used as coupling agent is an aldehyde with at least 2 aldehyde groups, especially glutaraldehyde.  the method for immobilising cells is particularly suitable for the determination of binding of antibodies. 
</ABSTRACT>
