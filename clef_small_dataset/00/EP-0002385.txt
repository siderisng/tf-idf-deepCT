<ABSTRACT>
a fuel supply system has a fuel source (12, 16) and a  double walled fluid line (18) for use, for example, in a fuel  injection system.  the fuel line (18) has inner (30) and outer  (70) nominally concentric tubes having an annular space  therebetween.  the inner tube (30) receives fuel from the  source (12, 16) and the outer tube (70) is connected back to  the source (12, 16) to return venting leaking fluid to the  source (12, 16).
