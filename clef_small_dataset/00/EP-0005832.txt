centrifugal blasting wheel for throwing granular material.

<ABSTRACT>
in a centrifugal wheel for granular material, a mechanism clearing the discharge orifices at the angle of throw is provided.  for the purpose of reducing the abrasion on the components of the centrifugal wheel to a minimum during the acceleration of the granular material, the throwing element consists of a bladeless drum (21).  the inner chamber of the drum (21) forms a container for the granular material rotating with it, which granular material, thanks to the centrifugal force, adheres to the inner walls of the drum (21) and protects it from wear.  arranged at the periphery of the drum (21) are discharge openings (26) which are preferably inclined against the direction of rotation and which are closed outside the angle of throw.  <image>
