<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a sheet transport drum on rotary printing machines, in which the freshly printed printed image on the drum coat comes to rest.In order to avoid leaving the ink, an air cushion (8) is generated between the sheet (1) and the drum jacket (9) to be transported by means of blowing air, on which the sheet (1) rests with its entire flap.The air cushion (8) should be built with little blown air consumption and be useful for all paper thicknesses up to carton.To produce the air pole sts (8), blow nozzles (10) serving the airflow (11) in a space between the drum wall (9) and sheets (1) and establish the air upholstery (8) by the resulting back pressure.
