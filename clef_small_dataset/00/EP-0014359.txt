<ABSTRACT>
In a color picture tube with shadow mask (4) heats up in operation of the tube this mask expands, thereby causing landing errors of the electron beams (5) on the phosphor layer (2) of the tube.These land errors are corrected by temperature-dependent acceleration or brake fields between the mask (4) and a metal layer (3) applied to the phosphor layer (2).The temperature dependence of the bat is achieved by temperature-dependent resistors between the mask and metal layer.
