<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method and a device for improving the definition of the homes of the X-ray tubes which consists in periodically varying the polarization voltage applied to the concentration piece (6) of the electron beam emitted by the filament (7.8) of the tube (12) between a minimum or zero value and a maximum, negative value, with a frequency voltage waveform greater than 10 kilohertz, allowing this polarization voltage to take at least two values.
