<ABSTRACT>
a sealing cup to be used for a hydraulic piston cylinder  device comprising at an outer lip (1) thereof leading edge (6)  thereof adapted to slide along a wall when fitted in the  device.  the angle α of the leading edge (6) with respect to  the cylinder axis is made smaller than the angle of a portion  other than the leading edge with respect thereto, and is  made as 1° ≦ α ≦ 15° in the free condition of the cup.  when  the sealing cup is installed to be slid in the cylinder, a  wedge-shaped clearance is formed between the leading  edge (6) and the cylinder wall due to provision of the angle α,  thereby admitting the fluid therein to decrease the slide  resistance of the sealing cup.  
</ABSTRACT>
