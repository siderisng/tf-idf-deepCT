<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a circuit arrangement for time-multiplex digitally switching telecommunications, in particular telephone exchange plants.It is that a special coupling field (KF) provided for the transmission of listening signs is also used for the reception of election information and the sounds.Furthermore, it is proposed according to the invention that the busbars associated with this coupling field, the individual sounds, electoral frequencies and announcements have fixed time channels.Each connection element is assigned a switching device (ul., UN) for the optional connection with the connection clock (KV) or the special coupling field (KF).This arrangement was chosen to freeient the connection network of hearth, announcement and incomplete compounds.
