<ABSTRACT>
quenching of fluoresence in water of fluorescent  rare earth chelates is avoided by incorporating them into  the hydrophobic phase of a loadable polymeric latex.  the  aqueous stabilized fluorescent labels so formed may be  used to label proteins, antigens, antibodies or enzymes  which may be used in immunoassay analytical processes.   the fluorescent labelled antigens and antibodies are particularly  suited to temporal resolution fluoresence measurement  techniques.
