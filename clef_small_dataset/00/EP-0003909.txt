<TITLE>
electronic musical instrument having multiplexed keying.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
an electronic musical instrument having tone signal  sources and keyers for the tone signal sources has a time  division multiplexer (21) for multiplexing the key-down  information in groups such as for the upper manual (17),  lower manual (18) and pedals (19).  the keyers are provided  in divider keyer packages (31) with drawbar, or harmonic  content, information being synchronously multiplexed with  the key-down information to a single set of keyers.
