<TITLE>
method and apparatus for binary word recognition
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
a stream of digital data containing an unknown word is compared bit by bit with a stored known word.  the bit comparisons are counted in such a manner that the maximum output identifies the known word in the data stream.  alternatively, the non-comparing bits may be counted so that a minimum output idenfities the known word.
