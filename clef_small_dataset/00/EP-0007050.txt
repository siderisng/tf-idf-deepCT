Process for the preparation of isocyanurate groups and, where appropriate urethane groups having plastics including foams by trimerization of polyisocyanates with the aid of phosphonate salts as isocyanate trimerization catalysts, optionally in the presence of propellants, foam stabilizers and other additives, optionally in the presence of a deflector of at least two hydroxyl groups on facing polyhydroxyl compounds ofMolecular weight 400 to 10,000, as well as optionally chain extension means with a molecular weight of 32 to 400.
