<ABSTRACT>
A method of stereoselective preparation of sex pheromones having a trans-trans-trans (EE) trans-formula diener alcohol structure (EE) of formula: wherein: R is a linear saturated alkyl radical having 1 to 4 carbon atoms and n is an integer of 1 to 5. This process is characterized by the fact that in a first step is prepared in a tetrahydrofuran medium a magnesium derivative of formula: XMG- (CH₂) N-CH₂OP (II) wherein: X represents a halogen preferably BR or CL , and P is a protective group, and that in a second step is added at a temperature between 0 and -20 ° said excess magnesium derivative to a tetrahydrofurannic solution containing a copper catalyst and a trans-trans pipe translation of formula: wherein: Ra the same meaning as above and R₁ represents a radical such as: -coch₃, -so₂CH₃ and -SO₂C₆H₄CH₃ and ENIN the reaction product to a hydrolysis for release r the protective group.
