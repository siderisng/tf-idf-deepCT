<ABSTRACT>
The indicator comprises a tubular probe (10) plunged into the liquid (32) and communicating with the gas chamber (20) filled with a gas pressure source (12), a locking device (14) and a warning system(16), an increase in pressure in said chamber (20) normally soliciting the locking device (14) in a position for which the alarm system (16) is not implemented.When the level of the liquid is insufficient, the implementation of the pressure source (12) has the sole consequence the exhaust of the gas by the probe, so that the locking device (14) remains in a position for which theAlarm system (16) is implemented.Application to the measurement of the oil level in the motor housing of a motor vehicle.
