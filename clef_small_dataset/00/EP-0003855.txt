<ABSTRACT>
Toothpaste containing reactive substances for controlling the brushing time of the teeth by the color change that occurs after a defined time of brushing, the reactive substances essentially comprising a citrrate / citric acid buffer of between 0.07 and 0, 35 m with a pH of between 4 and 5.5 to the dosage of 10 to 50% and the red chlorophenol at the dosage of 0.02 to 0.1%.
