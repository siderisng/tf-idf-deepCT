<TITLE>
method for admixing polyolefin powder with additives.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
in order to avoid damage during processing and/or to improve the processing and service properties of polyolefin powders, these powders are homogeneously mixed with additives.  this homogeneous mixing is achieved by mixing the polyolefin powders and the additives using rotating blades with a blade frequency of from 100 to 300 per second at a power of from 370 to 1280 wh/m<3> of powder.  during this operation, the powder is passed through a zone having an energy dissipation density of from 200 to 350 w/l of mixer volume. 
</ABSTRACT>
