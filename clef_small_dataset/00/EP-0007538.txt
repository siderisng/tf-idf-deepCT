<ABSTRACT>
by building expansion members (6) into closed receptacles (1), the volume change, on heating or cooling of the heat or cold storage medium (5), can be taken up in the receptacle (1).  such expansion members (6), when built into solar collectors (2), prevent bursting upon freezing in winter.  a further advantage is that the heat or cold storage medium (5) is not in contact with the air.  <image>
