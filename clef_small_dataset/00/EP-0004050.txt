<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to particulate propellant-containing styrene polymers containing an organic bromine compound as a flame retardant and an organic compound having Labiler C-C, O-O-O or N = N binding as a flame retardant synergist.The flame retardant is superficially applied to the particles, while the flame retardant synergist is uniformly incorporated in the particles.The polymers can be processed to self-extinguishing foams.
