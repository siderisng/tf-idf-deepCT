<ABSTRACT>
This sleeve is provided with an external thread 3 and an inner thread for a screw V.The external thread 3 adapts to that of a tapped hole 10 of the object o.It also comprises an extension 6 for being deformed by buckling to lock in the thread 11 of the hole 10. This extension is preferably provided with an external slot 9 improving the socket on the thread 11. The sleeve is replaceable and can be used.Especially when the object O is in a soft material, while the screw V is in a hard material.It can possibly have a brake 5 for the V screw.
