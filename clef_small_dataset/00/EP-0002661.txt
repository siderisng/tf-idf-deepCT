<ABSTRACT>
In a method for producing bipolar transistors by ion implantation, in particular, the base zone (32) is in a semiconductor layer (18) which is epitaxially grown into a semiconductor substrate (10) with subcollector (14), the collector zone forming semiconductor layer (18) by a final on the semiconductor layer, a uniform Thick-filled silicon dioxide layer (20) implanted. Subsequently, windows (37, 36, 35) for the emitter contact, the base contact and the collector contact in the silica layer (20) are opened. The emitter zone (42) is then selectively implanted by the window (37) for the emitter contact. Finally, the metallization forming the contacts and connecting lines is applied to the silica layer (20). Despite direct implantation of the emitter zone, this method ensures a uniform thickness of the insulating layer supporting the final metallization, resulting in uniform and estimable capacitances.
