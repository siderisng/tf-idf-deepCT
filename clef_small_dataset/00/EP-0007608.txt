<ABSTRACT>
Thermoplastic mixtures are used to produce thin plastic films from them by means of a blowing extruder.Such plastic films can be used, for example, for packaging purposes.To prevent the unwanted blocking effect, the thermoplastic mixtures contain powdered synthetic zeolites or zeolite mixtures in an amount of 0.05 to 70 wt.% As synthetic, powdered zeolite, zeolite A, zeolite Y, zeolite x, zeolite P or hydroxysodalith can be directed.These synthetic, powdery zeolites are prepared by known methods.
