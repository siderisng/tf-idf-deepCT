<ABSTRACT>
block copolymers are prepared having end blocks with  random copolymer of styrene and/or alkyl substituted deriva­ tives and α methylstyrene ranges from 1:0. 5 to 1:2. 5, a central  block of a polydiene polymer joins the terminal blocks.  the  block copolymers contain from 10 to 90 weight percent of  polydiene, the remaining portion being of random  alkylstyrene-α-methylstyrene copolymer.  the polymers  range from medium impact polystyrene type polymer of low  polydiene content to thermoplastic elastomers of high  polydiene content.
