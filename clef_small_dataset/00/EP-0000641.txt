<ABSTRACT>
the charge accepting capacity of electrographic dielectric  coatings is improved by dispersing in an organic solvent-soluble  thermoplastic insulating resin having carboxyl functionality  providing an acid value of from 5 to 100, from 0. 05%  to 3% by weight of calcium oxide or calcium hydroxide.  a  solution of the resin in an organic solvent and containing the  calcium oxide or calcium hydroxide dispersed therein is used  to coat conductive paper.
