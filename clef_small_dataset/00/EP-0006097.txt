<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a new constructive provision of a cable carrier.The cable (39) is supported and driven by two movable assemblies (6, 7) arranged in V and each preferably comprising a rectangular profile notched belt (8).Canting belt support rollers are supported by trees and are packed on these trees, whose lower ends are bonded.The latter as well as the motor member (5) do not exceed the interframe belts.As for the solidarization assembly (9) supported by an armature (10) to four uprights and not exceeding the drive belts either, it is removable and the uprights are articulated on the upper ends of the fixed axes ofintermediate pebbles.
