<ABSTRACT>
A high voltage testing system includes a series resonant circuit (4, 5) connected to a continuously variable frequency voltage source (2), wherein the insulation (5 ') which is checked in series with the inductance (4) is either exclusively or exclusively or little stens forms part of the capacity (5). The inductance (4) may consist of several, modularly constructed high voltage throttles (4a, 4b). It can be used purposefully rod core chokes. Preferably, the rod core throttle (29) is hanging in the mass priority by means of a support plate (17). The coil is suitably composed of two wicks (16) which have a trapezoidal coarse cross-section. As a voltage source (2) with variable frequency, for example, a voice circular converter or a direct converter operating in the resonant frequency and with pulse width control is suitable. Parallel to the ISO Lation 5 ', at least one high voltage capacitor (5 ") can be closed to adjust a desired frequency. The advantage of the test system for testing an insulation is that the test with frequencies is possible, which are smaller or greater than the mains frequency. The plant is small and very light and contains price-based elements.
