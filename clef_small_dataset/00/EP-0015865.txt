<ABSTRACT>
In an electrical compressed gas switch with a blowing device consisting of piston and cylinder, two fixed nozzle-shaped switching pieces (2, 3) are provided which are connected in the switch-on position by a pipe-shaped bridging switch (6) with each other.The bridging switch (6) consists of in the interior of a tubular sleeve (6a) resiliently stored contact lamellae (7) and a front side under the interposition of an electrical insulation made sliding contact ring (8) made of arc-resistant material.In order to cause an unionized commutation of the current during the switch-off operation from the contact blades (7) to the sliding contact ring (8), at least one of the contact blades (7) is constantly connected to the sliding contact ring (8) electrically conductively.For this purpose, the con tact lamella (7) can be resiliently pressed against the sliding contact ring (8) or electrically connected via a flexible current band.
