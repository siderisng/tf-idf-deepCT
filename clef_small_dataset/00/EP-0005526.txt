<ABSTRACT>
device for training skiers, skaters or the like, in which, at a height above the travelling surface (g) which can be reached with the hands by the skier (s) in travelling position, height-adjustable holding members (8) are arranged, which are moved approximately parallel to the travelling surface (g) by a continuous conveyor member (2a).  the conveyor member (2a), which is designed as a conveyor chain or rope, can run round in a continuous guideway (1) with round, oval or polygonal outline.  <image>
