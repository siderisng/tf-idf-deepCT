<ABSTRACT>
manganese(ii) ethylenebis(dithiocarbamate) of low  ethylenethiourea content can be prepared by mixing di­ sodium ethylenebis(dithiocarbamate) with formaldehyde in  aqueous medium then mixing a water-soluble manganese(ii)  salt to precipitate the maneb.  the product can be further  formulated with a metal salt and also with paraformaldehyde.   fungicidal compositions may be prepared by formulating  with conventional carriers or adjuvants.
