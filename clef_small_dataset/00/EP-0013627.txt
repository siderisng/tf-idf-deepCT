<ABSTRACT>
an upstroking compression, moulding press includes a  pair of side frame members (1,2) to support an upper, fixed  platen (3) which is pivotably supported between said mem­ bers (1,2) said platen (3) having locking means thereon com­ prising a bar (8) extending over at least the thickness of both of  said sideframe members (1,2) and the distance between  them, together with means (10,11,12) for displacing said bar  (8) from a first position in which it is spaced from said mem­ bers and a second position in which end portions of the bar  are received in notches (6) in said members (1,2).
