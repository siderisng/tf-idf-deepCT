<ABSTRACT>
Stop valve for ink projection printer nozzle.It comprises a thin and elastic element (21) whose longitudinal edges (22) and (23) slide respectively in grooves (24) and (25) of the print head.The element (21) may pass from a first position, in which the inkjet can be exit from the nozzle (11) by the opening (26) to a second position in which it closes in a sealed manner the orificeoutput of the nozzle (11).
