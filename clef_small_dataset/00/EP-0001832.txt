<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a device for transmitting electric current with high current thickness to a rotating electrode.The device consists of a tub (10) which is filled with a low melting metal alloy and to which a contact piece (8) is attached to which a flexible cable (9) is screwed.A lid (13) is electrically connected to the tub (10) and penetrated by a shaft (14), wherein in the region of the lid (13) on the shaft (14) a disc (15) is arranged.The shaft (14) carries at its one end a connecting piece (6) which is rigidly connected to the a rotating electrode-performing cylindrical container.
