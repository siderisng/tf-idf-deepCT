<ABSTRACT>
A process for the preparation of 4,6-dihalostriazines of the formula substituted in 2-position is claimed.For this purpose, trihalogen compounds of the formula Rc (Hal 2) ₃, in which R aryl, aryloxy or an aromatic heterocyclic radical can be, with a halogen cyan of the formula Hal¹-Cn at temperatures of about 50 to 200 ° C in the presence of Lewis acids and optionallyreacted in the presence of an inert solvent.The process products can be used, for example, as intermediates for dyes or pesticides.
