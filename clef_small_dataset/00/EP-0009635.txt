<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a method for the preparation of sulfidic copper concentrates of complex compound sulfidic concentrates which contain at least one of the non-iron metals and optionally precious metals in addition to copper, iron and optionally precious metals, lead, nickel and cobalt as the main component in which the complexMenious sulphidic concentrates of a copper-containing, predominantly chloride solution, and the suspension thus obtained at elevated temperature is treated under elevated pressure and at a pH below 3.
