<ABSTRACT>
Two beams (1) of a beam pair of a track brake, which lie on both sides of a wing rail (7) and extend along the same Ben parallel, are provided with juxtaposition brake segments (8, 12).The segments (8, 12) are arranged on the inner longitudinal sides of the beams (1) at a distance from each other.The segments (8, 12) of each beam paa res form between themselves a braking groille determined for the wheels (6) of a railway carriage to be braked.The SEG ments (8, 12) are positively installed in elongated recesses (2) of the beam (1).Through this arrangement, the squeezing noise caused during the braking of the draining railway cars are lowered west.
