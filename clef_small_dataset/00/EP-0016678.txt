<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a remote clock reconstitution device for digital donation transmission receiver.This device comprises first means (17, 22) for the preparation of a clock signal of the same nominal frequency (F) as the remote clock signal, the second means (7, 14, 15, 16) of detectionPulses of the received signals (R⁺ and R⁻), regeneration of these received signals and the development of synchronization pulses and third means (23, 24, 25, 26, 27) of regenerated pulses.The invention applies to telephony.
