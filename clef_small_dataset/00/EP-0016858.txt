<ABSTRACT>
A piezoelectric transducer element usable universally for the installation in pressure, force or barrier transducers has on both sides of a central support (3) each at least one piezoplatte (6) stressed on shear. On the outer sides of the piezo plates, circular segment-shaped pressure distribution pieces (4) plan. The arrangement of carrier, piezo plates and pressure-vertices are recorded in a slot in a compression molding (5), which is shrunk directly or indirectly to the pressure distribution pieces, whereby the piezo slattes are specifically set under preload.
