<TITLE>
apparatus for blowing glassware.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>
reinhausen maschf scheubeckde<sep>maschinenfabrik reinhausen gebrueder scheubeck gmbh & co. kg.<sep>maschinenfabrik reinhausen gmbhpostfach 12 03 60d-93025 regensburgde<sep>

</INVENTOR>
<ABSTRACT>
apparatus (1) for blowing glass articles (11) from liquid glass using a pipe (7).  the blowing inlet of the pipe can be connected by means of a coupler (6) to a compressed-air source (18), a control device (17) for the compressed air which responds to a control pressure being provided between the compressed-air source (18) and the coupler (6).  the inlet of this control device (17), which can be charged with the control pressure, is provided with at least one mouthpiece (12) for producing the control pressure.  <image>
