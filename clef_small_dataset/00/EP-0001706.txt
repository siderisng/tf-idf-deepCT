<ABSTRACT>
absorbent materials with enhanced dispersibility in  blood are produced by surface-treating water-swellable particulate  absorbent materials with one or more polyethers.   useful polyethers are those of the formula   ho(c₂h₄o)a(c₃h₆o)b(c₂h₄o)ch   in which a + c or b may be zero.  the particulate absorbent materials are contacted or  mixed with the liquid or liquefied polyether or a solution  thereof in a volatile solvent.  the produced materials are especially  suitable as hygienic disposable products and for medical  and surgical uses.
