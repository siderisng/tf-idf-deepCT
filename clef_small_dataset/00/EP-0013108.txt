<ABSTRACT>
a process for the preparation of dry, stable granular  water-soluble potassium and/or ammonium salts compris­ ing subjecting a dispersion of crystals of at least one said salt  in an aqueous liquid to the action of a granulating device (11)  in the presence of calcium sulphate hemihydrate and a hyd­ rophilic surface active agent to effect agglomeration of said  crystals, and subjecting the resultant agglomerated crystals  to a drying operation in a drier (13).
