<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to aluminum parts such as bars, profiles or apparatus for making electrical contacts resistant to mechanical and thermal solicitations.The method of carrying out these parts consists in depositing at least in the contact zone a large nickel layer on the aluminum substrate.The quality of the contact is further improved when the nickelized piece is in contact with a silver part.The parts according to the invention allow the production of plug-in contacts and also of all devices for establishing or interrupting an electrical circuit such as sectionments, switches, contactors.
