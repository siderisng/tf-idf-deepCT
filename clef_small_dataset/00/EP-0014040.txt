<ABSTRACT>
a method of adhering a textile fabric tennis ball cover  (16) to a tennis ball core (18) comprises providing a film of  hot-melt adhesive (12) between the cover (16) and the  core (18) when the cover (16) is assembled on the core  (18) and then causing the hot-melt adhesive (12) to melt in  contact with the core (18) and the cover (16), preferably by  r-f or microwave heating.  upon solidifying, the adhesive  (12) adheres the cover (16) to the core (18) of the tennis  ball (20) with a high degree of tenacity.
