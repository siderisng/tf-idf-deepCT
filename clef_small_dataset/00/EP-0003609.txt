<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an optocoupler with a radiation transmitter (1) and a radiation receiver (2).Between the radiation transmitter (1) and the radiation receiver (2), a coupling medium (3) is provided, which is surrounded by a wrapping composition (6).In order to avoid electrical carrots in the interface between the coupling medium (3) and the wrapping composition (6), an insulating film (5) is applied to the surface of the conductor bands (4).
