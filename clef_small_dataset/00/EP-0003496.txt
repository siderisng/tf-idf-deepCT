<ABSTRACT>
1.  control-gear for adjusting the inclination of awnings, comprising a worm-gear (2) fastened to the awning carrier pipe (12) and a worm-gear shaft (7) arranged for being driven by means of a crank handle, characterized in that the control gear is mounted on the awning carrier pipe (12) and that the worm-gear shaft (7) can be driven by reciprocating movements of the crank handle of the awning (14), via a ratchet wheel (8) and a reciprocating lever (13) coacting therewith.
