<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for the production of hollow body oriented in thermoplastics equipped with a properly calibrated orifice ring.blowing a preform comprising a neck orifice ring (5) molded in its definitive form and, after thermal conditioning of the preform, the final blowing of the hollow body, and in particular of the lower part of the neck.below the orifice ring (5).The invention allows the production of hollow bodies oriented that can be filled with a sealed closure and used for the conditioning of carbonaceous beverages.
