<ABSTRACT>
a reflection and refraction operative, d.H.Catadioptric lens larger light strength with opening ratio 1: 1.2, which includes a collecting front lens, a main mirror lens with the field lens system attached thereon and a mirror mounted on the front lens.The front lens is object-side convex, the main mirror attached to the image-side convex back of a negative lens.The central game of this negative lens is remained unregulated and collects the incident light on the near adjacent focal point with the field lens system attached thereto.This system allows the realization of small zone error of the spherical aberration.The abstract of optical elements on only two members enables economic manufacturing methods and low sensitivity to manufacturing and assembly errors.
</ABSTRACT>
