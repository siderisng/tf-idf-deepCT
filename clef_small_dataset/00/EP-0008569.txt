<TITLE>
process and apparatus for manufacturing solar collectors.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
the solar energy collector has a plane or curved absorber surface (1) which supports mirror elements (4) which have essentially triangular cross-section and reflect the solar radiation onto the absorber surface (1).  the mirror elements are advantageously designed as long expandable profiles and can thus, by expansion tension, be fixed positively on the absorber surface (1) simply by pushing onto corresponding counterprofiles (3) of the absorber surface (1).  <image>
