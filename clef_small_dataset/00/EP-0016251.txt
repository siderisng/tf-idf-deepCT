<ABSTRACT>
An electronic thin film circuit is proposed with a substrate plate (1) consisting of an insulating material. The substrate plate (1) is partially covered by a til metal-containing layer (2). This layer (2) forms a first, a second and a third pattern (M₁, M₂, M₃). Through the first pattern (M₁), conductor tracks and / or connection contacts, through the second pattern (M₂) temperature-independent resistors and by the third pattern (M₃) temperature-dependent resistors (sensors) are defined. In the region of the first (M₁) and the third (M₃) pattern, a boundary layer (4) and on the boundary layer (4) is arranged on the valve metal-containing layer (2), wherein the boundary layer (4) valves tall , valve metal oxide, nickel, nickel oxide and mixing phases of these ingredients and the nickel layer (5) in the region of the third pattern (m₃) forms the temperature-dependent resistors (sensors). In the region of the first pattern (M₁), at least at portions of the nickel layer (5), a metallic reinforcing layer may be applied, which may consist of a soft sliding layer (6) and / or a galvanically mounted layer. In the region of the second pattern (M₂), the valve metal-containing layer (2) forms the temperature-independent resistors. On this layer (2), a valve metal loxide layer (9) may be applied by anodic oxidation in the region of this second pattern (M₂). Instead of this layer (9), a protective layer may also be applied to the valve metal-containing layer (2) in the region of the second pattern (M₂), which from the non-soluble in a selective nickel etchant (30) of the boundary layer originally applied there (4) consists.
