<ABSTRACT>
the invention makes it possible to drive the asynchronous motors of a refrigerating plant with, on starting up, limitation of the charge rate of an alternator controlled by a combustion engine.  a centrifugal clutch 7 engages the transmission pulley 8 only when the speed of the shaft 6 is sufficient to drive the alternator 12 via 9 at a speed such that the variable output voltage is greater than a given value.  the invention is applicable to refrigerated lorries and other refrigerated installations.  <image>
</ABSTRACT>
