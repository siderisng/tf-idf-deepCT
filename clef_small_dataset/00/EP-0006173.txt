<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to novel photopolymerizable arylglyoxyloyloxyalkyl acrylates with built-in photoinitia tor and their use for the preparation of high-reactive photopolymerizable binders.The novel compounds correspond to the formula wherein Ar phenyl or naphthyl, R hydrogen, alkyl with 1-4 C atoms, alkoxy with 1-4 C atoms, halogen, dialkylamino having 1-4 C atoms, carboxy, alkoxycarbonyl with 1-4 C-ATO Men in the alkoxy group;X is a 3- to 6-well aliphatic, saturated hydrocarbon radical interrupted by heteroatoms or heteroatom groups R₁, hydrogen or methyl N a number of about 2 to slightly and N + M are a number of about 3 to about 6.
