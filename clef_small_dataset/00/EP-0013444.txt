<TITLE>
complexes between 1,4,7,10,13,16-hexaoxacyclooctadecane (18-crown-6) and dimethylcarbonate or dimethyloxalate and process for isolating the macrocyclic polyether via said complexes.

<ABSTRACT>
process for the preparation of novel complexes formed  between 1,4,7,10,13,16 -hexaoxacyclooctadecane (18-­ crown-6) of the formula    and dimethylcarbonate or dimethyloxalate, containing one  molecule of dimethylcarbonate or dimethyloxalate per  molecule of 18-crown-6, and process for isolating 18-crown-6  from mixtures which may also contain one ore more other  macrocyclic polyethers, e. g.  15-crown-5, by treating with  dimethylcarbonate or dimethyloxalate and separating the  selectively formed precipitated complex from the resulting  solution of non-complexed material and affording uncom­ plexed 18-crown-6 in high purity (>98% and often more than  99,5 %) and with high yield by dissociating the separated  complex, e. g.  by heating to temperatures up to 80°c at pres­ sures between 5 and 1 kpa.  
</ABSTRACT>
