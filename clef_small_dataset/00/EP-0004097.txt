<ABSTRACT>
According to the invention, elastic organopolysiloxane foams are by crosslinking diorganopolysiloxane, which is located in admixture with silica having a surface of at least 50 m 2 / g, with simultaneous foaming, wherein as a crosslinking agent at least one peroxydic compound is prepared, characterized in that a mixture which in additionDorganopolysiloxane, silica with a surface of at least 50 m² / g and peroxy-compound at least one organosilicon compound containing at least 2% by weight of Si-bonded hydroxyl groups and at least one organopolysiloxane with sig-bearing hydrogen, at most the pressure of the surrounding atmosphere to a temperature in the range of 280 to320 ° C is heated.
