<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for increasing the bulk weight of silica with a surface of at least 50 m² / g by means of negative pressure on a filter surface.According to the invention, the silicon dioxide, whose bulk weight is to be increased, is provided by a transport of this silica by means of a transport screw, the longitudinal axis whose longitudinal axis is arranged parallel to the filter surface and which preferably has a depressing tap decreasing in the conveying direction, the negative pressure is set.Furthermore, the invention relates to the use of silicon dioxide treated according to the invention as a filler in absence of water storable, upon access of water at room temperature to elastomeric curable masses based on diorganopoly siloxane.
</ABSTRACT>
