<ABSTRACT>
Storage stable suspension from 1) 0.5 to 80 wt.-% (based on total suspension) of an inorganic filler in FIG. 2) 20 to 99.5 wt.-% (based on entire suspension) of polyhydroxyl compounds with aliphatic or cycloaliphatically bonded hydroxyl groups, which, as a stabilizer for the polyol component (2), 0.05 to 10 wt.-% based on (1) + (2), containing a graft polymer.The graft polymer is by radical polymerization of a) 1 to 35 wt.-%, based on graft polymer, an olefinically unsaturated carboxylic acid and b) 0 to 60 wt.-% based on graft polymer;On other unsaturated monomers in the presence of a polyhydroxyl compound, wherein the carboxyl groups of the graft polymer are completely or partially neutralized by organic or inorganic bases.This suspension can be used to build foamed or non-foamed polyurethanes.
