<ABSTRACT>
a process for the preparation and separation of paraxylene  from mixtures of c₈ aromatic hydrocarbons  comprising:   a) the separation by adsorption of the feed mixture into  a mixture (i) of para-xylene and ethylbenzene and a  mixture (ii) of ortho-xylene and meta-xylene;   b) the separation of para-xylene by crystallization of (i)  with subsequent distillation in order to obtain ethylbenzene  with recirculation of the residue to the crystallization  step;   c) the isomerization of (ii) after its distillation;   d) the separation of para-xylene from the isomerizate by  isomerization;   e) the recirculation of the components, not adsorbed in  step d), to the isomerization step.  a crystalline silicate with the composition (in moles  of oxides) (1,0±0,3) (r) 2 / no (afe₂o₃·bal₂o₃·cga₂o₃)  ·y(d·sio₂·egeo₂),   wherein   r: one or more mono- or bivalent cations,   a ≧ 0,1 - preferably = 1   b ≧ 0   c ≧ 0 -preferably = 0   a + b + c = 1   y ≧ 10 -preferably less than 300   d ≧ 0,1   e ≧ 0 -preferably = 0   d + e = 1   n: valency of r  is used as well as adsorbent or as isomerization catalyst.
