<ABSTRACT>
a device for diffusion of substances between two  fluids (a, b) separated by a semipermeable membrane (2),  preferably oxygenator, by which a first fluid (a), for example  oxygen, is conducted along the one surface of the  membrane (2) with diffusion of one or several substances  from this fluid (a) through the membrane (2) to the other  fluid (b), which is conducted on the opposite side of the  membrane.  the first mentioned fluid (a) is thereafter mixed  direct into the other fluid (b).
