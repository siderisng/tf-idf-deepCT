<ABSTRACT>
potentiometric devices for the detection of halide ions  have a halide ionpermeable overlayer, which is superposed  on a layer of silver halide and which meets certain criteria  of thickness, permeability and/or composition.  the device  is capable of accurately carrying out the halide ion detec­ tion within five minutes of contacting the overlayer with a  sample.  fig.  1 shows the plots of potential versus time for  an electrode according to the invention (2) in comparison  with the plots of other electrodes (1, 4).
