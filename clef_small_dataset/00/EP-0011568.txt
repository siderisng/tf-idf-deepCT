<ABSTRACT>
1.  an appliance for the domestic use of gas, of the type gastight under forced draught, including an exhaust fan (5) upstream of the duct (4) for exhaust of the burnt gases proceeding from a combustion chamber, and a gastight enclosure enveloping this chamber and the mechanisms and connected to the outside through a duct (8) for the supply of fresh air at the level of the exhaust duct, characterized by the fact that the gastight enclosure is in addition put into direct communication with the atmosphere of the environment through an aperture (15) provided in the wall of the enclosure, which is shut off by a flap valve (16) which is normally open in the event of stoppage of the appliance and which is reclosed automatically as soon as the appliance operates, in order to restore the latter to the normal conditions of operation of a gastight appliance. 
</ABSTRACT>
