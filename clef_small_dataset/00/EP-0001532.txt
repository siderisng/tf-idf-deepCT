<ABSTRACT>
A multi-element explorer probe (1) which captures or emits the ultrasound, cooperates with a row of trans (3) elastic wave transcutters (3) and with a set of frequency changeman circuits (2), in order to constitute aUltrasonic lens.The system applies in particular to the non-destructive examination of ultra-sound permeable bodies, as practiced for example in medicine and during the control of industrial manufacturing.
