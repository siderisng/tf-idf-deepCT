<ABSTRACT>
A method of separating the americium of the curium of submitting an aqueous nitric solution containing the americium and the curium to an oxidation, at room temperature, in the presence of phosphoric acid or phosphate ions at a concentration at most equal to 0, 1 m per liter, to pass the americium to Valencia VI, to put the solution thus treated in contact with an organic soil (4) small reducing and having a strong affinity for americium to Valencia VI, ofway to extract the americium into the organic phase, to contact the organic phase containing the americium to Valencia VI with a washing solution (7), then to retreated the americium of the organic phase using the organic phase using the organic phase.an aqueous solution (10).Application to the processing of solutions from the reprocessing of irradiated fuels.
