<TITLE>
process for the production of stable aqueous emulsions or suspensions of combustion or heating fuel.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>
warop ind<sep>warop industrie aktiengesellschaft <sep>warop industrie aktiengesellschafthaus gafadura, mitteldorf 1fl-9490 vaduzli   <sep>
</APPLICANT>
<INVENTOR>
warop industrie aktiengesellsc<sep>warop industrie aktiengesellschaft<sep>
</INVENTOR>
<ABSTRACT>
the process comprises treating fuel and water in a disintegrator having 3 to 10 pin series at a speed of 100 to 300 m/sec.  the starting material contains a sufficient quantity of fuel as to ensure, on combustion of the stable emulsions or suspensions formed, a combustion temperature which is sufficient for the thermal dissociation of the water into hydrogen and oxygen, so that the latter supports the combustion process of the fuel, while the former burns with the ambient air to supply thermal energy.  <image>
</ABSTRACT>
