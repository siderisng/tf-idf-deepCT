<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a road vehicle with two vehicle parts connected to each other via a joint which is provided with damping and / or blocking devices to avoid the unwanted corruption of the vehicle parts.The damping and / or blocking means comprise on both sides of the joint each pair of support arms whose end plates on the engagement inner side of the joint are abut each other and to be pressed against each other for attenuation or blocking by, for example, hydraulic means.- so that these plates do not move against each other against each other, they are provided with interlocking elevations and recesses which can be made in the manner of a toothing.
