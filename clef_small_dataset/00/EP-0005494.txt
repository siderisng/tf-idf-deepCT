<ABSTRACT>
Vertical separator of a mixture of vapor or gas and liquid or solid particles, comprising in an external ferrule (101, 102) an intake chamber of the mixture, vertical tubes (106) provided at their input of blades ( 107A) Printers with a helical movement, and at the exemption of a coaxial tube of larger diameter (109) allowing in an internal duct the drain of dry steam or gas and in the annular space that of a mixture Richer or particles, the annular space opening into a volume released for the separator of the mixture richer in particles in a substantially dry steam or a gas and a small fluid in steam or gas. It comprises at the bottom of the free volume a background (110) upwards, an annular ring of fluid collection slightly charged with steam or gas (116), and passages between the vertical tubes allowing the steam or gas of S 'escape up. Application to the drying of water vapor.
