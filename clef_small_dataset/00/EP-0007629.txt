<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a filter circuit with the CTD lines existing from individual CTD elements under the use of four-pole resonators (6, 6 '), which are each formed as a closed line loop and determining the frequency-dependent transmission behavior of the filter scarf device, and in the Furthermore, successive four-pole resonators are connected to one of a coupling circuit (4). The object of the invention is to set up the intermediate couplings of adjacent four-pole resonators, as easy as possible and at the same time ensure that these coupling circuits are also fully accessible to an integrated assembly. In the invention, this object is achieved in such a way that parallel to the input and output of the individual four-pole sonator (6, 6 ') amplifier (V1, V4) are switched, the signal flow direction coincides with which the parallel is the CTD lines ( 10,10 '), and that successive four-pole resonators (6') connecting longitudinal lines (11, 11 ') of the coupling circuit (4) have unidirectional transmission via and contains an inverting amplifier (V3) in at least one of these longitudinal cables (eg 11) .
