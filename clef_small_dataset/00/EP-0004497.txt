<ABSTRACT>
Traffic analyzer and remote monitoring system through the telephone network with such an apparatus for analyzing public telephone (P) telephone traffic for one or more telephone exchanges. Detectors (10) and tax pulse counters (11) detect and account for tax pulses transmitted to telephone (P) positions. Each tax account is analyzed at the end of a period of observation determined by an analysis initialization circuit (16) in dependence on the pre-established medium traffic intensity of the associated station. A traffic analysis circuit (18) compares, at the end of each observation period, the tax account at specific account limits for the position to report the abnormal functioning of the position when the tax account recognized during the Observation period is not included between these limits and is possibly nil corresponding to the decommissioning of the position. Positions can be analyzed by groups. The assignment of the positions to the groups is carried out by a distribution circuit (15) which, depending on the result of the comparison of the average traffic intensity to limit values ​​characterizing the groups, establishes lists of positions assigned to the groups. All the tax accounts of the positions of the same group are analyzed successively at the end of a joint observation period compared to common account limits characterizing the group's posts.
