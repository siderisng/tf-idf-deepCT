<ABSTRACT>
Half or fully automatic method and apparatus for loading a film cassette with an endless winding of a left foil strap (1), wherein the film band (1) of the molded disk (9) is supplied under offset via guide element (10), so that the film scan after passing throughFrom winding chamber (11) and channel (12) on the clamping elements formed luke bores (14) of the shape plate (9) after switching on vacuum to be fixed.By rotation of the shape plate (9) about shaft (15), the film strap is inserted loosely from the outside inward into the winding chamber.Connecting with adhesive strips through tape thursions (17) creates an endless film winding.The finished film is pushed into the cassette via ejector (18).
