<ABSTRACT>
To increase the visibility with passive displays, a lighting of the display elements is achieved via decoupling points of the fluorescent body or by direct optical contacting with the fluorescent body.The passive displays can be switched between two optical conditions.In a switching state of the passive display, the fluorescent light is absorbed and reflects diffusely in the other switching state and coupled out.
