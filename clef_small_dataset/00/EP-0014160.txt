<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to severely basic resistant glass fibers.According to the invention, the fiberglass comprise, in weight percentages, from 25 to 54% of SiO₂, from 20 to 40% of Al₂O₃, from 24 to 40% MgO, from 1 to 5% zro₂, from 0 to 0 to10% P₂O₅, from 0 to 10% TiO₂, from 0 to 10% B₂O₃, from 0 to 5% F, from 0 to 2% CR₂O₃, considering that the sum 2 MgO + Al₂O₃ is at least equalat 70%.The invention applies in particular to the manufacture of fiberglass-reinforced cement products.
