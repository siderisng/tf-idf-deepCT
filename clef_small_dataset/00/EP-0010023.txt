<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to amplitude modulization emission systems and in particular those transfering television signals.The transmitting system according to the invention comprises essentially a separate amplification, followed by a combination of its fixed relative amplitude and phase conditions, of a carrier signal modulated by the videofrequency information with a carrier level.Reduced, with this same non-modulated carrier, which makes it possible to resiliently improve the efficiency and reliability of classic transmitters.Applicable to transmitters and re-emissors of television of any known standard.
