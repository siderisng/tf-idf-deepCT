<ABSTRACT>
a suitable bleaching agent for textiles is a storage-stable mixture of a bleaching diperoxydicarboxylic acid with 6 to 12 c atoms and of a water-soluble salt of 4,4'-bis(3-sulpho-4-chlorostyryl)diphenyl which acts as optical brightener, there being 0. 01 to 0. 5 parts by weight of the optical brightener per 1 part by weight of available peroxygen.  the mixture is used in place of chlorine bleaching solution in laundering.
