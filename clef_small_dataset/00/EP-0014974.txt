<ABSTRACT>
The coating agents with improved adhesive properties are described, which are particularly suitable as basic protractors for the application of cover layers of fluorocarbon polymers consisting of a) a copolymer of tetrafluoroethylene units, in a perfluoro (alkylvinyl) ether of the formula CF₂ = CF-OR, wherein R is a perfluoroalkyl group having 1 to 10 C atoms, and optionally units of the hexafluoropropylene or vinyl lid fluoride, b) optionally dispersed polytetrafluoroethylene particles, c) a mixture of lithium hydroxide and suspended finely divided, on ther mixed paths Generated SiO₂, wherein the molar ratio Lio₂: SiO₂ 1: 0.5 to 1:30 and D) from water as a liquid carrier. Component b) may also be a relatively low molecular weight polytetrafluoroethylene wax. The coating means are carried by known methods on substrates, such as metals, glass or ceramic surfaces, and then dried at temperatures up to 300 ° C at temperatures up to 300 ° C and then sintered at 290 to 460 ° C. When used as a primer, the cover layer can be applied immediately after at most minor drying requests. Area of ​​application is the coating of household devices and technical equipment, with high adhesion to the substrate, connecting with non-stick and sliding properties to the surface.
