<ABSTRACT>
the invention relates to gas sensors which are based on metal-oxide semiconductors and which are very sensitive and not appreciably temperature-dependent.  for this purpose, the invention provides gas sensors in which the metal-oxide semiconductor is on the substrate (1) in the form of an amorphous metal-oxide semiconductor thin layer (2).  the substrate may be amorphous or crystalline.  the metal-oxide semiconductor may contain at least one doping element.  gas sensors according to the invention are suitable for detecting and determining various pollutant gases such as, for example, co, cnhm, c6h6 and, in particular, ethanol in air and waste gases (figure 1).  <image>
