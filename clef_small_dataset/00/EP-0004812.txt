<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to the manufacture of nitropraffins by phase nitration under pressure, possibly in the presence of an oxygenated gas.The nitrer reagent is a mixture containing a substantial amount of propane, the temperature and the reaction pressure, the contact time as well as the quantitative ratios between the nitrant agent, the nitrer mixture and the oxygenated gas are chosen in such a way.The nitration reaction is carried out in the homogeneous gas phase.The process makes it possible to obtain a percentage of different nitropraffins in accordance with the industrial demand and also to use the less expensive raw materials at a given moment.
