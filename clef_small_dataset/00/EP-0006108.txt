<ABSTRACT>
a high torque speed tandem drive reduction gear, having  a low clearance for low profile applications.  an input shaft 17 drives a pinion 55 which in turn drives  two other pinions 61 and 62.  the driven pinions 61 and 62  are each mounted on a separate shaft 59 and 60 respectively  carrying a drive worm 75 and 99.  the two worm shafts  turn in unison, and the worms 75 und 99 are each meshed  with a separate worm gear 111 and 112, respectively.  the  two worm gears are operatively mounted on an output shaft  130.  one of the driven pinions is provided with an adjustment  for taking up the blacklash in the gearing system, to  provide equal loading on each of the pinions.  the speed reduction gear may be provided with either  an air cooling system or a water cooling system.
