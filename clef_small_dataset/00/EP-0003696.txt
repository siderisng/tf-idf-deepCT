<ABSTRACT>
Device for accurately locating a point light source and identifying a direction using at least three sources.It comprises two cylindrical diopters (2, 3) of focusing, a linear network of photosensitive elements (1) and, associated with one of the diopters, a prismatic mounting (4, 5) performing a 90 Â° image rotation, the assembly for determining by the calculation from the signals detected the direction of location of the source.With a triangle of three sources arranged on a hardware-free mobile annex structure with the device, the directional marking of an axis bound to the structure is obtained.The invention applies in particular in helmet viewfinder systems.
