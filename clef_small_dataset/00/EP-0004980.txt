<ABSTRACT>
the coupler comprises one or more optical systems  formed by or equivalent to a set of reflecting surfaces (4,5,6)  whose sections are segments of ellipses having two by two a  common focus (1a, 2a, 3a); each focus lies in the centre of  the end face of an optical waveguide (1,2,3) able both to  launch a light beam to be shared among other waveguides,  and to collect either a light beam resulting from a sharing or  more light beams to be combined.
