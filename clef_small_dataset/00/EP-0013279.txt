<ABSTRACT>
arrangement of a touch switch having position pressure fields (1) which are used for changing the switching action and which are located at the front of a glass pane (2) in the form of vapour-deposited metal alloy layers, and having contact areas (3) which are arranged at the rear of the glass pane opposite to the position pressure fields and can be conductively connected to a printed circuit, in which arrangement the contact areas are located at the rear of the support plate (4) of the printed circuit and the support plate can be placed and pressed with this side against the rear of the glass plate.  <image>
