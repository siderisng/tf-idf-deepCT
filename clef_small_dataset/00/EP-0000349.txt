<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to polyurethane modified polysiloxanes, which are characterized in that polysiloxane and polyurethane are present as a distinctive phases, optionally with partially chemical and / or physical binding.The polyurethane filled organopolysiloxane masses are prepared by intensive mixing of the organopolysiloxane liquid with a di- or polyol and subsequent addition of a diisocyanate to this mixture.The mixtures can be from about 3 to 95 wt.% Polyurethane - based on the total mixture - included.
