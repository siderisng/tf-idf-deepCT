<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method and a pneumatic ferter trans assembly of a pulverulent material contained in a cylindrical container.This method consists in introducing by a sealed passage (9) located at the top (5) and in the axis of the container (1), a suction cane (3) movable in translation along said axis and presenting to itssuction end located in the peak rewrite, an annular nozzle (19) capable of directing a gas jet to the walls of the container so as to scan these walls during suction, the level N₂ of this annular nozzle inThe container being maintained constantly above the level N₁ of the powder material contained in the container, until the total suction of the powdery material, including on the bottom of the container.
