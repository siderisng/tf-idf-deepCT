<ABSTRACT>
an improved tractor draft sensing mechanism of a spe­ cial construction so designed that it will transmit only draft  signals to the tractor draft control linkage.  the mechanisms  includes a rigid sensing arm (50) hinged (60) at one end  to a mounting clamp (52) disposed about a bendable sens­ ing shaft (18), the mounting clamp being provided with set  screws (70) which contact a surface (74) of a housing  (32) to resist relative rotational movement of the mounting  clamp and thus the sensing arm about the bendable shaft.
