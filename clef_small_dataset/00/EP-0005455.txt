<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an improvement in the methods for producing electrically conductive organopolysiloxane elastomers by crosslinking masses based on diorganopolysiloxane and electrically conductive carbon black by attaching Si-bonded hydrogen to aliphatic multiple bond or ionizing radiation.The improvement is achieved by the fact that the masses to be crosslinked as an electrically conductive carbon black 15 to 60% by weight, based on the weight of organopolysiloxane, Furnace carbon black with a surface of 40 to 100 m² / g and 5 to 25 weight percent, based on the weightof organopolysiloxane, acetylene radical included.The inventive method is preferably used for producing electrically conductive plates, coatings, bands and profiles.
