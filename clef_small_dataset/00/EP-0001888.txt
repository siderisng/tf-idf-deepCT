<ABSTRACT>
a sterilising solution for sterilizing contact lenses, particularly  soft hydrogel-type contact lenses, which sterilizing  solution contains, as the active bacteriostatic ingredients  thereof, a solution of sodium chloride in combination with  alcohol, particularly ethyl alcohol, and, optionally, a  protein-denaturing amount of urea.  soft contact lenses  immersed in and treated with such sterilizing solutions are  sterilized rapidly and effectively in a short period of time  against a variety of bacteria.
