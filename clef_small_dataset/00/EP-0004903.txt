<ABSTRACT>
A process for electrolysis of hydrochloric acid for producing chlorine and hydrogen according to the diaphragm process is described after the electrolysis is carried out multi-level and the brine is degassed after each electrolysis level before entering the next step.Further, an electrolytic cell suitable for the method will be described.
