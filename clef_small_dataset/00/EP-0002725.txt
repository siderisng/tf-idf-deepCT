<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a film-forming composition of an organic base material for soldering flow means (a), a carboxylic acid anhydride, an aldehyde or mixtures thereof (b), a non-reactive composition (C), a synthetic thermoplastic polymeric (D) and a plasticizer (e).The constituents of the composition are in one of the soluble.The composition is applied prior to the backside coating of modules on the terminal pins at a certain height and removed after applying and curing the encapsulating material by washing with water.Following this, the pins are placed again with the film forming composition, which also acts as a soldering filler before introducing into the solder bath.
