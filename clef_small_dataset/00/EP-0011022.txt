<ABSTRACT>
the invention relates to a pump allowing the circulation of a fluid and equipped with a vibration-damping system and to such a damping system.  to ensure an effective damping, whatever the pumped fluid, especially even if this fluid is a polyphase and corrosive fluid, the damper comprises at least one diaphragm, behind which is arranged a liquid communicating with an external capacity of variable volume, and the damper has no dead zone, in which one or more phases of the pumped fluid could accumulate.  such a pump is designed particularly for the pumping of a fluid, such as mercury, especially for circulating the mercury of a mercury-cathode electrolyser.  <image>
