<ABSTRACT>
Mountable and remotely removable nuclear fuel assembly and corresponding tools. One ends of each pulling (6) is constantly attached in the upper plate and the other end of each tie (6) is attached to the lower plate (4) by a remote assembly and remote removable, said assembly consisted both in a thread (6a) formed at the end of each pulling (6), a screwing member (18) having a threadable to cooperate with the thread (6a) of the pulling (6) and a head compror so much a hole to key and on its periphery, at least one bevel (18a, 18b) and machining in the lower plate (4) constituted by a bore (4D) comprising a portion (4b) of larger diameter opening in the outer face of said plate (4), said portion (4b) of larger diameter being surrounded by a collar (4a) carved in said plate (4), said rene glue (4a) being separated from the rest of the plate (4) by an annular groove (4c). The corresponding tools are: the screwing tool and unscrew the flange fitting tool and the crimping tool of the collar. Application to pressurized water reactors.
