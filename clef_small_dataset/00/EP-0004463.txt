<TITLE>
cartons impregnated with perfume
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

<ABSTRACT>
packs containing cleaning powders, the inside surface of the pack being impregnated, for example by spraying, with perfumes.  the packs are particularly useful for holding cleaning powders which contain components which degrade perfumes, for example chlorine bleaches and combinations of oxygen bleaches and acid bleach precursors.  the packs can be cartons or drums, or any other configuration and, if desired, can be impregnated while in blank form.
