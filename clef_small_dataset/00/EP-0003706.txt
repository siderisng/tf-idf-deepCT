<ABSTRACT>
Digital Division Division System in which each multipoint link is performed by means of a multipoint unit (4) between a main transceiver (P) and secondary transceivers (s) that communicate, each in turn, with the transceiver main. The network of connections (32) of the splitter (3) is connected to third order lines (30) multiplexing the data lines of the transceivers of the first order (1). Each multipoint unit (4) takes a multiplex multiplex at (n + 1) times the second flow of the multiplex lines of the second intermediate order (2), where n is equal to the maximum number of secondary transceivers communicating with a main transceiver of A multipoint link carried out by the multipoint unit, the n numbers associated with different multipoint units. The location of the temporary windows of the main data in the third order multresis is determined during the respective introductions of the multipoint units in the system.
