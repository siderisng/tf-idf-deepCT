<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an internal engagement motor valve This valve is of the mushroom type and forced circulation cooled with a refrigerant fluid.It is characterized in that the channels 9, 10, 11 provided in the valve head 2 are formed by sections of rectilinear channels which extend from the periphery of the head and open outwardly, andIn that the said rectilinear canals are closed at the level of their openings 14 outward.The invention allows the manufacture of the valve in a single piece.
