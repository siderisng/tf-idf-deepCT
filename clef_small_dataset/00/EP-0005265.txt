<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for contacting the adhesive-side electrode (4) of a electrical component connected to an electrically conductive support plate (1) by means of an insulating adhesive (5), which ensures not only a secure contact, but is also suitable for automation.This is achieved according to the invention in that the adhesive layer (5) applied to the support plate (1) applied to the support plate (1) is displaced on one or more places by contact-forming means (6) in the joining and adhesive operation taking place under heat and pressure action.As a contact-forming agent can be used on the adhesive coated carrier plate (1) embossed elements (6).
