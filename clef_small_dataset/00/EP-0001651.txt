<ABSTRACT>
(poly)alkylene glycol monoalkyl ethers are prepared by  reacting an olefin with a (poly)alkylene glycol at a temperature  of from 20°c to 250°c in the presence of an acid catalyst and,  as polar solvent, sulphur dioxide.  the use of sulphur dioxide  improves the conversion of the olefin and enhances the selectivity  to the (poly)alkylene glycol monoalkyl ether.
