<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for the thermal treatment of fine-grained good using a multi-stage cyclone preheater (2) and a rotary tube furnace (1), wherein additional fuel in one - in dependence of grain size, reactivity, moisture and the support of the fuel of fuel to non-flammableIngredients elected - step of preheater (2) is introduced, so that the largest part of the fuel burns almost completely in the lowest stage of the preheater (2).As a result, low treatment costs for the fuel can achieve a high calcination level of the good before a kick in the rotary kiln (1).
