<ABSTRACT>
in an installation block for the connection of a liquid heater to the heating circuit and for the connection of elements which require operation, such as circulating pumps (14), thermostatic and multi-way valves (20) and the like, but also for the flow-favourable design of the channels conducting the liquid and the arrangement of bypass channels (19'), a double wall (2) is provided, which, for the purpose of avoiding sealing inserts, is formed from sheet steel shells (7, 8) which are stamped out in the channel regions and which are connected along the channel edges (9) in a liquidproof manner by means of welding, connectors for the adjustment and control devices (14, 18, 20) being provided in the channel regions.
