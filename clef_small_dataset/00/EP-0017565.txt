<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a new method of inclusion of microorganisms in a poly mother matrix.This inclusion method consists of using a matrix based on at least one polysaccharide group polymer and is characterized by the polymer at least partial crosslinking treatment.Advantageously for drying the gel of a substance with large water absorption capacity such as silica is added.The method according to the invention applies in particular to the inclusion of microorganisms intended for inoculation of plants.
