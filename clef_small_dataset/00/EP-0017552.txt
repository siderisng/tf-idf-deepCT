<ABSTRACT>
The pump has several cylinders (11) attached to a frame (2) according to a barrel arrangement and the pistons (12) susceptible to slide in said cylinders (11) under the action of a training mechanism (3) andcomprises a pipe assembly channeling the liquid to move from a general suction piping (81) to the Dres (11) cylinders, through suction valves (61), and said cylinders (11) to a pipeGeneral discharge (82), through discharge valves (71), and this pipe assembly comprises on the one hand a central cylinder head (4) pierced by a central distribution channel (41) connected to the general piping ofAspiration (81) and feeding bypass (42) which radiate around it up to the lateral litters (43) and on the other hand side cylinder heads (5) drilled ducts (56) channeling the liquid.
