<ABSTRACT>
the invention provides an abrasive body suitable for  mounting in a tool for abrading operations, the body including  an abrasive compact (12) comprising a mass of abrasive  particles, present in an amount of at least 70 percent by volume  of the compact (12), bonded into a hard conglomerate  and support means (10) covering a part of the surface of the  compact (12) and bonded to the compact (12), characterised  in that the support means (10) covers more than 50 percent  of the surface of the compact (12) and the compact (12) presents  an arcuate formation (14), salient in relation to the support  means (10), capable of abrading a workpiece.  
</ABSTRACT>
