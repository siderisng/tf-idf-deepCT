<ABSTRACT>
for the preparation of light-stable polyurethane integral foams, in particular soft integral foams, aliphatic and / or cycloaliphatic polyisocyanates, preferably urethane and / or biuret-containing aliphatic and / or cycloaliphatic polyisocyanates having polyols in the presence of propellants, a synergistic catalyst combination consisting of alkali hydroxides, alkali alcoholates, Alkaliphenolates, alkali salts of weak acids and / or hexahydrotriacolor derivatives and an organic tin, zinc and / or iron (II) compound and optionally auxiliary and additives. Preferably used is a catalyst combination of tri (diMtylaminopropyl) -S hexahydrotriazine and dibutyltin dilaurate. Polyurethane integral foams in the flexible setting are suitable for the production of automotive parts such as neck supports, armrests, crash pads and bumper, shoe soles and sports mats; The hard settings are used as sporting goods, window frames and wood set.
</ABSTRACT>
