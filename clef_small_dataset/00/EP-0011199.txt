<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a holding clamp for rods, pipes or profile strips, which should be releasably secured to another profile strip.The holding clamp according to the invention is formed by a U-profile iron (1), through the flange in the middle a screw (4) is guided.At the gifting of this U-profile iron (1), two pipe bands (2,3) are arranged at their ends.that a tube band (3) carries a wedge (7) whose tip indicates to the other tube band (2);This wedge (7) is mounted in the lower pipe band (3) in slot holes (8) which leave the lower tube band (3) free game.
