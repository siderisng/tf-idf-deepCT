<ABSTRACT>
Gas covers in explosion-proof design for detection of flammable gases in the atmosphere, which a gas sensor (6) which operates according to the principle of catalytic oxida tion or contains a semiconductor gas sensor. The gas detector has at least two separate spaces in a housing (1). The one space (2) is compressed pressure resistant and includes an electronic evaluation circuit for the gas sensor (6) of the other space (3) performed in explosion-protected design contains the gas sensor (6) and a matching element (16) and is from the atmosphere by a Sintered metal bush (5) separated. For connecting the lines in the gas detector, a third, explosion-proof space (4) may be provided. The gas sensor (6) forms with the adjustment adapter (7) a unit which is pluggable in the lid (1b) of the room containing the electronics (2). In such a gas detector, a replacement of the gas sensor (6) in a simple manner on the site is possible without the pressure-encapsulated room (2) for the electronics must be opened. The testing and adjustment of the gas sensor (6) can be done in the factory.
