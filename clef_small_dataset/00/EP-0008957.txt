<ABSTRACT>
apparatus for transferring solid particles to a receptacle  comprises a loading tube (2) surrounded at or near its base  by a gas supply chamber (7) terminating in a coanda surface  (9).  a slot (10) between the outlet from the loading tube (2)  and the beginning of the coanda surface (9) forms a coanda  nozzle.  a throttle (3) is preferably fitted in the loading tube (2)  to form an annulus (4) down which the particles flow to the  slot (10).  a gas supply line (8) preferably enters a gas supply  (7) tangentially to supply gas to the coanda nozzle.  the  apparatus is particularly suitable for loading catalyst into a  reactor.  
</ABSTRACT>
