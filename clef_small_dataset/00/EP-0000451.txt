<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an object holding forceps, a snap, stalling and tiling system of such a clip.This is essentially constituted by a clamp (1) made of injected plastic, cut or molded, low thick, provided with a triangular opening (4) itself opened by a small slot (6) on the middle of the sideSuperior of the clamp that or does not have bosses for maintaining a steady spacing between several clamps carried by a storage pin before attaching to any transfer or handling system.Such clamp can cooperate effectively with a snap system allowing its passage of a fixed support to a movable support.It can also cooperate with a stall system ensuring the reverse movement and finally with a tiling system for transferring clamps from a low level to a high or vice versa.
