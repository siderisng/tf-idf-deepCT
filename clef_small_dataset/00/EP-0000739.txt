<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method and apparatus for producing cement by burning powdered raw material in a fluidized bed.The preheated raw material is recalced before it with fuel (15), preheated air (27) and a recycled part of the burned good (11) into the fluidized bed - in a preliminal concalation zone (2) with additional fuel to a high degree of donation.From the fluidized bed, the well in a directly underneath cooling zone (5), the cooling air stream is introduced partly from below (19), partly from the side (30) in the fluidized bed.This results in a very stable operation of the fluidized bed, a uniformly burned end product and a low heat consumption.
