<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a novel process for improving the resistance of thermoplastic polyurethanes to discoloration by using relatively small amounts of specific hydroxyl groups having compounds having compounds producing the thermoplastic polyurethane.The additives to be used in the present invention can be added to the reaction mixture before or during the polyaddition reaction.
