<ABSTRACT>
epitaxial voids (7) or tunnels may be formed in crystalline structures from a substrate (1) whose face (2) has a crystallographic orientation cutting two crystallographic planes (3) and (4) with preferential growthThese two shots cutting at a finite distance from the face (2) and thus forming the desired void.A layer of resistant material (5) is disposed on the substrate so as to prevent growth and delimit the vacuum (7).The crystalline structure is preferably made of GaAs, Gap, ZNSE or silicon.By croving the epitaxial layer (6) a P-N junction can be made or forming the point (5) in the point so as to produce punctual light sources.The layer (5) is preferably carried out in SiO₂, A1₂O₃ or Mo.
