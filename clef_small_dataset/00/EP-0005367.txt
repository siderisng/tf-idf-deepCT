<ABSTRACT>
an oral contraceptive is provided comprising as active  ingredient an extract from the leaves and stem of the plant  ecballium elaterium, linn, and a method for preparing it  comprising the steps of   (a) extracting the active ingredient from the plant, ecballium  elaterium, linn, with a solution of liquid ketone,  alcohol and hydrocarbons;   (b) adding a solution of alcoholic hydroxide to the extract  solution;   (c) further extracting for the active ingredient with an  immiscible solvent;   (d) soluting to a predetermined volume with high ionic  strength salt solution;   (e) concentrating the active ingredient from the extract  solution by column chromatography; and   (f) desiccating the concentrated solution
