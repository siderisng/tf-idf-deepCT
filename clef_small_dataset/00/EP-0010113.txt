<ABSTRACT>
apparatus for limiting the axial force, especially for valves, with spring-loaded latching elements (18) which release the spindle nut (8) in the axial direction, for the purpose of preventing overstressing of the threaded spindle (6), if the axial force exceeds a specified level which can be set by suitable selection of the springs (24, 25).  <image>
