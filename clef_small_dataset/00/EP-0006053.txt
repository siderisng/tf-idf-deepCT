<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a differential loading device.It mainly comprises: - two channels (1 and 2) of load transfer which respectively receive the two signals (E₁, E₂) which must be made the difference;- an electrode (GL) common to both channels;means for sampling signals in each of the channels, this sampling being carried out at the same moments in both channels;- a set of electrodes in each channel, ensuring on the application of pending potentials T, the propagation of the samples to the common electrode (GL) with a relative delay equal to T / 2 of a channel relative to the channel.'other.The signal representing the difference in the input signals is taken from the common electrode (GL).This device is in particular applicable to cross-cutting filters and load transfer lines.
