<ABSTRACT>
For a tilting bearing for bridges or similar structures, with upper (1), intermediate (4) and lower plate (7) and a curved sliding surface between the lower plate (7) and intermediate plate (4), is on the curved surface of the lower plate (7) According to their curvature upstream support (6) of corrosion-resistant material and on the underside of the intermediate plate (4), a coating (5) is provided from a sliding material.To improve the operating behavior of such bearing in simple construction and avoiding a hard chrome plating for the spherical surface of the lower plate (7), the sliding surface of the support (6) as the curvature surface of the lower plate (7) is concavely shaped.wherein the support (6) consists of a single-sidedly ground and polished, austenitic steel sheep, whose non-ground and polished surface of the curvature surface of the lower plate (7) is facing.
