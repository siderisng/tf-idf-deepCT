<ABSTRACT>
in a powered conveyor comprising a frame (14), a  plurality of article-supporting rollers (10) mounted in the  frame (14) and a drive belt assembly (16,18) for supplying  driving power to the articles on the rollers, the invention  provides that some at least of the belt guide pulleys (18) act  as control pulleys and for this purpose carry large diameter  rings (24) which are capable of being interposed between the  belt (16) and the pulley hub (22) in order radially to displace  the belt into driving engagement with an associated article-supporting  roller (10).
