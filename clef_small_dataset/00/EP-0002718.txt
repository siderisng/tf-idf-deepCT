<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a trimellitic anhydride ester mixture suitable for paints, which by heating trimellitic anhydride together with a diol in the molar ratio of 1: 1 to 2: 1 for at least 4 hours at 190 to 250 ° C and at least temporarily in an external pressure from 5 to 30 torr inAbsence of a catalyst is obtained.The mixture is used in a lacquer containing epoxy resin and optionally pigments as hardener for the epoxy resin.The paint is used for the interior coating of cans.
