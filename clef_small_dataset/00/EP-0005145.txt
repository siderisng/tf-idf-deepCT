<ABSTRACT>
5-ethenyl-4,4a,7,8-tetrahydro-4a-methyl-2(3h)-naphthalenone,  an intermediate useful in the total synthesis of  steroids, is prepared by dehydrohalogenation of 5-(2-bromo-ethylidene)-2(3h)-4a-methyl-4,4a,5,6,7,8-hexahydronaphthalen-2-one  by a pyrolytic procedure or by means  of dehydrohalogenating agents, for example, a mixture of  lithium bromide and lithium carbonate.
