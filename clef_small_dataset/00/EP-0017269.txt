<ABSTRACT>
a device 10 for unloading radiographic cassettes 18 in  daylight is disclosed which enables the rapid sequential  unloading of said cassettes.  therefore, use is made of an  intermediate storage magazine 14 to which the films 24 after  unloading are fed at a high speed whereinafter they are  dispensed towards a processing apparatus 12 at a speed  equal to the one of the processing apparatus.  as a consequ­ ence, the time necessary to unload a radiographic cassette  18 is substantially reduced, the unloading capacity of the  device 10 being tripled when compared with prior art  machines.  the device according to the invention may be controlled  with the help of a microprocessor.
