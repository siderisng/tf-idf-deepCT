process for preparing an aqueous dispersion of synthetic resin also containing a cellulose acetate alkylate.

<ABSTRACT>
an aqueous dispersion of synthetic resin also containing  a cellulose acetate alkylate is prepared by mixing water, a  protective colloid and/or a surfactant, a cellulose acetate alky­ late, an oil-soluble radical polymerization initiator and at least  one monomer in which the cellulose acetate alkylate and the  oil-soluble radical polymerization initiator are soluble, under  stirring, to form an aqueous dispersion and then subjecting  the monomer to radical polymerization to form a homogene­ ous mixture of the cellulose acetate alkylate and at least one  polymer in the particles in the aqueous dispersion.  the dis­ persion is suitable for the preparation of protective coatings.  
</ABSTRACT>
