<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an anionic polymerization method using an initiator based on alkali starch, as well as the polymers obtained by this method.The polymerization method according to the invention is characterized in that an initiator resulting from the association of an alkali starch selected from the group formed by sodium starch, the potassium amide.Lithium amide and an alkaline salt whose cation is chosen from the group formed by potassium, lithium or sodium, the anion being selected from the group formed by thio cyanate, nitrite, cyanide and cyanate anions.These initiators make it possible to proceed to the anionic polymerization of the vinyl, heterocyclic and dienic monomers, with a view to obtaining special polymers or large diffusion, with great flexibility and in advantageous economical conditions.
