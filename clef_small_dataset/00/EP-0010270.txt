<ABSTRACT>
The novel α-azolyl-α-phenyl-acetic acid derivatives of the formula I in the X hydrogen, fluorine, chlorine or bromine, M 1, 2 or 3, A of the group OR or NR¹R² (where R, R¹ and R² is the meaning given in the descriptionOwn), and AZ denote an imidazole 1-yl or 1,2,4-triazole-1-yl residue, e.g.b.α- (2,4-dichlorophenyl) - α- (1,2,4-triazol-1-yl) -acetic acid N-2-butyl-N-methylamide, as well as their acid addition salts and metal complexes, have good fungicidal effects.They can be prepared by reacting the corresponding α-halogen-α-phenylacetic acid derivatives with imida zol or 1,2,4-triazole.
