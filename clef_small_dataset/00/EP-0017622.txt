<ABSTRACT>
A filtration device has a chamber (5) with a helix (21) which rotates helically about the chamber axis (13).The helix (21) is formed by a gutter-shaped carrier, which spans a filter formed by a helix-shaped tube.In filtries of a suspension, the liquid component of the suspension passes through the filter into the gutter-shaped carrier and from this to a filtrate outlet (33).Since at least the active part of the filter effective for the filtration forms a substantially husband, wrinkled surface, a uniform utilization of the entire effective area of the filter is possible.Furthermore, the solid residue deposited on the surface of the filter can be easily carried out of the chamber (5) by turning the helix (21).
