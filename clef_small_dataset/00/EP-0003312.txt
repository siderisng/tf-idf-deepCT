An irradiation device for curing by radiation curable masses comprises a lamp (16) whose light radiation contains both the spectral parts required for curing as well as outside of these uses spectral parts.On the ellipsoidal inner surface of a glass body (18) surrounding the lamp (18), a thin layer filter (19) is applied, which is such that it reflects only the useful radiation, the non-desired spectral ranges, in particular the largest proportion of visible light and the heat radiation, on the other hand transmitted.If the lamp power is dropped by clumping the filament, the lamp supply is periodically interrupted, so that a flashing signal is generated for the warning of the user.
