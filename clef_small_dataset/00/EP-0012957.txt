<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a diagnostic agent for detection of proteolytic enzymes consisting of an absorbent support, a film layer, a powder mixture, a lyophilisate, a solution or reagent tablet containing one or more chromogenic, a suitable buffer substance and optionally additional, usually used auxiliaries, wherein as chromogenic indoxyl and / or thioindoxyl amino acid esters and / or peptide esters of the aligemic formula I in R₁, R₂, R₃, R₄ which may be the same or different, each hydrogen or halogen, a lower alkyl, low alkoxy, Aryl, aralkyl, aralkoxy, a hydroxy, a carboxy, a carboxy-lower-alkoxy, an aralkoxycarbonyl, an aralkoxycarbonyl-lower-alkoxy, a nitro or lower acylamino group or two adjacent substituents each optionally by halogen substituted benzoanneled radical, x a sulfur atom or an optionally A lower alkyl, an aryl, an aralkyl or acyl group-substituted imino group, A is an amino acid or a peptide rating, b a nitrogen protection group derived in the peptide chemistry or derived therefrom, are used. The invention further relates to the new, suitable as chromogenic indoxyl or thioindoxyl esters of the general formula I, their preparation and their use for the preparation of the diagnostic agent according to the invention. The preparation of the compounds of general formula I takes place by reacting the corresponding 3-hydroxy-indole or thioindole in a manner known per se with amino acids or peptides or suitable reactive derivatives thereof.
