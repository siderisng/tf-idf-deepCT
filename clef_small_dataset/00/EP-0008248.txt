<ABSTRACT>
the method and the device are used for correcting the rotation of the spine of a patient (1) lying on a horizontal support (2), the said spine having suffered a rotational deformation giving rise to a hump.  treatment of scoliosis.  this method and device are characterised in that there is arranged, under the said hump and between the said support (2) and the patient (1), a transverse flexible rest (17) securely anchored at its end (5) opposite the said hump, and in that an upwardly directed traction is exerted on the end of the flexible rest (17) near the hump and projecting with respect to the patient (1).  <image>
