<TITLE>
a method of removing metallic impurities from sewage sludge.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
a method of removing metallic impurities from sewage  sludge wherein the sludge is first acidified in the presence of  an oxidising agent (10) and held to take metallic impurities  into solution (15).  the sludge is then treated with a  flocculating agent (17) and thickened (18) by removing liquid  prior to dilution (21) with an aqueous liquid (22) free from  metallic impurities and wherein the sludge is finally dewatered  by a mechanical method (24).  
</ABSTRACT>
