<ABSTRACT>
in a pressure vessel having domed ends concave to the  pressure, the space within the dome is in certain situations  undesirable.  to minimize this space, one or both ends of the  vessel are equipped with space-filling inserts comprising  multiple plates (13) of aluminium or other light metal  secured in closely spaced parallel relationship.  the plates are  preferably thicker than the spaces between them and may be  formed with fluid ports (17).  
</ABSTRACT>
