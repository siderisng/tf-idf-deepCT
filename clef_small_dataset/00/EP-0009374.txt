<ABSTRACT>
a hollow filament separatory module (20) comprises  a tube (22) wound from layers (23 to 25) of hollow semi-­ permeable filaments.  each layer is formed from a helical  winding and the filament of each layer has a predetermined  length within plus or minus 10%.  one end of the annulus  is encapsulated in potting compound (66) and the com­ pound is sliced through to expose open ends of the fila­ ments in each layer.  the tube (22) is enclosed in a pressure  casing (32, 34, 36, 38) having a fluid inlet (44) at one end  and two outlets (50, 52) at the other end.  the outlet (50)  communicates with the open ends of the filaments and  fluid which permeates through the filaments flows from it.   the outlet (52) communicates with the outsides of the  filments and fluid which does not enter the tubes flows  from it.  the outlets (50 and 52) are separated by sealing  rings (58 and 60), which form seals between the periphery  of the potting compound (66) and the inside face of the  casing part (34).
