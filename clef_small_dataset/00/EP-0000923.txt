<TITLE>
device for detecting, measuring and indicating the energy consumption of electricity consumers.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

<ABSTRACT>
the device is suitable for measuring the power consumption of an individual consumer or of a specific group of consumers, in that it is connected upstream thereof.  the device includes an electricity meter operating in accordance with a principle known per se; in this case, the housing is provided, at the front, not only with a window (4) for reading off the kwh but also with a socket (2) for connecting up the consumer.  at the rear of the device (1) an earthing-contact-type plug is preferably provided for insertion into a normal earthing-contact- type socket.  <image>
