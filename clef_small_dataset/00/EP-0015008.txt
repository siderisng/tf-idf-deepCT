<ABSTRACT>
the alkali resistance of the glasses, in particular the cement lye resistance of glass fibres, is increased by providing the glass article with a metal and/or metal oxide coating.  the metals or metal oxides used are transition metals from groups iiia, iva and va of the periodic table.  preference is given to iron or iron oxide or vanadium, titanium, chromium, manganese, cobalt, zirconium, or copper, or oxides thereof. 
</ABSTRACT>
