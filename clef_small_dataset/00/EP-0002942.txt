<ABSTRACT>
the invention relates to a process for producing  3-amino-2-cyclohexenone which comprises heating 5-oxohexanenitrile  at 160°c to 220°c in the presence of a basic  catalyst in an inert organic polar solvent.  the temperature  of the cyclization reaction in accordance with the invention  is higher than that of known processes, but the desired  3-amino-2-cyclohexenone which is useful as an intermediate  in the production of m-aminophenol, cyclohexane-1,3-dione  and resorcinol, is produced at high conversion  and with good selectivity.
