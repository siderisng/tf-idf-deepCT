<ABSTRACT>
the manipulator has a baseframe (1) with a lifting column (2) and an arm (4) which can be swivelled about the vertical axis of the lifting column and moved vertically and at the end of which there is arranged a gripper (9), the arm (4) being articulated at its inside end on a slide (3), which can be moved on the lifting column (2), in such a way that it can be swivelled about a vertical axis (8) and, between itself and the gripper (9) supporting at least two further swivel arms (5, 6), each with a vertical swivelling axis (7, 7), each of which can be swivelled through a round angle.  <image>
