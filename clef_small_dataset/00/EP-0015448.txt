<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a vacuum-density, high frequency-transmissive window assembly between a gear-side (4, 5) and an output side (7, 8) coaxial line, in particular for hiking field tubes.In this Fen Sterananordnung, the dielectric losses are to be kept so small in high performance tubes in particular, that a too high thermal load and thus a jumping or undicht is avoided of the window arrangement.The invention provides that between the input side (4, 5) and the output side (7, 8) coaxial line a ceramic shear hollow cylinder (1) is arranged, which the inner conductor (4) of the input-side coaxial line (4, 5) in the region of the transitionProvides the output side coaxial line (7, 8) as the continuation of the outer conductor (5) of the input-side Ko axial line (4, 5).A window according to the invention is used in particular for high performance hiking processes.
