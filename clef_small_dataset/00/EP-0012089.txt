<ABSTRACT>
A machine for spraying slaughterboard animal skins includes a tank (1a, 1b) open upwards and a horizontal tray (3) disposed at the lower part of the tank and rotatively driven on a central pivot (4).On its surface in contact with the skins, the tray (3) is provided with bright edges (8) on the contour of the perforations of the tray.This plate (3) is delimited to its periphery by a fixed perforated cylinder (5) defining with the inner wall of the tank (1) an annular space (7) which ensures the evacuation of the hairstered and projected by the centrifugal force inThe openings of the cylinder (5).
