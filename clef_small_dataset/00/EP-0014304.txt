<TITLE>
method and apparatus for the contactless measuring of the potential on an electronic element.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The absorbent potential measurement is done with an electron beam, wherein the measuring point is placed on a predetermined potential and the output voltage of a control amplifier is set to a predetermined setpoint (US) by controlling the photomultiplier voltage (UPM) and then the photo multiplier voltage (UPM) is kept constant.The measuring voltage (UM) is then determined from the difference (UR -US) between the controller output voltage (UR) and the setpoint (US).The method allows quantitative potential measurement on tracks of an integrated circuit without manual setting.
</ABSTRACT>
