<ABSTRACT>
A method of electrochemical Bear control of metallic surfaces is indicated by the workpieces disposed for anode and cathode.The workpieces are contacted with an electrolyte, which contains for transmission electrical charge on the electrolistically conductive body suspended in the electrolyte to be machined in the electrolyte, which are charged in an electrolytic cell on the anode and prior to discharge to the cathode of the electrolysis cell by aCathode Abschirmende Diaphragma or an ion exchange Mem Brong are protected.To accelerate the removal of the metallic surface, a compound of the five valuent vanadium or a vanadium compound is dissolved in the electrolyte, which forms on the anode of the electrolytic cell vanadium (V) ions.Preferably vanadium pentoxide is used.
