<ABSTRACT>
1.  apparatus for preventing the lowermost shield (1) from sliding down or tilting over when advancing the shield support at a coal-face (4) up a steep incline, which comprises at least one hydraulic cylinder which does not form part of the means for moving the shields, characterized in that a suspended hydraulic cylinder (15), which is known per se, connects the canopies (13, 14) of the two adjacent shields (1, 2) by means of universal joints (16) and that, at one end of a floor-mounted hydraulic cylinder (9), which is known per se, there is arranged a trolley (10) which is arranged to move on a rail (12) which is fixed on the horizontal base (7) of the lowermost shield (1), whilst other end of the cylinder (9) is arranged on the horizontal base (8) of the adjacent shield (2) so as be vertically rotatable.
