<ABSTRACT>
The mixing device for chemically reading, liquid components, such.b.Polyurethane, has a mixing chamber to open inlet openings (5, 6) for the components and out of which one out of the outlet for the component mixture.In the mixing chamber, a control piston (2) is eliminated, which closes the inlet openings (5, 6) and releases the mixing chamber mechanically.Continue lead into the mixing chamber return lines (3, 4), which are connected in the closed position of the control piston (2) by return grooves (7, 8) in the jacket surface of the control piston (2) with the corresponding inlet opening (5, 6).Between the return grooves (7, 8), in particular for preventing mutually overlapping recesses are provided, which are designed for example as slots (10) or as blind holes (9).
