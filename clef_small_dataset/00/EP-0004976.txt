<ABSTRACT>
For a universal warning strip for attaching to the back of vehicles, in particular of motor vehicles, for visual information from vehicle guides, especially at night, about the presence, distance, width and length of a preceding vehicle. Light reflective elements (1), z. b. From a reflection film, or light-emitting elements (1), z. b. Light bulbs provided. For the purpose of providing and transmitting more detailed and receptional information, the light elements (1) in two rows are arranged one above the other under preservation of the same distances between the individual light elements (1) and form a simple warning information module, with arrangement of two such modules one above each other a double warning information module ( 5). The module number in a simple and double strip depends on the width of the vehicle. The light elements (1) and all distances (2, 3, 6, 7) are chosen so that with increasing observation removal of multiple apparent connection of the light spots to figures with different forms corresponding to the individual distance intervals takes place, and in the case of decreasing removal of the process is reversed .
