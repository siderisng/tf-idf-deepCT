<ABSTRACT>
Artificial knee joint with a joint upper part (1) and a hinge base (4) each having a front and a rear swivel (8-11), of which the two front and the two rear by rearwardly curved handlebars (5,6)interconnected.To simplify the structure, it is provided that the connection is formed by two links arranged on both sides of the joint center (5.6) that the connecting lines between the two front pivot joints (8.9) on the one hand and the two rear turn joints (10.11)On the other hand, are inclined at a small angle to each other, and that the connection line between the two turning joints (9, 11) of the joint subteil (4) extends approximately horizontally.As a result, the knee joint can be built room-saving with only two handlebars.
</ABSTRACT>
