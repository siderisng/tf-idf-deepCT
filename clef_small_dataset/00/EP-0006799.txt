In a diazocopie development machine, in the development chamber (1) is generated by ammoniacal vapors, a system having a set of "rear" walls (51), (52) and (53)and a set of "front" walls (54), (55), (56) and (57).said vapors from the cup (9) are thus accelerated, without the supply of external energy, and channeled towards the copy support which moves between the perforated wall (17) and the coating (5) of the rotary cylinder (6).The angle of incidence of ammoniacal vapors on the support of the copies is less than 45 ° and preferably less than or equal to 30 °.
