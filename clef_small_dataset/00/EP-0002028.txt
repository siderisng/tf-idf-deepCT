<ABSTRACT>
A cannula for blood collection is formed for controlling the flow from a puncture cannula (2) and a spacing cannula connected by a flexible hose (4), wherein the hose between the cannula can be clamped.In particular, the two cannula (2, 3) and the hose (4) are inserted into a housing (1) with a resilient, clamping the hose (5).Preferably, the lever (5) is arranged and formed so that it is lifted off by a screwed in the housing (1) tube (6) from the hose (4).As a result, when inserting a tube, the flow is opened, but closed again when removing the tube.
