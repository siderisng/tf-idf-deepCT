<ABSTRACT>
In a process for producing mineral fiber mats by bonding mineral fibers, an emulsion of water glass and isocyanate is used, which has been prepared in an upstream mixing aggregate;This mechanical mixing gives a relatively highly viscous stable emulsion that is still well to promote pumps and can be spread well with air.Preferably, commercially available water glasses are used, which is an alkali oxide SiO₂ ratio of 1: 1.8 to 1: 4.1 beschien.Optionally, suitable additives of the emulsion can be added as emulsifier aids, catalysts surfactants.
