<ABSTRACT>
Bicycle cyclic polyethers of general formula: where a amphiphilous assembly consisting of a hydrophilic portion and a lipophilic portion, connected to the cycle by the hydrophilic part, the hydrophilic portion consists of one or more elements that may comprise Amine, amine oxide, ammonium, ammonium alkanoate, ammonium alkoyl sufonate, ammonio alkyl sufinate, thioether, sulfoxide, sulfonium, sulfoxonium, ether, hydroxyl, ester, amine, acid; The lipophilic portion consists of one or more groups selected from aliphatic, aliphatic substituted and alkylaryl groups. These compounds can be prepared by reaction of the tetramer of epichlorohydrin with a reagent selected from amines, mercaptans, thioethanol, thioglycerol, a lower ester of the mercapto-acetic or propionic acid. In the product obtained the chlorine atoms are replaced by hydroxyls and the resulting compounds can be oxidized and / or salified and / or alkyl. Cyclical polyethers exert a surfactant action at low concentrations and are ulilizable in various industries.
