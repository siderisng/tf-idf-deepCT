<ABSTRACT>
in a method for the immunological analysis of trace  components by marking or labelling an antigen or antibody  with a marker, an immune reaction is caused using an  antigen or antibody marked with a fogging agent for silver  halide, the labelled antigen or antibody is separated from  the labelled antigen-antibody reaction product, the silver  halide is developed in the presence of either one of the  labelled antigen or antibody and the labelled antigen-anti­ body reaction product, and the density obtained is mea­ sured.  the method is comparable to radioimmunoassay in  having high reproductibility and sufficient sensitivity but  does not involve any risk due to radiation.  
</ABSTRACT>
