<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a precipitant to the phosphatization of wastewater, a method for its preparation and its use.The precipitant contains 5 - 75 wt.% active iron (III) sulfate, 25 - 95 wt.% active iron (III) oxide, as well as optionally 0 - 10 wt.% Wasserf Reius Magnesium sulfate and optionally an alkaline component.
