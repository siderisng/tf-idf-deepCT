<ABSTRACT>
A CVD coating apparatus for small parts has a suitably cooling or heat-bearing, double wall vessel (1), as a reaction room in which a susceptor (14) serving a support frame (8) serving simultaneously to be coated small parts with at least two thermoels Contains (12,13) ​​for temperature control, is arranged. The reactor (1) further has a gas supply (4,5,6,) adjustable in height and rotatable, and a gas discharge. The susceptor (14) can be brought to anneal by an induct tension coil (15). For the final quenching of the coated parts from the susceptor (14) as soon as possible, the support frame (8) is coupled to the gas supply line (4); For skewer len with gas, an additional gas line (18) is provided at the reactor (1).
