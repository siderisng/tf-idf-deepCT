<ABSTRACT>
the components of an exposure station of a copier  are contained within a housing.  a mechanical scanning  system scans an original document on a platen (50) to  direct imaging rays on to an imaging element through a  slot in the housing between light shield plates (25 and 26).   a blower (20) has two sets of outlet louvres (21 and 23).   louvres (21) provide an airstream (58) to cool the optical  components.  louvres (23) direct an airstream (59) on to a  coanda surface (24) and this surtace directs airstream  (59) to the slot to cause air to flow out of the slot and  thereby prevent the ingress of toner contaminated air.
