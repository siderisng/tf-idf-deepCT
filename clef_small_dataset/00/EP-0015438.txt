<ABSTRACT>
In a function input unit with contact buttons (5a-5d), in which a switching function is triggered when contacting a switching function, transparent contact surfaces (5) are applied to the user facing the user of a transparent carrier (4) during the opposite side, a possibly, replaceable display unit (2) is arranged.Preferably, individual regions (2a-2d) of the display unit (2) are assigned locally to the contact buttons (5a-5d).Individual contact surfaces can be freely clamped for variable design of key groups according to the respective needs.
