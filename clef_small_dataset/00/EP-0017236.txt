<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a voltammetry cell from a receiving vessel (1) with sealed cover (9) in which two projecting electrodes (5) (6) in the vessel are provided as auxiliary and reference electrode and a measuring electrode through guide (7). The latter ends immediately below the lid (9) in a coupling piece to which a measuring electrode (8) is coupled. Analyzer samples are introduced into this cell by means of a removable tie gel (2) which is inserted into a recess (3) on the vessel bottom. This is preferably formed in a removable ground insert (4) for the upright cone. The crucible (2) can be formed by the crucibles used for sample preparation (digestion, etc.). The voltammetry cell is characterized by convenient handling. It is easy to clean or equip with one-way levels. Extracent measuring electrodes are obtained by immersing a given factor a frontally adhesive electrodes material disk having metallic (hollow) rods in curing resin to achieve an adhering resin clumping, removal from the resin, hardening and to rotate on the desired electrode shell shape.
