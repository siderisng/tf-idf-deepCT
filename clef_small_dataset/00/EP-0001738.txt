<ABSTRACT>
1,3,5-s-hexahydrotrisubstituted triazines exemplified by  1,3,5-tris (furfuryl)-1,3,5-hexahydro-s-triazines are useful as  antimicrobial agents particularly when employed as a component  of metal-working compositions subject to fungal and  bacterial attack.  they are prepared by the reaction of a furane  derivative containing a chain substituted by a primary amino  group and a suitable amine with formaldehyde.
