<ABSTRACT>
Method and device for controlling the flatness of a cold rolled metal strip where the strip (D) passes, as a result of the cylinders (2, 3), a device for measuring the tractions on three zones of the width of the band.and for the determination of potential gaps in relation to theoretical values.The device comprises for this purpose three push rollers (B), which exert, via the support rollers (4), an extra large vertical thrust on stress sensors (6) carried by a transverse beam(7) secured with columns (8) of the rolling mill.The correction of the deviations can be made manually or automatically.
