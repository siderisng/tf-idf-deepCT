<ABSTRACT>
In a method and apparatus for continuous surface treatment of endless moldings with blasting agents, preferably corundum or quartz sand, the endless molding (1) for beam treatment is carried out for avoiding dust or noise through an encapsulated container (2) in which the blasting means (6)Current gas is held as a fluidized bed in suspension.Inside the fluidized bed, directed nozzles (10) are arranged on the surface of the endless molded body performed, from which a finer gas stream is pressed at high pressure, with a large surface enlargement being achieved with only low roughness depth.
