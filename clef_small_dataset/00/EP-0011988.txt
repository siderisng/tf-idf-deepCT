<ABSTRACT>
a laser beam diffuser for use in laser beam annealing  comprises a silica laser beam guiding pipe (3), having a diffusing  surface (6) or layer at one end and a polished surface (12)  at the other end.  the diffusing surface (6) may be a ground  surface.  a bend (8) or bends removes axial component of  speckle produced by the ground surface.  adjacent the  polished end (12) the pipe maybe of reduced cross section  (10,11).
