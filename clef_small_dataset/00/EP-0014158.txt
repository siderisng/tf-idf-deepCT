<ABSTRACT>
It consists of having an explosive load along a periodic curve, defined accurately, and whose axis coincides with the axis of the weld seal.There is simultaneously a significant decrease in the residual voltages due to welding, and an increase in the corrosion resistance of the weld seal by exploding the load.An additional charge can be associated with a base load to ensure reliable propagation of the explosion in the baseload.
