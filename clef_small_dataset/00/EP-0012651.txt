<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the smatch liquid crystal display cells in which the liquid crystal is at the isotropic state under the action of a light beam (11) and returns to the smectic state by becoming diffusing.It consists of exciting a photo-conductive layer (9) with this light beam.Under the effect of an electrical voltage the points thus excited heat and carry the underlying portions of the liquid crystal (1) in the isotropic state.The action of the light beam is thus indirect.It makes it possible to make display cells use as the source of this light beam a Laser HE does not of reduced power.
