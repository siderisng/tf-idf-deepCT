<ABSTRACT>
hook comprising a blocking opening 5 whose base is wide enough to receive a flat chain link, in an approximately horizontal position when the hook is suspended, a groove 6 with parallel faces, which is located in a plane perpendicular to that of the base of this opening, opening out into this base.  in order to permit the use of the hook with any chain, in a reversible manner, the blocking opening forms at least the lower part of the opening of the hook; the transverse ends 5b of the said opening are flared; finally, a groove 6 with parallel faces opens out into each of these transverse ends.  <image>
