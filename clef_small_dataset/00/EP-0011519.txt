<ABSTRACT>
The subject of the invention relates to a method of coating glass or ceramic objects, in particular of glass bottles, previously coated with a surface treatment agent, of the type of applying on objects a silane primer and thenA layer of a crosslinkable film-forming material comprising a spreading agent, selected from nonionic fluoroalkyl esters, epoxidized fatty acid derivatives, silicone oils and mixtures, and then crosslinking this layer of film-forming material.
