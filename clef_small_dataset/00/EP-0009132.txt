<ABSTRACT>
the invention relates to a method for the preparation of solidified bands of wadding for the production of tampons from synthetic fibres, in which method the synthetic fibres have added to them, generally in quantities of 1 to 50% by weight relative to the mixture, substances which become tacky under the influence of temperature and, in the course of the production of the bands, adhesion is brought about.
