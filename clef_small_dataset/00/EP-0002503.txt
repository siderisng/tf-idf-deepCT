<ABSTRACT>
Silica dioxide is in a plasma from a mixture of carbon tetrafluoride in an amount of about 5 to 15 vol.% and oxygen with about 5 times higher etching rate etched as a silicon so that it is possible to etch silica layers on a silicon base without the latter being excessed excessively.Since silica is etched compared to silicon nitride with about 3 times higher etching rate, the latter material can be used in the process as an etching mask.
