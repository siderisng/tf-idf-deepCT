<ABSTRACT>
The invention is an improved precipitating method for producing coating powders based on polyamides having at least 10 aliphatically bonded carbon atoms per carbonamide group and or copolyamides having at least 70% of these blocks, wherein the following combination of measures is applied: 1) preparing a solution at 130 to 150 ° C from the polyamides with ηrel 1.4 to 1.8 and at least twice weight amount of ethanol under mechanical movement. 2) Adjusting the precipitation temperature of 100 to 125 ° C under avoid local subcooling. 3) Stirring without further heat supply under the inert gas atmosphere with slower or high stirring speed depending on the desired particle size distribution to the termination of particle formation. 4) cooling the suspension to at least 70 ° C. 5) Dry removal of the ethanol, initially under reduced pressure at wall temperatures up to a maximum of 100 ° C and mild mechanical movement and then after starting flowability at wall temperatures up to 150 ° C under stronger mechanical movement.
