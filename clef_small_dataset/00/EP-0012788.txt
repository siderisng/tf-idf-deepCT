<TITLE>
ultrasonic method and apparatus for measuring the density of liquid.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>
terumo corpjp<sep>terumo corporation<sep>terumo corporation44-1, 2-chome, hatagaya shibuya-kutokyo 151jp<sep>terumo corporation  <sep>
</APPLICANT>
<INVENTOR>
ishizaka hideo<sep>ishizaka hideo<sep>ishizaka hideo3378-25, mannoharashinden fujinomiya-shishizuokajp<sep>ishizaka hideo<sep>ishizaka hideo3378-25, mannoharashinden fujinomiya-shishizuokajp<sep>
</INVENTOR>
<ABSTRACT>
ultrasonic waves are applied to a test liquid (12) of an  unknown density, and the acoustic parameters of the liquid  are measured.  the difference is then detected between the  acoustic parameters of the test liquid and those of a standard  liquid of the same temperature.  the difference thus detected  is added to the known density of the standard liquid, thereby  obtaining the density of the test liquid (12).  
</ABSTRACT>
