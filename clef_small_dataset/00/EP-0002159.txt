<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for polymerizing and anionic copolymerization of vinyl, dienic and heterocyclic monomers.According to the invention, the method comprises performing the reaction in the presence of an initiator consisting of a molecular association of an alkaline amide with the salt of the same alkali metal of at least one hydroxyl compound and in the absence of solvent.The initiator can be prepared by intimate mixing of its constituents or in the presence of a low polar solvent which is then evaporated or in situ in the monomer.application to the synthesis of special or large diffusion polymers and copolymers.
