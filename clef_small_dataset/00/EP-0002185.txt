<ABSTRACT>
In a method for producing a connection between two crossing conductors extending on the surface of a substrate conductor trains on the conductor trains (6 ') of the first metallization plane is formed as an etching system layer (8') of chromium.On this structure, an insulating layer (12 ') with connection holes is applied to the contact points and a metal layer in the second metallization plane.In forming the conductor trains (20 ') by reactive plasma etching, the chromium layer (8') prevents overrides of the conductor trains (6 ') of the first metallization plane.The chromium layer (8 ') at the same time serves as a diffusion barrier for silicon atoms attached to the metal of the first metallization plane.
