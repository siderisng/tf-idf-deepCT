<ABSTRACT>
Continuous process for the manufacture of monochloracetyl chloride, if necessary, accompanied by monochloracetic acid, from trichlorethylene and / or tetrachloro-1,1,1,2 ethane.It is characterized by the fact that a hydration reaction of trichlorethylene and / or tetrachloro-1,1,1,1.2 is carried out under pressure of hydrogen chloride in the liquid phase, in the presence of ferric chloride partially insuspension.Application of said process to obtain monochloracetyl chloride with very good selectivity.
