<ABSTRACT>
The coil holder for automatic coil change, in particular a turning coil holder, a pivotable catch and cutting blade (15 ') is associated with at least two axially pushed sleeves (16; 16') between them. The fishing and cutting blade (15 ') is arranged at the end of the inside of the coil holder (2) about the pin (18) pivotally mounted lever (17). At the other end of the lever (17) is the leaf spring (22). When changing coil change, the catching and cutting blade (15 ') can be pressed into the coil holder (2) that the sleeve (16') can be deported with the full coil without disability from the coil holder (2). To facilitate the pushing in pushing, the compression spring (19) is arranged and the knife holder (23) with a bevelled edge (27) provided. In the wall of the coil holder (2) for the catching and cutting knives (15 ') and for the leaf spring (22) corresponding openings (24; 25) are present. The upper and lower position of the lever (17) is limited by one stop screw (20; 21).
