<ABSTRACT>
In a converter with a detachable bottom (1), which is fixed to the converter jacket (4) or connected to a support structure (5) arranged by a number about its circumference, soil-shaped clamping lever (2), respectively, are the clamping levers (2) From the or in the engagement position with the floor (1) slidable and the end of the converter jacket (4) facing ends of the clamping levers (2) against the converter jacket (4) or against the support structure (5) by clamping means (3) supported.In order to carry out a longitudinal shift of the clamping lever (2) with little effort, a guide element, in particular a guide pin (9) is provided on the clamping means (3), to which the clamping means (3) from the vertical tension position along a curved guide track (10, 10 ') is led to a release position.
