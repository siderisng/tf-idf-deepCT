<ABSTRACT>
Continuous centrifugal filtration device for separation, washing or exhausting, characterized in that the rotating cylindrical wall consists of a helicoidal winding with joined turns with a long lanyard (1) mounted in a rotary cage consisting of cylindrical rollers (5) Arranged according to external generators of the cylindrical wall and driven in rotation at low speed all in the same direction by a suitable mechanism, the lanyard being curled in an endless set thanks to a return turne (3) which extends.One end to the other of said helical winding, thanks to which the cylindrical porous wall leads slowly in the direction of the axis constantly renewing itself.Application: Automatic coffee machine.
