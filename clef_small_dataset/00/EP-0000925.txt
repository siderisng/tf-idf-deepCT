<ABSTRACT>
the preparation of 2,2-dimethyl-3-alkenylcyclopropanecarboxylic acid or its esters is made possible by reacting 3,3-dimethylcyclopropene with alkenylmagnesium halide and/or dialkenylmagnesium compounds, reacting the resulting addition products with carbon dioxide or a chloroformic acid ester and hydrolysing the reaction product obtained.
