<ABSTRACT>
Largable buoy of an aircraft in the water, comprising a balloon 1 which swells during the fall by a forced air input by adjustable openings 16 at the base of the balloon.Inside the balloon is a flexible membrane, leaving air through the holes 13, the remaining balloon inflated at the maximum pressure encountered during the fall.A pocket system 6 having openings 7 and 8 are traversed by the air and prevent the rotation of the fall balloon.Application to sound radio buoys destined to monitor seabed.
