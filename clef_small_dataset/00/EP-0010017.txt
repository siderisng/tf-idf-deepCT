<ABSTRACT>
A disc comprises a layer (2 and 3) of a recording material, writable and legible optically, deposited on a transparent plate (1) traversed by the recording or reading beam, said layer being protected from the outsideBy a hood (5) formed, for example, from a metal sheet and attached to two surfaces (8 and 9) on the layer so as to spare a space (4).
