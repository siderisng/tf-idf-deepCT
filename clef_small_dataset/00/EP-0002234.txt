<ABSTRACT>
New prostacycline derivatives of the formula I (in the R₁ group OR₃ with R₃ as H, optionally substituted alkyl, cycloalkyl, aryl, heterocycle or the group NHR₄ with R₄ as acid group, B C₁₋₁₀ aliphatic alkylene radicals, a the groups -CH₂-CH₂, CIS or transd = CH- or -C≡C-, W optionally functionally modified -Choh or -C (CH₃) OH group, D and E together a direct bond or D a C₁₋₅ alkyleneand e oxygen, sulfur or direct binding, R₂ is an optionally substituted aliphatic hydrocarbon radical, cycloalkyl, aryl, heterocycle and R₅, optionally functionally modified OH group and when R₁ is OH, there are corresponding salts with bases) and the representation of such compounds.The prostacycline derivatives and the associated salts have a hypotensive effect and should be used as pharmaceuticals with appropriate carriers.
