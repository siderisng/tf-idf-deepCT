<TITLE>
articulated arm with revolving tray.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

<ABSTRACT>
the articulated arm with revolving tray and bracket for wall attachment consists of the bracket 1 with a holder for the revolving arm and joint.  a two-part arm 2+3 with bearing 5+6 can be bent in the centre and rotated at the same time by 180 deg  in the bracket.  mounted at the front of the arm is a revolving tray 4 which can be revolved right round.  instead of the tray, a tilting holder can also be attached.  the tray or the holder serves for receiving a television which can be moved into any desired position by the articulated arm.  <image>
