<TITLE>
combined linearity and side pincushion correction arrangement.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>
rca corp us<sep>rca corporation<sep>rca corporation30 rockefeller plazanew york, ny 10020us<sep>rca corporation  <sep>

<ABSTRACT>
a linearity inductance (23) and a side pincushion correc­ tion inductance (25) are wound on outer leg core portions  (21a, 21b) of a single e-shaped core.  a magnet (22) biases the  linearity inductance core portion (21a) for saturating that por­ tion each horizontal deflection cycle for producing a linearity  corrected horizontal deflection current.  a high reluctance path  for flux mutually linking both the linearity and side pincushion  correction windings is provided by the e-shaped core config­ uration.  the portion of the flux generated by the side pincush­ ion correction winding (25) that does flow in the linearity  winding core portion (21a) compensates for undesirable ver­ tical rate changes in the magnetic bias of the linearity winding  core portion (21a).  
</ABSTRACT>
