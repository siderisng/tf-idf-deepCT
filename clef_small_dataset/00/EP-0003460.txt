<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to the preparation of the tris (hydroxymethyl) aminomethane in crystalline form by formylation and then hyrogenation.Nitromethane and formaldehyde are reacted in a molar ratio of the Order of 1/3 in concentrated alcoholic medium consisting of a mixture of 2 to 5% methylene chloride in methanol, in the presence of 1 to 10 milliequivalents bymole of nitromethane of a solid basic catalyst selected from soda or potash, with stirring for a duration of between 3 and 36 hours at less than 50 ° C.The tris (hydroxymethyl) aminomethane is an intermediate of organic synthesis.
