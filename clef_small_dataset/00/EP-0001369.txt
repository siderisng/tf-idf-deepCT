continuously operating sludge expelling press and energy producing plant using it.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
a continuously-operating draining press for sludge containing combustible industrial waste, and energy producing plant in which the said press is integrated.  the press is characterised by the combination:   a) of a drum 7, containing cylinders 27 in which pistons 40 are displaced alternately,    b) of a sludge distributor 4,   c) of a fixed, hemi-cylindrical shield 57, which is concentric with the drum and covers the latter and the openings of the cylinders from the sludge distributor 4 up to a discharge station 58,    d) of a means 26 for the alternate displacement of the pistons,   e) of a conveyor 8 for cakes or pellets 9 of drained sludge out of the press.  application to the recovery of combustible sludge in the manufacture of paper pulp especially.  <image>
