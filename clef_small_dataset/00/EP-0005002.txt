<TITLE>
a mixing tray assembly.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
a tray assembly (10) is provided for use in mixing at  least one ingredient with a liquid to form a product having a  predetermined moisture content and then maintaining the  said moisture content while the product is disposed within  the tray assembly.  the tray assembly (10) includes a  receptacle having a surface on which the product is mixed  and supported thereby.  subtending in spaced relation the receptacle is a reservoir  (16) in which is disposed a predetermined amount of  liquid (l).  one end of an elongated wick (20) is immersed  within the liquid accumulated in the reservoir and the  opposite end of the wick is disposed in the receptacle (11)  and in contact with the product therein.
