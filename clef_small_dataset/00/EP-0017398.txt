<ABSTRACT>
a demolition agent which acts by means of expansive  stress generated upon hydration and which is used as a  slurry filled into holes of an object to be demolished is  obtained by mixing a calcareous material, a siliceous  material and gypsum in the molar ratio for cao/sio₂ of 5. 0 -  14. 4 and for caso₄/sio₂ = 0. 13 - 1. 19, roasting the mixture  under an oxidizing atmosphere to 1350-1500°c and com­ minuting the calcined material.  the main components of the  matter thus obtained are 24 - 65% by weight of 3cao. sio₂  crystals, 30 to 60% by weight of cao crystalline particles and  5 - 17% by weight of caso₄, more than 50% by weight of the  cao crystalline particles being included in the 3cao. sio₂  crystals.  
</ABSTRACT>
