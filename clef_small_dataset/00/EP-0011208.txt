<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for producing 3-phenoxy-benzaldehydes from 3-phenoxy-benzalcohols in which 3-phenoxy-benzene cohols are oxidized in the presence of aqueous sulfuric acid in the temperature range of 50 to 125 ° C with an aqueous solution of a dichromate.
