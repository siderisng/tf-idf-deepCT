<ABSTRACT>
a limit dextrin in the presence of an amplifying exocarbohydrase  is used as a substrate for alpha-amylase which  initiates a series of enzymatic reactions resulting in a  chromogen response which can be used to measure the  concentration of alpha-amylase in a body fluid.  a novel limit  dextrin and its preparation also are described.
