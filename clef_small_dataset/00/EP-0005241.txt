<ABSTRACT>
Water-soluble, non-self-crosslinking quaternary dialkylammonium groups-containing polyalkylene polyamines, which by reacting polyalkylene polyamines of the general formula H₂N- [CH₂-H- (CH₂) Y-NH] XH in the R hydrogen or methyl, Y equal or different the number 0 or 1 and XA number of 2 to 15, and bifunctional compounds of the general formula in the Z are a number of 1 to 6, R₁, R₂ low alkyl or lower hydroxyalkyl are obtained, are suitable as a tool for papermaking.
