<ABSTRACT>
An aqueous hemolysing solution which allows for mixing with blood whose glucose content to determine, according to dissolved constituents essentially a buffer (eg phosphate buffer pH 7.6), sodium chloride, at least one chelating agent (eg EDTA) and at least one SID (eg polyoxyethylene-10-alkylphenol ether) and expediently still an alkaliazide (eg sodium azide).Such a hemolysing solution is virtually unlimited bar;Glucose concentration in the blood hydrolyzed is still unchanged after twelve days.
