<ABSTRACT>
The mast is composed of mast tube elements (17) lifted in succession by means of a lifting device accommodated in a tripod head and connected to the next masculine element after reaching a certain height in the tripod head.The lifting device consists of three superimposed pawls, by friction self-locking pawls: support pawl (28), cinnamon (29) and lifting pawl (30), which in conjunction with springs (38,39,57,58) a slut lifting, lowering and locking theAllow mast tube elements.A caught lever (61) with a touch nose (62) ensures that the operator becomes aware of a faulty connection of the mast tube elements and that a mast tube element in the area of the lifting device can only be pushed out of the tripod head after proper attachment of the following mast tube element.
