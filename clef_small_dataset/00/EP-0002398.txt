<ABSTRACT>
the cells have instrument transformers 34, isolators 32, a cutout device 33, the cells being inside a prismatic space which can be opened on one side.  the cells are characterised in that function isolating units   1) consisting of partitions impermeable to solid, liquid or gaseous physical bodies or to electrical arcs from uninsulated live parts   2) each one including, embedded in an insulating material, the various components belonging to the current distribution cells   3) having on the front, accessible portion or "facade", control and isolating levers, can be inserted perpendicularly to the axis of the prismatic space.  application to high-voltage power supply.  <image>
