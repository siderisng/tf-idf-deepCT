<TITLE>
additive for a moisture pervious coating based on cement, and its application.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
An additive for cement-based dehumidation plutz, consisting of surfactants a) 15 to 50 wt.% of anionic Na salvation of alkylnaphthalene sulfonic acids, b) 25 to 75 wt.% of Na salts of alkylphenol ether sulfate and / or alkylbenzenesulfonate or Na salts of sulfated C₁₂-C₁₅ oxoalcohol polyglycol ether and C) 10 to 40 wt.% on a styrene mixing polymer having an acid number of 5 to 15, as is suitable for the preparation of aqueous plastic dispersions, and optionally D) 7 to 15 wt.% of glue-free highly concentrated paraffe dispersion and E) 7 to 15 wt.% of sodium salts of a naphthalene sulfonic acid formaldehyde condensation product.The additive can also be used for heat-insulating dehumidification plasters containing next to the cement sand and the additive still thermal insulating small piece material.
</ABSTRACT>
