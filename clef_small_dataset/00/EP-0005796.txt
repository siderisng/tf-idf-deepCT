<ABSTRACT>
the fibre extrusion (1) to be impregnated is drawn in a resin bath (3) at a speed of at least 2 m/min through at least one conically tapering die (4, 5, 6) and thereafter through a further shaping die (7).  directed glass-fibre extrusions with a glass fraction of more than 70 per cent by volume can be impregnated completely by connecting a plurality of dies in series.  the end of the die channel decreasing in cross-section in the filament running direction will not be bevelled or rounded on the exit side.  <image>
