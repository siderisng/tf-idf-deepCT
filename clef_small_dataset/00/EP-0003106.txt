<ABSTRACT>
In the production of Furanderivaten by transplexed zone β-dicarbonylene and α-chlorocarbonyl compounds, either only small yields have been achieved or an improvement in yields could only be achieved by using at least equimolar amounts of pyridine.However, through this large pyridine additive, the work-up of the reaction mixture was complicated, which complicates wastewater treatment by pyridine volumes.In the invention, stoichiometric amounts of alkaline earth carbonates, possibly at most 20 mol% of a monohetero cyclic monostick fabric compound, are, for.b.Pyridine, used as acid binder in the reaction.Through the inventive procedure, both the yields can be significantly improved as well as the use of pyridine completely or at least significantly avoided.
