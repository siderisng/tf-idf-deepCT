<ABSTRACT>
apparatus (20) for sag bending and tempering sheet  glass includes a vacuum holder (92) that is positioned within  a furnace heating chamber (50) above a conveyor (66).  the  vacuum holder has a downwardly facing surface (94) with  spaced openings (106, 108) in which a vacuum is drawn to  receive a glass sheet from the conveyor and support the  sheet above the conveyor.  the holder surface is disclosed as  being both planar and curved and as having a porous cover  (104) of ceramic fibers so as to distribute the vacuum and  prevent marring of the glass as it supported.  a carrier mold  is moved under the supported glass and the vacuum is then  terminated so the glass drops onto the ring and sag bends  under the bias of gravity in a manner that allows thin glass to  be accurately bent.  subsequently an actuator moves the  carrier mold to a quench unit to temper the bent glass.  
</ABSTRACT>
