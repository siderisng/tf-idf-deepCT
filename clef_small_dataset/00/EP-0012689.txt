The invention relates to a non-adherent electrode piezoelectric resonator of the drawer type.The electrodes 8, 9 are deposited on the windows of an opening 31 formed in a dielectric unit 3 supported by holding means 30. A piezoelectric stital cry 10, of the type comprising a support peripheral portion and a central part.active isolated from the peripheral portion by an intermediate zone, is inserted into the opening 31 in the way of a drawer and can be positioned precisely and predetermined with respect to the two electrodes 8, 9.
