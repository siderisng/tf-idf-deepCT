<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a pressure release device with quick release for the digestion of biomaterials.In a fixed pressure opening vessel (1) according to the invention, the closure is formed as a slightly movable lace car with a lower pressure receiving plate (4), an upper pressure receiving plate (5) and a fastener nut (8).The pressure receiving plates (4 and 5) are connected by strain screws (7) and spacer sleeves (6).The lock nut (8) can have a thread with a large slope and be large.A pressure-tight closure is achieved, which allows for a quick and easy movement of the closure cap as well as open and closing the printing optical device.
