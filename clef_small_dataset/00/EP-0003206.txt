<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a process for the preparation of ethyl or isopropyl esters by liquid reaction of the corresponding carboxylic acid olefin.The method includes the esterification of ethylene or propylene, in the liquid phase, in the presence of at least one catalyst of formula: CN F2N + 1 SO₃H in which n is an integer that can be zero, and being at mostEqual to 8. The process is suitable for the manufacture of isopropyl acetate, monochloroacetate and ethyl monochloropropionate.
