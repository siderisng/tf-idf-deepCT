<ABSTRACT>
Process for obtaining alkylaster copolyesters, characterized by the fact that it is reacted successively at temperature less than 250 ° C a dihydroxyaromatic compound and an aliphatic diacid of general formula Hoo (CH₂) NCOOH with 3 ≦n ≦ 10 with a molar ratio of the diaster to the diacid between 1.5 and4 up to a rate of progress of the reaction greater than or equal to 85%, then, at greater temperature than 250 ° C, a dicarboxylic acid that aromatic and/ or cycloaliphatic and the dense polyconne the product obtained under reduced pressure for a period of less than or equal to 1 h 30. This method allows the obtaining of copolyesters that can be transformed into wire, films, molded objects..
