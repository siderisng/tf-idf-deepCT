<ABSTRACT>
1.  double baking oven, especially built-in oven with two superimposed baking ovens (11, 16) enclosed by a common housing, one of said baking ovens (16) being a micro-wave baking oven, with switching, regulating and servicing means (34) for both baking ovens (11, 16) being accommodated in the upper part of the housing and cooling air ducts (43, 44, 45) being provided in the interior of the housing with inlet openings (41) at the lower side of the oven outlet openings (36) at the upper side of the oven, characterized in that the lower baking oven (11) is screened thermally against the upper oven (16) and against said cooling air ducts (43, 44, 45) by means of shielding plates (9, 42) and in that said inlet openings (41) for the cooling air are provided beneath said shielding plate (9) forming the lower limit of the lower baking oven (11).
