<ABSTRACT>
modelling apparatus for poultry provided with pivotably  interconnected shell members (52, 54) and flaps (58) pivotably  mounted for catching the wings which flaps can be  moved from an open or spread-out position towards a  position in which their end edges nearly engage each other,  so that the wings are guide and laid against the body of the  poultry before the shell members of the modelling apparatus  close.
