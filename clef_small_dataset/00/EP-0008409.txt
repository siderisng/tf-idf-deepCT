<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to metal-containing soluble polymeric catalysts for polymerizations, with an organic base polymer containing metals, metal ions or metal complexes bound via functional groups.The poly Meren catalysts are suitable for polymerization in a homogeneous solution under mild conditions.The invention also relates to a method for producing the polymeric catalysts, as well as their use for polymerization in a homogeneous solution.
