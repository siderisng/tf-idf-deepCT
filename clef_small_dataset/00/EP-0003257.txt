<ABSTRACT>
the char-forming characteristics of aromatic polymers  containing one or more combustion resistant components  are improved by incorporating therein a polybenzyl, a  polyallyl, or an allyl/benzyl moiety.  as an example, a styrene  polymer comprising a halogenated comonomer such as  bromostryene is rendered char-forming by the presence of  copolymerized vinylbenzyl chloride in the styrene polymer.   such polymeric compositions are useful in the manufacture  of shaped articles such as housings for electrical applicances.
