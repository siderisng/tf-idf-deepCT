Paper caskets based on 100 parts by weight of a finely divided pigment, 5 to 25 parts by weight of at least one polymer with a glass transition temperature of -40 to + 50 Â° C in the form of aqueous dispersions and 0.1 to 10 parts by weight of at least one water-soluble or water-swellable cobinder based onPolyvinylalcohol, modified cellulose, starch, casein, alginates or high molecular weight carboxyl-containing emulsic ion polymers contained, wherein the cobinder converts through emulsifying in a hydrocarbon oil into a water-in-oil emulsion and incorporated in this form into the paper coating compositions.The paper stress masses are characterized by high water retention and good processability and give papers, which have a significantly higher water resistance than with known papers of painted papers.
