<ABSTRACT>
stacks of metal sheets are pressed, heated to 120 deg  to 130 deg c and a cold curing epoxy resin is subsequently poured over them at room temperature.  in this way, the epoxy resin becomes liquid and penetrates into the narrowest gaps and cracks between the metal sheets.  once the resin has cured, the stack of metal sheets can be removed from the pressing device.  use in electrical machines and transformer construction.
