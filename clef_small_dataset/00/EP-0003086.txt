<ABSTRACT>
A method of attenuating the linearity errors of a ring laser gyrometer with n mirrors (28, 29, 30) arranged so that the laser wave beams travel in the opposite direction two identical polygons, characterized in that itconsists of oscillating angularly, relative to the body of the gyrometer, at least one of the mirrors defining the trajectories of the two laser beams around an axis perpendicular to the plane defined by said beams;The axis of rotation of each of the oscillating mirrors being preferably as close as possible to the perpendicular to the mirror considered passing through the contact point of the laser beams with said mirror.Fields of Applications: Air and Maritime Navigations.
