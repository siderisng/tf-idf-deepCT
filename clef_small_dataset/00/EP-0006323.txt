<ABSTRACT>
the invention provides shaped articles that are contact  lenses or are bonnets from which such lenses may be made.   the lenses have high gas permeability.  they are formed  of a polymer that is obtained by polymerising at least one  styrenetype monomer, optionally with at least one cross-­ linking monomer and optionally with other mono-olefin­ ically unsaturated monomer.  preferably the polymerisable  monomers comprise, or consist of, a styrene substituted  in the ring by at least one alkyl group.
