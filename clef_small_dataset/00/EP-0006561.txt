<ABSTRACT>
A positively operating photosensitive mixture is described for the production of printing plates, particularly flat printing plates, and photoresists containing an alkali-soluble binder, preferably a novolak, and an O-naphthoquinondiazide of the formula wherein R₁, R₁ ', R₂ are the same or different and hydrogen, and R₂ 'chloro or bromine atoms, alkyl, alkoxy or alkoxyalkyl radicals having 1 to 6 carbon atoms or alkenyl radicals having 2 to 6 carbon atoms, R₃ is a hydrogen atom or an alkyl radical having 1 to 4 carbon atoms, R₄ a hydrogen atom, one if necessary, substituted alkyl radical, whose carbon chain may be interrupted by ether oxygen atoms, a possibly substituted cycloalkyl group having 5 to 16 carbon atoms or a possibly substituted alkenyl radical having 2 to 16 carbon atoms, n zero or an integer of 1 to 4 and d 1,2-naphthoquinone-2-diazide-sulfonyl radical mean. The new naphthoquinondiazides are characterized by high solubility in organic solvents and good reprographic properties.
