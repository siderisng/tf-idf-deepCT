<ABSTRACT>
the invention provides a novel polycyclic ether antibiotic  produced by the micro-organism streptomyces hygroscopicus  atcc 31337.  the novel antibiotic is designated compound  47,224 and is characterised by solubility, melting point,  optical rotation, elemental analysis and ultra-violet and infrared  absorption spectra.  it is described as being useful for  controlling coccidiosis in poultry by administration in the diet,  and for improving feed utilisation in ruminants and monogastric  animals.
