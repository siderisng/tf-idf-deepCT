<ABSTRACT>
anodes for electrochemical purposes are composed of an electrically conducting support body and a layer of metallic silicon and/or germanium applied thereto.  the layer is doped with one or more metals of main group iii of the periodic system and also one or more metals of subgroup viii of the periodic system.  the anodes are suitable, in particular, as chlorine liberation anodes.
