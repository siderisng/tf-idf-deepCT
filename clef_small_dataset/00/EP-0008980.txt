<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a fiber fiber connector plug with connecting element to an optical cable.A flat connector with two parallel sheet fibers (18) easily connects to a round multi-grade optical cable (12) thanks to the fact that it comprises a separable connection element (R) and provided with guiding means (G5, G6)Compared to the rest of the connector (C), this element comprising a splitter (100, 101, 102) forming two distribution channels which gradually lead the entire fiber (18) coming out of the cable to the disposition into two tablecloths.Application to telecommunications.
