<ABSTRACT>
Modular modular element for hydraulic flow splitter comprising a piston flow controller adjusting the opening according to the variations of the upstream pressure between the inlet and the output of the fluid.The cylinder of the regulatory piston (11, 111, 211) leads, on the one hand, on a stacking face of the element and, on the other hand in the outlet hole (5, 105, 205) of theelement crossing its inlet hole (3, 103, 203) and communicates, by said stacking face, with an output pressures equalization hole (12, 112, 212), through the element between its facesstacking and connecting to these to the corresponding holes of the neighboring blocks, said equalization hole communicating, moreover, with the output hole (5, 105, 205) of the element.Applications with hydraulic fluid power supplies from a common source.
</ABSTRACT>
