<ABSTRACT>
in a device for the exact positioning of a second workpiece (2) at a predetermined distance (x) from a first workpiece (1) lying in a fixed position, the second workpiece (2), for the purpose of simplifying and improving the reproducibility, is first of all brought up against a stop at the first workpiece (1) via transport means (5) and then lifted to the desired distance (x).  the device is preferably used in automated welding processes in which two workpieces are to be welded to one another.  <image>
