<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to new 1-ethene-azole derivatives, a method for their preparation and their use as fungicides.The compounds of the general formula in which Y is nitrogen atom or the CH group and R¹ and R² have the designated in the description are obtained when reacting 1-halogen ethene derivatives with alkali salts of azoles in the presence of a diluent.As plant protection products, the ingredients according to the invention can be used with particularly good success for combating such mushrooms, which cause real mildew disorders, according to the control of erysiph species.
