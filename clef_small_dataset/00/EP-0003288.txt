<ABSTRACT>
a modular encoder with a dimensionally stable housing  (35) which can be easily aligned for mounting on a motor  housing.  the encoder utilized a monolithic, in-line, sensor  array (85) and a single light source (38) to achieve improved  long-term performance and includes improved alignment  means permitting a user to readily align the encoder with a  motor.  pre-alignment (figure 8) of the mask (64) and sensor  assembly simplifies the alignment performed by the user.  the alignment means include mounting holes (70, 71)  with diameters being greater than the diameter of the screws  and cutaway portions in the housing.
