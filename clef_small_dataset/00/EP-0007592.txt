limit switch device for a lifting apparatus.

<ABSTRACT>
lifting appliance having a limit-switch device, in which a nut (3) sits on a screw spindle (2) of a motor (1), the lifting member (8) being connected to the nut (3) via the sleeve (32).  the nut (3) is prevented from rotating with the spindle (2) by the helical spring (4), which spring clamping operates as long as the ends (40, 400) of the spring (4) are blocked in a longitudinal slot (50).  at either end, the appliance has one electric disconnecting switch (6) each as limit switch, which is actuated by the control rail (46) of the nut (3).  at the same time, one end (400) of the spring (4) passes into the recess (74), as a result of which the clamping action of the spring (4) relative to the nut (3) is loosened.  switch (6) and recess (74) are an integral part of a displaceable and adjustable guide plate (7) and enable the end position of the lifting appliance to be adjusted.  immediate interruption of the lifting movement is ensured by simultaneous electrical and mechanical tripping of the holding action, and no increase in force results in the appliance despite the momentum of the parts and the slowing down of the motor (1).  <image>
</ABSTRACT>
