<ABSTRACT>
the welding apparatus comprises a mains transformer (ntr), a rectifier (gr) and a function selector switch (a) and is provided with welding-current terminals (b) for a plurality of welding torches.  on account of terminals for an mig/mag welding torch, a tig welding torch and a stick-electrode welding torch and a function selector switch (a) having three switch positions, the welding apparatus can be used for mig/mag welding, tig welding and stick-electrode welding, in which arrangement the polarity and current-voltage characteristic of the welding voltage are automatically adapted to the type of welding selected by internal change-overs, and only the terminal which is allocated to the type of welding selected is activated.  <image>
