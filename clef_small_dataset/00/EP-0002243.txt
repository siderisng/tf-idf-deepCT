<ABSTRACT>
1.  solid phosphorous acid ester preparations having an improved stabilizing effect in polyolefin plastic materials, comprising from 20 to 97 weight % of a known, solid organic phosphorous acid ester stabilizer for a plastic material, and form 80 to 3 weight % of a wax selected from the group consisting of natural or partially synthetic ester waxes, amide waxes, esters of polyolefin wax oxidates and wax-like esters of long chain phosphorous acids.
