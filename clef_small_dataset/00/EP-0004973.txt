<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a molding composition of a mixture of a polyoxymethylene, an elastomer having a softening temperature below the crystalline melting point of the polyoxymethylene and with a freeze temperature of -120 ° C to + 30 ° C and a segmented, thermoplastic copolyester or a polyurethane.The molding composition according to the invention is characterized by improved mechanical properties, in particular with regard to the impact strength.
