<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a spinning or twisting machine with a plurality of upright or arranged textile spindles (11), wherein the spindle shaft (14) of each textile spindle is firmly connected to the rotor shaft of the rotor of an electric motor (19) driven by it, wherein the two bearing shield of the(21, 22) having housing of this electric motor is arranged on a spindle bench (10) and for the purpose of simple and accurate centering, the two bearing signs (21, 22) of the electric motor housing on the spindle bench are arranged independently of each other radially positionally adjustable.
