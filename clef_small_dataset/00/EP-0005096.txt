<ABSTRACT>
The solid state millimeter wave source uses avalanche diodes.Next to the polarization electrode (12) of a structure with a "pre-accredited module" type (10, 11, 12) is disposed, the polarization electrode (22) of a similar module (20, 21, 22) comprising a variable capacity diode.The source is modulated in frequency by varying the polarization voltage (V₂) of the diode with variable capacity.The source radiates in a waveguide.application to microwave transmitters.
