<ABSTRACT>
a device for raising and transport of goods.  the present  invention relates to a device for raising and transport of  goods in the form of sacs, wood boxes, cartons, cargo flats,  etc.  said device comprises a wheel carried frame (1) with  a handle (7).  by the present invention the design of such a device  is simplified and thereby the manufacturing costs and maintenance  costs are reduced and at the same time the reliability  is increased.  the present invention is characterized by a lever (9)  (10) means in the wheel carried frame (1) to insertable  under the goods for raising of the goods and to be swingable  during movement of the wheel carried frame in under  the goods to a transport position, in which the lever means  is directed in a direction opposite the direction in which  the lever means is inserted under the goods, and in which  position the goods rests on the frame.  
</ABSTRACT>
