<ABSTRACT>
a process is provided for producing alkenols and  alkanediols which comprises effecting selective reaction bet­ ween formaldehyde and an ethylanically unsaturated com­ pound, e. g. , isobutene, at an elevated temperature in the  presence of a catalyst comprising a crystalline luminosilicate  zeolite characterized by a silica to alumina mole ratio of at  least about 12 and a constraint index, as hereinafter defined,  within the approximate range of 1 to 12.
