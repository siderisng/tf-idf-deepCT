<ABSTRACT>
Method for transmitting information between devices (SP1, EP2) an indirectly controlled resistance system via time division multiplex channels (K11, K12), each with multiple repetition of the information.The information is each consisting of several bytes (S11, Q11, N11, (11th, W11) to which a Numerie Reenden the respective information is heard. The information is provided by the help of buffer memory (B11., B1x, B21.,, B2X) and multiplexers (M1, M2). The information also includes receipt bytes, which acknowledge the correct reception of previously transmitted information. The reception of acknowledgment bytes is also evaluated as a signal for the previously for the transmission of theacknowledged informa tion used time division multiplex channel for the transmission of further information has become available.
