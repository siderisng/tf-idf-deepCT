<ABSTRACT>
This method makes it possible to obtain, by self - alignment techniques using deposition and stripping steps, MESFET transistors in which the source (13) and drain (15) regions are located at low distances.(a, b) of the door region (14).The distances (a, b) are defined by the thickness of an insulating layer (20) deposited during the manufacture of such transistors.Transistors obtained by such a method have a very low parasitic resistance between the source and the drain.
