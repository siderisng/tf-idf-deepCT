<ABSTRACT>
This clamping device comprises a fixed jaw and a movable jaw sliding on the body of the fixed jaw;In addition, it comprises a structure for collecting the reactions of the stresses exerted on the fixed and movable jaws, this struct having for this purpose a front part against which the fixed jaw is supported and a rear portion pierced by a threaded orifice, tothrough which passes, and in which is in socket, the thread of the mounted jaw clamping screw;The front part and the rear portion of this structure are each constituted by a flange 4 and 5, and these two flanges are joined to each other, in their central part, by at least one pulling 6 parallel to the screw ofClamp 3, and at their lower part, opposite that where the clamping screw is located, at least one compression bar 7.
