<ABSTRACT>
photographic recording material comprising one or  more blocked photographically useful compounds.  the  blocked compounds are photographic image dyes and  photographic reagents.  these compounds are blocked with  a group which, under alkaline conditions, is cleaved from  the compounds by an intramolecular necleophilic dis­ placement reaction.  the compounds are resistant to  unblocking under storage conditions but are uniformly  unblocked under conditions encountered during photo­ graphic processing.
