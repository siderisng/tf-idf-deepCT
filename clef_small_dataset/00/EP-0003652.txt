<ABSTRACT>
multiple-stage sequentially-produced thermoplastic  polymers comprising four specific polymerization stages  are particularly useful as films for providing durable surface  protective layers to substrates when each of the first  and second stages contain 0. 1 to 1% by weight of graftlinking  monomer.  the films are conveniently produced by  melt extrusion to a thickness of 0. 1 to 50 mils.
