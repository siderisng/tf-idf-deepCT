<ABSTRACT>
The device, which preferably comprises an attachment arranged by the sewing mous chine, has key fields (1 - 5) for inputting sewing conditions such as type of sewing material and sewing operation.Accordingly, the data entered is addressed by places of an electronic memory to which instructions for the setting and equipment of the sewing machine are stored.These instructions are clearly displayed on certain locations on the device or preferably largely on the sewing machine itself, (34 - 43), so that the setting and equipment of the sewing machine can be done without special prior knowledge SE.It is possible to convey unique instructions directly to the points of the sewing machine for a lot of data combinations, where their adjustment and equipment must be carried out.
