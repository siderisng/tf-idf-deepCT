culture process for edible mushrooms.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
a culture process for edible mushrooms by fermenting materials of vegetable origin in the presence of microorganisms and sowing them with the mycelium of edible mushrooms to obtain a mushroom culture, which is characterised in that the mushroom culture is treated with 1 or more of the microorganisms arthrobacter nicotianae, arthrobacter globiformis and arthrobacter simplex of the arthrobacter genus before differentiation.
