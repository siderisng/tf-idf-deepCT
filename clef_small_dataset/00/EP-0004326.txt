<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a developing device for printing plates which contains a tank (7 and 26) with a treatment liquid treatment liquid. The tank (7) is attached to a slide (4) pulled out of the housing (1) of the developing device, on which a circulation pump (8) and optionally further aggregates such as solenoid valve, float switch, thermostatic valve or the like are fixedly attached. Within the tank (7 to 26) filter cartridges (18, 19 and 27, 28) are arranged. The filter cartridges (18, 19) are pressed by means of a locking plate (20) under the pressure of a spring (21) on the bottom (22) of the tank (7). The interior of the tank (7 or 26) is connected via a suction nozzle (12) or a branching piece (29) with the circulation pump (8), which, for example, at a pressure side via a hose (13) with one above a catch tray (15) arranged spray tube (14) is connected. From the deepest point of the drip tray (15), a return hose (16) passes through a lid (10) into the interior of the tank (7).
