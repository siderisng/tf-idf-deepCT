<ABSTRACT>
the invention relates to substituted phenylnitramins with  plant growth regulating properties, and to the use of these  compounds for the control of relative growth of graminaceous  crops, leguminous crops and cotton, especially for reducing  the relative stem growth and increasing stem stiffness.   the plant growth regulating phenylnitramines have the formula:    wherein r₁ is halogen, c₁-c₄ alkyl, cn, co₂ch₃, so₂ch₃, so₂f,  so₂cf₃, c₁-c₃ haloalkyl, c₁-c₃ haloalkoxy or no₂; r₃ is  halogen, methyl or methoxy; r₅ is halogen, cf₃, methoxy or  c₁-c₄ alkyl; with the proviso that not more than two of said r  groups may represent fluorine, or an ammonium salt thereof.
