<ABSTRACT>
The foam glass granules consists of fragments of a foam glass body and has at least 100,000, preferably at least 1'000'000 bubbles of at least approximately equal size.It is obtained by milling the raw materials into a flour, mixing with a blowing agent developing in the heat gas, the mixture blowing in a flatbed furnace into a flat foam glass strand, cooling it after puffy and shredded immediately after cooling to granual.
