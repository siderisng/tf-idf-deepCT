<ABSTRACT>
method and apparatus for coating a thin film upon a  substrate 6 in a vacuum chamber 1 using the sputter deposi­ tion technique on both sides of the substrate.  the substrate 6  is held between two movable rams 7 and is located between  two sputter cathodes 3 equal to or larger in size than the  substrate.  the rams 7 conduct r. f.  power to the substrate and  have a conduit 9 to conduct cooling liquid to the inner ends of  the rams to remove heat from the substrate.  a d. c.  bias is  maintained between the cathodes 3 and the substrate to pro­ duce the required asymmetrical sputter.  an earthed suppres­ sion ring 16 encircles the periphery of the substrate 6.  this  ring is located sufficiently close to the substrate to suppress  plasma glow.  the cathodes 3 of the sputtering system are  magnetically enhanced for concentration of the sputtering  plasma.  
</ABSTRACT>
