<ABSTRACT>
The new 3,4-dioxo-1,2,5-thiadiazolidine-1 oxides of the formula in which AR is optionally substituted aryl and R is alkyl, e.g.b.2- (3,5-dichlorophenyl) -5 -Isopropyl -3,4-dioxo-1,2,5 -thiadiazolidin -1-oxide, are obtained when substituted sulfur diimides of the formula Rn = S = N-AR with oxalyl chloridein the presence of an organic diluent and the first resulting 1.1-dichloro -3.4-dioxo-1,2,5-thiadiazolidines without their isolation in the usual way to correspond to the 3,4-dioxo-1,2, 5-thiadiazolidine-1 oxides hydrolyzed.The active ingredients (I) may be used in the form of crop protection coins with particularly good success to combat botrytis species.
