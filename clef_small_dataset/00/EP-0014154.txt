<ABSTRACT>
the invention relates to a towed implement for picking up and transporting materials, in which a bin 1, having a transverse conveyor 3 and an articulated arm 40 carrying at its end a pivoting movable wall 12 for picking up from a pile and for compacting a material, is mounted at its front on two arms 44 oscillating about an axis 45, and the height of the bin 1 is adjustable by means of jacks 48.  the invention is used for picking up from piles and silos, especially in the agricultural sector.  <image>
