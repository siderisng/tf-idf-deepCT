<ABSTRACT>
an infra-red target detection arrangement in which a  row of eight infra-red detectors (3) are scanned repeatedly  over a section (5) of a field (4) of view to perform eight line  scans simultaneously, the detector outputs being accumulated  in respective stores for each of a number of points in a  scan.  the detectors are then stepped by half of a line space to  provide repeated interlaced scans.  if a target is detected the  arrangement may be switched to a mode in which the field of  view is scanned at a rate compatible with a conventional raster  television display.  
</ABSTRACT>
