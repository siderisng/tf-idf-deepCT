<ABSTRACT>
The ceramic recuperator is preceded by a burner, in particular special an oil burner and serves to achieve combustion air, which is preheated to the burner for generating a ignitable fuel-air mixture.For the cold start, the recuperator has a heating (5,8,10,11) adjustable before entering combustion air, which preheats the combustion air leading channels (2).The heating serves an electrical substance heating (5,8,10,11).Preferably, the walls (4) of the canals (2) to be heated (2) consist of an electrically conducting the, ceramic material.
