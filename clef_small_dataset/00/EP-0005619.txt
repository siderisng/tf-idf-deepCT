<ABSTRACT>
in suspension polymerisation, for example in the production of polymer beads for use in the preparation of ion exchange resins, the formation of monomer suspension using a baffled in-line mixer, most preferably with a specified liquid flow rate, and subsequently polymerising, without bead coalescence, in a stirred polymerisation vessel produces beads of narrow particle size distribution.  preferred beads have an average particle diameter of less than 200 microns and a particle standard deviation of less than 100 microns.
