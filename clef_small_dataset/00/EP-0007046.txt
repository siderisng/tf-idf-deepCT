<ABSTRACT>
Tertiary N-vinylamides can be polymerized, optionally together with further monomers - in the presence of Ali phatic hydrocarbons with the aid of radically effective initiators.The polymerization is usually carried out at a temperature of 0 to 150 ° C.To reduce the residual monomer content, an initiator mixture is used, which is in part in the hydrocarbon insoluble and partially soluble in it.The implementation of the polymerization under an inert gas at mosphere is recommended.The polymer thus obtained, which falls as a powder or in granular form, is suitable as a protective colloid and dispersant for emulsion or suspension polymerizations.
