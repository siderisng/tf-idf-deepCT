<ABSTRACT>
Apparatus for positioning magnetic heads on selected magnetic traces of a magnetic disk in a magnetic disk space in which a magnetic plate axis displaceable in the direction of the magnetic disk axis (1) for a magnetic head (14) and a coil carrier for a thrust drive device are provided, wherein the drive device is made from one in the working air gapa magnetic arrangement projecting planar coil (6) and the magnet arrangement consisting of two flat magnets (7,8), with a coil carrier, which is formed as a displaceable guide part for the head carrier, and the plane coil (6) and the magnet arrangement are arranged so,that the force vector is substantially in the plane in which the guide part is mounted mounted.
