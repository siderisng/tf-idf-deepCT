<ABSTRACT>
The solid particles containing calcium peroxide are coated by means of a coating agent containing an insoluble solid compound having a melting point of at least 50 ° C.They can be manufactured by spraying on calcium peroxide granules in motion a solution, emulsion or suspension of the coating agent in a liquid medium and evaporation of this liquid medium.The brokets can be used for water treatment and ensure sufficient oxygenation for very long periods.
