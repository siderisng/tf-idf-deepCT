<ABSTRACT>
the invention relates to a process for the purification of  benzaldehyde and, in particular, the purification of benzaldehyde  prepared by oxidation of toluene with a gas containing  molecular oxygen.  according to the invention, pure benzaldehyde is prepared  by treating impure benzaldehyde with an oxidizing  agent and distilling it.  benzaldehyde purified according to the invention is particularly  suitable for application in the synthesis of scents and  flavours, because it can meet the demands of olfactory  specifications.
