<ABSTRACT>
It is described a positively operating photosensitive coil material, in particular for the production of flat pressure plates, whose photosensitive layer is an ester or an amide of an O-naphthoquinone diazidsulfone or carboxylic acid, an alkali-soluble, a seru soluble phenolic resin and a condensation product of a hydroxybenzophenone and formaldehydecontains.The material is characterized by increased photosensitive pressure.
