<ABSTRACT>
In a developing device for the development of diazocopic material according to the semi-humidity method, a plane roller (3) with a liquid adsorbing, elastic coating (12) and a worn-off, a grooved hard surface-constructing press roller (2) by a centrally arranged press roll (1) against each otherpressed, with a developer zone being formed between the order roller (3) and the press roller (2).A slat (4) is at the application roller (3).In order to apply uniformly small amounts of developer fluid to the diazocopy material, the equatorial moment of inertia (J₂) of the press roller (2) is dimensioned to the equatorial moment of inertia (J₃) of the application roller (3) according to a particular relationship in which the weight (G₂) of the press roller (2), the weight (G₃) of the application roller (3), the delivery rejection (P₁) of the press roll (1) and the delivery force (P₄) and the friction force of the lamella.
