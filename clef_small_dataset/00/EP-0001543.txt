<TITLE>
casket made entirely of polypropylene for storing small articles.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>
samsonite corpus <sep>samsonite corporation<sep>samsonite corporation 11200 east-forty-fifth avenue denver, colorado 80239 us<sep>samsonite corporation<sep>

<INVENTOR>
castelli renato<sep>castelli, renato<sep>castelli, renatokoning leopold ii laan 53b-9000 gentbe<sep>castelli, renato<sep>castelli, renatokoning leopold ii laan 53b-9000 gentbe<sep>
</INVENTOR>
<ABSTRACT>
1.  a tidy box made entirely of polypropylene suitable for keeping small items, office requisites, pencil, pen, rubber, visiting cards, staples, pocket calculator, small samples and, in some cases, small toilet and dress requisites, said box consisting only of two parts (1, 2), fitted together after moulding, characterized in that one of said parts, the lower one (2), forms the base of the box and the lid (3), the latter being provided on the one hand with a catch or fastening lug (5) on one side and with a pull handle (6) on the other side, and being on the other hand integrally connected to the front base portion (2a) by an hinge (4) ; in that the other part, the upper one (1), forms the side portions (1d), the top (1a), the back (1c) and the front portion (1b) of the box, the latter forming a fixed frame for the lid (3) without any bottom cross-member and having an upper cross-member with a notch (7), retaining edge, and in that the hinge (4) of the lower part (2) is formed by a polypropylene portion of reduced thickness, portion that is outer rounded and the thickness of which is reduced sufficiently to leave a gap (17) between the lower edge (3a) of the lid (3) and the edge of the front portion of the base, such as to permit the lid to drop and so release the catch (5) from the notch (7). 
</ABSTRACT>
