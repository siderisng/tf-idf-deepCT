<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a plasma cutting plant comprising a main generator feeding a plasma arc between a piece 5 and an electrode 2 of a torch 1 to nozzle 3, with pre-binning by an ignition arch between electrode 2 and nozzle 3, via the resistance 14 and the contact 15. According to the invention, it is provided in the mains contact terminals 13 a resistance circuit 16 and winding of an excitation 17 of a relay 18 allowing, when the torch 1 hasApproached at the correct distance of Exhibit 5, detect this correct distance and act on the advance engine 19 to stop it immediately.Application to the plasma cutting automatically.
