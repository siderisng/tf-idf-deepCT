<ABSTRACT>
A process for preparing a polyalcoyl pyrylium salt having at least 3 alkyl remnants in position 2, 4, 6, by reacting a branched alkane comprising at least one tertiary carbon atom with an alkanoyl halide or an alkanic anhydride and an alkanic anhydride andA halide of Alcoyle, in the presence of a Lewis acid, according to the probable scheme.R = identical or different;H, Alcoyle R₁, R₂ = Alcoyle X = halogen, m = al, Fe The pyrylium salts can be used to prepare alkoylpyridines and dialkoylphenols.
