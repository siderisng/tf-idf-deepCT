<ABSTRACT>
A process for the preparation of crosslinked unsaturated polyesters from a mixture containing a linear unsaturated polyester, a copolymerizable monomer, a catalyst, an accelerator, a load, an expansion agent, a hydrolyzable chlorinated derivative and a hydroxyl compound.The polymerization is carried out at room temperature and atmospheric pressure.The proportion of chlorinated derivatives is between 0.1 and 10% by weight relative to the mixture.Application to the manufacture of reliable alveolai materials that can be used as seafrisoner firewalls.
