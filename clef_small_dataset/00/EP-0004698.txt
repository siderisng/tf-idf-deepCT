<ABSTRACT>
a portable toilet having a sealed holding tank (20) and a  vent port (42) to equalise the atmosphere and tank pressures  opening and closing the inlet port (24) of the holding tank.   the holding tank (20) includes a valve assembly (34) for  opening and closing the inlet port of the tank, and the vent  port (42) in the tank has a valve member (40) associated with  the inlet port valve assembly (34) so that when the valve  assembly (34) is moved toward its open position, the vent  port (42) will be opened immediately prior to opening of the  inlet port, and that when the inlet port valve assembly (34) is  moved toward its closed position, the vent port (42) will be  closed immediately after closing of the inlet port (24).
