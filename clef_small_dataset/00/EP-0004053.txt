<TITLE>
binder for the preparation of insulating materials and method for their preparation
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>
cassella farbwerke mainkur agde   <sep>cassella aktiengesellschaft<sep>cassella aktiengesellschafthanauer landstrasse 526d-60386 frankfurtde<sep>
</APPLICANT>
<INVENTOR>
adam wilhelm drde<sep>adam, wilhelm, dr.<sep>adam, wilhelm, dr.schwabenstrasse 15d-6978 neu-isenburgde<sep>
</INVENTOR>
<ABSTRACT>
the binder comprises a from 5 to 80% by weight solution of a mixture of a water-soluble amino resin-crosslinking polyester and a water-soluble melamine resin in the weight ratio 1: (9 to 0. 1) and, for the production of insulating materials comprising fibres, is sprayed in an amount of from 2 to 10% by weight, based on the solid resin mixture, in the form of a from 5 to 50% by weight solution onto the fibres, and the impregnated material is then treated at elevated temperature. 
</ABSTRACT>
