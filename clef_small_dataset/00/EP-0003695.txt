<ABSTRACT>
The molding device according to the invention is of the kind comprising a molding cavity (19) defined together, on the one hand by two molding matrices (20, 21), and on the other hand by an annular seal (22) establishingA link between them.According to the invention, the peripheral edge of larger diameter of the molding cavity (19) is defined by the contact of a closing edge (24) with a support surface (25), the closing edge (24) belonging to any of the annular concave molding matrix organs (22), while the support surface (25) belongs to the other of these organs.Application in particular to the molding of contact lenses in flexible material.
