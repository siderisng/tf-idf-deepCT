<ABSTRACT>
Method for producing thiazolidin-2-thionen by reacting 1 mol of an aminoethanol with 2 to 2.1 mol of carbon carbon at 20-200 ° C and 0.1 - 5 bar pressure, which is characterized in that in a continuous classification processIn at least two rear-end reaction zones, the aminoethanol containing liquid phase is brought in intimate contact with the gas phase-containing gas phase, wherein said liquid phase is continuously introduced into the first reaction zone, continued in the direction of the last reaction zone and becomes entnomed behind the last reaction zoneand wherein said gas phase continuously introduced into the last reaction zone, continued towards the first reaction zone and removed behind the first reaction zone.The product made is usable as a vulcanization accelerator for rubbers.
</ABSTRACT>
