<ABSTRACT>
For thermoplastic processing of polyvinyl alcohol, it is necessary that the processing temperature is below the decomposition temperature of the polyvinyl alcohol; This is achieved by the incorporation of plasticizers. To produce a plasticizer-containing polyvinyl alcohol granules containing the plasticizer in uniform distribution, a polyvinyl alcohol granules are used as the starting material, which consists predominantly of particles with a certain diameter. The mixing of the plasticizer takes place in the presence of a small amount of water without the polyvinyl alcohol is dissolved thereby, and during the mixing process, a certain temperature control is made. The simultaneous presence of fine particles of a high molecular weight organic compound which is soluble or dispersible in water is advantageous. The plasticizer-containing polyvinyl alcohol granules is suitable for producing any shaped body, in particular films.
