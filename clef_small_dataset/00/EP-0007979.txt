<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a method for producing terephthal, isophthal and phthalaldehyde, and is characterized in that the corresponding xylylene glycols in the presence of oxygen or free oxygen-containing gases and water vapor in genewart of a dehydrogenation catalyst, preferably a silver catalyst, in the gas phase at250-500 ° C are dehydrated.
