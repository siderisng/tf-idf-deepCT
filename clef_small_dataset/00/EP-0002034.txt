1.  electrically heated roasting oven with lower and upper heating and with integrated microwave equipment, characterised thereby, that arranged in the roasting oven (1) is an additional heater element (5), which is controllable independently of the lower and upper heating (3 or 4), is capable of being switched on with the switching-on of the roasting oven heating consisting of lower and upper heating (3, 4) and/or switching-on of the microwave equipment (2) and which after attainment of a certain temperature in the roasting space of the roasting oven (1) is capable of being switched off through a fixedly set temperature switch (9).
