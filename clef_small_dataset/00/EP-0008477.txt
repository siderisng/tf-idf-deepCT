<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a drill mandrel of a drill rod.The mandrel comprises a tubular member (4) traversed by the drill rod and having aperture openings (9) applicable against this rod under the corners (11).The corners (11) are applied by springs (28) against the jaws (10) and repressed from them (10) by a hydraulic thrust.The corners (11) are mounted on a axially adjustable crown (16).
