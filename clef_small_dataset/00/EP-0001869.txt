<TITLE>
device for applying seamless textile wall covers.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

</INVENTOR>
<ABSTRACT>
a device for applying seamless textile wall covers, characterised in that it comprises a profiled section having: a first part (1) possessing two opposed parallel faces, of which one is plane and dorsal (3) and intended to be applied to the surface to be covered with textile and the other is frontal (4) and possesses a curved catching rib (6); and a second part (2) integral with the first part and projecting from its frontal face (4), this second part comprising a fold region (5) situated at a distance from the said frontal face substantially equal to the height of the said catching rib (6) and also a catching rib (7) intended to cooperate with the latter.  <image>
</ABSTRACT>
