<ABSTRACT>
The subject of the present invention is now a projection for producing polymers with diphenolcarbonate end groups, characterized in that polyester diols, polyether diols, polythioetther diols or polyacetal diols having mean molecular weights of Mn (number average) 300 - 6000, preferably 500 -4000, carbonic acid-bisyl ester (eg diphenylcarbonate) and diphenols (eg bisphenol a) together, optionally with the use of catalysts, are reacted at temperatures between 80 and 270 ° C and in vacuo between 200 torr and 0.1 torr.
