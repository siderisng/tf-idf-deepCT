<ABSTRACT>
a process for recovering from a wet process phos­ phoric acid which contains uranium, a uranium containing  concentrate and a purified phosphoric acid.  the wet pro­ cess phosphoric acid is treated with a precipitant in the  presence of a reducing agent and an aliphatic ketone.  as  precipitant may be used ammonia or an ammonium salt  or an inorganic fluorine compound.  the sulphate content  of the acid may be previously adjusted to a value below  0,6% by weight calculated in relation to the amount by weight  of p₂o₅.
