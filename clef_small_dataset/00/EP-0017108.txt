<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a closure, the z.b.Sitting on a bottle sitting hermetically sealed.The closure preferably has the shape of a cap (80) having at least one slit (82) by means of the cap side jacket (81) spreads when placing the cap.Further, the cap (80) has an inner ring bead (89) and a sealing force (86) which serve to the hermetic density of the cap on the bottle.Further, the cap (80) comprises an adhesive element (88) for easy reopening and at least one tearable tensioning member (84) that opens the slit (82), a spreading, and serves as a guarantee seal.
