<ABSTRACT>
In this film hall on a support frame (1), an inner wall (3) and an outer wall (4) made of crystal clear thermoplastic film are arranged and adjustable ventilation flaps (14) for regulating the temperature inside the hall are provided on the longitudinal sides of the hall.For the purpose of simplifying and chargizing this temperature regulation, an improved ventilation system is created in which the space between the inner and outer wall (3, 4) at its lower end above the ventilation flaps (14) with the outer environment over little stens an air inlet (6) with a controllable AHSerrodel (23) provided therein and at its upper end near the first of the hall with their inner over little stens an air outlet (27) is connected, which allows air via the ventilation flaps and / or the space betweenInner and outer wall in the hall.
