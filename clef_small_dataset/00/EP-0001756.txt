<ABSTRACT>
Circuit sanding order for lowering the free time of a thyristor by placing a negative shut-off aid pulse at the auxiliary emitter lying between the emitter (1) and control electrode (5) of the thyristor, for avoiding unadjusted ignition of the thyristor at positive overshoots of the shut-off aid pulses.Two separate pulse sources (10, 11) are provided, which are switched on between the control electrode (5) and auxiliary emitter electrode (4) or between the auxiliary emitter electrode (4) and the emitter electrode (3).A uerschwing of the shut-off aid impulses is thus harmless.The switching arrangement is preferably applicable to higher frequency operated thyristors.
</ABSTRACT>
