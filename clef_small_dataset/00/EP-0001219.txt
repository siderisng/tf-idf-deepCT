<ABSTRACT>
For the indirect connection of two parts, metal pads are applied to the connecting surfaces in vacuo and then these metal pads are welded in vacuo with one another.According to the invention, an intermediate layer of a lead-free glass is applied before applying the metal pads to at least one of the connection surfaces.
