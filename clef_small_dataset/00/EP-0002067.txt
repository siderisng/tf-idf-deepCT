<ABSTRACT>
The invention is a method for producing molded bodies made of borarbide, which contained beyondly 0.1 to 8% by weight of free carbon in the form of graphite, in the borocarbide in submicron powder form together with small amounts of a carbon-containing additive, such as carbon, phenol formaldehydeCondensation products or Steinkoh lenteerpech, pressed cold under shaping and is sintered at closing at temperatures of 2100 ° to 2200 ° C pressure.The molded bodies thus obtained are poly crystalline and - if they correspond to the stoichiometric composition B₄C - single-phase, they have a mean microstructure size of max. 10 μm, a te of at least 90% of the theoretical density of the boron carbide and a bending strength of at least 300N / mm².
