<ABSTRACT>
by the process of this invention, printed circuits are  prepared containing an electrically conductive wiring trace  from materials having adherent and non-adherent surface  areas, e. g. , printed circuit substrates bearing an imaged  photoadhesive layer.  onto the adherent surface areas of the  material is applied solder wettable metal particles, and any  excess particles are removed from the non-adherent areas.   the metallized areas are soldered to form an electrically  conductive wiring trace.
