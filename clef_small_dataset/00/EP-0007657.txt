<ABSTRACT>
the basic wear-lining of a steel converter is made of  refractory material at least partly composed of burnt magne­ site.  maintenance is performed by spraying refractory mater­ ial onto this lining or by "slagging-in".  the effect of this  maintenance procedure, in terms of the duration of the lining,  are improved if the particles of the magnesite component of  the wear-lining contain on average more than 6 1 / 4 percent  monticellite.  on its own material of this kind has not in the  past been found suitable for a wear-lining.
