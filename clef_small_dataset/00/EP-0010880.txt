<ABSTRACT>
an endotracheal and tracheostomy tube cuff (11) has  a passive external diameter not less than 4 times its internal  diameter and a length 8 to 10 times its internal diameter.   these dimensions and the wall material are chosen so as  to utilise viscosity and surface tension effects to achieve a  seal with the tracheal wall.  the carinal end (13) of the cuff  (11) has a concave configuration and the laryngeal end  (17) is optionally tapered.
