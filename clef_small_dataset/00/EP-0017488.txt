<ABSTRACT>
a phenolic material having improved cell structure and  surface skin characteristics is prepared by mixing, foaming  and curing a foamable composition comprising a phenol­ formaldehyde resin; an acid catalyst; a foaming agent-­ mixture containing a low boiling fluorocarbon, a surface  active agent and a polyhydric alcohol; and a filler/extender  mixture functioning as a cell structure control agent compris­ ed of a water soluble inorganic salt and ammonium lignin  sulfonate.
