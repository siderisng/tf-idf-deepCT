<ABSTRACT>
It becomes a new, improved process for the manufacture of pyridinaldehydes and hydrazone derivatives thereof, e.g.b.Pyridinaldehyde-phenylhydrazones described.In this case, an aminomethylpyridine having an aldehyde or a ketone to a compound of the formula is, the compound (III) isomerized in the presence of suitable catalysts to a compound of the formula and introduces the compound (V) in the presence an acid and optionally in the presence of a hydrazineinto a pyridi naldehyde or a pyridinaldehyde hydrazone.According to the process according to the invention, pyridinaldehyde and pyridinaldehyde hydrazones can be easily produced under mil the, environmental response conditions in good to very good yields.
