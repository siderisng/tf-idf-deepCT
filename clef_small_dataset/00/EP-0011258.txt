<ABSTRACT>
Device for the outer fixing of the parts of a broken bone, with an elongated medium body, which consists of two parallel to its longitudinal axis against each other displaceable parts (10,11), wherein each part of the middle body can be used a clamping device (17) for in a bone part nails Which at least on the bone-applicable portion (29) carries an external thread on wise, (25.29). At the two opposite parts (10.11) engages the mutually longitudinal displacement of the parts (10.11) pressure and accessory (14). When the nails are inserted into the opposite bone parts, the pressure and pulling means (14) is operated until the two bone parts are subjected to each other with the required pressure. Then, by locking devices (44, 45), the two parts (10.11) of the middle body are fixed in its position. Subsequently, the pressure and pulling device can be removed by the middle part who the. For safe guidance, the two parts (10,11) of the middle body are telescopically in one another and between the middle body and clamping devices (17) are provided for the same universal joints (16,16 ', 18,22). These universal joints are fixed after attachment to the bone to be slid by locking devices (22,23,24,16,16 ').
