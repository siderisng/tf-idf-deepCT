<ABSTRACT>
A process for producing low molecular weight polyhydroxyl compounds by self-condensation of formaldehyde in the presence of lead (II) compounds whose solutions or suspensions have a pH of over 4.5.The method is characterized in that the condensation reaction in the presence of a) 0.1 to 1 gramquivalent lead per kg formaldehyde, b) more than 15 wt.-% based on formaldehyde, on connections capable of endioluing compounds as CO catalyst and C) optionally low molecular weight and / or higher molecular polyhydroxyl compounds without further organic or inorganic bases is performed.Further, the use of the low molecular weight polyhydroxyl compounds or their hydrogenation products for the production of polyurethane imsfs is claimed.
</ABSTRACT>
