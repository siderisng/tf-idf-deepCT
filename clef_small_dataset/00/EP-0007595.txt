<ABSTRACT>
a new type of preparation of clotrimazole, such as gel  preparation, creamy preparation and liquid preparation,  which are useful for an external use for the treatment of  various trichophytoses, wherein the active clotrimazole is  contained as being dissolved in crotamiton.  said new type of  preparation of clotrimazole can be applied to the skin without  drawbacks such as skin irritation and soiling of clothes as seen  in the conventional tincture or ointments.
