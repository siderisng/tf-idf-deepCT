<TITLE>
a process for preparing 1,7-octadiene.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

<ABSTRACT>
1,7-octadiene can be prepared by hydrodimerizing  butadiene in the presence of solubilized palladium, a tertiary  phosphine, formic acid, a base and optionally an organic  solvent.  the yield of 1,7-octadiene is increased if the  hydrodimerization is also carried out in the presence of a  supported palladium, platinum and/or rhodium catalyst  and wherein the ratio of the metal in the supported catalyst  to the solubilized palladium ranges from about 0. 001 to 10.
