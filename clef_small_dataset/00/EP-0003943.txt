<ABSTRACT>
Method for determining the initial phase of the receiver clock from a synchronization signal whose spectrum comprises two rays at F₁ = FC - 1 / 2T and F₂ = FC + 1 / 2T where FC is the carrier frequency and 1/ t the signaling rate.The initial phase is obtained by calculating the phase of the coefficient at the frequency 1 / T of the discrete Fourier transform of a predetermined number n predetermined of samples of the high received signal S (Kτ).-Application for data transmission.
