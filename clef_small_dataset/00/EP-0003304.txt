<ABSTRACT>
NEW N-substituted 2,6-dialkylanilines and methods for producing N-substituted 2,6-dialkylanilines by reacting 2,6-dialkylanilines with carbonyl compounds and subsequent catalytic hydrogenation with hydrogen.The compounds producible by the method of the invention are valuable starting materials for the production of dyes and pest control agents.
