<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a circuitry voltage for the delivery of digital communication signals in the course of broadcasting connections via a data transfer system, wherein all the subscriber sites connected to the data negotiation system for the transmission of signals corresponding to one of two specified, different data transmission procedures and data formats are designed for each . It should be used with the lowest possible circuit-technical effort and under the lowest possible load of the data switching system. For this purpose, a separate round writing device (TTU) is connected to the data switching system, which contains a memory array (RAM) and a separate control one direction (MP, ROM). For the production of round writing connections, the broadcasting device (TTU) with a transmit subscriber site is merely connected for the duration of the transmission of message signals and at least the numbers of the entry-coming reception participants. Then, connectations via the data switching system (EDS) are constructed by the circular (TTU) one after the other via the data switching system (EDS) to the reception subscriber sites in question, which are then supplied to the message signals.
