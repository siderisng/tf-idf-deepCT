<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to finely divided, expandable particles of a styrene homopolymer or copolymer, which contain a blowing agent and a flame retardant organic halogen compound and additionally a 2,4-diamino-1,3,5-triazine derivative and process for their production by polymerizationin aqueous suspension.
