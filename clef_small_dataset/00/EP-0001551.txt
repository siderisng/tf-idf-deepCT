<ABSTRACT>
The starting point is an evacuable X-ray film cassette with two on-each other latched cartridge parts which are connected to each other by a hinge device with the two cassette parts in the closure position closed locking elements and with a suction line leading in the cassette space.In such X-ray film cartridges, the holder of amplifier foil and X-ray film as well as the light seal and the hinge facilities should be simplified.This is achieved by a light-tight film (9), which is formed on both cassette parts (1, 7) and thus the hinge means, which has sealing elements (11) on the unliapled side along the edge of a cassette part (1, 7).Thus, special hinges and light seals can be omitted between the two cassette parts and the footage extends to the edge of the cartridge.
</ABSTRACT>
