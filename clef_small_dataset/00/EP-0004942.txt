a new process is disclosed for the synthesis of alkylene  carbonates having from 2 to 4 carbon atoms in the chain,  starting from a corresponding halohydrin having the formula:    wherein ra, rb, rc and rd are independently hydrogen or  alkyl, aryl, alkylaryl or arylalkyl radicals and where x is a  halogen, characterized in that said halohydrin is reacted with  a bicarbonate of a quaternary -onium compound, where  "-onium" means ammonium, phosphonium, arsonium or  stibonium, preferably using an organic solvent as diluent  and operating in the presence of carbon dioxide.
