<ABSTRACT>
Device for detecting exceeding a threshold by variations in a magnetic flux.Voltage v by these variations in a winding is rectified in 20 and the rectified voltage obtained is applied to the terminals of a capacitor 16, 17 whose electrode is movable relative to the other and which contains an electret 18.Cinning an electrostatic force h tending to bring these two electrodes closer to the magnetic attraction of a magnet 21, the failover of the movable electrode due to exceeding the threshold having the effect of operating an electric switch 22. Applicationamong others: circuit breakers.
