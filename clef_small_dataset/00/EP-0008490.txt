<ABSTRACT>
the invention relates to moulded plastic spools for thread  having a groove in which thread can be trapped.  a spool 1 is  provided for carrying thread windings on which is included a  flexible flange (5) mounted at one end.  a thread trapping  member (8) is adapted to be fitted to such end of the spool so  that a surface of the thread trapping member abuts against  the surface (5a) of the flexible flange to form a groove bet­ ween the thread trapping member and the flexible flange.  the  groove can be opened on flexing of the flange for facilitating  insertion of thread therein.
