<ABSTRACT>
METHOD AND DEVICE FOR ATTRITION IN WAME SCIENCE INCLUDING MINERAIS already crushed.The process is characterized by the fact that one puts in pulp the already milled ore, and that one submits this pulp at an agitation in a little turbulent environment.Such agitation is obtained with the device according to the invention in which the pulp moves from each of the agitation members 3 and 4 green the median zone located between these organs.Application to the preconcentration of minerals.
