<ABSTRACT>
Process for the purification of solutions containing car bonarate, sulfate, hydroxide or hydrogenocarbonate of sodium or potassium as well as mainly, at least one metals belonging to the group consisting of vanadium, uranium or molybdenum under The shape of sodium or potassium salts, and the mineral and / or organic impurities, according to which the precautionary solutions of an adequate amount of lime are calculated or partially captured, the first precipitate containing essentially Calcium carbonate and concentrates the liquor separated by evaporation up to a hydroxide content at most equal to 50%, to cause a second precipitate consisting of sodium or potassium sulfate and then After separation from it, one recesses a liquor rich in hydroxide. This process is more particularly adapted to the traiments of liquors from the alkaline attack of Mine VanDifère and Uranifers.
