<ABSTRACT>
polymers containing polyalkylpiperidines, preparation  and use thereof as stabilizers for synthetic polymer compositions  are described.  in said polymers, sterically hindered  polyalkylpiperidines are laterally linked to the main chain.   the polymers are distinguished by an improved stabilizing  effect in various synthetic polymer compositions, such as  polyolefins, against light- and/or heat-induced deterioration  thereof.  the polyalkylpiperidine-containing polymers of the  invention are hardly volatile upon heat-processing or during  storage of shaped articles containing said polymers, and are  resistant to extraction with solvents.
