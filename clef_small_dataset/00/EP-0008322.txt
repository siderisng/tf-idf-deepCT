<ABSTRACT>
Resorcini are prepared by reacting δ-ketocarboxylic acid esters in the gas phase at temperatures of 250 ° C to 500 ° C in the presence of hydrogen on a catalyst.The catalyst consists of 2 components that are manufactured separately and only then brought together.The first component contains at least one connection of a metal of group VIII and / or at least one compound of a group Ib metal on one or more support materials.The second component contains at least one compound of a metal of the group IIa and / or IV A and / or III B and / or IV B on one or more carrier materials.A particularly preferred catalyst is the mixture of the same volume parts of platinum / coal as a second component and thorium / coal as the second component.
</ABSTRACT>
