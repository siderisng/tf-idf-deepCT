<ABSTRACT>
an improved oxygen sensor of the type adapted for instal­ lation in the exhaust conduit of an internal combustion engine.   the improved sensor uses a metal oxide element to sense the  partial pressure of oxygen in the exhaust gases to which the  sensor is exposed.  the sensor has a steel body and a ceramic  member which extends into the exhaust gases to permit the  exhaust gases to produce variations in an electrical character­ istic of one of the sensor components.  the improved sensor  has a perforated protection tube (18) that has a radially extend­ ing flange (78).  the flange (78) is located between the steel  body (12) and the ceramic member (16), which forms a seal  preventing exhaust gases from inside the engine's exhaust  conduit from leaking beyond the sensor to the atmosphere.
