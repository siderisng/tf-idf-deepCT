<ABSTRACT>
Monoazo compounds of the formula in the R for hydrogen, methyl and ethyl are obtained from diazotized 5-amino-benzimidazole 2-on-6-carboxylic acid, their methyl and ethyl ester and coupling to 2-acetoace tylamino-5-chloro-hydrochinondimethyl ether.The products are pigments which are particularly suitable for the pairing of burn-up paints and polyvinyl chloride.
