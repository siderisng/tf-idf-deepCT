<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the hydropneumatic piston accumulators with a lack of gas detector whose movable member 14 is pressed by the piston 8 when it approaches too close to the bottom 4, gas side, of the'accumulator.Connecting means 28, 32, with movement reversing and acting in traction are interposed between the piston 8 and the movable member 14, these connecting means having a dead stroke of predetermined length.The invention makes it possible to check the proper operation of the detector device, by emptying a portion of the liquid contained in the compartment 12, the piston 8, when it approaches the bottom 6, actuating the detector device.
