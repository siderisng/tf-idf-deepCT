<ABSTRACT>
the arrangement is used for measuring the load moment of a working machine, in particular a lifting appliance.  at the hinge point (3, 8) either of a load arm (2, 4) or of an operating member (6) for the load arm on the machine structure, the component (n, n') of the force (k) transmitted thereto is determined, which is at right angles to the plane (e) defined by the two hinge axes (3, 8).  this component is a direct measure for the load moment.  in a preferred embodiment one of the hinge bolts (8) is used at the same time as a measuring bolt, by having strain gauge strips (12) attached to it.  <image>
