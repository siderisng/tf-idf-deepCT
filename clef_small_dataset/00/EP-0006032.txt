<ABSTRACT>
an improved tamperproof container for lyophilized  material wherein the container comprises a vial (20) having a  flange (21) about its opening.  below the flange (21), but  spaced apart therefrom is a shoulder to define an indented  neck (33) therebetween.  the opening is sealed with a  pierceable stopper (24).  an overcap (10) extends over the  flange (21) and about the neck (33) to form a skirt (18) about  the neck (33).  the skirt (18) has at least one portion thereof  extending inwardly in gripping relation with the neck (33)  and limited in removal by contact with the underside of the  flange (21) whereby the overcap (10) cannot be removed  without destroying its structural integrity.
