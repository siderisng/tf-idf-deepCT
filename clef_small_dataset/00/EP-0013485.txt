<TITLE>
thermoplastic polyglutarimide compositions and methods of making them.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
compositions having high impact strength are achieved  by blending polyglutarimide, multiple stage polymer based  on butadiene or alkylacrylates, and polycarbonate in a weight  ratio such that the polycarbonate comprises about 12 to 25 %  of the blend.  the polyglutarimide contains imide units of the  formula    wherein r₁, r₂ and r₃ independently represent hydrogen or c₁  to c₂₀ unsubstituted or substituted alkyl, aryl, or mixtures  thereof.  the multiple stage polymer has a first stage polymer­ ized from a predominantly butadiene or alkyl acrylate mono­ mer system.  preferably the polyglutarimide is 20 to 80 % by  weight of the three components.  blending may be for exam­ ple by melting together or by dry blending.
