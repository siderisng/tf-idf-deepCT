<ABSTRACT>
In gas turbine engines, the first compressor is often coupled directly to the useful power machine.To create an intake system for the air to be compressed without deflections and without unsteaded cross-sectional changes, two separate suction channels (1) are arranged on both sides of the useful power machine (4).For the connection to the intake cross-section of the compressor (6), a transition part (3) is provided with vertical roof-shaped baffles (5) which ensure a steady reduction of the inflow cross-section.
