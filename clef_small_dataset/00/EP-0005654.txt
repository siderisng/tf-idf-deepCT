<ABSTRACT>
Process for transforming the compounds of the formula: wherein R is a hydrogen atom or a substituent and for which the carbon carbon of the vinyl group has the rectral configuration in the corresponding compounds of formula (I) for which the carbon carrier of the vinyl grouphas the sinister configuration, and vice versa, characterized in that temperature is heated above 50 ° C, within a protecal solvent or a mixture of protic solvents, in the presence or absence of formaldehyde, a compound of formula (I) for which the carbon carrier of the vinyl group has the rectus configuration or sinister, partially or completely salified, and isolates the compound of formula (I) for which the carbon carrier of the vinyl group has the configuration sinister orrectus thus formed.The compounds obtained are usable in particular as intermediate products for the preparation of drugs.
