<ABSTRACT>
All numbers of a given rank of Consti numbers killing the digital image (1) are recorded on the same support (1), constituted for example by a film, in the form of contiguous surface elements which, in a given example., are made conventionally opaque in the light when the corresponding digit is either 1 or 0 and only in this case, to constitute a sub-image (2-5).A surface element associated with a number occupies inside the sub-image a position similar to that occupied by the number containing this number in the digital image.The invention applies in all technical domains where it is necessary or advantageous to store digital images, particularly in that of the medical domain.
