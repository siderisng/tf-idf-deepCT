<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an optical system of projection of patterns for reproducing on a semiconductor plate (4) or on a mask covered with a photosensitive material the image of a modulator object (1, 10).According to the invention, reflective optical means comprising one or more plane mirroes (M₁, M₂₀) of which at least one is removable (M₁), are introduced into the optical jet of the exposure beam, allowing to associate with the photorepétition function.By creating at least two optical jets (a, b), different functions such as photocomposition or other photorepétition routes or to associate an alignment device from the OB jet-image planes comprising an optoelectronic detector (5).Application in particular to the manufacture of semiconductor components by direct photorepétition.
