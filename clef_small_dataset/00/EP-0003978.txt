A road roller consists of a front rotor (4) and a rear rotor (5) supporting central part (1) and of a mounted on this drive motor, which drives over at least one pump and at least one hydraulic motor the front or rear rotor, whereby the optionally witha vibration drive is directed over a hydraulic steering drive.In order to simply convert such a road roller for all accumulating work, the front rotor (4) and the rear rotor (5) with their optionally provided hydraulic motors, hydraulic steering drives and vibration drives each form one on the middle part (1) flangable assembly (2,3)which are interchangeable against different runner construction units (2.3).
