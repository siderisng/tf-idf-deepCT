<ABSTRACT>
Corrosion protection coating agent for metal surfaces containing as a corrosion inhibitor zinc or ply salts of five- or six-membered heterocycles substituted with at least one hydroxyl or mercapto group having at least one nitrogen atom and conjugated double bonds in the ring, of which at least one is present in a grouping of the formula.Particularly suitable are the salts of the mercaptobezthiazole, the mercaptopyridine and the corresponding S-triazine derivatives, for example the cyanuric acid and the thiocyanuric acid.
