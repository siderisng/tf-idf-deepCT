<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a device for the distribution in adjustable proportion of two pasty substances (30 and 34) contained separately inside a deformable tube (10), this device comprising a tip (14) adaptable on the orifice. exit (12) of the tube (10) for defining two chambers inside the tube capable of containing the two pasty substances (30 and 34) and two corresponding outputs (20 and 28) opening onto an outlet surface of the tip. ; A dispenser (36) mounted swiveling on the tip about an axis (XY) perpendicular to the axis of the outlet orifice of the tube between two extreme positions between which a cylindrical surface of the distributor comes to sweep the output surface of The tip, the distributor having one or two through passages (42 and 46) opening by an input section (44, 48) into the cylindrical surface of the dispenser and one end ending with a common output cannula (50), The input sections (44, 48) or less releasing the two outputs (20 and 28) of the outlet surface of the tip (14) as a function of the distributor's pivot position (36).
