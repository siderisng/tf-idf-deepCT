<ABSTRACT>
Foaming in residual monomer removal by vacuum distillation protective colloid-containing aqueous polymer dispersions obstructs a rapid and effective operation.Due to a water vapor in and-through line in aqueous polymer dispersions, this foaming no longer occurs, so that even in the most common cases on the use of dusting in the degassing process, which connects to the polymeric sation process, can be dispensed with.
