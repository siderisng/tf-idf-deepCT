<ABSTRACT>
A capacitance in an integrated circuit is made with conventional bipolar transistor technology.The stress-dependent capacitance is supplied by an emitter-based boundary boundary layer (10), parasiltic capacitances, z.b.Collector substrate (14) are separated from the emitter base capacity by remaining the base collector boundary layer (12) in the release direction.The capacitance (T5) is used, for example, in a bootstrap driver circuit whose bootstrap current is supplied by this transistor-like structure (T5);The internal load capacity leads to a substantially constant behavior of the circuit when the controlled load changes.
