<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an improvement of the method for producing polysiloxanes with hydrocarbon radicals bound via oxygen to hydrocarbon radicals by reacting halosilane with alcohol and water in the presence of the desired polysiloxane. The improvement is achieved in a bladder containing a column and optionally reflux condenser and already desired polysiloxane continuous alcohol, water and, if necessary, acidic catalyst, which promotes the condensation of Si-bonded, condensation groups, are introduced in such quantities that in the bubble at least 5 weight percent alcohol and 0.001 to 5 weight percent catalyst, each with respect to the total weight of alcohol, catalyst and polysiloxane present in the bladder, the contents of the bladder for boiling heated under reflux, to be resolved halosilane at least 1 m above the lower Column ends inserted into the column and polysiloxane is continuously withdrawn from the bubble to its formation.
