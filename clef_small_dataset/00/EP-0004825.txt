<ABSTRACT>
New device for loading powder or granular materials also containing fine plain-sensitive particles which comprises from the bottom up: a) a truncpyramidal or frustoconical charging enclosure comprising at least two polygonal or torch crowns made by pudding. Inflatable in different planes, rigidizing spacers between the pudding and a textile skirt integral with the semi-rigid structure thus carried out. b) A flexible binding hood connected in a sealed manner to the loading inflation. c) a bonding sleeve placed between the flexible hood and the supply tubing of powder or granular material d) finally, at least one suction tubing of the dust connected to a dusting device. The loading device according to the invention has the property of floating on the pile of materials being trained, and to remove the dust volutes that form during this operation.
