<ABSTRACT>
the invention relates to a process for the preparation of  β-lactam derivatives having a substituted hydroxymethyl  group at the carbon atom α to the lactam carbonyl group, to  the novel antibacterially-active derivatives so-obtainable and  to pharmaceutical compositions containing these.  the process comprises reacting under specified conditions  an α-halo-β-lactam starting material with activated zinc  in the presence of an appropriate aldehyde or ketone and  hydrolysing the resulting zinc complex.  the novel β-lactam derivatives are of the general  formula i    (with the meaning of r, r₁-r₄, y and z as given in the  specification) and include the pharmaceutically acceptable  salts thereof.
