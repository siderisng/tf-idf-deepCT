<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a device for passing a working medium by a continuously moving web by using a slot nozzle and a cover element.In this case, the cover element (7) extends over the entire width of the slot nozzle (1) and via a region located and behind it forms a flow space for the working medium together with a guide surface (8).In doing so, the working medium must enforce the web on a relatively long journey, which brings an intensification of treatment with it.
