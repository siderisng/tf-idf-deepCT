<ABSTRACT>
In an original closure for containers, the originality assurance consists of a sealing cap (1), which is plugged on the screw cap (6) and locked in the axial direction unavoidable.At the lower edge of the sealing cap (1), a mesh ring is integrally formed, which consists of a determination of the part (2) and a surge section (5).The expression section (5) is separated by incision recesses (3 and 4) from the fixed part (2) and connected to the outer shell of the cap (1) via tear-off webs (8).At the canister is a web (9) projecting into the space of the incision recess (3) and forms here by a twist stop.On which the a cut recess (3) opposite side of the circlip is arranged a boundation tab (10) limited by weakening notches (13).
