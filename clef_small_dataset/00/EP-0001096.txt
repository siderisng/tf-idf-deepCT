<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a halogen-containing copolycarbonate mixture which has improved critical thickness and improves flame retardancy and fire resistance and contains the reaction product of an aromatic diphenol, an aromatic thiodiphenol and a carbonate derivative such as phosgene or carbonyl bromide in a mixture with an effective amount of a halogen-containing compound.In a preferred embodiment, the copolycarbonate mixture also contains small amounts of walls perfluorosulfonic acid or carboxylic acid salt.
