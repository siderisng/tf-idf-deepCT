<ABSTRACT>
The lens carrier (13) of a microfilm reader carries at its one end two or more floating lenses (16, 17) and is mounted by a rotary axis pivot about a to the deposition beam path (10).The objective not in function position (16) thus alternates automatically from the microfilm or the transparent plate (9a) located above.The lens carrier (13) is also pivotable towards a spring force to the axis of rotation, so that the entire lens carrier (13) can be lifted from the microfilm or the transparent plate (9a).
