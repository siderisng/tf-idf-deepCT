<ABSTRACT>
Fat-containing animal starting material is extracted with a gas in supercritical condition.The extraction takes place in a static column without cycle.Cases used as solvents for extraction can be regenerated after extraction and recirculated who.As a solvent arbitrary gases can be used whose critical data falls into the pressure and temperature ranges determined by the extraction class.As a final product, animal fats with a water is less than 0.2 wt.% available.
