<ABSTRACT>
The deposition of metallic or ionic mercury from industrial wastes was previously carried out by reduction and impulse filtration of the wastewater with the addition of filter aids.Another method was in the adsorptive binding at a large excess of metal hydroxides and subsequent filtration of the wastewater.On the other hand, the inventive method of reduction, clarification in a leaving basin and subsequent filtration of the preset waste water in a depth filter and subsequent removal of the filtered mercury on the depth filter due to backwashing in a fluidized bed has the advantage that the metallic mercury in a simple filtration method largely from theWastewater can be removed and can be brought without the addition of filtration aids by simply settling in a sewage tank for deposition.
