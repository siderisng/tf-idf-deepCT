<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to propulsion powders based on nitrocellulose, nitrieted oil and polyvinyl nitrate as well as their manufacturing process. The powders according to the invention contain between: - 50 and 75 parts by weight of nitrocellulose, - 5 and parts by weight of nitrieted oil, - 5 and 25 parts by weight of polyvinyl nitrate. The manufacturing process involves mixing energy bases in the presence of acetone / alcohol mixture until a homogeneous paste is spinned, removed, carved, tempered and dried and is characterized in that: (I ) - The content of the mixing acetone dough is between 30 and 36% by weight relative to nitrocellulose and polyvinyl nitrate and in that the acetone / alcohol weight ratio is between 1.10 and 1, 50. (ii) - It is started by mixing nitrocellulose with nitio oil in the presence of solvents and in that the solid polyvinyl nitrate is added by fraction to the mixture, (iii) - spinning before cutting is carried out. temperature below 30 ° C. The powders according to the invention are particularly suitable as cannon powders for combat tanks.
