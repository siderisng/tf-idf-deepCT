<ABSTRACT>
Method for obtaining pure neopentyl glycol (NPG) with a purity of over 98%, determined by gas chromatographic analysis, by catalytic high pressure hydrogenation of a raw hydroxipivalal aldehyde, which includes, among other things, the mono-ISO butter acid ester of the NPG as immunity and small amounts of water, in the liquid phase over with barium activated copper chromite catalysts in two stages, wherein in the first stage temperatures of 120 to 160 ° C and loads of 0.05 to 1.0 l hydrogenation a set / 1 contact · h and in the second stage temperaturesfrom 170 to 200 ° C and loads of 0.05 to 1.0 l of hydrogenation on the set / L contact · h.
