<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a filling for tightly adapting an element of clothing or an accessory to a portion of the human body.This liner is constituted by a thermo-formable plastic foam 2 placed in a non-extensible envelope 1 and formed an open cavity intended to receive the part of the body, but the dimensions are generally lower than that part of the body.The housing is then formed by the reciprocal compression of the foam and the part of the body placed in the cavity, while the temperature of the foam is carried at the temperature of thermo-forming.This invention can be used to adapt, shoes, helmets, gloves, swimming palms, etc...
