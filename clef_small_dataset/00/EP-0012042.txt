<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a rigid device to support a reactor tank and the steam generators associated with it.This device consists of a metalli structure that comprising on the one hand, a lower part (5) wiring the tank (1) and consisting of two internal (5a) and outer coaxial envelopes (5b) welded to a thick metal sole (7) and serving as a support for the tank (1) and its associated genera (2) via square tight (8, 11) adjusted in fixed bearings with corresponding square bore ball joint (24), and DAn upper part (6) which is terminated to form at its central part the swimming pool of the tank (1) and which comprises on both sides of two side parts (16) enveloping and now the generators (2), these parts (16) being gathered at their upper part by stiffening cresses (17).
