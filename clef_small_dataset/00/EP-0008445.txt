<ABSTRACT>
Method for producing benzoic anhydride by reacting benzotrichloride with benzoic acid in the molar ratio of 1: 2 to 1: 3 at temperatures in the range of 100 to 200 ° C.The conveyed benzoic anhydride falls as undestiled raw material in high purity and almost colorless.The yield is about 95% of theory (based on benzo trichloride).
