<ABSTRACT>
compounds of the formula    and their agriculturally suitable salts, wherein:   r₁ is thienyl or optionally substituted phenyl,   r₂ is selected from various organic groups,   x is ch₃, ch₃o, ch₃ch₂o or ch₃och₂,   y is ch₃ or ch₃o, and   z is ch or n,   can be employed as general or selective herbicides and  as plant growth modifiers.  the compounds can be formulated  for use in conventional manner.  they can be prepared  by alkylation of an appropriately substituted carbamimidothioic  acid salt.
