<TITLE>
dispenser for liquid soap and container therefor.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

</APPLICANT>

<ABSTRACT>
this application discloses a dispenser (100) for liquid  soap including two telescoping housing members (110, 150)  generally cylindrical in shape and having the longitudinal  axes thereof inclined upwardly, the inner housing member  (110) being mountable on a wall (50) and the outer housing  member (150) having a broad contact area accessible to a  user.  a support (120) extends upwardly and forwardly from  the inner housing member (110) and telescopically receives a  guide (175) on the outer housing member (150) with a spring  (185) therebetween.  a discharge member (200) is removably  mounted on the inner housing member (110) and has a soap  outlet (247) in registration with an opening (171) with the  outer housing member (150) and has structure for removably  attaching a liquid soap container (250) thereto.  pump means are provided on the discharge member  (200) for pumping charges of liquid soap from the liquid soap  container (250) to the soap outlet (247).  an actuating  member (170) is provided on the outer housing (150)  operating the pump means upon shifting of the housing  members from the rest positions thereof to the actuating  positions thereof.  also disclosed is a novel container (250)  for liquid soap for use in the dispenser (100).  
</ABSTRACT>
