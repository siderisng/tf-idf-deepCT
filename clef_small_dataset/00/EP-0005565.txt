<ABSTRACT>
the full throttle or kick down engine speed of a vehicle  provided with an infinitely variable transmission had to be  high because of performance requirements and had to be  low because of comfort (noise level) requirements.  to meet  both requirements the full throttle engine speed depends on  the actual transmission ratio in such a way that at lower  transmission ratios the full throttle engine speed is increased.
