<ABSTRACT>
The invention has for its object to provide a method for frame synchronization, which requires only low transmission capacity for high synchronization security.According to the invention, before reaching a synchronous state, a synchronizing alarm (SA = 1) is given, and after detection of two consecutive synchronization words (10010), the beginning of a test time (P) is determined.If during this test time (P) continuously synchronizing words (10010) are received, the synchronizing alarm (SA = 1) is switched off.If only a single synchronization word (10010) is not detected during the test time (P), then the synchronizing alarm remains.Starting from the synchronous state, the synchronizing alarm is given if multiple synchronization words are not detected.
