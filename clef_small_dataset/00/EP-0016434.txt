<ABSTRACT>
For cleaning liquid leading channels, particularly sewer channels, on the channel sole (2) under the liquid pressure, a hollow cleaning body (1) decreases, in whose cavity is contained in the waistbe movement lossy disguising fluid and / o of the part filling, so that the speed of the cleaning body (1) is slower than the flow rate speed of the liquid. As a result, deflecting currents of the liquid are induced, so that dirt deposits are bleeded and washed away from the cleaning body (1), laterally thereof and in the follow-up stream. By an unbalanced surface shape of the cleaning body, the tread generated behind it is laterally deflected from the rolling path of the cleaning body (1) laterally, so that dirt accumulations (46) in the rolling path gradually be removed laterally. Through a throttle structure (38) in the cleaning body for a liquid which can be switched off with it, a resistance of the cleaning body is prevented.
