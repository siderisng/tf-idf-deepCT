<ABSTRACT>
An arrangement for electro-optical voltage measurement on a high voltage conductor (16) in which the voltage to be measured is derived from the phase difference of the components of the light wave.At least one of the conductors (14, 21) for the polarized light beam (4) consists of a crystal fiber with longitudina Lem, linear electro-optic effect of piezoelectric material.At the high voltage conductor, the crystal fiber is guided so that the mold double refraction is canceled within the crystal fiber.The measuring arrangement has the advantage that the voltage is integrated over a large distance and the measurement result is independent of the field distribution along this path.
