<ABSTRACT>
drum brake adjuster in an assembly having a pair of  brake shoes (22, 24) which are movable by a hydraulic  actuator (16), from a non-braking position to a braking  position, a strut (30) cooperating with the pair of brake shoes  (22, 24) to substantially define the non-braking position, and a  pawl (42) cooperating with the strut (30) when the pair of  brake shoes are released from the braking position to impart  movement to the strut (30).  the strut (30) comprising a unitary  single member (32) engaging the pair of brake shoes (22, 24)  and one (24) of the pair of brake shoes including means (38)  engageable with said unitary single member (32) in order to  adjust the non-braking position of the pair of brake shoes.
