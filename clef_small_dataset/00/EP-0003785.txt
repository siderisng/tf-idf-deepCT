<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to coating compositions based on aqueous, emulsifier-free, sedimentation stable polyurethane dispersion, consisting of a) of the dispersion of a polyurethane with a softening point below 205 ° C and a shore A hardness below 97 and b) a polyurethane with a melting point above 210 ° C anda Shore D hardness of more than 50, as well as the use of these coating agents for coating PVC.
