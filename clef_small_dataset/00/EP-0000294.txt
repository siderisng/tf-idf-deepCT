<ABSTRACT>
Tool Removal Device compared to Machine-Tool Machine Parts comprising a moving machine support of the tool (9) or the part (10) having a game catch-up guidance (6) by lateral solicitation.This device comprises means (14) for moving laterally said mobile assembly away from its game catch-up position to remove the tool from the part after machining.For indexable rotating tool holder tool, it is provided an indexing of the tool in a plane approximately parallel to that of the gaming catch-up prior to the implementation of the aforementioned means used to remove the tool from the part.
