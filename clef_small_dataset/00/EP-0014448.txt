<ABSTRACT>
When depleting fibrous suspensions according to the FLO TATION method, impurities are bonded to the foam (3) resulting in the flotation (3) which is filtered off with suction and then depressed. The upper layer of the fiber suspension loaded by foam (3) detached layer of fiber suspension is recycled and foamed again. The method is carried through by means of a container (1), the bottom of which has air chambers (12). About the width of the container (11) extend close above the liquid ensue mirror (2) trigger elements (4,4 ') for discharging the foam (3), tightly below the liquid mirror (2) drain channels (5) for removing the fines from the Obe ren area of ​​the pulp suspension. The trigger elements (4,4 '), perforated or slotted pipes, are located under a vacuum of 0.5 - 2 MWs and are arranged 10 - 50 mm above the liquid mirror (2). The drain channels (5) are opened in the lowest part of the hook-shaped cross section (14) for removing the fines. The cross section of the drain opening (26) is greater than the sum of the cross sections of the openings (14).
