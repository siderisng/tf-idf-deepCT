<ABSTRACT>
a corrugated fibreboard shipping box for packing a  stack of sheet material stacked into a flat rectangular shape  having a certain thickness is made of a single box blank.  the  box blank is comprised of a rectangular bottom section (1), a  pair of rectangular side sections (3a,3b) connected to the  opposite sides of the bottom section (1), a pair of top half  section (2a,2b) connected to the sides of the side sections  (3a,3b), and flaps (4,6,5a,7a,5b,7b) connected to the opposite  ends of the bottom section (1), side section (3a,3b) and  top half sections (2a,2b).  the flaps (4,6,5a,7a,5b,7b) are provided with parallel  folding lines at which the flaps are folded inside to form  folded end portions (4p,5p,6p,7p).  the folded end portions  close the open ends of the box.  the corrugated fibreboard  shipping box packs and protect the stack of sheet materials  without pads.
