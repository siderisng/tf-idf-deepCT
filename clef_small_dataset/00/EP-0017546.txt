<ABSTRACT>
A removable one-piece sealing coating support is engaged in a turbojet blower housing.This support is formed of three concentric ferrules: an inner ferrule (1) carrying the sealing coating (8), an intermediate ferrule (2) and an outer ferrule (3) constituting the workplace, and a ferrule with profile in profile.U (19) Now the external and intermediate ferrules.The ferrules are made from epoxy resin and winding or fiberglass tablecloth.The outer ferrule has litters (9,10,11) cooperating by fitting with parts (12,13,14) of the housing.
