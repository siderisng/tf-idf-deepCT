<ABSTRACT>
The device uses a television camera (36) and at least one projector (45) for the lighting of the area to be inspected.The camera (36) is suspended from a flexible tube, rigid torsion inside which pass the power cables from the camera (36) and the projector (45).The device comprises a rigid tubular guide (20) for the guidance and orientation of the flexible tube on its path from the outside to the interior of the speaker (1), a means of drive (25) of theFlexible rotating tube about its axis, a tube retaining means and the camera (36) against vertical, unlockable movements, precise means for tracking the angular and vertical positions of the camera and a centering device(40) of the camera in the enclosure (1).The device applies in particular to the inspection of pressurized water nuclear reactor pressurizers.
