<ABSTRACT>
1.  apparatus for processing films (74) in successive steps, such as cutting, joining and/or viewing, comprising a housing base portion (12), film carrier plates (68) which are mounted movably on the housing base portion (12) in a guide means, a cutting means (24, 26) for cutting the films (74), and a means for joining the cut films (74) by folding an adhesive sticker (102) on to the cut ends of the film, characterised in that the films (74) on the film carrier plates (68) are movable over an arcuate path or a path similar to an arc, with their broad side tangentially on said path.
