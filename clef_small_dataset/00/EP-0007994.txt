<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a magnetic core from a soft magnetic amorphous alloy.According to the invention, the amorphous alloy 1 is at least one point 2 along the core at least over a part of the core cross section at this point in the crystalline is transferred.The zone led into the crystalline state acts as an air gap, since the permeability in the crystalline state is considerably lower than in amorphous state.The magnetic core according to the invention is thus suitable for applications in which a saved hysteresis loop is required.
