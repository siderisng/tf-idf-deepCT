<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a basket bottom optical connector for establishing an optical link between a printed circuit board and an optical cable connected to this connector.It consists in providing means allowing a lateral mobibility of the connector to ensure the centering between an optical tip (104) carried by the card and an optical tip (302) of the cable (303) inserted into the connector (301),and means (311) for squeezing these two tips against each other and maintaining this pressure continuously.In particular, it allows optical links between the cards of the same apparatus.
