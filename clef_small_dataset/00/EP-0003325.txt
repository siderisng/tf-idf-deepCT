<ABSTRACT>
a process for the preparation of optionally foamed polyurethane compositions from organic polyisocyanates, polymer-polyol dispersions and, if desired, additives and auxiliaries, in which aqueous polymer dispersions, prepared by copolymerisation of styrene, acrylonitrile and a monomer containing a bonded zerewitinoff-active radical, having a polymer particle size of from 1000 to 10,000  angstrom  and a solids content of up to 65% by weight, are mixed with at least one polyhydroxyl compound, and some or all of the water is then removed. 
</ABSTRACT>
