<ABSTRACT>
It is desired to simplify the problem of storage and extraction of cassettes by avoiding, moreover, to dispose of the standard boxes.The cabinet can receive bids (5) for cassettes such as standard trade huts.Organs (3) tilting on an axis (4) retain the boxes (5).Each organ (3), in the rest position, is retained by a disengageable lug.Boxes (1) can be associated in row with grooves (10) and eagle tail ribs (11).A cover (2) can be removably attached by projections (12) engaging in grooves (13).The hood can serve as a handle.The hood can receive a superimposed box with the first, with removable connection (14, 15).
