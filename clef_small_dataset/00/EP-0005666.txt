<ABSTRACT>
The invention convences a transverse axial tomography apparatus using a computer.This apparatus comprises a unique toe-shaped sheath (1-3), carriers of many X-ray tubes (4), which can be triggered simultaneously or successively in a very fast way, thus radiating a body (5) placed at thecenter of the sheath, and a plurality of detectors (7) collecting the radiation attenuated.Such an apparatus makes it possible to perform tomographic cups of moving organs such as the heart.
