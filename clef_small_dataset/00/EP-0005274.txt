<ABSTRACT>
photopolymerizable compositions comprising a photopolymerizable  monomer, hexaarylbiimidazole as an initiator  and a sensitizing amount of a selected compound derived  from aryl ketones and p-dialkylamino-arylaldehydes.  the  photopolymerizable compositions which optionally contain  inert components such as organic polymeric binders, solvents,  etc.  are photoactivated in the visible region of the  spectrum.  the photopolymerizable compositions are useful  in lithographic and letter press printing plantes, engineering  drafting films, lithographic films, photoresists and solder  masks.
