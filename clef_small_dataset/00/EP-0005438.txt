<ABSTRACT>
a method and an apparatus for the ignition of pulverized  coal to furnish energy for warming-up or low load operation  of a coal burning furnace, the method comprising forming a  fuel stream consisting of a mixture of pulverized coal and air,  in a pipe (16), the fuel stream having an air-to-coal ratio  and/or a flow velocity which fluctuates.  the fluctuating fuel  stream is introduced into a combustion area where the coal  is ignited by an energy source (23).  the fluctuation of the  air-to-coal ratio and/or the fluctuation of the flow speed  provide for the air-to-coal ratio and or the flow speed to be  swept through a range of values which include the optimum  conditions for ignition.  
</ABSTRACT>
