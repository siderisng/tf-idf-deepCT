<ABSTRACT>
1.  process for the production of blowing agent concentrates from blowing agents and thermoplastic aromatic polycarbonates or abs polymers with blowing agent contents of 5 to 20 % by weight, based in each case on the total weight of the thermoplastic and blowing agent, characterized in that the production of the concentrates is carried out using the polycarbonates or abs polymers in powder form and with the addition of 0. 5 to 30 % by weight, based in each case on the total weight of polycarbonate or abs polymer and the additional compound, of an additional compound which has a softening temperature 20 degrees c to 200 degrees c lower than that of the polycarbonate or of the abs polymer and which consists of low molecular weight polyethylene and/or polycaprolactone. 
</ABSTRACT>
