<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to higher molecular weight phosphoric or phosphonic acidaryl esters prepared by reacting phosphoric acid or phosphonic acidaryl ester with formaldehyde or paraformaldehyde or trioxane.The method for producing these higher molecular phosphoric or phosphonic acidaryl esters is characterized in that phosphorus or phosphonic acidaryl esters may be achieved in an inert solvent at temperatures between about 10 ° C and 120 ° C in the presence of sulfuric acid with formaldehyde or paraformaldehyde or trioxanebecome.
