<ABSTRACT>
the tubular covering (10) is used for sleeving pipes or cable groups.  it has a rip closure (12) consisting of two profile parts (13, 14) which are arranged along a film sleeve (11, 17, 19) and hook together.  one profile part (13) is provided with a strap (20) covering the closed rip closure (12) from the inside, the strap having a means of adhesion (21) on its outside and being rigidly bondable to the inner side of the film sleeve (11, 17, 19) with its free end.  <image>
