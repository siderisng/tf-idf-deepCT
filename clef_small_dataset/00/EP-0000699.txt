<ABSTRACT>
The production of polyvinyl butyral is usually carried out by acetalization of polyvinyl alcohol with n-butyraldehyde, either in an organic solvent or even in water.By varying the reaction conditions can be achieved that polyvinyl butyralizes coarse-grained or finely divided.The recovery of polyvinyl butyral with improved properties is possible by using polyvinyl butyral with a content of 17 to 24 percent by weight of vinyl alcohol units as the starting material for aftertreatment in the presence of emulsifying organic sulfonic acid.The obtained polyvinyl butyral is particularly suitable for the production of composite films.
