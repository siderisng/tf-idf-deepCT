<ABSTRACT>
an internal combustion engine is disclosed which  includes a plurality of cylinders split into first and second  groups, and an intake passage (12) provided with a throttle  valve (16) and bifurcated downstream, of the throttle valve  (16) into two branches (12a, b) one communicated with the  first group of cylinders (1 - 3) and the other communicated  through a stop valve (20) with the second group of cylinders  (4-6).  the second group of cylinders (4-6) are bypassed by an  egr passage (22) provided therein with an egr valve (24).   control means is provided for causing the air valve (20) to  open a predetermined time after the egr valve (24) closes  when the engine operation is shifted from its low load condiÂ­ tion to a high load condition.
