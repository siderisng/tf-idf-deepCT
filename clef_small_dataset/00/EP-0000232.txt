<ABSTRACT>
Item is a method for producing blocked polyisocyanates by reacting polyisocyanates with optionally substituted imidazolines at 0 - 150 ° C.The polyisocyanates can be subjected to the reaction of a molecular magnification by chain extension, such as water, polyols or polyamines.Further device is also the new, imidazolines-blocked polyisocyanates, in particular those based on the isophorone diisocyanate.
