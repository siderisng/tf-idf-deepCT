<ABSTRACT>
A method for two-layer enamelation is described in a fire, wherein the email order takes place on metallic carriers in the electrotistical field.The specific electrical resistance of the basic enamel is to be between 10 clock and 10¹⁴ ohm.CM and that of the cover mix between 10¹⁴ and 10¹⁶ ohms.CM lie.Preferably, the difference in the surface tension of base and topmails at burnt temperatures is at least 0.050 N / m.Advantageously, basic emails are used with special maintenance of alkaline earth metal, zinc, manganese and iron contents.
