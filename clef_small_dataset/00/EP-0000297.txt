Ice Love, the type comprising an ice support (5,36) sliding substantially in rectilinear translation, guide members (2) and actuating members (3.6) of this support consisting of a rack (6)whose den ture (22) is provided on both arms of a generally U-shaped element, which is articulated in the region of the top of the U on said support (5,36) by a pin 24, by a motor pinion(9) fixed axis in connection with the rack (6) and by a fixed pivot (4) received in a longitudinal groove (23) of this rack.The consecutive gear of the motor gear (9) with the teeth (22) of the two arms (18) of the rack (6) makes it possible to use a rack whose height is about half of the stroke of the window.
