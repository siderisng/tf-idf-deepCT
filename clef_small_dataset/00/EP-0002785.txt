<ABSTRACT>
strips of amorphous metal containing embedded particles  of abrasive material are useful for working the surfaces  of solid articles by abrasion for forming or surface  improvement.  the method of making such strips involves  forcing molten metal (2) or a glass-forming alloy containing  admixed particulate matter onto the surface of a moving  chill body (1) under pressure through a slotted nozzle  (3, 4) located in close proximity to the surface of the chill  body.
