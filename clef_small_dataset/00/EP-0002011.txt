<ABSTRACT>
in a carbon black reactor (2), a quench  fluid containing combustible material is introduced through  conduits (32) or (26) in a generally  tangential direction into a combustion zone (22).   the combustion gases produced tangentially enter a connected  reaction zone (23).  the quench fluid serves to control  the temperature of the combustion gases before their  introduction into the reaction zone.  control means is provided  for controlling the quench fluid input in response to  the temperature of the combustion gases.
