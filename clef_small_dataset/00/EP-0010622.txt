<ABSTRACT>
Device for simultaneous thermographic recording analog curves and alphanumeri characters on a recording medium. Heat-sensitive registration paper (10) is guided via a profile body (12) and via a viewing plate (14). The profile body has two parallel to the transport direction of the registration paper transversal edges (22, 24) which limits a narrow plane surface (20). One of these edges (22) serves as a recording pad for the write pins (28, 30, 32) operated by means of heated, galvano metrically powered by means of the analog curves. The flat surface (20) serves as a pad in simultaneous printing of alphanumeric characters by means of a thermographic printhead (38) arranged above the surface. This has a series of selectively heatable elements Ten (40) which are arranged transversely to the paper transporor direction. The flat surface may be equipped with an elastic surface (44) at least in the area of ​​the printhead.
