<ABSTRACT>
The aqueous dispersion colors according to the invention contain UF-Weisspigmente with very characteristic physical properties.Compared to known aqueous dispersion paints, which in addition to TiO₂ only up to 20 wt.-% In total Weisspigment UF-Weisspigmente may contain, according to the invention, the TiO₂ can be replaced up to 100% by the UF-Weisspigment.The resulting paints are light and weather resistant and abrasive.
