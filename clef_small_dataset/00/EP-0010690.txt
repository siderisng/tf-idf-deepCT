<ABSTRACT>
Photopolymerizable mixtures for the production of water developable with water pressure and relief forms based on a photoinitiatature containing water-soluble or dispersible mixture of a) at least one water-soluble or dispersible polymers with polymerized N-vinylpyrroli Don units and / or one Polymers with vinylal carbon units and b) at least one olefinically unsaturated photopoly merizable monomers contain as an additive or as the sole polymer, as a serless modified vinyl alcohol polymer which is 5 to 75, and more preferably 10 to 60 wt. % chemically contains denser oxyethylene units. Thus modified vinyl alcohol polymers are particularly oxethylated polyvinyl alcohol or saponified graft polymers of vinyl esters on polyethylene glycols. The mixtures show improved processing to printing plates and relief forms and high resistance during storage.
