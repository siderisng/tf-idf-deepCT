<ABSTRACT>
Integrated filter circuit with a CTD transversal filter (1), which is followed by a scanning and holding stage (8).In such filters, a smoothing of the staircase gene signal output voltage emitted by said step is sought.The smoothing is done by means of a RC low pass (10), which is realized by a "switched Capacitor" circuit.This has two capacitors (C₁, C₂) and two alternately actuatable, electronic switches (105, 106).The application area includes in particular CTD transversal filters, which are set in systems with different clock frequencies.
