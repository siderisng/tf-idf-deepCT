<ABSTRACT>
Plastics can be prepared if at least one, sulfonic-acid group-containing compound in the presence of a hydroxy compound in an acidic concentrated aqueous solution with a water content of 20 to 30% with at least one aldehyde with stirring is reacted with or without heat.In the same way and using the same monomeric components, but the addition of a surfactant in a conventional for foam herstel lung dilution to the aldehyde, foam plastics can be produced in a conventional foaming system without prior polymerization of the monomeric reaction participants.
