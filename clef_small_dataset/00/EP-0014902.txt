The invention relates to a process for the preparation of needle-shaped, magnetic iron oxide, consisting of a core of gamma-iron (III) oxide and one, based on the total amount of magnetic material, 0.2 to 12% by weight of cobalt (II) and 0,1 to 15 weight percent iron (II) ion-containing ferrite shell, by applying a cobalt (II), iron (II) hydroxide cases on needle-shaped gamma-iron (III) oxide particles and heating to 100 to 200 Â° C and the useThe materials thus produced for the production of magnetic recording media.
