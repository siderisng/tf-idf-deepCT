<ABSTRACT>
the invention relates to a scale device for continuous sorting by weight.  the device comprises a balance arm 10 mounted to oscillate between two stops 17 and 21 about an axis 0, two levers 5 and 6 articulated about two axes 7 and 8, opposing draw springs carried by the levers 5 and 6 and stressing the balance arm on either side of its axis 0 under a constant tension, and means for adjusting the inclination of the levers 5 and 6 in a manner such as to vary the distance thereof from the axis 0.  application to the sorting of fruit and vegetables.  <image>
