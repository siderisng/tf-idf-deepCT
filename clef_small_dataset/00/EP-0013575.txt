<ABSTRACT>
the granulator for strap or filiform (22) synthetic material comprises a cutting cylinder (12), a pair of driven transport rollers (18, 20) located upstream and forming between them a slot (24), and a support (38) for the straps (22) and located before the rollers (18, 20).  the support (38) is located outside the plans which are tangential to both rollers (18, 20) in the region of the slot (24).  the slot (24) depends on the bigger diameter of the straps (22) of synthetic material and the rollers (18, 20) have a steel jacket.  a sheet iron guide (32) for the straps (22), which is arranged immediately before the second roller (20), has a curved configuration in the direction of the first roller (18).  this guide (32) is preceded by a ceramic pressing rod (36a) bearing against the straps (22) of synthetic material.
