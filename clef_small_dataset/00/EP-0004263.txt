<ABSTRACT>
coin separator for two types of coin of different diameter.  a limiting device retains all coins whose diameter is between the coin diameter values.  a coin deflector is arranged in the region of the real coin output and, in the quiescent state, is directed towards the chute for the smaller type of coin.  in the case of a larger coin, the coin deflector is displaced by this in the direction of the chute for the larger type of coin.  <image>
