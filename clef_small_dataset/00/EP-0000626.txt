<ABSTRACT>
the invention concerns a pull-type fastener-setting tool  comprising a pulling device (40) arranged to grip a pulling  stem of the fastener, the pulling device being movable to pull  the pulling stem by means of a rotatable cam (62) turned by a  rotatable handle (76).  the pulling device (40) having an arm  (80) pivoted thereon and carrying a cam follower (84) which  bears on the rotatable cam (62) the arm being pivotally connected  to a link (90) which guides the cam follower in alignment  with the pulling device.  the link is also pivotally connected  to the housing of the tool within a handle portion  thereof.
