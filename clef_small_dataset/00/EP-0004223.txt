<ABSTRACT>
Process for manufacturing synthetic capsules in lipid materials containing biologically active materials.A mixture of lipids are prepared in an organic solvent and an aqueous mixture of the material intended to be encapsulated, the mixture obtained is emulsified, the organic solvent is extracted and the resulting gel is suspended in water.Various kinds of biologically active materials can be encapsulated, such as drugs, bacteria, insecticides and the like.
