The polyester soluble in water contains phosphonic acid ester groups of the general for Mel wherein R = hydrogen, an aliphatic, cycloaliphatically, aromatic or araliphatic radical, and has a seemingly average molecular weight of 800 to 5000. It is prepared by 100 mol% Diocarboxylic acids and / or their derivatives and / or b) for polyester production and / or b) phosphonic acids and / or their derivatives and 80 to 140 mol% for the polyester production of suitable a) dialcohols, ether group-containing dialcohols and / or b) di-ester of Phosphonic acids and / or polyphodic sphonic acids with dialcohols, wherein the 180 to 240 mol% starting components contain 5 to 100 mol% of phosphonic acid groups and / or phosphonic acid ester groups are condensed in a conventional manner to a seemable mttthen molecular weight of 800 to 5000, wherein Instead of a molecule of two-baser Acid and a molecule of dialcohol can also be used two molecules of hydroxy acid.
