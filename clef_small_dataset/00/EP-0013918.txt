<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a circuit arrangement for the temperature-dependent voltage mode for piezoelectric writing nozzles in ink mosaic writing agents. For temperature-dependent power supply of the peizoelectric write nozzles operable via control circuits with an adjustable voltage Peizoelectric write nozzles in ink mosaic writing devices is a control circuit (16) for all control shells is provided for generating a temperature-dependent output voltage, at the output the individual control circuits (2) are connected in parallel;The individual partition of the operating voltage takes place via the control circuits upstream adjustable resistors (12).The invention is applied to inkmosaik write in motions.
