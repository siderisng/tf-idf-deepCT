<ABSTRACT>
open-ended pores of a porous granule are filled with  a liquid material which is at least partially immiscible with  water, and sealed with a porous polyurea membrane which  limits the release rate of the material into the surrounding  medium.  the membrane is formed by   (a) applying to the bare granule an organic solution  comprising the liquid material and an organic polyisocyanate,  followed by   (b) applying to the granule an aqueous solution  comprising water and a catalytic amount of a catalyst  selected from the group consisting of a basic organic  tertiary amine and an alkyl tin carboxylate.
