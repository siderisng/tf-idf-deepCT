<ABSTRACT>
The object of the application is an amplifier network operated as a proportion of a telecommunication device, alternatively operated amplifier network, whose feed takes place via the telecommunication connection line.The task of the application is to prevent a signaling in the switching operation on the amplifier network possible signaling of a connection state setting due to the load change occurring due to the leveling operation of the ver stronger operating point.To solve the problem, a variably shaped bare circuitry will be described, the purpose of which is neither the quick loading of the lock capacitor of the amplifier network or the supply of a load from the same for the telecommunication terminal line.
