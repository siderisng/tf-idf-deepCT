<ABSTRACT>
design of the bearing for the fan barrel of a cross-flow fan for use at high temperatures.  the rotatably mounted fan barrel is held by the end opposite to the drive motor in a thrust bearing which is formed from a thermoplastic which is dimensionally stable at high temperatures and is resistant to frictional wear.  the thrust bearing is formed by a pot-shaped bearing cap (5) which is inserted into a recess of a bearing bush (6).  the bearing cap (5) and the bearing bush (6) enclose a cavity which accommodates a lubricant packing (7).    <image>
