<ABSTRACT>
In the case of a luminous screen for particular flat picture devices, it is proposed to use on the screen glasses side depressions (2) in the luminous point grid and the phosphor (5) not only on the soils of the recesses (2), but to increase the luminous efficacy also on the dying to apply.A contrast margin layer (3) serves as an etching mask for the recesses (2) and as a mask for spraying the phosphor (5).
