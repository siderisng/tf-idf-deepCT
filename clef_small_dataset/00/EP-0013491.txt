<ABSTRACT>
dispersions of colloidal siliceous particles, preferably  silica, may be prepared in organic liquids by means of a  synergistic mixture of dispersing agents comprising a phos­ phorus oxy-acid derivative and a long-chain amine.  disper­ sions containing a high weight fraction of siliceous solids  having a lower viscosity than is normally achieved are poss­ ible.  dispersions useful as dental compositions are made,  using a polymerisable organic liquid either consisting of a  vinyl urethane or the reaction product of a bisphenol with a  glycidyl acetate.
