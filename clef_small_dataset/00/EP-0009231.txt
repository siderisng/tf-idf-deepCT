<ABSTRACT>
1.  a circuit arrangement for measuring the test diagonal voltage of a resistance bridge circuit by determination of a null, wherein the supply diagonals of a resistance bridge circuit (b1 ) and a second resistance bridge circuit (b2 ) that can be unbalanced at will are connected in parallel, characterized in that the test diagonals of resistance bridge circuit are connected in parallel, and a device (s, se) is provided for unbalancing the second resistance bridge circuit (b2 ) to eliminate a common test diagonal voltage, the degree of unbalance being a measure of the test diagonal voltage of the first resistance bridge circuit (b1 ).
