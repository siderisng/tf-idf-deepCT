<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an electrode comprising a valve metal substrate and an active surface without precious metals.This active surface comprises cobalt oxide and an oxygenated or hydrogenated compound of the titanium in which this metal has a valence less than 3. The electrode of the invention is intended in particular to be used as anode in the electrolysis of chloride.sodium.
