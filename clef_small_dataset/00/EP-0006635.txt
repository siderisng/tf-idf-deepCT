<ABSTRACT>
Methods for producing N-acylaminomethane phosphonic acids by a single-stage reaction of a nitrile with formaldehyde or formaldehyde cleaving compound and phosphorous acid or a compound forming under the reaction conditions phosphorous acid in elevated temperatures and given if in the presence of a solvent.As a solution, a polar aprotic solution means is preferably used.The reaction can advantageously be carried out in the Genewart of an acidic catalyst.Instead of the reaction partners Nitrile and formaldehyde, the products of their implementation, 1,3,5-triacyl triazans can also be used.
