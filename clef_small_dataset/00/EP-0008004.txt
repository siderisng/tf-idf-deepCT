<ABSTRACT>
1.  a lighting fitting for a tubular fluorescent lamp which comprises a housing and a trough-shaped mirror system arranged therein and which consists of a top mirror secured above the lamp in the housing and of two side mirrors, the side mirrors, together with cross bars which connect them, form an insert which is detachably held in the housing, characterized by at least two carriers (4) for holding the top mirror (3), which are fastened on the base (11) of the housing (1) and extend transversely to the longitudinal axis of the housing, each of said carriers having two abutment surfaces (41) for the edge strips (211) of the side mirrors (21) across which the insert (2), which is centrally secured in the housing (1) only at its front ends, is led.
