<ABSTRACT>
an electrohydrodynamic (ehd) exciter (3) is disclosed for  promoting the formation of droplets (26) from a column (1) of  electrically conductive fluid emitted under pressure from a  nozzle (2).  the exciter is composed of one, two, three or more  pump electroes (8) (9) (10) of a length equal to about one half  the droplet spacing (5).  multiple pump electrode embodiÂ­ ments are spaced at intervals of multiples of about one half  the droplet spacing or wavelength (5).  the exciter may be  used in an ink jet printing system of the type wherein selected  droplets (26) from a continuous stream of droplets are  charged and deflected toward a target.  the droplet placement  on the target is determined by the amount of charge.
