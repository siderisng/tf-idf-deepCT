<ABSTRACT>
a process for the treatment of incinerated sewage  sludge ash containing silicic compounds and metal values  including phosphorus and at least one precious metal  comprises leaching the ash in an acid solution in a first leach  to dissolve phosphorus.  the leach solution is separated from  the undissolved residue, which is then treated to recover at  least one precious metal.
