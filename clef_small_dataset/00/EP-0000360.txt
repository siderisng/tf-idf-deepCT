<ABSTRACT>
Polyurethane urea elastomers with improved temperature behavior, in particular low temperature properties, good mechanical properties over a large temperature range and excellent hydrolysis resistance are obtained by reaction a) of a cycloaliphatic NCO groups comprising prepolymers prepared from substantially difunctional polyhydroxy compounds and organic diisocyanates with at least one cycloaliphatically bondedNCO Group in the ratio of total hydroxyl: NCO groups of 1: 1.2 to 1: 10 with b) 3,3 ', 5,5'-tetramethyl-4,4'-diamino-diphenylmethane in molar treatment of 1: 0.8 to 1: 1.2.The polyurethane urea elastomers are suitable for making machine parts, rolls, rollers and sealing elements.
