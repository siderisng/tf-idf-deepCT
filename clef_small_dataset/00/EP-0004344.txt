<ABSTRACT>
A presentation stand for the sorted display of flat goods packages includes goods holders (14), which are attached to a vertical stator column (12) or a wall.The goods holders consist of horizontal curtail (18) with comb-like ironing machines (20), wherein the kragarme can also be formed as a profile rails (32), in which the brackets can be convertible and lockable in it.The respectively superimposed goods holders with the iron attached thereto are arranged so that superimposed bracks are crosswise or angled to each other.
