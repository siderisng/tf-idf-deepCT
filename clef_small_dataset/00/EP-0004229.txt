<ABSTRACT>
1.  device allowing to control and electronic alarm system, consisting of a relay fitted with flip-flop contacts, a reed switch and a permanent magnet, characterized in that the relay is of bistable type, unbiased, with a single so-called mechanical hook-up coil, controlled by the pulses emitted by this sole reed switch.
