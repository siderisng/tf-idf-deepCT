<ABSTRACT>
Circuit allowing to increase the stability of the sawtooth signal towards thermal drifts.It comprises a conventional generator (AI, CI, RI, II) whose output is controlled by a buckle buckling: a switch (I2) controlled in opposition with that (ii) by derivation on the integration capacity, alow-pass feed filter (R2, C2) determined to substantially select the continuous and an operational amplifier assembly (A2, R3, R4, R6) determining the gain of the loop, so that the loop produces a voltage (VO)) corresponding to the average value of the signal.Continuous voltages (VI, V2) and passive components (RI, R6, CI) are determined so that the average value of the sawtooth is preferably corresponding to the reference zero potential.The invention applies in particular to shooting cameras.
</ABSTRACT>
