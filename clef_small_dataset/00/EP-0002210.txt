<ABSTRACT>
disclosed are 6- and 6,6-disubstituted-2-substituted-pen-2-em-3-carboxylic  acids of the following structure:    wherein: r¹ and r² are independently selected from hydrogen,  -nhr1′ (r1′ is h or acyl), alkyl, alkoxyl, aralkyl, aryl,  heterocyclyl and heterocyclyl-alkyl; r³ is selected from  hydrogen, -r, -or, -sr, -nr₂; r is substituted and unsubstituted  alkyl, aryl, aralkyl, hetero-heterocyclyl, or heterocyclyllalkyl;  n is 0 or 1; when n=1, r³ is not -sr.  such compounds  and their pharmaceutically acceptable salt, and ester derivatives  are useful as antibiotics.  also disclosed are processes  for the preparation of such compounds, pharmaceutical  compositions comprising such compounds and methods of  treatment comprising administering such compounds and  compositions when an antibiotic effect is indicated.
