<ABSTRACT>
Process for the preparation of abrasives with at least two binder layers using, at least a aqueous resolse I) of monovalent phenol (molar ratio of phenol: formaldehyde 1: 1.1 to 2.5) by applying a first binder layer to a carrier material, after sprinkling of the abrasive grain, drying the coating layer in the horizontal drying plant up to the nucleut strength, then cooling with air flow, applying a second binder layer and as in first stage drying to the reel strength and then cooling, whereby heating and cooling in each case by means of transversely to the direction of the abrasive path slot nozzles and the final hardening after loose roll up by means of a conventional method in the roller, characterized in that the coated carrier material with speeds over 5 m / min by the sharply limited and individually regulated heating and cooling zones And thereby flush from an air flow at a speed of 5-200 m / s by the slot nozzle with air outlet temperatures of 20-300 ° C and the air is sucked out by adjacent suction nozzles.
</ABSTRACT>
