<TITLE>
method and device for supplying lubricant to a piston and cylinder arrangement.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
The method and means for lubricant to guide operate with introduction of the lubricant into a gap space (3b) between a, for example, tubular, deformation-soft sealing element of a piston-cylinder arrangement and a support surface within the working handsheet with comparatively low working pressure.As a result, a relatively low-lubricant feed pressure is sufficient.Advantageously, the resignation of lubricant is excluded into the supply by a return shaft valve assembly (8d, 15).Preferably, a check valve member (15) is arranged in the region of the lubricant feeding mouth (9), resulting in a provisional function with secure pressure maintenance in the lubricant space (3b).
</ABSTRACT>
