<ABSTRACT>
a copy reproduction machine is controlled by a digital  control system (15 and 16).  during operation of the machine,  fault conditions are logged into a non-volatile digital store  (175).  to diagnose errors, a scan of the store is made to  retrieve the logged fault conditions and these are displayed  on a display (64).  this scan is initiated by a control button  (62) which, when depressed, activates the maintainance  portion (16) of the control system.  the store (175) also  contains control words for controlling all possible operations  of the machine, including portions or features thereof which  may not be initially fitted.  access to these control words is  effected by reference to authorisation data, which permits or  prevents such access.  this authorisation data can be  amended, as portions or features of the machine are added  or removed, by the entry of suitable data through the  keyboard (57).
