<ABSTRACT>
In a single lenses (L₁-L₃) arranged by inguinal lenses (L₁-L₃), during the imaging projecting relative to the template (V) and the copy (K) movable exposure arrangement for copying devices, an intermediate image plane of the imaging system is disposed between image mask (B) whose apertures Shape and size of the image fields of the single lens arrangements Be Voices. In order to avoid the formation of over- or underexposed strips in the limit circuits of the frame fields in the case of tolerance-related mutual displacements, the field fields, the apertures have lateral boundaries, which are below a determination th angle (α) relative to the solder to the direction of movement (V) run. The apertures can be formed by triangles, diamonds, trapezoids or six corners or have a lateral limit curved, for example, in the form of a sin² or cos² curve.
