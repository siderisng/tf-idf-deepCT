<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method and apparatus for focal length determination of long-ferrous electron noptic lenses. According to the invention, an annular beam is held on the lens with the radius R in the cross-sectional beam. This beam can be hidden by pivoting a ring field panel (3) in the beam path between a condenser lens system (2) and the lens (4). By a hole panel (7) with a diameter, which is approximately equal to that of the error slip of the lens (4), the position of the rear focal plane is determined. in a reclining Z₂ behind the pinhole registration device (8), z. b. On a photo plate, the radius R of the ray bundle passed through the hole panel (7). The focal length F is determined according to the formula F = R / R Z₂. Due to the beam of beams used annular cross section, the error slimming decreases. As a result, the indeterminacy of the distance Z₂ and thus the error in the focal length determination. The method can be applied to micro-projectors for the combinationweight determination in the imaging lens.
