<ABSTRACT>
the process involves the preparation of modified, liquid or organic polyisocyanates by the free radical-initiated polymerisation of olefinically unsaturated monomers, the reaction medium used being a mixture of organic polyisocyanates containing from 1 to 25 equivalent- % of polyhydroxypolyethers in the molecular weight range from 300 to 10,000 and/or a mixture, produced from a mixture of this type, of urethane group-free organic polyisocyanates and nco prepolymers containing urethane groups.
