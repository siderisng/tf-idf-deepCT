<ABSTRACT>
1.  member for securing an elongate object (18) on to a bar (14), in particular a bar of roof-rack of a vehicle, perpendicularly to the bar, which is of an elastomer material and comprises a part in the form of a bifurcated member intended to receive the object and a part enabling it to be secured on the bar by gripping action, characterized in this that the member has in its de-mounted condition the form of a block of which each of the end portions is in the form of a bifurcated member (2a-2b) and of which the central part is adapted to be bent about the bar (14), constitutes the securing part on this bar, by partial surrounding of the said bar, and in this that the securing means (15, 16, 17) disposed with respect to the bar (14) on the same side as the object (18), connects the said end portions, which acts on the one hand to hold the central part in a bent condition and on the other hand to secure the member on to the bar (14).
