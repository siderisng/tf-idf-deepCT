<ABSTRACT>
in a timing circuit with an adjustable duration in a time region of seconds to minutes for the excitation of a circuit, the discharge current flows via the emitter-collector junction of a transistor (t3), the base of which is connected to the division point (tp) of a voltage divider circuit (r1, r2).  the area of applicability of the timing circuit is motor vehicle electronics, where the circuit can be used for automatically switching off a rear screen heater or also for the delayed switching off of the interior lighting.  <image>
