<TITLE>
method of making metal sheets for reflectors.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>
metallgesellschaft agde<sep>metallgesellschaft ag  <sep>metallgesellschaft agpostfach 10 15 01, reuterweg 14d-60015 frankfurtde<sep>metallgesellschaft ag  <sep>

</INVENTOR>
<ABSTRACT>
1.  a process of manufacturing reflector plates from aluminium or aluminium alloys, particularly for socalled three-band fluorescent tubes, comprising roughening at least one surface by rolling, chemical or electrochemical surface-smoothening, and anodizing, characterized in that irregular, furrowor crater-shaped depressions which are uniformly distributed throughout the surface in the statistical mean, are formed by means of a patterning roller, planoparallel surface portions amounting to 25 to 97% of the entire area are then formed by rolling over, and the chemical or electrochemical surface-smoothening is succeeded by anodizing to form a cover layer having a thickness below 5 x 10**-3 mm. 
</ABSTRACT>
