<ABSTRACT>
a vitreous material, for example silica, is deposited on  the inner surface of a tube (11) from the decomposition of a  volatile silicon compound in a hot intense inductive plasma  material by the application of radio frequency power from a  generator (17) via a coupling coil (15) and a matching coil  (16).  the use of such an inductive plasma feed with gas at a  pressure within the range of 0,1 to 50 torr, provides fusion of  the deposited silica on the inner surface of the tube, the outer  surface of the tube (11) being water cooled.  the process may  be employed in the production of preform tubes for step or  graded index optical fibre manufacture.
