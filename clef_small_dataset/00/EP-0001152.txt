<ABSTRACT>
Pallet conveyor for manufacturing systems, which are conveyed on the pallet (21) on a circular path.There are several translation points where pallets (21) are at right angles to the circular conveyor track and is passed away.In the center of the circular pallet conveyor is a pallet shift device (26), which is pivotable about the axis of the circular path into the various output points to guide the pallets (21) to the conveyor track and away from her.
