<ABSTRACT>
Continuous production of aqueous solutions of salts of alkandicarboxylic acids of 6 to 12 carbon atoms and alkandiamines having 6 to 12 carbon atoms by reacting the corresponding alkandicarboxylic acids with the respective alkandiamines in a weighing solution of the respective salt to be produced, wherein the aqueous saline solution from a first mixing zone of aPromotion zone and a second mixing zone feeds back between the first and second mishzone liquid alkandiamine and an aqueous solution of alkandicarboxylic acid, with the proviso that one supplies less than the equivalent amount of alkandiamine, after the second mixing zone admits the residual amount of liquid alkanediamineand from the first mixing zone aqueous salt solution to the dimensions as they are formed.The salts obtained according to the invention are used for the generation of polyamides.
