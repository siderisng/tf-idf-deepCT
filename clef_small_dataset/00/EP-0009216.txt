dry photoresist sheet composition and storable, dry, non-reticulated photopolymerizable photoresist sheet

<ABSTRACT>
the dry photoresist compositions are based on urethanated reaction products of 1,2-polyepoxides and olefinically unsaturated carboxylic acids as photopolymerisable component, customary photoinitiators, organic solvents and optionally customary assistants and are characterised in that the photopolymerisable component consists of a reaction product of   a) an epoxy-acrylate obtained by reaction of a diglycidyl ester of a hexahydrophthalic acid and acrylic or methacrylic acid or mixtures thereof in which 90 to 100% of the 1,2-epoxide groups are esterified with methacrylic or acrylic acid, and   b) toluylene diisocyanate or isophorone diisocyanate in which two gram equivalents of reaction product a) are reacted with 0. 5 to 0. 9 mol of diisocyanate.  this composition may be used to prepare a storable uncrosslinked photopolymerisable dry resist sheet on a plastic support sheet.
