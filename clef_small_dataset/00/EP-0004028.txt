<ABSTRACT>
Use of a regenerator to use the heat content of gases.So far, the heat content of gases containing corrosive ingredients and / or dust particles could not be used only at a low part or at all because the sensitivity of the waste heat boilers used herein for corroding substances and dust particles to frequent operating interruptions and a only incomplete use of the heat contentled.This disadvantage is corrected by the present invention thereby transmitted to the heat content of the corrosive components and / or dust particles containing gases using regenerators (2) to other gases containing no corrosive constituents and dust particles.As a storage mass for the regenerators (2), stones, preferably quartzite stones, or commercially available temperature-resistant fillers, such as rapid rings, can be used.
