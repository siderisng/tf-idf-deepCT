<ABSTRACT>
The control device (11) serves manually infinitely adjustable supply of electric power to electric power plates (12) in individual power pulses.It contains a switch actuated by a bimetal (19) switch (18), whose heat (20) is controlled by an electronic circuit and the heating supplies the current in individual half-waves.It is provided an anchor automatic with an electroni shen timer, which reduces the power supply to the heater (20) in a predetermined ratio during the anchor mode and thus increases the power supplied to the electric carbon disk.The access of the ankochematic is automatically made by the adjustment knob of the control device.By rotating the adjusting knob (24) via a mean power limit (20), the automatic arm is turned off.
