<ABSTRACT>
this circuit breaker has, between a mobile contact 11b and a fixed contact 11a, a hooking mechanism 13 capable of holding the mobile contact 11b in the closed position, and which, in order to free this mobile contact, is submitted by triggering, on the one hand, to an electrodynamic relay 32, and on the other hand, to an electromagnetic relay 31.  for a direction of displacement at least corresponding to the freeing of the mobile contact 11b, the mobile armature 34 of the electrodynamic relay 32 is connected, in terms of motion, to the mobile core 24 of the electromagnetic relay 31, this mobile armature 34 resting on a shoulder 25 of this mobile core 24.  <image>
