<TITLE>
adhesive laminate and method for its use.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

<ABSTRACT>
laminate sheet comprising a support at least one of whose faces is coated with an adhesive layer.  the support and the adhesive layer(s) are deformable when extended, allowing the sheet to adapt to curved outlines without its qualities being impaired.  such a laminate sheet is used to make surfaces of complex shape adhesive or to assemble different materials intended for the manufacture of articles by thermoforming.  <image>
