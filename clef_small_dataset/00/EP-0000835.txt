<ABSTRACT>
process for forming multi-component oxide catalysts.  according to the invention the key catalytic phase of the  catalyst is preformed and the elements of the host-catalyst  phase are added together or separately.  the key catalytic  phase contains a molybdate or a tungstate of bi, te, sb, sn cu  or mixtures thereof.  a representative catalyst is   50% [bi₂mo₃o₁₂]  1 / 2 [k0. 1ni2. 5co4. 5fe₃p0. 5mo10. 5  ox] + 50% sio₂ in the production of this catalyst a preformed bismuth  molybdate slurry is added to a host-catalyst slurry.  the mixture  was then dried, calcined, ground and calcined once  again.
