<ABSTRACT>
Extraiscopic feeding system for using two frequency ranges simultaneously and separately on an antenna.The system comprises a periscope (60) to four mirax, azimuth-axis movable, a separate device (30) of the two frequency ranges, and two available devices of excitation of the low frequency ranges and Hau te (40-50), which each retain a fixed position regardless of the movements of the periscope around the azimuth axis.
