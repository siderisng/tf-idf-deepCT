<ABSTRACT>
In a zigzag sewing machine with a plurality of control curves (15, 65), these are connectable by a selecting a direction (32) with transmission means for adjusting the overstitch width of the needle rod (57) and / or the fabric slider (80).For affordable relocation of the center of gravity of the machine, the control curves (15, 65) and the dialing device (32) are mounted on a joint in the base of the machine carrier (11).In order to achieve a crowded and easy design of the control agggra gate, the actuator (18) for controlling the feed size and direction of the fabric slide (80) is mounted co-axially to the control curves (15, 65).
