A method for regenerating a used in chemical machining metallic surfaces Ten, copper (II) chloride and / or iron (III) chloride contain the etching solution and a device for carrying out the method. The regeneration of the etching solution takes place in an electrolytic cell (2) which has a diaphragm between the anode (8) and cathode (9) a diaphragm, or an ion outer cherm membrane (10). The AZT solution flows through the electrolytic cell for the anodic oxidation of copper (I) and / or iron (II) ions formed in the etching of the metallic surface (5). In order to avoid a development of chlorine in regeneration, without causing the environment of the environment, toxically acting substances, in the anodes space (12) of the electrolysis cell are active in the etching solution active coal powder particles are suspended.
