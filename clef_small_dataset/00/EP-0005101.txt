<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a pressure regulating valve reigning in an enclosure (4) comprising a movable shutter member (2) between a closing position and an opening position, and a means (22) for a counterPressure on this element (2) to maintain it in its closed position when the pressure reigning in the enclosure is less than a given pressure.The means (22) is constituted by a pneumatic cylinder which acts on the shutter member by means of a bond comprising coupling means (30, 32, 34) capable of disapproving the cylinder (22) ofThe shutter member (2) in the event of a rapid increase in the pressure reigning in the enclosure (4).Application to the realization of valves for regulating and limiting at a given value the pressure in a speaker.
