<ABSTRACT>
New process for the preparation of high alkalinity dispersants for lubricating oils.New process for the preparation of dispersing detergents consisting - to sulfurize an alkylphenol in the presence of tulfonate alkylbenzene.b.not.low or zero, an alkaliner base and alkylene glycol, - to precarbonate the mixture thus obtained, - to overcalinize and carbonate the precarbonated mixture obtained, using an alkaline earthy, CO₂ and D base.An alkylene glycol, - and eliminate excess alkylene glycol.These detergents obtained can be used to improve the detergent-dispersing power of lubricating oils.
