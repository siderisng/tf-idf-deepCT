<ABSTRACT>
Pneumatic in particular with radial and reinforced top carcasse, in which the beads each comprise at least one rod (12) surmounted by a filling profile (14) in a strumped general section towards the flank, made of rubber mixture reinforced by short fibers.The strengthening fibers of the profile are, at least in part, oriented in the radial direction of the tire so that the mixture of the profile has an anisotropy ratio at least 2/1.
