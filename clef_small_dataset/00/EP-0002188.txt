<ABSTRACT>
1.  treadmill for the therapy and rehabilitation of persons hampered in walking, comprising a drivable endless running belt (1) extending around front and rear rollers and a safety frame consisting of vertical posts (3) and essentially horizontal rails (4, 4'), said safety frame resting on a housing (2) and said posts and rails being adjustable in the vertical direction, and comprising at least one shoulder support (5) adjustable in all directions, characterized in that upper rails (4) and lower rails (4') are provided, that said lower rails are adjustable vertically and horizontally independently of one another by means of adjusting and fixing means (8, 8') which are provided at the posts (3), and that said shoulder supports (5) are provided with rotatably mounted supporting pads (6).
