<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a lifting device, in particular to a muffle.This device is characterized in that the axis comprises at least one fixed rea (14) disposed on the outside of the screed (2) and which can cooperate, in the event of a descent use with the mobile rea (9) bythe intermediate of an indentation (15) carried out in the wing (4) of the screed (2) interposed between the mobile rea (9) and the fixed rea (14) and allowing the passage of the rope (13)The invention relates to the lifting devices used mainly for work performed on airlines.
