<ABSTRACT>
The control of the proper operation of a hydraulic servoverin is obtained by comparison with a signal referred to sensing the reel (VX) displacement of the cylinder rod (T) and a signal representative of the displacement that should theoretically (VX)Perform this stem (t) in line with the control signal (V).This last signal is provided by an electronic model (C5) simulating the servoverin.An alarm signal (AL) is transmitted when the difference between the two signals (Vx, V'X) exceeds the permissible values.The invention applies, inter alia, to the control simulators to the control in which an acceleration is communicated to a mobile platform by means of an arrangement of hydraulic servovérins.
