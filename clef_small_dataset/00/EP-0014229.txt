<ABSTRACT>
A self-healing electrical capacitor is proposed, in particular a capacitor intended for AC voltage-setting capacitor, which has a preferably made of plastic film having a moored covering layer, which is preferably made of alumi nium.To solve the problem of the field strength handler, a thin lining layer is used to contain a small cover layer which contains a small, evenly distributed proportion of at least one further metal from the group Al, Ni, Mg, Ti, HF, BE, Bi.By incorporating at least one of these additional metals into the covering layer, the formerly observed layer replacement is practically completely prevented.
