<ABSTRACT>
Hydrophobic polyurethane foams with densities of 8 to 25 g / liter, in which the number of closed cells 3 to 30% and the open cells is 97 to 70%, based on the total number of cells, are obtained from conventional output components for polyurethane foams under additionalUse of lipophilic compounds, in particular optionally substituted saturated and / or unsaturated aliphatic fatty acids with 10 to 25 carbon atoms and their esters and amides in certain amount ratios.The hydrophobic polyurethane foams are excellent for absorbing oil and / or optionally halogen-containing, hydrophobic solvents made of water.
