<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a process for preparing a fraction containing antioxygen substances from a vegetable material rich in these substances, the fraction thus obtained and its use to preserve food products and cosmetics of oxidation.The process is characterized in that the plant material has been mixed with shadow or an extract by a light solvent of this plant material, a distillation vector and an oil, which is suspended by a molecular distillation and thatCondensate containing the antioxygen principles is collected, the distillation vector and a portion of the oil implemented.
