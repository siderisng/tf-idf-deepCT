<ABSTRACT>
this invention concerns a closed cooling system (8) for  submersible working units such as an electric motor unit (1)  for a pump.  the cooling system contains for instance oil or water  and the circulation is obtained by a pump impeller (9)  arranged close to an outer surface (7) of the system which  surface is cooled from its opposite side by an outer passing  medium.
