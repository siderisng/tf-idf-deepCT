<ABSTRACT>
In a method for preparing metallorb surfaces for subsequent coating with organic coatings, such as paints, adhesives and plastics, a phosphate coating is applied by wetting the metal surface with a phosphating liquid containing at least 2-valuable cations and then dried the liquid film . Thus, layers are obtained, which are not toxic, but still distinguish from good corrosion protection as well as good adhesive and adhesion properties, who used the phosphoring liquids that have a pH of 1. 5 to 3.0 are chromium-free and in addition to metal phosphate soluble Molybdate, tungsten, vanadate, niobat and / or tantalate ions included. Furthermore, the phosphating liquid may contain fluoride, reducing agent, silica, organic polymer. The treatment method is particularly intended for the treatment of aluminum, preferably in sheet metal or band form.
