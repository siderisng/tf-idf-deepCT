<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an elastic high limit iron-based alloy, resistant to corrosion by seawater. The alloy contains in weight less than 0.15% carbon, less than 2% manganese, less1.5% silicon, less than 0.03% sulfur and phosphorus, from 34 to 40% nickel, from 16 to 21% chromium, from 6 to 18% cobalt, from 2 to 3.5% molybdenum, less than 0.25% aluminum, from 2.5 to 3.5% titanium, less than 2% tungsten, less than 0.015% boron, the rest with the exception of inevitable impurities,being made up of iron.The alloy is intended, in particular, to the manufacture of parts for submarine cables or for periscopes.
