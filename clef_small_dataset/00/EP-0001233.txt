<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to semipermeable membranes of copolymers consisting of copolymerized units of a) 99-50 wt.-% acrylonitrile b) 1 to 50 wt.-% of an acrylonitrile copolymerizable ethylenically unsaturated monomers containing one or two disulfide moieties, and C) optionally further olefinically unsaturated with acrylonitrile copolymerizable monomers or mixtures thereof.The semipermeable membranes are used in reverse osmosis and ultrafiltration and are characterized by a water permeability and a low partition limit.
