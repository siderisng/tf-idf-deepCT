<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the domain of the bustible object objects used in arming and in particular to that of the combustible sockets. The combustible objects according to the invention are green with a thin layer of organic binder varnish containing hollow microbeads, said varnish being based on a binder selected from the group consisting of chlorinated rubbings, polyvinylidene chloride, the polyvinylidene chloride, Cellulosic binders, glycerophthalic binders, poly urethane resins, polyepoxy resins. The varnish contains between 5 and 20% by weight, with respect to the dissolved polymer, and preferably approximately 10% of hollow microbeads and possibly between 2 and 15% by weight of an external feedstock with a superior self-inflammation point. at 200 ° C. The combustible objects according to the invention have a delay to self-inflammation if they are put in contact with a hot wall without alteration of their thermodynamic properties.
