<ABSTRACT>
the fluidising chamber (2) of a combustor is in thermal  contact with a surrounding heat exchanger (3).  a lined base  portion (6) of the chamber capable of containing the bulk of  bed material while the bed is slumped is adapted to retard  heat transfer from the bed.  thermostat linked devices in the  bed and external load are arranged to switch the fuel and gas  supplies to the bed so as to enable normal bed temperature  to be attained without excessive heat removal to the heat  exchanger.
