<ABSTRACT>
The automatic endzals of the switchwires is the prerequisite for automation of other transactions for assembly of switch cables.In this case, the cable (12) is attached to a motor shaft (7) so that its abandoned ends (13, 14) remain completely free.During rotation, two profiles (9, 10), on both sides of the cable rotation plane of the wire ends (13, 14), are approached, so that the wires are steered twice per revolution from the rotation plane and thereby excited to vibrations.
