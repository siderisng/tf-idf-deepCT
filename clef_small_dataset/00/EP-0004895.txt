<ABSTRACT>
The new dichlorobenzodixol derivatives of formula (I) were found in which R is alkyl, alkenyl, aralkyl, aryl, O-alkyl, O-alkeny, O-aralkyl or O-aryl.The new dichlorobenzodioxol derivatives are obtained when converting benzodioxol derivatives of the formula (Ia) with sulfuryl chloride.It has also been found that the new dichlorobenzodioxol derivatives of the formula (I) are synergistically influenced the effect of carbamates and / or carboxylic acid esters and / or phosphoric acid esters and / or haloalkanes.
