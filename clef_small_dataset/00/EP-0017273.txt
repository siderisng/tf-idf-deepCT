<ABSTRACT>
a device (10) for positioning a radiographic cassette (11)  comprises two mutually perpendicularly arranged stops (16,  17) against which one side edge and the front edge of the  cassette (11) is positioned.  in order to guarantee a correct  positioning, a continuous force is applied to the radiographic  cassette which is so directed as to urge the latter against at  least one stop.
