<ABSTRACT>
graft copolymers are disclosed in which the backbone  comprises a hydrocarbon polymer, such as an ethylene/  propylene copolymer and the grafted units are the residues  of a monomer system comprosing maleic acid or anhydride  and one or more other monomers, the grafts being postreacted  with a polyamino compound.  the graft copolymers  impart combined detergent and viscosity index properties to  lubricating oils and hydrocarbon motor fuels.
