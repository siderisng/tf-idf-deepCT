<ABSTRACT>
new anthracycline derivatives, 1-hydroxy-13-dihydro­ daunomycin and n-formyl-1-hydroxy-13-dihydrodaunomy­ cin are produced by microbial transformation of ε-pyr­ romycinone and ε-isorhodomycinone with daunomycin-­ producing streptomyces and their mutants.  the derivatives  herein are useful as cancer chemotherapeutic agents.
