<ABSTRACT>
A method of valorizing lead and zinc contained in the secondary purification sludge of the blast furnace gases.The method according to the invention consists in submitting said sludge to a granulometric classification so as to concentrate the lead and zinc contained in particular in the fine, and to recover said fraction containing the lead and the zinc, the other fraction which can be recycled toThe blast furnaces.Application: Preparation of supply loads of the Waelz-Berzelius furnace.
