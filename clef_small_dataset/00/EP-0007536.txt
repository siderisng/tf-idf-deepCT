<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method and a transmission assembly for granulating a molten metal to powder (23).A casting beam (18) is atomized in a closed nen container of a gas beam (22) directed on the casting beam (22) with a cross-sectional cross-section and thrown out of the granulation part of the container on a parabolic web into the powder compartment part of the container.Further, an auxiliary beam (26) is generated, which has substantially the same flow direction as the channel-shaped gas beam (22).This auxiliary beam is directed so that it meets the gutter-shaped beam (22) and the casting beam (18).The granular part of the arrangement is provided with a casting box (5) and with one or more nozzles (19) forming the atomizing, Rin nen-shaped gas rays (22).Further, it contains one or more nozzles (20) which generate the auxiliary beam EN (26).
