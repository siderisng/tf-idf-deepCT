<ABSTRACT>
a supported titanium catalyst component for use in  propylene polymerization is prepared by copulverizing a  mixture of magnesium dichloride and titanium teterachlcoride  ethylbenzoate 1:1 adduct, and then treating the resulting  titanium containing solid with n-heptane solution of hexachloroethane  in slurry state.  the titanium catalyst component thus treated has larger  surface area and pore volume than untreated one and gives a  characteristic x-ray diffraction pattern.  the supported titanium  catalyst with organo aluminum and ethylbenzoate as  cocatalyst gives high catalyst efficiency and heptane insolubles  in polymerization of propylene.  
</ABSTRACT>
