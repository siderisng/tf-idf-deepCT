<ABSTRACT>
In a method of galvanizing wire, the wire (4) occurs perpendicularly from a zinc bath (1).The adherent liquid zinc forms the zinc layer after its solidification.For the purpose of magnification of the layer thickness of the adhering zinc, the surface of the wire is cooled strongly immediately after its escape from the zinc bath by a cooling medium, preferably liquid nitrogen, highly cooled.
