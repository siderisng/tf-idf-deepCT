<TITLE>
sporting device for conveying a person over the ground.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

<ABSTRACT>
the sporting device, such as a grass-ski, is intended  for attachment to a person's foot, or to be ridden on, for  conveying a person over the ground, and comprises a gui­ de forming an endless track, a support (14) connected to  the guide for supporting a person's foot or the body of a  person, and an endless ground engaging device (16, 17)  mounted on the guide and moveable along the track in or­ der to propel the sporting device along the ground.  the  guide comprises first and second track members (11, 12)  forming respectively upper and lower runs of the track,  and a removable connecting member (13) forming part of  the track and interconnecting the first and second track  members (11, 12).
