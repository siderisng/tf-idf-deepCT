<ABSTRACT>
a tube guide and guide pulling arrangement for guiding  tubes into apertures through the tube sheet of a steam  generator including a tool for extracting said guide once said  tube is properly positioned, said arrangement including a  tube guide having a conical leading end and a cylindrical  trailing end adapted to be disposed within the front ends of a  tube, wherein a stem portion (28) of reduced cross section  between the tip (30) of said guide and its trailing end (22),  and said tool comprises an extracting member (10) pivotally  supported on a tool-holder arm (43) and having a hook  portion (66) with a notch (67) to thereby define a pair of  spaced, identical configured hooks (68, 70) adapted to receive  the stem (28) of said tube guide (20) for extraction of said  tube guide (20) from a tube.
