<ABSTRACT>
Method for cleaning raw caprolactam obtained by rearresting cyclohexano oxime in the gas phase on boron trioxide-containing catalysts, wherein one from a melt, the 100 parts by weight of the raw scaprolactam, 5 to 30 parts by weight of primary alkanols or fatty acids, which a carbon chain with 6 to 10Having carbon atoms and having alkyl radicals having 1 to 5 carbate Toff atoms as substituents, as well as 0 to 5 parts by weight of water, crystallized by cooling caprolactam and separates from the mother liquor.As a result, boric acid, uncomfortably smelling and high UV numbers contaminants are easily and effectively separated.For example, 5.6 parts by weight 2-ethyl hexanol (1) and 3.1 parts by weight of water are used on 100 parts by weight of the rawcaprolactam 17.6 parts by weight.
