<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for the gentle work-up of waste consisting of reaction products containing isocyanate groups containing organic compound with alcohols and / or water.It can be used on the mechanically crushed waste in a closed reactor at temperatures of 120 to 200 ° C, with respect to their weight, at least 20 wt.% Ethylene oxide and / or propylene oxide act and then removes unreacted alkylene oxide.The meshed wastes can be in a polyol in amounts of 10 to 50 wt.% Dispersed and reacted in this form with alkylene oxide.Homogeneous liquid moistening products are obtained which can be set to the polyurethane foaming polyols determined for reaction with isocyanates.
