<TITLE>
keypad for telephone.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
keypad for telephone of the kind consisting of a two-part  case, one of these parts 1 comprising sockets 4a receiving  the keys 4, the latter being arranged so that each of them  causes two contacts fitted onto a substrate 10 to be closed  simultaneously, these contacts being installed by means of a  number of modular supports 11 each consisting of two  independent elements 15 and 22 which bear wafers 16 and  23 respectively, the keys 4 comprising two opposite protuberances  14 for the simultaneous closure of two contacts,  said keys comprising internal means to restore them to their  idle position, said means being formed with a pushing  element 26 having an elastic action and reacting against the  substrate 10.
