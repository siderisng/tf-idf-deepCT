<ABSTRACT>
For the production of composite membranes consisting of a porous support membrane and a thin separating membrane, the separating membrane is first prepared on a soluble carrier film.The carrier film is then placed on the support membrane coated with the separation membrane coated side and subsequently dissolved in a suitable solvent.
