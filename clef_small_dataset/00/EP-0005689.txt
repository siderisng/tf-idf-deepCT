<ABSTRACT>
New lactam-n-acetic acids and their amides, their preparation processes and therapeutic compositions.These compounds meet the formula in which R₁ and R₂ = H, alkyl, aryl or halogen-substituted aryl;R₃ = Oh or -nr₄r₅, R₄ and R₅ = H, C₁-C₄ alkyl, cycloalkyl, aralkyl or nitrogen heterocycle;M = 3, 4 or 5 and N = O, 1 or 2. The alpha- (hydroxyalkyl) acids -lactam-n-acetics and their amides (n = 1 or 2) are prepared by reaction of an alkaline metal derivativea lactam with an alphabromolactone, followed by hydrolysis, respectively of the aminolysis of the lactone obtained;Alpha-hydroxy-lactam-n-acetic acids (n = O) are obtained by reaction of glyoxylic acid with lactam;The corresponding amides by condensation of acids or their esters with a nitrogen compound.These compounds include an activity on the mneetic processes and cardiac activity.
