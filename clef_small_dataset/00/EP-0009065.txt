<ABSTRACT>
a method for taking a stereoscopic photograph with  a camera wherein a lenticular screen (11) and photosensi­ tive film (12) are exposed to an object field through a wide  effective aperture (16), the effective aperture size being  controlled so that the image of a point on each of the near­ est foreground object (a) and the farthest background  object (b) in the object field is confined with a region (p)  of the lenticular screen which is not greater than 20 or pref­ erably 10 lenticules in width.
