<ABSTRACT>
Dynamic audio video interconnection system for connecting at least one TV and several peripheral apps.The system S is constantly connected to a TV TV and peripherals P1, P2, P5, P23, P26.By a CT remote control keypad, the user sends interconnect orders that are managed by a micro-pro MP cessor to operate electronic switches that perform the desired interconnection.Application to complex domestic audio-visual installations.
