<ABSTRACT>
a gas panel arrangement for storing, displaying and  selectively shifting information from one place therein to  another includes a panel filled with an illuminable gas, and  horizontal and vertical parallel conductor arrays disposed on  opposite sides of the panel, the coordinate intersections of  the conductors defining gaseous discharge cells which are  grouped into rows and columns of character generating matrices.   the vertical conductors are sequentially driven by a  multiphase drive system (ø₁, ø₂, ø₃,), and the panel includes a  plurality of data lines (d₁, d₂, d₉ . . . .  d₃₇) for entering information,  there being one data line for each four adjacent character  positions.  the data entered in each row may be sequentially  shifted left or right in a horizontal direction, the arrangement  providing left or right shifting of one character row while  holding stationary the display of the other character rows.
