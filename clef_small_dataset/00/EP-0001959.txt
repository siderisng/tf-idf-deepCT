<ABSTRACT>
Inviolability device making manifest the first opening of a screwed capsule container.This device consists of an extension of the neck (2) in the shape of a blind tube (5).This blind tube (5) is broken during the first opening and remains in the capsule (3).This device will be particularly used in fabric cations of flexible tubes for pharmaceutical or dietary products.
