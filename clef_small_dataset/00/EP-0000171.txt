<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a novel process for the preparation of carboxylate, amide and optionally urethane groups having polyisocyanate polyaddition products by reacting polyhydroxyl-Ver bonds with tricarboxylic acid monoanhydrides or tetracarboxylic acid monoanhydrides, part neutralization of the carboxyl groups present in the reaction product, implementing the part-neutralization product with excess polyisoyanates to NCOPrepolymers and their terminal chain extension, as well as the compounds available after this method.
