<ABSTRACT>
Printers on the fly to rotating print disc.Logic circuits respectively control the excitation circuit (21) of the carriage motor (8) and the excitation circuit (22) of the engine (3) of the disc (2) so that a chosen character of the disc (2)Normally arrives in print position at the same time as the carriage (1) on which the disk (2) is mounted.If this is not the case, the carriage (1) is brought back to the preceding print position while the disc (2) continues to turn to the chosen character where it stops upon the arrival of the character.position.The carriage (1) is then delivered to the next position.Device that can be used in particular in the disc printers on the fly, whose trolley is placed at different speeds and whose hammer has several typical forces.
