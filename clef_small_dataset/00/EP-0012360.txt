Charger for surgical purposes with a disc (1) in which a slot (7) is formed, which connects a central axial passage (5) for passing a thread with the disc circumference and that of a clamping means (2) for clamping the thread inthe passage (5) is closed.The clamping means consists of a disc segment (2), which is parallel to the shit via the slot (7) to a locked end position movable, in which its inner edge part (12) is the pass (5).As a result, the thread is clamped anti-pressurized by squeezing and friction.
