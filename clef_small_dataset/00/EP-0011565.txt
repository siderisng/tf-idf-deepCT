<ABSTRACT>
photographic elements are described wherein ballasted  n-alkyl- or n-aryl-benzisoxazolone compounds are used to  scavenge oxidized electron transfer agents in negative-­ working, color image transfer elements.  the scavenger com­ pounds are located in an interlayer between photosensitive  silver halide emulsion layers.  the compounds have the for­ mula:    wherein   r is a substituted or unsubstituted alkyl or aryl group,  preferably an alkyl group of from 1 to 20 carbon atoms or an  aryl group of from 6 to 10 carbon atoms, or ball;   ball is at least one organic radical of such molecular size  and configuration as to render said compound nondiffusible  in the photographic element during development in an  alkaline processing composition; and   n is a positive integer of 1 to 3, with the proviso that when  n is 1, then r is ball.
