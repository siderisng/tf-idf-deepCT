<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a device and method for manufacturing unit members of agglomerated powder.The device comprises an enclosure (10) of isostatic compresses, and molds (11) of elastic material, substantially constant thickness having a barrel form comprising a metal axial core (22) and a removable cap (13)closing their open end.The invention relates to the manufacture of unit elements in, agglomerated powder, for example unit loadings for ammunition.
