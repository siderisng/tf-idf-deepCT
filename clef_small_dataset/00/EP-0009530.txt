<ABSTRACT>
an atrial lead assembly (55) is provided which is adapted  for curving into position within the atrium, the lead having a  flexible length without an preset curve, and comprising  stylet means (58,59) for introducing a curve into the distal end  of the lead.  the stylet (58,59) enables the operator to set the  axis of the lead along a curvilinear path adjustable by the  operator.  the lead has a tip (56) of a continuous helical type  adapted to be rotated into contact with the trabeculae, the  lead being rotatable about the stylet while the stylet maintains  the lead axis in a desired position, whereby the tip is caused to  engage the trabeculae.  after such engagement, the stylet can  be removed, leaving the lead tip in good fixation with the atrial  wall.  
</ABSTRACT>
