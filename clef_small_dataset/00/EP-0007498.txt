<ABSTRACT>
New α-haloalkylcarbamic acid halides and a method for producing α-haloalkylcarbamide acid halides by reacting alkenyl isocyanates with hydrogen halide.The α-haloalkylcarbamic acid halides prepared by the process of the invention are valuable starting materials for the preparation of lacquer raw materials, tex til coating agents, dyes, pharmaceutica and pesticides.
