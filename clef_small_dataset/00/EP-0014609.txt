<ABSTRACT>
The image to be visualized is formed in a small liquid crystal cell and then is projected by an optical device on a screen.The invention consists in dividing this image into a set of pieces which each inscribed in separate crystal cells (6-7).These image fragments are projected by a set of lenses (2-3) on a screen (1) where they restore the complete image.The cells are of the type functioning by reflection, and to illuminate a transparent plate (5) forming a light guide and on which networks (8-9) have been spared (8-9) for extracting the light to illuminate the cells.The invention makes it possible to produce low thick visualization devices.
