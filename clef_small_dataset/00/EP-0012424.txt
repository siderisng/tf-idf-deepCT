<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for the preparation of β-isobutyrylaminoCrotonic acid amide by setting β-aminoCrotonic acid amide with dimethyl kets in liquid or gaseous state. The reaction is carried out without external heat supply at temperatures in the area of ​​room temperature up to 70 ° C. As a dispersion medium, an organic solvent having a boiling point is used above 40 ° C, which contain up to 0.1 mol of water and 0.2 to 2 moles of a carbon acid with 1 to 4 C atoms, each moles used β-amino crotonic acid amide can. In the case of undiluted addition of the dimethyl ketting, a protective gas atmosphere is worked under oxygen exclusion in ge gene. The β-isobutyrylaminoCrotonic acid amide falls in the form of two stereoisomers (Z and E-shape), which differ in terms of melting point and solubility in organic solutions. In case of heating from 40 ° C, the e-shape is converted into the z-shape.
