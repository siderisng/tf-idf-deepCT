<ABSTRACT>
According to the invention, it is determined, on the one hand, the rate of carbon dioxide in the exhaust gas and, on the other hand, the rate of carbon dioxide in an area (5) of the admission manifold.Downstream of the point (4a), (4b) of introducing recirculated gases, the ratio of these two parameters providing an exact value of the recirculation rate of the escape gases.Application to the control of recirculating devices equipping internal combustion engines.
