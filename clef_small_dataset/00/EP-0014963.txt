<ABSTRACT>
Preparation of Bicyclic Enol ethers (I) (N = 3-12; R¹, R², R³ = H, C₁-C₄-alkyl) by radical addition of Chr¹ = CR²-Chr³-O-R⁴ (III, R⁴ = Tert. -Butyl; tetrahydrofuran-2-yl; tetrahydropyran-2-yl) to a cyclic ketone (IV) and subsequent acidic catalyzed cyclization.New compounds II I and II are intermediates for the synthesis of musk-like fragrances.
