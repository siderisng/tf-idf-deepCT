<ABSTRACT>
Oxymethylene polymers (POM) are obtained in granular form by a POM solution or dispersion whose temperature is above the sintering temperature of the POM is cooled to a temperature just below the sintering temperature of the POM and extracted from the resulting suspension only such POM particleswhich have a corridor diameter of more than 70 μm.Suitable solutions or dispersing agents as well as precipitation and coolant serves a mixture of methanol and water with a methanol content of at least 75% by weight.The obtained POM is suitable as a technical material for producing molded parts with improved mechanical properties.
