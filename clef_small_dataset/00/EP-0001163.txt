<ABSTRACT>
linkage mechanisms for industrial manipulators are disclosed  which are based on so-called straight line mechanisms.   the preferred straight line mechanism is of the conchoid  type.  the linkage mechanisms may be employed to  move functional elements of industrial manipulators in  straight lines without the use of lead screws or revolute  jointed arms.
