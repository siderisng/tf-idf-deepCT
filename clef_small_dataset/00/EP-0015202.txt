<ABSTRACT>
It is a seal (10) which, for the molding of an ophthalmic or optical lens in organic matter, comprises, axially remotely one of the other, at its internal periphery, two edges (16a,16b) By which it is fit to cooperate in sealing with two molding shells.According to the invention, the edge (16a) is formed at the end of an elastically deformable annular flange (18a) which extends generally in bias vis-à-vis the axis A of the seal, this flange.Processing not only on the corresponding transverse face (19a) thereof, but also on its internal peripheral face (15).Application to the molding of ophthalmic or optical lentils in organic matter.
