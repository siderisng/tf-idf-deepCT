<ABSTRACT>
In the direct reduction of iron oxide-containing mate rials in the rotary kiln using solid, coal material-containing reducing agents with a high content of volatile constituents into the feed end of the rotary kiln, a reoxidation of the sponge iron in the final part of the reduction zone takes place, if there is not a certain excess of solid reducing agent therestill exists.To reduce the required excess and yet safe avoidance of the reoxidation are introduced into the final part, starting with at least 75% metalli sampling, reducing gases and / or liquid carbide materials by means of nozzle sticks at least partially inserted into the feedthrough via nozzle lenses.
