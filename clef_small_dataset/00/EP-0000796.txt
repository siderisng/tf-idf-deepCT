coating composition and process for coating metallic surfaces.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
a coating composition contains an epoxy resin, an alkylated melamine- formaldehyde resin, an acid catalyst, a block copolymer of ethylene oxide and propylene oxide and water.  the composition is applied to a clean metal surface e. g.  ferrous or tin by any conventional technique and cured to provide a protective, paint-receptive coating.  it is especially useful for blackplate or tin- plated cans.
