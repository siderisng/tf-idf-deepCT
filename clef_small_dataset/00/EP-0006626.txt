<ABSTRACT>
A radiation-sensitive mixture will be described (a) a compound and (b), in particular UV light, acid-forming of actinic radiation, and (b) containing a compound containing at least one N-acylminocarbonate group and their solubility in a liquid developer through actionincreased by acid.The N-acylminocarbonate is preferably a compound of general formula I in the R₁ to a n-valve aliphatic, cycloaliphatic or aromatic radical, R₂ and R₃ alkyl or aryl radicals, wherein two of the radicals R₁, R₂ and R₃ are connected to a heterocyclic ringcan be, and n is an integer from 1 to 3.The mixture is illustratively irradiated in the form of a radiation-sensitive layer and developed by washing out irradiated locations with a developer to a positive relief image.
