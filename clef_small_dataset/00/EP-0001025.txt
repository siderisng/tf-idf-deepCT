<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a bagging machine for the conditioning of powdery products. This bagger is characterized in that it comes to take a post (2) for the input and opening of the bags (5) of the sucking type connected to a vacuum source, a station (3) filling bags comprising a chute (10) feeding the product, connected to a dosing machine and / or weighing, means for opening and closing the hole, means for introducing this orifice into the open mouth of the bags and to apply the lips of these Inactively in contact with said chute, MOVs (11) to compact the product being filled with wise bags and means (13 to 15) to suck the air contained inside the bags being filled , a post (4) for closing bags by welding and / or seafrust, means (6, 18) retention of bags by suspension and transfer successively from one station to another, and means for synchronizing and chain To tomatically the various movements and operations in the different positions. Application to packaging in bags of powdery products.
