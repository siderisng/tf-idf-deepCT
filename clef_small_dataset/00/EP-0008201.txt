<ABSTRACT>
a ferrous wire having a homogeneous coating deposi­ ted thereon, which coating comprises from 0. 3 to 6 per  cent by weight of nickel, from 50 to 75 per cent by weight  of copper and from 50 to 25 per cent by weight of zinc.   the homogeneous coating is applied in an amount of from  0. 25 to 16. 0 g/kg of the combined ferrous wire and coat­ ing.  the coated ferrous wire is adapted to provide increas­ ed and superior precured humidity-aged adhesion and  postcured steam-aged adhesion to rubber-based products  such as tires, hose and belts.
