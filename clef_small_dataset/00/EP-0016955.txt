<ABSTRACT>
A method of voltage measurement on charged, dry-storable lead acid conumulators and for controlling the correct installation state of the electrodes provides, only the lattice frame surfaces of the plate block, which have sufficient traces of active mass by adding sufficient traces to bridge electrolyte conductive.For this purpose, a brush-like instrument (1) serving whose bristles consist of capillaries (3) and communicate via an electrolyte container (2).In an already covered multicacle battery (8), each plate block through the lid openings through the capillaries each of an instrument (1) supported with holder (7) over the lid opening is contacted on its grating frame (9).The otherwise only after filling with the electrolyte batten Riespannung is thereby measured on the final poles.
