<ABSTRACT>
1.  a circuit arrangement for reducing the recovery time of a thyristor (1) to which a rectifier (2) is connected anit-parallel, characterized in that the rectifier is further thyristor (2) and an rc-element (3, 4) is connected in parallel to the latter in such manner that a terminal of the resistor (4) is connected to the anode and a terminal of the capacitor (3) is connected to the cathode, and that there is provided a diode (5) whose anode is connected to the junction point of the resistor (4) and the capacitor (3) and whose cathode is connected to the control terminal of the further thyristor (2).
