<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a static memory element with random access.This element powered by a constant current T₁ is two complementary bipolar transistors T₂ and T₃ forming a structure p n p n or n p n p, with a side transistor and a transverse transistor.The current source T₁ is connected to a transmitter P₁ from the lateral transistor away from its P₂ manifold.This transistor comprises a second transmitter P₃ close to this collector, this second transmitter receiving the control voltage capable of switching the set.This element specially applies to random access memories.
