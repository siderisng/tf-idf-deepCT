<ABSTRACT>
a device for rotating the nuts (3) a plurality of screw bolts (2) arranged on a punch circle contains clamping elements and attached thereto axes of rotation (14), which each drive a pinion (15), which via one of the screw bolt (2)surrounding drive bush (17) transmits torque to the nut (3).In order for the torque to be transmitted evenly without the use of an outer teeth on the nut (3), the nut (3) is connected to a coupling half (29) of a positive coupling coupling in the axial direction, the other coupling half (21) of the drive bushing (17), on which they, secured against rotation, is arranged longitudinally movably.The drive bush (17) carries on the end of the nut (3) to the end an outer toothing (16) into which the pinion (15) of the rotary aggate engages.
