<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to novel fluorosubstituted phenylbenzyloxycarbonyl derivatives of formula I in which R¹ is hydrogen, cyan or ethinyl and R² for the residue where R³ and R⁴ are the same and stand for chlorine, bromine or methyl or represents the remainder, wherein R⁵ is optionallyby halogen, alkyl, alkyl thio or alkoxy with 1-4 carbon atoms, nitro or methylenedioxy substituted phenyl ring.These compounds are characterized by strong insecticides and acaricidal effectiveness.They will obtain, when a) carbonyl halides of the formula II HAL-CO-R² (ii) is reacted with fluorosubstituted phenoxybenzyl alcohols, or when b) converted carboxyl derivatives of the formula IV HO-CO-R 2 (IV) with fluorosubstituted phenoxybenzyl halides.
