<ABSTRACT>
1.  a plastic door (1), especially for rail and road vehicle, which is provided in the region of its borders with a reinforcing frame (2), consisting of metal profiles, and an encasement (3) which encompasses this frame (2), is made foamed plastic, forms the leaf of the door (1) and contains all the necessary recesses for windows, handles etc. , characterized in that two adjacent elements (2a) of the surrounding reinforcing frame (2) are in each case removably connected together in the sense of an extension compensation.
