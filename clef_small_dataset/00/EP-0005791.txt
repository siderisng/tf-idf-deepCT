<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a storage device for dental handpieces.The storage device contains for each handpiece (3) a sleeve or annular support member (2) and a switching device with switching means (7, 8, 10, 11) in the region of the support part (2), which when removing or filing the handpiece (3) be operated from the or in the holder part by the handpiece.As a switching means, the invention provides contactless responsive switching elements in the form of light barriers (7, 7a, 8, 8a) or approximate switches (10, 11).
