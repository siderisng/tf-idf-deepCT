<ABSTRACT>
in such a reel, the expansion mandrel consists essentially of expansion mandrel segments (2) which are radially adjustable by an expansion mandrel shaft (1) via a wedge gear (3).  in order to obtain a gap-free surface of the expansion mandrel in the expanded state, the expansion mandrel segments (2) are resiliently enclosed by a slotted spring steel envelope (4), the spring steel envelope (4) being connected so as to be fixed in displacement with an expansion mandrel segment (2) in the region of a slotted edge (5).  the circumferential length of the spring steel envelope (4) corresponds to the maximum expansion width of the circumference of the expansion mandrel.  <image>
