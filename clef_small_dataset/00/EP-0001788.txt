<ABSTRACT>
1.  method of using a thermoplastic composition for the manufacture of medical apparatuses and instruments, which come into contact with parenteral liquids, characterized by said thermoplastic composition consisting of from 50 to 99. 5 parts by weight of chlorinated polyolefin having a chlorine content of from 15 to 45% by weight and an average molecular weight of from 30,000 to 300,000 and of from 0. 5 to 50 parts by weight of polymers of acrylic and/or methacrylic acid esters.
