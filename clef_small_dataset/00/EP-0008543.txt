<ABSTRACT>
this invention is a video disc which has fairly uniform  optical transmission characteristics so that a reading beam  can enter the disc, be reflected from the information track and  leave the disc with little distortion.  this is achieved by injec­ tion moulding a composition of polymethyl-methacrylate  with acrylic rubber, with an extended drying time before heat­ ing and injection moulding at a temperature between 266 and  298°c with a moulding cycle between 0. 5 and 1. 5 seconds.   the result is a fairly uniform birefringence (characteristic of  light retardation and distortion) of about 5 nano meters.  the  invention covers the moulding composition, the method of  moulding, and the clear plastic sheet or video disc so pro­ duced.
