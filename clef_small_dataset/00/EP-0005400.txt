<ABSTRACT>
Mercaptan synthesis by the photochemical reaction of an olefin with hydrogen sulphoreous e R presence of an initiator.The initiator is constituted by benzophenone, thiobenzophenone or by a mixture of both, preferably supplemented by an organic compound of a group element ranges from the periodic classification.These initiators can wear substituents in one or both benzene nuclei.The light used can have a wavelength of 300 to 600 nm.The process allows the obtaining of different mercaptans with good yields.
