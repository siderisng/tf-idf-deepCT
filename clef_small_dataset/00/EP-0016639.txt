<ABSTRACT>
abrasion testing, and hence particle content of fluids  including liquids and gases is carried out by abrading a thin  film (2, 25, 27) of an electrical conductor with the fluid, and  measuring the resulting change in electrical resistance of the  film.  the method can be used to monitor metal particles in oil,  and hence the state of wear of oil-lubricated metal parts, and  the level of particulate pollution in the atmosphere.
