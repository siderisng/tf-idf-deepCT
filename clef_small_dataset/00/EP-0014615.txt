<ABSTRACT>
It is a process for the heat transfer decal, the pattern to be printed at the substrate (10) to be decorated with the aid of sublimable inks on a flexible support film (15) and the latter being applied to saidsubstrate (10) in a vacuum box (20) to which it is associated with the heating means (38).According to the invention, the closure of the vacuum box (20) is closed by the flexible bracket (15).Application in particular to the decoration of a face of glasses mount.
