<TITLE>
process and apparatus for mashing and conditioning green plants
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

<ABSTRACT>
Process for fractionation of green plants, such as alfalfa, and pulp conservation allowing: - to collect the juice of the pulp separately to various percentages and the desired rate for a more judicious use of the components and, in particular, abetter valorization of protean;- to maintain commercial fraction pulps by an anaerobic assay-pressing and conservation process, eliminating any dehydration, from which significant economy of energy.Process characterized in that it comprises a primary phase of grinding and bursting of the cellulose without mixing stems and a secondary phase in which a definitive fractionation is made to the desired degree.Device for implementing the method.Application: Agro-food industries.
</ABSTRACT>
