<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a construct element (1), in particular plaster-based.This construction element, in plate, comprising reinforcements (2) distributed in most of its thick wire, is such that along lines parallel to the faces of the plate, the reinforcements are concentrated in a dedicated area born of theThickness and, possibly later, the material of the matrix, in particular the plaster, is released on the plumb of these lines which can thus become folding lines to make a non-plan element.The invention also relates to a method and a provision of manufacture of these building elements.The invention applies to the manufacture, in particular, of U-shaped plaster trays, for the realization of ceilings and the development of building structures.
