<TITLE>
device for detecting rhythm irregularities
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
Apparatus for detecting arrhythmias in the electrocardiogram signal by evaluating the time distance between time consecutive, for a heartbeat characteristic signals, with a circuit for generating pulses at a frequency tracking multiples of the current heart rate with a time constant, and at least one gate circuitwhich outputs an arrhythmy indicating signal when a further such signal appears before the nth pulse generated after the last heartbeat pulse, where n is smaller than multiples.
</ABSTRACT>
