<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to novel pharmaceutical preparations for topical treatment of infections by herpes viruses, in particular those caused by herpes virus homini, e.g.b.Herpes Genitalis and herpes dermatitis.The preparations contain as antiviral agents a synergistic combination of an acidic sulfated polysaccharide or polymeric, in particular heparin, with zinc ions, and optionally an additional content of polyoxyethylene sorbony monolaurate and / or monooleate in conventional topically applicable medicament shapes such as creams, ointments, tinctures,Aqueous solutions and especially gels.
