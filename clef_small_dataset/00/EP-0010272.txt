<ABSTRACT>
Azo compounds of the formula in the R chlorine, methyl or methoxy and x carbamoyl or acetylamino, and when R chlorine or methyl is also methyl or chlorine, are by diazotizing the corresponding m-aminobenzoic acid anilide and coupling to 5-acetoacetylamino-benzimidazolone(2).The product is colorants and are particularly suitable for pigmenting plastics and paints.
