<ABSTRACT>
1.  manufacturing process realting to a rolling bearing with a cage (4) arranged in an outer sleeve (1), particularly for longitudinally moveable ball bearings, with axially extending cage elements (8) joined at one end by webs (9) distributed about the circumference of a circle which are, prior to being mounted in the outer sleeve (1), essentially arranged in the shape of a star in the same plane and which are bent in axial direction at recessed links at the juncture, characterized in that the bending of the cage elements (8) is performed by pushing the web area into the outer sleeve (1).
