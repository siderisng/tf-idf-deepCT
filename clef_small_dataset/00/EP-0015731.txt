<ABSTRACT>
a hand saw guide (10) comprising a member having two  right angled related surfaces (15, 16) so that where one sur­ face (16) is in face to face contact with a component to be  sawn the other surface (15) is perpendicular to the compo­ nent.  the said other surface (15) having incorporated therein a  magnet (35) that creates a magnetic field to hold the blade of a  saw against said other surface (15).  the saw can thus be oper­ ated to cut the component while held in guided relationship  with said other surface (15).
