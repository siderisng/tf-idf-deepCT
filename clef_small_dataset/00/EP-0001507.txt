<TITLE>
a method and apparatus for simultaneously conveying and freezing containerised comestibles.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

<ABSTRACT>
in order to provide a flow-through system by which  containerised comestibles can be frozen efficiently and  quickly in an installation which utilizes a conveyor of minimum  length and dispenses altogether with the need for  mechanically moving conveyor mechanisms, pressurized  air is utilized in conjuction with a substantially flat surface  (10) both to aid in or contribute to the conveyance of the  containers (c) as well as to effectuate rapid freezing of  the comestibles contained therein whilst the containers (c)  are being conveyed to a freezer store.  the invention has  particular application in the frozen food processing industry.  
</ABSTRACT>
