<ABSTRACT>
tubular heating element, in particular a screw-in heating element for enamelled hot water boilers or similar equipments with a protective anode in which the connecting elements of the tubular heating element are passed through a holder, the ends of the tubular heating element (1) being embedded in an element (5) of dielectric material, which on the one hand ensures the mechanical retention of the tubular heating element (1) in the holder which is configured as a screwed flange (3), the holder, however, on the other hand ensuring electrical insulation of the tubular heating element (1) to the screwed flange (3).  <image>
