<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a process for the preparation of alkyl ester phosphoric acid dichlorides by reacting phosphorus oxideloride with aliphatic alcohols after the reaction equation in the R is C₁-C₅ alkyl, which is characterized in that the reaction at temperatures of 10 to 50 ° C using one5 to 400% phosphorus oxiculide excess performs continuously.
