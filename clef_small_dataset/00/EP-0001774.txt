<ABSTRACT>
derivatives of kallikrein trypsin inhibitor and especially of the kallikrein trypsin inhibitor from the organs of cattle are obtained from bpti or its derivatives with free carboxyl groups by reaction with nucleophilic amino compounds in the presence of carbodiimides, where appropriate followed by deamination and/or conversion into azo derivatives.  the bpti derivatives are used as medicines.
