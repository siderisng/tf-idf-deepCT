<ABSTRACT>
Process for the preparation of crystal violet lactone by oxidation of 2- (4,4'-bisdimethylaminobenzhydryl) - 5-dimethylaminobenzoic acid in aqueous alkaline solution, wherein the oxidation takes place with water-soluble salts of the Hexa Cyanoferrat-III or in the presence of hexacyanofrrat-III.In high yield, a very pure crystal violet lactone is obtained.
