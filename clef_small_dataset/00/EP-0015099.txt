<ABSTRACT>
a catalyst for use in producing olefin polymers or  copolymers comprising (a) a solid titanium catalyst compo­ nent having magnesium, halogen, titanium, phosphorus and  a carboxylic acid ester and (b) an organometallic compound  of a metal of groups i to iii of mendelejeff's periodic table  provides improved results, in that stereoregular products can  be obtained over a wide range of melt flow indices, when the  solid titanium catalyst component (a) is one in which the  molar ratio of the phosphorus to the carboxylic acid ester is  from 0. 05:1 to 2:1 and which has a specific surface area of not  less than 40 m²/g, and also contains (c) an electron donor.  the catalyst can be used to produce an olefin polymer or  copolymer by polymerizing an alpha-olefin having at least 3  carbon atoms or copolymerizing at least two such alpha-­ olefins or such an alpha-olefin with up to 20 mole%  of  ethylene and/or a diene.  
</ABSTRACT>
