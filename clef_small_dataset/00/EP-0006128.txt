<ABSTRACT>
the diastereomer of 2,3-dihydro-2-methyl-3[2- (dimethylamino)propyl]-3-phenyl-1h-indole-1-carboxaldehyde of the formula <image> and its two enantiomers, and salts of these compounds are prepared by formylation of 2,3-dihydro-2-methyl-3-[2-(dimethylamino)propyl]-3- phenyl-1h-indole or by reductive formylation of 2-methyl-3-[2- (dimethylamino)propyl]-3-phenyl-3h-indole, and these compounds are used in pharmaceuticals.  the compounds have antitussive effects, and the racemate and the (-)enantiomer also have bronchodilator effects. 
</ABSTRACT>
