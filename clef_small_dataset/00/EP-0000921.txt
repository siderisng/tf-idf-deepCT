<ABSTRACT>
high strength composites prepared by (a) mixing (1)  an aqueous slurry of negatively charged, water-insoluble  natural or synthetic fiber such as cellulose fiber, (2) a latex  having a high density of ph independent cationic charges,  such as sulfonium, bound at or near the particle surface,  (3) a water-soluble, high molecular weight anionic polymer  co-additive, such as a hydrolyzed polyacrylamide  and optionally (4) conventional wet and additives; (b)  draining water from the resulting aqueous suspension  and (c) drying by heating.
