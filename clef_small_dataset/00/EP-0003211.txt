<ABSTRACT>
Imidazole derivatives of the formula in the R¹ for branched alkyl radicals having 6 to 20 carbon atoms, for unbranched alkyl radicals of 6 to 16 carbon atoms, for branched alkenyl radicals having 10 to 20 carbon atoms, for unbranched or branched alkynyl radicals having 3 to 6 carbon atoms, for 2-methyl-3-Phenyl-propyl or 2-methyl-3-phenyl-propene-2-yl radicals, wherein the phenyl radicals may be simply or multipled by halogen or alkyl group with 1 to 10 carbon tons, and R², R³ and R⁴ for hydrogenor methyl.The imidazoles or their salts are designed as a development of sickness inhibitors and ovicides to combat insects from the class of lepidopters, coleopters, dipters, hymenopters, heteropters, homoperous and isopter.
