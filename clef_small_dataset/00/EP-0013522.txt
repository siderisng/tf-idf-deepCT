<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the manufacture of disks soured from engraved matrices, by means of a compression press.The manufacturing method according to the invention consists, from a roll of plastic material, to bring the web of material between the open trays (3,10), a compression press without having before hole on thisbandaged.A front hole is practiced when closing the trays with a punch (7), suitable for a complementary punch (1), these punches serve as centers for the two matrices, then by guidance of the punch (7)in the punch (1), to the centering of the matrices relative to each other.The cutting operation of the central hole is carried out, when the trays are closed, by the punch (1) which is held in the up position throughout the end of the pressing cycle.Application in particular, with double-sided disk pressing.
