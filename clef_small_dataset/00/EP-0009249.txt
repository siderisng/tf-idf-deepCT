<ABSTRACT>
the indicating and operating mechanism for electrical appliances having a program-controlled sequence, especially for domestic appliances like a dishwasher, use a transparent insulating panel (1), for example a glass plate, on which, adjacent to transparent sensor fields (2, 3, 4) for controlling the appliance, letter and symbol fields (10, 11) and/or electrically drivable display fields (8, 15-19, 20), for example lcd or led areas, are arranged and/or are placed behind the sensor fields (2, 3, 4).  this results in a unit which combines in itself all control and display functions.  <image>
