<ABSTRACT>
an installation is described, in which the energy of a solar generator is used, via the thermal or electrolytic decomposition of water, for the production of hydrogen.  the device works in the form of an automatic "plantation" which is designed as an island or is situated in the country.  the hydrogen released by the decomposition of the water is collected, automatically stored and regularly carried off.  <image>
