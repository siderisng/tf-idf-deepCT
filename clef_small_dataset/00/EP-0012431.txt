<ABSTRACT>
in this ironing and embossing machine for leather, at least two or more ironing or embossing rollers (1) to (4) are arranged in the frames (5) of the machine to be rotatable about an axis (6) which extends parallel to the axis of rotation (7) of the individual ironing rollers (1) to (4), these ironing rollers (1) to (4) being selectively adjusted into their working positions (8) by means of a rotary movement about the axis (6).  the ironing rollers (1) to (4) act on the leather (19) in the working position (8) either from above or from below.  the ironing rollers (1) to (4) are equipped with separately controllable heaters (12), and the heaters (12) of the ironing rollers (1) to (4) which are not adjusted to working position (8) are connected to the electricity mains when the ironing roller situated in the working position (8) is not connected to the mains.  the ironing or embossing rollers (1) to (4) are heated by means of radiant heaters (12).  <image>
</ABSTRACT>
