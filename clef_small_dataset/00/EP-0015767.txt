compositions, methods and elements for detecting or assaying peroxidatively active substances.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
an assay for substances demonstrating peroxidative  activity is based on the perioxidase catalyzed oxidation of  dichlorofluorescin to dichlorofluorescein by peroxide.  the  assay composition comprises diacetyldichlorofluorescin  and a source of hydrogen peroxide.  the dichlorofluorescin  is formed in situ from diacetyldichlorofluorescin.   immunoassays for antigens or antibodies using species  labelled with a substance having peroxidative activity may  be performed.
