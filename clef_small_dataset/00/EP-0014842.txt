<ABSTRACT>
With a knife, saw, screwdriver blade (1) or the like hand tool, the handle consists of two equal long U-brackets (5, 6), which are perpendicular to each other at their free leg ends (7,8) pivotally hinged to a blade holder piece (3) and swiveling successively over the blade as this enclosing protective housing.To avoid a gap between the two bottom legs (9, 10) of the fabricated U-bracket (5, 6), the two axes (7, 8) in blade longitudinal direction approximately the thickness DEA bottom legs (9, 10) of the U-bracket(5, 6) offset against each other.
