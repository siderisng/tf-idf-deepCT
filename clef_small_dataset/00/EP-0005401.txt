<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an optical device of the periscope type carried by a vehicle, comprising a rotary mounted wave tube in the vehicle around its optical axis, and a mirror placed at the upper end of the tube and mounted movable around thean orthogonal axis to the optical axis.The device comprises a first resolver (31) whose rotor is driven into synchronism with the sighting tube (3) and whose primers are connected to a vertical plant providing the pitch angle (θ₁) and the roll angle. roll.(θ₂) of the vehicle, a second resolver (41) providing a voltage representative of a site angle (S₀), a third resolver (33), a reaction loop (34, 35) for enslaving the output voltage of the thirdresolver (33) on the output voltage of the second resolver (41), and a ratio (15) of ratio 2: 1 mounted between the rotor of the third resolver (33) and the axis of the mirror (14).
