<ABSTRACT>
The furnace differs from the known glass melting furnaces by the ratio of effective electrode surface to the horizontal cross-sectional area of the melt chamber.The electro oven according to the invention is further preferably characterized by the molten bath depth, the size ratio of melt chamber to work chamber and a scattering entry of the raw materials.The oven is preferably operated so that the entire melting bath surface is covered with a layer of unmolidated raw material, the average residence time of the melt in the oven is 3 to 6 hours.Furthermore, nitrate-free raw materials are preferably used.
