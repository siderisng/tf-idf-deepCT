<ABSTRACT>
Electric circuit for lighting a projectile detonator.A generator charge two capacitors (C₁, C₂) in series.The voltage of the capacitor (C₂) is limited by the zener effect occurring between the trig and the cathode of Thyris Tor (T₁).The latter becomes conductive after a time determined from the load of the capacitor (C₁), which allows, when closing the contact, to cause the start of the primer (5).The goal is to get by simple means a safety of Três precise mouth.
