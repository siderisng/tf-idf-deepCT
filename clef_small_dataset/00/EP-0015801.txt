<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to double porosity alumina balls, their process for preparation and their AP plunges as catalyst supports. The alumina beads have a porous volume to Tal between 0.55 and 1.7 cm³ / g, their specific surface is between 80 and 350 m² / g, their resistance to the break is greater than 1 kg, the Alumina is essentially in the form of at least one of the phases taken in the group PE constituted by éta, gamma, theta, delta. The method of manufacturing these balls comprises the mixture at a pH in front of 7.5 of an ultra-thin or pseudo-boehmitite soil with spheroidal alumina particles in a proportion of between 30 and 95% in weight. The boehmite ground is free of amorphous alumina, its concentration is between 5 and 25%. The spheroid particles of alumina are in the form of at least one of the phases included in the group consisting of eta, gamma, theta, delta. They have a microporous volume included in 0.4 and 1 cm³ / g, their specific surface area is included in Tre 100 and 350 m³ / g and their diameter is between 1 and 50 microns; spheroidal shaping and gelation of the drops of the mixture; The recovery of the golf beads, their drying and calcination at a temperature between 550 and 1100 ° C.
