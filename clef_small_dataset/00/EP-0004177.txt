<ABSTRACT>
the invention provides a method of metal coating  diamond or cubic boron nitride particles, the metal being  capable of forming a chemical bond with the particles,  including the steps of providing a mass of metal for the coating  in powdered form in contact with the particles, heat treating  the metal powder and the particles at a temperature  below the melting point of the metal to deposit a layer of  metal on the particles, and recovering the particles as discrete,  metal coated particles, the heat treatment taking place  in a non-oxidising atmosphere and being chosen to allow  chemical bond formation between the particles and the coating.
