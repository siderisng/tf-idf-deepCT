<ABSTRACT>
this invention relates to a process for increasing  solubility rate ratio or shortening the development time of  quinone-diazo or quinone-triazo sensitized phenolicaldehyde  resins.  in accordance with the present invention,  the resists are contacted with a trialkylamine containing  from two to eight carbon atoms in each alkyl group.  this  process increases the ratio between the rate at which the  exposed region of the resist is dissolved and the rate at  which the unexposed region is dissolved.
