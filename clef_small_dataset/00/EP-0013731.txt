<ABSTRACT>
sheet-like components made from pur foam which have been moulded into a three-dimensional shape are provided, before installation or assembly, with a uniform adhesion-promotion layer which covers the pores and is itself covered with a release film, the adhesion- promotion layer having significantly lower adhesion to the release film than to the sheet-like component.  the adhesion-promotion layer is expediently first applied to the release film and then bonded together with the latter to the sheet-like components (fig.  1).  <image>
