<ABSTRACT>
The pendulum (1) rotates in all directions thanks to the ball (20).Normally, it is maintained in a fixed position by the conical tip (13a) of the retaining member which is pressed by the spring (17) against the end of the lock head (21).In the event of a shock in a direction different from that of the axis of the reinforcement (13) the pendulum pivots and the armature (13) comes in the plicip shown in the drawing.The contact element (32) separates from the plot (34a) which cuts the grounding of the battery.At the same time, the bridge (41) connects two contacts (42) which closes an aillate circuit.The displacement of the armor (13) entails that of the contact holder (31) that displacement being shorter than the first.The coils (3 and 4) respectively allow recollection and remote control of the circuit breaker.
