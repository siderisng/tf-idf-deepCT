<ABSTRACT>
a brush for the collector of a carriage travelling in an electrified tubular monorail used in handling facilities.  this brush includes a base (19) made from insulating material provided with snap-fastening means (26) integral with electrically conducting elements (34) connected to the collector and engaging with a projecting head (43) made of conductive material and which constitutes a blade-shaped contact finger (24) made from an electrically conductive material such as a sintered alloy or one based on copper and carbon.  the invention relates to motors for transporting loads inside handling facilities.  <image>
