<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for passivating the surfaces of semiconductor elements.Silicon layers (8) evaporated to the surface of semiconductor elements stabilize the characteristics of the semiconductor elements permanently.To lower the barrier currents, these silicon layers are tempered.This achieves a permanent lowering of the barrier current level.The invention can be applied to all semiconductor devices.
