<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a composite based on polymeric substances that is flame retardant and consistently good processing properties, high strength and a consistently smooth surface.The composite material contains aluminum trihydroxide Al (OH) ₃ whose single crystals or grains have a rounded shape, wherein the proportion of single crystals or grains of the aluminum trihydroxide having a diameter of less than 2 μm below 0.1 vol.-% and the middle grain or.Crystal diameter is below 35 μm.For producing the aluminum trihydroxide, fixed Al (OH) ₃ is fissolved from the Bayer process with aluminum-free alkali eye and then filtered.
