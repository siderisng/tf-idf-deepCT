<ABSTRACT>
Method for separating phenol of cresol in which phenol-containing cresol is distilled in the presence of a component which has a dipole moment of 0 to 0.5 debye and boiled at 1 bar in the temperature range of 120 to 220 ° C or a dipole moment of 05 to 2.5 Debye has and at 1 bar in the temperature range from 150 to 190 ° C boiling.The separation is carried out in general by azeotropist distillation.
