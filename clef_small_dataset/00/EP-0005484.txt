<ABSTRACT>
The application relates to a method for producing monomeric acylcyanides of the general formula R-CO-CN, wherein R is respectively substituted alkyl, cycloalkyl, aryl or certain heterocyclic radicals, by cleaving the corresponding dimeric acylcyanides (O-acyl-Tartronic acid dinitrile).The cleavage reaction is carried out in the presence of basic-reactive compounds, if necessary in the presence of a diluent, in the temperature range of 50 to 300 ° C;The monomers formed are removed as fast as possible, suitably by distillation, from the reaction mixture.Acylcyanides can z.b.as intermediates for the production of herbicides of the Triazinon series are used.
