<ABSTRACT>
Inverted Cassegrain antenna to use, on the one hand, the qualities of a conventional fine beam for the standby and pursuit functions and, allowing, on the other hand, to have an expanded beam either in the plansite, either in the plane of deposit.This antenna is provided, in an exemplary embodiment, a mirror (M₁) consisting of two reflector-polarizer elements (E₁ and E₂) together with one to the other by means of a hinge (C₁) allowing their articulation., a remote control device regulating their relative orientation.Application: multiple functions radar.
