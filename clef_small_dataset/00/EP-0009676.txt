<ABSTRACT>
The transport device has a common drive device (54, 56, 62, 76, 80, 82, 84, 86) for a machine-internal recording medium (50) and an external, through a channel (64) introducible record carrier (58).Normally the internal drive device (54, 56) transported to the drawing carrier (50) is separated by via sensor (106, 108) controlled reversibility of the drive motor (86) from the internal recording carrier (50) as soon as the presence of an external recording medium (58) is detected.The drive then acts only on the recording medium (58), until the sensors (106, 108) a renewed reversing of the motor (86) takes place.
