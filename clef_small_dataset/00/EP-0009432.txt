<ABSTRACT>
The subject of the invention relates to a bitumen composition, characterized in that it consists of a ternary mix of (a) 52 to 78% by weight of bitumen;(b) 20 to 40% by weight of polyolefin and (c) 2 to 8% by weight of a butadiene-styrene elastomer.This composition is used alone in sealing in melted or solution and also serves the manufacture of leaves and comprehensive sealing complexes of both frames.
