<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to novel N-alkylated amino alcohols of the formula wherein Ar is an unsubstituted or hydroxy-substituted radical aromatic character, N the values of zero or 1 and alkood an alkylene residue having 2 to 5 carbon atoms, wherein the nitrogen atom and the oxygen atom, or, if nFor zero, the phenyl radical are separated by at least two carbon atoms, and their salts, processes for their preparation and pharmaceutical preparations containing such compounds and their application.The main effect of the new compound is a stimulation of cardiac β receptors;They also cause a blockade of adrenal α-receptors;And a blood pressure reduction.They can therefore be used as β-stimulators, in particular as positive inotrope effective means for the treatment of heart failure.
