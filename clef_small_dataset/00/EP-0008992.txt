<ABSTRACT>
A method for recovering the Indium contained in the oxidation state III and in the form of impurity in aqueous solutions of other metals.This method includes the following steps: (a) the indium by contacting said aqueous solution with an organic phase containing as an active group a diester of phosphoric acid;b) The indium of the organic phase is reversed by contacting an aqueous phase.Application to metallurgy of non-ferrous metals.
