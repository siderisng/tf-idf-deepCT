<ABSTRACT>
a process for the removal of acid gases from mixtures by  contacting the said mixtures in an absorber (10) with a solvent  which comprises a chemical absorbent, 5-55%w water, and, if  desired, a physical absorbent, regenerating the loaded sol­ vent (18) thus obtained by steam stripping in a regenerator  (20), withdrawing the resulting water vapour (34) and  desorbed gases from the regenerator (20) condensing the  water vapour to yield a condensate, and recycling the regen­ erated solvent (16) to the absorber (10) afterat least part of the  condensate (4 ) has been added back thereto.
