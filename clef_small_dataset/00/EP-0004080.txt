<ABSTRACT>
If one adds to an acidic aqueous solution with pH values of 0-3 simultaneously nitrite and sulfite in the molar ratio 1: 1 to 1: 1.5, the emonic monoxide arises.The process works at temperatures of 0-100 ° C.Instead of sulfite, SO₂ can be used.The formed gaseous nitrous oxide can be freed from contaminants (SO₂, NO) by washing with liquor and alkaline permanganate solution.
