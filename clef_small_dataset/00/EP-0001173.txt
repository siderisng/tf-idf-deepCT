<ABSTRACT>
a process for the diffusion welding of copper and stainless  steel, which comprises sandwiching a thin layer of at least  one metal selected from the group consosting of ni, ni-base  alloys, cr, ni-cr, and cr-ni with low gas content, as an insert  metal, between the surfaces of copper and stainless steel to  be donded, and then diffusion welding the sandwich.
