<ABSTRACT>
For detecting a blood leak in a hemodialysis system, a pulsed light beam is sent by an optical transmitter (18) in a predetermined angle of incidence (θ) between the central axis (30) and the normal axis (32) in the dialysis current (36), and the changesThe reflected light is measured with a photodetector (20) which is arranged in a predetermined reflection angle (Δ) on the same side of the dialysis current (36) as the transmitter (18).The light beam is pulsed in such a way that the transit time substantially corresponds to the interruption time.
