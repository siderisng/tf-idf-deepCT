<ABSTRACT>
The invention is a footrest that is to be provided in particular to work tables.The footrest is in its height and inclination separately adjustable.After releasing the adjusting elements (3,4,5), it can be brought to the desired height and inclination with the feet against the upward force of a spring and then locked with the adjusting elements in this position.With the foot plate (1), an electrical switch may be operable for the purpose of execution of working functions on the work table.
