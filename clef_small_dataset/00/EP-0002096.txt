<ABSTRACT>
Item is a method for producing blocked polyisocyanates by reacting polyisocyanates with optionally substituted tetrahydropyrimidines at 0-150 ° C.The polyisocyanates can be subjected to the reaction of a molecular magnification by chain extension, such as water, polyols or polyamines.Further objects are also the new polyisocyanates blocked with tetrahydropyrimidines, especially those based on the isophorone diisocyanate.
