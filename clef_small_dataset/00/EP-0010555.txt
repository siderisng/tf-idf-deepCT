<ABSTRACT>
a process for producing an upgraded plastic surface by means of a liquid, polymerisable polyester resin mixture in which the surface tension of the liquid polyester and that of the unsaturated, organic solvent are increased, compared with those of the substances to be added, by adding a crosslinking agent, to such an extent that rapid lowering thereof is facilitated in the still-liquid mixture.
