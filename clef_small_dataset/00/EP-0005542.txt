<ABSTRACT>
For the pneumatic emptying of a transportable silo container (4a, 4b), a device for dispensing of measured amounts fine-grained bulk material is provided, which has two scales whose measuring containers (1, 2) alternately to the conveying lines (3a) leading to the silo container (4a, 4b).3b) can be connected.During the filling time of a measuring container, the execution of the bulk material takes place from the other measuring container.
