<ABSTRACT>
aqueous coating agents, in particular anionically depositable coating agents, contain polymeric film forming agents which are rendered water-soluble or water-dispersible by means of an organic or inorganic base, the coating agents also optionally containing further additives apart from water.  in order to improve the throwing power and corrosion prevention effect of such polymers, the polymeric film forming agent consists of a copolymer of 40 to 90% conjugated diene containing 4 to 8 carbon atoms and 10 to 60% of a copolymerisable, ethylenically unsaturated compound containing carboxyl groups. 
</ABSTRACT>
