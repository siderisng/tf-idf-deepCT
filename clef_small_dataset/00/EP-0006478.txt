<ABSTRACT>
Programmable control signal locking device in a data processing system comprising locking circuits (11, 12, 13 and 14) receiving on one of their inputs (1, 3, 5 or 7) of the gnaldata from a first group of bits positions (W, X, Y, Z) of the instruction register (15) and on the other input (2, 4, 6 or 8) of the load signals inProvenance of logical circuits (17, 18, 19, 20).These logic circuits receive on one of their inputs from the signals from a second group of bits (a, b, c, d) and on another input the output signal of the decoder (16).The device thus makes it possible to position, by means of a statement detected by the die encoder (16), the locking circuits designated by the second signal group (A, B, C, D) according to the states designated by theFirst group of signals (W, X, Y, Z).
