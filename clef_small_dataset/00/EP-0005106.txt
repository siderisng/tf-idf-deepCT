<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an endocavitary cardiac stimulation probe.This probe includes, for example, a tubular electrode 1, connected to the cardiac stimulator by a flexible conductor, tubular, isolated 2;A harpoon 5a, in a solid, biocompatible and resorbable material in a duration of the order of 3 weeks to 3 months, is mounted sliding in the tubular electrode 1, so as to be extracted, for the purpose of implantation.In the endocavitary wall, by a thrust transmitted for example by a wire 8, passing through the tubular conductor 2. This probe has the advantage of being immobilized first by the harpoon, then, after its resorption, by the natural formation ofFibrosis around the electrode.
