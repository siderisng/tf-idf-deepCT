<ABSTRACT>
a catalyst system for the homo- or copolymerization of  olefins, which comprises   (a) at least one organometallic compound of a typical  metal belonging to group i, ii or iii of the periodic table,   (b) a reaction product of (i) a solid organomagnesium  compound of the formula: mgrℓx2-ℓαd wherein r is a  hydrocarbon group having 1 to 20 carbon atoms, x is a  halogen atom, d is a lewis base, ℓ is a number in the range  0<ℓ<2 and α is a number in the range 0≦α<1. 5, (ii) a phenol  or thiophenol compound, (iii) a lewis base, and (iv) a  titanium compound and/or vanadium compound, and op­ tionally   (c) a lewis base in an amount of up to 1 mole to 1 mole  of the component (a).  
</ABSTRACT>
