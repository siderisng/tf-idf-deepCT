<ABSTRACT>
The present invention is a method of modifying thermoplastic aromatic polycarbonates with aromatically bonded halogen, primary alkyl, secondary alkyl and / or alkenyl substituents, which is characterized in that these polycarbonates are added after addition of 1 wt.-% to 5 wt.-%, preferably from 1 wt.-% to 3 wt.-%, based on weight of polycarbonate, photoinitiators and optionally of 0.05 wt.-% to 5 wt.-%, preferably from 0.1 wt.-% to 2% by weight, based on the weight of polycarbonate, on flame retardants, irradiated with UV light, wherein preferably an irradiation time between 15 sec.and 180 sec.is complied with.The object of the present invention are also modified polycarbonates, of the method according to the invention.
