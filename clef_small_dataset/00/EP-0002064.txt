<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a material as a spongiosacement with foaming and absorbable effect for connection and discernment of and with human and animal hard tissue, which is also used for the replacement and connection of bone parts or tooth roots for attachment and incorporation of foreign bodies, in particular for surgical procedures, use.The material consists of a polymethyl methacrylate as a carrier, calcium carbonate as foaming and sodium phosphate as a resorbable substance and can be used in plastic or solid form.
