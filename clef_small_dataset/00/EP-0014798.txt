mounting ring for a sunshine roof assembly and method of installing such an assembly.

<ABSTRACT>
in order to mount a sunshine roof assembly in the roof of  a vehicle a mounting ring (10) is fitted in an aperture in the  vehicle roof.  the mounting ring (10) has a downwardly  extending flange (20) on three of its sides which project  through the aperture in the vehicle roof.  the fourth side (22) is  of u-shaped cross-section so as to embrace the correspondÂ­ ing edge of the aperture.  the mounting ring (10) is fitted into  the aperture from above and secured to the vehicle roof.  the  sunshine roof assembly is then fitted from below and secured  to the downwardly extending flange (20) on three of the sides  of the aperture.
