machine for coiling helical springs.

<ABSTRACT>
In a device for wrapping coil springs, the spring wire is clamped at the front end of the winding mandrel (14).The winding mandrel is rotarily driven, so that it subtracts and winding the spring wire from the reservoir coil, wherein the effective nobody shifts axially according to the increasing spring length.In order to wrap coil springs (28) with any length of the length of the winding mandrel (14) in length, a takeover head (30) is provided which is rotated in sync and coaxial with the mandrel.The takeover head absorbs the at the end of the axial displacement of the winding mandrel (14) from this free end of the coil spring (28).Rollers (46) of the takeover head (30) are located under pressure radially at the front end of the winding mandrel (14) and hold the coil spring (28) on the mandrel so that the spring wire can be further withdrawn.
</ABSTRACT>
