<ABSTRACT>
a clothespin-like gripper (18) has a pair of clamp jaw  portions (48) resiliently urged together and adapted to  frictionally embrace and thereby grip a magnetic diskette  (22) or disk-jacket assembly for pulling the diskette (22)  from an original position in a diskette magazine (28) into  a final position for data transfer.  a swingable blocker (20)  is provided on the gripper (18) so that the diskette (22)  may be selectively blocked from being embraced by the  gripper (18) whereby the gripper (18) may be used to mo­ ve the diskette (22) back from its final position into its  original position without any embracing action by the grip­ per (18).
