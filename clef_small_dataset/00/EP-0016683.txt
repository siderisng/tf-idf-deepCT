<ABSTRACT>
Improvement with mirrors for vehicles, comprising a mirror fixed on a support member articulatedly mounted in the central part of the bottom of a housing.At the back of the support member 3 of the mirror is mounted at least one elastic member 10, whose two ends are supported against the opposite side faces of the housing 1, said elastic member 10, being engaged in at least at leastTwo guide members 14, 15 integral with the support member 3 of the mirror.The invention is used in remote adjustable mirrors.
