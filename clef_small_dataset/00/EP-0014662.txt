<ABSTRACT>
The secondary circuit comprises at least one intermediate exchanger (4), placed in the reactor (2) vessel, a vapor generator (6) and a circulation pump (12), of the secondary liquid metal.This circuit is characterized in that the storage reserve (24) of the secondary liquid metal and recovery of the products created by a possible reaction metal liquid-water, also serves as a steam anti-ram tank for the vapor generator.For this, the output of the steam generator plunges directly into the tank and a gaseous coverage is maintained above the liquid metal in the tank.Application to the realization of rapi neutron reactors cooling by a liquid metal.
