<ABSTRACT>
The objective refers to a device for identifying objects and persons, consisting of an information carrier attached to the object to be identified and a reading unit.The notification arrangement (C₁, C₂) consists of a plurality of at least input side parallel-connected delay elements (V₁, V₂) of different, defined delay time, which optionally electrical transducers (W₁, W₂) with defined frequency bandwidth and adjustable steam elements (τ₁, τ₂) with definedDamping are connected in series.The parallel-connected delay branches are connected via adaptation vier poles (SP) and connecting lines with a transmitting insertion antenna (AES).
