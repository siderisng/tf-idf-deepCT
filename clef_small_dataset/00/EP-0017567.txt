<ABSTRACT>
In the rotor drum are provided radially extending alveoli and in which the blades are engaged.The blades (4) have successive platforms (4D, 4F) by which they are supported on the wall of the cells (3), said blades being locked radially on the drum (1) by a key (5) engaged between the feet.(4g) of two adjacent blades and support against the inner face constituted by the drum (1) bore.The invention is used for fixing the blades on a compressor rotor.
