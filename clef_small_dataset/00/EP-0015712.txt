<ABSTRACT>
in a method of producing multiple-wail composite pipes  an outer pipe (1) is diametrically expanded by heating (2) and  an inner pipe (3) is diametrically contracted by cooling with a  coolant introduced into the inner pipe (3) to produce between  the pipes a clearance just sufficient for insertion of the inner  pipe (3) into the outer pipe (1), and then the coolant is pressur­ ized to expand the inner pipe (3) against and together with the  outer pipe (1), the pressure being removed after the pipes (1  and 3) have expanded to a specific diameter of the joint there­ between.  the tightness of the fit between the pipes (1, 3) due  to the expansion is further increased by the subsequent ther­ mal shrinkage of the outer pipe (1) and thermal expansion of  the inner pipe (3).  
</ABSTRACT>
