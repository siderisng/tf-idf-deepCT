<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a self-switching switch with the contact system opacuible with power forces.Such a current-limiting self-switching switches are associated with the movable contact parts (4) of the individual phases of a multi-pole self-switch on a rotatably mounted switching shaft made of insulating fabric (insulating shaft 25) for attached halters (holder 6, 6 '), one of which is provided with the opening system.This ensures that all contact members (1, 4) of the multi-pole switch are pulled apart exactly when the totality of all forces acting on all contact members (1, 4) exceeds a predeterminable value or when such a large overcurrent in a phaseoccurs that the predetermined limit is exceeded.
