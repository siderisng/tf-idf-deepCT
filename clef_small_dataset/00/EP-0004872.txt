<ABSTRACT>
Solution and method for stripping silicon slices.A quaternary solution comprising ethylene-diamine, water, pyrocatechin and a predetermined amount of a diazine, provides a controlled speed of stripping.In a preferred embodiment the solution comprises from 700 to 800ml of ethymene-diamine, from 200 to 250 ml of water, from 100 to 150g of pyrocatechin and 2 to 4g of diazine per liter of ethylene-diamine.It seems difficult to use more than 4g of diazine per liter due to presence when stripping insoluble residues, despite the sensitive increase in the stripping speed.The preferred diazines are 1,2-diamine and 1,4-diazine.The invention can be ultilic to manufacture silicon nozzles for inkjet printers or V-MOS field effect transistors.
