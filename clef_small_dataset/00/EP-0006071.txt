<ABSTRACT>
Electro-erosion machining method of a part comprising a cyclindrical orifice of any section by means of a displacement electrode relative to the part.According to the invention, a first fast blank pass is carried out by descent from the electrode parallel to the generators of the orifice and lift.It is then operated, then a translation of the electrode perpendicular to the generators of a distance R and the electrode is lowering at lower speed and we rise, one operates a circular translation of radius R, we bring down and reassemble theElectrode, and we repeat the operation n times to operate a complete turn.Application to the machining of the extrusion chains of the metals.
