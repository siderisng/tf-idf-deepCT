<ABSTRACT>
Filter light pigments are made of luminous pigment particles that are covered with a filter material.A luminous pigment emitting by cathode beams reduced by cathode beams can be coated with red iron oxide by producing a thin layer of pyrite on the luminous pigment particles and then transferred them into hematite.As a result, the surface of the luminous pigment particles is treated evenly with a closed, extremely firmly adhering color pigment layer.The filter luminaire pigments thus obtained are used in cathode ray tubes.
