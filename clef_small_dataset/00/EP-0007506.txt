<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to novel α-azolyl keto derivatives, several methods for their preparation and their use as fungicides, as well as methods for producing the starting materials.Neue α-azolyl keto derivatives of the general formula in which R¹ to R⁷, N, X¹, X² and Y have been given the meaning given in the description, and their physiologically acceptable acid addition salts and metal salt complexes found.They have strong fungicides, especially systems mixing fungicidal properties.
