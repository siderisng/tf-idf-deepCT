<ABSTRACT>
This device comprises a piezoelectric medium (1) associated with a semiconductor and photosensitive medium (2) on which the optical image (1) is projected.FOUR TRANS DUCTORS (T₁ - T₄) arranged on the piezoelectric medium (1) emit four elastic waves (S₁ - S₄) in separate two directions (OX, OY), with wave numbers such as the signal resulting from the interactionOf these waves has a null wave number.Depending on the form of the signals applied to the transducers, the point point by point of the image is made, or the Fourier transform of this image.
