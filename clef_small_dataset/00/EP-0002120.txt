<ABSTRACT>
insulation comprising a multiplicity of small chips (18)  of corrugated cardboard (12, 14, 16).  the chips have  various external configurations and various orientations of  the long axis of the flute(s) (16) with a side of the chip.   the chips may be utilised as loose, bagged or block insulation  for structures.
