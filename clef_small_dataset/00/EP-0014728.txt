<ABSTRACT>
1.  hollow compression dowel with an attachable closing plug for a leak-proof drill-hole closure, the dowel shank being partially insertable into a structure bore and being fixable in the drill-hole by means of a hardenable filling material which can be pressed into the drill-hole through the dowel-shank cavity, and to the freely projecting portion of which can be fastened lining parts such as façade panels, or other constructional parts, characterized in that the cavity of the dowel-shank (1, 1',1'') is subdivided into separate longitudinal filling and venting chambers (3, 3a) by means of at least one inner partition wall (2) extending over the shank length and stiffening the dowel.
