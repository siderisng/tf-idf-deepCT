<ABSTRACT>
An electrical connection device comprising an elongated body (3) for contacting at its end (5) with a molten metal mass (6), including a steel bath and comprising internal cooling means.These cooling means consist of a heat tube (9), known in the trade under the nation "Caloduc", drowned inside the body (3) in a longitudinal direction until the neighborhood of the extremity (5) in contact with the bath (6) and whose free end (10) of the body (3) is placed in a heat exchanger (11).The body (3) further has at its end (5) means (18) for locally limiting the convection movements of the melt (6).The device according to the invention finds a pre-ferential application as a sole electrode for DC arc furnace.
