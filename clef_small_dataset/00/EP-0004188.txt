<ABSTRACT>
the invention provides a heating unit capable of  generating heat upon the passing of an electric current therethrough,  which comprises a matrix of a hardened hydraulic  material (preferably cement or gypsum) and electrically conductive  fibers (preferably carbon fibers) dispersed therein in  an amount sufficient to impart electric conductivity and a  method for producing the heating unit, which method comprises  kneading a hydraulic material, water and conductive  fibers, optionally molding the kneaded mixture, and then  hardening the mixture.  the heating unit can have an insulation  coating thereon.  the heating units can be used in various  applications such as domestic- or space-heating systems  for general household, civil engineering, industrial and rail-road  applications.  they can be incorporated in a wall, floor,  ceiling or roof of a building for example.
