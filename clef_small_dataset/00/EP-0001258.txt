<ABSTRACT>
Opening bath formed of two parts 2,3, one of which can pivot about a vertical axis relative to each other.The fixed part 2 of the bathtub body 1 comprises a stop valve 21 controlled by a push-up to the closing of the bath to prevent the supply of the water with the filling valve 9 before complete closure of the bathtub and the bathtub.Mobile part 3 comprises a float device 23 blocking the closing device 5 in the closed position as long as the bathtub contains water.Application: including bathtubs for the elderly or disabled.
