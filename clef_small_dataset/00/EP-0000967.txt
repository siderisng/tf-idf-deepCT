<ABSTRACT>
method for the continuous coating of webs having  spliced joints, which avoids at high coating speeds coating  defects that are caused by the web joints in coaters  that are closely spaced from the webs.  the webs (10) and  (17) are spliced by means of a tape (16) that runs from  the front side of the old web to the rear side of the new  web, whereby only step-up discontinuities are formed a  the splicing joint.  the method is in particular valuable in the manufacturing  of delicate materials, such as photographic light-sensitive  film.  
</ABSTRACT>
