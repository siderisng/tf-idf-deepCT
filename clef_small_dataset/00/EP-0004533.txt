catalyst, process for its preparation and its use for the polymerization of 1-olefins
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
By reacting a carrier-containing carrier-containing carrier-fixed chromate ester with an aluminum organic compound in the absence of an unsaturated pomyerizable hydrocarbon and subsequent activation with a further aluminum organic compound is obtained a catalyst for the polymerization of 1-olefins.In the implementation, no washing liquids that would have to be reprocessed.The catalyst provides a wider molar weight distribution polymer in high yield containing little highly reflective portions so that the films produced from this polymeric films are virtually stick-free.
