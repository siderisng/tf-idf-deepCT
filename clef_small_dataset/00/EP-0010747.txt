<ABSTRACT>
1.  apparatus for filtering solids-containing liquids, in particular for filtering water of a flume for conveying sugar beet in a sugar beet factory, having an endless circulating filter band (10) with sieve or filter openings (33), the upper run (18) of which serves as a sieving surface, and having downstream of the upper run (18) a filter band cleaning station in the region of which the filter band is extensible so as to enlarge the sieve or filter openings characterised in that the cleaning station comprises at least one guide roller (13) over which the filter band (10) is guided and which is provided on the outer surface of its jacket with ribs (34) which extend the filter band (10) in the transverse direction over its entire width.
