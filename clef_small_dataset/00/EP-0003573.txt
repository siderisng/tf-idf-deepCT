<TITLE>
method and apparatus for bringing together articles in a packaging machine.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

<ABSTRACT>
To combine parts of a packaging, in particular a slider and a sleeve, a cigarette pack of the type «slider and sleeve», the parts are conveyed in a packaging machine along a subcircular arc, namely on a correspondingly trained, circulating revolver, and during this transport by axially parallel displacementUnited together.The device is thereby set up on a continuous workflow.One after the other supplied parts of the packages are handed over by a correspondingly designed transfer device one after the other at the continuously circulating revolver.
</ABSTRACT>
