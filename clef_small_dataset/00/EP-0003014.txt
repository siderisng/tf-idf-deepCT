<ABSTRACT>
A method for producing alcohols having 2-4 C atoms by hydration of the corresponding olefins to acidic catalysts at elevated temperature and increased pressure, wherein the insert product before entering the reactor are supplied by-products resulting in the passage through the catalyst bed.These are ether, low molecular weight hydrocarbons and unwanted alcohols in the process process.The by-products are used in amounts of 0.01-3 parts per part resulting by-product.
