<ABSTRACT>
in an automated assay for qualitative determination of  thyroid hormone binding capacity of a serum sample, serum  and t₃ tracer are caused to flow through a chamber contain­ ing a t₃ binder supported on a solid support, t₃ being  triiodothyronine.  the amount of t₃ tracer which passed  through the chamber, and the amount of t₃ tracer bound to  the binder in the chamber are determined thereby to deter­ mine the percent of bound t₃ tracer.  such percent is then  compared with the percent of t₃ tracer bound in a reference  sample to determine qualitatively the t₃ binding capacity of  the serum sample.  
</ABSTRACT>
