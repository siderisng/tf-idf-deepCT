<ABSTRACT>
continuous process for the purification of polycarbonate  solutions in organic solvents from process impurities, said  process consisting of a series of aqueous washing stages  where in each stage the washing was accomplished by  emulsifying the organic solution with an aqueous phase of  suitable ph comprised between 2 and 14, and by the  successive separation of the two phases, characterized in that  said separation is carried out by sending the emulsion with a  spacial velocity of 0. 01 to 2 cm/sec.  through a layer of fibres  10-500 mm thick and having an apparent density of 0. 2 - 0. 7  g/cc, and by then continuously removing the phases that  separate in the form of two layers, beyond the layer of fibres.
