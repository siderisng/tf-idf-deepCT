a method and apparatus for producing plastic parts (1) containing oblong reinforcing elements (3), in particular glass fibres, of great length, at least one groove (2) being moulded in a soft surface area (4) of the plastic part (1) by means of a grooving device (6), the reinforcing element (3) being laid into this groove, and the groove subsequently being resealed by a sealing device (8) before the surface area (4) is hardened.  <image>
