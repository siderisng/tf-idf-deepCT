<ABSTRACT>
In a device for preparing coffee or tea, a fresh water tank (2) is connected to a water chamber (7), from which from the heated water over a riser (3) and a water overflow (4) and optionally via a filter (6) in The collecting vessel (13) reaches. To achieve a good thermal contact and transition between the heating plate (11) containing the water chamber (7) and the collecting vessel (13), e.g. b. to achieve a coffee product and also to reduce the length of the pipeline between the water tank (2) and the heated water chamber (7) and between this and the riser (3), the heating plate (11) is arranged laterally from the collecting vessel (13) and surrounds this segment-shaped. the bottom (12) of the housing (1) of the device is provided with a round guide edge (17), which ensures that the collecting vessel (13) when placed on the floor (12) in the correct position opposite the heating plate (11) in which it rests laterally against them.
