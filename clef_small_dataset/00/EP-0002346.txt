<ABSTRACT>
a bulk sulphonation process for sulphonating an elastomeric  polymer wherein the polymer and sulphuric acid,  chlorosulphonic acid or so₃ as sulphonating agent are  mechanically mixed whereby the polymer is in a state of flux  in the substantial absence of solvent at a temperature below  250°c and above the glass transition temperature or crystalline  melting point.  either the free sulphonic acid is recovered  or the sulphoric acid is neutralised before recovery.
