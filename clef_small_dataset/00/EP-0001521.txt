<ABSTRACT>
1.  an electrical sliding contact device comprising a conducting track and a brush in contact with the track, the track and brush having in operation a relative movement transverse to the direction along which the brush is applied onto the track and the brush having at least one bundle of wires having an end portion in sliding contact with the track and the other end portion which is retained against rotation, characterized in that the wires are of metal, are stiff, have a diameter lower than 80 microns, have a tight connection at said other end portion and in that the brush (16) and the track are maintained in a dry atmosphere which is non-oxidyzing or under vacuum.
