<TITLE>
mercury contamination indicator and its use as an indicator and as a decontaminator.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

<ABSTRACT>
a powder mixture of either cuprous iodide or silver iodide  with elemental sulfur is a mercury contamination indicator of  high visibility and sensitivity.  the buff or cream color of this  mixture changes to black or dark brown in the presence of  mercury or mercury vapor.  the mixture may also be used in  areas of light mercury contamination to decontaminate the  area by reaction with the mercury.
