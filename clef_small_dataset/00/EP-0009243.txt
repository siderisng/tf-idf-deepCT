<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a display present with two carrier plates (2, 3), which are distanced by fibrous spacers (12) from each other.The object of the invention is to provide an economical drive for the production of fibers with a well-defined diameter <100 μm.For this purpose, it is proposed to convert a wire from a transition metal of the fourth to sixth subgroup of the periodic system to convert this wire to its surface into a Kar bid, boride, nitride or silicide and then crush it.This method uses the fact that metal wires can be produced with an exact thickness;Due to the chemical conversion of the wire sheath, it is charged that the starting material loses its ductility and can be easily crushed.The invention is particularly suitable for (high) multi-plexible liquid crystal displays.
