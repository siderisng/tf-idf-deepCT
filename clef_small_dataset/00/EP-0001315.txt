detergent-compatible antistatic compositions are  sprayed, in the form of particles, on to base detergent  granules which contain surfactant, builder and other  detergency additives.  the antistatic particles comprise a  quaternary ammonium compound and a heat-softenable  organic material and are preferably sprayed with air through a  two-fluid atomizer nozzle.
