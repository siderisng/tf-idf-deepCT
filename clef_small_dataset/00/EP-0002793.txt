<ABSTRACT>
Soft and hard resins made of urea and a CH-Aciden aldehyde are prepared by obtaining ureas of general formula (I) R¹-HNNH-R² (I) where x oxygen or sulfur and R¹ and R² is hydrogen, same or different alkyl, arylor aralkyl radicals or optionally an alkyleneurea residue, with at least 2 moles of a CH-acid aldehyde of the general formula (II) wherein R³ represents hydrogen or such as R⁴ same or different alkyl, aryl or aralkyl radicals, in the presence of a strong acid to a condensation productConvert and then aftertressed in an anhydrous medium with a base.These soft and hard resins are used in combination with other film images as paint binders.
