<ABSTRACT>
On a hot yarn plug (1), a closed liquid beam (7) is passed in a cooling line with rod guide (6), resulting in a fast, very uniform cooling.When deducted results in thread speeds of 2000 m / min and more a quiet thread course.For drying usually a deflecting of the thread is sufficient (10), since the liquid is fired.
