<ABSTRACT>
a multi-stage gear crank for a cycle comprising a crank  shaft (1) having a pair of crank arms (3, 4) one of which  has mounted thereon at least one larger diameter and at  least one smaller diameter gear wheel (7, 8) each having  a plurality of teeth for meshing with a drive chain (10).  in order to facilitate displacement of the drive chain  (10) from the larger diameter gear wheel (7), the latter is  provided in the vicinity of upper and lower dead points  (01, 02) opposite said crank arms (3, 4) with at least one  tooth (7a) having chain detaching means.
