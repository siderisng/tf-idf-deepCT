<ABSTRACT>
the invention relates to a pressure controller.  in the high-pressure chamber (2), there are arranged vertically a casing (4) of the pressure controller, a metal cylinder (10) containing a small magnetised bar (13), which is thus subject to the influence of external fields, and this cylinder is made to open at some distance from a metal armature (15) carried by the diaphragm (3) in such a manner that the latter, in the stop position of the stopping device (9), remains stuck against the armature, and that a certain low pressure acting on the diaphragm is necessary in order to free it.  application in control and safety systems for the flow of air necessary for the combustion of a gas burner.  <image>
</ABSTRACT>
