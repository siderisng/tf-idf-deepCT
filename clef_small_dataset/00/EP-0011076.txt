<ABSTRACT>
An arc gauge on transfer drums of multicolor rotary printing machines has the purpose of absorbing the freshly printed bow on the circumference of the drum that damage the print image is avoided.For this purpose, arc gauge elements are used with a support edge.On which the bow can hang up.The invention makes it possible to arrange the arc gate elements to support the sheet on a non-printing site, for which individual arc gate elements are folded out of the working position and the sheet carrying device can be pushed axially and radially on the drum jacket.
