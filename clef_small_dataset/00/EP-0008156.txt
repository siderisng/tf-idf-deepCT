<ABSTRACT>
crosslinked addition polymer microparticles are pro­ duced by dispersion polymerisation of monomers in an  aliphatic hydrocarbon liquid in the presence of a dispersion  stabiliser and also of a specified type of amino resin which  is insoluble in the hydrocarbon liquid, at least one of the  monomers polymerised carrying a group which can react  with the amino resin under the conditions of polymerisa­ tion.  coating compositions having improved application  characteristics and/or giving improved film properties are  obtained by dispersing the microparticles in combinations  of suitable film-forming polymers and diluents.
