<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to stabilized perchlorethylene which is 0.001 to 0.1 wt.% N-alkylmorpholine, 0.001 to 0.01 wt.% alkylphenols, 0.0005 to 0.002 wt.% Diisopropylamine and 0.05 to 0.5 wt.% Cyclohexene oxide contains.By the mixture of these stabilizers, the perchlorethylene is stabilized in an excellent extent, in particular against oxidation reduction in contact with metals or metal salts, optionally under the action of light.Another improvement in the cooperation can be achieved by the addition of 0.005 to 0.1 wt.% epoxifropanol can be achieved.
