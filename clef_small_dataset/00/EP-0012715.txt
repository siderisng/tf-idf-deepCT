<ABSTRACT>
Hot-curable mixtures of one or more epoxide compounds which have an average of more than one epoxide group in the molecule, and one or more β-aminoCrotonic acid derivatives such as β-aminocroton acid adril, β-aminocrotonic acid amide or β-aminocotone acid ester, as well as optionally a polyamine, polycar bonsor anhydride ora malimide.The new hardening mixtures are characterized by excellent storage stability, can be used well and provide materials with good mechanical properties.
