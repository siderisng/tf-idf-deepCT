<ABSTRACT>
Polyalkylene terephthalate molding compounds containing a molecular weight of 200 to 2000 as a stabilizer and a catalyst of the catalyst is an alkali metal salt of a chelating agent which carries at least a -n (CH₂-COO) ₂ group in the molecule.Preferred catalysts are the trisodium salt of the Ni trilotriacetic acid and the tetranodium salt of ethylene diamine tetraacetic acid.The molding compounds can be used to produce form bodies, films, monofilaments and fibers.
