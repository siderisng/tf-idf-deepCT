<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for adjusting an implantable pacemaker, a programmer (2) and a stimulator (1) for the implementation of the method.The stimulator comprises a logical module (12) having a central unit (19) with a data register (20) in which is stored a number of different characteristics of the stimulator.The programmer (2) comprises two terminals (28a, 28b), an electrical energy source (29), an input amplifier (30), an output amplifier (31) between which is interposed a logical module (33)..
