<ABSTRACT>
a granular acidified calcium phosphate fertilizer has at  least 85% by weight of the particles having a particle size of  0. 5 - 4 mm, a total p₂o₅ content of at least 30% with 50 - 80%  of the p₂o₅ citrate soluble and 30 - 55% of the p₂o₅ water  soluble.  it may be made without the need for granulation by  mixing phosphate rock particles of less than 4 mm size and at  least 40% of which are 0. 5 - 4 mm size with at least 45% (as  p₂o₅) phosphoric acid in a weight ratio of particles to acid of  2-4:1.
