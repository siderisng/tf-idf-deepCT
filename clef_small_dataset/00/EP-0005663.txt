<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates on the one hand a therapeutic process of transmitting to a substratum Vivand any signals that can have a computer organization compatible with the computer organization and the self-regulation of the substratum in order to act on its functional behavior to balance it.or energizing it and on the other hand, a transmitting device of these signals.Each signal is constituted by a succession of magnetic fields generated by selective couplings in four homonymous strokes 5 and 7 and heteronyms 5, 6, defining a selected wave train, said wave train being repeated at least three times, theBasic frequency of magnetic waves being of the order of 40 to 80 kHz, the duration of the signal being of the order of 100 to 200 microseconds.application to the treatment of living tissues.
