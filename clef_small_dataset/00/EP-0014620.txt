<ABSTRACT>
The coplanar coupled line microwave circuit comprises on the same face of a dielectric substrate (1) a plurality of conductive strips (2A₁, 2A₂, 2AI, 2B, 2BI), forming a coupled line system.The com mun (3) of these coupled coplanar lines is itself constituted by nested conductive bands (4) in the previous ones.The proposed structure is very compact and allows for reduced circuits.On the other hand, phase speeds of the different modes of propaga tion are very close.Application to hyper-frequency circuits, in particular interdigital filters.
