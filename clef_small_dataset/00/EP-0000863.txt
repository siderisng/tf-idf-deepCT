<ABSTRACT>
To compensate the temperature cycle of integrated semiconductor resistors, the knowledge is applied that the value of a resistor is also a function of the potential difference between the resistor (8) itself and the surrounding epitaxial layer 4.The temperature compensation is effected by connecting a temperature sensor to the epitaxial layer and provides a compensation voltage which influences the potential difference in opposite directions to the resistance temperature coefficient of resistance.In this way, the resistor (8) is kept constant over a wide temperature range.
