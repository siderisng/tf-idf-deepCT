<ABSTRACT>
a grounding electrode useful in electrosurgery and easily  contoured to body surfaces which comprises a flexible, perfo­ rated metal sheet (2), the lower side of which is coated with a  conductive adhesive (6) and the upper side of which is adhe­ sively secured to a fenestrated film (1), said fenestrated film  (1) being adhesively secured to an open cell polymeric foam  (3) and menas (5) for conducting an electrical current secured  to said metal sheet (2).  preferably, the metal sheet (2) is aluminum foil, the con­ ductive adhesive (6) is a quaternary polymer and said open  cell foam (3) is polyurethane.
