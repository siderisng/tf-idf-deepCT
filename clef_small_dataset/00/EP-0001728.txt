<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a transmitting and receiving lighting diode.This diode comprises an active zone (2) which on the transmission is the seat of a strong current.The emission surface (4) has a semiconductor central portion (41) which channels the current lines and an insulating portion (40) transparent to light.At the reception the photons pass through the entire receiving surface (40-41) which is none other than the transmitting surface and the active zone (2) has a sufficiently high thickness and a sufficiently low doping for beingspace load.The invention applies to optical fiber telecommunications.
