<ABSTRACT>
for the contracting states : be, fr, gb, it, nl 1.  a process for the preparation of polymerisate foams of copolymerisates containing ethylene units, vinyl alcohol units, acrylic acid ester units and acrylic acid units or methacrylic acid units and, in some cases, vinyl ester and acrylamide, where - the ethylene proportion is 35 to 98 % by weight - the vinyl alcohol proportion is 1 to 30 % by weight - the acrylic acid proportion is 0 to 7. 38 % by weight or the methacrylic acid proportion is 0 to 2. 4 % by weight - the proportion of acrylic acid ester of secondary or tertiary alcohols is 1 to 30 % by weight and - the vinyl ester proportion is up to 5 % by weight and the acrylic acid amide proportion 0 to 2. 28 % by weight related to the entire mixture, heated to 250 to 350 degrees c under the exclusion of oxygen over a period of 5 to 60 minutes characterized in that mixtures of various ethylene copolymerisates are used.  for the contracting state : de 1.  a process for the preparation of polymerisate foams of copolymerisates containing ethylene units, vinyl alcohol units, acrylic acid ester units and acrylic acid units or methacrylic acid units and, in some cases, vinyl ester and acrylamide by heating under the exclusion of oxygen for 5 to 60 minutes to 250 to 350 degrees c, characterized in that mixtures of various ethylene copolymerisates are used, where - the ethylene proportion is 35 to 98 % by weight - the vinyl alcohol proportion is 1 to 30 % by weight - the acrylic acid proportion is 0 to 7. 38 % by weight or the methacrylic acid proportion is 0 to 2. 4 % by weight - the proportion of acrylic acid esters of secondary or tertiary alcohols is 1 to 30 % by weight and - the vinyl ester proportion is up to 5 % by weight and the acrylic acid amide proportion 0 to 2. 28 % by weight related to the entire mixture.
