<ABSTRACT>
an end fitting assembly for a conduit (10) having an  internal screen (11), comprises a nipple (15) adapted to be  inserted in at least an end portion (12) of the conduit (10) and a  sleeve (23) adapted to co-operate with the nipple (15) about  which the screen (11) can be folded, the arrangement being  such that the folded portion of the screen (11) is clamped  between the sleeve (23) and an adjacent co-operation portion  of the nipple (15).
