<ABSTRACT>
in an apparatus for feeding commercially available metallic bar stock (1, 2) successively welded by a welding machine (3) to form an endless bar (8) to a bar-processing machine tool (11), a guide unit (10) is provided which receives the endless bar (8) and feeds it on an essentially curved path to a processing unit (11), the guide path itself being determined by two limit radii (r, r') in accordance with the different processing times of welding machine (3) and processing unit (11).  <image>
