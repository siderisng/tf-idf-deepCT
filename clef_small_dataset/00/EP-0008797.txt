<TITLE>
sport and game apparatus with a ball fixed to a cord.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>
paar kurt<sep>paar, kurt<sep>paar, kurtauenstrabe 1d-8100 garmisch-partenkirchende<sep>
</APPLICANT>
<INVENTOR>
paar kurt<sep>paar, kurt<sep>paar, kurtauenstrabe 1d-8100 garmisch-partenkirchende<sep>
</INVENTOR>
<ABSTRACT>
a sport and training apparatus, e. g.  for tennis, badminton, squash and the like.  situated on a bar (1, 1a), which is expediently height-adjustable and can be set up vertically, is a bearing (2) which can be rotated freely horizontally and to the rotatable part (2b) of which there is attached a cord (5) which bears the ball (4) at its other end.  when the ball (4) is hit, it is guided on a random circular path, or on part of the latter, until it reaches the player again or the next player.  <image>
