<ABSTRACT>
a connector for coupling an optical fibre (27) to an  optoelectric device (28) comprising a one piece body (10)  including a sleeve portion (11) for receiving, in optical align­ ment, a terminated optical fibre (56) and an optoelectric  device (28) in respective opposite ends and a foot portion (14  or 15) adapted for attachment to a circuit board (53) with the  sleeve portion (11) extending generally parallel to the circuit  board (53); a retention plate (32) being attachable to an end of  the body (10) in an axial push fit to retain the optoelectric  device (28) in the sleeve (11).  the retention plate (32) may  have resilient pins (34, 35) receivable in apertures (20, 21) in  the body (10) which may be metal and provided with a trans­ verse flange (13) defining slots (18, 19) for locating a shielding  can covering the one end.
