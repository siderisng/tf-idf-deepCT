<ABSTRACT>
In the pyrogenic production of finely divided oxide of a metal or metaloid, evaporable halo gene compounds of the metal or metaloid are fed to a burner with fueling gases and oxygen.In addition to the desired metal or metalloid oxide elemental halogen falls on, which is removed from the reaction exhaust gases.For this purpose, hydrogen is conducted into the cooling section of the reaction by means of a double jacket tube (7), wherein additionally by means of the same double jacket tube (7) an inert gas, which is guided between the outer (14) and inner jacket (13) of the double jacket tube (7),is initiated.The moistened hydrogen reacts with the elemental halogen beneath the reaction temperature of the flow gas mixture, which is also in the cooling coil.
</ABSTRACT>
