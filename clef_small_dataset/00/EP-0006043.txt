<ABSTRACT>
1.  body of plastics material, in particular transparent, for an egg box, of the type formed by a generally rectangular planar sheet (3) from which downwardly project at least two longitudinal rows of upwardly open convergent cups (4) and upwardly project downwardly open central spigots (9, 10) located between these two rows and downwardly open lateral spigots (12) located alongside each longitudinal edge of the sheet in positions corresponding to the central spigots, this body being characterized in that each lateral spigot (12) has the shape of a pyramid the outer lateral face (15) of which is formed by a planar inclined face, and in that all the outer lateral faces (15) located on a same side are coplanar.
