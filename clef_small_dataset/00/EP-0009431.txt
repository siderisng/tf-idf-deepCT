<ABSTRACT>
capacitive coupling device for electronic tube which consists of a coupling electrode (2), located inside the vacuum enclosure of the tube (1), means (3) insulating this electrode of the anode (10) of the tube and means (4, 5) ensuring the connection between the insulating means (3) and on the other hand, the coupling electrode (2) and the anode (10).The connecting means (4, 5) serve as conferences for connecting the device to one or more external coopectedirs.A capacitive coupling is thus established between the coupling electrode (2) and the upper cap (9) of a tube grid, this cap being closest to the coupling electrode.
