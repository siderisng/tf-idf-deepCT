<ABSTRACT>
Isoindoline dyes of the general formula in the R¹ C₁- to C₄-alkyl, R² hydrogen or C₁ to C₄-alkyl and X oxygen, sulfur, imino or N-cyanimino, and wherein the ring C is optionally substituted by non-soluble groups.The dyes give very colorful and briilant yellow dyes in paints, printing inks and plotes, which have good to very good light and overcoat adjustments.
