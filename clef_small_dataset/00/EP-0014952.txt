<ABSTRACT>
The underside of a flexible master holding plate (5) is covered to obtain a permanently pure white, dirt and far Babweiser surface with white paper, which is laminated with a crystal-clear plastic film.The lamination may be formed by a polypropylene film and brought to the carrier layer of the holding plate by means of a commercially available cold adhesive or a hot melt adhesive.For the paper layer, art paper or synthetic paper is also usable next to normal paper.
