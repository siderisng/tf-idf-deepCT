<ABSTRACT>
continuous counter current multistage process for freeze  concentration, including a step whereby the ice crystals in the  first stage are separated from the mother liquor in a final  separation step, the ice crystals being rejected and the mother  liquor from the laste stage recovered as a product, whereby in  a stage of said process ice crystals are introduced, said ice  crystals having a larger medium size than the crystals in said  stage formed without said introduction of said ice crystals.
