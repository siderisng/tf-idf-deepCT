<ABSTRACT>
A text processing device has a display device for a single display of a text section TES.Each entered character is shown in an entrance position (13) and shifted to the left by subsequently incorporated benene.On the right of the entrance position (13), further indicators are arranged, in which when the edge zone of a line of text is reached, a number of edge zone marks (15) corresponding to the size of the edge zone (14) can be illustrated.Entering characters after reaching the edge zone (14) causes the reduction of the edge zone marks (15) in the right part of the display device by a peripheral zone mark (15) each input character.
