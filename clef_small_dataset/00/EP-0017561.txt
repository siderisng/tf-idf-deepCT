<ABSTRACT>
Device ensuring the reading of a quantity of electrical charges, then the injection into a load transfer device of a quantity of loads which depends on the quantity read previously.This device comprises a reading grid (GL) storage electrode and operating as connected to the source of a transistor MOS injection of the loads operating in saturation and consisting of two diodes (D₁ and D₂) and a control grid.(GC).
