<TITLE>
level-meter marker protractor.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
level meter marker consisting of a body equipped with a groove in which various measuring and marker elements can slide.  this body is preferably equipped with an angled spirit level.  a sliding element equipped with a blade or another cutting tool is also provided, it being possible to adjust the depth of the latter according to the thickness of the material to be cut out.  the apparatus is intended to be used in the construction and finishing of buildings.  <image>
