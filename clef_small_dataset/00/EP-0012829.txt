<ABSTRACT>
Directions (1) with a plurality of directional rollers arranged in a plane on opposite sides of the workpiece (2..,, 8, 14.,,, 16) whose axes of rotation are aligned at right angles to the passage direction of the work piece (23).At least the first three directions (14th,,, 16), the workpiece (23) can be supplied from the side (C-shaped machine structure).The first three directions (14th.,, 16) are lateral support elements (19.,,, 22) assigned to center the workpiece (23).The second roller (15) and the connected support elements (20,21) have a particularly large stroke, which is effected by a quick adjustment device.A support member (20) on the second roller (15) is eliminated to facilitate the side drop.The first three directions (14th.,, 16) are preferably formed as independent machines in a heat (25).
