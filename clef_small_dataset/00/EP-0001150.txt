<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for separating the polyolefins manufactured at low pressure to the intervention of catalysts, such as those comprising a chlorinated titanium compound.The reaction medium is added to the polymer, carbon monoxide.The method makes it possible to obtain perfectly homogeneous polyolefins that can be used for the manufacture of films and fibers.
