<ABSTRACT>
the invention is concerned with a novel solid form  cosmetic base having self-emulsifying properties and com­ prising:   a) a mixture of compounds of the formula:   moocch₂ch₂nrch₂ch₂coom   wherein r is a fatty alkyl group and m consists of salt  forming cations and hydrogen in a respective weight  ratio of from about 1:1 to about 1:10⁻¹⁰; and   b) a saturated fatty alcohol;   the weight ratio of component (a) to component (b) being  from about ¹/₁₀:1 to about ²/₁₃:1.  the cosmetic base is useful in the manufacture of cos­ metic compositions in the form of oil-in-water emulsions.
