<ABSTRACT>
The radar telemetry device comprises a first remote tracking loop of a movable echo comprising a detector (1) of a position gap of an echo signal and a trademark of telemetry, numerical computing means(2) of the distance from the echo, generating means (33) of clock pulses and generating means (3) of a reference tracking tracking window by counting clock pulses.A second analog (4) copying loop of the reference tracking window connecting the output of the discounting-counting means to the deviation sensor (1) provides time filtering from the reference continuation window.APPLICATION TO TELEMETRIC DEVICES OF PURSURING RADARS MOBILE TARGET INDICATORS.
