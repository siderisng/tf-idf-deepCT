<ABSTRACT>
Screen printing method and apparatus for the haunting printing of a web-shaped good (25), wherein the continuously continuous good (25) is guided via a side pressure roller (17) disposed on the squeegee (16) on the side of the screen printing template (23) and the good (25) During the pressure stroke on the template (23) is fed out. Rakel (16) and counterpressure roller (17) can be moved synchronously against movement in the direction of the web-shaped good (25) or the screen printing template (23) during the printing operation. the portion of the portion of the web-shaped good (25) expelling from the printing unit can at least in the region within which the counter-pressure roller is shifted during the pressure stroke to each other parallel and at an acute angle to the substantially horizontally arranged screen printing template (23 ) Run down, wherein the printing unit is moved at least during the pressure stroke parallel to the aforementioned portions of the web-shaped good (25).
