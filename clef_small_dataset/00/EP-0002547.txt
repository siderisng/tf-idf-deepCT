<ABSTRACT>
an optical read unit for reading radiation-reflecting  video discs comprises a semiconductor laser which with the  aid of a scan wobbler and a focussing wobbler is moved at  high frequency in two directions relative to the video disc, a  radial direction (s) for tracking purposes and a direction (f)  normal to the disc for the purpose of automatic focussing.   the semiconductor diode laser (41) is mounted on a metal  radiation source carrier (48) which is connected to a heat sink  (50) for carrying off the heat with a low temperature gradient,  and is driven by piezo-electric plates (45, 46).
