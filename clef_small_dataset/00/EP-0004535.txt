<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a single-stage process for the preparation of 4-methyl-5 - [(2-aminoethyl) -thiomethyl] -imidazole dihydrochloride by thiomethylation of 4-methylimidazole.The compound prepared by the method according to the invention is an intermediate for further imidazole derivatives, in particular for the medicament cimetidine.
