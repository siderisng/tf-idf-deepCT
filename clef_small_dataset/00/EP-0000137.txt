<ABSTRACT>
Polyglycol esters characterized by partial esterification of an oxalkylate of the 1,1,1-trimethylolethane or pentaerythritol having a mixture of ethylene oxide and propylene oxide in molar ratio 2: 1 to 4: 1 with substoneometric amounts of a carboxylic acid having 10 to 20 carbon atoms or an acylating derivative of suchCarboxylic acid are available as surfactants.The polyglycol esters increase or stabilize the viscosity of aqueous systems and are also suitable as emulsifiers.
