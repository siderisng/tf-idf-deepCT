<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a thyristor column of a stack of thyristors (5a) and cooling boxes (5b), two end pieces (8, 9) and a frame-like clamping device (1) made of plastic, which electrically the stack and the end pieces (8, 9)insulating comprises.The clamping device (1) consists of two same half shells (2) with grooves on the outer surfaces in which winds of glass fiber bundles (23) are wrapped after assembly, which are cast with plastic.In each of the end pieces, a clamping element (10, 11) is screwed, few of which is formed as an elastic force memory.Since the clamping device consists of a plastic and contains in the region of the stacking curved parts (4), a space-saving design is achieved.
