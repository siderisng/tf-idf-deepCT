<ABSTRACT>
Multifile still stretchable supply margin of thread-forming polyesters are claimed, which can be processed into voluminous filament yarns with a poultry-protruding filament.At least, the supply margin must consist of individual filaments, which have a knicking resistance of less than 1500 tours and a crack index of over 5.By using suitable spinning preparations, the crack index can be varied and thus the hairiness of the gerzene bombing voluminous filament yarns are adjusted independently of the crimp values and pill behavior.
