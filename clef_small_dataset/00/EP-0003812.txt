<ABSTRACT>
1.  an elastomeric thermoplastic mixture containing a) 10-70% by weight of an ethylene/propylene or ethylene/propylene/diene rubber partially crosslinked by the use of vulcanising agents and b) 30-90% by weight of polyolefin, characterised in that component a) has a gel content of 10-25% by weight, determined by stirring or shaking a sample in cyclohexane at 20 degrees c for 24 hours.
