process and apparatus for joining fluid flow ducts
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
process for joining two fluid flow ducts (1, 2) by positioning on the opposite ends of the duct sections to be joined flanges formed by frames (11, 12) consisting of hollow profiled sections (13) and of right-angled corner pieces (14), whose arms are inserted into two consecutive profiled sections, and then by joining the opposite corner pieces of the two flanges.  according to the invention, this process is characterised in that the corner pieces (14) are integral with the corresponding duct (1, 2), whereas the said profiled sections (13) are not fastened onto the ducts.  joining of fluid flow ducts.  <image>
