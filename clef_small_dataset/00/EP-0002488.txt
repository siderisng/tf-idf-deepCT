<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for producing water-emulsifiable air-drying paint binders based on modified drying oils.The oils or esters of natural or artificially produced oil fatty acids and polyols are modified by reaction with reaction products of polyethylene glycols and phenol-formaldehyde condensates and serve as raw materials for alkyd resin emulsions.The binders according to the invention have improved stability, drying and water resistance.
