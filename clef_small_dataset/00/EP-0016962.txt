In the elimination of ascorbic acid from aqueous solutions by addition of ascorbate oxidase, the solution is also used to use catalase and H₂O₂, e.g.b.in the form of an H₂O₂-containing adduct, too.A corresponding means for removing ascorbic acid in aqueous rivers has a solid, with ascorbate oxidase, catalase and H₂O₂ and optionally stabilizers and / or buffer substance impregnated carrier material, wherein preferably the H₂O₂ is present separately from the enzymes.The carrier material is itself expediently made of a first compact support material (1) at which little stens is secured to a second porous absorbent support material (2, 4) impregnated with one or more of the active ingredients.
