<ABSTRACT>
The invention is located in the field of liquid crystal displays and relates to the preparation of an orientation layer into which parallel grooves with the aid of a flat, standing shaft field are introduced. According to the invention is provided to build the wave field from ultrasonic waves. In a preferred method variant, adjacent grooves have a distance corresponding to about half the wavelength of the illumination light and contain the bays of the orientation layer located between adjacent grooves each of a strip of a conductive or dichroic material. Such a layer oriented and polarized. The strips can be obtained by giving the layer material a conductive or dichroic addition or that a strip pattern applied to the orientation layer in another step. The proposed method is suitable for all types of liquid crystal displays.
