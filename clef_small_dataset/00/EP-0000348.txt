<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to novel polyuret polyisocyanates, a process for their preparation, which consists of the primary or secondary monoamine with excess amounts of organic diisocyanates to form triauret or higher polyuret groups, as well as the use of the new compounds asIsocyanate component in the production of polyurethane imsfs.
