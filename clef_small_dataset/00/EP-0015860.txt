<ABSTRACT>
The method includes reacting at least one polyphenol alkaline salt on at least one alcohol-2,3 alkane, anhydrous and substantially aprotic, in the presence of compound of formula (I) N [CH₁-CHR₂-o (chr₃-chr₄-o) n-r₅] ₃ wherein: - n is an integer greater than or equal to 0, and less than or equal to 10, - R₁, R₂, R₃, R₄, identical or different represent an atomhydrogen or alkyl radical having from 1 to 4 carbon atoms, and R₅ represents a hydrogen atom, an alkyl or cycloalkyl radical having from 1 to 12 carbon atoms, a phenyl radical (-φ), a radical radical-CMH2M-φ or CMH2M + 1-φ- In which 1 ≦ M ≦ 12. The method is particularly suitable for the preparation of glycidic polyethers of bisphenol-a.
