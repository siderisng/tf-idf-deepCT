<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a process for recovering ruthenium present in an nitric solution.This method consists of extracting ruthenium into an organic solvent by contacting the nitric solution with an organic phase comprising an organo phosphorus compound, for example a dialkyl-dithiophosphoric acid in the presence of a compound capable of moving the ions No⁺ of the complexes of complexes.Ruthenium present in the nitric solution, for example in the presence of hydrazine or sulfamic acid.Application to the recovery of ruthenium from nitrical solutions obtained by dissolving irradiated combustible elements.
