<ABSTRACT>
In a device for the volume salt of water, a mixed bed of cation and anion exchanger resins is arranged between the terminals for the water inlet (4) and the water drain (19) of a cylindrical, press-resistant jacket (1).To simplify and exploit the replacement of the exhausted exchange resins, the mixed bed made of them is arranged in a formed as a disposable container, water-permeable cartridge (6), which is removably fixed in the jacket fuel (1) and sealed against lateral creepage.
