<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the original of an information carrier consisting of a recording layer applied to a carrier material.On the recording layer are at least four relief partial images, which is superimposed on a relief guide with different lattice depths in the areas of the individual relief partial images.In the process for producing the original, the recording layer is exposed by separate color clipping templates for violet-blue, green, yellow, and red projection colors, which are transparent in the areas of the respective projection color and their partial image areas of the projection colors overlapping-free bounds.
