<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for the preparation of aqueous suspensions containing at least 65% by weight of calcium carbonate.According to the invention, calcium carbonate is crystallized in a diluted aqueous medium, in the presence of a water-soluble polyelectrolyte, the dilute aqueous suspension of calcium carbonate is separated, a wet carbonate concentrate of calcium, using a means ofMechanical separation, and the calcium carbonate concentrate is fluidized and homogenized in the presence of a dispersing agent.The invention applies to the preparation of concentrated suspensions, stable calcium carbonate crystals, intended for the paper industry.
