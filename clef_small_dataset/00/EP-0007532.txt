<ABSTRACT>
Aqueous emulsions of polyvalent isocyanates suitable for gluing wood, in particular for the production of wooden panels are obtained by emulsifying the isocyanates by means of or in the presence of a water-soluble polymeric acid amide, e.g.b.of the polyacrylamide.
