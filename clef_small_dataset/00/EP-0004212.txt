<ABSTRACT>
The two parts 13a and 13b of the steering column have a limited relative angular set, allowing an angular offset under the action of the pilot means, which allows to move angularly the two disks rotors 26 and 27 one in relation to the'other.These discs 26 and 27 are arranged between two stators 25 and 28 having annular grooves 34 and 39, 40. The disk 26 has a circular series of passages 36 which extends according to an average radius whose value is intermediate between those of the radii.Means of two circular series of passages 43 and 44 formed in disk 27. Applications: vehicle assisted directions.
