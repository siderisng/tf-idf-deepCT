<ABSTRACT>
Aqueous ethylene-vinyl chloride-vinyl acetate and / or acrylic copolymer dispersions with inconsistent particle size distribution, wherein a particle size distribution maximum is 0.03 to 0.2 μm and a second at 0.15 to 1 μm and wherein 5 to 60 wt. % of the particles belong to the amount of particles characterized by the second maximum, are prepared by radical polymerization at 0 to 90 ° C with stirring in the presence of emulsifiers which are in amounts of 0.5 to 10 wt. %, based on solid resin, only added when a polymerization conversion of 1 to 60 wt. % of the total monomer amount is reached. The copolymer dispersions have solid contents of 20 to 70 wt. % of copolymers from 20 to 95 wt. % Vinyl chloride, 5 to 60 wt. % Ethylene and 3 to 75 wt. % vinyl ester or acrylic acid ester as well as up to 15 wt. % Further copolymerizable monomers which are metered in the production together with the emulsifiers of the polymerization approach.
</ABSTRACT>
