a process for polymerizing alpha-olefins in the presence  of a catalyst comprising two components.  component a is  obtained by mixing a milled mixture of magnesium and par­ ticulate inorganic solid with an alcohol and then contacting  the resultant product with a tetravalent halogenated titanium  compound.  component b comprises a metallic hydride or an  organometallic compound, e. g. , an organoaluminum com­ pound.
