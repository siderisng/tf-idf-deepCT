<ABSTRACT>
1.  process for washing and/or drying liquid-containing sheet products produced by precipitation with electrolytes or thermal or electric coagulation from natural and/or synthetic rubber, characterised in that a washing liquid and/or an inert gaseous medium is caused to flow through the sheets by means of a pressure difference of 1 mbars to 6 bars acting on them and the sheets are thereby freed from impurities or the washing liquid and/or dried. 
</ABSTRACT>
