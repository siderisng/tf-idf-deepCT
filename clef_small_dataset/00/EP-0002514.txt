<ABSTRACT>
1.  process for flameproofing thermoplastics by means of a mixture of flame retardants consisting of a.  10 to 80 % by weight of a brominated oligomeric styrene, b.  5 to 50 % by weight of a synergistically acting metal oxide, and c.  to 85 % by weight of a skeleton-forming filler, characterized in that the mixture is used in the form of a homogeneous concentrate obtained by melt mixing.
