<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a device for sealing between a fixed portion (10) and a tower shaft (20) of the type comprising a friction ring (22) linked to an elastic membrane (25) attached to the shaft.(20) and recalled by a spring (23) in contact with a backbone (11) fixed on the fixed part (10).According to the invention, the friction ring (22) has a smooth outer surface that tightens in the opposite direction to the contact face.
