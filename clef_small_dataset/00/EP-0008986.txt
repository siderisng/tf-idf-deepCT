<ABSTRACT>
1.  method of covering a stuffing (2) with a cover sheet (1), on which said cover sheet (1) is maintained in a conveniently stretched and folded state by button (3, 4, 11) which are maintained in a pressed condition against the stuffing (2) in which they produce a hollow zone, characterized by the steps of realizing each button with two parts (3, 4), placing a first part (3) of the button under the sheet which is folded around and under said part, locking the second part (4) of the button under the first part (3) by enclosing between said two button parts (3, 4) a sheet portion (1a) surrounding said first part of the button and folded back under it, and positioning on said stuffing said second part (4) for anchoring and hooking it in said stuffing.
