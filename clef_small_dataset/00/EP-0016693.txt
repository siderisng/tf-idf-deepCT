<ABSTRACT>
This device comprises a first glass casing (2), and a second glass casing (1) so as to create an enclosure (4) between these two envelopes.The first envelope (2) is associated with means (3) absorbing solar radiation constituted by a coating deposits on its outer wall comprising a first layer (31) of a glass-metal compound as well as at least a second layer(32) of an oxide of this metal, absorbing solar energy.A layer (30 ') of the glass-metal compound can also be deposited on the inner wall of the first envelope (2).
