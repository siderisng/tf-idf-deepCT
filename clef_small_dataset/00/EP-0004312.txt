<ABSTRACT>
1.  a planing head, in particular for portable planers, having exchangeable knives and at least one stop element (20) which is adjustably connected to the planing head (1) and which serves as stop for the associated knife when the latter is being adjusted to the cutting circle (16), characterized in that each stop element (20) comprises a stop cone (21) the axis of which extends substantially vertically to the plane of the knife (14) which is of the reversible type, that the stop cone (21) can be adjusted along its axis and that the inner cutting edge (15) of the reversible knife (14) rests again the said stop cone.
