<ABSTRACT>
an optical circulator comprising a first optical path and a  second optical path formed between a first polarizer element  (p1) and a second polarizer element (p2), faraday rotators  (fr1, fr2) interposed in the first and second optical paths, the  polarizing direction of the faraday rotators being rotated by  an angle of 45° by a magnetic field and the direction of polariz­ ing rotation being reversed with respect to the travelling  direction of light which passes therethrough, and polarizing  direction rotators (oa1, oa2) interposed in the first and sec­ ond optical paths, the polarizing direction of the polarizing  drection rotators being rotated by an angle of 45° and the  direction of polarizing rotation being non-reversed with  respect to the travelling direction of light which passes there­ through.  
</ABSTRACT>
