<ABSTRACT>
a process for racemizing an optically active phenyl­ acetic acid of the formula (i):    wherein x an y, which may be the same or different, each  represents a hydrogen atom, a halogen atom, a lower alkyl  group, a lower alkoxy group, a halogen-substituted lower  alkyl group or a halogen-substituted lower alkoxy group,  or x and y may jointly form an alkylenedioxy group; m and  n, which may be the same or different, each represents an  integer of 1 to 5, and the sum of m and n does not exceed 5;  and r represents an isopropyl group or a cyclopropyl group,  comprises heating the acid to a temperature of at least  150°c in the presence or absence of an inert solvent.
