<ABSTRACT>
a nozzle injection unit for an oil fired fluid combustor  has a pair of co-axial tubes (7, 8) sealed together at one  end.  the outer tube (7) has an inlet and lateral outlets (5)  near the sealed end and is connected to an air supply.  the  inner tube (8) is connected to a fuel oil supply and has a  number of circumferential lateral outlets (9) communicating  with the outer tube.  the oil emerging from the inner tube  is carried by the air flow in the outer tube into the fluidised  bed where it is ignited and consumed.
