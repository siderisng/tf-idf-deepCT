<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a continuous process for the manufacture of carboxylic peracids.The method includes reacting the carboxylic acid corresponding with the hydrogen peroxide in the presence of a catalyst and an inert organic liquid, solvent of the peracid and capable of forming with water a heterogeneous azeotrope, to eliminate from theIn the reaction mixture by distillation of organic water-liquid azeotrope, and to maintain in the reaction mixture sufficient water for formed a distinct aqueous phase of an organic phase containing the organic liquid.
