<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a dental X-ray diagnostic device with a vertical axis-rotatable unit consisting essentially of an X-ray tube (4) and a film carrier (6) and an intermediate, for the installation or system of a patient head (8) serving head holder () . The head holder (7) is arranged to fix the head for different object shots in different positions (I-III) in a horizontal plane against a holder (26) adjustable. The adjustable positions (I-III) are marked according to the various object shots on a overview field (14) and selectable by position selector (29-31). With the position selectors (29-31), a sampling device (19-25) is coupled with signal generators (23-25) through which the position of the head holder (7) is detected with respect to its holder (26) and those upon reaching a selected Position a signal to an indicator arranged in the overview field (14) and assigned to the respective position selector (29-31) assigned to the display member (35-37).
