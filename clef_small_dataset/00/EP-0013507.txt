<ABSTRACT>
a high-silicon-nickel-chromium steel resistant to concen­ trated nitric acid with a good workability and a good weldabil­ ity, comprises   carbon in an amount of not more than 0. 03% (c ≦ 0. 03%),  silicon in an amount of from more than 5% to not more than  7% (5% < si ≦ 7%),   manganese in an amount of not more than 10% (mn ≦  10%),   chromium in an amount of from not less than 7% to not  more than 16% (7% ≦ cr ≦ 16%),   nickel in an amount of from not less than 10% to less than  19% (10% ≦ ni < 19%),   and the balance being iron and inevitable impurities,  percentages being by weight.  this steel can be used to provide corrosion resistant sur­ faces in apparatuses to be brought into contact with concen­ trated nitric acid.  
</ABSTRACT>
