<TITLE>
acylacetanilide compounds and their use as yellow forming colour couplers for silver halide photography.

<ABSTRACT>
novel colour forming couplers for use in silver halide colour  photography are described.  these couplers upon  oxidative coupling with an aromatic primary amino colour  developing agent produce dyes of improved stability against  light and dyes having favourable spectral absorption characteristics.   the couplers have high coupling activity and thus  produce high colour densities.  the novel couplers are acylacetanilide compounds  whereof the phenyl nucleus of the anilide group carries  oxyhydrocarbon groups, preferably alkoxy groups in the 2-,4-  and 5-position with respect to the amido group.  
</ABSTRACT>
