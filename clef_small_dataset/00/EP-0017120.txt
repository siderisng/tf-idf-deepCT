<ABSTRACT>
Textile detergent, which is 0.5 to 10 wt.-% of quaternary groups comprising ethers of polysaccharides containing, possessing apprenting and rigid properties and make a corresponding aftertreatment of the wasted textiles superfluous.The polysaccharidether preferably derived from the polygalactomannan of the commercial and starch and contain per anhydrogalactoman NaN unit or anhydroglucose unit 0.05 to 0.2 N-trialkyl-N-alkyloxy groups.The surfactants contained in the detergents are nonionic and possibly zwitterionisch, but not anionic.Builder salts and other usual detergent components can also be present.
