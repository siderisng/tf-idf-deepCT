<TITLE>
circuit arrangement for the ungrounded transmission of signals across points of isolation in telecommunication installations.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a circuit arrangement for the ground free transmission of signals via separation points in telecontrols with the aid on the primary side of the separation point signal controlled high-frequency vibrations, which are evaluated on the second of the separation point by equalization for reproducing the signals.In addition to the secondary use signal evaluation, a DC voltage is derived from the high-frequency vibrations, which is used to compensate for the voltage component corresponding to the amplitude of the signal-controlled high-frequency voltage voltage in the evaluation of the rectified signals.
</ABSTRACT>
