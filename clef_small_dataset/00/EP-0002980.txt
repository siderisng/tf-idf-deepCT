<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to the storage and handling devices of propulsion powders and in particular the hoppers and containers for such powders.The propulsive powder (1) hopper (2) comprises an upper (4) loading opening and a lower powder flow (5) (2).This hopper (1) comprises a lateral vent (13) comprising means for preventing powder (2) from exiting by this vent;An area (12) of the side wall of the hopper substantially located between the vent (13) and the loading opening (4) is carried out in a destructible material under the combined effects of the pressure applied by the powder and flamesgenerated outside the hopper near this area (12).Application to the handling of propulsive powders.
