<ABSTRACT>
The stabilized powdery red phosphorus consists of a homogeneous mixture of red phosphorus, aluminum hydroxide and lead athydroxide as oxidation stabilizers.Aluminum hydroxide and lead hydroxide are in the homogeneous mixture in a total amount of about 0.1 to 6 wt.% based on the amount of red phosphorus included.
