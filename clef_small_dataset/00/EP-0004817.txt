<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an apparatus for completely or partially extracting the pulp of a fruit according to a cylindrical block or stacking circular slices after separation from the central fibrous material.It comprises a central axis 1 carrying a cookiece equipped with a maneuver handle 2 under which are implanted sharp means such as a blade 3 intended to cut the pulp according to a cylindrical cutting plane.One or more radial blades 7 fixed around the central axis 1 cut the pulp according to one or more horizontal planes.The device applies to all fruits or vegetables with bark, and more particularly pineapple.
