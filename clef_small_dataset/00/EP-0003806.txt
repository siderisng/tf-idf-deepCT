<ABSTRACT>
the apparatus has a cylindrical housing (10) with a hopper (11).  in this housing (10), which is closed on one side, a screw conveyor (20) is rotatably mounted in a self-supporting manner and coupled to a drive unit.  the open side of the housing (10) is provided with resilient holding elements (16) which reduce the cross-section of the outlet opening.  in the lower peripheral region, the housing (10) is provided with openings (18).  this apparatus is simple and robust in terms of structure and permits fully automatic dewatering and compacting of the material fed in via the hopper (11).  <image>
