<ABSTRACT>
The solenoid valve with electronic control for the mounting of the suit has a pathogen coil (AE) which receives in the suit phase via a closed switch (T2) an increased excitation stream.In the holding phase, the current coil (AE) is supplied to a retaining stream at the open switch (T2), which is lower than the excitation current.For controlling the switch (T2), a timer is provided, which is formed from a resistor (R2) and a capacitor (C2).The solenoid valve is optional with alternating current or DC power.A part of the exciter coil (AE) is formed from the series resistor (RV) formed as a substitute wire and is wound on the exciting coil (AE).
