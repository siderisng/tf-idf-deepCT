<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to 40-50 wt.% finely divided, stable, aqueous dispersions (particle diameter 0.1-0, 15μ) of copolymers of copolymerized units of 20-48 wt.-% acrylonitrile, which can be partially replaced by styrene, 47.5 - 70.5 wt.-% n-butyl acrylate, 3.5 - 4.5 wt.-% methacrylic acid which may be replaced up to 50% by acrylic acid and 0 - 5 wt.-% N-methoxymethyl-methycrylamide prepared according to a specific method characterized by a critical combination of known process measures.The dispersions are excellently suited as binders in unpigmented or pigmented, aqueous dispersion coating means for metals, wood, plastics and mineral substrates.
</ABSTRACT>
