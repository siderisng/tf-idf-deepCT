<ABSTRACT>
a process for surface treating an aluminium article  comprises anodically oxidizing the article and dipping the  anodically oxidized article in a polymer latex.  to render  the polymer coating layer this formed water-insoluble, the  polymer latex is prepared by polymerizing one or more  ethylenically unsaturated monomers in such a manner that  polymer particles of the polymer latex are negatively char­ ged, the polymer latex being adjusted to a ph value be­ low 3.
