A telephony with a housing includes a dialer and a station circuit whose speech capsule and hearing capsule are arranged in the handset.In the housing of the telephone application integral is a call-bearing response with at least one first memory to save the speech and reproduction of an announcement text to the speech veins.In the housing, a programmable control circuit is further provided, which is actuated by selecting buttons bar and serves for controlling and selecting the operating state and operation of the answering machine.Further, a receiving channel and a playback channel are arranged in the housing, which are optionally connected to the first memory to receive or reproduce the announcement text.
