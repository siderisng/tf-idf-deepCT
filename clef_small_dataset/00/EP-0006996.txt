<ABSTRACT>
For liquid crystal segment displays, an overwater circuit is proposed: A test line is connected to the segment electrodes.At least in part of the segments, the voltage levels of control and test line are compared, and when not matching the display is controlled.For multi-digit displays in multiplex mode, the testing line of a segment may be the control line of a notable, equal segment.
