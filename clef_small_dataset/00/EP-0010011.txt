<ABSTRACT>
Centrifugal pump, of the type comprising a body, a rotating shaft in the body via a bearing, a turbine attached to the shaft and, between the turbine and the bearing, a rubbing liner with rubbing rings (10) comprising an internal sleeve (11) in the ELAS mattestomaged on the shaft, said sleeve comprising a portion (12) adjacent to the turbine on which is attached one of the rings and at the end side of the bearing.A radial scrub (15) to maintain the stain lineup in the assembled state before assembly.The sleeve extends axially, beyond the said radial scrub (15), by a lip (16) which rubs on the adjacent wall of the bearing.
