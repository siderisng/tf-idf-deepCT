<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to density polycrystalline moldings of α-silicon carbide with small amounts of aluminum, additional carbon, optionally nitrogen and an extremely low residual oxygen content, wherein practically only the carbon is detectable as a separate phase.The molded bodies are characterized by a high heat resistance with bending strengths of at least 500 N / mm² up to 1. 600 ° C and a transcrystalline break mode.For the production of the shape body, α-silicon carbide in submicron powder form together with small amounts of an aluminum-containing additive, such as aluminum powder or a non-oxidic aluminum compound and a carbon-containing additive, such as carbon black, phenol-formaldehyde condensation products or coal tenerpech under shaping pressed and then at temperaturesFrom 2. 000 ° to 2. 300 ° C depressurised.
