<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an angle adapter for an electrical component (1), in particular for a relay.It consists of a base body with a support plate (11) for the component (1) and a connection strip (12) for receiving connection pins (13).Both parts are made of plastic and at least the terminal strip (12) is formed as a flexible conductor, wherein the connecting elements between the terminals (2) of the component (1) and the terminal pins (13) of the adapter are formed as conductor tracks.
