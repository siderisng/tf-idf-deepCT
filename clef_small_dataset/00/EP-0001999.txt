<ABSTRACT>
Methods for obtaining maleic anhydride of maleic anhydride-containing distillation residues, as accrued in the technical work-up of raw maleic anhydride to pure maleic anhydride, by distillation with the addition of phthalic anhydride-containing technical residues.The yield of maleic anhydride in the maleic anhydride workup process is increased by about 5 to 8% by the method according to the invention.
