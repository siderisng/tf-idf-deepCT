<ABSTRACT>
In a virtual addressing device for use in small computers, the basic address register is divided into segment register (24) and distance registers (22).This facilitates the arithmetic operations because only the spacer part of the virtual address must be processed by the computing unit (26).The segment part does not burden the aluminum equipped with only narrow data channels.This significantly increases the power of small computers.
