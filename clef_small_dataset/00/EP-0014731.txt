<ABSTRACT>
Fire protection components for thermoplastic plastics to be added to the plastic granules prior to processing must have previously had a melting point, which is under the processing temperature of the plastic to avoid inhomogeneous sites with deteriorated physical properties in the moldings.In contrast, according to the invention, two organic bromine compounds are used as fire protection components, of which one melts in the range of 80 to 200 ° C and the other in the range of 280 to 400 ° C.Thus, for the fire protection equipment of polystyrene and ABS polymeric mixtures of decabromiphenyl ether and tetrabrombisphenol A, for glass fiber reinforced polybutylene glycolterephthalate mixtures of polytetrabromxylene terephthalate and tetrabrombisphenol A and polyolefins mixtures of polytribromine styrene and tetrabrombisphenol A-dibromopropyl ether.
