<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to novel esters of the glyzerin, in which one of the OH groups of the glycerin is esterified with a phenolecarboxylic acid, while the two other OH groups are esterified by long-chain alcohols, amines, mercaptane or phenol compounds substituted phosphorus or phosphoric acid.The products serve as light and thermal stabilizers for plastics.They are characterized by high hydrolysis and extraction resistance.
