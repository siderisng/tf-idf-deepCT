<ABSTRACT>
For manufacturing control in the production of in particular deep-drawn pipes, it is very important to control the thickness of the tube.Since the pipes are redglow during production, an arrangement of the X-ray radiator and the filter within the pipe is not considered.The invention therefore provides that the radiator (1) is arranged outside the tube (2) and this tangent is irradiated.The maxima and minima of the radiation relief generated in a plane perpendicular to the pipe axis indicate the position of the pipe outside and inside wall (7, 8).From this, the wall thickness (D) of the tube can be derived.
</ABSTRACT>
