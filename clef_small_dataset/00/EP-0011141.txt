<ABSTRACT>
the invention relates to a process and an apparatus for recovering the heat from the vapours generated during the production of beer.  during the preparation of beer, vapours form in the wort kettles which are extracted from the copper and are often cooled in copper-steam condensers.  in this arrangement, only a relatively small part of the heat of the vapours is used e. g.  for the preparation of hot water.  in order to be able to use the vapour heat completely and without environmental pollution, according to the invention it is proposed that the vapours be cooled in the heat exchanger (6) with the working medium of a closed-loop system in which, after being heated in the heat exchanger (6), the working medium is then decompressed (3), liquefied (4) and converted (7) to a higher pressure in the course of the operation.  <image>
</ABSTRACT>
