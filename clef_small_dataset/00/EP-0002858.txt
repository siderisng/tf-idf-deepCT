<ABSTRACT>
Method for the rehabilitation of sewage treatment plants in which at least one treatment stage is overloaded in continuous operation, wherein the total amount of wastewater or a substream thereof is supplied from and / or after the overloaded treatment stage (3) of a filtering device (1) of hanging woven filter elements, there on the tissue a filter layer from the solid particles located in the water and hereby not only separates the deductible substances, but also colloids and dissolved water ingredients by gravity filtration and the draining filtrate as such or after untreated partial flow untreated in the usual manner and a sewage treatment plant for carrying out this Method. The construction of the sewage treatment plant can be variable z. b. If the filtration device can be arranged in each case via the existing treatment basin, or it may be equipped with a gravity filter, which is combined with a biological filter to a building.
