<ABSTRACT>
This device is intended to avoid the disadvantages of conventional tie rod and tensioners and notably ensure a corrected bush centering with respect to the heel of the nozzle.It mainly comprises a support (11, 12) attached to the shield (1) of the blast furnace, which delimits the slides in which a plate (41) integral with the lower cone (4) is received (4) from the windshield.Means are also provided for securing the cone of its support before disassembly, means which are dismissed in normal functioning.
