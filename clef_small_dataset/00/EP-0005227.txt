<ABSTRACT>
Akaricides and insecticidal means containing a combination of compound I (7-chloro-bicyclo [3.2.0] hepta-2,6-diene-6-yl-dimethylphosphate) with II (2-sec. -Butyl-4,6-dinitrophenyl-3-methyl crotonate) or III (2-sec-butyl-4,6-dinitrophenyl-isopropyl carbonate), wherein the weight ratio of the compound I to the compounds II and III preferably 1:20 to 20: 1which is characterized by a synergistic effect against acarides and their eggs, as well as against insects.
