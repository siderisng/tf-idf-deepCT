<ABSTRACT>
the method involves first heating the film and then passing it between an embossing roll (3) and a rubber roll (4).  during the period in which the film is in contact with the embossing roll, it is both embossed and perforated.  it is possible to carry out the method in two operations, either separated in time or separated in position on the embossing roll.  <image>
