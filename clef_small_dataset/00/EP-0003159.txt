<ABSTRACT>
an ethylene polymer having an improved processability  particularly in injection moulding processes, has a density in  the range of about 0. 940-0. 960, a melt index in the range  100-200 and preferably a ratio of weight-average molecular  weight to number-average molecular weight of less than 5.   the polymer is copolymer of ethylene and at least one  Î±-olefin having 4-10 carbon atoms, e. g.  butene-1, hexene-1  or: octene-1.  the preferred polymer is a copolymer of  ethylene and butene-1.  the polymers may be used in the  injection moulding of thin-wall containers e. g.  containers  having a wall thickness of less than 0. 7 mm, especially a  thickness of less than 0. 5 mm and in particular a thickness of  less than 0. 4 mm.
