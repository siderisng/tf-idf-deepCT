<ABSTRACT>
Self-extinguishing thermoplastic molding compositions based on rubber-modified styrene-acrylonitrile copolymers containing halogenated styrene oligomers as effective flame retardants.The effective flame retardant is sufficient thermostable, does not interfere with processability on conventional Maschi NEN, does not tend to corrosion and coating on these machines.In addition, the impact strength of the polymers is almost hardly affected by the addition.The effective flame retardant generally has a degree of polymerization between 3 and 200;It can be incorporated in the usual way into the polymer, the polymer may optionally contain additional synergists and processing aids incorporated in the usual manner and in conventional quantities.
</ABSTRACT>
