<ABSTRACT>
To completely remove pollutants-containing solid and / or liquid media of, for example, fixed documents or liquid surfaces, a negative pressure is generated by a water jet pump (18) up to 0.1 bar through which the media can be sucked from nearly 10 meters over height differences.In this case, the water jet pump (18) is located in a self-contained water cycle (14, 16, 18, 20, 22).As a drive unit for the water circulation, a centrifugal pump (22) is preferably used in the form of a fire-extinguishing pump.In order to prevent the pollutants from entering the water cycle, there is a collecting container (32) in the suction line for the media containing the pollutants.
