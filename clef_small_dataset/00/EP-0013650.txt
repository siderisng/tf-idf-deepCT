<ABSTRACT>
sealing glass with a coefficient of absorption of the infrared rays of at least 98% (in the range of 0.7 ± 4 microns), a means of average thermal expansion between 20 and 300 ° C between 88 and92.5 x 10⁻⁷ / ° C and low volatilization at the sealing temperature, characterized in that it has the following composition, by weight: SiO₂: 67 to 73%, A1₂O₃: 3.5 to 6, 0%, Li₂O: 1.0 to 3.0% Na₂O: 10 to 16%, Bao: 3.0 to 5.9%, FE₂O₃: 2.0 to 4.0%, F: 0 to 0.4%, TIO₂: 0 to 3%, Zo₂: 0 to 3%, ZNO: 0 to 2%, with TiO₂ + ZO₂ + Zno: 0 to 4%, the sum of the above constituents representing at least 98% by weight of the glass.Use in the manufacture of flexible metal blade switches.
</ABSTRACT>
