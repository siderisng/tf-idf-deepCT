<ABSTRACT>
to avoid the formation of burr on the inside when welding steel pipes, a device is proposed which is supported in the interior of the pipe (1) and with which a sliding block (9) is hydraulically pressed from the inside up to the weld (5, 28).  the device is here supported on the inner wall of the pipe (1) opposite the weld (5, 28) by an expanding lever (18, 19) which is designed as a bell-crank lever and on one leg (18) of which a hydraulic cylinder-piston unit (20) acts, as a result of which the other leg (19), arranged essentially parallel to the pipe axis, and thus the sliding block (9) are pressed against the inner wall of the pipe (1).
