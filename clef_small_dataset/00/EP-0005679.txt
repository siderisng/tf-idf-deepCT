<ABSTRACT>
New continuous process of obtaining pure alumina comprising in new combination, the chlorosulfur attack (b) of aluminous ores aluminously containing impurities, followed by a concentration of the liquor obtained after etching and separating the sterile (S₂), the Sulfuric treatment of the concentrated liquor, then its chlorination (G) causing the precipitation of almost all the hexahydrate aluminum chloride, the separation of the hexahydrate aluminum chloride precipitate (S₆) and chlorosulfuric mother liquor (L6 ) charged in impurities, the pyrohydrolysis of the precipitate giving the pure alumina sought with recycling of hydrochloric acid, the degassing (k) of the chlorosulfuric impurity liquor with recycling of the gaseous HCI in chlorination (G) and the Obtaining a sulfuric liquor of impurities (L11), finally the removal of impurities present in this last liquor as a sulfur precipitation by concentration (l) in prese NCE of potassium ions.
</ABSTRACT>
