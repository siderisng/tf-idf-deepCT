<ABSTRACT>
In a gas discharge display with cathode strips (8.9), the attachment is carried out according to the invention, the cathode strips (8.9) by means of pressed spring washers (10.11) on short pins (6.7), which in the RÖH Renrückwand (2) are melted to fasten.The cathode strips (8.9) may move on the pins (6, 7) and the spring washers (10, 11) in the longitudinal direction.
