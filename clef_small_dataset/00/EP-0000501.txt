<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a compressed gas switch, in particular a blow piston switch, with a movable insulating nozzle which surrounds the extinguishing distance and which forms a closure member for the gas flow channel with the aid of pressure springs.According to the invention, at least one pressing spring (32) is coupled to the relative movement of the contacts (2,4) so that their effect is canceled on the insulating material body (6) as soon as the minimum extinguishing distance is reached when the switch is open.The arrangement has the advantage that the installation of the arc does not use before the switching contacts have reached their deletion distance.
