<ABSTRACT>
apparatus to feed sheets one at a time from a stack  comprises a separator (10), rotating about a plane substantially  normal to the stack (30).  the separator rotation  axis is tiltable in a first direction to contact the stack on one  side to shingle the stack and move the top sheet rearwardly  from under a restraint (51) and in a second direction to  contact the stack on the other side to restore the stack to  its unshingled state and move the top sheet over the restraint  to a sheet feed roller (52).
