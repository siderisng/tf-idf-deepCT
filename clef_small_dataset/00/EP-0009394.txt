<ABSTRACT>
flocs prepared by mixing cationic and anionic fine  particle size ion exchange resins are useful in filtration and  ion exchange processes.  such flocs comprising weakly  acidic and weakly basic ion exchange resins may be regen­ erated thermally.  the resins comprise substantially spheri­ cal beads of crosslinked copolymer having diameters of  0. 01 to 1. 5 micrometers and bearing 0. 7 to 1. 5 ion exchange  functional groups per monomer unit.
