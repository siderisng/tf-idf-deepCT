<ABSTRACT>
the invention relates to a guide rail (18) for sliding doors (2).  in the region of the top side of the sliding door, arranged on this is at least one roller (16), by means of which the sliding door is movable along the guide track.  the guide rail (18) extends downwards in the closing direction.  at the same time, the inclination of the guide rail (18), at least within a particular portion (22), decreases essentially continuously at an increasing distance from the lower end (20).  <image>
