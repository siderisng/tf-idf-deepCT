<ABSTRACT>
1.  an apparatus for the even distribution of materials such as fertilizers with a supply box (1) for the material, two trough-like booms (2, 3) running out each on one side of the box from an outlet of the same, an endless conveyor (7), placed in each case, in one of the booms, the conveyors having driving dogs (13) and having upper runs (8) for running over inner floor structures (18) for conveying said material outwards towards the outer ends of the booms and with pushing out part placed thereunder for causing distribution of the material, said pushing out parts moving along an endless part, characterized in that the inner floor structure (18) has evenly spaced openings (19) for the material to make its way through and in that under the inner floor structure (18) there is a gutter (26), in which the finger-like dogs (13) used for pushing out the material, of the conveyors of the lower run (9) are moved in endless paths, the lengthways edges of the gutter having sides (27, 28) over which the material is forced by the dogs (13).
