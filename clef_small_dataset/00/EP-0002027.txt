<ABSTRACT>
Improvement of the method for producing 2-butene-1-ol compounds by isomerization of 3-butene-1-ol compounds in the presence of palladium catalysts and hydrogen by doping the palladium catalyst with selenium or tellurium.The 2-butene-1-ole prepared according to the invention are partly solvent, on the other part valuable starting materials for the production of solvents, dyes, paints, paints and pest control agents.
