<ABSTRACT>
The method for the columns of heavy coal coating fabrics is carried out by thermal cleavage of an insert after its hydrogenating pretreatment.As an insert, an atmospheric residue is used, which is initially subjected to vacuum distillation.Asphalt ingredients are separated from the vacuum residue formed.The asphalt-free vacuum residue is blended with vacuum gas oil and supplied to the hydrogenation.
