<ABSTRACT>
In a transport line for hot fluids, the flow space (2) of the fluid is used at least partially surrounding ceramic hollow body (3) for internal insulation of the transport line, which are arranged at a low distance to the outer wall (1) of the transport line.In order to be able to absorb high fluid pressure and high axial flow pressure losses, the ceramic hollow body (3) by clamping elements (5), which enclose the hollow body on its side facing the outer wall (1) of the transport line, is biased in the unloaded state to pressure.
