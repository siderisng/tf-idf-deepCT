<ABSTRACT>
the access arrangement with keys for a television set controlled electronically and having a dialogue and distribution function comprises a manual apparatus and a table apparatus.  the manual apparatus contains within the keys field (a) operable means as basic functions and may have in the space located opposite this field a second field with means operable as control functions.  the latter means are arranged in a similar manner in the right hand part of the keyboard (b) of the table apparatus.  the major part of the keyboard (b) of the table apparatus is occupied by operable means for alphanumeric signs.  the other means correspond to editing and special sign functions.  the means located on the upper left hand side comer correspond to switchings intended for the implementing of extended dialogue functions.  the manual apparatus is usable only for simple distribution and dialogues.  the table apparatus may be operated only with the manual apparatus.  the parts functionnally connected in particular for the switching on the keyboards (a) and (b) are arranged taking into account the anthropotechnics. 
</ABSTRACT>
