<ABSTRACT>
Process for preparing thieno derivatives (2,3-C) and (3,2-C) pyridines of the following respective formulas: wherein R₁ and R₂ represent hydrogen or alcoyl, and their addition salts with pharmaceutically acceptable acids, characterized in that: a) a compound of formula VII or VIII is condensed with a sulfonyl chloride of Formula CISO₂R₃, wherein R₃ is an alkyl radical or a phenyl radical optionally substituted with a halogen atom or a lower alkyl, in a biphasic solvent system and in the presence of sodium carbonate; b) The alcohols obtained from Formula V or VI: C) are treated by the following formula III or IV ketones: by a basic agent of formula Ro⁻m⁺, in which R is an alkyl alkyl radical. alkoyl and M⁺ is a cation of an alkali metal, in an alcohol of Formula RoH as a solvent and at the reflux temperature of the reaction mixture, to obtain respectively the compounds of formula I or II respectively. The derivatives of formula I are endowed with an anti-inflammatory activity.
