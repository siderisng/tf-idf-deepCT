<ABSTRACT>
1.  time switch with a member determining the time and a contact device controllable thereby, whereby the member determining the time is formed as an electronic generator of which the period is shorter than a preselected time interval to be measured, and whereby the frequency of the generator is divided in a resetable electronic frequency divider, characterized in that the time switch is adapted to be connected to a common switching-on and reswitching line (e, n), in such a way, that the frequency divider is at least partially resetable from any state through this common line.
