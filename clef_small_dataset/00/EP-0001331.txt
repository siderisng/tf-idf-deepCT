<ABSTRACT>
herbicidal phosphonates and preparation and use  thereof having the general formula    wherein r and r₁ are various aliphatic or aromatic organic  groups, q is hydrogen or chlorine, x is oxygen or sulfur and r₂  is alkyl, alkenyl or cycloalkyl.  the compounds may be made  by a variety of procedures, mostly involving the introduction  or abstraction of chlorine at the 2-position.  they can be formulated  for agricultural use with a surface active agent and/or a  solid or liquid diluent, and can be used pre- or post-emergence  for controlling broadleaved and grassy weeds.   they show a selective action in certain crops, e. g.  cotton,  soybeans and sugarbeet.
