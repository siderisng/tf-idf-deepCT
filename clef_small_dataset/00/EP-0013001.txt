in a trans port wagon (1, 2, 3, 4) arranged on one above a furnace for heating glass and subsequent bending tion (5), on which the glass panes on pliers are arranged-shaped gripping devices (33) are arranged hanging , a horizontal support bolt (16) for the tongs-shaped gripping devices (33) in the middle rigidly attached to the base pike (1) of the trolley, while it is releasably supported on the remaining locations in the horizontal direction. The trolley can be locked in the bending station by a coupling (24, 39). The coupling part (24) arranged on the trans port carriage is in the middle the carriage (1), d. H. In the area where the support bar (16) is rigidly connected to the trolley (1).
