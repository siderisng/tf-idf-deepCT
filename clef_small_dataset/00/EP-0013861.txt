<ABSTRACT>
in the training appliance, the one end of a bar (11) is mounted so as to be swivellable on a frame (1, 2, 3, 4) and its other end bears a ball (12) which is held in a hitting position by a fixing stop (9) determining an end position of the bar (11).  a spring element (7), likewise arranged on the frame (1, 2, 3, 4), automatically moves the bar (11), which is moved out of its starting position under the effect of a hit exerted on the ball (12) by a tennis racket, back into the starting position.  <image>
