<ABSTRACT>
A low voltage circuit breaker has an insulating material housing (1), which is divided into a top (3) and a lower part (4) along a partial joint (2).At at least one side wall (10) of the lower part (4), a groove starting at the partial carrier (2) and up to the lower edge of the lower part (4) groove groove is provided, in which leads to an auxiliary device (11) are laid out.For these lines (20 to 27) an outlet is provided on the partial carrier (2).The groove (14) makes it possible to arrange several same low voltage circuit breakers with connected auxiliary wires directly next to each other.
