<ABSTRACT>
The device uses the optical properties of a light beam through which fluids are presented, which are brought, by a vertical path, in the space (III ') between two transparent glass blades (III).The fluids used are locked in a fixed volume, deformable under the action of a geared motor, with the volume of fluids, in the chassis (VIII) supporting the screen.The power supply of the motor is made using secure connection integral with the hinges of the chassis where the screen device is mounted.
