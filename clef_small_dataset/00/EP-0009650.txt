<ABSTRACT>
the invention provides a package for the containment  and display of goods.  the package has a base and a lid, and the lide is pivotable  to an open position for display of the goods.  an over centre  link (28) connects the base and lid so as to urge the base and  lid to the closed position or to the open position depending  upon whether or not the over centre link is to one or the other  side of the over centre position.
