method of preparation of o-alkyl-s-alkyl-dithiophosphoric acid chlorides

<ABSTRACT>
o-alkyl s-alkyl dithiophosphoric acid chlorides are prepared by reaction of salts of o,s-dialkyl dithiophosphoric acid esters with phosphorus pentachloride in the absence of a diluent or solvent.  phosphoric acid ester salts employed here are those which are liquid under the reaction conditions.  according to this process, o-alkyl s-alkyl dithiophosphoric acid chlorides of the formula <image> can be prepared in which r<1> and r<2> are identical or different and denote alkyl radicals having 1 to 4 carbon atoms.  these phosphoric acid chlorides are useful starting substances for the synthesis of insecticidal active substances.
