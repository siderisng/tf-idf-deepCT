<TITLE>
process for the preparation of acetoacetylaryl amides.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>
ciba geigy agch<sep>ciba-geigy ag<sep>ciba-geigy agklybeckstrasse 141 4002 baselch<sep>
</APPLICANT>

</INVENTOR>
<ABSTRACT>
acetoacetylaryl amides are prepared by addition of diketene to primary carbocyclic or heterocyclic aromatic amines, the diketene being metered into the heterogeneous suspension of the amine in a solvent or solvent mixture consisting of saturated aliphatic and/or cycloaliphatic hydrocarbons having 5 to 18 c atoms.  this novel process permits extremely pure products to be obtained in almost quantitative yield and under ecologically problem-free conditions. 
</ABSTRACT>
