<ABSTRACT>
Preparation of Nitro-2 methyl-2 propanol by reaction of Nitro-2 propane and formaldehyde, mostly in the form of polyoxymethylene, in a molar ratio of between 0.9: 1 and 1.1: 1, in the middleconcentrated and in the presence of 1 to 10 milliequivalents per mole of nitro-2 propane of a mineral basic catalyst, with stirring, at temperature between 40 and 58 ° C;the reaction medium pH is between 7 and 11;The reaction takes place in the presence of a quantity of water representing 1.5 to 10% by weight of the entire reaction mass.The method makes it possible to obtain the Nitro-2 methyl-2 propanol in crystalline form of high purity and with returns greater than 95% o.
