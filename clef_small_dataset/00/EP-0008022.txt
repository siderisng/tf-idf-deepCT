Preparation of saturated aliphatic, cycloali phatic and araliphatic aldehydes by selectively hydrogenating the corresponding olefinically unsaturated aldehydes with hydrogen to palladium-containing catalysts in the liquid phase by using a catalyst system whose active ingredients of 2 to 90 wt.% palladium and 10 to 98 wt.% of an oxide or a salt of a rare earth metal or a mixture of different such oxides and / or salts.
