<ABSTRACT>
Device for protecting an inertia flywheel (2) with a horizontal axle mounted inside an enclosure comprising a floor and at least one vertical wall.This device is characterized in that it comprises a capot (6) of general hemicylindrical shape whose axis is parallel to that of said steering wheel and surrounding it and a horizontal arm (12) whose first end is rigidly attached to the neighborhood.of the summit of said hood and at least 3 sets of shock absorption (10a, 10b, 16), one of said assemblies being fixed between the second end of said arm and said vertical wall and working in a horizontal direction, 2 other sets being fixed betweenThe lower edges of said hood and the floor and working in a vertical direction, each shock absorber assembly that can work in one direction and the other.Application to the protection of inertia ruffles in the electrical part of a nuclear installation.
</ABSTRACT>
