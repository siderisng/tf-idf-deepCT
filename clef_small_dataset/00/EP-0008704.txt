<ABSTRACT>
For a printing unit determined for pricing devices, the printing types (2) are adjusted by side by side by one axis rotatably mounted adjusting wheels (5). The adjusting wheels (5) have an internal payment (12), in which a driver (11) is engaged, which is engaged at the free end of an axially adjustable and rotatably rotatably in a plain bearing (13) in a plain bearing (13)-guided adjusting shaft (9) with a Conducting locking device, which according to the number of adjusting wheels (5) side by side at intervals corresponding to the widths of the adjusting wheels (5), arranged parallel to the adjustment shaft (9) arranged locking recesses (17) and a resilient catch (18) and at which is provided with the setting shaft (9) with a central longitudinal recess (14) into which a pin (15) engages, which is held on the housing wall (7) opposite to the slide bearing (13). In order for the latching device to be easily and with low on wall different wider printing types (2) or pressure type tapes (1), the pin (15) is easily replaceable to the housing wall (7), and the locking recesses (17) are at the pin (15) arranged and the catch (18) is formed by an inwardly resilient tongue (19) of the adjusting shaft (9).
