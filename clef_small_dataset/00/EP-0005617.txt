<ABSTRACT>
a novel rubber compound comprises:   (i) 100 parts by weight of a rubber component comprising   (ia) 40 to 100% by weight of an elastomeric  copolymer composed of   50% by weight or more of c₄ or c₅  conjugated diene,  styrene in an amount such that the  amount in the elastomeric copolymer,  (ia), of the conjugated diene plus the  styrene is 80 to 99. 5% by weight, and  0. 5 to 20% by weight of glycidyl-containing  monomer provided that the  amount of glycidyl-containing monomer  in the rubber component, (i), is 0. 5% by  weight or more; and   (ib) 60 to 0% by weight of a vulcanizable rubber;   (ii) 0. 1 to 15 parts by weight of an amine salt of a  carboxylic acid; and   (iii) 0 to 7 parts by weight of sulphur.  such a rubber compound can readily be formulated  whereby it does not undergo scorching at the time of  processing and gives, when vulcanized (crosslinked), a  vulcanized rubber having a high modulus of elasticity.  
</ABSTRACT>
