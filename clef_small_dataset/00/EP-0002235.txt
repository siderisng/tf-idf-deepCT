<ABSTRACT>
Vertical separator of a vapor-liquid mixture, comprising in an external ferrule (1) an intake chamber of the vapor-liquid mixture, vertical tubes (6) provided at their fixed blade inlet (7) printing to the mixture aHelicoid movement and at the exit of a coaxial tube of larger diameter (8) allowing in its internal duct the drain effacuation and in the annular space (10) that of a mixture richer in liquid, said spaceAnnular leading into a free volume wherein said liquid-rich mixture separates into a dry vapor and a steam-loaded liquid, and said internal ducts opening into a dry steam pick-up chamber (16).It comprises an annular flange (13) allowing the steam subsisted in the liquid to escape upward with a peripheral annular passage (15) between the annular flange and the external ferrule.
</ABSTRACT>
