<TITLE>
device for joining two profiled bars.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

<ABSTRACT>
1.  a device for connecting two profiled rods, one of which comprises a shaft and the other of which comprises at least one c-shaped rail, wherein a parallelipiped-shaped core (1) inserted into the shaft has a recess (4) for the accommodation of a spring and a clutch bolt (5) provided at its end with a clutch which engages into the c-shaped rail, which rail possesses a recess (7) for its fixture by means of a screw (8) which can be inserted through a hole in the wall of the shaft, characterised in that between its surface and said recess (4) for the accommodation of the clutch bolt, the core (1) possesses a second recess (2) for the accommodation of a square nut (3), into a thread of which said screw (8) is engaged, and which possesses a collar (9), around the thread, which can be snapped into the hole, where the width of the second recess (2) in the longitudinal direction of the core corresponds to the width of the square nut (3). 
</ABSTRACT>
