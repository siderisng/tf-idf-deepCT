<ABSTRACT>
Obstacle bar restraint, comprising a cleat with two plug-in pins in the holes (20) of a candlestick (21) and a part (6) U U, serving as a support for the bar (9).The support (6) is swivelly mounted around at least one axis parallel to the pins so as to allow the bar (9) to escape freely out of its support (6) in case of transverse shock.Application to horse jump tests.
