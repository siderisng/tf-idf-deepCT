<ABSTRACT>
In a container for receiving and dispensing liquids with a reservoir for storing a liquid and a locking room connected to the reservoir area, when generating a negative pressure in the caching room liquid is liquid from the reservoir into the caching room.In order to dissipate a metered amount of the liquid packaged in the container, the caching room is connected via a capillary tube to the reservoir, which is dimensioned depending on the viscosity of the liquid so that the liquid from the reservoir onlyThen exit when the pressure in the caching room is lower than in the reservoir.
