<ABSTRACT>
In a rotatable refining furnace for non-ferrous metals, in particular copper, an entry device (9) for the liquid metal, an adjacent oxidation chamber (2), which has a slag tray opening (5) on the oven coat, and a reduction room (3) which via oneSiphon-like separation wall (4) is connected to the oxidation space (2), present.In order for the oxidation and reduction treatment to be possible with a largely arbitrary degree of filling and also the refining furnace has a significant buffer capacity with respect to metal inlet and metal lab stitch, the metal stitch (7) on the oven coat is on the side of the slag peel opening (5), but deeper,appropriate.
