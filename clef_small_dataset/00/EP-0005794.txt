<ABSTRACT>
Of the 1-aryl-2- (1H-imidazole-1-yl) -ethanonoxime ethers known as isomer mixture, the two isomers of the formulas can be synthesized stereospecific in pure form. For this purpose, the corresponding stereoisomeric ethanonoximes are assumed, which after transfer to the oxime alkali salt in a polar solvent, in particular acetone or DMF, are not converted at temperatures not higher than 40 ° C with the corresponding halogen compounds suitable for ether formation. The isolation of the products takes place via the precipitation with aqueous nitric acid. The ethanonoxime of the two isomeric shapes can be synthesized stereospecific in such a way that in both cases of a corresponding 2-haloethanone of the formula A-Co-CH₂-HAL (IV) is assumed, which first produces the CIS oxime (IIC) reacted with imidazole or a correspondingly substituted derivative and then oximized in the heat by means of hydroxylamine, for the production of the trans-oxime (IIT), on the other hand, oximized under mild conditions with hydroxylamine and then reacted in the cold with imidazole or a correspondingly substituted, derivative.
