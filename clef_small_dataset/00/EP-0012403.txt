<ABSTRACT>
For producing a cooking vessel (32) with a planar thick-walled bottom (18) and a much thinner shell (19), a cup-shaped blank is formed in on a substantially cylindrical, rotatable molding (11) by a rolling operation in the manty region (19).The rolling tool (20) consists of a rigid outer ring (21) and numerous freely rotatable balls (25) circumferential in a groove (24).In the soil region (14) of the molded part (11), this is baked, so that a cavity (17) is formed, in which the material can form in the ground-near area.
