<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a rifle with a roll-shaped closure part which is rotatable about its axis, which in turn cuts the pipe axis vertically.In the roll-shaped closure part, a radial cartridge bearing is incorporated, which is accessible in a pivot position of the closure member for inserting a cartridge and aligned with the pipe in another pivot position of the closure member.The roller-shaped closure member is rotatable by a gear assembly in a single direction, for which a malters cross drive serves, which at the same time stops and locks the closure part in the desired positions.Preferably, the closure member is symmetrical to a transverse center plane and can take two shot positions that differ by rotation of the closure member by 180 ° from each other.The weapon is suitable for shelling sleezzly ammunition.
