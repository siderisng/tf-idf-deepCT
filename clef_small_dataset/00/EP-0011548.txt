<ABSTRACT>
1.  a measuring device, more particularly for the cutting of mitred battens, said device being adapted for mounting on a work table (1) on which there is fixed a ledge (2) extending longitudinally and having at least one v-shaped slot (10) to allow the passage of two cutting tools which are inclined with respect to the longitudinal direction, abutments (3, 5) whereby a batten for cutting can be held in position, and a graduated rule (7), the start of the graduation of which is situated at the edge of said slot, the said device being characterised in that the said graduated rule is mounted to slide in a plane parallel to the work table and moves in a direction which forms an angle of 45 degrees with respect to the said longitudinal direction, and in that the end (14) of said graduated rule (7) is bevelled to an angle of 45 degrees so as to be slidable along the movable abutment (5) enabling said batten to be pressed against said ledge (2). 
</ABSTRACT>
