<ABSTRACT>
Daily totalizer comprising a plurality of TAMs boring rotated by a frame (4) a swivel (13) swivel mounted relative to the frame, a series of gears (8) integral with the rocker for the drive of drums (1) a cam(23) integral with each drum and a maneuvering finger (22) associated with each cam and integral with the BASCU to cause the reset of the corresponding drum when pivoting the dipper.It is provided a square (24, integral with each pinion (8) and a ramp (25) integral with the frame that cooperates with the said square (24) during the pivoting of the gears, the contact surface of the ramp being a circular sector.centered on the pivot axis.
