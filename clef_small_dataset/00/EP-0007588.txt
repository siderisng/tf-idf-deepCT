<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to novel tetrahydro-1,3-oxazines of the formula in the R a unbranched or branched haloalkyl radical with up to 3 carbon atoms, R¹, R², R³, R⁴ and R⁵ are the same or different and water substance or unbranched or branched Alkyl radical with up to 3 carbon atoms, R⁶ hydrogen or unbranched or branched alkyl radical with up to 8 kOh pfal substance atoms, R⁷ hydrogen, an unbranched or branched alkyl radical having up to 8 carbon atoms, an alkoxyalkyl radical having up to 6 carbon atoms or a dialkoxyalkyl radical of up to 8 Carbon atoms mean and R⁷ and R⁶ together can form a methylene chain with 4 or 5 carbon atoms. The new tetrahydro-1,3-oxazines are by means of a corresponding tetrahydro- 1.3 oxazine compound which is substituted on the nitrogen atom with hydrogen, with an acid chloride R-Co-Cl, in which R has the above meaning, in preserves the presence of a hydrogen chloride-binding agent in an inert solution or diluent. The connection as antagonistic agents increase the crop compatibility of herbicidal acetanilids. Herbicidal agents containing said new tetrahydro 1, 3-oxazines in combination with halogen acetanilides are suitable for controlling unwanted plant growth SES in corn and grain cultures.
