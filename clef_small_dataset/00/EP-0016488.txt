<ABSTRACT>
a light-tight cassette for holding and dispensing a roll of  light-sensitive material comprising an extrusion moulded  shell and injection moulded end caps.  difficulties which may arise in fitting the end caps to the  shell are avoided by providing the inner face of the end caps  with axially projecting corner portions (26,27,28) having a  three-dimensional form providing outwardly facing surfaces  which are outwardly convexly curved both in radial planes  containing the axis of the hub portion (44) of the cap and in  planes normal to said axis.  the said corner portions fit inside  corner regions of the shell and have a self-centralizing effect if  the caps and shell are pushed together in abaxial and/or  relatively angularly skewed relationship.  
</ABSTRACT>
