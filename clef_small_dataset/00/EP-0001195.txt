<ABSTRACT>
Electrovanne comprising a movable armature (30) watched to move from a fixed core (28) in response to the excitation of a winding (20).a movable flexible wall (40) sensitive to the difference in pressures at the inlet (18) and outlet (16) is provided with a passage (62) in which is defined a valve seat (38)solicited by elastic means (54) against a valve (36) defined on the armature (30).In a first position, the movable armature (30) solicits the wall (40) against a stop (48) so that any communication is struck.In a second position of the armature (30), the wall (40) is capable of moving relative to the frame to play the role of an anti-return valve between the inlet orifices (18) and fromoutput (16).Application to the control circuits of accessories equipping the motor vehicles, in particular to the mandates of glamplashes or flagship washers.
