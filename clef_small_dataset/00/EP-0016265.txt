<ABSTRACT>
a power composition having good melt flow properties  under zero shear condition comprises a melt blend mixture of  a thermoplastic elastomer, e. g.  a block copolymer of styrene  and hydrogenated butadiene, and a melt flow modifier, e. g. , a  partially hydrogenated styrene/alpha methyl styrene random  copolymer.  the composition may be made by ,1) melt blend­ ing the elastomer and the flow modifier, (2) cooling the blend  to form a solid; (3) pelletizing the solid blend and (4) grinding  the pellets to the appropriate particle size.  the powder com­ position may be a non-tacky composition.  such non-tacky  powder compositions may be made by dry blending the pel­ lets generally like those of from stage (3) with a melt flow  modifier having a glass transition temperature of at least 20°c  and grinding the blend to the appropriate particle size.  the tacky and non-tacky poweder compositions are useful  in the coating of glass containers, particularly to produce  glass bottles which are fragment retentive if broken.
