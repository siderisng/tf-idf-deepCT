<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for improving the effect of antifouling colors by a targeted adjustment of the exit speed of the toxins from the color.This is achieved by applying a cover layer from a particular epoxy resin system.This system is incompletely crosslinked when applying and finally hardens after application.The antifouling effect is surprisingly long.
