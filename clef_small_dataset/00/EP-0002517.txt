<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to paint binders and their use.These paint binders are reaction products of (a) Mannich bases of at least one ether-free condensed phenol containing at least two phenolic hydroxyl groups per molecule, at least one per molecule-containing butadiene or isoprene homo or copolymer having an average molecular weight between 500and 6 000, at least one secondary amine containing at least one hydroxylalkyl group or a mixture of such a secondary amine with another secondary amine and formaldehyde or formaldehyde-supplying compound having (b) at least one epoxy resin.These paint binders are used in particular for the cathodic electrodeposition painting of metal parts.
