<ABSTRACT>
a buckle for a safety belt or harness (1) has a latch plate  (33) provided with a locking tooth (39) which is biassed into  engagement with an aperture (10) in a locking plate (7) and is  provided with a slider (5) operated lever to move the latch  plate (33) to release the locking plate (7).  a protrusion (47) is  provided on the slider (5) which lies adjacent a portion (41) of  the latch plate (33) when in the locking position to prevent the  latch plate (33) being disengaged from the locking plate (7)  even if the device is subject to a sudden shock.
