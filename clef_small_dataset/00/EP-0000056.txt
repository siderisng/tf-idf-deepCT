<ABSTRACT>
The invention operates non-combustible insulating materials with high fire resistance duration and a density of 80 to 230 g / 1.They consist of a.99 - 80 wt.% inorganic light particles, preferably bloated perlite or vermiculite, b.0 - 20 wt.% fibrous or granular inorganic additives and c.1 - 20 wt.% of a temperature-resistant organic high molecular weight binder, preferably a phenol / formaldehyde condensate.To prevent the water intake they contain as additional binders C '0.2 - 4 wt.% of an alkylphenol / phenol / formaldehyde condensation product.The insulation materials can be used as lenses or building materials.
