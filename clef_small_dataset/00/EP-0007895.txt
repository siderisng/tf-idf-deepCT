<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to biological and pharmaceutical chemistry.It aims to address the disadvantages to which the high stability of certain pharmaceutical vehicles charged with a biologically active substance, replaced these vehicles by biodegradable particles with a diameter of less than 500 nanometers formed by the micellar polymerization of a cyanoacrylate.Alkyl where the term "alkyl" refers to an alkyl radical of 1 to 4 carbon atoms and containing a biologically active substance.These particles are suitable for antimitotic, antineoplasi treatment, antibiotic or hormonal or as a means of ICT diagnosis.
