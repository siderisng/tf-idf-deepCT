<ABSTRACT>
The method for the preparation of derivatives of the 3 (4-oxyanilino) carbazole of the general formula wherein R = hydrogen, alkyl with 2 to 8 C atoms, halologized nalkyl with 1 to 8 C atoms is characterized in that3-aminocarbazole of the general formula with p-hydroquinone or p-aminophenol or with a mixture of p-hydroquinone and p-aminophenol in Miminde stoichiometric amounts is heated to temperatures of 150 to 300 ° C with exclusion of Sau substance.z.b.In this case, in the case of reduced pressure, unprated distiller bares 3- (4-oxy-anilino) -9-ethyl-carbazole and 3 (4-oxy-anilino) carbazole are obtained which are suitable for the production of sulfur dyes.
