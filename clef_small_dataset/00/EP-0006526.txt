<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a tranquil for obtaining anhydrous hydrogen chloride from chlorine contain the organic residues by burning the residues in an oxygen-containing atmosphere and separation of the hydrogen chloride from the water resulting in combustion.In the process, the combustion of the residues in an oxygen-containing atmosphere is carried out at elevated pressures and temperatures of more than 2000 Â° C, which are then cooled and passed into a first cooling zone which contains a saturated solution of hydrogen chloride in water, the mixtureFrom water, hydrogen chloride and the other internal combustion products is cooled therein and the mixture of gaseous hydrogen chloride and carbon dioxide from the cooling zone is discharged and cooled to a lower temperature in a second cooling zone and separated from the aqueous hydrochloric acid.
