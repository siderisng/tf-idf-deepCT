<ABSTRACT>
According to the invention, a mixture of (a) at least 0, 1 weight percent Si-bonded hydroxyl groups containing organopolysiloxane containing at least 50 mole percent monoorganosiloxane units (RSIO3 / 2, R = hydrocarbon radical), (b) organosiloxane having 2 to 10 silicon atoms per molecule, wherein at least substantially all other than siloxane-oxygenated silicon valences are saturated saturated by SiC-bonded hydrocarbon radicals, and an average of at least 2 such hydrocarbon radicals per Si-atom, wherein at least one of the reactants (A) and (B) contains at least one SiC-bonded vinyl group, and ( c) heat-activated bleaching earth heated to 90 = 5 ° C, whereby, during this heating by condensation sige-bearing hydroxyl groups, water-released water is left in the reaction mixture. The reactant (A) can z. b. A methylsiloxane resin, reactant (B) can z. b. 1,3-Divinyl-1,1,3. Be 3-tetramethyldisiloxane. The resulting resins are suitable for wrapping electrical insulation, for producing moldings, including the production of spoons for dental pressures, and for producing coatings.
