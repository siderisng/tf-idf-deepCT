<ABSTRACT>
a document feed mechanism comprises a tray (10) for  supporting a stack of sheets.  the sheets are fed by a shingling  system including a wave generator wheel (13) to a ramp (23).   they are then directed by a guide 25 to a feed roll pair (19, 20).   a restraint pad (24) positioned below guide (25) prevents  double sheet feed to the roll pair.  the guide (25) extends past  the feed roll pair to direct sheets to a platen (31) on to which  they are fed by feed roll pair (27, 28) and a belt drive system  (30).  single sheets may be fed through guide (22) directly to  the feed roll pair (27, 28).
