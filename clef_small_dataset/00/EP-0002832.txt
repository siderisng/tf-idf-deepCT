<ABSTRACT>
sputtering apparatus for applying a photoconductive  coating onto endless flexible belts or cylinders shaped in  the form of an elongate oval within the sputtering machine  and adapted to be passed over supports whose diameter  is substantially less than the diameter of the cylinder if it  is a cylinder or of the cylinder which is capable of being  formed out of the belt.  one support can be a drum and the  other support is a structure which may be another drum,  preferably idling.  the distance between the second support  structure, whether drum or otherwise, and the first support  is capable of being varied to enable cylinders or belts of  different size to be sputtered.  the second support is biased  to move away from the first support to maintain tension  in the substrate member being sputtered.  
</ABSTRACT>
