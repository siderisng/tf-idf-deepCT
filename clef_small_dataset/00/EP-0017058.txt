<ABSTRACT>
Methods for continuous production of phthali MID by reacting phthalic anhydride with ammo NAK in approximately stoichiometric ratio in a mixing device and then in a reaction tube in a certain flow rate and temperature.The phthalimide producible by the method of the invention is a valuable starting material for the manufacture of dyes, pesticides and pigments, in particular copper phthalocyanines.
