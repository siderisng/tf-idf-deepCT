<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a voluminous, false wire turbine filament yarn with a plurality of individually abstract filament ends, wherein the filament yarn bil denominated single filament is constructed with reduced kink displacement of the same polyester raw material.The strength of such filament yarns must be 5 cm clamping length between 1. 3 and 1. 7 CN / DTEX and at 200 cm clamping length less than 75% of strength values at 5 cm clamping length, but at least 0.8 CN / DTEX and the uniformity of the yarnBe better than 3 Ustertom.
