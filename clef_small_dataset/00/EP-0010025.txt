<ABSTRACT>
mixture of nondiffusible redox dye-releasing compounds  for curve shape control, especially for lower scale contrast, of  a photographic element is achieved by employing with a  silver halide emulsion layer two nondiffusible ballasted redox  dye-releasing compounds of different relative reactivity rates,  the reactivity of the compound which is more reactive being at  least 1. 5 times the reactivity of the compound which is less  reactive.  the compounds have color-providing moieties  attached thereto through a group which is alkali-cleavable  upon oxidation.
