<ABSTRACT>
a garment according to the invention is composed of a jacket 1 and trousers 2, which are assembled to each other by means of a standard removable strap, comprising on the side of its edges means 5, for example press studs for interacting with elements complementing these press studs fastened to the jacket 1 and to the trousers 2; in order to permit the height adjustment of the strap 4, the jacket 1 comprises at least one row of three complementary elements 3 disposed in the longitudinal direction of the garment; according to an embodiment, the strap 4 is produced in two portions 4a/4b assembled together by  means of strips of fabric comprising a plurality of curved bristles 6.  <image>
