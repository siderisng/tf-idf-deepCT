<ABSTRACT>
N- (acetylaminomethylene) - (mono- or dithio) phosphoric acid diester amides of the formula wherein R and R¹ are the same or different alkoxy or alkylthio, R² for alkyl and X for oxygen or sulfur.These compounds are characterized by strong insecticides, acaricides and nematicidal properties.They can be prepared from the corresponding N-aminomethylene phosphoric acid diester amines by acylation with acetic anhydride.
