<ABSTRACT>
In a dispenser for pasty products with a container containing the respective product (1), which closed at one end with a sealing at the container inner wall gel (2) and at the other end with an applicator (9) having head piece (3) The head piece is provided, the head piece forms a volume-changeable pump chamber by external actuation, which is separated from the container by a first check valve to be opened only in the direction of the pump chamber and relative to the mouth of the applicator by a second rejection valve to be opened only in the direction of this mouth. For the purpose of achieving a uniformity of the per duct quantities emitted in the individual donors, the pump chamber (16) is formed substantially cylindrical and limited relative to it piston surface (4, 15) and it is a resistor causing the relative movement in one direction Spring (8) provided.
