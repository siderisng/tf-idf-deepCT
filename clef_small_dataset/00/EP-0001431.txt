<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to cathodically depictable elek troating paint binders based on amino groups relapsing polycondensation products, which are at least partially in the form of their ammonium or quaternary ammo nium salts.The polycondensation products containing amino groups are prepared by attaching p-hydroxyaceto phenon to polymers with epoxide groups and subsequent reaction of these attachment products with formaldehyde and secondary amine.The electrical paint binders according to the invention are suitable for coating metallic substrates and provide particularly corrosion and adhesive coatings.
