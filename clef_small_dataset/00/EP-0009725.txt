<ABSTRACT>
Cooling or heat cabinet releasably composed of individual flat wall elements which have in the connection regions formed groove spring connections and at least one wall element in addition to the grooves and / or springs located on the end faces and / or springs has a side surface and / or springs.The grooves and springs each have an approximately trapezoidal cross-section and run at the edges of the wall elements so endlessly, that the grooves and or springs of the side surface in corner areas in springs and / or grooves of the front side.The wall elements can be accompanied by two clamping bands around the cabinet body, which are closed by a releasable closure.
