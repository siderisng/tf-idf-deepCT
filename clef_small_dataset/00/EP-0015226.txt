<ABSTRACT>
The invention has for its object to propose a scarf of a shawl arrangement for storing the phase position of a switching voltage, which also stores the original phase position even when the AC voltage is disappeared or a jump change in the phase position.The circuit arrangement therefore contains two monostable tilting stages (1, 2) at their outputs (1, 2) whose gears are interconnected via logic circuits (3,4);In addition, the negative and positive half-waves of the AC voltage (UW) are corresponding to the logic circuits (3, 4).Further, a control assembly (13) controlled by the AC voltage (UW) is connected to the logic circuits (3, 4).The circuitry voltage is mainly suitable for use in the spacer protection devices and directional relays.
