<ABSTRACT>
process for the preparation of ethylenically unsaturated  isocyanurates comprising reacting polyisocyanate with a  monohydric alcohol containing a vinylidene group in the  presence of a copper salt to form an isocyanate-containing  urethane and then trimerizing the isocyanate-containing  urethane to form an ethylenically unsaturated isocyanaurate.   the ethylenically unsaturated isocyanurate may be  homopolymerized or copolymerized with ethylenically  unsaturated monomers to form high molecular weight  polymers having excellent physical properties.
