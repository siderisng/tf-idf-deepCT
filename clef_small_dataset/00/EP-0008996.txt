<ABSTRACT>
Method of heat treatment of aluminum alloy products of the 2000 series containing 3.5 to 5% copper, from 0.2 to 1% magnesium, from 0.25 to 1.2% silicon, withSi / mg> 0.8 comprising solution, quenching, maturation and income.Income comprises at least two stages: 1) a main income at a temperature above 225 ° C and less than 280 ° C of a duration of between 6 s and 60 mn, 2) a complementary income at a temperature between 120° C and 175 ° C of a duration between 4 and 192 hours.This method makes it possible to improve the compromise between mechanical traction characteristics and resistance to the intercrystalline corrosion and undervoltage.
</ABSTRACT>
