<ABSTRACT>
Device allowing the automatic alignment of characters to be recognized in the video space of a character recognition sys.It is fixed a characteristic CA of alignment of the character to recognize, for example the center of the framework circumscribed in the character.The coordinates of this center are measured and compared to ideal coordinates.An error signal makes it possible to corri the alignment of the next character, or generate a rejection SIG when the error is too large.This device is used in a Caracter Recognition system.
