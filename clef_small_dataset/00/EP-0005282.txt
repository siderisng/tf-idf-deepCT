<ABSTRACT>
The potting compound consists essentially of a chlorinated aliphatic or aromatic liquid hydrocarbon having at room temperature of low viscosity, a small amount of in the chlorinated hydrocarbon, acting as a HCl acceptor, to aliphatic amines inert, cycloaliphatic epoxy resin, a low molecular weight soluble in the chlorinated hydrocarbon, bromine-limiting Polybutadiene having a viscosity of about 2 x 10⁵ MPAs at room temperature, a coating at room temperature enabling aliphatic polyamine or polyaminoamide and a small amount of antimony trioxide. Further, the mass contains a mineral filler, color pigments, aging protection agents and up to 50% by volume of a gaseous substance in fine distributed state. The potting compound is particularly suitable for completing cavities in electrical apparatus, has good electrical dielectric strength and a low dielectric loss factor Tgδ and is self-extinguishing and compressible.
