<ABSTRACT>
there is disclosed a novel process for the production of a  separator for use in a cell to prevent the shortcircuiting of  positive active material and negative active material, said  process comprising irradiating a polyolefin film 150 Âµm or  less thick with ionizing radiation, followed by contacting the  irradiated film with an aqueous solution of a terminally  unsaturated monomer such as acrylic acid, methacrylic acid  and the like at a relatively low temperature to thereby cause  grafting of the monomer onto the polyolefin film before the  grafted polymer film is treated with an aqueous alkaline  solution to finally obtain the separator for use in a cell, which  is substantially improved in that it shows low electrical  resistance and is excellent both in its acid resistance and  mechanical strength.
