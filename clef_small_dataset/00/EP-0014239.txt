<ABSTRACT>
Method for producing tertiary 2-carboxivethyl and carboximethylphosphines as well as their salts of general formulas P (CH2 - CH2 - COOME) 3 I or RP (CH2 - COOME) 2 II, wherein Me is hydrogen, alkali metal or NR'4 radical with R 'equal to hydrogen and / or alkyl or aryl radical and R may be an optionally substituted aryl group with 6 to 14 carbon atoms by increasing the corresponding cyanoalkyl phosphines with an aqueous alcoholic alkali metal hydroxide solution, then treated the aqueous solution of the acid salts with an acidic ion exchangerand the free acids optionally neutralized with an alkali metal hydroxide or ammonia or an amine.The tertiary 2-carboxivethyl and carboximethyl phosphines thus produced are used as a catalyst component in the oligomerization of ethylene by means of a nickel catalyst.
