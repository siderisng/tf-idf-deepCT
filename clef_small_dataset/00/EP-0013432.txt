<ABSTRACT>
1.  use of a multi-layer woven fabric as canvas, wherein the woven fabric consists of at least two layers of fabric (a, b) which are independent of one another in their weave, yarn and colour, and which are joined together by a cross-over (c) of the layers of fabric from one side of the woven fabric to the other, and the cross-over (c) is formed over the entire width of the woven fabric or only partially and at intervals of from 2 to 80 mm.
