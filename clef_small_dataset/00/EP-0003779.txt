<ABSTRACT>
For processing garbage and waste materials, a treatment apparatus having a subcritical rotational speed (1) with a stationary cover, in which an input device (28) is arranged, at least one high-speed rotor (21, 22) on which several gap tools (25 ) are attached at a distance from each other. Preferably, the axis of rotation (2) of the plate is arranged approximately horizontally, and the 채sere circumference of the cleavage tools of the rotor (21, 22) is at a considerable distance from the inner wall of the plate (1). When it provides for the rotary axis (23, 24) of the rotor (21, 22) in the vicinity of the horizontal halve line (75) of the plate floor, the cleavage tools (25) are outside of the treated material, so that the Machine after standstill without difficulty can start again. Very expedient is also the distributed arrangement of motagers (4) provided on the inner wall, so that the treatment material is guided safely up to the desired vertex site, from which the well crashes and flows directly into the rapid rotor (21, 22).
