<ABSTRACT>
The hollow profile sections are embedded on brackets for their assembly, the joints being watertight in the angles region by means of an injected packing.The device has an injection head (4) associated with a pusher (7) pressing against it the angles in question.A work table supports the elements of the frame to be installed;It is carriers of two side assembly organs, namely - a cleat (20) associated with placing organs consisting of a coaching roller (12) and a pressing roll (13) that putin front of the pusher (7) the section of profile (1) provided with a square (3) and - a vice (28, 29) mounted on a trainar (26) which moves transversely to its direction of clamping forEmboon the profile (2) on the square (3).
