<ABSTRACT>
Hydraulic rotating distributor comprising two or a rotating ganes, respectively, respectively of a tree of in Tré and an output shaft, these organs controlling by their relative angular position the fluid supply under pressure of a hydraulic assistance engine.The invention provides the addition of supplemental reaction chambers (94, 96) fed under the same pressure as the corresponding chambers (50, 52) corresponding, the pressure in said reaction chambers, however, limited to a maximum valuepredetermined by means of devices automatically controlling their communication with the chambers of work.Application in particular to the assisted steering mechanisms for motor vehicles.
