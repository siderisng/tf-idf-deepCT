<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an impregnated electrical capacitor.The capacitor consists of a winding with swellable plastic films (1) and paper straps (5).After the swelling of the plastic films (1), a residual impregnation gap (6) remains.The invention is preferably suitable for power capacitors.
