<ABSTRACT>
a high pressure discharge lamp comprising a discharge  vessel (3) in which an electrode comprising a resistive  element (4) is present, said resistive element consisting of  two parallel arranged branches having substantially the  same electrical resistance.  with such an electrode construction it is achieved that  the starting point of the discharge on the electrode is  stabilized.
