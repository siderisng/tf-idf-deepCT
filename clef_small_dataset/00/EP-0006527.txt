Fuels for gasoline engines containing small amounts a) Imide or amide-imide or mixtures of nitrileotric acid and / or ethylenediamine tetraacetic acid and amines or amineemic with 7 to 18 carbon atoms, formula I as valve and carburettor in the X same or different radicals-HN-R or adjacent radicals x to form a ring wherein M = 0 or 1 and R is unbranched or branched aliphatic radicals having 7 to 18 carbon atoms and b) mixtures of highly hydrogenated petroleum distillates in the boiling range of the petroleum fraction and highly refined solvent traffinates with viscosities (At 40 ° C) of 50 to 500 mm² / s, wherein the mixing ratio is 20:80 to 80:20 parts by weight, it is suitable for cleaning the carburetor and valves in gasoline motors.This achieves better combustion of the fuel in the engine.
