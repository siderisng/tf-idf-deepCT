<ABSTRACT>
price tags of extruded synthetic material serve to identify goods by being clipped onto such goods, in particular onto goods in roll form, for example rolls of carpeting.  they accommodate an inscribed strip 5, which contains information on the product, in particular the price thereof.  in order to be able to attach such a price tag both to a horizontal and to a vertical edge of such a product, the price tag possesses on the one hand two legs 9,10, which, if they are attached in the manner of a clip, receive the product or a part thereof in a clip aperture between them.  the inscribed strip 5 is received in a clipping manner between two lugs 6,7, which are arranged with their free ends almost abutting with the outer surface 8 of the leg 9 of the price tag, so that a clip aperture 12,13 is formed in each case, together with the surface 8 of the leg 9, for the fixing of the inscribed strip 5, so that the latter is fixedly connected to the price tag.  this price tag holds the inscribed strip 5 firmly in any desired relative position on the product.  <image>
</ABSTRACT>
