<ABSTRACT>
For a suspension device for a low temperature tank which is thermally insulated in an outside container, the task is to be solved that the suspension device guarantees a highly secure suspension of the low temperature tank both in terms of mechanical and thermal stresses.The suspension ranging direction is designed so that the mounting tapes (12) are each composed of a plurality of single-by-another solids (18) different fiber material together and the tanner-in-individual element (18. 4) of each fastening tape (12) from the fiber material with the relatively lowest thermal expansion coefficientconsists.
