<ABSTRACT>
at the transition from cml circuits to ttl circuits for which only one common supply voltage is provided, the reference potential must be changed.  to transform the signals, taking into consideration possible fluctuations of the supply voltage, a current balancing circuit is used in accordance with the invention.  the invention is preferably used in large-scale integrated cml circuit arrangements with ttl-compatible signal inputs and signal outputs.  <image>
