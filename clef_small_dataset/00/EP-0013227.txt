<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a hydraulic device of Frei swimming for a load subjected to shocks and vibrations.It comprises a cylinder (1) in which slides the piston (4) of a rod (7) carrying two valves with valve (18) and (19) to curb the high speed of hydraulic fluid of a room (15) or (16) to the other.These valves communicated with a tank (17) pressurized by springs (31) to (34) and accumulating the dilatations of the fluid.A SECON DAIRE (29), whose displacements vis-à-vis the rod (7) are reduced relative to the displacement of the tank piston (27) vis-à-vis this rod (7), acts as'Leakage indicator.Application to load stabilization such as high temperature steam lines in thermal or nuclear power plants.
