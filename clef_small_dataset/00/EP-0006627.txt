<ABSTRACT>
A radiation-sensitive mixture will be described, which (a) contains a compound and (b), in particular UV light, acid-forming of actinic radiation, in particular UV light, acid, which contains at least one enolate group, and their solubility in a flower developer by the action ofAcid is increased.The enol ether is preferably a compound of general formula I in the R₁ a n-valve alphatic radical having at least 2 carbon atoms, R₂, R₃ and R₄ are the same or different and means hydrogen atoms, alkyl or aryl radicals, or in each case for two to a saturated orOlefinically unsaturated ring are connected, and N is an integer from 1 to 4.The mixture is irradiated in the form of a radiation-sensitive layer as imagewise and washed on the irradiated locations with a developer, so that a positive relief image is formed.
