<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a cuboid insulating hood, which is set from above, for example, on a transport palette.The four side walls and the roof consist of an outer and an inner layer of vinyl plastic with an insulating intermediate layer made of flexible foam.Front and back wall as well as roof are releasably connected with the two surfaces via continuous zippers, wherein the separating points behind the zippers are sealed by overarching sealing tabs.
