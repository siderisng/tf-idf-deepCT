<ABSTRACT>
Process for the preparation of hydroxylammonium salts by catalytic reduction of nitrogen monoxide with hydrogen in dilute aqueous solutions of mineral acids in the presence of suspended platinum catalysts at elevated temperature, wherein the reaction mixture is carried out in the circle, the gas mixture containing in the gas space above the reaction zone and hydrogen-containing gas mixture at least one fluid beam exiting from above of a nozzle opening in the circle-guided reaction mixture in at least one rotating zone which is located in the reaction zone, which extends on both sides, which extends in the direction of the occurring liquid jet and completely covered by the reaction mixture, which is below the rotating zone emerging unreacted gases deflecting reaction mixture, in which the circulating zone outer surrounding annular zone leads upwards and at the same time from egg ner under the reaction zone separating zone gas-armed reaction mixture.
