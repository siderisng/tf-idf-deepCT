<ABSTRACT>
an exhaust-gas catalyst system includes a substrate,  carrying a wash coat of alpha phase alumina, and a catal­ yst containing rhodium deposited thereon.  by using alpha  phase alumina as the wash coat, the rhodium does not  dissolve into the wash coat during periods when the catal­ yst system is subjected to high temperature oxidizing con­ ditions.
