<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a MIS field effect transistor which is made of a semiconductor body (1) covered with an insulating layer (2) with a Source area (3) and drain region (4) contacted each of an electrode (16, 17) First guide type and a gate electrode layer (18) arranged therebetween. These areas are enclosed by a lessly doped area (10, 11) from the first line type. On the source side, a further area (14) is followed by the second conductor type, which adjacent to the semiconductor surface below the gate electrode layer (18). The doping is generated by differential implantation with different penetration depths. As a implantation mask, a mask with bevelled edges on the windows via drain region (4) and source region (3) is used, which is advantageously formed by the insulating layer (2, 7) and / or the gate electrode layer (18) . This field effect transistor is particularly suitable because of its high breakdown voltage and the high switching speed for integrated semiconductor circuits (L) caused by the short channel length (L).
