<ABSTRACT>
1.  a hand-held implement for welding webs or sheets of thermoplastic material, comprising a pair of motor-driven pressure rollers (2a, 2b), which on a retaining and guiding device (1') that is s-shaped in cross-section and, if desired, is provided with rollers (25) is mounted in such a manner that one roller (2a) is adapted to be adjusted by means of a pivoted supporting member (14) as regards its distance from the other roller, and a heater (19), which precedes the pair of pressure rollers (2a, 2b), characterized in that the axles (9, 11) of the pressure rollers (2a, 2b) include an angle of preferably 1 to 3 degrees with each other in the plane which is parallel to the webs (3, 4).
