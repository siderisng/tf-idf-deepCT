<ABSTRACT>
a lightning arrester (2a, 2b) device comprises a serial  connection of a non-linear resistor and linear resistor as a  lightning arrester and a pair of electrodes (11) disposed  with a gap at both ends of the lightning arrester thereby  preventing a damage of the lightning arrester caused by  direct lightning to a power transmission line (10).
