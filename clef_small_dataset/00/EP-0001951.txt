<ABSTRACT>
Projection device, particularly intended for photography, cinema and television, characterized in that it comprises: a housing (1) receiving an object to be projected, and a light source (3), a reflective system (16,18) placed inside the housing so as to reflect the light from the source on the object, the upper face of the housing (1) being pierced to allow the passage of the luminous flux in an optical system (5) placed at the-Dessus of the housing and intended to cooperate with a shooting apparatus (6) to photograph the object disposed in the housing, the latter being adjusted in height adjustable at a support (7) of support, and in thatAdjustable means are provided for optical coupling between the apparatus (6), and the optical system (5) and the housing.
