<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to organosiloxanes with SiC-bonded ester groups. The organosiloxanes according to the invention contain at least one unit of general formula ROCCH₂CH (COOR) CH₂CH₂SI (CH₃) AO in this formula means R same or various aliphatic multiple bonds free, optionally an etheric oxygen atomic hydrocarbon radicals having 1 to 12 carbon atoms, R 'hydrogen or CH₃ and A 0, 1 or 2. The organosiloxanes according to the invention may be, for example, by hydrolysis or mixed hydrolysis of silanes of the general formula ROCCH₂CH (COOR) CH₂CH₂SI (CH₃) AX3-A, where R and A each have the meaning given above and X represents the same or various hydrolyzable atoms or identical or different hydrolyzable groups, or prepared by attaching a diester of allyl succinic acid and / or methallyluccinic acid to organosiloxane with Si-bound hydrogen. A preferred use of the new organosiloxanes is their use as lubricants and hydraulic fluids.
