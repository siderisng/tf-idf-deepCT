<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a carrier catalyst of Oxi of the cobalt and phosphorus as well as at least one of the oxides of the molybdenum and potassium in the atomic ratio CO₁ P₁₋₂ M₀₋₀₅ K₀₋₀₅ on a porous carrier material and a method for its production, wherein a) dry, porous support material until reaching 40 to 80% of the previously determined saturation value with water; b) the pre-impregnated carrier material either at least once at a maximum for complete saturation sufficient amount of an aqueous solution of as serosile compounds of the cobalt and phosphorus as well as the molybdenum and / or potassium or to another, but with intermediate drying of valley according to C) with a maximum Complete saturation sufficient amount of an aqueous solution of a water-soluble compound of only one of these elements are impregnated; c) the carrier material after each impregnation for 2 to 20 hours at 350 to 500 k dries and d) finally sintering 0.5 to 4 hours at 550 to 1000 k in the airflow. The support catalyst can be used to produce styrene by oxidative dehydration of ethylbenzene with molecular SEM oxygen in the gas phase.
