<ABSTRACT>
a method of and a device for manufacturing a hydrodynamic  groove bearing, in which a cage (3) is arranged around  a pin (2) or in a sleeve, said cage (3) comprising at least one  ring of holes (5) accomododating balls (4) having a diameter  which is larger than the wall thickness of the cage (3), after  which the pin (2) or the sleeve and the cage (3) are moved  through the bearing bush (1) or over the bearing shaft (6) in a  translatory and rotary manner.
