<ABSTRACT>
The device for mounting and disassembling a tire on a wheel rim of a vehicle comprises a horizontal rotary tray (22) with claws (21) and a column (2) on which is fixed an arm (6)can oscillate above the plate (22).In this arm (6) is mounted a bar (17) which relates to its lower end of the wheels (19-20) of guide and pressure.It is provided between the column (2) and the arm (6) an eccentric (9) for the movement of the arm (6) and the adjustment at a small distance from the pressure roulet (20) relative to the rim of thewheel.
