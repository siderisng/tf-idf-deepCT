<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a device for allowing the mounting of an apparatus such as a valve (88) between the extremi tes (10, 12) of two pipes.In order to allow remote manipulation of the valve (88) and to avoid moving the pipes when it is placed in place, the device comprises an extensible member (18) carrying the valve (88) and constituting a sub-Unit mounted retracted together between flanges (14, 16) integral with pipes, then extended to be supported against the flanges.The expandable member (18) is traversed by a passage (60, 62, 64) by which the pipes communicate with each other.
