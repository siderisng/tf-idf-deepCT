<ABSTRACT>
A method is proposed, which serves to produce press compositions with soft magnetic properties.With this method, it is possible to produce inexpensively magnetic nuclei, which can be used in familiar magnetic circles with exchange magnetization up to frequencies of 100 kHz.They can be processed by means of the methods conventional in the plastics technique due to the flowability of the pressing masses too complicated shaped components.The method is that a mixture of sintered iron powder and carbonyl iron powder mixed with a thermosetting resin in liquid shape, the press composition thus produced filled into a heated press mold and then pressed.
