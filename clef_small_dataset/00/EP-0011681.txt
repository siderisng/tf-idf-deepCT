<ABSTRACT>
For a barometer with stabilized membrane caps, the deflection of the membrane caps is transmitted by means of a via supporting unit to the drive element of the pointer unit.The transmission unit consists of a lever shaft (40) with a first lever arm (44) coupled to the drive element (32) of the pointer mechanism (26, 28), and with a second lever arm (48) whose free end directly on the membrane capsule (12) and its axis extends obliquely to the plane of the membrane capsule.
