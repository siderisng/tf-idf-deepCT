<ABSTRACT>
1.  a process for the production of a high voltage duct, in which together with a web of insulating material (2), a comparatively thin synthetic resin film (3) is wound onto a mandrel (1) so as to form common windings, characterized in that the thin synthetic resin film (3) is led over the insulating material web (2) onto the mandrel (1) ; and that the thin synthetic resin film (3) is wound under tension with elastic stretching.
