<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the laser sources which use a semiconductor block (5) whose emitted radiation is collected by an optical fiber (7, 8).The invention relates to a laser source in which the support (2) of the semiconductor block (5) and the optical fiber (7, 8) is metallic.The support (2) received a stampprint (3, 4) which comprises a seat (3) for the semiconductor block (5) and at least one groove (4) serving as a housing for the optical fiber (7, 8).The invention applies in particular to the sources that can be used in the optical telecommunication systems.
