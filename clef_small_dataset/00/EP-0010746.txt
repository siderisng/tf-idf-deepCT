<ABSTRACT>
components of catalysts for the polymerization of ole­ fins, consisting of the product obtained by reacting:   a) the product of the reaction between:   a1. ) a compound of trivalent ti or v, such as ticl₃;   a2. ) an organic compound containing at least one oh  group, such as an aliphatic alcohol with 2 to 8 c atoms or  phenol; and   a3. ) a mg compound, such as mgcl₂;   with   b) a compound selected from sicl₄, cl₃sich₃,  clsi(ch₃)₃, cl₃sih, ticl₄, alcl₃, al(c₂h₅)₂cl, al(c₂h₅)cl₂,  al(c₂h₅)₃, sncl₄, bcl₃ and hydrogen halides.  catalysts con­ sisting of the above components and al-trialkyls or al-alkyl  halides allow the production, with high yields, of polyethy­ lene and poly-alpha-olefins having a narrow distribution of  molecular weights.
