<ABSTRACT>
An emergency stop is triggered when a parameter representing the bullition crisis or the power release per unit length unit exceeds a limit value.For the determination of these parameters, a representative of the distribution of the path is used in the core of the most accurate reactor possible.This distribution is obtained by measuring the axial power by ionization chambers and determination of radial power, by measuring the position of the control bars.The invention applies to all types of operation of a pressurized water nuclear reactor.
