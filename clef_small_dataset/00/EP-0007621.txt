<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an oxygen sensor for the risk of oxygen content in exhaust gases, for example of motor vehicles. The oxygen sor according to the invention has a transition metal oxide element (4) whose electrical resistance changes depending on the change in the oxygen partial pressure in the surrounding gas. The invention is characterized in that an approximately 50 to 100 Î¼m thick porous film of the transition metal Loxide (4) and two thick film metal electrodes (21, 22), both of which are in contact with the transition metal oxide thickfiime (4), on a ceramic Substrate (1) are arranged, and that a ceramic protective layer (5) completely covers the free surface of the transition metal oxide thick film (4) and the free surfaces of the metal electrodes (21, 22) at least partially covered. The invention provides a mechanically resistant oxygen sensor with a sensitive response and with a low manufacturer's cost, which can be used for environmental monitoring.
