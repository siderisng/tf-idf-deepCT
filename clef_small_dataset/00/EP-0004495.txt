<ABSTRACT>
The method of preparation of oxalyl chloride consists in: - reacting ethylene glycol on trichloracetyl chloride, - perform a photochemically chlorination of the formed ethylene glycol bistrichloracetate, - carry out a rearrangement of the tetraethylene glycol bistraichloracetate.oxalyl chloride and trichloracetyl chloride, said method being characterized in that the three operations are carried out in the absence of any solvent other than trichloracetyl chloride, and in that the chlorination operation is carried out at a temperature.between 50 and 200 ° C.The method is carried out in the absence of metal salts in quantities greater than 1 ppm, for example in a glass, steel or polymeric apparatus free of metal loads.
