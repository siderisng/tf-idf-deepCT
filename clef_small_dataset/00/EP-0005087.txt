<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a system of connecting accessories for asymmetric profile power rail.A Main Accessory said online connector makes it possible to electrically connect the conductors of two rails that this bond is carried out on the right or to the left of the rail without the need to respect the position of a kerneur;Combined with a universal supply box and a binding die it allows to mount these rails in all figures: online, in T, L, Cross.
