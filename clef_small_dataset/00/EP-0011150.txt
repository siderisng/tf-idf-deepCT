<ABSTRACT>
Method for producing methanol by transplexing a gas mixture of carbon monoxide, carbon dioxide and hydrogen at elevator temperature and at elevated pressure in the presence of a catalyst containing zinc, copper and aluminum, the catalyst of a mixed crystal of the formula Cu2.2zn2.8(OH) ₆ (CO₃) ₂ The aluminum hydroxide contains as a structural primer, produced by calcination and reduction at temperatures between 160 and 350 ° C.The advantage of using the new catalyst is a substantially improved space-time yield as well as in increasing the life of the catalyst.This fact is by FIG.1 illu, where curve 1 is the space-time yield (A) on methanol depending on time (T) with the help of the login-sizing catalyst and curve 2, the space-time yield depends on the time with the aid of a catalyst of the contemplation of the technique of higher copper content.
