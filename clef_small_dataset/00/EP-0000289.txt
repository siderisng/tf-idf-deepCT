<ABSTRACT>
an improved apparatus for sharpening and resharpening  cutting blades provides for very accurate positioning of a  plurality of cutting blades (22) relative to the flat grinding  plane of a grinding wheel (16).  the apparatus includes  means for positioning the cutting blades in precise relationships  to a reference axis (26) so that the reference axis can be  used for establishing critical geometric surfaces on the cutting  blades.
