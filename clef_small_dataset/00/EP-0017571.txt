<ABSTRACT>
The modulator comprises a two-arm interferometer (2, 3) in which one creates, by electrooptic effect, a phase shift between the light waves propagating in both arms, controlled by a modulating voltage.It is characterized by a retroreaction loop comprising a light separator (6) placed on the output guide (4) of the interferometer, and a photodiode (7) with the exit intensity fraction from the separator and providing aElectrical voltage that is superimposed in the interferometer at the modulating voltage.There is thus obtained a linearization of the modulating voltage output intensity response curve.The invention applies, in particular, to optical telecommunications.
