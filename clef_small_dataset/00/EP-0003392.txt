<ABSTRACT>
a system (10) and method disclosed for use in bending  glass incorporate a vacuum holder (12) having a surface (28)  with spaced openings (32) in which a vacuum is drawn to  support a heated sheet of glass and in which pressurized gas  is subsequently supplied to force the glass sheet away from  the holder surface.  a control unit of the system includes a  damper (24) that initially communicates a vacuum blower  with the holder and that subsequently communicates a  positive pressure air blower therewith to first draw the  vacuum and then supply pressurized air to the openings in  the surface.
