<ABSTRACT>
the fastener according to the present invention comprises a first part (4) fixed in a recess of the bottom of the case, this first part being connected by means of an elastic junction part (7) to a second part (6) comprising a hook (10) ensuring the retention of the cover (12), and a projection (8) with an actuating surface.  when the said actuating surface is pressed, the said second part bends in relation to the said first part and its hook (10) releases the cover (12).  the invention is used for fasteners for cases of cosmetic products.  <image>
