<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a photoconductive sensitive to gallium arsenide laser radiation.The photoconductor of the invention, which is sensitive to radiation of about 1.5 eV, consists of an amorphous material of silicon and hydrogen with a content of germanium, tin or lead.In a preferred embodiment of the invention, the amorphous composition of the general formula Sixge1-xHy values for x from 0.78 to 0.93 and for y from 14 to 20 atomic percent.
