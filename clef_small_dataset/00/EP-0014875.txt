<ABSTRACT>
Device for continuously preparing monofilaments made of polyvinylidene fluoride with a single-stage Schnekenextruder with a single spindle snail (12) surrounded by a coaxial cylinder housing (11) with a feed (I) compression (II), meterings (III) and mixing head zone (IV)Plasticizing the polyvinylidene fluoride and a nozzle outlet tool (B) for molding the monofilaments (15) with a compression ratio of the screw from 1: 1.2 to 1: 2.5 and in the area of the compression zone (II) alternately consecutive incoming and two-axis portions (A,B, C) same slope and in the mixing head zone (IV) alternately successive mixing zones (24,26,28) and shear zones (25,27,29).
