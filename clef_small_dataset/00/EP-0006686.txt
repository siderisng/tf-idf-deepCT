<ABSTRACT>
uv stable linear polyesters, e.  g.  pet, contain in  their chain copolymerised bis(hydroxyalkoxy)xanth-9-ones,  preferably 3,6-bis-(2-hydroxyethoxy)xanth-9-ones.  the  polymers are obtained by polymerisation in the presence  of a tetrahydroxy benzophenone, preferably the 2,2′,4,4′-­ isomer.  the method of polymerising in the presence of  dihydric phenols to obtain ethoxyaryloxy polyesters is  also described.
