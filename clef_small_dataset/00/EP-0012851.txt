<ABSTRACT>
The rail (7) receives a rail support (10) in the form of a flat profile for increasing the grip for the drive wheels of the vehicles (1).This has for forming gripping edges (11) recesses (12) which can have projecting elevations for additional increase in the grip across the surface of the rail support (10), where mostly elastically deformable drive wheels claw.The elevations are on the valley edge of the recesses (12).The above gripping edges (11) can arise with correspondingly shaped tools immediately when punching the recesses.The rail support (10) can be made of stainless steel or galvanized.It can be bolted or riveted with the surface of the rail.The gripping edges allow driving of a rail laid with gradient up to 50%.Also icy rails can be driven.
