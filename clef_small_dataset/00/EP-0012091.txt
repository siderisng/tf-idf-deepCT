<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the treatment of waste to recover, in the form of calories, the energy they contain and possibly recover certain recyclable constituents.The flow rate and o₂ content of the combustion chambers of the waste (11a) and fuel combustion (12a) of an oven (10) by pipes (311, 321) connected to air ducts (312, 322) and pure oxygen (313, 323), are adjusted by a control unit 350 which receives the flow detector signals (314, 315, 324, 325) of temperature detectors (332, 342) andO₂ content of the fumes (335, 345) and control of flow control valves (316, 317, 326, 327).The invention applies to solid, pasty or liquid industrial waste.
