<ABSTRACT>
a run flat tire for motorcycles having a high handling  stability, cornering stability and durability even when the tire  is punctured during running and the pneumatic pressure  inside of the tire becomes equal to atmospheric pressure.   the run flat tire has a pair of sidewall-reinforcing layers each  comprising an elastic filler (13) extending from an end location  adjacent a bead core (7) in a bead portion to the other  end location in a tread portion through a sidewall portion of  the tire and at least one reinforcing ply (14), which extends  along one side surface of the elastic filler toward the tread  portion, is turned up around the end of the elastic filler in the  tread portion and which then further extends along the other  side surface of the elastic filler toward the based portion.  
</ABSTRACT>
