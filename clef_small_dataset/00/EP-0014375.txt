<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a two-beam changing layer colorimeter in which the measuring beam (8) coming from a lighting optics (2) with the aid of a control body and fixed mirror (10, 14) in exchange on a first measuring cuvette (9) and subsequently by a second measuring cuvette (15) is deflected on a common photo element (11), which communicates with a measuring and evaluation circuit (16).A particularly simple and disturbing structure is achieved in that the control member is a mirror (10) pivotable in the beam path of the measuring beam (8), which deflects the measuring beam generated by the light source (3) by 90 °.
