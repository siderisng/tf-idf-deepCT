<ABSTRACT>
A method of producing a sensitive wafer for exolectrons dosimeter per embodiment on a support of a deposition of a mixture of two compounds, on the one hand, by a body having traps for the electrons and able to emit exopelectrons.By thermal or optical stimulation and, on the other hand, by a body with good ionic conductivity.The method comprises the following steps: a homogeneous mixture of the powders constitute the two compounds is carried out;and - said powder mixture is projected by means of an atomization projection device to obtain an adhering deposit on said support.Preferably, said method comprises a subsequent heat treatment step adhering at a temperature between 300 and 900 ° C.
