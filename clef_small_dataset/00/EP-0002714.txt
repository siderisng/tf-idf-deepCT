<ABSTRACT>
a neutron absorbing article, preferably in flat plate  form and suitable for use in a storage rack for spent nuclear  fuel, includes boron carbide particles, diluent particles and  a solid, irreversibly cured phenolic polymer cured to a  continuous matrix about the boron carbide and diluent  particles.  the total content of boron carbide and diluent  particles is a major proportion of the article and the content  of cured phenolic polymer present is a minor proportion.   by regulation of the ratio of boron carbide particles to  diluent particles, normally within the range of 1:9 to 9:1  and preferably within the range of 1:5 to 5:1, the neutron  absorbing activity of the product may be controlled, which  facilitates the manufacture of articles of particular absorbing  activities best suitable for specific applications.
