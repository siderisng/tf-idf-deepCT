<ABSTRACT>
In a photodiode arrangement, an epitaxial layer (33) of a second conductivity type surrounded by an insulation ring (34) of a first conductivity type is set on a semiconductor substrate (31) of a first line type with a subcollector (32) and within the epitaxial layer, adjacent to the surface, a thin layer (34a) of the first line type.To achieve a nearly 100% quantum yield over a large wavelength range of the incident light and a faster response and a reduction of the arrangement, the surface layer (34a) extends on one side over the insulating ring (34) and is connected to it, such that betweenThe surface layer (34a) and the epitaxial layer (33) and between the epitaxial layer (33 and 32) and the substrate (31) is formed a single, continuous PN transition.
