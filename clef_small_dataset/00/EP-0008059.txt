<ABSTRACT>
Method for mechanical dishwashing in which a rinsing liquor is used which holds 2-to 4 thesian alcohols, assembly (hydroxy) carboxylic acids and / or esters from the alcohols and (hydroxy) carboxylic acids, wherein the alcohols and carboxylic acids 5 to 9 cAtoms include, of which is a quaternary nature, the Alko is bonded only primarily and the carboxyl group at the quaternary C atom only primarily and the carboxyl group.Particularly advantageous are mixtures of alcohol, (hydroxy) carboxylic acid and the esters thereof.Particularly preferred are mixtures of neopentyl glycol, hydroxypivalic acid and neopentyl glycolhydroxy pivalic acid esters.With these means, it is possible to obtain clear, stain and veil free dishes.
