<ABSTRACT>
The water-soluble monoazo dyes of the formula I wherein R¹ = alkyl with 1 to 2 C atoms or 6-methyl-7-sulfozenzthiazolyl- (2), R² = methyl or alkoxy with 1 to 2 C atoms, R³ = alkyl with 1 to 2C atoms and n = 0 or 1, wherein the cores I and / or II may be substituted by another alkyl group having 1 to 2 C atoms and the sulfo groups may also be in the form of their alkali and / or ammonium saltsThis prepares that a diazocomponent of the formula II is diazotized and coupled with a coupling component of the formula III.The dyes of formula I are used in the form of powder or granules or in the form of concentrated aqueous solutions for dyeing and printing hydroxyl-containing and nitrogen-containing natural or synthetic materials and writing means.
