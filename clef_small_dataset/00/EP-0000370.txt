<ABSTRACT>
a post designed for sign or guide marker use having  both sufficient longitudinal rigidity to withstand a force  driving it into the ground and sufficient elastic character  to permit nondestructive deformation upon impact by a  moving object, with subsequent restoration to original,  upright position.  various construction materials, arrangements  of reinforcing fibres (16, 17) and/or structural configurations  are disclosed for obtaining this dual character.   by employing a structural configuration whose moment of  inertia decreases due to deformation at a transverse cross  section, post flexibility improves to preclude shearing during  impact.
