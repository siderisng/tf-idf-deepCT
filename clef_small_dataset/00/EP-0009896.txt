<ABSTRACT>
sulphur concretes and mortars are provided which  display improved ductility and in which water-resistance is  provided in otherwise water-disintegratable compositions  due to the presence of swelling clay in the mineral aggre­ gate, the improved properties being obtained by the use of  petroleum additives, such as crude oil, and/or polyol add­ tives, such as glycerol.
