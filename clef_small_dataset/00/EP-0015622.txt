<ABSTRACT>
In order to achieve a hygienically perfect and rapid prediction of greedy artificial sheaths in the sausage telment, only the intestinal suction pipe raised on the filling tube should be operated with the aqueous medium.For this purpose, a device for external use of the artificial material shell (3) and / or within the artificial medium cover (3) for the internal loading is provided around the filling tube (2).For outer covering, nozzles (14, 15) are considered.For the internal loading is provided on the filling tube (2) provided with oral tions (13) outer tube (9), wherein the aqueous medium is introduced by the openings (13) in the formed annulus (8).
