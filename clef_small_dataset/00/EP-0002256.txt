<ABSTRACT>
In a latent energy storage on the basis of Na₂H-PO₄.12H₂O is incorporated to maintain a reversible phase change from the solid to the liquid state via a virtually unlimited number of cycles a small amount of water.The latent energy storage is prepared by to Na₂HPO₄.12H₂O is mixed with a small amount of water with shaking and the mixture is considerably heated above the melting point, cooled to crystallization and becomes melted again.The latent energy storage is suitable for solar and heat pump heaters and climate systems.
