<ABSTRACT>
The magnetic fiatiter has a magnetic roller (2) mounted in a housing (1) with a sloped strainer fer (14).The roller (2) is enveloped below of a jacket (5), wherein between the roller (2) and the jacket (5), a flow channel (6) represents the medium to be filtered.For altering the flow channel cross-section, a part (5b) of the jacket (5) may be formed movable.A reduction of the channel cross-section provides an increase in the flow resistance resulting in a backwater, so that even with small amounts the level and thus the inlet characteristic of the filter corresponds approximately that of the full load amount.
