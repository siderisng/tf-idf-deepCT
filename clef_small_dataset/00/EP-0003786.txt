<ABSTRACT>
The stereoselective cleavage of DL-phenylglycine derivatives by hydrolysis of the ester or amide group of N-acyl-L-phenylglycine esters or amides in N-acyl DL-phenylglycine esters or amides by the action of enzymes, separation of the N-acyl-D-phenylglycine estersor amides from the N-acyl-L-phenylglycins and optionally subsequent acid hydrolysis of the ester or amide groups of the D-enantiomers and the acyl groups, it is improved by providing the substrate in solution and the enzyme is carrier bound.
