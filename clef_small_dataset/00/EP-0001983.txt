<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for obtaining N, N-dimethylaminoacetonitrile from the nitrile water mixtures incurred in the production of dimethylamine, hydrogen acid and formaldehyde by extraction with 1,2-dichlorobenzene.It consists in extracting the N-dimethylacetonitrile / water mixture obtained from dimethylamine, hydrofluoric acid and formaldehyde with 1,2-dichlorobenzene.The extraction of the dimethylaminoacetonitrile succeeds according to the extraction method according to the invention with high yields when working at temperatures between 5 and 90 ° C.
