<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for the separation of hydrophobic, organic liquids, for example crude oil, of water. For this purpose, the contaminated water is allowed to flow without pressure with an average flow rate of 5 to 300 m³ / m² per hour by a columnar reaction vessel filled in loose bed or by stacking with hydrophobic, preferably open-cell polyurethane foams, that with complete filling 50 to 95 Volume percent of the total volume of polyurethane foam particles and then compresses the absorbed organic liquid from the polyurethane foam. The polyurethane foam usable as absorbent contains bound 2 to 50 wt. % based on the total weight of the polys and polyol, a hydrophobizing compound having at least one group having a CerewitinOff active hydrogen atom and the solubility parameter of 7.5 to 10.5 (C CM³). 1/2, reversible is deformable and is immersed by its own weight in the loaded state and by weight of the contaminated water to less than 25%
