<ABSTRACT>
A photographic film unit for producing colored transitional images contains an acidic system consisting of acidic polymer layer and brake layer, wherein the acidic polymer layer consists of a mixing polymer that is 5-50 wt.-% Polymerized carboxyalkyl (meth) acrylate units.Preferably, the mentioned carboxyalkyl (meth) acrylate units derived from dimeric acrylic acid.The acidic polymer layers have high acid number with reduced glass transition temperature.
