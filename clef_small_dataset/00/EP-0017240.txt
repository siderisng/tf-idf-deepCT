<ABSTRACT>
a liquid crystal device is disclosed wherein each part of a  display section exhibiting a display pattern is divided into a  plurality of segments by a spacer (3) liquid crystals of  different absorption spectra are sealed in these segments; a  transparent selective electrode (2a, 2b) is disposed in each  segment; and these selective electrodes are selectively made  to conduct, thereby enabling a display of different colors.
