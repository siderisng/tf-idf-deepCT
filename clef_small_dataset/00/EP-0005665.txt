<ABSTRACT>
absorber of solar energy which comprises an external flexible and transparent tube (1) and an internal flexible and black tube (2).  under the combined effects of the hot-house constituted by the external tube and of the black body constituted by the internal tube, water or another heat-exchanging fluid, which is situated or circulates inside the internal tube, is heated.  <image>
