<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the protection of zinc surfaces by protecting coating deposition.The method according to the invention comprises forming a pyrophosphate coating by electrolytic oxidation of the objects treated in contact with a solution containing pyrophosphate ions or ions capable of giving pyrophosphates.The invention is particularly useful for the protection of galvanized objects in contact with aqueous media.
