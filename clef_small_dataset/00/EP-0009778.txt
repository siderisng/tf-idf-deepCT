<ABSTRACT>
DEI device for switching off vehicles consists of at least two platforms (6, 7) superimposed on top of each other, which are jointly lifted and lowerable for driving, wherein the interconnected platforms are guided by means of a len Kerparallelogram (1, 2).The platforms are additionally tiltable, in particular by the fact that the handlebars of the parallelogram are not exactly aligned parallel to each other.
