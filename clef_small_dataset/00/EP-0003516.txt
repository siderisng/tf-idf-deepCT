<ABSTRACT>
This invention relates to aqueous dispersions of carbonyl group-packaging copolymers which contain each molecarbonyl group 0.02 to 1 mol of organic hydrazine derivatives having at least 2 hydrazine residues, and a content of 0.0002 to 0.02 mol, based on 1 mol of the hydrazine derivatives, to heavy metallions.The dispersions can mainly as a particularly slightly crosslinking coating, impregnating or coating agent, for.b.as binders for nonwoven fabrics and refining leather.
