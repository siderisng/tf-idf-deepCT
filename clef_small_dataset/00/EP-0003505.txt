<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a novel process for the preparation of urea groups and / or biuret groups having polyisocyanates dispersed or dissolved molded and dissolved form by nozzles of at least two primary amino groups having organic polyamines in excess, presented polyisocyanate at -20 to 250° C, wherein a smooth beam nozzle is used to this injection, as well as certain dispersions of urea diisocyanates obtainable by this method in excess urea group-free diisocyanate.
