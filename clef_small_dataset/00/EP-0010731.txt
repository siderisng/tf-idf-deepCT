<ABSTRACT>
quaternary modulation system for digital data trans­ mission applied to a quaternary phase shift keying (qpsk)  system that corresponds to the sum of two bi-phase carriers  duly phase-shifted, adapted to the transmission of digital  data previously subdivided into two binary signal trains, in  which the spurious amplitude modulation is compensated  for, at least in part, by altering the amplitude of that compo­ nent of the quaternary phase carrier which does not change  its state in correspondence with the phase transitions of the  modulated signal.
