<ABSTRACT>
A substrate based on particulate, with determinable groups such as reactive dye groups derivatized strength for determining the α-amylase consists of superficially crosslinked starch or amylosecreps with controlled reduced swelling capacity, which have a diameter between 0.01 and 0.20 mm.Its production takes place by assuming non-swollen starch or amylosecreins with 0.01 to 0.20 mm diameter suspended in a solution with a substantially superficial crosslinking content of difunctional crosslinking agent until the crosslinking agent is consumed and then theGranules are derivatized with determinable groups.
