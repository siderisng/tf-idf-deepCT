<ABSTRACT>
Method and device, in particular for aerobic breeding of microorganisms.The device consists of a plurality of modular interconnected non-bonded-bond ventilation units forming a lying container with hemisphered ends.A stationary by a PUM pin running wheel (8) arranged guide carrier (9) allows a translation flow into both longitudinal directions of the Behäl Ters.A rotational and translation flow generates an overshooted spiral flow by means of an axially sucking and radially emerging impact wheel (8) and the leading apparatus (9).
