process for polymerising 1-olefins and polymers produced therefrom.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
process for polymerising ethylene or mixtures of  ethylene with up to 40 weight % of other 1-olefin(s) by contact­ ing the monomer with a catalyst (a) in the presence of a  catalyst modifier (b), catalyst (a) being prepared by support­ ing chromium trioxide, or a chromium compound calcinable  thereto, on a refractory oxide support material and heat  activating, catalyst modifier (b) comprising (1) a trihydrocar­ byl aluminium compound and (2) an α,ω-aliphatic diene con­ taining a chain of at least 6 carbon atoms.  the refractory oxide  is preferably silica, alumina, zirconia, thoria or silica-alumina  the trihydrocarbyl aluminium compound is suitably an alkyl,  aryl or alkaryl aluminium.  the α,ω-diene is preferably 1,5-­ hexadiene or 1,7-octadiene.  the polymerisation is preferably  carried out under particle form process conditions in a fluid  medium.  
</ABSTRACT>
