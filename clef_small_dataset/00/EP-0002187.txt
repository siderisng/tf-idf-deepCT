<ABSTRACT>
Control valve arrangement for tentic devices in which more than two control valve blocks are summarized with channel sections for control and supply media to a control group. In order to make such arrangements slightly variable in cost-saving manner and to form their building blocks such that a space-saving, cost-effective total arrangement results, it is proposed that the different control valve blocks (I to V) are constructed from the originating state of the same basic bodies (8) whose under Intermediate layer of sealing bodies together to be connected to the end faces below and wherein the mouths of channel sections (eg 14) lying in these may be arranged symmetrically into a vertical center plane (ZX) lying in the clamping direction (Z). Inside any basic body (8), a transverse cross chamber is provided with a valve seat, the geometric axis (Y) extends perpendicular to the clamping direction (Z).
