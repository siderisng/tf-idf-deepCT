<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a device for reprocessing waste, in which a first component separation independent of the screening takes place before, during and after crushing.For this purpose, a hammer roll arranged in a rotatable drum is present, which is extended to a loosening drum and in which a suction head is provided at the exit end, which ensures a negative pressure in the drum.The invention further relates to a method for operating the device.
