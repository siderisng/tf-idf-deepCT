<ABSTRACT>
3-pyridazones are prepared according to the invention by heating 4,5-dihydro-3-pyridazones in the liquid phase in the presence of a dehydrogenation catalyst.The reaction temperature is 150-350 ° C.The dehydrogen catalyst contains a noble metal of the 8th subgroup of the periodic system, which is generally applied to a carrier.Preferably, one works in the presence of a solvent.
