<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method and a device for welding edge bars to grate rust, wherein the method and device is to be installed in a continuous conveyor track.Here, the boundless lattice frosts are supplied to a transport device of a workstation and stopped a rust on both sides of the workstation.The edge bars are guided into a height-oriented position aligned and in the conveying direction of the lattice frosts, wherein the lattice frosts for attachment to these edge bars and in addition to a stationary festival are guided in the conveying direction extending stop.
