<ABSTRACT>
Pressure-contacted semiconductor device with a sandwich arrangement made of a semiconductor body (1) two ductile silver electrodes (16, 17) and two molybdenum pressure discs (8, 9).This sandwich is centered in the manufacture and must remain in this centered arrangement.In order to ensure the centering even in mechanical shocks, a cage (2) is made of insulating material about the sandwich structure.The cage (2) consists of two parts;It has an inner, polygonal, preferably hexagonal recess (6).The polygonal surfaces (7) tang the sandwich made of silicon body (1), ductile silver electrodes (16, 17) and molybdenum disks (8, 9).The two parts of the cage (2) are held together by an elastic clamping ring (18) which is inserted into a groove (19) on the outer circumference of the cage (2).
