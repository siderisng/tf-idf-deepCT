<ABSTRACT>
1.  a process for the preparation of 4(2' - carboxyphenylamino) - 7 - chloro - quinoline - alpha-monoglyceride, characterised in that : (i) glycerol is condensed on isatoic anhydride in a basic medium in order to obtain glyceryl anthranilate, and (ii) glyceryl anthranilate is condensed with 4,7-dichloroquinoline and the desired product is recovered.
