<ABSTRACT>
New enzymatic marker for immunoenzymatic dosages.The δ₅, 3-ketosteroid isomerase is used.The enzymatic activity associated with the antibodies, in particular by: 1) direct reading on the supernatant or the pellet obtained by a solvent, such as polyethylene glycol, can be advantageously disclosed.soluble antibody;2) Reading in solution after release of the enzyme of the antigen-antibody complex by a reducing, such as dithiotritol;3) Immediate reading after filtration of a substrate solution on the insolubilized antibody-antigen-enzyme complex.Application to immunoenzymatic assays achieved automatically on substances present at very low concentrations, such as pituitary hormones and plasma steroids.
