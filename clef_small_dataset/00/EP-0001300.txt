<ABSTRACT>
a method of manufacturing a circuit element, for example  a transistor, in a semiconductor body.  the p-n junctions  between the zones adjoin a sunken oxide pattern.  in order to  avoid emitter-collector short circuit, a first base doping step is  carried out after providing the sunken oxide pattern, after  which a mask is provided which, together with the sunken  oxide pattern, forms an aperture, and a second base doping  step is carried out, followed by emitter doping and emitter  metallization via the same aperture.  channel stopper diffusion in the epitaxial layer may be  omitted, which presents advantages in particular in iÂ²l.
