<ABSTRACT>
Continuous preparation of anthranilic acid by reacting phthalamide acid and / or phthalimides acid alkali with alkali hypohalite in a reaction tube, which treats the obtained anthranilic acid in two steps with different reducing agents during or after the reaction and used in the second treatment step as a reducing agent alkalidith ionite or alkaliformaldehyde sulfoxylate.The compounds producible by the method of the invention are valuable starting materials for the production of dyes, pesticides and fragrances.
