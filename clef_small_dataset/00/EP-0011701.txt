<ABSTRACT>
Improvement for priority interface selection systems for telecommunication network communication controllers.In order to identify service requesting adapters for which which should be satis made the first, and in order to reduce the access time to the requesting adapter, the adapter interfaces (ADAPT) and the attack interfaces ofLine (RDVP and RDVC) are divided into subgroups within a pyramidal architecture.Each subgroup is provided with standalone pre-selection means for building the path leading through the pyramid, CCU bus to the adapter to serve the first.This device is used in communication systems and more particularly in communication controllers.
