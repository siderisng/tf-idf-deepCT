<ABSTRACT>
wall fouling during the suspension polymerization  of vinyl chloride containing monomers in stainless steel  reactors can be inhibited by wetting the surface(s) of the  reactor with a solution containing from 3 to 50 parts by  weight of alkali metal hydroxide prior to charging the reactor.
