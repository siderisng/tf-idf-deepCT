<ABSTRACT>
a picker for driving the shuttle of a webbing machine, consisting of a bar of longitudinally and tranversely directed carbon fibres embedded in plastic, is described.  the bar is designed as a hollow bar (3), is equipped with a core (1) of hardened foam material and is surrounded by a plastic coating designed as a protective sheath (4) consisting of at least one tubular fabric (5) with a diagonal structure, the threads of which intersect the carbon fibres (2) arranged underneath.  <image>
