<ABSTRACT>
Method for producing monofilaments of polyvinyli Denfluoride, wherein the extruded monofilaments (2) having a cooling medium (5) comprises a viscosity at 100 ° C. which is cooled over 0.5 cpas, at a temperature of 60 to 140 ° C.The extruder for producing the monofilament has a designed as a multi-hole head extrusion mechanism (3) on where the nozzle is formed with an annular nozzle outlet openings, which are surrounded outside and inside of a controllable heater.
