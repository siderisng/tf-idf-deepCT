<TITLE>
a composite driving belt provided with transverse elements with coupling means, and a transverse element for a driving belt.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

<INVENTOR>
van der hardt aberson frederic<sep>van der hardt aberson, frederic ernst carel<sep>van der hardt aberson, frederic ernst careltweelingenlaan 22eindhovennl<sep>van der hardt aberson, frederic ernst carel  <sep>van der hardt aberson, frederic ernst careltweelingenlaan 22eindhovennl<sep>
</INVENTOR>
<ABSTRACT>
a driving belt for application on v-shaped pulleys  comprising an endless carrier and a plurality of transverse  elements abutting against each other with the head sides and  slideably assembled on the carrier.  the transverse elements  are provided with coupling means comprising at least one  projection (6) at one head side (2) and substantially  corresponding recess(es) (7) at the other head side (3) of a  transverse element.  around the coupling means there is some clearance (8),  i. e.  the thickness of the transverse elements (distance  between the two head sides of a transverse element) around  the coupling means is smaller than the nominal thickness (n)  of the transverse element (distance between corresponding  head sides of successive transverse elements).  in this way an  improved engagement of the coupling means can be  achieved.  
</ABSTRACT>
