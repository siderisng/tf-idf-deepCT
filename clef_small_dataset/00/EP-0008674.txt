<ABSTRACT>
Integrated filter circuit with a CTD transversal filter (1) and a low-passed, further filter, which is preceded by the transversal filter (1), to improve its "aliasing" properties.For such filter circuits, a surface-saving, easily integrated shape is sought.This is achieved by a formation of the upstream filter as a four-pole circuit, which has two capacitors (C₁, C₂) and two alternating electronic switches (7, 8) operable electronic switches (7, 8).In particular, the scope includes CTD transversal filters which are used in systems with different clock frequencies.
