<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a sheet deceleration device with two corner boilers (2) which are mounted on a guide sting (3) vertically displaceable bar (1).Each of the ends of the strip (1) is taken up and guided by a guide slot (7) of the cylindrical guide parts (3).Both or only one of the corners of the lower holder (2) along the strip (1) can be added to adjust the distance between the corner ferrules to the respective sheet formats.
