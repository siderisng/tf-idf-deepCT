<ABSTRACT>
A photosensitive copying material is described from a film support and an optically homogeneous light curable layer containing a condensation protrography of an aromatic diazonium salt and a water insoluble macromolecular polymer.The MA terial is characterized in that the layer of a stable dispersion of the water-insoluble poly merisate has been applied under such conditions that after drying an optically homogeneous layer is formed.The material can be prepared without the use of organic solvents and develop with aqueous solutions, preferably with pure water.
