<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a method for preparing ethyl esters by liquid phase reaction of ethylene on a carboxylic acid.The method includes the esterification of ethylene by a carboxylic acid, in the liquid phase in the presence of at least one acid catalyst and at least one dissociating solvent, inert and stable, and capable of increasing the aciditymiddle.The process is suitable for the manufacture of ethyl acetate.
