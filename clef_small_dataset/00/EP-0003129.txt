<ABSTRACT>
Copolymers made of ethylene and higher 1-olefins suitable for the production of thin films are preferably prepared in a two-stage process, wherein in the first stage, a high molecular weight polymer, in the second stage, is obtained with low molecular weight.The films produced from these copolymers show better mechanical data, as films made from known film raw materials.The mechanical data are also compensated in the longitudinal and transverse direction.
