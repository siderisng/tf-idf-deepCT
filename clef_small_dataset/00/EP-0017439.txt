<ABSTRACT>
in the coating of concrete with latex coating composi­ tions, such as paints, adhesion of the latex to the concrete is  improved by the use of a primer comprising air curable,  water soluble polymer comprising (a) water reducible alkyd  or (b) linear addition polymer containing pendant, oxidative­ ly curable groups.  preferably the primer polymer is an acrylic  polymer having pendant groups derived from drying oil acid  which may be provided by reaction of acid polymer with  drying oil fatty acid hyroxyamide.
