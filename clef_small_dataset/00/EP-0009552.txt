<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to novel mono- and oligosaccharides with a molecular weight of the carbonyl group with a molecular weight until 2000. The invention further relates to a method for producing α-methylolated mono- and oligosaccharides, which is characterized in that one is a natural sugar or aDerivative, where appropriate, in the presence of water and / or monohydric alcohols having a molecular weight of 32 to 10,000 at a temperature of 40 to 110 ° C and a pH of 7.4 to 11 is reacted with formaldehyde.The invention further relates to the use of the α-methylolated sugars as polyhydroxyl compounds for the production of polyurethane imsves, as starters for the polymerization of ethylene oxide or propylene oxide, for the production of polyesters and non-ionogenic surfactants.
