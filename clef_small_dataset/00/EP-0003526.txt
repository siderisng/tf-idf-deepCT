<ABSTRACT>
1.  apparatus for sheathing an electrical conductor strand (13) with an insulation (14), the apparatus being equiped with an extruder (12), a vulcanising unit (1) and a cooling unit (5) located between the two, characterized in that the cooling unit (5) is connected in a fluid-tight manner to the vulcanising unit (1) by means of a sealing arrangement associated with the latter, and is constructed as a coolant vessel onto which spray-heads (10) can act and into which a downwardly inclined slide chute (8) clips.
