<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the treatment of melting metals, in particular the steel or melting, by blowing a gas through a porous refractory piece housed in the refractory coating of the container in which the fusion is operated. In order to remedy the well-known wear parts, the invention provides a new porous refractory piece constituted by weight of (A) 60 to 90% grain from a granulometry of 5 mm to 0.2 mm formed of a chamotte containing at least 42% Al₂O₃, mullite, calcined gibbsite at more than 90% Al₂O₃, sintered corundum or electrofunted to more than 95% Al₂O₃, natural chromite, MgO sintered, zircon sand and / or stabilized zirconia, bound by (b) from 10 to 40% of a cement or binder constituted, by weight: (a) from 10 to 30% of calcium aluminate, ( (b) 14 to 54% of a consti with particle size between 100 Å and 0.1 micron and selected from silica, alumina and chrome CR₂O₃ oxide, and (c) of 14 to 14 to 54% alumina having a particle size of between 1 and 100 microns.
