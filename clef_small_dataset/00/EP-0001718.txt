<ABSTRACT>
apparatus and method for use in the removal of material  from body cavities by suction.  the apparatus comprises a  catheter (3), a suction unit (1) and a collection container (2)  disposed between the catheter and suction unit, the collection  chamber (2) being provided with a filter (14).  to facilitate preparation and examination of material collected  in the container (2), it is connected to a source of fluid (4  or 5) for washing and/or treating material collected in the  container.  in accordance with the preferred method, the washing  and/or treating is carried out continuously.  also disclosed is a flexible catheter for use in such  apparatus or otherwise.  to facilitate removal of persistently  adhering material, the or each suction opening is surrounded  at least in part by rigid material providing a hard edge.  there is  further disclosed a suction catheter having a closed end and a  lateral opening.  to facilitate manipulation, the closed end is  asymmetrically rounded.
