<ABSTRACT>
Method and installation of the treatment of rurgy-fried fumes from Hauts-Fours, electric ovens, converters and culpots.The method according to the invention, first consists of separating iron and oxide dust from iron with a solid particle contact, and then cool these fumes to solidify non-ferrous metal vapors.that they contain, then finally to separate solidified non-ferrous metals.It is possible to recover, at least fresh, on the one hand, iron and iron oxides dust and, on the other hand, non-strong metals in solid form.The Chas can also be recovered.
