<ABSTRACT>
Bi-piezoelectric resonator with non-adhesive electrodes to crystal. The bi-resonator comprises at least the first and second trays (1, 2) of dielectric material on which are arranged respectively of the first and second electrodes (11, 21.) the bi-resonator further comprises a triosum lawgone (3 ) of dielectric material having faces opposite respectively to the first and second trays on what are deposited respectively of the threesome and fourth electrodes (31, 32) connected to each other. The piezoelectric body consists of two piezoelec trics in independent quartz (4, 5) and similar, one of which is interposed between said first and third electrodes, without contact with them, and the other is intermediate. Second and fourth electrodes without contact with these, the quartz piezoelectric pellets being arranged so that their axes are opposite. Application to oscillators constituting frequency references.
