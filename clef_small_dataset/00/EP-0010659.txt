<ABSTRACT>
the desk type console (1) consists of a keyboard (2), a screen (3) and a document holder (4).  the screen and the rear supporting surface (16) of the document holder are arranged contiguously, contacting each other centrally, behind the alpha-element (5) of the keyboard, the screen surface (11) being arranged obliquely.  a loosely arranged additional document holder can also be displaced into a position in which the alpha-element (5) of the keyboard is covered.  <image>
