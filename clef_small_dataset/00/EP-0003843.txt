<ABSTRACT>
In the rust covering for mechanically moving step-shaped firing frames of large firing, two fixed rust bars (1) are each absorbed between themselves a relatively moving rust rod (2), connected to each other through a clip (8), the end-side stops (10) at the ends of a staple lock (9) Sitting and together with this make the clip (8).The clamp bar (9) engages through a longitudinal slot (20) of the vertet tab (2) movable relative to the neighboring bars (1).The stops (10) of the clip (8) lie on the inner surfaces of the outer ribs (13) of the fixed rust bars (1).
