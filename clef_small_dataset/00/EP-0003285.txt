<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a method for producing linear or branched silicon functional polyorganosiloxanes by reaction of chlorosiloxanes or chlorosilanes with linear or branched polyorganosiloxanes in the presence of a combination of catalytic amounts of nonvolatile proton acid equilibration catalysts and monocarboxylic acids, such as.b.Acetic acid or propionic acid.
