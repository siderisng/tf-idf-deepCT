<ABSTRACT>
A low voltage circuit breaker (1) has a fixed contact piece (7) and a movable contact piece (10) attached to a pivotally mounted switching piece carrier (15).Between the switching piece carrier (15) and the movable contact piece (10), a contact force spring (17) is arranged, which is formed as a helical compression spring.The shift piece carrier (15) has an arm (36) which extends to approximately near the contact end of the movable contact piece (10).At this arm (36), a set screw (37) is mounted, which is arranged centrally effectively with respect to a contact pad (11) of the movable contact piece (10).The adjusting screw (37) limits the withdrawal of the movable switching piece (10) or the contact pad (11) to a desired low level of which a high current flows over the power switch (1) and exerts electrodynamic forces on the movable contact piece (10).
</ABSTRACT>
