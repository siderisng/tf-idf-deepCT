<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for the separation of 1-amino-4-bromo-anthraquinone-2-sulfonic acid or their alkali metal salts from the reaction mixture resulting in their preparation by bromination of 1-amino-anthraquinone 2-sulfonic acid in sulfuric acid by mixing by mixingThe reaction mixture with optionally sulfuric acid-containing water sets such a sulfuric acid concentration that the 1-amino-4-bromoanthrachinone 2-sulfonic acid fails as sulfate.The sulfate is insulated and converted by the action of water, optionally in the presence of alkali metal hydroxides, oxides or salts, in 1-amino-4-bromo-anthraquinone-2-sulfonic acid or their alkali metal salts.1-amino-4-bromo-anthraquinone-2-sulfonic acid is an important intermediate product for numerous valuable dyes.
