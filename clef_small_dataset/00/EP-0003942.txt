<ABSTRACT>
Grate-grating tool consisting of a rotating cylindrical drum in both directions Axis, provided with cutting elements protruding outwards that can act in both directions of rotation.The sharp elements are fixed on a set of removable bars disposed on the outer surface parallel to the axis, each of the bars having at least one cross row of sharp elements.Each cutting element of the same row has two parallel cutting edges and opposite directions making with the axis of the drum with the same acute angle.The cutting elements of an adjacent row has cutting edges that are substantially equal to the previous but opposite direction.The barrettes are equipped with a quick fixture of the dovetail type.Application to the preparation of the outer surface of a tire for retreading.
</ABSTRACT>
