<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a device for stabilizing the scrolling of an air cable (18).The cable is tense in a loop between a motor pulley (26) and a return pulley (20) for the transport of material or people.The return hen (20) is coupled to a device for stabilizing now the rotational speed of the pulley equal to the drive speed of the motor pulley.The device is applicable to air cable conveyors.
