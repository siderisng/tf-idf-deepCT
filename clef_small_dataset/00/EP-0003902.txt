<ABSTRACT>
a multi-spindle, biaxial turning machine provides for  independent tool offset or compensation in either of the axes  of tool movement.  the turning machine includes a compound  slide (26) movable in a first direction (z) with respect  to the spindle axes of rotation, a cross slide (30) mounted in  sliding relation on the compound slide (26) and movable in a  direction (x) transverse to the spindle axes of rotation, and  first and second auxiliary slides (44, 46), each mounted in  sliding relation to the cross slide (30) and carrying respective  first and second tool holders (48, 50) movable in mutually  transverse directions (w, u).  the compound slide (26), cross  slide (30), and auxiliary slides (44, 46) each has an associated  drive means (40, 42, 58, 60, 66, 68).  a motion senser (43,  62, 70) is associated with each drive means.  a control circuit  is responsive to an operator command, and includes a plurality  of independent control channels, each associated with,  and controlling, a slide (26, 30, 46, 44).  
</ABSTRACT>
