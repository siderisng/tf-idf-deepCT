<ABSTRACT>
an electrical current transfer assembly includes a slip  ring and a brush ring (2) having an annular array of contact  elements (6) engaging the slip ring as one ring rotates with  respect to the other, the brush ring (2) being divided into a  plurality of equal sectors each carrying a group (g1-g4) of  the contact elements (6), the contact elements (6) being  interlaced with respect to length among the plurality of  groups (g1-g4) to provide a distributed balanced engagement  of the contact elements (6) with the slip ring.
