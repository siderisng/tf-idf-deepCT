<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to improved processing methods under controlled atmosphere.A formed atmosphere of a carrier gas consisting of or containing industrially pure nitrogen and an active gas consisting of a hydrocarbon such as CH₄, c₄h₁₀, c₃h₈, c₂H₄, C₂H₂, C₂H₆ and by oxide ofcarbon introduced in the form of pure gas or obtained by decomposition of methanol, the treatment being carried out between 750 ° and 1150 ° C.The invention applies to the heating before quenching, annealing, carburing and carbonitriding of steels.
