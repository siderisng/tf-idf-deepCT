<ABSTRACT>
a thin film inductive head is formed with nickel-iron  binary alloy or nickel-iron-x ternary alloy pole pieces exhibi­ ting negative magnetostriction and having a saturation  magnetostriction coefficient in the range of -1×10⁻⁶ to  -5×10⁻⁶.  the binary alloy has a nickel to iron ratio in the  range 81:19 to 84:16.  magnetic switching of the pole pieces  occurs by rotational switching rather than domain wall  motion and this reduces noise.
