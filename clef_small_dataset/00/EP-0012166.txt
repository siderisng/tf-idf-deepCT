<ABSTRACT>
Sealing ring made of rubber-elastic material for a tubular connector between the tip end of a pipe having a sitting rib at least in the sealing area and an innumerous smooth sleeve.When used as intended, the sealing ring is in the bead of the tip end.The cord profile of the sealing ring is substantially adapted to the profile of the bead, with respect to the tubes in the axial direction radially inside narrower than the width of the sole of the bead, radially outwardly wider than the opening width of the bead on the upper edge of the bead and in the radial direction largerAs the depth of the bead, wherein the side surfaces of the sealing ring are formed concave and the radially outlined surface of the sealing ring is designed as a annular bead.
</ABSTRACT>
