<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for reducing the residual monomer contents of polymers obtained by copolymerization of vinyl esters and N-vinyllactames in a solvent with the aid of radical polymerization initiators at higher temperatures.Following the main polymerization, the reaction mixture is 0.05 to 0.5 wt.%, based on the monomers used, di-tert.- Butyl peroxide, di-tert.-Amyl peroxide, dicumyl peroxide, 2.5-dimethyl-2,5-bis (tert-butyl peroxy) - hexane, 2,2-bis (tert-butyl peroxy) - butane, 1,1-bis- (tert.-Butyl peroxy) 3,3,5-trimethylcyclohexane or 4,4-di- (tert-butyl peroxy) - butyl valerate or mixtures thereof, and performs the uppolymerization at a higher temperature than the main polymerization.
</ABSTRACT>
