<ABSTRACT>
This method consists of thermoformer in molds (1) of the rough-walled blanks (2).The demolding problems are resolved thanks to a regular layer of alumina or magnesia artificially formed on the surface of the blank.This process will find its main applications in thin-walled packaging fabrications as well as parts for automotive parts.
