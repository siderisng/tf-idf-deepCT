<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an X-ray tube comprising a device for reducing the divergence of its useful beam.The device consists of a slit diaphragm (13) provided with opaque blades (18) with X-rays, placed very close to the focus of the anode, inside the tube glass chamber.The opaque blades (18) divide the fireplace into several small open households generating low divergent X-ray beams, which are grouped, at a distance from the device, to form a very low divergence range flat beam.The beam obtained by such a device allows the irradiation of an object on a precise slice, which recommends its use for transverse axial tomography apparatus.
