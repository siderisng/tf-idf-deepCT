<ABSTRACT>
catalytic dimerization in solution of alpha,beta-un­ substituted 1-alkenes having at least three carbon atoms by  using a catalyst comprising a component cp₂tixy, an  alkylaluminium halide and a nitrogen lewis base, cp being  unsubstituted or alkyl-substituted cyclopentadienyl, x being  halogen or alkyl and y being halogen.  the reaction product  consists of substantially one single dimer and virtually no  higher polymers.
