<ABSTRACT>
an apparatus for mounting a scintillation crystal (40) in  a detector assembly of a gamma imaging device.  the  mounting apparatus effectively isolates forces applied by a  spring biased array of phototubes (12) from a frangible  scintillation crystal (40).  the crystal is separately biased to  the light pipe (16) using only the minimum force needed to  insure good optical coupling.
