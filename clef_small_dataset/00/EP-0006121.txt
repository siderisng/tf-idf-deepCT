<ABSTRACT>
In the receiver of a system using a quadrature amplitude modulation (QAM) in which in normal operation the data is detected relative to a first constellation A, the equalizer is initialized by detecting the data relative to a second constellation.b having a point lower than that of the first constellation and adjusting the coefficients of the equalizer from an error signal obtained from this detection operation.Application to multi-network network QAM transmission systems.
