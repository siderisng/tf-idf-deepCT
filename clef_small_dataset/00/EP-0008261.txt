<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a molded block (1) of a refractory material (2) comprising within it a steel element (3) which extends over most of the length of the block and opens the block on the extrados ofThis, the refractory material constituting the block being a refracting material electrofonned with at least one refractory oxide, and the steel element being embedded in the block during the molding of the latter and having a trans. Versale section.Suitable shape and surface for conferring the composite block, apparent thermal conductivity, between 500 and 1000 ° C, at least 8 kcal.m / m².° C.h.The block of the invention is useful for the construction of steel ovens.
