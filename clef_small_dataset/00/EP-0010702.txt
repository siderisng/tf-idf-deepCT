<ABSTRACT>
Process for the preparation of butadioldiacetate by reacting butadiene with oxygen and acetic acid or a compound which releases acetic acid under the reaction conditions containing a solid catalyst containing a platinum metal and at least one element of the 5th or 6th main group, and in the presence of Butandioldi acetate, butanediol monoacetate, butanediol or whose gems, as well as the use of the thus produced butene dioldiacetate, for the production of butandlol-1.4 by hydrogen and selling the diacetate. The method is carried out, for example, according to the associated figure, wherein in the 1st stage (1) butadiene with oxygen and acetic acid in butanedioldiacetate, monoacetate, butanediol or mixtures thereof as solvents (5) is set to but endiolacetates. In a further stage (2), excessive butadiene is separated and recycled (3), carbon oxides are also separated (4). Optionally, acetic acid and water is separated (6). After hydrogenation (7) of the designed butediol derivatives to butanaged derivatives, the reaction mixture is hydrolyzed (8) and distilled (9). In the bottom of the subsequent distillation column then the main amount of butanediol remains.
</ABSTRACT>
