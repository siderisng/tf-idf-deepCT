<ABSTRACT>
S-triazine derivatives which carry at least one polyalkylpiperidine residue and at least one N-methylolamino group or their ether as substituents in 2-, 4- and 6 positions are effective stabilizers for plastics, especially against light splitting.At the bottom of their N-methylol group with many plastics, they use a chemical bond, which makes them consistent against migration and elution.
