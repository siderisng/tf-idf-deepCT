<ABSTRACT>
Mixtures of a polymer containing chemically bonded polyalkylpiperidines in any form and a metal compound Melw where Me is a 2- or 3-valuable metal ion or a 2-well oxometallion or a 2-well dialkyltin ion, l the monovalent anion of a carboxylic acidrepresents, a phosphinic acid, a phosphonic acid monoester or an enol, and W 2 or 3 are effective lighting agents for plastics. The piperidine-containing polymers may be, for example, polyesters, polyamides, polyamines, polyaminotriazines or poly (meth) acrylates.Examples of metal compounds Melw are nickel-oenanthat or nickel acetylacetonate.To stabilize plastics are in particular the polyolefins.
