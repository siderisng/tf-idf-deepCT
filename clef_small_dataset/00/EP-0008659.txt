<ABSTRACT>
In a method and apparatus for ablie remote, removing and combining a fiber flor, the fiber flor from a delivery roller (6, 7) of a card and is composed in a florist (8) to a fiber ribbon, which is subsequently peeled off.In order to avoid rupture of the fiber flor, in particular in the region of its edges, the fiber flor from rollers (6, 7) occurs, which are not vertically superimposed on each other.The fiber band formed in the florist (8) is peeled obliquely or vertically downwardly by trigger rollers (9, 10).
