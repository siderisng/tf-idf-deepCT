<ABSTRACT>
a process for making matrix-type tablets with retarded  liberation of the active agent.  the matrix is of a  polymethacrylate plastics material.  before the tablets are  compressed, an ester of a large-molecule fatty acid or a  derivative thereof produced by hydrogenation is added to the  mixture as a controlling agent to adjust the rate of liberation of  the active agent.
