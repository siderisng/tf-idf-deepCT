<TITLE>
titanium trichloride compositions, preparation thereof, catalyst system containing them and polymerisation of olefins using this system.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
a titanium trichloride composition has the formula:   ticl₃(alrxx3-x)nealb   where e is an ether or thioether; l is an organic sulphur-containing  compound; and a and b are each greater than  0. 001 and not more than 0. 50.  the composition has a low  surface area, typically less than 50 m²/g.  the composition  can be prepared by reacting titanium tetrachloride with an  organoaluminium compound, optionally heating the reaction  product, contacting the reaction product at an elevated  temperature with at least e, and preferably l and washing  the product.  in the composition, e is conveniently di-n-butyl  ether or di-isoamyl ether and l can be phenoxathiin or diphenyl  sulphone.  the composition can be used as a component  of a catalyst for the polymerisation of olefine monomers  such as propylene.  
</ABSTRACT>
