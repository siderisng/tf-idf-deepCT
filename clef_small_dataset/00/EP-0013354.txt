method of preparing even synthetic coatings on working models for the dentistry technique.

<ABSTRACT>
Method for producing uniform plastic covers on working models for the dental technique, in which a catalyst is initially introduced on or in the working model.Then, a polymerizable plastic containing a second catalyst is applied to the thus-treated working model, which is capable of curing the polymerizable plastic together with the first catalyst.The polymerized plastic is finally allowed to cure in the form of a thin layer.
