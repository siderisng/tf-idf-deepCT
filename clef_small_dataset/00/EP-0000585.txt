<ABSTRACT>
an electronic control system is disclosed which automatically  determines the validity of an apparent overheating  signal from an overheat monitor of a gas-cooled dynamoelectric  machine and reduces machine load or indicates a monitor  malfunction.  solid-state comparators and logic gates are  included in the control for verifying the monitor signals, which  vary during overheating due to the presence of submicron  particles released into the machine coolant or the removal of  these particles from the coolant by a filter activated by the  control.  the control system initiates a scheduled reduction in  machine load either subsequent to validation of overheating  or a verification is initiated, and if a valid indication of overloading  persists, trips the excitation and remaining load from  the machine.  
</ABSTRACT>
