mercaptans on solid carriers and their application in the stabilisation of synthetic thermoplastic materials that contain chlorine.

<ABSTRACT>
mercaptoacetates and mercaptopropionates of monohydric to hexahydric alcohols on solid carriers can be used as free-flowing and low-odour stabilisers for chlorine-containing thermoplastics, in particular for pvc.  examples of suitable carriers are inorganic fillers, metal carboxylates, urea-formaldehyde resins, in particular silicic acid aerogels.  some of the mercapto esters are novel compounds, in particular the mercapto esters of n-hydroxyethyl isocyanurate and n-hydroxyethylhydantoins.
