<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a method for producing 1-amino-4-bromo-anthraquinone-2-sulfonic acid or its alkali salts of 1-nitro anthraquinone by first with sodium sulfide and / or sodium hydrogensulfide or by replacing the nitro group with ammonia converted to 1-amino anthraquinone, then treated the raw 1-amino anthraquinone at elevated temperature with oleum with the addition of alkali metal sulfates and then at elevated temperature with bromine and then the 1-amino-4-bromo-anthraquinone 2-sulfonic acid or their alkali salts from the reaction mixture by adjusting a certain sulfuric acid concentration or stirred for the reaction mixture in optionally alkali sulfate water fails, filtered off and optionally the 1-amino-4-bromo-anthraquinone-2-sulfonic acid or their alkali metal salts of water are redone. 1-amino-4-bromo-anthraquinone-2-sulfonic acid is an important intermediate for numerous valuable anthraquinone dyes.
