<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an optical fiber centering device in a tip, in order to constitute a connection between fibers.The device comprises a reference dihedron (2) which is found on the tip and also on the case in which the tip is mounted.The dihedral receives on the one hand the tip, on the other hand a set of shims (25, 26) and the positioning cylinders (23, 24) of the optical fiber.The fiber, after positioning, is set in a defined manner by overmolding.Application to monofiber or multi fiber connectors for optical lines.
