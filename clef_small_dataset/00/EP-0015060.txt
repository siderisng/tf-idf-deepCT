<ABSTRACT>
a toothbrush comprises a head (15) which encloses a pair  of brushes (43,45) disposed to brush both sides of a tooth at  the same time.  the bristles (47,49) of these brushes extend at  an angle to the sides of the teeth whereby these bristles also  enter and clean the sulcus area and embrasures.  a third or top  brush (110) may additionally be provided to clean the biting  surfaces of the teeth. ] a handle (19) is used to pull the head  along a row of teeth.  preferably, the head (15) is mounted on a  swivel (17) so that the head can be turned relative to the  handle (19), facilitating the brushing of teeth in the various  parts of a mouth.
