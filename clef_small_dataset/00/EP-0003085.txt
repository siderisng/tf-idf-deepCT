<ABSTRACT>
The shooting pipe device is associated with a sights (11-12) and it comprises on the one hand a collimator integrated in a laser rangefinder and composed of a lens (54) and a reticle (61) pointing of the rangefinder whose image is viewed in the bezel to serve the location of the distance measuring direction, on the other hand a shift collimator of the collimation line composed of a lens (21) and of A fireback reticle (23) placed at the focus of this objective and associated with a reflective optical element (3) for returning the image of the reticle (23) from the shot in the bezel, the two collimators being arranged so that the Image of the telemetry pointing reticle (61) passes through the reflector optical element (3) for returning by the firing reticle (23) to said reflector optical element (3), the shooting reticle (23) being provided with 'A reflective face to return the image of the telemetry pointing reticle (61). The device is used to equip an artillery vehicle.
