<ABSTRACT>
the invention relates to a method of making a silane-modified  ethylenic resin by reacting a silane compound  with an ethylenic resin.  in the method of the invention the  reaction is carried out by melt-kneading in the presence of  zinc oxide.  the silane-modified resins thus produced are more  stable and have better mouldability than resins produced  in the absence of zinc oxide; the latter resins tending to  cross-link in the presence of atmospheric moisture.
