<ABSTRACT>
1.  device for the contamination-free taking of samples of liquids out of a tank, characterised in that it shows a container (11, 21, 31) with suction device which, on the one hand, serves for feeding-in the sample out of the tank and, on the other hand, is able to be connected, via a vacuum-tight valve device, with an evacuated sample receptacle (14, 24, 33) of transparent material, and that the valve device which is mounted to the container or to the sample receptacle consists of a pointed cannula (14b, 23b, 32b) being open at the top or at the side and a covering (14c, 23c, 32c) of elastomeric material which is sliding and overlaps the opening of the cannula, which covering, upon piercing through a stopper (13, 24a, 33a) of elastomeric material closing the sample receptacle or the container, sets free the opening or the cannula and closes it automatically upon removal of the sample receptacle. 
</ABSTRACT>
