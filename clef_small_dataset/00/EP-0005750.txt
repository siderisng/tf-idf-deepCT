<ABSTRACT>
Photopolymerizable mixtures of alkali-soluble binders, monomers and photoinitiators are described as monomers of polyurethanes of formula I CH₂ = C-COO-Z-CONH-Y (NHCOO-X-CONH-Y) N-, where Y is a saturatedaliphatic or cycloaliphatic radical with 2 to 12 carbon atoms, R₁, R₂ and R₃ hydrogen atoms or methyl groups, n a number of 2 to 15, m a number of 2 to 4, p a number of 1 to 4, k a number of 2 to 12, R is a number of 4 to 12.The mixtures are preferably used for the production of dry resist materials.The layers have good flexibility, good adhesion to copper and low cold flow.
