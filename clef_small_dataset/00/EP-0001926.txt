<ABSTRACT>
1-(naphthylethyl)imidazole derivatives of the formula    wherein z is esterified hydroxymethylene, or ketal-, thioketal- or  hemithioketal-protected carbonyl, and the pharmaceutically  acceptable acid addition salts thereof, are useful as  anticonvulsant and anti-secretory agents.  they can be  prepared by esterifying the corresponding compounds in  which z is hydroxymethylene, or by ketalisation,  hemithioketalisation or thioketalisation of the corresponding  compounds in which z is carbonyl, or by reaction of a  halomethyl 2-naphthyl ketone ketal with an alkali metal salt  of imidazole.
