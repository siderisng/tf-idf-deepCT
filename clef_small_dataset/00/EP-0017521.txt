<ABSTRACT>
lead additive containing lead oxide and an alkaline silicate, which resists abrasion, do not include segregation phenomena and does not produce dust and whose weight ratio of the amount of lead, calculated as a PBO, to the amount of alkaline silicate, calculated as SiO₂, is greater than about 95: 5, whose humidity is less than or equal to 2%, and whose density is less than 5 g cm³.The process for obtaining such an additt has a mixtop of the lead oxide and alkali silicate, a step of conditioning the pellet paste and a thermal treatment step of these granules.
