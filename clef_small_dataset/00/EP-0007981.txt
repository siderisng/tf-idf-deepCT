sidewall protector for a tire.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
a resilient sidewall protector (11) is connected to a  rim (13) for protecting a sidewall (18) of a tire (16) mount­ ed on the rim (13) from cuts and bruises.  the protector  (11) extends from its connection to the rim (13) radially  outwardly along the sidewall (18) and has an intermediate  portion (34) shaped to receive the bulge (21) in the side­ wall (18) of the tire (16) at the footprint area.  the outer  peripheral portion (33) of the protector (11) is resiliently  urged into continuous engagement with the sidewall (18)  in response to the protector (11) being connected to the  rim (13).
