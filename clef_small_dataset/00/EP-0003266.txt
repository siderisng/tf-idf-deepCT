<ABSTRACT>
apparatus for contacting solid particles with a liquid in a  vessel having an agitator within a vertical draft tube, for  instance for dissolving phosphate rock particles in acid, is  characterised by means for supplying fluid under pressure to  a bottom portion of the vessel to prevent solids settling and  to resuspend solids that have settled, e. g.  after shutdown.  apparatus for similar purposes is disclosed having a  vent for minimising foaming.  also disclosed is a method of start up a continuous  phorphoric acid production process in a multi-vessel system  comprising four vessels (2), (16), (28) and (48).
