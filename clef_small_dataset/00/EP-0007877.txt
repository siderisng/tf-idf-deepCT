<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a racking head for drinks distribution apparatus and especially soft drinks.The head comprises a spout formed of a tubular element of vertical axis, whose internal section increases gradually downwards, depicting the inlet port of the gaseous water to the outlet orifice and DA chute 20 disposed under said tubular member in slightly inclined position relative to the horizontal, the passage of the concentrated liquid jet in substantially vertical flow in front of the chute and downstream of its end.
