<ABSTRACT>
Method for treating boron-containing steel for continuous casting, in particular for draining in small formals with unregulated spout.In the formed in the casting stage steel melt is introduced by an inert carrier gas and in powder form calcium or a connection thereof, at least one element and boron, or a boron compound stable nitride and a boron compound.The thus treated steel is protected on its path from the pan into the mold before re-reaction with the air.
