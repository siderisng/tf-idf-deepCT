<ABSTRACT>
1.  partition wall consisting of a metal framework to which two series of panels (7, 8) constituting the two faces of the wall and a frame (1) adapted to be anchored to the floor, ceiling and walls of a building are detachably secured, said frame having fitted thereto the casing (2) of the framework consisting of section members having at least one u-shaped portion, means (2a, 53, 54, 59, 60) being provided for positioning the framework in relation to the frame (1), characterized by the fact that the section members constituting the casing (2) are of the perforated web type, that the framework comprises intermediate uprights (3) and cross members (4) consisting of identical i- or u-sectioned members with perforated webs (4a) connected to one another and to the section members (2) of said casing (2), that the panels (7, 8) are fastened to the uprights (3), the cross members (4) and the casing (2) by means of rotary fastening members (9) mounted to said panels (7, 8) and provided with at least one wing (9a, 9b, 9c, 9d) engaging in the fashion of a clamping cam the inner face of the wing of at least one section member of said casing (2), uprights (3) or cross members (4). 
</ABSTRACT>
