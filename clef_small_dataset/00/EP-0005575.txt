<ABSTRACT>
prefabricated chimney, more especially industrial chimney, characterised in that at least the outer shell consists mainly of a combination of various superposed rows of elements (2) placed side by side in the form of ferrules, preferably of reinforced concrete, the various elements (2) being connected to one another and to the base of the chimney (1), both in the vertical direction and in the horizontal direction, by stressing means such as cables (21), rods or similar elements.  <image>
