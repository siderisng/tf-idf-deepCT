<TITLE>
process for the production of non-agglomerated gamma ferric (iii) oxide.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
the process comprises oxidising iron(ii) hydroxide in suspension in alkaline or acid medium with oxygen-containing gases, separating off the resulting iron(iii) oxide hydroxide, preparing a pumpable mass from the iron(iii) oxide hydroxide with a liquid which is inert thereto, spraying this mass into a container by means of a rotating disc and simultaneously drying the iron(iii) oxide hydroxide using countercurrent inert gases heated to temperatures above the boiling point of the liquid used.  <image>
</ABSTRACT>
