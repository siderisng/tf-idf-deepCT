<ABSTRACT>
a vapour-cooled semiconductor device such as is used throughout current converter technology.  the semiconductor device is composed of a stack of disc-shaped semiconductor components (2) and evaporators (3) at each of whose ends an insulating piece (4) and a bearing-type part (5) on an insulating piece (4) are arranged.  the stack (1) is clamped along with the components (4) and (5) in a clamping fastener (13).  the stack (1) is surrounded by a coolant container (14) comprising a jacket (20) and two terminal plates (21, 22).  the terminal plates (22) have an aperture for feeding through one end of the stack (1).  this stack end is connected mechanically in a gastight manner to the terminal plate (22) via a corrugated tube (19).  in the event of changes in length of the stack (1) which occur on clamping the stack in the clamping fastener (13) and as a result of thermal variations, the one stack end slides in the aperture of the terminal plate (22).  the corrugated tube (19) is also moved under these conditions and ensures the mechanically gastight joint between the container (14) and the end of the stack (1).  since the clamping fastener (13) runs outside the coolant container (14), the container (14) has a smaller volume than previous designs.  freon is used as coolant.  <image>
</ABSTRACT>
