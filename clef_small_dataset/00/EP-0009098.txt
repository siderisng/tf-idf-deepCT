<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to Schottky barrier film diodes with low potential threshold using a metallurgical diffusion barrier layer between a transition metal barrier contact (2) and an aluminum-existing metallization pattern (3) for preventing an interaction between the two layers.The diffusion barrier layer (4) consists of a discrete layer formed layer of an intermetallic connection from the transition metal and aluminum.
