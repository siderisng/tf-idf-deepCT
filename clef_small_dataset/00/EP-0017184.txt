<ABSTRACT>
A tough-plastic, sugar-free confectionery composition, which is described 20 to 50 wt.-% of a particular sugar-free source, 20 to 35 wt.-% xylitis, 0.5 to 3 wt.-% water, optionally 0.1 to 5 wt.-% conventional adjuvants and sorbitol contains a residue, a process for their preparation and their use for the production of confectionery, in particular of tough-plastic sweets.This confectionery does not contain the common sugar harmful to the caries, has a rounded taste, can be easily manufactured, can not be produced and sometimes do not show the strong crystallization tendency of sorbitol and xylitol, and their emerging, partly unwanted flavor properties.
</ABSTRACT>
