<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a differential olfactometer.This olfactometer comprises two sprinklers (1, 2) located on either side of a nasal support (3) and respectively associated with a circuit (4) of olfactory stimuli;a retractable anti-scratching device (5) for hiding the sprinklers during time intervals between nasal stimulation periods;a breath detecting device associated with an electronic control unit (6) for controlling the hocks occultation;This olfactometer is characterized in that the feed circuit of the sprinklers comprises means for supplying them permanently.Application to the measurement of olfactory thresholds and odorous intensities.
