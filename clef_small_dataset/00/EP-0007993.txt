<ABSTRACT>
conductor board (2) for mechanically supporting and electrically connecting unencapsulated semiconductor chips (1).  in order to make a high packing density possible, the same material is chosen for the substrate (2) of the conductor board as for the substrate (1) of the semiconductor chips.  this enables very fine wiring structures to be achieved and thermal stresses to be avoided.  the invention can be used for logical aggregates in general and for memories in particular.  <image>
