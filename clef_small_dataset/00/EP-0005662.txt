<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for the rapid detection of a wind gradient.This method uses the relationship between the aerodynamic slope γa, the total slope γt and the anemometric velocity V, the relationship that can be of the shape in which the laplace operator, A is a value which is substantially constant or none, inCase of normal flight without disturbances, and which crisps in the case of a wind gradient, wind before becoming a rear wind, and VSEL is a setpoint value.This value A is mixed at a value representative of the ability of the aerodyne to overcome a wind gradient, so as to obtain a composite signal compared to a reference value.The invention can be used at the time of the approach phase of an aerodyne.
