<ABSTRACT>
the halogenoacetyl group is a useful protective group  for amino compounds, but the known reaction for removing  this protective group with thiourea has disadvantages.   according to the invention, a halogenoacetyl group is  removed from a halogenoactyl amino compound in a short  period of reaction time by reacting the halogenoacetyl amino  compound with an n-substituted dithiocarbamic acid or a  salt thereof.  this reaction is useful in the chemical synthesis  of compounds containing amino groups.
