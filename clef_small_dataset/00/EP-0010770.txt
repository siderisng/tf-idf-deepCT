<ABSTRACT>
1. N- (2-substituted-4-pyridyl) enhances of the general formula (I) wherein X is a halogen atom, a hydroxy, lower alkoxy, lower alkylthio, lower alkoxycarbonyl. Amino, never of the alkylcarboxyamino, benzoylamino, low alkoxy carbonylamino, cyano- or trifluoromethyl group, wherein the lower alkyl or alkoxy group should be lower than C₆, R₁ is a hydrogen atom, a halogen atom or a lower alkyl group, R₂ one, optionally by A lower alkyl or alkoxy group, a hydroxy group or a halogen atom, substituted, aromatic group and Y is an oxygen or sulfur atom, where x should only be a chlorine atom if R₁ is a HA logenatoma, as well as its acid addition salts. These compounds are suitable as plant growth regulators. They can be prepared in which a) a corresponding 2-substituted-4-amino-pyri din reacted with a corresponding phenyl isocyanate or b) a corresponding 2-substituted-4-pyridyliso cyanate is reacted with a corresponding aniline or c) a corresponding 2-substituted-Isonikotinoyl azide is reacted with a corresponding aniline.
