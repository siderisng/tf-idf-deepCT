<ABSTRACT>
In a field effect transistor, of the MOSFET or MISFET type, comprising a source (2), a drain (3), a door (5) and a channel region (4), impurities, such as Tellu Rium., with deep energy levels and low diffusion speed, are implanted in the area of apparatus (11).This implantation makes it possible to reduce the sibility of the MOSFET transistor to the variations in the voltage applied to its substrate.
