<ABSTRACT>
New 1,4-dihydropyridazine-3-carbonyl compounds of formula I in which R is hydrogen, an alkyl radical, an aryl or aralkyl radical, R¹ for an alkyl, aryl or aralkyl radical or for the group OR⁵ or, z.b.The compound of formula Ia are obtained by reacting compounds of formula II in inert organic solvents with ozone and then with hydrazines of formula III.Herein R², R³, R⁴, R⁵, R⁶, R⁷, R¹ ', R²', R³ ', R⁴' and X have the meaning given in the description.The compounds thus obtained can be derivatized in accordance with generally known methods of saponification, esterification, transesterification or amidation.They can be used because of their circulatory influencing, spasmoly tables and anti-inflammatory action as antihyper tensive agents, as vasodilators, as coronar therapeutics, as spasmolytics and as anti-inflammatory inhibitors.
