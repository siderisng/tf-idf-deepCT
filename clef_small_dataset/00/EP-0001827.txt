<ABSTRACT>
A multiple relay comprises an electromagnetic control relay (9) and electromagnetic relays (8).The semi-fixed contacts (10, 11) of the relays are carried by a common bar (4), insulating, pivoting about an axis (5) under the action of the control relay.Each semi-fixed contact of a relay is connected to a conductive bar (6) (a conductive bar for a fixed semi relay contact) carried by the common bar.The electrical contact between a fixed semi contact and a movable contact is established by supply of the control relay and the relay, and deleting the supply of the control relay and the relay;The semi-fixed contact locks the mobile contact.The rupture of the electrical contact is obtained by momentary supply of the relay of COM MANDE.
