<ABSTRACT>
the specification relates to retro-reflectors of the type  used for road signs and markers and vehicle licence plates  to make these objects more noticeable to road users after  dark.  in the invention, the surface of a metal substrate is  made retro-reflective by applying a layer of an organic poÂ­ lymeric material and adhering glass beads thereto, and  then passing the substrate through a roller mill or the like  with the glass beads covered by a platen so that the beads  form indentations in the metal surface.  the presence of the  platen allows the pressing (indentation) step to take place  without demage to the glass beads or to the layer between  the beads and the metal surface.  by this means, a hard  metal article can be made directly retro-reflective.
