<ABSTRACT>
compounds of the formula    and their agriculturally suitable salts wherein   q is o, s or    r, r₂, r₃, r₄ and r₆ are various organic groups, or in some  cases h; r₅ is h, ch₃ or och₃;   r₁ is a pyrimidine or tirazine residue; and   w is o or s;   are useful for the regulation of plant growth and/or as  pre-emergance or post-emergence herbicides.  the compounds can be prepared e. g.  by reacting an  appropriate benzenesulfonyl isocyanate or isothiocyanate  with an appropriate aminopyrimidine or aminotriazine.  the  compounds can be formulated for agricultural use in conven­ tional manner.  also the production of isocyanates of the for­ mula    wherein the substituents r, r₂ and r₃ are previously defined,  is described, which are used as raw material for the prepara­ tion of the compounds with the above-mentioned formula.
