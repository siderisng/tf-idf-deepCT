<ABSTRACT>
Device for cooling a long scrolling product, comprising an elongated tube traveled by a cooling liquid and in which also passes the product to cool.According to the invention, the tube 1 is equipped with an interior of a centering member 13 of the scroll product, comprising a central passage 14 of lower diameter to the diameter inside of the tube 1. Advantageously the cren organ 13 presentsPeripheral longitudinal channels 16 distributed around the central passage 14 to facilitate the circulation of the coolant.The invention makes it possible to improve the homogeneity of the edition and preferentially applies to large format products, such as steel bars which, because of their weight and low travel speed, are more subjectsthat others coming into contact with the inner wall of the tube.
