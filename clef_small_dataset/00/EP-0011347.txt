<ABSTRACT>
Condenser with a housing (1), closed by a bottom (2), containing a winding (3) of MU capacitor or electrical connections (5, 6).The housing (1) is provided with an extensible fold (7) whose plane separates the winding (3) of an auxiliary piece (8) for the anchoring of one of the electrical connections.The auxiliary piece (8) is enclosed between the PLI (7) and the bottom (2) and comprises an opening (9) which evade towards the bottom (2).This opening (9) is filled with a synthetic resin (10) in which a loop (11) of the connection (5) is anchored.When a suppression occurs in the condensate, the fold (7) is stretched and the connection (5) is broken.Application: Lighting by fluorescent lamps.
