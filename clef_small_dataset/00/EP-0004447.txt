<ABSTRACT>
a process for preparing a trifluoromethylphenol and  trifluoromethylphenyl benzyl ether is described.  it comprises  heating a trifluoromethylhalobenzene with a sodium  benzylate to form a trifluoromethylphenyl benzyl ether of  the formula    wherein r and r¹ when taken individually are c₁-c₃ alkyl,  methoxy or nitro and when taken together and occupying  adjacent carbon atoms in the phenyl ring, methylenedioxy,  and n and m taken individually are 0 or 1, and optionally  hydrogenolysing the trifluoromethylphenyl benzyl ether to  give the corresponding ether.  the trifluoromethylphenyl  benzyl ethers are new compounds.
