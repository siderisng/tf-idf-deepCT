<ABSTRACT>
The process relates to the creation of a contact on the surface (23) of a semiconductor body (21), by a screen printing method using a deposition (25) of conductive pulp containing the addition of a doping element susceptibleto increase the concentration of surface impurities during the vitrification treatment of said dough.According to the invention, the method comprises a second deposition (28) of conductive pulp, located at the only location provided for welding a connection (27), the paste of said second deposit being devoid of the doping element.
