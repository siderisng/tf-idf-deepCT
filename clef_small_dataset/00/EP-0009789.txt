<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a cuff for sphygmoma nometers consisting of a tubular band having a buckle at which one end of the tape is permanently stilized and by which the tape forming a loop is performed with the free end, with either afirst embodiment for the temporary fixed release of the loop on the tape (10) a coating (16) is provided from a magnetizable film which adheres to correspondingly magnetized relative coating (12), or according to a second embodiment for the temporary detection of the loopThe tape (10 a coating (16) is provided which is allowed to lapse on a correspondingly applied coating, wherein according to the invention a bracket (17) of the buckle (11) carries at least a portion of the clotted coating (12).
