<ABSTRACT>
Aqueous tissue treatment machine in pieces.This machine comprises: a dyeing system laid to the rise of the fabric, as a result of which are provided mechanical means of return of the tissue in a storage compartment (4) equipped with mechanical means of movement of the fabric (12),A receiving and guide device (3) of the tissue to said compartment, provided between said mechanical means and said compartment.A second dyeing system can be provided at the descent of the fabric.Folding means may also be associated with the machine, with unloading device of the treated tissue.Application to simultaneous treatment (dyeing, bleached or other) of a plurality of fabric strands.
