The invention relates to a foot supporting sole whose boundary line (24) on the foot outer side in front of the patch bale (28) and at the foot inner side behind the large toe bale (27). In order to be able to use such sole in all commercially available shoes and to achieve that no accurate adaptation to the foot of the respective carrier is required, it is proposed to produce the sole (2) with substantially equal thickness of resilient ela static material and to train them so that they are curved at the foot inner side (3) upwards, wherein the stiffness of the sole (2) is sufficient at least in the area of ​​the external external external way to achieve that the foot is rotating toward the large toe during running Rollen.
