<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method and an image processing device.The method comprises the steps of comparing the level of the luminance signal S (I, J) of each point P (I, J) of the image and the level S (I - K, J - P) of an adja pointACENT POINT P (I, J), correction of the level S (I - K, J - P) of at least one adjacent point by algebraic summation at this level of a correction level + Δ LSM and- Δ LSMfollowing the sign of the difference S (I, J) - S (I - K, J - P).The correction levels Δ LSM and Δ LSM are marked RE values of the luminance gap histogram of two adjacent points of the image.The device comprises means 1 of comparison of the signals S (I, J) and S (I - K, J - P) and the correction means 2 of the level S (I, J).application to the visualization systems of radiological devices.
