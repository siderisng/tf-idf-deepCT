<ABSTRACT>
Method and apparatus for the installation and daemon Tage of a so-called "Incredible" room inside a tire. In accordance with this method, the placement of a chamber of this type is carried out by having a prior extension according to an axis disposed in a plane perpendicular to the median general plane of the corresponding tire (2). Then, while maintaining this extension effort, the front end of this chamber is placed inside the adjacent portion of the tire, and then pushes, inside the opposite part thereof, the Other end of the room, while relenting the expansion efford exerted so far there. For this purpose, the implementation apparatus comprises an extension compass whose branches (12) and (13) make it possible to push successively the one and the other ends of the chamber within the corresponding parts. tire. This method and appliance can be used for the establishment and retravention of the prevailing chambers previewed on the tires of certain vehicles, such as armored vehicles of the army and the gendarmerie or certain public works gear.
