<ABSTRACT>
In order to be convinced with negative ionic etching in the production of monolitically integrated semibles and nitrides, in the vacuum chamber, a magnetic field of the negative ions is generated in the vacuum chamber by means of a grid of a soft magnetic material to the direction of movement of the negative ions.The field absorbs such a strength that the negative ions to be etched can pass the grid, which can not exit the plasma for maintaining the gas discharge needed electrons.
