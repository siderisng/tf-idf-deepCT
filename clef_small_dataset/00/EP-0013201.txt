<ABSTRACT>
The subject of the invention relates to a cathode with electronic thermo emission with direct heating.It comprises a support (1) in pyrolytic graphite and a thermosetting material (2) based on lanthano hexaboride, these two elements being separated by a diffusion barrier layer (3) constituted by a tantalum carbide or hafnium, a platinum family metal, or a boron compound.It can be used in particular in the high frequency tubes of the triode type, tetrode or pentode.
