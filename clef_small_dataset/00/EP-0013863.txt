<ABSTRACT>
the prosthesis comprises a socket cap (1) and a head cap which are provided with in each case one central journal (2) and, arranged concentrically around this, a number of smaller anchoring journals (3), all the journals having sawtooth-like anchoring means.  the socket cap (1) can be made from carbon fibre reinforced plastic and the head cap can be made from aluminium oxide ceramic.  in order to strengthen the anchoring, the two caps are coated, on the side facing the bone, with apatite or apatite-containing biological glass.  <image>
