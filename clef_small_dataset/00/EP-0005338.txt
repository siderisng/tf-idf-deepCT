<ABSTRACT>
carbamates of the formula    wherein r₁ can be certain aliphatic imino groups, r₂ can  be certain aliphatic imino groups or aryl groups and x is  selected from certain alkylene groups, exhibit insecticidal  and nematicidal activity.  the compounds may be made by appropriate coupling  reactions of alkane disulfenyl halides.  they can be formulated  for use in conventional manner and applied in an  effective amount to the locus to be protected.
