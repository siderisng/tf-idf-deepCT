<ABSTRACT>
The patent application is a transparent film for the overhead or daylight projection with a color layer held in a dark hue.This dark hue can be changed by means of a write or painting device such in a contrasting light color that the dark hue is transferred with the help of a reduction in a colorless shape.Since in the same writing instrument, in addition to the reduction causing the reduction of liquid still one with this non-reacting light liquid is located, with the same line of the bright hue on the colorless location of the film is visible and contrasting to the dunk len hue of the other film contrasting.The bright dye may be contained in both the writing / erasing fluid of the writing instrument as well as in the coating of the film and also in the film itself.
