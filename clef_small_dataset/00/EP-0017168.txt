<ABSTRACT>
in this oil-scooping apparatus, a perpendicularly running rail (4) is arranged in the after region of a ship (2), which rail (4) accommodates an air tube (1) via a roller arrangement (5).  the air tube (1) forms with the ship (2) a scooping funnel (3) open in the travelling direction and adapts itself to the position of the ship and the wave movements through the roller arrangement (5) and the rail (4).  the collected oil is scooped up in the vertex region of the formed collecting funnel (3) via a suction device (13). 
</ABSTRACT>
