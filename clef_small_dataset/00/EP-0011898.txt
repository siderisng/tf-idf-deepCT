<ABSTRACT>
method of manufacturing a semiconductor device com­ prising a body which consists of semiconductor material of  the iii-v compound type and which is formed by an n-type  substrate (1, 2, 3, 4) and a substrate-adjoining region (5, 6)  having conductivity properties differing from the substrate,  zinc or cadmium being introduced at least over a part (13) of  the surface (14) of the region into the region and being dif­ fused to the substrate, characterized in that the net donor  concentration in the substrate near the junction (10) between  the region and the substrate is chosen to be so high that the  diffusion of zinc or cadmium in the substrate near the junction  (11) between the region and the substrate is retarded.
