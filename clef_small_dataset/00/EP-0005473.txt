<ABSTRACT>
a process for the production of elastic laminates from rubber and/or elastomeric plastic granules and a polyurethane binder which cures on contact with water and is accompanied by shaping and curing with water, in which the binder used is a mixture of an isocyanate group-containing prepolymer based on 2,4- and/or 2,6-diisocyanatotoluene and polyhydroxypolyethers with a polyisocyanate or polyisocyanate mixture from the diphenylmethane series, and a binder of this type which is suitable for carrying out the process.
