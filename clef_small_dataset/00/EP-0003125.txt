<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for electrolytic etching of a recrystallized aluminum foil.A recrystallied aluminum foil is roughened by potentiostatic etching.This gives films with particularly high roughing factors.In particular, the etching takes place in two stages, wherein in the first stage, an etching current density above the pitting potential of aluminum is used and the etching with an etching current density takes place below half of the pitting potential.The invention is particularly suitable for etching aluminum foils from which electrolytic capacitors are produced.
