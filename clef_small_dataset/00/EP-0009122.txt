<ABSTRACT>
Aqueous dispersions of polyesters or alkyd resins for the coating area application is obtained according to the invention by incorporation of 10 to 100 mol%, based on polycarboxylic acids, on multi-based carboxylic acids in which the carboxyl groups are bound to secondary and / or tertiary C atoms.These dispersions are characterized by excellent storage stability and protruding properties of which are produced therefrom.
