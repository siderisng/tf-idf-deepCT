<ABSTRACT>
Device for measuring the compactness of cylindrical-shaped smoking articles, such as cigarettes, cigarettes - filters or filter sticks comprising an ali-post unit (3, 4) to bring articles to a positioning assembly andof distribution, two units of measuring the compactness mounted on either side of this assembly, which together comprises a recti line path extending substantially from one unit of measure to the other to allow each article ofMove from a central receiving position to an extreme measurement position, means (1, 2, 10, 11) for positioning the articles in the receiving position alternately in two different angular positions and means (17) for moving theArticles following said path alternately towards one and the other unit of measure.
