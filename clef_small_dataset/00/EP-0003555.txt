<ABSTRACT>
Preparation of alkanes substituted by chlorine atoms and / or sulfochloride groups by reacting alkanes with chlorine and optionally sulfur dioxide in a particular flow ratio and in a response space inclined to the horizontal reaction space, wherein the starting materials are passed through the reaction space in direct current and the reaction space in the lower partwith light of the wavelengths of 500 to 700 nanometers and in the upper part with light of the wavelengths between 200 and 500 nanometers is exposed.The substituted alkanes or paraffins produced by the process of the invention are pesticides, plasticizers, solvents and valuable starting materials for the production of such substances, as well as leather fatigues, wetting agents, detergents, lubricating oils, synthetic resins, lubricants.
