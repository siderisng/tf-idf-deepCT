<ABSTRACT>
a chain wheel and crank assembly for a cycle having  a chain wheel (1, 11) mounted on a crank shaft (41) of a  crank (4) by means of an adapter (2) having a plurality  of legs (22) extending radially outwardly from a boss (21)  of the adapter (2).  in order to increase strength whilst reducing weight,  the adapter has at least one generally annular member (23,  24) interconnecting the outer ends of the legs (22), and  at least one of said legs (22) and said annular member  (23, 24) is provided with mounting means (51, 52) for  mounting of the chain wheel (1, 11) on the crank (4).
