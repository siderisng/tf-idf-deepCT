<ABSTRACT>
For testing container walls having protrusions (4), they can use rails (9) between the projection gene which leads to a test device (41) carriage (25).The tester (41) may be reacted with the aid of a carriage (12) to the individual rails, which is movable on one of the ends of the guide rails (9) connecting rail (11).The carriage (25) is connected to a tensile and pressure-strip thrust hose (37), which is guided on the rail (9) and is operated with a drive (65) arranged on the carriage (12).The drive (65) is assigned to a boom (13) of the carriage which is dimensioned for the toe of the carriage (25) with the test device and arranged movable for adaptation to the rails.The new device is used in particular for testing the lid (1) of reactor pressure containers in the area of the nozzle (4) for control rod drives.
