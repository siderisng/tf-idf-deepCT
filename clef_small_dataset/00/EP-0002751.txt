<ABSTRACT>
Inkeeping compounds of semiconductor structures a terminating resistor changeable by a reference voltage is provided. By either adapting to the transmission resistance of a guide connection can be prepared, which is formed from a circuit card via a module to a chip. Various line capacities of such a guide include various impedance values. The termination resistor is formed by a so-called pinch resistor (60). Signals transmitted from a guide compound are supplied to a terminal of the pinch resistor and a first control input of a differential amplifier (transistors T₁, T₂) whose second control input is connected to a selected reference voltage. The output of the differential amplifier is connected via an intermediate amplifier (transistor T₅) with one or more control electrodes of one or more pinch resistors, each of which forms the termination resistance of a guide compound.
