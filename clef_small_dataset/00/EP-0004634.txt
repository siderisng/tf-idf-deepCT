<ABSTRACT>
the incombustible insulating material contains inorganic lightweight particles with or without fibrous or granular inorganic additives bonded together by organic polymeric binders.  to suppress the evolution of toxic gases in the event of a fire, the insulating material contains from 0. 1 to 15% by weight of a compound of trivalent boron.
