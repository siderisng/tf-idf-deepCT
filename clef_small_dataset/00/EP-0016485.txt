<ABSTRACT>
In the anode disk according to the invention, the focal stain is arranged on a pyrographitring, which is oriented so that the surfaces of the larger thermal and electrical conductivity are parallel to the axis of rotation of the ANO disk.As a result, a good heat dissipation is secured without the bearings being thermally overloaded.
