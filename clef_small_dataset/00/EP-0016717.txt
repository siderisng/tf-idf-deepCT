<ABSTRACT>
This support arm (1) keeps the slide (2) parallel to itself.A basic pivot (4) turning around a vertical axis (oz), is controlled by a first cylinder (7).An arm (10, 15), articulated with the base pivot (4) about a horizontal axis (W), is controlled by a second cylinder (12).A support block (18) is mounted rotating at the end of the arm, its rotation being controlled by a motor (20).On this block is articulated a cradle (23) supporting the slide (2) and controlled by a third cylinder (25).The motor (20) and the third cylinder (25) are enslaved to the position parameters resulting from the movements controlled by the two first cylinders (7) and (12), via calculators, so as to automatically maintain the slide.(2) Parals to herself.The invention applies to the drinking devices for the advancement of mine galleries and the digging of tunnels.
