<ABSTRACT>
The object of the present invention relates to new dichloroacetamide and trichloroacetamide derivatives, their methods of preparation and application as antidotes of HER bicides. The existing herbicides currently can be classified into two categories: total herbicides, which destroy all plants, and selective herbicides. The latter, which are the most valuable in agriculture, make it possible to destroy the weeds present in cultures without harming the cultivated plant and are said, for this reason, selective from the cultivated plant. Unfortunately it happens that this selection is not perfect at the dose of use and that the selective herbici have some phytotoxicity vis-à-vis the cultivated plant. It is also used, in combination with herbicides, so-called herbicide antidoted products that have the property of giving herbicides increased selectivity or new selectivity, without harming their quality herbicide. In other words, the antidote makes it disappear completely or at least largely, the phytotoxicity of the herbicide vis-à-vis the cultivated plant without reducing its phytotoxicity vis-à-vis unwanted plants.
