<TITLE>
continuous kneader for polymers.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

<ABSTRACT>
the kneader comprises a body (1) containing an internal bore (5) in which is rotatably mounted a rotor (6) equipped with blades (7).  injection means (8, 22) enable polymers (23) to be injected under pressure into the bore (5).  an adjustable clearance (j) is provided between the tip of the blades (7) of the rotor (6) and the bore (5) of the body (1).  the kneader comprises means for automatically adjusting the clearance (j) based on parameters which depend on the resistance encountered by the rotor (6) during the kneading of the polymers.  the application is especially for the production of semi-finished plastics intended to be extruded, laminated, etc.  . . .
