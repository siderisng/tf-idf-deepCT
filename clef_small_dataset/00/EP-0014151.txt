<ABSTRACT>
The filter of the invention comprises several membership cells (C₁ to CK) each comprising a RAM (M₁ to MK), a dead memory (H₁ to HK), and an arithmetic unit (U₁ to UK).Are common to all cells: a page address counter (CT₁), a word address counter (CT₂) and a weighting coefficient address counter (CT₃).The transfer of discrete values Rétar deed is made by relative addressing of the words of the memories.Application: Half-band extrapolator filter in telephoning.
