<ABSTRACT>
photographic recording material containing polymers  which coordinate with metal ions, complexes produced  thereby and novel polymers are disclosed.  the recording  material comprises a support, a photosensitive silver halide  emulsion layer, an image dye or dye-forming material and  a layer containing a dye mordant.  the mordant layer has  associated therewith a source of metal ions and a polymer  which is capable of forming a complex with metal ions.  the  complex provides improved light stability to image dyes.   the image dyes are preferably dye ligands or dye ligand  forming materials.
