<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a mechanism for remotely adjusting a wheel attached to a rotating table, the tool of a device used to place and remove an automobile tire, which mechanism consists in the main order of a vertical column., of an arm which can move transversely in this column and in which it is placed an axially movable bar in height and which, at the lower part, has a tool to place and remove the tire.The arm (8) transversely movable in the column (6) is mounted as an oblique and drafted to the wheel (4) and between the arm (8) and the vertical column (6) it is provided a means (20 to 25)for moving and blocking the aforesaid arm (8).
