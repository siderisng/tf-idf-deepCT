<ABSTRACT>
1-oxo-1H-pyrimido (6,1-b) benthiazoles of the general formula in the R₁, R₂, R₃ and R₄ each hydrogen or halogen, a hydroxy, nitro or trifluoromethyl group, a lower straight-chain or branched alkyl , alkoxy or alkylthio residue, wherein R₂ and R₃ can also be introduced together an alkylenedioxy group, and X is a hydroxy, alkoxy or tetrazolyl-5-amino group, with antiallergic, anti-indema-sufferer and anti-inflammatory effect, method for Production of these compounds as well as these compounds containing medicines. The preparation of the substances according to the invention is carried out by rearranging 4-oxo-4H-pyrimido (2, 1-b) benzthiazoles or of N- (benzthiazol-2-yl) -amino-methylene-malonic acid diesters to 1- (2- Mercapto-phenyl) pyrimidine-2,6-diones and their cyclization to the desired end products. The invention further relates to the 1- (2-mercaptophenyl) pyrimidine-2,6-dione-pyrimidine-2,6-dione occurring as intermediates and their use for the preparation of the 1-oxo-1H pyrimido (6, 1-b) according to the invention) benzthiazole derivatives.
