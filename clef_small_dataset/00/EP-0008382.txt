<ABSTRACT>
the embossing of films of thermoplastic art substances, in particular the two-sided embossing, by means of a pre-roll pair, is carried out according to this method so that the embossing roller (4.9) is held at a temperature, which is in the plastic temperature range of the thermopla stable plastic, thefed film (1) has a temperature in the temperature range between the glass transition temperature of the thermoplast and a temperature up to about 70 ° C, and that the foil supplied has a speed of 5 to 200 m / min and a pre-pressure pressure of 100 to 500N / CM is exposed.Through this method, it is prevented that during the two-sided presents the first applied embossing is undesirably changed by the second predisposition in the undesirant manner.The pre-foils can be used as decorative, furniture or writing foils.
