acoustic flowmeter with reynolds number compensation.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
an acoustic flowmeter wherein pulses of acoustic ener­ gy are transmitted between upstream and downstream  transducers through the field under measurement and  wherein the arithmetic computation of the velocity based  upon the difference in time measurement of the oppositely-­ directed pulses is modified by predetermined factors to  automatically compensate for errors caused by a reynolds  number change due to flow velocity change.
