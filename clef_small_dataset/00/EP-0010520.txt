<ABSTRACT>
New substituted 11-amino-andeca-4, 8-diene and 11-aminocoanal derivatives of the formulas Ia wherein R₁ to R₆ are, for example, alkyl, Y are preferably OH and M preferably 1, and methods for their preparation of 1-AZA-1,5,9- cyclododecatriender derivatives or from 1-azacyCyclododecode derivatives, preferably by transplexing with hydroxylamine.The compounds of the formula Ia and Ib may be catalytically hydrogenated to new 1.11 diaminepholeecanes, which in turn may be used as an example manner for the preparation, polycondensation products, mainly transparent polyamides, or as hardener for epoxy resins.
