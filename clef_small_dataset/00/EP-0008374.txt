<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a CCD input circuit according to the "fill and spill" principle, in which by arranging a distribution and a barrier electrode (Ø3 *) (G4) and a suction diode (ID *) side next to the electrode series an undesirable equal partcan be separated from the change in the signal and can be sucked off.The optimal operating point is in contrast to known Ten Ten CCD input circuits for fully control.With the invention there is an arrangement with high linearity with high gain.
