The pneumatic tire consists of 2 side parts and a belt-like tread.At each side part is formed half a tread surface;In the tread base tapes are attached;The bands are on gaps and come in depressions in the other tread base, so that the tread pad is flat in the assembled state.The beads present at the ends of the bands hold the tread.
