<TITLE>
a vertical indicating device and a device for maintaining a stable vertical in two dimensions
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

<ABSTRACT>
a gyroless vertical indicating device which tracks the  local gravity vector independent of horizontal acceleration.   a compound pendulum (20) and an angular accelerometer  (10) each has a single rotational degree of freedom about  a common axis (35).  rotational motion of the pendulum  (20) and of the angular accelerometer (10) about the axis  (35) is coupled by a quartz fibre suspension (40a, 40b) the­ realong.  a signal representative of the relative angular ve­ locity between the pendulum (20) and the angular accele­ rometer (10) is fed back through a coil (50) to a torquing  system (60) which maintains the pendulum's orientation  along the local gravity vector.  
</ABSTRACT>
