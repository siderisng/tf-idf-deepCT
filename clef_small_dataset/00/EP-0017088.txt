<ABSTRACT>
Monolithically integrated filter circuit, in which a charge shift arrangement (CTD) is provided with a closed transmission channel (15) to which the channels of further CTD's (16) are attached to predetermined nodes.Such filter circuits seek low transfer losses and high transmission frequencies.This is achieved in that each additional channel (16) with the transfer direction of the first channel (15) in each case an angle (32) is enclosed, which is much smaller than 90 °, but is so great that the further channel (16) With its entire width in the area of a single displacement electrode (18 15) of the closed channel (15).The application manner of this circuit includes integrated filters with resona gate properties.
