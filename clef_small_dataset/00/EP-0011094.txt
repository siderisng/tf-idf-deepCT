<ABSTRACT>
The measuring device contains two modulators (11.4: 21, 4) for the formation of pulse sequences (S1U; S1i) whose pulse duration represents a measure of the instantaneous value of the voltage or the current.Pulse counter (13; 23) determine from the pulse sequences (S1U; S1L) digital voltage and current measured values (MU: MI).To determine the average (MMU, MMI) of the pulse sequences (S1U; S1i) serve a mean value Bil of the (14; 24) which can be pulse counter.Differential members (16; 26) form the difference from the measured values (mi; mi) and the mean value (mmu; mmi), whereby the influence of asymmetries in the measuring device and of equal voltages and equal currents in the network compensates for.The average values (MMUi) are smoothed in smoothing filters (15; 25).which operate after a smoothing algority, which is stored in a memory of a microcomputer (32).
