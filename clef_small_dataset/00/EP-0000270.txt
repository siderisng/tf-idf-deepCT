a composition comprising a liquid polymerisable resin  and an alloy of iron and silicon, known as ferrosilicon, for use  as a metal repair or reclamation material.  the invention also  includes a two-component pack including the liquid  polymerisable resin as one component and a catalyst or hardener  therefor as the other component, ferrosilicon being  present in one or both components.  also described is the use  of the composition and of the pack in the repair of metal  parts.
