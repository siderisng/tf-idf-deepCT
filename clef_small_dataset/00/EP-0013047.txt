<ABSTRACT>
Device with a basket (3) with unwinding pipes (5), a back rotating device (7) and a take-up reel (13).The back rotating device has two eccentric SCHEi Ben (31, 33), which are mounted in a rotatable guide ring (35), wherein in the two washers eccentric axles (65) are arranged with an axial bore (75) and a clamping element (77).The guide ring and one of the two discs are independently touched ben.The return effect can be changed from 0 ° to over 360 °.With the device, cores of both torsions and with predetermined torsion can be roped to a cable.
