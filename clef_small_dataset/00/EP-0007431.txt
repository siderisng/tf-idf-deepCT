<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a construction profile strip suitable in particular for the manufacture of window frames and a method for their preparation.The profile strip has a thermoplastic plastic-extruded shell (10) in the form of a hollow profile which is filled to achieve good heat and sound insulation properties and a high strength firmly with a profile bead (11) of polymethyl metacrylate and hollow silicate beads.To improve the bending stiffness, in particular, glass threads standing under a longitudinal train are embedded in the profile bead core.In the case of continuous production of the construction profile bar, the extrusion tool for the hollow profile serves to introduce methyl metacrylate, silicate beads and glass threads into the inside of the plastic coat.
