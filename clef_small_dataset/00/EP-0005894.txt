<ABSTRACT>
method and apparatus for rotating the bushings (26)  encircling the pins (38) of the pivoted interconnections of  track links (34,46), such as employed in crawler tracks or in  conveyor chains, in order to ensure regular wear of all parts  of said bushings in contact with a sprocket.  the improvement involves vibrating the links relatively  to one another by means of an exciter (68) acting between  members (48,52) secured to respective ones of the links  while applying a rotational force to the bushing by means  (18).
