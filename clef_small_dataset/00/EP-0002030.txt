<ABSTRACT>
Compounds of the general formula in which X is a trifluoromethyl group or a fluorine atom, N 1 or 2, Z an oxygen atom or NR grouping in which R is hydrogen or a substituted alkyl group, wherein in the Benzoringen there are other non-chromophor substituentscould be.These compounds can be prepared by reacting the dichloride of the 1,4-naphthalene-dicarboxylic acid having 2 moles of an O-aminophenol or O-nitroanilin and subsequent cyclization of the obtained acylamino compound.The products are suitable as optical brighteners.
