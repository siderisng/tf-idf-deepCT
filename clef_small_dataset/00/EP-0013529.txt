<ABSTRACT>
Ice equipped with a pyrotechnic cutting device for obtaining a backup outcome by cutting said ice according to a specific route.The ice is constituted by two parallel walls (2b, 2a) respectively internal and external between which is handled an empty space (13).The device consists of means (20) inserted between said walls capable of producing a detona and mounted in such a way that the detonation of said means produces a detonating anisotropic effect such that said detonation causes simultaneously according to said plotted the sound and ejection.from said outer wall on the one hand and the fragiliation of said inner wall on the other.Application to the evidence of aircraft for aircraft susceptible to constitute an emergency exit.
