<ABSTRACT>
a dual mode preamplifier for a data recording read/write  system comprises a plurality of read/write channels (a to l)  and a monitor channel (m).  each channel (a to l) includes a  magnetic head (16) and high resistance resistors (18 and 20).   during the read mode, the read preamplifier functions con­ ventionally.  during the write mode, the write signal is attenu­ ated and monitored.  a monitor circuit (m) senses failures in  the write operation and can detect the absence of write cur­ rent transitions.  isolation between the nonselected  read/write channels and the selected read/write channel is  effectively provided.  a detection circuit (26) is coupled to  detect electrical shorts in the magnetic heads and wiring.  
</ABSTRACT>
