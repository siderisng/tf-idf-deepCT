<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an electro-optical display device, especially a liquid crystal display whose carrier plates are versed with a guide layer and a dielectric coating. In a liquid crystal cell, this coating is usually used to orient the liquid crystal layer. In order to antix with such a display, the conductive layers are proposed according to the invention, proposed to give the conductive layer and the coating the following thick D₁ or D₂: 0. 5 d₁ ≦ d₁ ≦ 1. 5d₁ and 0. 5 d₂ ≦ d₂ 1.5 d₂, wherein with D₁ = thickness of the guide layer, D₂ = thickness of the dielectric layer, λ = mean free wavelength of the operating light, N₁ = refractive index of the conductive layer, N₂ = refractive index of the di electric layer, N₀ = refractive index of the carrier plate and N₃ = refractive index of the medium At rest. In a particularly preferred embodiment, 0. 85D₁ ≦ D₁ ≦ 1. 15D₁ and 0. 85D₂ ≦ D₂ ≦ 1. 15 d₂. It is possible in the context of the invention. The thickness d₁ and d₂ should be dimensioned so that the guide layer-free behind basic surface of the display can be held reflection-free or that the reflections of the conductive layer and the guide layer-free background surface are the same.
