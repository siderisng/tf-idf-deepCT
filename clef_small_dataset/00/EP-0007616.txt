<ABSTRACT>
The isothiocyanobenzthiazole derivative of the formula as well as its non-toxic salts for warm-blooded salts have a particularly outstanding efficacy against lecturer in sheep in addition to generally good anthelmintic properties.The active ingredient is administered to the Teren together with proper carrier and additives in the form of solutions, emulsions suspensions, powders, tablets, bolisms and capsules peroral or intra-ruminal.
