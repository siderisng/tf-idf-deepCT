<ABSTRACT>
A pivot pad for devices, preferably for screen devices, allows the variable inclination and rotation about a spherical convex support surface (12) in a foot part (6) of the device, on the support surface (12) is a bearing on the bottom of the device Liche concave storage pan(20) supported with friction adhesion and which is guided with a guide web (21) in a groove groove in the support surface (12).The bearing element has a central terminal of the support surface of narrowing opening (17), in which a clamping pin (22) clamped between the bearing pan (20) and sliding surface (18) is adjustable.By spring elements (25) in the clamping device, the friction force of the support surface can be adjusted exactly.
