<ABSTRACT>
Process for the preparation of weather-resistant, hard-shaped polyurethane-based molded foams with a cellular core and an integral density distribution across the cross section of the molded bodies by foaming a foamable reaction mixture to a polyurethane, wherein the isocyanate component of the foamable mixture having an isocyanate groups and urethane groups having reaction product of at least one cycloaliphaticBound isocyanate group and at most an aliphatically bonded isocyanate group (cyclo) -aliphatic diisocyanates with suggested amounts of organic polyhydroxyl compounds.
