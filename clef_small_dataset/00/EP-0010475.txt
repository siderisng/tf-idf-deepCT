<ABSTRACT>
set of profiled and complementary parts consisting of a main profiled part whose section is in the form of a h, the ends of which are folded and brought together so as to form two parallel faces (1, 2) each comprising a slot (14, 15) and the joining web or core of which (3a, 3b) comprises at its centre a tube (20) which can be internally threaded.  the section of this profiled part has two axes of symmetry perpendicular to each other (xx', yy') passing through the centre of the tube (20), which permits bevel-cut assembly along one or the other of these axes of symmetry.  fastening is effected by means of a screw passing into a hole pierced in the end of one of the profiled parts and screwed into an insert (49) itself screwed into the end of the central tube of the other profiled part.  the slots (14, 15) are closed by a closure profiled part (25, 38, 40) comprising two wings (26, 27), locking by elastic deformation onto the edges of the slots (14, 15).  the use of the main profiled part in the form of spacers (55, 56) makes it possible to assemble panels (57) disposed between these spacers, which panels are held by a threaded rod (58) screwed into the tube of the profiled part after a compressible washer (62) has been placed in between.  the invention is applicable to the production of various tables, desks, storage furniture, shelving, book cases and cupboards, as well as to framing for windows, balconies and terraces and to the production of hand rails.  <image>
