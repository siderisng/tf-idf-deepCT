<ABSTRACT>
Partially oriented melamine-formaldehyde resins with a molar ratio melamine: formaldehyde = 1: (1.25 to 1) can be prepared by neutral or weakly alkaline condensation of melamine with formaldehyde when condensation in the presence of a glycol mono ether of the general formula IR (OCH₂CH₂)NOH, (i) wherein R alkyl with 1 to 4 C atoms and n represents an integer from 1 to 5.The partially oriented melamine formaldehyde resins are suitable for producing pressing materials with high flow capability and minimal processing and sustainability.
