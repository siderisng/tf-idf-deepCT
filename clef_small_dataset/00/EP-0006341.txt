<ABSTRACT>
a method for improving the colour and impact strength  of a nitrile polymer, in particular an acrylonitrile or meth­ acrylonitrile polymer, produced by the polymerisation  of an olefinically unsaturated nitrile and a preformed rubber-­ component, said polymer having at least 50 weight per cent  of the nitrile, characterised in that the polymer is heated  to a plastic state in the absence of oxygen and in the pre­ sence of an inert gas.
