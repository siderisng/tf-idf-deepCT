<ABSTRACT>
a reprographic camera is adapted for being used in day­ light conditions by the provision of a positioning mechanism  (20) which automatically positions a photosensitive material  in the middle of the glass screen (31) prior to exposure.  the  photosensitive material is supplied from a roll (16) located in a  light-tight dispensing station (17).  the positioning mechan­ ism (20) comprises a knife (29), sheet transport rollers (30) and  a timer (25), the latter controlling number of revolutions of the  transport rollers (22, 23 and 30) necessary to dispense and  position a length of photosensitive material corresponding  with a predetermined size.
