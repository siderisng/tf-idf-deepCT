<ABSTRACT>
a process for the production of three-layered laminates comprising an outer layer of polyolefin, an inner layer of an ethylene copolymer and an outer layer of an optionally foamed polyvinyl chloride.  in this laminate, the middle layer comprises an ethylene copolymer with a vinyl ester of a c2- to c4-alkanecarboxylic acid and/or with an alkyl ester of a c2- to c4-alkenecarboxylic acid and optionally with an ethylenically unsaturated c3- to c4-carboxylic acid, the copolymer also containing small amounts of an organic peroxide.  the copolymer is preferably an ethylene-alkyl alkenecarboxylate-alkenecarboxylic acid terpolymer, and the outer layer is preferably polypropylene. 
</ABSTRACT>
