<ABSTRACT>
The coding of the television or trans television signals put in digital form is performed using the MO Differential coded pulses (MICD). A quantization feature that allows to reduce the digital flow to be transmitted or process. The characteristic is, at least partially, non-symmetrical with respect to zero, with regard to the number and goes their thresholds and levels of reconstruction of the differences. Digital examples are given. In a variant, the quantization characteristic varies according to the PN prediction value. The variation range of PN prediction is cut into zi pre-diction zones. At each prediction zone is associated with a characteristic AC quantization. Prediction areas are not symmetrical compared to the mid-dynamics of PN prediction. The quantization characteristics may or may not be segments of an extended characteristic, which is preferably not symmetrical.
