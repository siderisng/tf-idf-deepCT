<ABSTRACT>
armored vehicle with a motor, rolling members and motion transmission means to the rolling members.The vehicle includes: a) a central main cell (3 to 16 or 32 to 38) heavily shielded and surrounding the essential mechanical staff and organs.(b) outer protection (22-23 or 44 to 46) lighter, surrounding the main cell and overflowing beyond the rolling members.The invention applies to military cars of liaison or recognition.
