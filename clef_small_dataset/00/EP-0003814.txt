<ABSTRACT>
The invention is new [3- (4-hydroxy-5-pyrimidyl) -ure ido] penicillins of the general formula I, their salts or methods for producing these compounds and these compounds containing medicaments. In the general formula I, a optionally substituted phenyl radical, a thienyl, cyclohexyl, cyclohexenyl or cyclohexadienyl radical, and R in addition to hydrogen is an alkyl, alkenyl, cycloalkyl or cycloalkenyl radical, an optionally substituted phenylalkyl radical, a cylclopropylalkyl radical Hydroxy group, which may optionally be substituted by an alkyl, alkenyl, cycloalkyl, phenyl or benzyl group, an optionally substituted mercapto group, an alkylsulfinyl group, a free or substituted amino group, optionally substituted piperazino or phenylalkylamino group, an acylamino group, or one Alkyl, aralkyl or arylsulfonylamino group. The compounds of general formula I and their salts are characterized by good antibacterial properties against gram-positive and gram-negative germs in good general compatibility, with high serum and tissue levels, as well as high urine levels, according to parenteral and oral administration. The compounds show surprisingly good effects against typical hospital germs and are clearly superior to all known and chemically related penicillins. The preparation is carried out by attaching an α-aminobenzyl, α-amino-p-hydroxybenzyl or α-amino-α- (1,4-cyclohexadien -1-yl) - methylpenicillin to corresponding pyrimidylcyanates or their reactive derivatives or by reacting Ureidocarboxylic acids or their derivatives with the 6-aminopenicillanic acid or their derivatives.
