<ABSTRACT>
Acetic acid, ethanol, acetaldehyde and optionally their follow-up products are prepared by catalytic reaction of carbon monoxide with hydrogen.The catalyst contains on a support of metallic rhodium, halide ions and magnesium salts or complex compounds or compounds of magnesium with oxides of elements of groups III to VI of the periodic system.
