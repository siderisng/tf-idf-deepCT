<ABSTRACT>
planar integrated semiconductor circuit in which the layout of the wiring channels on the semiconductor surface is independent of the layout of the active switching circuits and components inside the semiconductor substrate.  in this way, the compact arrangement and/or the accommodation of the wiring channels on the surface of the semiconductor chip can be altered so that the utilisation of the wiring channels is thereby increased.  <image>
