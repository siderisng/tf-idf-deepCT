<ABSTRACT>
1.  a container (1) for receiving and pressing of waste material, particularly of paper waste material, which can be closed by a lid (3) projecting into the container (1), the lid (3) being pivotably supported and being connected to actuation means, characterized in that the actuation means comprises a beam (4) traversing the front side of the container (1), the beam (4) being supported by the container (1) and being horizontally movable by a horizontal rod (5) on both sides of the container (1), respectively, and by two links (6, 7) articulated to the rods (5) and to a container wall (8), respectively, and that the rods (5) comprise a driver (11), respectively, the driver (11) being engaged with a curved guide (12), respectively, the guide being rotationally fixed to the lid (3) and being formed and located with respect to the pivoting axis (2) of the lid (3) in such a manner that when shifting the beam (4) in the direction of the container (1) the lid (3) is pivoted upwardly.
