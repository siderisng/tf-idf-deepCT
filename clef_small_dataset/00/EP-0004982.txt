vibration-damped rotary annular bellows assembly.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
a split-ring (34) radially expandable under centrifugal  force for damping vibrations of a rotary annular bellows (10)  by pressing against the inner surfaces (38) of at least some of  the convolutions (12) of the bellows (10) as the speed of the  bellows (10) increases.  preferably, the split-ring (34) is  positioned in a channel (46) for rotation with the bellows (10)  and for radial expansion as a function of the rotary movement,  while restrained from axial movement with respect to  the bellows (10).
