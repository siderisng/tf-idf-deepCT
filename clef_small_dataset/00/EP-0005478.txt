<ABSTRACT>
A device has a cylinder (1) with a slidably guided therein, adjustable by a hydraulic fluid piston (3). The device further comprises a capacitive transducer arranged in the cylinder (1). This has a piston (3) attached and arranged in a longitudinal opening (13a) of this, electrically conductive with the cylinder (1) connected tube (23) and an insulated on the cylinder (1) mounted in the pipe (23) projecting into the tube (23) Staff (27). The latter is connected via an electrical operation (35) with the input of an outside on the cylinder (1) attached electronic part (43). This has an oscillator which has an electrically connected to the cylinder (1) and a differential amplifier connected to the non-inverting input of a capacitive counter-coupled differential amplifier. The inverting input of the differential amplifier is electrically connected to the insulated from the cylinder (1) fixed rod (27).
