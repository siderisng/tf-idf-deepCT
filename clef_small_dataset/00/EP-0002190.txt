<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for illustrating an image from an optically contrasting background by means of a range between different optical states (idle state, excited state) switchable display device. The device includes a preferably plate-shaped body (fluoresceller body) (1) and a light valve (5) in front of the fluorescent body. The fluorescent body consists of a material with a refractive index greater 1, is provided with light exit windows (3) and has released particles that fluoresce in a first frequency range (emission belt). The light valve has a number of the light exit windows, respectively, in a second frequency range (absorption tape) blocking display elements. For image display, individual regions of the display device are switched by generating an electric field. According to the invention, it is provided that the circuit by change of the possibly additionally narrowed emission tape of the fluorescent particles ("useful band") or / and the absorption tape of the light valve is happening. The proposed method allows a relatively large viewing angle range, allows control franchisements and requires no pure AC operation. The invention applies to a so-called "fluorescent activated display (flad).
