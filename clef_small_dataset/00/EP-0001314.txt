<ABSTRACT>
The device makes it possible to perform a concentration and sorting of solid waste without broken recyclable elements.For this purpose, the ferrule 10 of the apparatus which further comprises an extractor means of flexible products, comprises lifters 11 of beveled shape and filled with coating 12 shocking the shocks on the elements to be sorted.Part of these is eliminated in C with large perforations 15, the large flat elements being collected in B1, the elements having three large dimensions being evacuated in B2.The invention applies in particular to the concentration and sorting of solid waste from industrial or housewife.
