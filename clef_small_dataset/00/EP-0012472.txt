<TITLE>
a process for preparing 1,7-octadiene.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

<ABSTRACT>
1,7-octadiene is by hydrodimerizing butadiene in the  presence of a catalytic amount of palladium and a tertiary  organophosphinite or tertiary organophosphonite ligand,  formic acid or a salt thereof and optionally a solvent.    the tertiary organophosphorus ligand has the general  formula:   (ro)aprb   wherein the r groups which may be the same or different,  attached to the phosphorus or oxygen atoms, are aryl,  alkyl, alkenyl, cycloalkyl, aralkyl or alkaryl groups having  from 1 to about 20 carbon atoms, at least one r being a  sterically hindering group, a + b equals 3 and b is 1 or 2  or   z-p(or)hri   wherein z is a cross-linked resin, the r groups, which may  be the same or different, attached to the phosphorus or  oxygen atoms, are aryl, alkyl, alkenyl, cycloalkyl, aralkyl or  alkaryl groups having from 1 to 20 carbon atoms, h is 1 or 2  and i is 2-h.  
</ABSTRACT>
