<ABSTRACT>
Process for obtaining a ferromagnetic material based on chromium dioxide and rho dioxide dioxide.It comprises oxidation, under conditions ordered from temperature and pressure, CR₂O₃ or a precursor of CR₂O₃, in the presence (1) of a suitable amount of a compound of Rhodium III likely to give RHO₂ inThe conditions of the reaction, and (2) of an oxidizing agent constituted by an oxygenated compound free of metal cations other than chromium and, possibly (3) of dilution water and / or at least one complementary doping agentacting as a buffer of the reaction medium and / or at least one classic modifier.Application to obtaining a ferromagnetic material having improved magnetic characteristics and in the form of a solid solution CR1-X RhxO₂, O <x <0.5.
