<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an improvement in the methods for producing acyloxysilanes and acyloxysiloxanes by reacting chlorosilane with aliphatic carboxylic acid at elevated temperature in a column.The improvement is achieved according to the invention by the fact that aliphatic carboxylic acid, z.b.Acetic acid, in evaporated form from bottom to top Chlorsilan, z.b.Ethyltrichlorosilane, opposite, aliphatic carboxylic acid in amounts, which at least not substantially exceed 1 mol of grammathom Si-bonded chlorosilane, and in the dimension of their consumption into the column, in the production of acyloxysiloxanes in addition to carboxylic acid and chlorosilane up to 10% by weight, based onThe weight of the carboxylic acid, water is introduced into the column, and drain at the lower end of the column acyloxysilane and / or acyloxysiloxane.
