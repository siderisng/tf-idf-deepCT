<ABSTRACT>
An aqueous, cold-stable detergent concentrate is characterized by the following composition: a) 22.5 - 35 wt. -% of a potassium fatty acid so far, which consists of saturated fatty acids (C₁₂₋₁₄) and / or oleic acid, b) 5 - 9 wt. -% of an ethoxylated primary linear or 2-position methyl branched alcohol (C₈₋₁₄) with an average of 5 - 10 ethylene glycol groups, C) 4-10 wt. -% sodium dodecylbenzenesulfonate, d) 0. 05 - 1 wt. % optical brightener from the class of substituted stilbenesulfonic acids in the form of Na or K salt, e) 0. 1 - 5 wt. -% potassium hydroxide, f) 0 - 6 wt. -% Triethaholamine, G) 8 -20 wt. -% of an alcohol (C₁₋₃). The concentration of substances mentioned under a) to e) should a total of 50 wt. -% Do not exceed. The detergent concentrates are applied using softened water in an amount of 3 - 10 g / kg troc kennel. They are suitable as pre-or main detergents for textiles of all kinds. Preferred a sentence area are commercial laundry companies.
