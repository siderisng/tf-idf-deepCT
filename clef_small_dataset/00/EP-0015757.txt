<ABSTRACT>
a process for the preparation of a chlorodeoxy deriva­ tive of a reducing aldose having a primary hydroxy group in  an exocyclic -ch₂oh group, in which the said primary  hydroxy group is replaced by a chlorine atom to give a  chlorodeoxy reducing sugar with a free anomeric centre,  characterised in that the free reducing sugar is reacted with  a reagent comprising an n,n-dialkyl (methaniminium)  chloride obtained by reacting an n,n-dialkyl formamide with  a chlorinating agent.
