<ABSTRACT>
The invention are infusion solutions for low-calorie parenteral nutrition, which are contained in 10 to 200 g essential and non-essential amino acids such as 50 to 10,000 μg of kinine in a liter of infusion solution and mineral salts.The preferred amounts, based on a liter of infusion solution, are 50 to 100 g of amino acids and 200 to 1000 μg of kinine.
