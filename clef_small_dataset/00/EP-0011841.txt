<ABSTRACT>
The preparation of silicon carbide moldings, in particular pipes, starting from a porous pre-body (1) of carbon or carbon and silicon carbide by impregnating and implementing elemental silicon at at least 1400 ° C, is improved by the invention that silicon powder (5) on the approximately 1400 to 1500 ° C hot pre-body (1) is spared, which is then subjected to a filtration between 1800 and 2000 ° C for Si-C implementation. The pre-body (1) is preferably rotated during the suspension. Tubular bodies are in particular by sprinkling silicon powder (5) in which coated by about 30 ° against the horizontal and rotated tube coated or impregnated. Impregnation and implementation are carried out under inert gas, in particular argon. As a device, a tube furnace (3.7) having two separately controllable heating zones for impregnation (3) and reaction (7) and means for rotating (13) and axial feed of the pre-body and means (4,6) for sprinkling silicon powder suitable, which is preferably inclined towards the reaction zone against the horizontal.
