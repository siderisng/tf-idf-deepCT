<ABSTRACT>
Method of connecting two sets each having connecting terminals using conductive connections.A flexible insulating support is defined having two connection ends and a central portion, at least one of the connection ends comprising at least one recess, the recess or the recesses being able to superimpose to the terminals of the corresponding assembly.It is sticked on said supple support n electrical connections, one of the ends of each connection being disposed above a recess at the right of the terminal to which it must be connected, and the electrical contact is ensured between each end of the electrical connections.Application to the realization of high density circuits.
