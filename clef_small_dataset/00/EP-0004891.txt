<ABSTRACT>
1.  method for producing packs (11) with a turned-down flap (16), which overlaps an opening slot, from a foil sheet or the like, the folded together foil sheet being longitudinally cut open, characterized in that at least one of the resulting superimposed foil strips (12, 13) is laterally displaced in such a way that the turned-down flap (16) are formed in the vicinity of the longitudinal cut.
