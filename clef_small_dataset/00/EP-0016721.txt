<ABSTRACT>
The illustrated gear measuring machine has the object to measure toothed data such as the teeth profile, the teeth pitch, or the toothing profile, to measure the teeth or the toothed-actual deviations on a test object (10).A radial slide (16) is supported via radial guides (14) and displaceable by means of a radial drive.The latter comprises a servomotor (16a), a tachogenerator (16b) and a position sensor (16d).In addition, both a tangential carriage (26) and an axial slide (30) is controlled in the same manner, as is the case with the radial slide.The different drives are also connected to position controllers.From a signal of a button (34) and correction sizes of the corresponding position controllers, the target-is deviations can be determined.
