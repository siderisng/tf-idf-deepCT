<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a method for producing 1-amino-4-bromoanthrachinone-2-sulfonic acid of 1-amino anthraquinone by heating 1-amino antthraquinone with SO₃-containing sulfuric acid and then treated the mixture with bromine at elevated temperature.The advantage of the method according to the invention is in the single-stage procedure.1-amino-4-bromo-anthraquinone-2-sulfonic acid is an important intermediate product for numerous valuable dyes.
