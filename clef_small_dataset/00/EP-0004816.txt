<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a sizing composition for the coating of continuous glass filaments at the time of their manufacture from melted glass, as well as the treated glass fibers, used in the reinforcement of plastic materials.The new sinimage composition comprises a system of several crosslinkable tights between them, in particular a compound having many N-methylol and / or N-CH₂or functions, R being an alkyl radical, at least one acrylic polymer, at least one agent ofbridging likely to react with the sticky system.The treated glass fibers are mainly used in strengthening SMC prepregs.
