<TITLE>
4,4'-thiodiphenol-formaldehyde condensation products, their preparation and their use in the treatment of polyamide fibers.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
fixatives or agents for the aftertreatment of acid dyed  polyamide textiles, to increase the wet-fastness of dyeings,  comprise condensation products having the structure    wherein r is a radical derived from a phenolic compound  capable of reacting with formaldehyde; x is 1 or 2; y is 0, 1, 2  or 3; z is 0, 1, 2 or 3 and y + z is 1, 2 or 3 and m is h, na, k, li,  nh₄ or a mono, di or tri alkanol ammonium.  the compounds  are made by reacting 4,4′-thiodiphenol with formaldehyde,  condensing the reaction product with a phenolic compound  and reacting the product with a formaldehyde-bisulfite  adduct.  these products are also useful as reserving agents  for polyamide fibres when blended with cellulosic fibres and  the latter dyed with direct dyes.  
</ABSTRACT>
