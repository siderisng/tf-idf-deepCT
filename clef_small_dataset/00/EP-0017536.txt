<ABSTRACT>
NEW process for step-by-step preparation of somatostatin making it possible to obtain a high purity product, with high yields from cysteine in position 14 whose acid function is protected by the phenylazobenzylsulfonyloxy group and the thiol function is protected by theAcetamidomethyl group, all peptide coupling reactions being carried out in the liquid phase in the dimethylformamide, the intermediate peptides being precipitated by addition of a second solvent and purified solid phase by washing using suitable solvents dissolving impurities.
