<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method and a device for producing in particular tubular multilayer winding bodies with fiber assembly changing fiber layer to fiber layer.The method provides that the winding bodies are made by winding an inner core by means of a plurality of, in the passage direction of the inner core and lying to this coaxially extending coil carrier.The winding device is designed particularly so that the individual coil carriers (2,4,6,8) each independently adjustable speed adjustment devices (30) with infinitely variable adjustable and rotational direction reversible transmissions (22) are zazzen.
