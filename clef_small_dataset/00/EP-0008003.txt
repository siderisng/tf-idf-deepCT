<ABSTRACT>
novel 16-phenoxy and 16-(o, m or p)-substituted phen­ oxy derivatives of (dl)-9-keto-11α,15α-dihydroxy-17,18,19,20-­ tetranorprosta-4,5,13-trans-trienoic acid, the pharmaceuti­ cally acceptable, non-toxic lower alkyl esters and salts  thereof and processes for the production of such com­ pounds.  these compounds possess prostaglandin-like ac­ tivities and thus are useful in the treatment of mammals  where prostaglandins are indicated.  they are particularly  useful as inhibitors of gastric acid secretion; and as agents  for the control of asthmatic attack, because of their broncho-­ dilating activity.
