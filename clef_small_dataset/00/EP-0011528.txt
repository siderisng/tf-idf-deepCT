<ABSTRACT>
Osteosynthesis pieces, having the form of a solid tridi, based on a resorbable biocompatible polymer.They come in the form of composite parts constituted by a lactic poly-acid matrix or a copolymer thereof, said matrix containing reinforcing elements, embedded in the matrix, based on polyglycoli acid than or ofA copolymer thereof, and possibly containing a load capable of stimulating poly mother's resorption for the benefit of a neoformed tissue.Application to the repair and consolidation of fracture bone.
