<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the devices for positioning a sensitive plate (105) in the plane image of a projection lens (106) of an optical device.It consists in ensuring this optical apparatus of a set of extensible cylinders (202) placed in crown around the lens (106) and defining by the ends when they are output the desired image plane.A ball joint device (102) then places the plate (105) against the ends of the cylinders (202).The joint (102) and the cylinders (202) is retracted (202), which makes it possible to move the plate (105) into the image plane while remaining at the focus distance.It allows to build photo-repeaters.
