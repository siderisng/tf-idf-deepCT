<ABSTRACT>
the moulding compounds comprise 100 parts by weight of wood particles, from 50 to 900 parts by weight of an olefin polymer or styrene polymer and from 1 to 15 parts by weight of an mgo powder.  the olefin or styrene polymer preferably contains carboxyl groups; polymers containing carboxyl groups are taken to mean graft polymers of monoethylenically unsaturated carboxylic acids on polyolefins or polystyrene.  the moulding compounds are suitable for injection moulding and extrusion and are distinguished by a pale inherent colour.
