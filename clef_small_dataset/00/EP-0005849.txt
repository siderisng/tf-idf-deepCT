<ABSTRACT>
According to the invention, α-chloroacetoacetamides, including both α-chloro-n monoalkyl, and α-chloro-n, N-dialkyl-acetoacetamides whose alkyl radicals comprise 1 to 3 C atoms, by reacting the corresponding acetoacetamidesChlorine in aqueous solution made at temperatures in the range of -1 ° C to -25 ° C.In the case of a conversion of at least 95%, a selectivity of at least 97% and a yield of at least 95% of theory, based on reacted acetoacetamide, is achieved.
