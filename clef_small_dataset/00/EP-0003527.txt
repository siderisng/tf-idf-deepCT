<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method and arrangement for the focus adjustment on an electron beam or a charge carrier beam system for compensation of surface irregularities of a semiconductor pane or the inclination of a mask.In an electron beam system with an alignment system, which measures the position of four alignment marks by means of the beam and calculates the disclosed magnification error of a given semiconductor wafer, means are provided to use the magnification and rotation error for calculating a height error factor and therefrom a compensation current for the dynamic focus coil for the dynamic focus coil forDissrease the electron beam, thereby shifting the effective or effective focal plane to a position adapted to the semiconductor or mask plane at the site of each semiconductor tile.
