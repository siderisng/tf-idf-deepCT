<TITLE>
method of electro-optical distance measurement and apparatus for carrying out the method.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
In an electro-optical rangefinder, a reference signal is shifted by means of an electronic phase slide (20) until there has a predetermined PHA metrication to the measurement signal.The two signals are compared to the phase comparison in a double-balanced mixer (22) whose output signal in a control loop (20, 22, 23) controls the electronic phase shifter (20).The size of the resulting phase shift shift is measured by frequency conversion of phase-shifted or non-phase-shifted reference signal by means of two other double-balanced mixers (24, 25).The method allows the structure to clear compact precision distance blades working with measuring frequencies by 500 MHz.Control circuits for laser diocensender (1), avalanche diode receiver (13), PHA slide (20) and intermediate frequency of the measurement signals, as well as processor control (5, 29) for automatic MES condition.
</ABSTRACT>
