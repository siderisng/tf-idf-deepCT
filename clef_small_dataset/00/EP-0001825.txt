<ABSTRACT>
a method of preparing 2,4-difluoroaniline by reacting 2,4,5-trichloronitrobenzene  with a fluorinating agent in the presence  of a solid-liquid phase interface, and utilizing a phase  transfer catalyst to form 2,4-difluoro-5-chlorinitrobenzene,  followed by hydrogenation with hydrogen in the presence of a  hydrogenation catalyst to form 2,4-difluoroaniline.
