<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a deaeration valve for the bagging of fluidized powder products in open-mouth bags.The valve 2 comprises a filter plate 14 housed inside 12 of the bag 1 to be completed, this plate having a texture and a thickness E such that it multiplies the suction effect through the valve and plays the role ofa "wick" to drain the air in a large volume, the dimensions (H, L) being important vis-à-vis those (H, L) of the deaeration valve 2 and its surface up to thatFrom the bag 1 spread flat.Application to the deaeration of waterproof bags intended to receive a micromized pulverulent product.
