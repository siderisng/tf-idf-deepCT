<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the realization of video discs by molding from a engraved matrix (11). It is intended to deposit on an edge of the matrix (11) an elongate bead (23) of a curable resin: photo-polymerizable or thermosetting, and to spread progressively the resin between the matrix and a flexible film (3) a substrate that is applied by means of a presser roller (6). The resin is then cured according to the cases by an ultraviolet (16) or infrared raw through the film. The method may also include drilling the cen treal hole (T) and disk cutting. The realization of a succession of discs on the same film can be made automatically, the movie advancing step towards each operating and / or cutting. It is also possible to reduce dual-face discs. The invention applies to the average manufacture rose video disks, possibly on the very places of use.
