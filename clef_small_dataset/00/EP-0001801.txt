<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for producing a condensation product of phenothiazine and p-nitrosophenol containing the indophenol S-oxide of the formula by reacting phenothiazine with p-nitrosophenol in sulfuric acid, wherein phenothiazine before or during the reaction with an additional oxidizing agentis oxidized.The resulting condensation product or the reduction product obtainable from the condensation product can be converted by sulfurization in the baking or cooking melt into sulfur dyes which supply green green and polyamide green to black dyes with good fastness.
</ABSTRACT>
