<ABSTRACT>
Flame-resistant thermoplastic molding compositions based on styrene polymers containing 0.05 to 5 percent by weight of an organic bromine compound as a flame retardant and optionally a propellant.The bromine compound contains in the molecule a heterocyclic ring with the grouping N = C-CBr₃, it is thermally stable at a temperature of at least 150 ° C.The preferred bromine compound has the formula the molding compositions can for the production of self-extinguishing moldings, e.g.b.Foams, used.
