<TITLE>
cutting device with saw teeth.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>
lach diamantwerkzeug jakobde <sep>jakob lach diamantwerkzeug-fabrik<sep>jakob lach diamantwerkzeug-fabrik bruchköbeler landstrasse 39 d-6450 hanau/mainde<sep>

lach horstde<sep>lach, horst<sep>lach, horstbruchköbeler landstrasse 41d-6450 hanau/mainde<sep>
</INVENTOR>
<ABSTRACT>
in order to reduce the proportion of fine dust during the cutting- off of materials and at the same time to increase the stability of cutting-off devices (10) having saw teeth, of which a portion of the saw teeth (16) project beyond the other saw teeth (12), a portion of the projecting saw teeth (16) are formed by diamond laminae (20).  in this arrangement, the projecting saw teeth (16) preferably project beyond the other saw teeth (12) in accordance with the thickness of the diamond laminae (20).  <image>
