<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a device for producing hexagonal phosphorus pentoxide consisting of a combustion chamber (1) provided on its inner wall with cooling tubes (10) and further relating to a gas cooler (3) with a condensation chamber (4), whereinThe gas cooler (3) consists of a cooling tube system (5) which ends in the manner of an open dipping tube above the bottom of the condensation chamber (4) whose walls are provided with a cooling device (6).
