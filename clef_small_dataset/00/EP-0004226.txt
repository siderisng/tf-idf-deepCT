<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a solvent extraction separation method of metal compounds contained in aqueous solutions.According to the invention, the aqueous solution is connected with an organic phase comprising at least one substituted hydroxyquinolein.The method of the invention is more particularly applicable to the separation of at least one element selected from the group consisting of rare earths and the yttrium of an aqueous solution containing it.
