<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a glass feedthrough, preferably for housing for semiconductor devices.To increase the creepage path between the metal parts (1, 4, 5) of the glass feedthrough, melting on the glass part (2) a preferably manner made of ceramic existing insulating body (3).The end faces of the ceramic ring (3) may be chamfered so that a capillary gap (9) is formed between the glass part (2) and the ceramic ring (3).
