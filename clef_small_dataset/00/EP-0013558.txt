<ABSTRACT>
Method for producing naphthalene-1,3,5-trisulfonic acid by sulfating 1.5 -disulfated naphthalene in the form of a mixture obtained by combining naphthalene and SO₃ in the presence of an inert solvent at temperatures of -40 to + 20 °C, obtaining a SO₃ / naphthalene ratio of 2.5 to 10 throughout the combination, in anhydrous sulfuric acid with oleum under prior or simultaneous separation of the inert solvent at 60 to 110 ° C.
