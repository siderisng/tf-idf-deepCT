<ABSTRACT>
an electrode for electrolytic processes such as the  recovery of uranium dioxide from seawater comprises an  electrically-conductive corrosion-resistant substrate having  an electrocatalytic coating which is preferably a mixture  of 30 to 80 parts by weight of platinum, 20 to 70 parts  by weight (as mn metal) of β-mno₂ and 2 to 10 parts by  weight (as sn metal) of tin dioxide.
