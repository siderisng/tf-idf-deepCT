<ABSTRACT>
The invention is within the scope of a method for producing homo- and copolymers of α-monoolefins by means of a Ziegler natta catalyst system of (1) a titanium halide of the formula TiCl₃ · M AlCl₃ wherein M stands for a number of 0 to 0.5, (2) an oxygen compound of the formula wherein R¹ is hydrogen, a C₁ to C₄ alkyl group or a C₁-C₄ alkoxy group, R² for a C₁ to C₄ alky group, and N for an integer from 0 to 3 and (3) a conventional aluminum alkyl, wherein the titanium halide (1) and the oxygen compound (2) have been ground with each other before their use. Character for the inventive method is that a catalyst system is used, which contains as a further component (4) a phenolic substance of the formula, wherein R³ is a C₁ to C₆ alkyl group, R⁴ for hydrogen or a C₁ to C₆ -alkyl group, R⁵ for hydrogen or a no more than 30 coales fatomas and - optionally - up to a total of 6 ether groups and / or ester groups saturated hydrocarbon radical, R⁶ for a C₂ to C₂₄ alkyl group, and O for an integer of 1 until 6.
