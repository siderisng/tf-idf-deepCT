<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the preparation of aqueous solutions of olefinically unsaturated monomers designed to be subjected to ultraviolet irradiation in the thin layer state.This process is characterized in that an aqueous solution of said monomers is introduced at the top of a column, a photoinitiator is introduced into the same column, an inert gas is introduced at the bottom of this column, only in the column.The gases circulate ascending and the liquids downwardly, that the inert gas exits at the top of the column and the ready mother solution for UV irradiation is drawn at the bottom of the column.
