<ABSTRACT>
the invention relates to a solar energy panel comprising a glazing, an absorbent layer and a heat-exchanging fluid circulating in the channels.  it is characterised in that in a main element between the glazing (1) and a non-deteriorating absorbent layer (2) in contact with the heat-exchanging fluid there are arranged channels (3) and in that appropriate end elements (6) are fitted to the ends of the main element where the channels (3) open.  can be used for domestic applications.  <image>
