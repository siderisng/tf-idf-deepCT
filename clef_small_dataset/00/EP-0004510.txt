<ABSTRACT>
Physiological measuring head, intended to be put in place on / or in body parts, especially on the cranial skin of the child not yet born.This measuring head is destined in particular to obtain electrocardiographic signals from the child, and the measurement of the tissue pH of the child.The invention facilitates the introduction and fixing of the head, ensures the attachment, and allows safe measurements.The body of the measuring head actively presents at the time of placement one or more electrodes (1a) sensitive to electrical activity and at least one electrode (6) sensitive to needle-shaped pH, as well asa reference electrode (12).Preferably, the electrodes (1a) and (6) are retractable and capable of being controlled from the outside, said electrodes being preferably connected mechanically, for simultaneous motion.The electrode (12) is fixed or disposable.
