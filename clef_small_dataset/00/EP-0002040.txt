<ABSTRACT>
A liquid photopolymer (M) with a viscosity of 500-1200 MPa S is applied to the printed circuit (GS) in the preamble casting (MV).The curtain height (H) is chosen so that the falling speed of the curtain is at the press on the printed circuit 60-160 m / min.The throughput speed of the printed circuit is chosen approximately equal as the impact speed of the curtain.The applied layer (LM) is explained in the usual manner under recess of the solder joints and developed with a substantially solving the unexposed areas.
