<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to high failure frequency devices and more particularly the heterojunction field effect transistors with majority carriers.The transistors according to the invention use the properties of the GaAs / Alxga1-XAS junctions of the same type, doped N, GaAs being weakly doped, favorable to a large mobility of the fillers in the accumulation layer: a structure according to which the source regions.and drain are partially covered by the grid region, itself covered by an insulating layer, decreases access resistors and increases transition frequencies.Application to microwave transistors for TV communications and integrated circuits.
