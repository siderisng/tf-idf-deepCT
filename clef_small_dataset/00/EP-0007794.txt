<ABSTRACT>
a device for use in determining ionic activity in liquid  solutions by the use of electrodes (14, 16) and a capillary  bridge (18) which promotes ionic migration between the elec­ trodes (14, 16).  the bridge (18) is formed of a porous material  layer into which liquid solutions are absorbed, a bottom non-­ porous layer between the electrodes and the porous material,  and a top nonporous layer on the other side of the porous  material.  preferably, the three layers are apertured at each  electrode to provide wells (19, 20) for receiving drops of liquid  solutions, and the top layer is hydrophobic to retain the solu­ tion drops in the region of the apertures.
