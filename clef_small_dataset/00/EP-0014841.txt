<ABSTRACT>
foldable carton for the packaging of glass articles, such as, for example, drinking glasses, etc. , which is formed from a folded shape made of cardboard or the like.  the underlying object of the invention is to provide a foldable carton of the type mentioned above which is easy to manufacture and to handle and, furthermore, not only has a satisfactory appearance but also allows a good display of the packaged glass articles.  this is achieved in that the shape has a panel (1) forming the base or the covering of the foldable carton, which panel has an essentially triangular shape and from whose sides (2) arms (3, 4, 5) project, at least one (4) of which is provided at its end facing away from the panel (1) with a tab (9) or the like, forming the base or the covering of the foldable carton, or with several tabs (6, 8) or the like, complementing each other to form the base or the covering.  <image>
</ABSTRACT>
