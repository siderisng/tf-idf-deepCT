<ABSTRACT>
1.  an awning provided with a casing (27) comprising a plurality of posts (32) which are spaced laterally apart from one another and to which a roof (28) covering the retracted awning (26) at the top and/or a bottom (29) covering the awning (26) at the bottom can be clamped, characterized in that the posts (32) have at their bottom end (41) a claw (34) by which they are pushed over a carrier (17) which at the same time carries the awning shaft (20) and the drive for the awning (26), and that the posts (32) are locked on the carrier (17) by clasps (38) which engage at least partly around the carrier (17) and engage in the claws (34) of the posts (32).
