<ABSTRACT>
The apparatus for transporting concrete retainers comprises a wire rope sole (6) to be embedded (6) which can be detected by a releasable member (1) in a recess (19) of the precast concrete part.This member (1) has a lower hook part (2) which is substantially spherical and an upper plate-shaped part with a breakdown for the hoist.The member (1) can be produced in high-quality cast steel because of its special design and has a good mobility in the recess (19) for slanted trains.
