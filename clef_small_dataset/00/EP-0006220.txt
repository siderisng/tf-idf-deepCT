<ABSTRACT>
3-acyl-2-alkyl and 3-acyl-2-aryl-1,3,2-benzoxazaphos pholanes are prepared by reacting dichloro phosphans, 2-N acylaminophenolenes and chlorine water-binding organic nitrogen bases in molar ratio is about 1: 1: ≧ 2 in an inert solvent without isolating an intermediate stage in the entry method.The products, which are large part of new compounds, are intermediates for the production of alkyl and aryl-1-oxophospholanes, which in turn are catalysts for the production of polyurethane hard foams.
