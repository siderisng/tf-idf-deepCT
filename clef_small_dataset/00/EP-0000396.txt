<ABSTRACT>
The present invention is the preparation of aromatic polycarbonates according to the known two-phase boundary area method from diphenols, preferably of halogen-free diphenols, characterized in that as chain burners are aromatic monosulfonic acid chlorides in amounts of 2 to 20 mol%, preferably in amounts of 2.5 to 10 moles% based on moles of the diphenols used.
