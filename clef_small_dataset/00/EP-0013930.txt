<ABSTRACT>
An immunogen consisting essentially of thymosin α₁ will be described.This immunogen can produce the formation of thymosin α₁ specifically antibodies in host animals.With these antibodies with labeled thymosin α₁ and a stand-curve, the thymosin behavior of a sample can be determined.
