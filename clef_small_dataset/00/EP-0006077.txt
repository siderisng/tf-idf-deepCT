<ABSTRACT>
In the flat, removable polygonal glass watch box, the glass extends it to the edge of the box, except on both sides, where it is taken between projecting parts 3 of the box.The glass is retained between the parts 3 by cylindrical pins 6 engaged in corresponding passages 7, 8 formed by half in the box and by half in the glass.The glass and pins 6 are retained in place transversely by the pressure that the lining 10, 11 exert on the glass.Rough walls of 7.8 passages and tender pins that the box and glass increase the blocking effect of the lining 10, 11. A mask under the edge of the glass, extending to the right of the inner faceFrom the rehautor 14 hides the filling 11. The sides of the glass attached to the box can also be curved.
