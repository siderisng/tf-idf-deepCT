<ABSTRACT>
in a method of and apparatus for injecting gas into a  liquid, in particular the reactor core coolant water for a pressurised  water nuclear power reactor system, only the gas  quantity needed to achieve a predetermined gas concentration  in the liquid is supplied, via a gas dosage pump (1), to  nozzles (5, 5a) within a bubble chamber (6).  the liquid also is  supplied to the bubble column (6), via an inlet (20).  a mixture  of the gas and water flows through static mixers (7) in the  bubble column (6) to produce the desired gas concentration  in the liquid, mixing taking place before the gas and liquid  leave the bubble column.  
</ABSTRACT>
