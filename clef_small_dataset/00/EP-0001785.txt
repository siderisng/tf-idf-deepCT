<ABSTRACT>
1.  classified electrostatographic toner material characterized in that it contains particles having the following size distribution : a) less than 2% by weight of the particles being greater than 16 microns ; b) from 9 to 15% by weight of the particles being smaller than 5 microns ; c) the remaining particles being between 5 and 16 microns in size ; and that it has a particle median size by weight between 8. 5 and 9. 5 microns.
