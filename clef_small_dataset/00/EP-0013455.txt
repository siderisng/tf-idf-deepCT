<ABSTRACT>
swivel comprising a plurality of annual chambers (4)  formed by identical inner and outer wall portions (3 and 2)  resp), said wall portions (3,2) being rotatably supported with  respect to each other by means of inner and outer bearing  race ways (7 and 10 resp. ) which are connected to an end face  of the corresponding inner and outer wall portions (3,2).  the  outer wall portions (2) may be interconnected by means of  bolts (15) and the inner wall portions (3) by means of cams (19)  engaging into recesses (17,18) of a ring (16) located at the  level of the bearings (6) present between adjacent chambers  (4).
