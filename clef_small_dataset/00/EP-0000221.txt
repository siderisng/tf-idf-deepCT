<ABSTRACT>
Statistical copolymers of maleic anhydride and dicyclopentadiene or reaction products of these copolymers are used as anionic paper surface surgical means.The preparation of the copolymers takes place in the usual manner in suspension or solvent polymerization at elevated temperatures in the presence of radical initiators.In order to make the copolymers water-soluble, they transfer them into their salts with bases, but not all carboxyl groups need to be neutralized.The copolymers can also be used in the form of half-amides.
