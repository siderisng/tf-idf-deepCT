<ABSTRACT>
Process for the preparation of hydroxylammonium salts by catalytic reduction of nitrogen monoxide with hydrogen in diluted aqueous mineral acids in the presence of suspended platinum catalysts at elevated temperature, wherein the reaction is carried out in reaction vessels whose walls of known, copper-free, austenitic, molybdenum-containing chromium-nickel steel steelsconsist of a content of 16 to 28 wt.% chrome, 20 to 50 wt.% nickel, 1 to 4 wt.% molybdenum, at most 0.1 wt.% are carbon and additionally at least 5 times the amount of titanium, based on the amount of carbon, but not more than 1.0 wt.%, or at least 8 times the amount of niobium or diet, based on the amount of carbon, but not more than 1.5 wt.%, contain.
