<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for crosslinking radically crosslinkable polymers and for stabilizing oxidative and / or thermal degradation.This method should be designed such that a rapid and economical crosslinking of radically crosslinkable polymer in the presence of oxidation inhibitors without inhibitor-related impairment of the crosslinking process is achieved.The invention provides that the crosslinking in the presence of oxidation inhibitors whose inhibition activating OH and / or NH functions are substituted at least partially by Silyl and / or Silylene protection groups, wherein the inhibitor molecule contains at least one Si-alkenyl group.The method according to the invention is suitable for use in radical and in radical polymers.
</ABSTRACT>
