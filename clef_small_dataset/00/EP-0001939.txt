<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to the closure of glass pi meters by thermosales of composite capsules comprising a thermoplastic layer.The method consists in applying on the opener 4 in position on the container 1 a flexible membrane extended by its periphery on a support frame 12 integral with a body 6 articulatedly mounted on the end 7 of a rod of cylinder.8, to submit the face of the opposite membrane 13 to a controlled heat stream created by heating means 35 on the body 6, then clearing the membrane of the sealed opercule on the container.According to a particular embodiment, the membrane 13 is metalli that and defines in the rest position with a contact face 17 of the body 6 a chamber 16 in which a fluid under pressure is brought.Application including the packaging of dairy products.
