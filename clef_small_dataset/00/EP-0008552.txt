<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for recovering and concentrating uranium (vi) contained in an organic phase.The organic phase is continuously treated in a contact zone, by means of a complexing aqueous solution of uranium (IV) containing an oxiduously reduced reducing agent, the aqueous solution from entirely or part of theCathodic compartment of an electrolytic separator cell, on the current current voltage.The outward aqueous phase of the contact zone feeds entirely or partly the anodic compartment of the electrolytic cell.Application to the recovery and concentration of uranium contained in a wet phosphoric acid.
