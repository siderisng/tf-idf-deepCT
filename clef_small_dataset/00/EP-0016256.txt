<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a circuit arrangement for correcting step distortions in a transmission of data using frequency mOs to dulated data signals (D1).The circuit arrangement contains a demodulator (DM), which compares the time periods between the edges of the frequency mo-dulated data signals (D1) with a measuring time generated in a timing (Z) and generates after integration demodulated data signals (D2).The demodulated data signals (D2) are compared in a comparator (VG) with reference signals (B) and the signals at the output of the comparator (VG) are integrated in an integrator (JG).The integrator (JG) outputs control signals (R) to the timer (Z) with which the measuring time is changed so that the step distortion of the demodulated data signals (D2) is counteracted.
