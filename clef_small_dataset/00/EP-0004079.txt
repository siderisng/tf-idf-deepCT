<ABSTRACT>
The carriers contain conventional active components, namely precious metals of the 8th subgroup or their compounds, as well as activators of alkali compounds, alkaline earth connections or cadmium compounds.The carriers also consist of conventional materials, such as silica.New is the shape of the carrier particles: There are strand sections with a star-shaped cross section (Figure 1-7) or ribbed strands (Figure 8).Relative to the bulk weight and thus relative to the precious metal amount used, a much higher space-time power is achieved than with carrier particles of conventional shape.
