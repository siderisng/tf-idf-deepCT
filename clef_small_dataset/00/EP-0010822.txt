<ABSTRACT>
an alkaline dishwasher detergent composition provid­ ing optimum cleaning, spotting and filming characteristics  comprises defined amounts of an alkali metal tripolyphos­ phate, sodium or potassium carbonate to provide reserve  alkalinity, sodium or potassium silicate, a dichlorocyanurate  source of chlorine bleach and a relatively high level (2% to  10%) of a nonionic surfactant which is the condensation  product of one mole c₁₆-c₂₀ fatty alcohol and from 10 to  15 moles of ethylene oxide.  the composition has a specific  critical ph of from 9. 8 to 10. 7 and is substantially free of  highly alkaline materials and organic builders.
