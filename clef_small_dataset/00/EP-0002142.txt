<ABSTRACT>
Complexes de Borans of formula: R₄R₃HB: R₁SASHR₂: BHR₃R₄ where: A represents a straight or branched hydrocarbon radical containing from 1 to 9 carbon atoms, R₁ and R₂ represent: - each separately a lower alkyl radical in right chain or Branched, comprising up to 5 carbon atoms in the longest chain, or a benzyl radical optionally substituted with one or more lower alkyl radicals comprising up to 5 carbon atoms or by one or more halogen atoms, - either together a hydrocarbon radical defined as a above then constituting with the latter and the two atoms of sulfur, a heterocycle, R₃ and R₄ each represent a hydrogen atom, a lower alkyl radical comprising up to 6 carbon atoms or a Mono or bicycloalcoyl group. The invention also relates to the preparation of these bodies as well as their use in hydroboration and reduction reactions.
