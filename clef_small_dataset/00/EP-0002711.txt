<ABSTRACT>
if a filamentous structure (2) is first wound, with the windings in firm contact with one another, around a mandrel (1), around which a wound grp container (3) is built up, this core can easily be removed if the filamentous structure is withdrawn first.  synthetic filaments are particularly advantageous; they are drawn on withdrawal and reduced in cross section.  use of profiled filamentous structures (2) makes it possible to structure the internal surface of a container.  <image>
