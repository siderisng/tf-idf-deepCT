<ABSTRACT>
A centrifugal wheel for skidding of skittling material consists of at least one wheel disk (1, 2), a plurality of uniformly distributed, in radial grooves (8) of the latter screwdriving blades (3), which have side strips (7) on their longitudinal sides, and from stops (21, 23, 24) which limit the movement of the centrifugal blades (3) in the radial direction to the outside. Here are the sidebars (7) and or or the grooves (8) wedge-shaped at least in a plane to the blade longitudinal axis (25), wherein preferably side strips (7) and grooves (8) symmetrically to the longitudinal axis (25) in two mutually perpendicular Layers to the blade longitudinal axis (25) in the insertion direction downward downward. The wedge-shaped design of groove (8) and sidebar (7) facilitate the disassembly of the drainage blade (3) worn during operation, wherein by a, when pushing away the spinner blade (3) from the groove (8), immediately enlargement of the blasting means Column (26) between sidebar (7) and groove (8) is made possible.
