<ABSTRACT>
This food processing appliance has a bayonet-fixed lid, a motor control switch in a basement of the base, an actuating push button on the cover and three positions: withdrawal position for which itis off the housing;arming position for which it penetrates it partially;Extension position for which it penetrates it deeper and causes the start.According to the invention, when the pusher (40) is in the arming position, an elastic lock (52) prevents it from returning from it to its withdrawal position.The invention applies, for example, to choppers.
