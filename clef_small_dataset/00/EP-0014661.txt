<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method and a device for improving a nuclear propulsion vessel.According to the invention, a vessel is carried out in two prefabricated parts 2, 3 and a housing 5 is provided in part 2, in which, before assembly of the parts 2 and 3, a block 6 forming a nuclear reactor is introduced by horizontal translation.The invention applies to the construction of various nuclear nuclear ships, such as for example the icebreakers.
