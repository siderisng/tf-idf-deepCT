<ABSTRACT>
contact material which can be used in electrical devices which permit the opening or closing of a circuit, and a method for making this product.  this material is characterised by the combining, with silver, of mixed crystals of tungsten carbide and of titanium carbide in the weight proportion of 20 to 40% relative to the total mass of the material.  the method consists in mixing the products in the pulverous state, in compressing them and in sintering them at a temperature greater than 1050 deg c.  this material finds application in circuit makers and breakers equipping domestic premises, domestic appliances and industrial equipment.
