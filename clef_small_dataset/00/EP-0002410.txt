<ABSTRACT>
The vials 1 and 2 are clipped on the base 3 by means of two tabs 5 of the latter between which are inserted said flasks, a locking bead 10 being provided on the inner face of each tongue and cooperating with a recess provided on the adjacent face of each bottle; Two guide ribs are provided on said internal face of each tongue to cooperate each with a corresponding section groove provided on the adjacent face of a corresponding bottle. The invention applies in particular to perfume packaging. Liquid conditioning device such as perfume. The present invention relates to a liquid conditioning device, of the type comprising two stolaled flasks, mounted on a common base. In particular, the invention is to provide a device for easy handling of vials. This object is reached, according to the invention, because the vials are clipped on the base by means of two tabs of the latter between which each of which are inserted from said vials, each vials being in contact with each of said tongues, a protrusion. blocking on the inner face of each tongue for cooperating with a recess provided on the adjacent face of each vial and that two guide elements are provided on said internal face of each tab to cooperate each with a recess provided on the adjacent side. of a corresponding bottle, these guide elements and the corresponding recesses being specially arranged and shaped to guide the vials with respect to the base according to a path leading to a final position of said vials, position for which these flasks are both clipped. on the base by the cooperation of the locking projections with the corresponding recesses, and are based on The base. Thanks to the simultaneous presence of these locking projections and these guidance elements each cooperating with a respective vessel of the vials, the assembly of the latter on the base is facilitated and their locking in the clipped position is reinforced.
