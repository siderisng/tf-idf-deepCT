<ABSTRACT>
A spin gear for centrifugal blasting machines consists of two rotating wheel disks (3, 4) and a plurality of equally distributed with side strips (15), in radial grooves (16) of the wheel disks (3, 4, a plurality of wheel discs (3, 4) and a plurality of wheel disks (3, 4) ) Inside insertable spinning blades (6), which are held in the radial direction after outside by fixed stops (30). The length of the sidebars (15) of the centrifugal blades (6) is smaller than the diameter of the central free space (7) of the centrifugal wheel formed by the inner edges (22) of the centrifugal blades (6). In addition, the width (19) of the portion of the centrifugal blades (6) beyond the side strips (15) is smaller than the free distance (20) between the two wheel disks (3, 4). Through this dimensional dependence, each centrifugal blade (6) can be inserted from the outer between the wheel disks (3, 4) to the centrifugal radian axis, until the sidebars (15) are in the central free space (7) and then from the inside in the Grooves (16) of the wheel disks (3, 4) to a fixed stop (30) are pushed back, whereby arbitrarily long spinning blades (6) are usable.
