<ABSTRACT>
Fixing and mounting system on a railway vehicle bogie with dynamic braking winding only by eddy currents.It comprises a rigid coil in an oblong electrical precinctor material (4) surrounding the lower tie of the wheel (1) and whose ends are loked in steel supports (5a, 5b) in the shape of Gou Lotteclosed by a thin sheet (8) and equipped with fastening ears (6a, 6b).The plane straight parts of the coil extremities are fixed in the supports between seals (10, 11), by injection of a composition capable of giving, after crosslinking, an Ela stomer product.The invention can be used for fixing bobi swimming subjected to very important heating and cooling.
