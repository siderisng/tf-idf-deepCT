<ABSTRACT>
the invention relates to an opening arrangement for  packaging containers of thin plastic film, whereby a part of  the package adjacent one side thereof is folded over and  fixed in the folded position, and that a thin cutting thread  of low extensibility is arranged in the fold formed, in such  a way that the cutting thread is accessible from the outside  of the packing container.
