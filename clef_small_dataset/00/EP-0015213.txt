<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a self-supporting roof for buildings, composed of modular elements each comprising a self-supporting outer wall (2), a layer of thermal insulation, an inner wall (3) carried by the outer wall and of the same form forSupport the insulation layer, and stiffening hoops (27) attached to the outer wall according to its slope.The inner wall (3) is rigidly fixed to the so-called arches (27), so as to form with the outer wall (2) a unitary structure vis-à-vis the forces.
