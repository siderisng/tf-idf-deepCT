the invention involves a novel melamine group-containing  (meth)acrylate, e. g.  hexakis(2-acryloyloxypropyl)-melamine,  hexakis(2-acryloyloxyethyl) melamine, etc. , and its polymer.   said (meth)acrylate has excellent reactivity and storage stability,  and said polymer has excellent dimensional stability, durabilities  (especially water resistance and moisture resistance) and surÂ­ face hardness, and is useful as a molded product and a coating  layer of a molded resin article.  for example, a polyester film  coated with said polymer has excellent adhesivity, flexibility and  dimensional stability as well as high abrasion resistance, and  can be used to a wide variety of uses as an abrasion resistant  film.
