<ABSTRACT>
Liquid cooled holder for the tip of an elec trodes of an arc furnace with a cylindrical clamping part (2) fastened at the electrode train, a metallic cooling system (3) which is mounted on this electrode stream, the on-free end of a threaded part (8) for screwing on the electrode tipcarries, further with a pipe shield surrounding the cooling system with a distance surrounding tubular shield.The hide roller consists of a metal tube (11), preferably of a steel tube, which is electrically insulated from the current-carrying cooling system and is sufficiently coolable via a refractory substance (17) between the cooling system and the metal tube.
