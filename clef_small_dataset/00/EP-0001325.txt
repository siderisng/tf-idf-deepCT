<ABSTRACT>
2-butene-1,4-diol is obtained by reacted butadiene with  water and oxygen in the presence of a copper, nickel, cobalt,  chromium, manganese, molybdenum or iron catalyst and  preferably in the presence of carbon dioxide or sulphur dioxide.   lithium hydroxide may be present and an organic sulphur  compound can be added.
