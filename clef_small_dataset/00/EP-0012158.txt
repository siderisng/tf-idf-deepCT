<ABSTRACT>
A method for the protection of crops from aggressive agrochemical chemicals is described, whereby Oxim derivatives of the formula (with the definition given herein for the substituents AR, X and Q) used as antidotes.It can be chosen to treat the acreage of crops or culture plants themselves or parts of the plant (seeds, tubers, stem pieces, etc.) in the sense of a stain with these oxim derivatives.
