<ABSTRACT>
strong, low basis weight, spontaneously dispersible  sheets of modified cellulosic fibers.  the sheets are prepared  from modified cellulosic fibers such as cross-linked carboxymethyl  cellulose or essentially acidic sodium carboxymethyl  cellulose.  mixtures of modified fibers with unmodified fibers  are also disclosed.  the process of preparing the sheets comprises  the steps of airlaying the fibers to form an airfelt,  increasing the moisture content of the airfelt, and compacting  the moisturized airfelt.
