<TITLE>
device for moving the position of an objective in two directions.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

<ABSTRACT>
With the device, the position of an objective (O) to the image plane is adjusted in two directions (X, Y).For this purpose, the lens (O) is attached to one end of a rod (S).The rod (S) is slidably and rotatably mounted in a tube (R) attached to a housing (PL).At the other end of the rod, a lever (H) is rotatably arranged, the other end is applied to a support (FR).The lever (H) can be influenced by a setting mechanism (EM) so that the rod (S) is eliminated in the axial direction and in the direction of rotation.
</ABSTRACT>
