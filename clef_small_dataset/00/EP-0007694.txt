<ABSTRACT>
a process for preparing 3,4,3′,4′-benzophenonetra­ carboxylic dianhydride which comprises condensing an  impure ortho-xylene mixture with acetaldehyde in contact  with an acid catalyst to obtain an impure 1,1-bis(3,4-­ dimethylphenyl) ethane mixture, oxidizing the 1,1- bis(3,4-­ dimethylphenyl) ethane mixture with nitric acid to obtain a  mixture of carboxylic acids, from which by crystallization and  dehydration substantially pure 3, 4, 3′, 4′-­ benzophenonetetracarboxylc dianhydride is recovered.
