<ABSTRACT>
disclosed is a portable dialysis apparatus of the type  comprising a first blood circuit and a second circuit for the  ultrafiltrate and the dialysis liquid.  the exchange between  the blood and the dialysis liquid is taking place through a  semipermeable membrane.  both the blood circuit and the  second circuit include a drawing and circulation pump of  the type in which a flexible tube is periodically throttled by  a member of the pump head.  the pump of the dialysis liquid  is having the suction side in communication with the outlet  of the dialyzer either through a supply tank of dialysis liquid  or directly through a shunt line.  its delivery side is in communication  either with the inlet of the dialyzer or with an  ultrafiltrate collecting bag.  a pressure measuring device is  inserted upstream of the pump of the dialysis liquid in the  shunt line.  
</ABSTRACT>
