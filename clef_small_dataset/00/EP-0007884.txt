<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the foundry of light alloys.It relates to a method and a device for treating a metal or liquid alloy by means of liquid and solid flows.It consists of passing the metal or alloy to be treated through a column with an outlet siphon garnished with solid granules and liquid flows, at least one of the granules being constituted by a solid flow whose melting point.is greater than that of the liquid flow and the temperature of the metal to be treated.The solid granule can be introduced into a removable cartridge, easily interchangeable.Application to the thrust purification of aluminum alloys by eliminating so-called ultimate inclusions.
