<ABSTRACT>
Aqueous dispersions suitable as coating agents contain paint resins, e.g.b.Air-drying alkyd resins and also aminoplastic resins and nonionic surfactants, which of addition products of the ethylene oxide and allyl glycidyl ether to fatty alcohols, fatty acids, fatty amines, fatty acid amides, or alkylphenols having 8 to 26 carbon atoms, wherein 1 mol of the aforementioned compounds a) 2 to 10 allyl glycidyl ether units and b)5 to 25 ethylene oxide units are eliminated, prepared in an amount of from 0.5 to 10, in particular 1 to 8% by weight, based on the resin content.The alkyd resins are intended to have an acid number between 5 to 40 and neutralized with volatile amines.The use of low solvent quantities may be useful.The resulting films show improved water resistance to known water-dilutable paints.
