<ABSTRACT>
1.  a binder for coating compositions, drying on physically from an organic solution or to be applied from a hot melt, for coating metals, plastics and fiber-containing and mineral materials, based on vinylaromatic copolymers containing carboxyl groups, characterized in that the binder is a copolymer composed of a) 60 to 95% by weight of a vinylaromatic, b) 2 to 20% by weight of at least one copolymerizable alpha,beta-olefinically unsaturated carboxylic acid or a half-ester of an alpha,beta-olefinically unsaturated dicarboxylic acid with a monoalkanol of 1 to 18 carbon atoms, and c) 0 to 35% by weight of at least one ester of acrylic acid and/or methacrylic acid with a monoalkanol of from 1 to 12 carbon atoms, the total of the percentages given under a) to c) being 100. 
</ABSTRACT>
