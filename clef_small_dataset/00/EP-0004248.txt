<ABSTRACT>
a method and a device making it possible to form batches of fragile, flat objects in stacks of a predetermined length, which objects are arranged edgeways in joined rows (1).  these stacks of objects are intended to be packaged in a subsequent operation.  the device consists essentially of a set of stops (5,9) and of a transverse pusher (6), which is capable of pushing, over a lateral area, the objects selected to form the desired stack without breaking them.  the invention will mainly be employed in the packing of biscuits and particularly fragile and coarse objects whose dimensions are poorly defined.  <image>
