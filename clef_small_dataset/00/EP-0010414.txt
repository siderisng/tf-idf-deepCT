<ABSTRACT>
a process for separating phosphorus trichloride from a  phosphorus trichloride-containing gas stream characterised  in that:   (a) the gas stream is contacted with a condensing  amount of liquid phosphorus trichloride to condense a  portion of the gaseous phosphorus trichloride to liquid  phosphorus trichloride; and   (b) the liquid phosphorus trichloride is separated from  the gas stream and an apparatus for the realization of this  process is disclosed.  the process is particularly useful in preventing or reducÂ­ ing the discharge of gaseous phosphorus trichloride to the  atmosphere due to the accidental discharge of a high volume  gas stream from a phosphorus trichloride reaction vessel.
