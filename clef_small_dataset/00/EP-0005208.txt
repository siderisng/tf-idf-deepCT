<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an electrical circuit with an insulating gas-filled metal encapsulation. The metal encapsulation is supported by an insulating gas-free base (16) and an insulating gas-free attachment (17). Screens (20) surrounded within the base (16) arranged cable connections (9). The umbrellas (20) are attached to a passage plate (22) which is supported by the attachment (17). The passage plate (22) carries a container (24), which encloses the vacuum switch tube (4) as part of the metal encapsulation. This tube (4) also rests on the implementation plate (22). The free end of the vacuum switch tube (4) is connected via a flexible conduit (10) with a vertical bolt (26) which projects through a bulkhead (27) and grimed into a container (28), the part which is metal encapsulation. The container (28) encloses a busbar (14), which carries a counter contact piece for a movable contact piece (12) of a disconnector (11). The separation switch (11) is supported by the bolt (26). At the vacuum switch tube (4) and the disconnector (11), drive elements engage, which are operable from elements outside the encapsulation.
