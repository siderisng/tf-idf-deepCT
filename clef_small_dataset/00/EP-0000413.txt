<ABSTRACT>
Drive device for demanding transportation or switching a ribbon and to turn the printhead in printing devices. On the one hand, to perform the ribbon transport and the color band switching and on the other hand the turning of the printhead from the pressure resist bearing of a printing device with only one motor (1), a directional body (65) is provided between the drive wheels for the coil carrier and the drive motor. The pulling cable (46) for turning the printhead is attached to a shoe lever (58) which rolls on an eccentric cam (40) of a half tour coupling, which via a further directional lock (44) with the common, driven by the electric motor (11) driven axis in active connection is. Both directional lock (40, 65) locks into ent opposite directions. The common drive motor (11) is reversible and is electrically controlled by the half tour coupling. The switching of the trans port direction of the ribbon (17) is carried out by means of others connected shift levers (19, 20), which depending on the trans port direction against one of the two arranged on the switching shaft (8) gears (7, 9) by the ribbon (17 ) be pivoted. In this case, a roll lever (24) is moved against a spring force by the switching lever (19, 20). In the range of activity of the roller lever (24), the shift lever (19, 20) has a roof-shaped from the rolling layer.
