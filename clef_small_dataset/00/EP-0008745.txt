<ABSTRACT>
For soot-free generation of a carbon monoxide, water fabric and gaseous hydrocarbons containing fuel gas mixtures with partial oxidation of liquid hydrocarbons with air at air numbers below 0.3 and temperatures between 600 and 800 ° C is used according to the invention a metal catalyst used from an aluminum retraining15 to 35 wt.-% silicon, which processes to form bodies and at least 10 hours at temperatures between 600 and 800 ° C of an oxidizing the atmosphere and gaseous or gasolated carbon hydrogen (advantageously a gas stream of air and koh lina hydrocons with a C: O₂ ratio between2 and 8) was exposed.Of the alloy originally available as an alertektikum with distributed Si primary phases, 5 to 30% of the aluminum is transferred to α-Al₂O₃ and 1 to 10% of the silicon in α-SiC during the heat treatment.
