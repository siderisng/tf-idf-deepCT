<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for removing saturated liquid residual hydrocarbons made of polyolefins obtained in the catalytic α-olefin polymerization by means of steam and water, and is characterized in that a) the hydrocarbon-containing polyolefin enters aqueous, emulsifieralitge solution, and the mixture converts into a homogeneous dispersion by stirring; that b) introduces the dispersion into the upper part of a column extracted with at least 5 sieve bottoms and treated hot water vapor in countercurrent with about 100 to 120 ° C, the free cross section of the individual sieve bottoms in the column from the bottom to the top bottom of About 3% to about 90% of the bottom surface increases continuously and increases at approximately the distance of the distance of the individual soils from the bottom upwards, and that C) from the bottom of the column tesets the hydrocarbon-free aqueous polyolefind dispersion and the polyolefin separates from the water.
