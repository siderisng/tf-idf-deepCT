said material contains a quinone-type compound  which corresponds to one of the formulae (a) or (b) and  which is capable in reduced state and under alkaline conditions  of releasing a photographically useful group e. g.  a  dye:    wherein:   each of (nuox)¹ and (nuox)² represents an oxidized nucleophilic  group,   z represents a bivalent atomic group which is electronegative,   q together with the z group represents a releasable  photographically useful group,   each of r¹, r² and r³ is hydrogen, halogen, alkyl, alkoxy,  or an acylamino group of r¹ and r² in adjacent positions  on the ring form and ring fused with the remainder  of the molecule, or r² and r³ together are fused with the remainder of the molecule.    each of r⁴ and r⁵ represents hydrogen or a hydrocarbon  group.  at least one of the substituents r¹, r², r³, r⁴, or r⁵ is  a ballasting group x.
