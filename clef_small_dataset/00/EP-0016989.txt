<ABSTRACT>
Sturdy, flowable dispersions of rubbers in the form of discrete particles of a mean diameter of 100 to 3000 nm in organic liquids which contain: A - as a rubber 1 to 20 wt.%, based on velvet dispersion, a crosslinked diene rubber or a crosslinked, rubbery acrylic ester polymer;B - 0 to 15 wt.%, based on total dispersion, resulting in the form of a water-in-oil emulsion;C - as a continuous organic phase 99 to 45 wt.%, based on total dispersion, a mixture of 95 to 60 wt.% of an aliphatic C₁-C₂₀ or aromatic C₆-C₁₂ cohy hydrogen or a halogen derivative thereof or 5 to 40 wt.% of a C₁-C₁₂ alkanol, a ketone having up to 8 C atoms, a formamide or a nitrile having up to 6 C atoms, and a method for their preparation.
</ABSTRACT>
