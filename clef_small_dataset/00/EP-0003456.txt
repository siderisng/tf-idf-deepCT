<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to novel adminoidal derivatives, represented by the general formula: wherein M and N, identical or different, represent an integer from 0 to 4;R₁ R₂ R₄ R₅ and R₆, identical or different, represent a hydrogen atom or a hydroxy, alkoxy, or acyloxy group;R₃ represents a lower alkyl, aldehyde, haloalkyl, hydroxyalkyl, acyloxyalkyl, or ethylene-dioxyalkyl;R₇ represents a hydrogen atom, or an alkyl group, R₈ represents a hydrogen atom, an alkyl, acyl, alkyloxycarbonyl group, or aralcoxycarbonyl: R₉ represents a hydrogen atom or an alkyl group;R₈ and R₉ may form a heterocycle in conjunction with the nitrogen atom;R₁₀ represents a hydroxy, alkoxy, aralcooxy group, or an amino residue of an amino acid or oligo peptide.Application to the manufacture of drugs, especially for the treatment of cardiac conditions.
