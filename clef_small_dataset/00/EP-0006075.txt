<ABSTRACT>
A glasses hinge comprises two parts (1 and 2) of which one (1) has a portion (6) with a cam profile (7), the other part (2) having a housing (11) cylindrical blinds inwhich are arranged at least one spring (15) and a ball (14), the spring (15) soliciting said ball (14) against the cam profile (7).In order to avoid excessive wear of the cam profile (7) and to facilitate disassembly of this hinge, the latter comprises an intermediate piece (16, 23) disposed in the housing (11) between the ball (14) and theSpring (15), this intermediate piece giving rise to friction with the ball (14) below the friction of this ball on the cam profile (7).In addition, the housing (11) is provided with means of restraint (20) of its contents, these retaining means decreasing its free section near its opening.
