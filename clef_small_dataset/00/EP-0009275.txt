<ABSTRACT>
Forming compositions consisting of coal liquefaction protocol, fillers and thermoplastic mixtures of polyolefens as well as their polar, aromatic or cyclic monomers containing mixing or copolymeric are tolerated levels of coal liquefaction products up to 70 wt. -% In the preservation of mold, press, casting or extrudability on plastic processing machines, when the coal liquefaction product is a residue resulting in the ex-tractive carbohydrogenation with a softening point of 70-200 ° C, preferably 120-200 ° C according to DIN 1995 . The carbohydrogenation residue can be 0-33 wt. -% solid, non-melting substances contain. The polyolefins preferably have a melt index MFI 190 / 2.5 from 0.1 to 1.7 / 10 min. The monomer content in the copolymers is preferably between 10 and 80 wt. -%, the softening point between 70 and 190 ° C according to DIN 1995. increases the proportion of the polar, aromatic and / or cyclic monomers, the filler content may be up to 40 wt. -% increase. From these molding compounds, after mixing and kneading at 140 to 190 ° C in extruders or injection molding, shine molded bodies, including for the construction and in the stable sector, such as pipes, profiles, containers or abdich machines and protective purposes such as films are produced.
