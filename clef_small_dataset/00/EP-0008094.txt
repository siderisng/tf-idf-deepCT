<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a concrete or mortar additive compositions of a cellulose ether and a nonionic nen, weakly foaming surfactant, wherein the surfactant has a larger proportion of hydrophobic than hydrophilic groups.The preferred surfactants preferably include modified block polymers of propylene oxide and ethylene oxide or Al Kylarylpolyethylene glycol ethers having a small amount of ethylene oxide molecules.Conveniently, the addition means with a proportion of about 0.005 to 0.5 wt.% of cellulose ether, based on the weight of dry proportions of a mortar or concrete, and from about 0.5 to 20 wt.-% of surfactant, based on the weight of the CEL lulose ether.The addition of the present invention means can serve as a pumping mist for concrete or mortar.
