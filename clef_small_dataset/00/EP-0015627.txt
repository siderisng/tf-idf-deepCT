<ABSTRACT>
an epm or epdm type of elastomer is modified by  graft copolymerization with acrylic ester type of monomeric  material whose homopolymer has a glass transition tem­ perature of not greater than 6° c, in an amount sufficient  to provide an ester group (-coo-) content of from 25 to  40% by weight, to yield a mixed copolymerizate vulcanizable  to a heat and oil resistant elastomeric state.
