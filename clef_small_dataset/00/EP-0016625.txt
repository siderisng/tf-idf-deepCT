<ABSTRACT>
a device for arranging in order a random supply of  articles including a plurality of article orientating members  (16, 17; 24a, 24b) movable relatively to each other.  the  members are spaced so that the articles, (a; b) when  orientated, can drop between the members.  the members  may comprise intercalating elongate blades, alternate blades  being arranged to move in unison relative to intermediate  blades and means (37) may be provided for conveying the  orientated articles from between the blades to a receiving  station.
