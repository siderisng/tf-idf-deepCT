<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a diagnostic agent for detection of esterolytic and / or proteolytic enzymes in body fluids containing in addition to a conventional esterase and / or protease substrate and conventional additional auxiliary substances, one or more activators.In particular, pyridine and imidazole derivatives, certain alcohols and metal complexes are suitable as activators.The invention further relates to the preparation of these diagnostic agents as well as their use for the detection of leukocytes in body fluids, especially in urine.
