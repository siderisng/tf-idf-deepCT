<ABSTRACT>
Water repellent composition with cement-based powder, comprising by weight of dry matter: a) from 20 to 60% by weight of cement, b) from 30 to 70% of a mineral or organic filler, c) from 2 to 10%of zirconium silicate fibers, haf nium, vanadium or cesium, d) from 0.2 to 1% of cement plasticizers, e) from 1 to 3% glue, f) from 0.1 to 0,5% of a water repellent agent.This composition may also contain 0.1 to 2% of alkalinity buffer salts and can be used for the manufacture of molded coatings and products, such as elements for the capture of solar energy.
