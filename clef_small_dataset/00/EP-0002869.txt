<TITLE>
a device to apply a paper sheet to a closet seat.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

<ABSTRACT>
a device for applying a paper sheet to a water closet seat includes  a paper sheet container (2) adapted to move between a substantially  vertical position and a substantially horizontal position over the closet  seat by manually operating a tilting mechanism (4a, 5a, 4b, 5b) to which  said container (2) is pivotally secured.  a rotatable roller (17) is  journalled within the container and is driven to feed a paper sheet  through an opening in the container into position on the closet seat as  the container moves either to the horizontal or the vertical position.   further the device includes a pressing means (16, 18, 21) to press  part of the paper sheets on the paper driving roller (17).  
</ABSTRACT>
