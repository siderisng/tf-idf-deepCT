<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a machine for the preparation of unit doses of beverages contained in sealed doses comprising at least one flow channel primer to facilitate the evacuation of the dose.The machine comprises a frame (18) on which are mounted vertically two press trays (21, 22), a seat formed on the frame and on which the envelope-dose (12) is maintained between the press trays with theflow channel primer (16) facing down and protruding under the trays, two moving jaws (24, 26) moving to each other so as to tighten the channel primer, organs (28,(30) sectioning of the channel primer, the expulsion means of the empty envelope of the press trays, and the motor means (32) of any suitable type intended to control the movement of the trays in sequence,Jaws and sectioning bodies.Application to the distribution of drinks.
