<ABSTRACT>
Nuclear reactor comprising a vertical axle tank containing the core of the reactor and a coolant volume of the reactor, this tank being suspended under a horizontal closing slab, said reactor comprising at least one auxiliary exchanger.According to the invention, the auxiliary exchanger comprises a set of modules each comprising a circuit traversed by a cooling fluid, the modules of the same assembly being introduced one by one in the tank through a unique vertical cylindrical passage in theslab and closed after introduction of the modules by a protective cap, these modules being then arranged in the tank around an axial extension of said plug.Application to nuclear reactors with fast neutrons.
</ABSTRACT>
