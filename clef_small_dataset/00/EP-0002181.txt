<ABSTRACT>
The substances according to the invention correspond to the formulas Ia or Ib wherein Y represents a divalent organic radical and Z -NH₂, -NH-alkyl with 1-4 C atoms, -OH, -COOH, -COC1, -O-CO-CH = CH₂, -COO-CH = CH₂ or -OCH = CH₂ represents.These new 3- or 4-acidophthalic acid derivatives are suitable for producing polymers crosslinkable under the action of light.Compared to corresponding photosensitive polymers of the prior art, these new polymers have the advantages that they are still photosensitive and also absorb in the long-wave UV range (over 320 nm).
