<ABSTRACT>
a stripper arrangement is provided in a regenerated  catalyst standpipe for removing or displacing combustion  flue gas products with fuel gas products of hydrocarbon con­ version freed of hydrogen sulfide.  a catalyst containing the flue gas passes downward  through the first annular section (4) which is provided with  downward sloping stator blades (6), imposing a horizontal  circulating moment on the catalyst stream, thus separating  the flue gas from the catalyst by centrifugal force.  fuel gas is  introduced tangentially (14) in contact with the annular flow­ ing catalyst stream, displacing co₂ from the catalyst and  being withdrawn through the central tube (8), which is open at  its bottom end and closed at its upper end, through the hollow  baffles (6) and a second annular zone (10).
