<ABSTRACT>
a process for producing a polymer or copolymer of an al­ pha-olefin comprises continuously polymerizing or copoly­ merizing the alpha-olefin or the alpha-olefin with up to 5 mole%  of a diolefin in the presence of a catalyst composed of (a) a  solid magnesium compound obtained by reacting (i) an ad­ duct formed between a magnesium halide and an electron  donor, with (ii) an organometallic compound of a metal of  groups i to iii of mendelejeff's periodic table, (b) a liquid tran­ sition metal compound, and (c) an organoaluminum com­ pound, while feeding the compound (b) separately from the  other compounds (a) and (c) into a polymerization or copoly­ merization zone.  by means of this process it is possible to produce a po­ lymer or copolymer of narrow molecular weight distribution,  while yet avoiding the requirement of known processes of  supporting a transition metal compound on a magnesium-­ containing carrier before carrying out a polymerization or co­ polymerization reaction.  
</ABSTRACT>
