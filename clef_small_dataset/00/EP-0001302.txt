<ABSTRACT>
The metallic housing parts (1, 15) of personal demand devices (electric shaver, electric tahn brushes, etc.) are provided with a plurality of perforations (5) through which nub-like or tongue protrusions (4), which integrally with a film (3)are formed throughout and protruding to a low level of the housing outer surface (6), the film (3) at least in sections, with the inner surface of the housing parts (1, 15) is firmly glued.In the region of housing pleats or housing joints (16, 22), the film (3) is formed as a bellows (21) and prevents the penetration of moisture and dust in the housing interior.In the non-bonded, elastic area A of the nub field, a switching function can be triggered by depressing the nubs in the direction of the arrow.Suitable switches (18) or switches (10,11) are located in the housing interior.
