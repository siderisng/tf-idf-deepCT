<ABSTRACT>
As a lubricant for applying a clamping profile strip (1) to a holding flange (9), a solvent (10) is used, which evaporates without a sticking residue.At least a part (5.7,8) of the surface of the profile strip (1) applied to the retaining flange (9) is made of a material that is softened by the solvent (10).This enters an intimate and optionally adhesive connection with the retaining flange (9).The profile strip (1) can therefore be easily applied and still adheres to.
