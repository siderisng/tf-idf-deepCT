Herbicide effective aminoalkyl ester of the 2-nitro-5- (ortho-chloro-para-trifluoromethylphenoxy) -benzoe acid and their acid addition and quaternary ammonium salts are used for selective weed control in crop cultures, such as.b.Soy and rice.The aminoalkyl esters correspond to the formula wherein A is a C₂-C₄ alkylene bridge or the direct binding and an open and an annular amino group.The preparation of these compounds is done in a conventional manner.
