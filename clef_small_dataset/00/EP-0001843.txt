<ABSTRACT>
acrylic ester elastomers are prepared by polymerizing  together a major amount of an acrylic ester monomer and a  minor amount of 2,3-dibromopropyl acrylate monomer  optionally with another vinylidene monomer.  when admixed  with vulcanizing agents, the elastomers exhibit improved  scorch resistance even during extended processing, and  when vulcanized, provide improved oil resistance and low  temperature properties.
