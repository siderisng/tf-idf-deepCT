<ABSTRACT>
In a method used predominantly for LWC papers (12), an aqueous coating composition (17) containing inorganic pigments and at least one plastic dispersion is applied to a string papers with a pH less than 6.5.The pH of the coating mass itself is greater than 6.5.The particles of the plastic dispersion carry a non-catio nactive charge, preferably they are anionactive.The coating composition contains a substance which is strongly cationable when contacting the string papers and causes a solidification of the coating composition in the borderline area of stroke papers and coating composition.Due to the solidification of the coating compound, a too deep penetration or by beating the coating composition is prevented
