<ABSTRACT>
herbicidal pyridine compounds of formula (ii):    wherein z is cf₃, cf₂h, cf₂cl or hydrogen; y is hydrogen, ci,  or cf₃, provided that at least one of y and z is a  halogenomethyl group; and r is a cyano group, or a carboxy  group or a range of specified functional derivatives thereof;  and wherein more than 50% by weight of the compound is in  the d optical isomer form.  the compounds are useful as  selective herbicides for grass weeds in broad-leaved crops.
