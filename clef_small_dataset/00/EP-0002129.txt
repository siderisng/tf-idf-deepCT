<ABSTRACT>
vinyl ester urethane resins, of defined formula, prepared  by reacting (a) the reaction product of a polyoxyalkylene  bisphenol a and a polycarboxylic acid or anhydride, (b) a  polyfunctional isocyanate, and (c) a monohydroxyl-terminated  ester of acrylic acid are useful in coating compositions.   for each bisphenol a molecule there are from 9 to  30 oxyalkylene groups.
