inlet air cleaner and mist eliminator assembly for gas turbine engines.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
an inlet air cleaner and mist eliminator assembly is pro­ vided for the air intake of gas turbine engines or other air  breathing devices comprising, in combination, in flow  sequence, a first array (10) of vortex air cleaners (69) remov­ ing coarse and heavy liquid droplets and solid contaminant  particles, but not light and well dispersed liquid and solid con­ taminant particles; a second array (50) of sheets of filamen­ tary woven or nonwoven material, arranged to receive partial­ ly cleaned air from the vortex air cleaner array, and remove  and coalesce light and well dispersed liquid contaminant  droplets and particles suspended therein, and a third array  (60) of vortex air cleaners (69), arranged at the same or pref­ erably a higher density and flow-through capacity per unit of  inlet open surface area than the first array (10) to receive re­ sidual cleaned air from the second array, and removing addi­ tional light and well-dispersed coalesced liquid droplets and  solid contaminant particles.  
</ABSTRACT>
