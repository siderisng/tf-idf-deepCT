<ABSTRACT>
The key to a safety lock has two grooves on each flat side, whereby pairs of equal grooves are centrally symmetrical.In order to raise the number of combination options, the grooves (2, 3) and the tagging cams are carried out in pairs with different dimensions.In this case, both the width and the depth of the grooves can vary, whereby the width and / or depth of a groove can each be consistent or changing.
