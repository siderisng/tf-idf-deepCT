<ABSTRACT>
a tool exchanger wherein an exchange arm (12) fixed  on one end of a rotatable and axially slidable shaft is formed  with a pair of semi-circular openings (23, 24) at opposite  ends thereof.  a pair of gripping plungers (25, 26)  slidably received in the exchange arm (12) are spring-Â­ biased toward opposite ends of the arm (12) for gripping  tools (17) held in the semi-circular openings (23, 24).  a pair  of guided pins (35) are protruded respectively from the  gripping plungers.  a cam (47) engageable with the guided  pins (35) is disposed in position to inwardly retract either  of the gripping plungers (25, 26) which approaches to a  tool socket of a tool magazine, against the force of an  associated bias spring (34) when the exchange arm (12)  is pivoted from a parked position toward a grip position  for gripping tools (17) received respectively in a machine  tool spindle (20) and the tool socket.
