<ABSTRACT>
Remote control system in which the engagement or trigger orders of the circuit breaker are transmitted from the control cabinet to the circuit breaker, by a hydraulic pressure drop.The pilot pilot valves (24) and trigger (26) of the control block (12) of the circuit breaker (2-4-6) are actuated by a hydraulic device (28-30) controlled by a pressure drop appearing inThe pipes (44-44 ') in response to the fleeting opening of a purge solenoid valve (52-52') located in the control cabinet (46).Application in particular to the centralized control of several circuit breakers or modules of circuit breakers.
