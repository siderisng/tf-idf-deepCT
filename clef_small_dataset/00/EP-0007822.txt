<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a device for transferring materials immersed in a liquid. This device is characterized in that it comprises a shaft (6) integral with a helical ailet (8) and partially immersed in the liquid, one of the ends of the fin being located above the free surface of the free surface liquid, means (10) for transmitting to the shaft a helical back and forth movement, placed above the free surface of the liquid, a tube (16) of the same axis as the shaft and rendered integral with its inner wall of the fin and, by its upper edge of the shaft, a ferrule (24) extending the tube beyond the lower end of the shaft, means (20) for bringing said materials into the Lower part of said tube, means (22) for evacuating said materials to the upper part of said tube, means (26) for supplying compressed air the internal space at said ferrule. Application to the transfer of radioactive waste.
