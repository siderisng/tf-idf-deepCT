<TITLE>
a process for preparing 1,7-octadiene.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

<ABSTRACT>
1,7-octadiene is prepared by hydrodimerizing butadiene  in the presence of formic acid or a salt thereof, optionally  a solvent and a catalytic amount of palladium and a first  ligand having the formula (ro)aprb and a second ligand  having the formula (ro)a,prb, wherein a + b equals 3, a  is 0, 1 or 2, a′ + b′ equals 3, a′ is 0, 1, 2 or 3, a ist not the  same as a′, wherein the r groups, which may be the same  or different, attached to the phosphorus or oxygen atoms,  are organic groups and wherein at least one r group of said  first ligand is a sterically hindering group selected from  benzyl or branched alkyl, aralkyl, alkenyl or cycloalkyl  groups having from 3 to about 10 carbon atoms with bran­ ching occurring at a carbon atom no more than two carbon  atoms from the phosphorus atom.  
</ABSTRACT>
