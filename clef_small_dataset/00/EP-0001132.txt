<ABSTRACT>
The circles deposited on the conveyor belt recovers partially and are sufficiently free to undergo any treatment, for example a treatment by means of pressure vapor. For this purpose the device according to the invention consists of the combination of a hollow shaft (1) with a side opening (9) and controlled so that it can rotate; an arm (13), fixed on this shaft and provided with a guidance eye of the wire (14); a motionless drum (18) whose axis is in the extension of the aforementioned hollow shaft and on which the wire is wound via the hollow shaft (1), of the lateral opening ( 9) of this shaft and the guide carnation of the wire (14); a pressure disc (21), arranged for oblique relative to the drum and operating in cooperation with this drum; and a means (10) from oscillating the disc (21) to reject into a wire circles on a conveyor belt (27), the wire wound on the surface of the drum coat.
