<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a method and apparatus for carrying out the method for cleaning waste water.According to the invention, the wastewater is optionally after a mechanical pre-cleaning (1) in a so-called coupling reactor (4), in which the water is pushed upwards is very strong with finely divided air (oxygen).In the coupling reactor, filling and materials for neutralization, biological cleaning, filtration and / or adsorption are accommodated in a variety of layers.Subsequently, the water is guided over a special filter system (6.8), whereby an air entry (7) is repeated between individual filters.The filters contain in addition to sieves, which is partially formed from porous plastics, materials for biological purification and optionally adsorption.
