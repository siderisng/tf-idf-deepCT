<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a switching follower filter with n switches, of the bandpass type.This filter comprises means (m, t) for taking a pre-filtered synchronization signal of path switching.It is characterized in that the means (m, t) for taking the pre-filtered signal is constituted in such a way that in said pre-filtered signal remains a portion of the input signal (E) of the filter.Application to filtering a signal having a varying frequency component, associated with significant background noise.
