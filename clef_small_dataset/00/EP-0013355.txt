<ABSTRACT>
For spinning fabrics from thermoplastic plastics, which in one direction have a higher tear strength than in the perpendicular direction, the tear strengths are approximated to each other by using these nonwovens in which the threads are in approximate confusion and which are solidified by needles, 85- 25 ° C temperatures located below the crystalline melting point in the direction of the lower tear strength by 20 - 200% of the original length, wherein in the perpendicular direction the length is either retained or changed in the range of ± 10%.
