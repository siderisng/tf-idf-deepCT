<ABSTRACT>
1.  process for the manufacture of compounds of the general formula see diagramm : ep0014454,p15,f1  wherein x and y each signify hydrogen, halogen with an atomic number of at most 35 or trifluoromethyl and n signifies 0 or 1, characterized by treating a compound of the general formula  see diagramm : ep0014454,p15,f2 wherein x and y have the above significance and z signifies a hydroxyamino, an amino or a protected amino group, under acidic conditions with a mercury (ii) salt or in the presence of water with a strong acid, where z is a protected amino group cleaving off the protecting group and, where a compound of formula i wherein n is 0 is obtained, if desired oxidizing this compound to a compound of formula i wherein n is 1.
