<ABSTRACT>
disclosed is a coupling arrangement for a dc welding ap­ paratus, particularly intended for mig welding.  the coupling  comprises a controllable fast dc voltage source, first circuit  elements (sh, r1, r2) for detecting both load current (l) and  load voltage (u₂) and for generating voltage signals corre­ sponding thereto, second circuit elements (a1-a4, r3-r11,  c1-c3) for forming a control signal (uo) in response to said  voltage, signals so as to correspond to the values of desired  current pulses and current increase rate in a drop short-cir­ cuit instance, and third circuit elements (oe, t1, t2) which  receive the control signal and effect control of the voltage  source in response thereto.  said second circuit elements  may include means for receiving a setting signal by which the  characteristic values of the current pulse and the current in­ crease may be set and scaled and the open circuit-voltage of  the voltage source can be determined.  
</ABSTRACT>
