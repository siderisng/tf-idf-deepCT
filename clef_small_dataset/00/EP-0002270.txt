<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a device for depositing erythrocytes having a rotor which has pivotable pipe-containing pipe holders.At the rotor (1) stops (10) for limiting the swinging of the brackets (3) are arranged.The location of the stops is changeable.Preferably, the stops are summarized to a rotor displaceable and detectable ring.Optionally, the subsequent can only be grouped together.The ring (10) can be sagen-formed or with steps in its lower part.With a scale (12), the position of the stops can be displayed.The invention is preferably used for depositing erythrocytes in a blood sample, more generally suspended particles suspended in a liquid.
