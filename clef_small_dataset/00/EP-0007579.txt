<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a circuit arrangement for monitoring the state of signal plants, in particular road traffic light signal systems. In this case, different signals can be safely monitored for their admissibility or unregistered, without changing the signal states in adaptation to changed persons or in an extension of the signal to be monitored, manual wiring work. For this purpose, test signal states are determined in a memory which are held in a memory, with the respective actual signal states of the signal generator specify the signals in at least one microprocessor such that each signal state-contaminated signal with all stepwise successively retrieved from the memory Test signals is compared. The main application of the invention is in the field of monitoring road traffic light signal systems.
