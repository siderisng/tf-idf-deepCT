<ABSTRACT>
Fast synchronization device of a clock on an external clock.The clock to be synchronized (h) is obtained at the output of a phase locking loop (1), by division of the frequency of a reference clock (HR) in a frequency divider (4) whose factordivision is controlled by a phase comparator (6) which determines the difference between the transitions of the two clocks to synchronize.According to the invention, the pulses of the reference clock (HR) are applied to the phase locking loop (1) via a door (8), means provided for blocking this door by theCharacteristic transition (TH) of the clock to synchronize that follows the appearance of a resynchronization control signal (SR), and to then unblock this door with a characteristic transition (Tha) of the external clock.
