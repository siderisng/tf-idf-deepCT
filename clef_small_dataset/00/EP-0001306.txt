<ABSTRACT>
the service temperature of hot melt adhesive compositions  containing a partially hydrogenated thermoplastic elastomeric  block copolymer, a tackifying resin and a mineral  hydrocarbon oil is increased by the presence of a low molecular  weight polyphenylene ether resin having a viscosity average  molecular weight (mvis) of between 6,000 and 25,000 and  a softening point (tg) of between 170°c and 210°c.  the improved compositions are preferably used in the  preparation of pressure-sensitive adhesive tapes which are  used where high service temperatures are required, for  example, in paint shops where service temperatures of 110-120°c  are desired.
