<ABSTRACT>
Bipolar vertical transistors (TE1, TS1), created from a substrate (100) supporting two epitaxial layers (101, 102) of opposite conductivity types, being epitaxial base (first epitaxy (101) for the first transistor ( tc1), second epitaxy (102) for the second (ts1), the collector of one (ts1) and the emitter of the other (tc1) each comprise a portion (2, 1) substrate, both portions (2, 1) being contiguous, opposite types. These transistors are separated by a dam (17) insulating passing through the epitaxial layers (101, 102) and by a junction (3) which goes from the lower face (104) of the substrate to said dam and whose junction (3) which separates the two said portions (2, 1) of substrate is at least a portion. The manufacturing process comprises the localized introduction, into a substrate of an initial type, of impurities of the opposite type, by thermalog or diffusion. The invention is applicable to Darlington Mix amplifiers TES and "PUSH-PULL".
