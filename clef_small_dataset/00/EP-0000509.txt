<ABSTRACT>
Aliphatic secondary amines are prepared by aliphatic nitriles and aliphatic alcohols in the liquid phase, in a molar ratio of alcohol: nitrile of 90: 10 to 10: 90, in the presence of hydrogenation-dehydrogenation catalysts at a temperature of 120 to 260 ° C and under practical Pressurless conditions with at least 2 mol of hydrogen per mole of nitrile are reacted. Also described are secondary amines of the formula wherein R₁ is a straight-chain or branched alkyl, alkenyl or multi-ethylenically unsaturated hydrocarbon radical with 8-26 C atoms or an alkoxyalkyl radical of the formula R₂ (OX) M- With a molecular weight of at least 130, R₂, a straight-chain or branched alkyl, alkenyl or multi-ethylenically unsaturated hydrocarbon radical with 1 to 26 C atoms, X is a radical -CH₁CH₂-, -CH (CH₃) -CH₂ or -CH₂-CH (CH₃) - and M can be a whole or broken number between 1 and 20 and can also be present within M Binations of the radicals X, Y is a number of 1 to 20. The secondary amines obtained are valuable Zwi shen products that can be processed versatile, for example for the production of plasticizers for textiles, microbiocides, from preparation aids in synthetic fibers or compounds for liquid extraction of metals from highly acidic solutions.
