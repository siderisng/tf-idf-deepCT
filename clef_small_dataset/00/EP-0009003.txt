<ABSTRACT>
The armature of an articulated bed essentially comprises a folder 1, a seat 2 and a leg press 3, mounted on a cradle 4. The folder 1 and the applui-legs 3 are mounted pivoting on this cradle 4. The folder1 and the leg support 3 have bores 12, 17, 30, 31, spaced apart from each other and likely to cooperate with pivots 13, 29 of solidarity articulation of the cradle 4. Next the bores 12, 30 or 17, 31, in which the pivots 13, 29 are mounted, the useful prime of the reinforcement is different.This useful length can be changed without difficulty depending on the size of a user.
