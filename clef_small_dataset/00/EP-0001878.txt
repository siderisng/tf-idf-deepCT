<TITLE>
adhesive composition and process for bonding.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
an adhesive composition effective in bonding low-energy  plastic surfaces to each other which provides improved  low temperature performance containing the following  components: (1) a first ethylene-vinyl ester copolymer  having from about 4 to about 35 weight percent of a vinyl  ester; (2) a second ethylene-vinyl ester copolymer having  an excess of 35 weight percent of a vinyl ester; (3) a  tackifier selected from the following group (a) a rosin,  (b) a rosin ester, (c) a terpene resin, (d) a terpene-phenolic  resin and (e) a rosin-modified phenolic resin; and  (4) a polyethylene wax having a molecular weight of about  600 to about 3000 and a process for bonding low-energy  plastic surfaces to each other.  
</ABSTRACT>
