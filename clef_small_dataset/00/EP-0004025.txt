<ABSTRACT>
In a rotary drum collecting container (20) for receiving and carriage of garbage or other bulk material, the container even rotates in a box carrier (23), which is supported on a mounting frame (34) connected to the chassis (21).For better recording of the forces acting on the box carrier (23) and thus the mounting frame (34), the box carrier is lying lying on the mounting frame and all forces are summarized so that they act on the box carrier, whereby the mounting frame and thus the chassis ofForces of the collecting container are free.
