<TITLE>
vertical support for a radiator element.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>
aga radiator bvnl<sep>aga radiatoren b.v.  <sep>aga radiatoren b.v.postbus 2206nl-6360 aa nuthnl<sep>aga radiatoren b.v.<sep>
</APPLICANT>
<INVENTOR>

</INVENTOR>
<ABSTRACT>
the rigid vertical holder is fixed to a building wall so as to accommodate a radiator element.  the lower end (4b) of the holder extends away from the wall and terminates in a round-section groove (6) accommodating the flat horizontal portion (2) of the element (1).  the upper end pref.  includes a bar, extending horizontally from the wall, and with a spring steel component (6) fixed to its outer end and extending upwards.  the component is bent so as to form a horizontal part parallel with the bar, and one or more upright parts (10), also an end portion bent into an inverted u.  the arms of the u are of unequal length, the outer one being shorter and pref.  bent back towards the inner one. 
</ABSTRACT>
