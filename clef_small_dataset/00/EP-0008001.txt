<ABSTRACT>
A resilient shielding element (1) is intended according to a resilient soft magnetic alloy.Particularly favorable are alloys that have a static coercive force thickness of less than 200 mA / cm, a rela-tive static permeability (measured as equivalent meability at 4 mA / cm) of at least 2000 and a vicker hardness of at least 180, and whose yield limit is at least 500 n /mm² is.Suitable are both crystalline and amorphous soft magnetic alloys.The resilient shielding elements are suitable for MA gnetic tape cassettes, in particular compact cassettes, as well as for other purposes where a spring effect is required simultaneously with the shielding effect.
