<ABSTRACT>
Method for electrostatic charging of a dielectric layer as well as device for carrying out the travel.The invention relates to a method and a device for electrostatic charging of a dielectric point on a predetermined potential.For this purpose, an electrode (7) is arranged at a distance from the dielectric layer (8) and connected to the one output (21) of an AC voltage generator gate (2), the other output (20) with the power-carrying outer (11) of a DC generator (1) is connected.Between the electrode (7) and the layer (8) there is a further electrode (5) which is connected to the output (11) of the DC voltage generator (1).The layer (8) is on a counter electrode (9) to the electrode (5) and is placed on ground.Each of the electrodes (5,7,9) may consist of a plurality of single electrodes isolated from each other.
