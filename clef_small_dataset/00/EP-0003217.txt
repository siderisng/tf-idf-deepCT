<ABSTRACT>
Precipitating pebble granules are used to determine the filler of precipitating dieselic acid using the smallest possible transport space in to be vulcanized rubber blends in a simple and dustbreaking manner as precisely as possible. The inventive precipitation pebble granules with the following physical chemical characteristics: show a low dust content and a low abrasion tilt. These precipitating pastry acids are prepared by shaking a precipitating pebble acid suspension which filtered off precipitating pastry acid, washed, to which precipitation pebble filter cake thus obtained is optionally powdered precipitation silica, which thus obtained precipitation pebble acid meter at a solids content of 28 wt. -% to 40 wt. -% with a granulator to precipitate precipitation pebble granules and subjected to a short-term drying. The resulting precipitation pebble granules show very good dispersibility in rubber mixtures to be vulcanized and have a very low dust content.
