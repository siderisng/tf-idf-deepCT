<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a gas laser having a cylindrical discharge piston, in which at least one end face is completed with a metallic cap which carries a mirror body in its central area and a metallic pumping star.In such a laser, it is provided according to the invention that the metal cap additionally has at least one thermal compensation element, which deforms the metal cap in the same manner as the pump stem in the operating temperatures of the gas laser, such that its influence on the orientation of the mirror body relative to the piston longitudinal axis at least approximately compensatedis.With the proposed measure, the laser without effort receives a spatially constant output beam, which retains its direction even after a relatively long operation and after many operating cycles.The laser is used primarily in image disk players.
</ABSTRACT>
