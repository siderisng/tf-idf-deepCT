<ABSTRACT>
In the process for continuous production of acetic acid and acetic anhydride by oxidation of acetaldehyde with oxygen gas in the liquid phase in the presence of copper acylate and cobaltacylate as a catalyst and in the presence of an aliphatic carboxylic acid ester as a diluent, the reaction is carried out at temperatures of 62 to 90 ° C and residence times of lessThus 20 min with a weight ratio of the diluent to acetaldehyde from 60:40 to 40: 60, the reaction products of a distillation zone leads to, distilled over head carboxylic acid ester and water, separates in two phases, the organic phase performs under a reflux of at least 1: 1 back to the distillation zone, the bottom product of the distillation zone leads to an evaporator zone and gains there 2 to 12 parts by weight of an acetic acid / acetic anhydride mixture as distillate per 1 parts by weight of catalyst solution as a marsh product.
</ABSTRACT>
