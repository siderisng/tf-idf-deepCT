<ABSTRACT>
Substituted pyrrolidines of general formula I wherein R¹ is a hydrogen atom or an alkyl group having 1 to 7 carbon atoms, R² a chlorine atom, a hydroxy group, an alkyl group having 1 to 4 carbon atoms, or an alkoxy group having 1 to 4 carbon atoms and R⁷ a hydrogen atom, an acyl group, oneAlkyl group with 1 to 7 carbon atoms or a tetrahydropyranyl group, and their acid addition salts with inorganic and organic acids are new compounds.They have a highly analgesic effect and are therefore suitable for the treatment of pain conditions of all kinds.Method for producing the new, pharmacologically active compounds as well as the intermediates required for their preparation are given.
