<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to active, hydroxyl and halogen-containing polymers of phosphoritrides whose halogen atoms and hydroxyl groups are bonded to phosphorus, wherein the phosphoritrides about 35 to 54 wt.-% phosphorus, about 35 to 47 wt.-% nitrogen, about 1 to 20 wt.-% oxygen and about 0.1 to 8 wt.-% chlorine included.
