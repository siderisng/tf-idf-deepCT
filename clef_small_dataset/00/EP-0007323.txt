<ABSTRACT>
in the production of wooden garden furniture which is subjected to weathering, there is a problem in connecting the individual wooden components with crossing grain directions to one another so firmly that no moisture can penetrate this connection point.  for this purpose, it is proposed that, at least in the connecting surface (5) of one wooden component (1), two rims (3, 4) are formed, arranged running at a distance from one another in the longitudinal direction of the grain and having an apex at the front, and that the wooden components are pressed onto one another such that the rims press at least partially into the connecting surface of the other wooden component (2).  <image>

kunststoff schicht umgeben werden, dass die eigentlichen holzflechen der witterung nicht mehr ausgesetzt sind. diese bekannten konstruktionen haben den nachteil, dass sie entweder arbeitsaufwendig
