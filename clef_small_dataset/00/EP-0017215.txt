<ABSTRACT>
Paint binders based on at elevated temperature by polyurethane forming epoxy-amine adducts, which are water-soluble after neutralization with acids, are prepared by 3-70 mol% of the epoxide groups of a polymer with an epoxide equivalent of 180 - 1500 at 80 - 160 ° C with a polyisocyanate adduct and previously or after with a secondary aliphatic or cycloaliphatic amine and or alkanolamine reacts. The polyisocyanate adduct used has no free NCO groups and per molecule at least one carboxyl group. Through the reaction with the amine, at least 50% of the epoxide groups, which are not received with the polyisocyanate adduct, reacted, and the final product has a surge time of at most 5 mg of KOH G and an amine number of 24 - 150 mg KOH G . These paint binders have excellent resistance to solvents and corrosive substances, and thus produced overlay agents are water-dilutable.
