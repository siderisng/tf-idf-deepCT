a linear trimer of p-isopropenyl phenol having the  formula:    is provided which can be readily prepared, easily isolated  and is useful as a curing agent for epoxy resins.  the linear  trimer may be a cis-isomer (m. p.  225. 5-227°c) and a transisomer  (m. p.  167-168°c).  it can be prepared by reacting  p-isopropenyl phenol or a linear polymer thereof in the  presence of an acid catalyst such as sulphuric acid, boron  fluoride or activated clay and an organic solvent such as  an aromatic hydrocarbon, halogenated hydrocarbon, substituted  aromatic hydrocarbon or aprotic polar solvent (e. g.   benzene, carbon tetrachloride, nitrobenzene or dioxane).
