<ABSTRACT>
This invention relates to a combustion head for a fluid fuel, in particular liquid, burned in particular in particular of a gas whose concentration of oxygen is substantially lower than that of the air.This head comprises, adjacent to the burner itself, a jar (6) of generally cylindrical shape, having an input opening (6a) for creating a loss of pressure sensitive to inject the oxygen carrier gas into the pot and aDisk (6c) located at the outlet of the pot and sized to create a second lower pressure loss.The intense combustion produced in the pot (6) heats its walls at a temperature located substantially above the end point of the fuel distillation curve to prevent coke deposition.
