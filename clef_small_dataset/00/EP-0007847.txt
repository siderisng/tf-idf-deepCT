<ABSTRACT>
Hydraulic rotating distributor comprising two rotating organs solidarized respectively with an input shaft (16) and an output shaft and controlling the pressure fluid supply of an assist engine according to their relative angular position..External controlled locking means (76, 78) prevent the relative rotation of the rotating organs (36, 26) until the torque applied to the input shaft becomes greater than a predetermined value.
