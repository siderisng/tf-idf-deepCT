<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an improvement in the exclusion of water, with elastomerically crosslinking compositions of condensation end groups-containing diorganopolysiloxane and at least three silicon-bonded, hydrolyzable groups of hydrolyzable groups having hydrolyzable groups having hydrolyzable groups having hydrolyzable groups of water at room temperature.The improvement is achieved by a content of such masses of 30 to 100 percent by weight, based on the weight of condensation end groups having diorganopolysiloxane, produced by polymerization or compound polymerization in the aqueous medium of homopolymer of acrylonitrile or mixed polymer of acrylonitrile.
