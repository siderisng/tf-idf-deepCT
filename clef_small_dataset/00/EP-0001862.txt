<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for the polymerization of alkylenene I or copolymerization of alkylenene I under each other or ethylene.According to the invention, a catalyst system is used, which is composed of a titanium halide component on an at least practically anhydrous magnesium halide as a carrier and an organoaluminum component containing a complex of an organic aluminum compound with an ester of an oxygen-containing organic acid, characterized in that as a carrier one at leastVirtually magnesium oxide-free magnesium halide used was won by heating a water and / or magnesium oxide-containing magnesium halide in a gas stream of carbon halide vapor and / or a mixture of carbon monoxide and halogen.The process according to the invention results in a high polymer output of the invention per G used Ti compound and a high content of isotactic polymer.
