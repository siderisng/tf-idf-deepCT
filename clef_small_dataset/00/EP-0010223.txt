<ABSTRACT>
In this method for decomposing a gas mixture, gap gases of an ethylene system are partially liquefied by stepwise cooling (2, 14, 19).The thus formed th condensates are led to a first rectification column (33) after scribbling the most easily volatile constituents (9, 15, 20) for further decomposition.The non-condensed ingredients are disassembled in a second rectification column (24), wherein the bottom product is guided into the first rectification column (33) after scribbling the most easily volatile constituents (26).
