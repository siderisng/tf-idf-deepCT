<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the preparation phase of the glass blank as a stick for the draw of the optical fiber.A first stage of the process comprises the melting of a mixture of rates of impurities of impurities so strong transition (<10⁻⁴) than the rate of the final blank (<10⁻⁷>).A second stage is to cause separation into two solid phases (hard phases, rich in silica and soft, poor silica), by drawing the blank from the molting glass bath and subsequent thermal annealing.A heart attack and a peripheral attack is then carried out to remove the soft phase and its impurities, differently depending on the depth, which creates a radial gradient of refractive inseca and an optical sheath.Application to optical fiber transmissions.
