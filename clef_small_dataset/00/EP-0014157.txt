<TITLE>
a two-chamber device for an internal combustion engine.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>
boitelle georges<sep>boitelle, georges<sep>boitelle, georges85, rue des deux frères laportef-78680 eponefr<sep>
</APPLICANT>

</INVENTOR>
<ABSTRACT>
the device comprises two chambers (1, 11) each comprised of a cylinder (2, 12) wherein a piston (3, 13) slides.  the revolution axises of the cylinders (2, 12) and of the pistons (3, 13) of these two chambers (1, 11) are in register.  the two pistons (3, 13) or the two chambers are integral and transmit their reciprocating linear motion to the motion transforming system of the engine via a rigid rod (6).  the device allows to dissociate the cycle phases of internal combustion engines so as to obtain more power and a better efficiency. 
</ABSTRACT>
