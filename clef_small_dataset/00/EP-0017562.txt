<ABSTRACT>
Microphone source with a solid state, disposed within a rectangular waveguide (2) of which one of the ends is closed by a sliding short circuit (7).MICRONDE SOURCE comprising: a negative resistance diodes (IA to IN), this diodes network being disposed on the inner wall of larger waveguide and perpendicular to the guide transmission direction, a resonant microband line(12) suspended by means of a dielectric substrate (11);A connecting means (13) between the diodes network and the resonant line, connecting means (14 and 15) with an external polarization (V) source.Such a source finds applications in the telecommunications equipment operating in the extreme frequency of the radio spectrum.
