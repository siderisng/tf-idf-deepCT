<ABSTRACT>
In a device for automatic rolling on seat belts, a friction brake is provided, which reduces the belt feed force when the belt is applied and released for the feed of the stored belt.The friction brake consists of a coating element (20) connected to the belt shaft (20), which is constantly engaged with a second friction element (22).The second friction element (22) can be easily rotatably mounted or held by means of a lever (46).According to the invention, the latching lever (46) is an arm of a two-armed rocker lever (32) which is inevitably controlled by noses (24) on the circumference of the second friction element (22) so that the latching lever (46) the second friction element (22) during the belt extractionReleases, automatically holds at the end of each belt extraction and releases again for the belt feeder for a short strap extract.
