<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a cutting device, in particular a cutter consisting of two cutting blades (23, 24) articulated around two fixed axes (21, 22) held by two flanges (2, 3) and actuated respectively by two arms.levers (29, 30) pivoting around two other fixed axes (25, 26) maintained by the same flanges (2, 3).This device is characterized in that, during the cutting phase, traction is exerted on each posterior end (31, 32) of each cutting blade (23, 24) via at least one element ofArticulation (33 to 36) interposed between each posterior end (31, 32) of each cutting blade (23, 24) and each anterior end (27, 28) of each lever arm (29, 30).The invention relates to the hand tools used for the cutting of bolts and / or round irons.
