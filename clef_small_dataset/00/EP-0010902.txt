<ABSTRACT>
(meth)acrylic acid is produced by reacting, at a tem­ perature from 200°c to 450°c and in the vapour phase,  oxygen and isobutane or propane respectively over a cata­ lyst which contains mox, sby, pz and combined oxygen  wherein x, y and z represent the atomic ratios of mo, sb  & p and x is 9 to 24, y is 0. 25 to 2 and z is 0. 5 to 2.  in  carrying out the process the amount propane or isobutane  in the feed gas is from 10 to 70 mole per cent.
