<ABSTRACT>
Coating compositions, consisting of a) 50 to 95 wt.-% of a non-branched prepolymers with 2 to 8 terminal, blocked isocyanate groups and a mean molecular weight between 500 and 25,000 prepared from a higher molecular weight polyhydroxyl compound and an excess of a polyisocyanate subsequent blocking of free NCO groups, b) 2 to 20 wt.-% of an aliphatic and / or cycloaliphatic and / or aromatic amine having at least 2 primary and / or secondary amino groups, C) 3 to 50 wt.-% of a stable aqueous polymer dispersion and / or aqueous polymer solution having a solids content of 20 to 70 wt.-% and d) 0 to 10 wt.-% of an organic solvent.
