<ABSTRACT>
Fungicides dichloric acid diamide derivatives of the formula in which R is an optionally substituted aliphatic residue having up to 8 carbon atoms, for a cycloalkyl group having 3 to 6 carbon atoms or for a substituted phenyl radical, when halides of dichloric acidic acid having an amine in a diluentconverted into the presence of an acid binder.The active ingredients have a wide range of effects and can be applied against parasitic fungi, which affects upper ground plant parts or attack the plants from the ground, as well as against seed-transmitting pathogens.They show a particularly good effectiveness against Phytophthora Infestans and Pyricularia Oryzae.
