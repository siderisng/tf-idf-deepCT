<ABSTRACT>
a projector for an automobile vehicle headlight.  the projector comprises, inside a reflector (1) having the form of a portion of a revolution ellipsoid, an upper cover (6) and a lower cover (7) placed in front of the lamp bulb (2), so as to cover respectively the upper and the lower parts of the reflector starting from a nearly horizontal plane going through the axis (8) of the reflector.  use: non-dazzling projector.
