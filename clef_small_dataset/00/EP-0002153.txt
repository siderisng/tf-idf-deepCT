<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a panoramic ion detector spatially distributed in different beams.This detector comprises a diaphragm (1) with flat symmetry, pierced with an opening allowing the passage of the beams;This diaphragm is carried to the potential of the Earth.an electrode (5) converts the ions into electrons;This electrode has the same plane of symmetry plan that the diaphragm and it is brought to a negative potential with respect to the potential of the Earth.The detector also includes a flat electrode (9), located opposite the conversion electrode and worn to a positive potential relative to the potential of the conversion electrode.Application to the detection of charged particles.
