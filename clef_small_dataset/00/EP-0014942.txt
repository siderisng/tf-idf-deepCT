<ABSTRACT>
an electronic valve block assembly (10) for controlling  the operation of glassware forming machinery.  a selector  mechanism (30) is utilized to couple either a high pressure  plenum (24) or a low pressure plenum (22) to the input port of  a solenoid operated valve (14).  a pressure regulator (34) is  interjected between the selector mechanism (30) and the  valve (14) to finely control the pressure, providing ample flow  of output air over a wide range of pressures.  a preferred  embodiment of pressure regulator (34) is described.  also  described is an adapter plate (110) for making the valve block  compatible with varying is machine manifold configurations.
