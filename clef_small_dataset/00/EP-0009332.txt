<ABSTRACT>
a display unit for a head up display wherein the com­ biner (11), which serves to reflect light from a display sur­ face (14) towards an observer to provide the observer with  an image of the display superimposed on his view through  the combiner, comprises first and second tuned reflective  coatings (15c and 27c) disposed in such angular relation­ ship with one another and the display surface that narrow  waveband light from the display surface (14) reaches the  observer after being successively transmitted through the  first coating (25c), reflected at the second coating (27c),  reflected at the first coating (25c) and transmitted through  the second coating (27c).
