<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a device for electron beam adjustment for an electron beam system with a deflection system for the temporally controllable deflection of the electron beam from its provided beam axis for beam sampling, wherein the deflection system has a deflection device with the runtime effect for the deflection signal adapted to the beam speed.The deflection device (31, 32) relative to the beam axis (15) has symmetrical parts (33, 34, 51) to be applied to the deflection signals with respectively opposite signs, so that the same axial potential is present even in beam deflection.The invention is used in scanning electron microscopes and electron beam writing devices.
