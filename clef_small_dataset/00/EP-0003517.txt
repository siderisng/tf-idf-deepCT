<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a new herbicide effective ester, namely the cyanomethyl ester of the 4- (p-trifluoromethylphenoxy) -α-phenoxypropionic acid, the formula and herbicidal agent containing it and its use for selective control of monocotyler weeds in dicotylene crops.The invention also relates to methods for producing the novel ester by means of known methods by reacting a corresponding trifluoromethyl-phenoxy-α-phenoxy-propionic acid halide with glycolsic acid nitrile or by reacting 4- (p-trifluoromethyl-phenoxy) phenol with an α-halopropionic acid cyanomethyl ester.A method for selectively controlling monocotylene weeds in dicotylene crops is also described, under post-emergent use of a herbicidal agent that contains the new ester as an active component.
