<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a compressed gas power switch having a valve bent (14) burning in a annular gap between two coaxial electrodes (1.2), which is placed in rotation by the magnetic field of a coaxially arranged coil (5).The electrodes (1,2) are displaceable against each other for contact separation or contact, and to achieve an additional transverse liner of the arc (14), a compression chamber (3) is provided with outflow bores (4).
