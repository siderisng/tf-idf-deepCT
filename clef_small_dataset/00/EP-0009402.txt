<TITLE>
a method of forming laminated hoses and laminated hoses obtained.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>
insituform int inclr<sep>insituform international inc.<sep>insituform international inc.80 broad streetmonrovialr<sep>
</APPLICANT>
<INVENTOR>

</INVENTOR>
<ABSTRACT>
a tubular lining for lining passages is formed, such  liner being constructed from a laminate comprising a  resin absorbent layer (10) and an impermeable skin (12).   the laminate is wrapped to tubular form with the skin  outermost and the free edges are butted together and are  connected in this condition by a sewing operation.  a sealing ribbon (18) is applied over the sewing to  seal the seam and stitching (16) to prevent the flow of liquid  or synthetic resin through the seam when the lining is used.
