<ABSTRACT>
alkali metal chloroferrates(ii, iii) are compounds from the system comprising alkali metal chloride/iron(ii) chloride/iron(iii) chloride.  they are prepared by melting together alkali metal chloride with anhydrous iron(iii) chloride and iron powder.  instead of a single alkali metal chloride it is also possible to use a mixture of a plurality of alkali metal chlorides.  the products are used in the colouring of ceramic material.
