<ABSTRACT>
a probe for electronic scanning type ultrasonic diagnostic  apparatus has a linear array of similar electro-acoustic  transducers (2-1 to 2-n) and one or more pseudo-transducers  (7-1, 7-2) arranged in a row.  in order to equalize the  ultrasonic directivity characteristics of each of the actual  transducers, the pseudo-transducers (7-1, 7-2) are arranged  adjacent to the end transducers (2-1, 2-n), and have approximately  the same shape and dimensions as the actual  transducers (2-1 to 2-n).
