<ABSTRACT>
For the operation of an electrical control device of an extruder, a ramp generator is used to generate a timing signal.The ramp generator has an integrator (2) at the output of ramp signals.The generation of each ramp signal is triggered by a start signal output from the extruder.By means of a correction circuit, the rising time of the integrator (2) can be adjusted, so that the ramp signal then reaches its maximum amplitude when a new start pulse arrives.In order to avoid manual and thus inaccurate correction, an automatic correction is made by supplying the correction device (4, 5) the start signals, whereby the correction device (4, 5) outputs a control signal which depends on the time distance of two successive starting pulses.
