<ABSTRACT>
grass or hay processing machine having at least one raking wheel (3) which is rotatable about a vertical axis and on which are mounted a plurality of supporting arms (8) which are directed outwards from the region of the axis of rotation and are rotatable about their longitudinal axis and to each of the inner ends of which a pivoting lever (13) controlled by a cam track is attached, whilst a prong carrier (5) is attached to the outer end of each supporting arm, each supporting arm (8) being assigned adjacent to it a further supporting arm (18), and each prong carrier (5) being selectively attachable to one of the adjacent supporting arms (18).  the machine can be converted simply and very quickly from a windrowing machine to a tedding machine, and vice versa, in that each adjacent supporting arm (18) is mounted on the raking wheel so as to be rotatable about its longitudinal axis and is coupled to the associated supporting arm (8) by means of a gear (19) for oppositely directed rotation.  <image>
</ABSTRACT>
