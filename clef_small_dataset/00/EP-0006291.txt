<ABSTRACT>
blends of polycarbonate resin and an interpolymer  modifier comprising crosslinked (meth)acrylate, crosslink­ ed styrene-acrylonitrile, and uncrosslinked styreneacrylo­ nitrile polymeric components.  the blends have improved  processing as compared to the processing characterists of  the polycarbonate resin alone and are impact resistant.   they are more weather resistant than blends of the poly­ carbonate resin and abs graft copolymers.  they may be  shaped into articles by molding such as by injection or  compression.
