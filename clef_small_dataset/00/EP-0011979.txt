<ABSTRACT>
an apparatus for bonding wires onto semiconductor is  provided with a processor controlled vertical drive mechan­ ism (26,27,28) coupled to a bonding head of the type having a  bonding tool (21) mounted therein.  known numerical control  arrangements for tools are too slow for such bonding opera­ tions.  in this apparatus height of the bonding tool (21) at a  reset position and at a bonding position is programmed into  the processor for each bonding operation on a semiconductor  device of workpiece, the processor learning from the first  operation.  sensing contacts (56,59) are provided on the bond­ ing head for establishing and sensing the reset position and  the bonding positions.  the processor is programmed to com­ pute intermediate positions between said predetermined ver­ tical positions to obtain optimum high speed movement of  the bonding head and the bonding tool (21) during subse­ quent bonding cycles.
