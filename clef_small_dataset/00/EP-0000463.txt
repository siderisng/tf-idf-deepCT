A separation method without decomposition, completely continuous and automatic, TDI of the heavy manufacturing products, consisting of evaporating TDI in an agitated and scraped evaporator, under a vacuum of 1 to 50 mm Hg at a temperature of 210 to 260 ° C,With a minimum residence time of 15 minutes in the evaporator, and extract residues continuously.
