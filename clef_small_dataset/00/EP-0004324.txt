<ABSTRACT>
multiway valve having a small elastic-rubber valve body, designed as a throwaway part, and an operating mechanism as is used, in particular, as a combination of hose crushing valves in equipment of clinical chemistry, for example analysis equipment.  in order to provide a low dead volume and better cleaning conditions, it is recommended that all channels (a, b, c, d) are configured in a single valve body (1) and extend from the outer limit thereof to a node point in the interior.  by means of the operating mechanism (2, 5), the valve body (1) can be deformed alternatively in different directions so that alternative connections between channels can be made correspondingly in or at the node point.  the valve body (1) may be a hose junction, in particular a hose crossing (fig.  1).  <image>
