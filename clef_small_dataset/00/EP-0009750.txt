<ABSTRACT>
A screwdriver (12) cooperates with the head of a stud (2) so as to withst it and lock it into rotation.The upper (1) nut (2) nut (2) has a threaded outer periphery (8).The screwing and unscrewing the upper nut (1) is made by means of two drive rollers (31) with their periphery of cylindrical grooves of the same step and the same profile as the thread (8).The two rollers (31) can be spacked or closer to the nut (1) via a pneumatic cylinder (32).The device of the invention allows the screwing and unscrewing a power-on nut of a lid fixing stud of a nuclear reaction vessel.
