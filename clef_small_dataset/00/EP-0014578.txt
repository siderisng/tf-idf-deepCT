<ABSTRACT>
the disclosure herein describes an automatic stepless  transmission with a continuously changing gear ratio; the  transmission comprises two differential gear mechanisms  (16,18) interconnected through a pair of gear connections  (36-38-46-48-52-54-60-62-34; 24-64-32) so that the power flow  from the first differential gear mechanism (16) to the second  differential gear mechanism (18) is divided into two  components when the transmission is 'in gear'.
