<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a treatment head for electromedical diagnostic or therapeutic treatment of body parts, in particular with ultrasonic and / or stimulating current.Depending on the shape of the body surface to be treated, separate treatment heads (1) are used with different treatment surfaces (7, 10).Under purchasing increased production costs, they have already constructed such treatment heads (1) with the first treatment surface, in which a different further treatment surface is fueled by means of advancable adapter pieces (8).According to the invention, the adapter piece (8) by means of paste or coupling part (11) on the first treatment surface (7) in the sense of switching from first (7) to second (10) treatment surface is paved.This makes it possible to change the change of first (7) to second (10) treatment surface without a large technical additional effort.
