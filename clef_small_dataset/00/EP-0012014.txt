<ABSTRACT>
in a process for producing a crosslinked polyethylene  insulated cable having an outer semiconductive layer, an  internal semiconductive layer and an electrically insulating  layer are provided on an electrical conductor in conventional  manner.  then a resin composition comprising 100 parts by  weight of an ethylene/vinyl acetate copolymer having a vinyl  acetate content of at least about 55% by weight or polyvinyl  acetate and about 5 to 100 parts by weight of carbon black and  a crosslinking agent are coated by extrusion on the electrically  insulating layer.  thereafter, the coated composition is heated  to at least 230Â°c to crosslink the resin composition.  when the ethylene/vinyl acetate copolymer used has a  vinyl acetate content of at least about 80% by weight the outer  semiconductive layer can easily be peeled off by hand.
