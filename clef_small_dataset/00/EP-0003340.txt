<ABSTRACT>
A reagent for the photometric determination of hemoglobin content in the blood is shown, which shows a characteristic color after adding the blood sample.This reagent consists of an aqueous alkaline solution containing a water-soluble nonionic detergent.This solution causes the conversion of all hemoglobin and hem derivatives contained in the blood into a single product having a particular absorption maximum in the visible spectral range.The invention further relates to the method for determining the hemoglobin content in the blood using the above-mentioned reagent.
