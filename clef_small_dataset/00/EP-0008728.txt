preparation of yeast autolysate.

<ABSTRACT>
For the production of yeast autolysate, the autolysis is in aqueous suspension with the addition of 0.03 to 15 wt.-% of fatty acids (C₄₋₁₄) and / or their glycerol esters, based on yeast drying mass, leads through 20 - 60 ° C over 6 - 36 h.Preferred additives are mono- and diglycerides of the capyl and / or capric acid.The yield envelopes are then separated from the liquid and the Autoly Sat won by evaporation.Advantageous is also the addition of 0.1 to 10 wt.-% saline for aqueous yeast spension.The autolysis is accelerated when the yeast before autolysis a temperature shock of 60 - 90 ° C rend 1 - 120 sec.Exposed.Also, the operating amount of fatty acid can be reduced by the shock treatment.Suitable yeast species for the autolysis method are edible yeasts of Candida utilis, saccharomyces cerevisiae, sacch.Fragilis and Sacch.Carlsbergensis.
