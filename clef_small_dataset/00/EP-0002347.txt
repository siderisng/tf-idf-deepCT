<ABSTRACT>
neutralised sulphonated elastomeric polymers are extended  by the inclusion of 5 to 200 wt.  % of a non-polar  process oil.  alro included are 25 to 350 wt.  % of an inorganic filler  and 3 to 60 wt.  % of a preferential ionic plasticizer.  the final  composition has a viscosity of 0. 73 sec⁻¹ at 200°c of 5 x 10⁴  poises to 1 x 10⁶ poises.  this extended polymer can be  formed into an elastomeric article.
