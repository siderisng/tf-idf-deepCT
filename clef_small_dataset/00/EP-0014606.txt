<ABSTRACT>
The device comprises means for supporting the said cables between a fixed point (42) and a second point on the vertical passing through the axis of rotation of said movable part (18) constituting the end of the support means..Guidance means (52) of the pipe portions (34) are arranged between the end of the support means and the center of rotation of the movable part (18).The guide means (52) are able to keep the pipe portions regularly on a set of revolution around the axis of the moving part (18).Means are planned to support the lower end (32) with the guiding means of the moving part at its rotation center.Application to the crossing of the rotating plug of a nuclear reaction.
