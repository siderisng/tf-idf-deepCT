N-azolylmethylsubstituted halogen acetanilides of the all mean formula wherein R, R¹, R², A and X have the meaning given in the description, e.g.b.The compound of the formula is prepared by reacting 2 halogen-n halo-methyl acetanilides and at least molar amounts of azoles, e.g.b.By reacting the verbin, the formula and the at least molar amount of 4,5-dichlorimidazole with at least molar amounts of aqueous alkalis in a two-phase system, possibly using a phase transfer catalyst, for example a quaternal ammonium base or a crown ether.Under "two phase system" is a mixture of water and an organic solvent which is not miscible with water.The liquids contain chemical connectors dissolved or in the form of a dispersion.
