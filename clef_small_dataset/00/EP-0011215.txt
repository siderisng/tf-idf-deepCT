<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to foamed plastic particles in star shape with three legs lying in a plane.The ratio of particle thickness (D) for particle height (H) is 1: 2.75 to 1: 3.3, the ratio of thigh width (B) to part Chenhöhe (H) 1: 2.2, to 1: 2.5, and the particle height (H) 10 to 60 mm.The particles according to the invention are suitable as free-flowing packing material, which has good flowing ability and equally good toothing property.
