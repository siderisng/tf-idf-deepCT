<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a display in which a liquid crystal-based light valve (3) is in optical contact with one of the image brightening serving fluorescent plate (2). In order to achieve sufficient optical contrast in this display device in the so-called "absorbent drive", the following construction is proposed according to the invention: the display is pulled backwards from a contrast filter (4) and a reflector (5) behind the filter; The contrast filter (4) is such that it allows the excitation light to pass for the fluorescent material of the fluorescence plate, the remaining proportions of the surrounding light, however. Such a display has a sufficient color contrast and an improved brightness conassae, since the reflector (5) extends the distance for the excitation light in the fluorescent plate and thus increases the useful fluorescent light. The proposed display device also provides a parallax reisuit image in "light-light operation" and can also be used in less favorable lighting ratios.
