<ABSTRACT>
In a doctor blade device in a printing plate development machine with a stripper compartment consisting of elastic material, which is releasably secured in a guide strip, the guide strip (2) is associated with a carrier strip (3).For easy handling, the ends of the carrier strip are inserted in each one upwardly open U-profile piece (14) with two legs (15, 16) spaced apart by a web (17).Each U-profile piece is attached to its web (17) each on a side plate (20) such that its two legs (15, 16) are directed transversely to the printing plate transport direction (29) to the inner of the printing plate development machine.On the top of the carrier strip (3) support means (9 to 12) are mounted on both sides, with which the carrier strip (3) is supported on a U-profile piece (14).
</ABSTRACT>
