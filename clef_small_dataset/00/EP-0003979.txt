<ABSTRACT>
The implantable pharmaca depot for the treatment of bone or bone marker diseases consists of a matrix of resorbable, sintered calcium phosphates which are composed of CaO and P₂0₅ in a quantity ratio of 5: 1 to 2: 1, preferably 3: 1.The matrix contains the active ingredient and an excipient for controlling the pharmaca dispensing kinetics.As auxiliaries, derivatives of the saturated or unsaturated aliphatic carboxylic acids are preferably used with 10 to 20 C atoms and resorbable polymers.
