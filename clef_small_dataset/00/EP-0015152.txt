<ABSTRACT>
multivalent heavy metal ions are removed from metal  plating waste streams by passing them through an unex­ panded vermiculite cation exchange column.  the column is  effective to remove copper, zinc, and nickel ions and mixtures  thereof.  where cyanide and cyanide-metal complexes are  also represent in the plating waste stream, the stream is pretre­ ated to destroy the cyanide and cyanide complexes prior to  passage through the ion exchange column.
