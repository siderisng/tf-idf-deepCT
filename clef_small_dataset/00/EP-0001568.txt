<ABSTRACT>
1.  swivelling and laterally displaceable plug-in connection for the connection of a transformer winding to a rigidly guided plug component at the end of a lever arm which carries out imposed swivelling movements characterised in that a plug socket (2) which accommodates the plug pin (1) is arranged in a sleeve (3) mounted in a universal joint, behind the universal joint in the direction of insertion of the plug pin (1) and that the sleeve (3) can be laterally displaced across axis in the joints of the universal joint from a centre position.
