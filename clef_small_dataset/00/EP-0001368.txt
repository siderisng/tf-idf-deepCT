<ABSTRACT>
Metasilicate granulation process leading to a product containing 2 to 6% water from the weight of the finished product: in a first zone (1), the anhydrous meta silicate and a metasilicate liquor;the contacting of the solid and liquid phases of the liquid phase is carried out in a stirred pulverulent mass of the solid phase;said zone to the hot gas thermal action so as to give the granules formed sufficient coherence while allowing subsisted in said granules about 1 to 8% of water content;Then we submit in a subsequent zone (2) so-called maturation, the granules obtained at a heat treatment in a stirred speaker, so as to at least partially provoke the transformation into metasilicate pentahydrated.
</ABSTRACT>
