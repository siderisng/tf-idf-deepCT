<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a process for the preparation of self-crosslinking cathodically deposited electro dip coating binders by reacting given polyether polyols containing solid-capped diisocyanates or partially capped poly isocyanates, wherein the capped isocyanate moieties containing reaction products (A) are reacted with a poly amine (b),The fact that at least one amino group of the polyamine (B) is formed with a capped isocyanate group of the reaction product (a) a urea group, and the self-crosslinking region of (a) and (b) is converted in a conventional manner by protonation with an acid in water-dilutable shape.The electrochemical paint binders prepared according to the invention are suitable for the cathodic electro dipping coating of metal parts.
</ABSTRACT>
