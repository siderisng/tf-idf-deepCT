<ABSTRACT>
in the process, which is carried out using polymeric organic dispersion aids, a combination of 2 encrustation inhibitors is used.  one type of encrustation inhibitor is based on inorganic iodine compounds, which may be present in amounts of from 1 to 1000 ppm.  the other group of encrustation inhibitors is based on water-soluble inorganic phosphates of alkali metals or alkaline earth metals.  these are used in amounts of from 0. 01 to 1% by weight, based on the monomers to be polymerised.  the use of a combination of two encrustation inhibitors prevents the formation of deposits on the walls of the reactor and its internals in the polymerisation of vc.  this effect also occurs in a suspension process in which homogenisation is carried out before the polymerisation (microsuspension process). 
</ABSTRACT>
