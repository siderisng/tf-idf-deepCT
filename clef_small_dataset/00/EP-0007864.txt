<ABSTRACT>
Training of ways for Panoramic passive Sonar by the technique of load transfer devices.The offset register (30) receives the multiplexed signals of the m transducers forming the sonar listening base.A set of racks (310. 1;...., 310. R) of the register makes it possible to obtain after summation (33. m) an interpo value of the signal of a transducer delayed precisely.The weights (al. m;..... M) perform a transverse filter.Application to a panoramic sonar having a diameter listening base greater than 1 m and having reduced secondary lobes and the diagram does not vary with the frequency.
