<ABSTRACT>
A hydraulic control device for the load unable flow control of an oil flow and the substantially pressure-free dissipation of a flow rate to the tank (25) has a horic piston (2) which controls the connection between the pump (26) and tank (25) depending on the load pressure. For this purpose, the horizontal piston (2) is acted upon on one side (5) with the pump pressure and on the other side (8) with the sum of the forces made of load pressure and a spring force (10). When the load pressure is switched off, a Ver Lustarmer free flow between pump (26) and tank (25) should be ensured by the fact that when the load pressure is switched off, the influence of the spring force is turned off. For this purpose, either the spring (10) is supported by a retaining piston (13), which brings the spring (10) only when the load pressure is switched on to its operative position or the spring force is by a holding cask acting on the waidge bulb, which is switched off the load pressure with the Pumper pressure is acted on, in whole or partially compensated.
