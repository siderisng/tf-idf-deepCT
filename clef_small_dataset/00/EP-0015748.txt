<ABSTRACT>
a process for preparing a carboxylated latex contain­ ing an n-alkylolamide of an unsaturated carboxylic acid  comprises carrying out the polymerisation reaction in two  or more stages in which the majority (or all) of the n-­ alkylolamide monomer is supplied to the second stage.  the  reaction may be carried out using a batch procedure but a  continuous reaction is preferred.  the product latex is par­ ticularly suitable for paper and textile applications and  especially for use in paper coating compositions.
