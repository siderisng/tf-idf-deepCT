<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a melting metal sample holder vent, type device comprising a mold having an input passage giving access to at least one chamber, communicating with the outside of the mold.It is characterized in that it is constituted by a barrier (10) housed in the chamber (7), this barrier being shaped to offer, on the one hand, a plurality of passages for the air during the filling of the device byMelting metal and, on the other hand, a mass and a surface sufficient to guarantee cooling until solidification of the metal reaching said barrier.The invention is applicable in the field of metallurgy.
