<ABSTRACT>
Riveting device, intended to attach a first element 1 to a second element 2 made of hard metal.This device comprises a support 3 which is common to the first element 1 and a hollow load 4 and a nail or metal rivet 5 which is drowned in the loading of the hollow load 4, in the axis XX of the coating 6 thereof.The support 3 comprising a surface 7 intended to apply on the second element 2 and approximately CEN téré on the FIer F of the hollow load 4. Applications: metal frames, shipbuilding, etc....
