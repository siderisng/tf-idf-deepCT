<ABSTRACT>
Mixtures of linear trans-quinacridones, which are a component (I) from the series unsubstituted, chloroder methyl-substituted quinacridone and at least one component (II) from the row of the bis-carbamoyl quinacridone, wherein the carbamoyl group unsubstituted, mono- or dialkylatedis, contain pigments that are suitable for dyeing plastics and paints.Advantageously, these mixtures are obtained by common ring closure from the corresponding Dianilino terephthalic acids or esters.
