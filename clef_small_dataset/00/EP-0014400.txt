<ABSTRACT>
The cutting depth of the cutting charge is substantially dependent on the insert and the charge strength. The embossed or otherwise shaped roof or semicircular cutting charges must be produced with the utmost care and high accuracy during production. If the shape and thickness of the metal insert, the explosive occupancy and the optimum distance to the target object is not in relation, no optimal cut depths can be achieved. The aim of the invention is to provide a precise accurate insert for improving the cutting performance in cutting charges and for simplifying production and inexpensive production, in which after the explosive no remnants of the insert are present in section. Furthermore, these inserts should be able to be mounted quickly and accurately into the cutting charge housing. For this purpose, the deposits (5, 6, 10 - 17, 20-23) are at least two or more parts. wherein the inserts are pressed, rolled or pulled in any form and have any profiles.
