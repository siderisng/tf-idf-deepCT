<ABSTRACT>
This winder comprises a spindle 2 receiving the wire e to be coil and an oscillating crew 6, 8, 10 carrying a wire guide 10 and a probe 14. The latter is in contact with the winding and controls an excitation element (valve 20) on continuous operation and acting on a motor device (cylinder 27) actuating the oscillating crew.Thus, the latter moves progressively and by a continuous movement of the spindle 2 according to the growth of the coil.The probe 14 is balanced around an oscillation axis Z-Z to exert on the coil a minimum effort.Application to wire winding consisting of filaments coming out of a glassware industry.
