<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a circuit arrangement for the ground free transmission of signals via separation points in telecontrols with the aid on the primary side of the separation point signal controlled high-frequency vibrations, which are evaluated on the second of the separation point by equalization for reproducing the signals.For the secondary side equal direction, the emitter collector path of a transistor, which is connected in series with a separation transistor.This is controlled by signals which are generated by separate equality of the secondary-side alternating signals.Between the rectifier transistor and the separation transistor, a filter device is arranged, which blocks the output of the circuit against high-frequency signal components.To transmit binary signals, two separation points can be provided.
