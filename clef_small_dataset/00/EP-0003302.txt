<ABSTRACT>
1.  process for the production of dust-free plant protection agent granules, characterized in that a) a carrier (core material) is initially introduced into an open mixer optionally with a reversible direction of rotation b) a binder and a coating material, which can contain up to 100 % of a plant protection agent, and if appropriate additives are then added, whilst mixing, and c) the mixture is then dried in the open mixer optionally with a reversible direction of rotation, a gas being passed in.
