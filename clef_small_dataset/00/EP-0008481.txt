<ABSTRACT>
Device for longitudinal stretching of thermoplastic preforms having a closed bottom and an open collar, usable during their blow molding to produce hollow bodies.The device comprises a stretching rod 3 provided with a drive sleeve 4 ceasing to activate the rod rod when the thrust force exerted by this rod on the bottom of the preforms 19 exceeds a predetermined value, therollers 16 retracting.The device is usable for the production of hollow bodies such as bottles that can be bioriented.
