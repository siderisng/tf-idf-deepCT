<ABSTRACT>
When digesting raw phosphates with sulfuric acid, diluted phosphoric acid applies, which must be concentrated for fertilizer production.The concentration takes place in a plurality of vacuum evaporator stages (2, resp. 13) with separate circulation guide (3,4,5,7,9) and (3,4,6,8,9) resp.(14,15,16,17,18) of phosphoric acids.The phosphoric acids (1) resp.(12) are indirectly heated outside the evaporator.This heating should be carried out as simple as possible in conjunction with a sulfuric acid system and with extensive avoidance of the use of valuable energy.This happens by the heating by means of the heat, which results in the sulfuric acids (27) and (32) of the SO₃ absorption systems of the sulfuric acid contact system and must be dissipated from them.
