<ABSTRACT>
Train of renewal of a railway with the car transportation of a screening machine and a shinding wagon, a working car train comprising a removal wagon (5), a clearing wagon.(6) and a laying wagon (7), connected by a connecting frame (8) provided with a track chassis (11) for keeping the work cars in the selected trajectory.The removal wagon is equipped with an elevator to lift and rotate at least one cross member or span around the ballast carriers, to put it on a transport element.This train allows you to carry out all the operations of the old way, the excavation and screening of the used ballast, as well as the installation of the new way on the ballast screened without arresting its advance at the time of the removal of theold way.
