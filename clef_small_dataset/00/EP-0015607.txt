<ABSTRACT>
an ebert monochromator e. g.  for a spectrophotometer,  in which the diffraction grating (18) is positioned of the  central optical axis (17) of the collimator mirror (13) by  interpositioning a plane mirror (15) between collimator  mirror (13) and grating (18).  the plane mirror may be  movable to select one of several diffraction gratings.  by  arranging the diffraction grating about an axis (19) parallel  with the central optical axis (17) a simple mechanical  wavelength drive system with a common cam-shaft (30)  driving the grating (18), a filter holder (36) and a rotatable  slit plate, can be provided.  further gratings may be driven  from the cam-shaft (30) as may the movable plane mirror  which selects them.  
</ABSTRACT>
