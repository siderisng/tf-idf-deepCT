<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a device for the lateral clamping of parts to be machined on a particular machine tool for the clamping of difforish or high height pieces.This device is characterized in that it consists of at least one slider (1) provided with an anchoring element (6), movable in translation in the slider, under the thrust of a pressure member (10), the active part of the anchoring element (6) projecting relative to the frontal face of the slider (1), comprising at least one rigid stop zone (65) and a slightly flexible zone (6₁-6₃)
