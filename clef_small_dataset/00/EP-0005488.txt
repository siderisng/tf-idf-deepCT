<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a circuit arrangement for unlocking a keyboard storage (TS) in remote or data write machines in which the keyboard storage (TS) is locked after its full filling for further input of characters.An unlock button (ET) is pressed before entering further characters.The characters can only be entered in the keyboard storage (TS) when it was completely emptied.The circuit arrangement contains a switching stage (SS), which generates a signal (S4) to the blocking of the keyboard storage (TS) after pressing the unlocked key (ET) and after the keyboard memory (TS).
