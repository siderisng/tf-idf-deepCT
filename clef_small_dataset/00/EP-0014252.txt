<ABSTRACT>
A reconstituted dry reagent for determining the lipase, which forms an emulsion in water addition, contains O, 2 to 1O wig.-% Liquid triglyceride, 2o to 9o wig.-% protective colloid, 5 to 6o wig.-% bile acid alkalis salt, O, OO1 to O, 1 wt.-% colipase, O, 1 to 2, O wig.-% preservatives, 5 to 2o wt.-% urea, 3 to 5o wt.-% buffer substance for pH 6, O to 1O, 5 and O, 5 to 5 wt.-% activator.It can be prepared by producing an aqueous emulsion of the triglyceride which gallen acid salt, colipase, at least 1O wig.-% Protective Colloid, at least part of the preservative and optionally activator, which emulsion lyophilizes and the lyophilisate buffer substance, urea and optionally residual protective colloid and preservative and optionally further activator.
