method of manufacturing fibre-reinforced concrete structural elements and structural elements manufactured according to this method.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
For the production of fiber armed concrete, a grid-like, chain and weft threads (1, 2) existing thread plating is first made.This is wetted, for example, with pressure-sensitive adhesive and then exposed to a stream of fibers (3) which adhere to different alignment on the thread plating, whereby a multidirectional reinforcement scaffold consisting of the thread plating and the reinforcing fibers (3) adhering thereto forms.In addition to or instead of the fibers (3) attached to pressure-sensitive adhesive, a roughening of the threads of the thread contact can also be provided.The resulting reinforcement frame is incorporated into cement masses.
</ABSTRACT>
