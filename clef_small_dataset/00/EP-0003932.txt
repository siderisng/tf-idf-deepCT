<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to crosslinkable saturated compositions for powder paint providing a non-brilliant surface paint leaf.The compositions comprise a first resin / crosslinking system which, in a stoichiometric ratio of the reactive functions, gives the mechanical spectrometer a curve whose rectilinear part has a slope between 0.5.10⁵ and 10⁶ dynes / cm² / minute and a second resin / crosslinking system which in a crosslinking ratio / resin between 1.5 and 4 times the stoichiometric ratio gives the mechanical spectrometer a curve whose rectilinear part has a slope of less than 10⁴Dynes / cm² / minute.Application to paintings for metal.
