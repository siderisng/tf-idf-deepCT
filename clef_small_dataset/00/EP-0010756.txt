<ABSTRACT>
a process and apparatus for forming a nonwoven web  in which a bundle of untwisted filaments are charged  upstream of a pair of elastomer-covered counter rotating  squeeze rolls (16, 18) and propelled through the nip of the  rolls to a moving laydown belt (20) with the assistance of an  electrostatic field developed between the rolls end the belt.
