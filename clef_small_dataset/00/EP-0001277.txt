<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for producing sulfonic acid chlorides by reacting aromatic compounds with chlorosulfonic acid and phosgene, in which one first brings about stoichiometric amounts of the aromatic compound with chlorosulfonic acid, optionally in the reaction of a sulfonation aid and then converted into the presence of a catalyst with phosgene.
