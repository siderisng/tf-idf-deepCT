<ABSTRACT>
Two solution concentrates (A, B) and dilution water (W) are used. The concentrate contains at a pH below 7 hydroquinone, sulfite and sodium form maldehyde bisulfite, the other alkali, buffer substances and possibly composer former. Both concentrates are free of bromiones. The two concentrates are separated and simultaneously dosed with a variable portion of dilution of water each time when a film piece to be developed is inserted into the developer tank (11). The concentration of the regenerator mixture or the addition of the diluent water is controlled in accordance with the mass of the machine throughput, Wiebie's concentration at low machine throughput is high and vice versa.
