<ABSTRACT>
a patient lifting decive has a pivotally mounted boom  liftable by a hydraulic ram (14) which is extended by pumping  hydraulic fluid from a reservoir (18) to a ram chamber  (36) by means of a pump piston (32) reciprocable by a  pump handle (28).  for lowering of the boom the handle (28)  is moved beyond the end of the suction stroke of the piston  to depress a plunger (58) and open a return valve (48).
