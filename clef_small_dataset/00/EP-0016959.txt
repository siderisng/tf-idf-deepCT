<ABSTRACT>
In a veneer composition machine with which an adhesive charge is glued via the joint of two veneer strips, an adhesive charge coil (26) on a machine-side base plate (27) in a receiving ring (31) is releasably attached.A ring approach (28) encompassing in the receiving ring at the end of the adhesive charge coil (26) has sliding webs (29) with subsequent recesses (30) into which a machine-side locking pin (38) engages.An in the receiving ring (31) spring-mounted pressure ring (33) pushes against the ring attachment (28) from below and tenses the coil (26).
