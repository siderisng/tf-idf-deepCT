<ABSTRACT>
Process for the preparation of high rigidity thermoplastic materials of high rigidity and good stroke Toughness in the cold by reacting a) one or more organic polyisocyanates, b) optionally one or more substantially linear polyols having molecular weights between 400 and 10,000 and (c) of one or more Multiple hydroxyl groups and / or ami niogroups having chain extension means having a molecular weight below 400, wherein the ratio of the NCO groups of component a) to the cerewitinoctive groups of components b) and c) is between 0.9 and 1.2, in the presence of a Prefabricated thermoplastic polymers, preferably ABS polymer, polycarbonate or polyurethane, in extreme, characterized in that it is 4 to 65 wt. -%, based on process product, of the prefabricated thER moplastic polymer brings via a first feedstay into the extruder, then via a second and optionally further feedstocks 35 to 96 wt. -%, based on process product, components a) to c) additionally dosed on molten thermoplastic polymers and after the reaction of components a) to c) is substantially completed, the procedure is output from the extruder. The process products serve, for example, for the production of films, gears, cable coins and seals.
