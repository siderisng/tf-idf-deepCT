<TITLE>
method of bonding elastomers to fibres and an elastomer composition.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

<ABSTRACT>
the adhesion of fibres, treated with a resorcinol-formaldehyde-latex  dip, to ethylene-propylene-nonconjugated  diene elastomers is improved by the presence of an additive  in the elastomer composition.  the additive is an acid of  the formula a(cooh)x or an anhydride thereof, where x is  1 or 2, the acid groups being vicinal if x is 2, and a is phenyl,  c₂-c₁₀ alkylene, c₂-c₁₀ alkenylene, c₆-c₁₀ bridged or  nonbridged cycloalkylene or cycloalkenylene, with the proviso  that a is c₁-c₆ if x is 1.  elastomer compositions and a  method of bonding elastomers to fibres are described.  
</ABSTRACT>
