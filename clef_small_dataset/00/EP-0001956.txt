<ABSTRACT>
Guidance and control device for waterproof sliding vertical door.The door 2 is suspended by two trolleys 1 each comprises a square 6 sliding in a slide 9. On each carriage is fixed a sewer shaft 12 around which is journalled a folded plate 17 carrying the door 2, via a patella.18. The plate 17 carries a manoeuvrable roller 42 with a range 42 integral with a plate 31 jourillonning on the shaft 12 and controlled by a cylinder 35. The arrival of the roller 45 end of the raceway 28 allows a rotation ofThe plate 31 which gives the door a movement composed of descent and appaction on the wall 4. Application to the waterproof doors, especially for cold rooms, operating blocks, intercirculation in the air-conditioned trains.
