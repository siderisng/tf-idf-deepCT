<ABSTRACT>
A new erection member (9), in particular for paint pins for repairing paint damage to force vehicles, is described, which is provided in the manner of a blower on the wheel with shovels (12) provided on the circumference for the axial direction.This erection organ surrounds the brush (6) of the lacquer pin with a tubular inner part (10) and can pass the paint fluid into a rotating mixing movement by axial shelf of the container.
