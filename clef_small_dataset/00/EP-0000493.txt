<ABSTRACT>
Process for the preparation of H-acid monoalkalic acids by reacting T-acid and / or salts of T-acid and / or isomeric mixtures, the T-acid and / or their salts contain with alkali hydroxide solution at elevated pressure and elevated temperature and deposition of H- Acid monoalkalic salts by acidifying, whereby the transplexity is carried out in the presence of alcohols and / or alcoholates.The method provides high yield, few by-products are formed and the H- acid monoalkalis salt is in a simple manner in particular isolate the pure form.
