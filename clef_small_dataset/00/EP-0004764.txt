<ABSTRACT>
a rotary motion transducer for generating strobe signals  includes a first conductive surface (40) substantially parallel  to the motion, and an electret disk (34) permanently polarized  in a spoked configuration in a direction perpendicular  to the motion to provide a selective flux pattern and secured  to the first conductive surface, a second conductive surface  (30) having a spoked conductor pattern lying in a plane  adjacent the electret disk, such that relative rotation between  the conductive surfaces generates strobe signals between  them by electrostatic induction of the electric charges  trapped in the electret.  a linear motion transducer of similar  construction using a comb configuration for the electret and  second conductive surface is also described.  
</ABSTRACT>
