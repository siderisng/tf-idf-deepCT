<ABSTRACT>
In a transfer machine with circular installation movement for the workpieces successively machining in individual machining stations (I to V), a stepwise continuous workpiece carrier (10) is mounted on a center column (8) on which clamping devices (11) for the workpieces are arranged. In order to be able to absorb high machining forces at the machining stations (I to V) without the risk of deterioration of the machining accuracy and to achieve good accessibility of the workpieces and the tools of the machining units (14, 15, 16), the transfer machine has a rigid cage (1 ) with two wall parts opposite, rigidly interconnected wall parts (2, 3), wherein on the upper wall portion (3) of the workpiece carrier (10) arranged inside the cage (1) is rotatably mounted and the cage (1) with The lower wall portion (2) is placed on the center column (8), as well as the machining units (14, 15, 16) in the regions of the individual processing stations in the cage (1) are inserted.
