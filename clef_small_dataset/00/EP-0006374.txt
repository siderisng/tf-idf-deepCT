<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the field of hand tools for cutting of various material tubes.The chain tube cutter consists of a tensioning device formed of a deformable system comprising a cylindrical rod (1) made integral with a support (3), and a slider (8) on the rod (1).The support carries hinged returns related to the bolt by two rods (26), the lower part being in the form of a hook (40) for the attachment of the chain.The cylindrical rod (1) cooperates with a sleeve (14) by means of a thread (16), the handle being consisting of two cylinder curing cylin superimposed and comprising four bosses (29) forming a cross.
