<ABSTRACT>
Dental materials based on organic plastics in pastoeilized form, in which at least one phase consists of fine-distributed, crosslinked plastics containing at least 50% copolymerized esters of methacrylic acid, and the second phase is at least 50% of esters of methacrylic acid.These pastes, especially those in which the finely divided, crosslinked plastics are polymeric beads with a mean particle sizes of 10 - 100 μ, are excellent as tooth filling material.
