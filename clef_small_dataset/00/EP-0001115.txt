<ABSTRACT>
The new compound 2-bromomergosine is obtained according to selective bromination of the natural alkaloid ergosin in 2-position and serves, optionally in the form of its acid addition salt, as an enteral and parenterally applicable medicament for the treatment of arterial hypertensions and heart-arhythmias.
