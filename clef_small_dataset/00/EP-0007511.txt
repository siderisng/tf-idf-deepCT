<ABSTRACT>
a strip-shaped louvre having two lateral reflectors and a plurality of transverse strips, having v-shaped extending limbs, arranged therebetween.  in known louvres of this type, the end edges of the transverse strips have a shape which corresponds to the shape of the lateral reflectors.  the connection of lateral reflectors and transverse strips is served by bending lugs projecting therefrom, which are plugged through slots in the lateral reflectors and are bent round behind the latter.  it is the object of the invention to simplify this complicated mode of installation.  according to the invention, this is achieved by a combination of spring lugs (314, 316) in the transverse strips (3) and stops (312, 12) in the lateral reflectors (1, 2) in accordance with the main claim, the result being that the installation is reduced to a simple plugging together.
