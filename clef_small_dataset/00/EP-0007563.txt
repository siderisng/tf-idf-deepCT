<ABSTRACT>
a pile yarn for a carpet and a cut pile carpet.  a bulky yarn  of a polyamide multifilament is fed to a false twisting and heat  setting device where filaments in the yarn are thermally and  partially adhered to each other while the yarn is false twisted.   a bulky cohesive continuous multifilament yarn thus  obtained has alternate twists therein along the lengthwise  direction thereof and a latent torque therein, and after it is  tufted on a substrate (12) of a carpet as a pile yarn and after the  piles are cut, the carpet is heat treated, preferably by means of  saturated or superheated steam.  the torque in the cut piles  (11) is developed to create true twists.  
</ABSTRACT>
