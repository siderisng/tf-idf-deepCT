<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a pump control device, in particular for membraneless pneumatic pumps, which comprises fluid level detectors, such as in particular contractors / magnetic switches cooperating with a magnetic float, provided for controlling the operation offilling and refoulement of the pumped fluid in, respectively out of the body of the pump;The device can in particular comprise (at least) three level detectors and further a device for delaying the fill and or discharge of the pump, at least one of said detectors being adapted to control the delay device and the other detects beingplanned to control the filling and discharge operation itself.
