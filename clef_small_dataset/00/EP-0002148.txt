<ABSTRACT>
Compositions containing at least one aminoalkoxyamine of formula: wherein X is an integer between 1 and 3. They are prepared by cyanoethylation of the tris (hydroxy-5 oxa-3 pentyl) amine by acrylonitrile, then hydrogenation.They can be used as intermediates for manufacturing additives for lubricating oils and in the synthesis of polyurethanes.These compositions include NNN Tris (amino-9 dioxa-3,6 nonyl) amine.
