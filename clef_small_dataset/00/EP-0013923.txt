<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to room temperature liquid or paste-shaped or by heating to a maximum of 80 ° C liqueficable suspensions of isocyanateous thirty acids based on aromatic diisocyanates in isocyanate prepolymers based on organic diisocyanates and organic polyhydroxyl compounds of the molecular weight range 500 to 8000;a method for producing these suspensions, in which one mixes aromatic diisocyanates with isocyanate prepolymers of said type and then brings the mixture to 0.4 to 0.8 mol of water per mole of the aromatic diisocyanate or a corresponding amount of water-draining compound;and the use of the suspensions as an isocyanate component in the production of polyurethane imsfs.
