<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an amplifier distributed for microwave.The amplifier comprises two lines of input and output transmission I and II connected by a plurality of active quadrupole arranged in parallel on these lines, in which are inserted the elements necessary for the adaption of the quadruples.In a solid state embodiment, the active quadruposes, T₁, T₂, T₃, T₄, are formed in a substrate in arsenide of gallium 1 and the lines carried out by the technique "microbend", by metal deposits on the substrate 20, 22, 24, 26. The adaptation elements comprise inductance I₁ to i₄, between which are arranged C₁ to C₃ coupling capabilities.The device allows a broad band of operation.Application to X-band amplification devices.
