Information transmission device whose propagation zones of the information signals are independent of the sections of the cantonment system.A generator 11 of alternative signals modulated in 12 by the signals 13 to be transmitted is coupled to the rail lines 1 and 2 by induction, by means of a SPIRE 6 downstream of the zone.Each information transmission zone is delimited upstream and downstream by resonant circuits 4 and 5 granted on the carrier frequency of the transmitted signals.This frequency is chosen so as to avoid interaction between the channel circuits and the information transmission device.application to rail traffic.
