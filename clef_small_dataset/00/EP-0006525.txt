<ABSTRACT>
Fire protection material based on alkali metal silica cats with a water content of 20 to 60% by weight percent.The fire protection material contains for improvement in aging resistance 1 to 10 weight percent of a borate or aluminate, preferably sodium aluminate.The fire protection material can be used to isolate openings and passages to building bodies against the passage of fire and smoke, as well as a fire protective glass.
