<ABSTRACT>
Diaminophenylureas of the formula in which R represents an inert substituent are obtained from the corresponding dinitrophenylurea by reduk tion, in particular catalytic hydrogenation.By ring closure under ammonia discharge, the corresponding aminobenzimidazolones are obtained, advantageous without intermediate insulation of the diaminophenylureas.
