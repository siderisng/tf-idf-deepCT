<ABSTRACT>
Water-soluble, methylol groups having NCO groups - free oligour ethanes with a molecular weight between 600 and 5000 based on a) polyethylene glycol ethers from the molecular weight 400 to 1500, b) polyisocyanates, optionally c) to isocyanates monofunctional or polyfunctional compounds having a molecular weight under 300 andd) formaldehyde, characterized in that the proportion of component a) in oligour ethane 40 to 90 wt.% and that components a) and / or c) have a total of between 20 and 200 mval per 100 g of oligourethane solids on tertiary nitrogen atoms, of which 20 to 95% by treatment with alkylating agents and / or acids in quaternary ammonium nitrogen or in theAmmonium salt form were transferred.
