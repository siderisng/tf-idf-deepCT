<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for producing mixtures of polyamines of the polyamino-polyaryl polymethylene series, wherein the proportion of the O-substituted products is significantly increased.This increase is achieved by the acid catalyst added to the reaction mixture of aromatic amine and formaldehyde in two or more stages.
