<ABSTRACT>
Device for carrying a liquid at a given temperature.The device comprises: - inside a vertical cylindrical speaker, a vertical tube, in which is placed a heating resistor, a first thin annular space of the liquid ascending circulation, a second annular descending circulation space of the liquid, aThird Annular Ascending Circulation Space, - Liquid Power Means at the bottom of the first annular space and liquid extraction means to the upper part of the third annular space, - means for controlling and maintaining constantThe temperature at which said liquid comes out of said device.Application to heating each liquid phases incorporating into a liquid-liquid extraction column.
