<ABSTRACT>
Toothpaste based on a per se, corroding aluminum surfaces, which as anti-corrosive addition 0.05 to 5.0 wt.% of zeolite-type synthetic alkali aluminum silicate, in particular a compound of general formula X (M · alO₂) · YSIO₂ · ZH₂O, where M alkali or ammonium, x a number between 1 and 64, y is one of x dependent number with the proviso,that it contains one to six times of X and Z a number between 0 and 256.
