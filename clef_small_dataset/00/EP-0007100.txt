<ABSTRACT>
The present patent application claims an improved method for producing low molecular weight polyal alcohols by alkaline two-stage hydrogenation of a mixture of low molecular weight hydroxydehyde, hydroxycetones and polyvalent alcohols, as producing the self-condensation of formaldehyde.This hydrogenation in the presence of metal catalysts is carried out in two stages at a pH of 7.5 to 12.5 each so that the content of reducible groups in the reactor is the value of 2 wt.-% Do not exceed and that the solution is adjusted to a pH of 1 to 6 after the first hydrogenation step and then alkaline is adjusted alkaline for the second hydrogenation step.The patent application further claims the use of the process preparations according to the invention for the production of polyurethane imsfs, as a start component for polyether and polyester polyols and for nonionic surfactants.
