<ABSTRACT>
Fardening comprising several superimposed layers of unit loads and the Mouls a link belting all the layers.It is provided in the base layer at least one free space (4) which has a predetermined width and an elongated shape in a direction parallel to the axis of the loop formed by the link (3), and which is overlapped transversely.by said link.When supporting the burden (1) by the fork (5) of a forklift, the link (3) is extended by the fork (5) and when the burden (1) is then deposited on the top ofAnother burden, the link (3) relaxes so that the unit charges (2) can resume their initial form and that the burdens can be stalled on the other.
