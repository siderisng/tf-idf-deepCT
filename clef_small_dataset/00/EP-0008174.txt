<ABSTRACT>
a method of making an organic metal salt or complex  is disclosed by reacting a heavy metal hydroxide with an  organic lewis acid or salt thereof in the presence of a form  of co₂.  the reaction may be controlled by the addition of a  buffering agent.  the method produces organic metal com­ plexes or salts in a pure second salt-free state either in a  solution or solid state.
