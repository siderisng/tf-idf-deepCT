1.  connector for two intersecting rails, namely a crossing rail (1) and a retaining rail (2), of which at least the crossing rail (1) has a u-shaped section and is bent diagonally inwards on the longitudinal edges (11), the connector being in the from of a u-shaped clamp, the central piece (31) of which covers the retaining rail (2) and the sides of which (32) have al length greater than the height of the retaining rail (2), characterized in that the u-shaped clamp has a width substantially equal to the clear width of the crossing rail (1), and that each side (32) is provided on each lateral edge with an oblique notch (36) in which the bent part (12) of the longitudinal edge (11) of the crossing rail (1) engages.
