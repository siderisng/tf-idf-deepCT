<ABSTRACT>
The removable closing device, to be used during the hydraulic pressure test, comprises a body (4) in the inside of the tank on an inner portion (10) thereof resistant to the thrust forces,When pressing, a means (25) realizing the meage between the inside and the outside of the tank mounted on this body (4) and an organ (30-36) for the attachment or, on the contrary., disassembly of the device, maneuverable from outside the tank.The invention applies in particular to pressurized water nuclear reactors.
