<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for producing 2,2-dimethylolal channels by reacting aldehydes with excess formaldehyde in the presence of bases such as hydroxides and / or carbonates of the alkali and / or alkaline earth metal and / or unbranched tertiary amines.By hydrogenation of 2,2-dimethylolalkanals, the corresponding trimethylolalkanes can be prepared, which are intermediates of technical meaning for the production of plasticizers, paint raw materials, polyesters and polyurethanes.2,2-dimethylolalkanals are also required for the production of dimethylolcarboxylic acids, for dyes and pesticides.
