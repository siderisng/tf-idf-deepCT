<ABSTRACT>
in a method and a device for synchronizing with the  on-board timing of a regenerative satellite information  symbols regenerated with the timing of the emitting earth  stations, in time-division multiplex-access digital transmissions,  there is effected a frequency conversion, so as to bring  to the nominal on-board operation frequency the frequency  of the regenerated signal, and a phase correction at the  transitions between adjacent bursts, so as to eliminate phase  discontinuities in the symbol sequence sent to the on-board  processing device.
