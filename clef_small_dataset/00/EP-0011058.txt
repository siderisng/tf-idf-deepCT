<ABSTRACT>
the article, made of ferrous metal, preferably a band or strip, receives a coating of an aqueous suspension of cement on the surface intended to be protected from the galvanisation and is then dried, heated to a temperature above the melting point of zinc and moves into the bath of molten zinc.  it finally undergoes a cleaning stage to remove the cement deposit forming the mask and, optionally a final rinsing.  the articles obtained in this way may be steel bands galvanised on only one face, intended for use in the motor vehicle industry.  <image>
