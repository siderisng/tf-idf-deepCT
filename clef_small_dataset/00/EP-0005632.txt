<ABSTRACT>
a coating composition which may be plasma sprayed  onto the surface of an article to protect the article from heat,  wear, corrosion or radiation comprises a mixture of finely  divided silicon carbide and finely divided silicon.  this  coating composition is applied to a substrate by plasma  spraying.  the resulting coating, consisting essentially of beta  silicon carbide and silicon, is tightly adherent to the substrate.   the substrate may be any material, either metallic or  non-metallic, to which the coating adheres.  examples of  possible substrates are machining tools, valve seats and  rocket nozzles.
