<ABSTRACT>
Bee-nest assembly assembly used in particular in the aeronautical construction, COM carrying a substantially tubular set for being placed in an opening in the honeycomb panel;It is crossed by an assembly member such as screws, spindle or other.The tubu lair assembly comprises a sleeve (2) which is placed in the opening of the panel and which is connected to the skins therein by platelets (3) and (4) which are pasted on the outer faces of the panel.The platelets provide the transplant effort transversion, exerted on the assembly device, on the skin of the bee-nest panel.
