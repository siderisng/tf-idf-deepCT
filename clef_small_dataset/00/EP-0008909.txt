a hydraulic master cylinder (11) and brake valve assem­ bly (12) for sequential operation of a friction clutch (16) and a  friction brake (17) for a drive member driven through the  friction clutch, is arranged such that up to a first given dis­ placement of the master cylinder piston (24) the clutch  release mechanism only is connected to the master cylinder  (11) and, above a second given displacement of the piston  (24) the brake actuator only is connected to the master cylin­ der (11), there being a step change in thrust applied to the  master cylinder piston (24) between said first and second  given displacements.  such an assembly is particularly applicable for operating  heavy road vehicle clutch brakes.
