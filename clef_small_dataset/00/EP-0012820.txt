<ABSTRACT>
Liquid-crystalline carboxylic acid esters of the formula (I) wherein one of the rings A and B is a phenyl ring and the other a cyclohexan ring, and R₁ and R₂ alkyl with 1 - 10 C atoms are liquid-crystalline dielectrics for lowering the dielectric anisotropy.At the same time, the temperature range of the nemetic phase of these dielectrics is usually widened.Dielectrics prepared with the liquid crystalline carboxylic acid esters of the formula (I) with negative dielectric anisotropy advantageously find use in dynamically scattering liquid crystal display elements.
