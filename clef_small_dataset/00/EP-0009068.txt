<ABSTRACT>
Abrasion-resistant fluid bed catalyst for the production of synthetic natural gas by co-methanization.The output substances (nickel oxide, carrier material, binders) are pressed in moistened state at more than 1600 kg / cm 2 to form bodies which are calcined, cured, calcined and crushed.From the crushed material, a particle fraction of about 40 to 350 μm is obtained.
