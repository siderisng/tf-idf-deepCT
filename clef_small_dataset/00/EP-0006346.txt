<ABSTRACT>
aqueous polytetrafluoroethylene dispersions obtained  by dispersion polymerization and having high solids con­ tent and small polytetrafluoroethylene particles are ob­ tained by employing, as initiators, disuccinic acid peroxide  and an inorganic persulfate, and, as dispersing agents, an  anionic surfactant and a nonionic hydrocarbon surfactant.   these initiators and dispersing agents being precharged  to the polymerization mixture, and by adding a mixture of  disuccinic acid peroxide and an anionic surfactant during  the course of the polymerization.
