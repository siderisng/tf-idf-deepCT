<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to polystyrose polystyrose polydiorganosiloxan block copolymers.These copolymers are obtained by reacting a α-Ω-dihydrogenopolysiloxane on a polystyrunic polymer completed at each end with vinyldiorgano silyles radicals, the preceding polymer being itself obtained by reaction of vinyldorganochlorosilane on a polystyrenic living polymer completed at each end ofChain by alkali metal atoms and obtained from possible styrene substituted.These copolymers are thermoplastic elastomers having good resistance to temperature.
