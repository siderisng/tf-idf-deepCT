<ABSTRACT>
sulfenamide derivatives of the general formula    wherein r₁ - r₅ are h or various substituents and z is ccl₃,  ccl₂f, ccl₂ccl₂h or ccl₂cfcl₂, are useful for the control of  mites, fungus diseases of plants and insect eggs.  they can be  made by reacting a corresponding diphenylamine with a sul­ fenyl chloride of formula clsz.  the derivatives can be formu­ lated in conventional manner and applied in an effective  amount to the locus to be protected.
