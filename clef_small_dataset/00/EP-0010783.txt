<ABSTRACT>
according to the method of the invention two of the  samples to be considered are alternately fed and not fed  to the detection device.  in consequence of this fewer  interference products are obtained, so that the processing  time can remain relatively short.  moreover the mfc-­ samples of two channels can be processed simultaneous­ ly.  a device examines on a time division basis the  signalling time slots of two pcm-frames.  under the con­ trol of a microprocessor circuit (27) the samples of the  input control circuit (28) and the samples of a reference  series buffer (29) are correlated with one another.  the  correlation results are fed to accumulators (30, 33).  under  the control of a second microprocessor circuit (40) the  correlation results are processed and transferred to the  equipment that follows.
