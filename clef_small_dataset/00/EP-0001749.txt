<ABSTRACT>
In a central-controlled federal system, stationary control rates of transmitting and receiving stations are connected to a multi-core address line connected to a central program controller.All tax rates are connected to an operation decoder to a multi-core control line connected to the program controller.Upon submission of a target information transmission initiating operation information from the program steering device to the control line, the operation decoder of the selected transmitting station addresses and sets signal voltage to a target selector connected to the address line parallel to an address decoder to the address line.The control can be used in particular in pipe mail systems.
