<ABSTRACT>
Apparatus for establishing temporary contacts on electrical circuits, such as thick layer circuits or printed circuits, which are desired to control or desire to adjust the components, such as resistors.The apparatus comprises at least two key tips (5), support brackets (7) respectively carrying the key tips, the respective displacement units controlled by program to move the brackets so that the keynuts canOccupy any desired position in a plane parallel to that of the circuit measuring, and means (13-20) to move the brackets between a high position and a low position in which the tips are in contact with the electrical circuit(g).
