<ABSTRACT>
For the production of sheets or bands with surface of the catalyst structure, a mixture of powder shaped support skeleton material, such as iron, nickel or cobalt, and powdered Raney alloy, z. b. Based on iron, nickel, cobalt or silver, on which made of gang plate or tape sintered, sintered at temperatures above 600 ° C in a reducing atmosphere and the soluble component of the Raney alloy is dissolved with lye or acid. In order to achieve sufficient adhesion of the surface catalyst structure, the winding of the aforementioned mixture takes place with simultaneous cold deformation of the sheet or tape with a deformity degree of 20 to 60% within a deformation stage. The layer thickness of the surface catalyst structure should be 10 to 300 μ. The coated sheets or bands or given spherical bodies produced therefrom serve as the elek trode in the electrolysis, z. b. The chloroalkalic or water livotary and the production of salts of chlorosauer substance acids, or as a catalyst at chemical redoxpro.
