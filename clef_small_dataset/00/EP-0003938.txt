<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a high degree of safety container applicable in particular to the storage of hazardous products or the protection of hazardous or delicate installations.This container comprises a tank (6, 9, 13) which rests on a foundation (1, 5) formed a space (7) under said tank.The foundation continues with an outer wall (3) delimiting around the tank an annular space (10) which can be garnished with a protective product (12).
