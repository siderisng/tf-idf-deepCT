<ABSTRACT>
a partition head assembly for a partition wall panel is  characterized by a telescoping head (14) in the top of the  partition panel (12) and a strut (16) at at least one end of  the head which is adjustably vertically secured to the parti­ tion panel (12) at all vertical positions thereof by a strut  retaining clip (18).  vertical adjustment of the head assem­ bly (10) may be obtained without dis-assembly thereof and  with the panel (12) assembled in a partition wall.
