<ABSTRACT>
Bath guidance device for a bath (1) for the electroless deposition of copper, wherein in addition to a copper salt, at least formaldehyde and sodium hydroxide solutions are used as other components.The concentrations of each component should be regulated to constant values.This is done by removing a sample for each of said components (2) and diluted them with a certain amount of water, then independently of each other, the copper ion concentration (CCU) by colorimetry (6-12), the sodium leach concentration (NaOH) by potentiometric (16-20) and the formaldehyde concentration (CCH₂O) are determined by amperometric titration (26-32).
</ABSTRACT>
