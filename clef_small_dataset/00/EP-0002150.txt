<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a disc brake in which the replacement of the friction members (16 and 22) is carried out through an opening (26) formed in a caliper (10) overlapping the disk (20).a key (32), arranged between the friction members (16 and 22) and one of the edges (30) of the opening, is capable of being moved parallel to the axis of the disc (20) of aNormal operating position to a disassembly position in which notches (36, 34) formed in the key (32) are brought to the friction members (16 and 22) to allow their replacement.Application to the braking of motor vehicles.
