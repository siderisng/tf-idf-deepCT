<ABSTRACT>
Entrance, on the constant voltage, of a material tape occurring from a power supply to a receiving coil.The drive device according to the invention comprises a first inextensible belt (41) in contact with the periphery of a receiving coil (13) receiving the ribbon (11).The first belt (41) is driven at a constant speed by a motor pulley (40) and dragging without sliding the receiving coil (13).The first belt (41) transmits its movement, via transmission means (49, 51, 60, 61), to a second inextensible belt (52), which is caused at a constant speed slightly lower than that ofThe first belt.The belt (52) is in contact with the periphery of the feed coil (12) and the draught entrain.Applicable in particular to the drive of ribbon coils in a printer.
