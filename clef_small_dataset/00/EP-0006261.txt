<ABSTRACT>
A shredder for bulky and / or stubborn parts to be destroyed comprises two trees (2 and 3) carrying shredding crowns (4) arranged with a longitudinally alternately alternately alternately frame.These crowns are formed of segments (8, 10), at least a portion of which are shredding segments with shredding spouts (9).These shredding segments have a profile of U reversed their gift from a shape of a clevis and are attached to the tree by cable on collets integral with the shaft.Screws, passing through precision holes of the flanks of the segments and collets, securely attach the segments to the collar and therefore on the shaft.
