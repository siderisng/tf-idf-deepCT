<ABSTRACT>
New shell catalysts and methods for their preparation by atomizing mixtures of water and vanadium V compounds and optionally further metal compounds and order on itself in self-movement carrier particles, drying and repeated short-term treatment of the catalysts located in self-movement with flames or plasma and rapid coolingunder certain conditions of temperature and order rate.The shell catalysts thus obtained are advantageously used for the production of anthraquinones by oxidation of Indanas.
