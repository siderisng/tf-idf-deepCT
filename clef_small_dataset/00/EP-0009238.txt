<TITLE>
low-temperature impact-resistant moulding compositions from a mixture of polypropylene-polyisobutylene-polyethylene, and their use in mouldings.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>
basf agde<sep>basf aktiengesellschaft<sep>basf aktiengesellschaftcarl-bosch-strasse 3867063 ludwigshafende<sep>
</APPLICANT>
<INVENTOR>
moorwessel dieter dr de<sep>pfirrmann guenther dr de  <sep>zeitler gerhard drde<sep>moorwessel, dieter, dr.<sep>pfirrmann, guenther, dr.<sep>zeitler, gerhard, dr.<sep>moorwessel, dieter, dr.dilsberger strasse 3-5d-6800 mannheim 51de<sep>pfirrmann, guenther, dr.osloer weg 38d-6700 ludwigshafende<sep>zeitler, gerhard, dr.lessingstrasse 11d-6711 hessheimde<sep>
</INVENTOR>
<ABSTRACT>
moulding compositions which comprise from 50 to 90 parts by weight of polypropylene having a density of from 0. 80 to 0. 90 g/cm<3> and a melt index of from 0. 1 to 20 g/10 min, from 5 to 40 parts by weight of polyisobutylene having a molecular weight mv of from 300,000 to 4,000,000, from 5 to 40 parts by weight of polyethylene (density = 0. 930 to 0. 965 g/cm<3>, mfi = 0. 01 to 20 g/10 min) and, if desired, up to 30 parts by weight of conventional fillers, and the use of the above moulding compositions for low-temperature impact-resistant mouldings.  the moulding compositions can be processed by injection moulding to give mouldings such as, for example, automotive bumpers. 
</ABSTRACT>
