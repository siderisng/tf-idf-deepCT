<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to high molecular weight products based on N-vinylpyrrolidone in which blocks of vinylpyrrolidone polymers or vinylpyrrolidone copolymers are linked by ester, amide, urethane or urea groups supporting intermediate members, whereby these high molecular weight products are biodegradable and are suitable for hemorrhage liquids,as well as such hemorrhage liquids.
