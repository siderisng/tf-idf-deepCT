electrical connector provided with an ejector for disengaging the connector.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
an electrical connector (10) for attaching an electrical  cord (12) to an electrical appliance (11) is provided.  the connector  (10) has an ejector (21) which allows for one hand  disengagement of the connector (10) and cord (12) from the  appliance (11).  the ejector (21) has a first portion movable through and  out of the connector (10) and an integral hinge shaped second  portion.  by application of finger pressure on the second portion  to flatten it, the first portion is moved out of the connector  (10) and against the appliance (11) causing an oppositely  directed movement of the connector (10) thereby disengaging  the connector (10) and cord (12) from the appliance (11).  
</ABSTRACT>
