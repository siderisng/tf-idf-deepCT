<ABSTRACT>
Method for producing a high-precision and dimensionally stable analyzer system for a multipole mass filter in which a tube (7) is pulled out of electrically conductive, thermally softening material over a mask-protective mold core (1) with higher expansion coefficients and parallel grooves (3), whereupon the Pipe material is snapped by heating the grooves (3) of the mold core (1) and then subtracted from the mold core (1) after solidifying cooling with the embossed pipe addictions (9). Before heating the tube (7), placed between the mold core (1) and tube (7) in the region of the grooves (3) layers (5) made of electrically well conductive, slightly connectable with the softening tubular material slightly connectable metallic components. The laying material is connected to the tube material when softening and tightening the tube (7) to the grooves (3). When removing the so shaped tube (7), the layers connected to the embossed pipe addictions (9) are withdrawn from the mold core (1). A analyzer system prepared by the method according to the invention is in FIG. 2 to 4 shown.
