<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to carbon supports for electrothermal reduction processes that extend to form a slag.These carbon supports contain 5 to 80% by weight of the slag incurred in the relevant reduction process for increasing their reactivity.Another subject of the invention is a Ver driving for the preparation of such carbon carriers.
