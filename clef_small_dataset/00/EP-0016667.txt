<ABSTRACT>
Electrolyser constituted by stacking element cells said stacking being placed in a horizontal tubular speaker.The enclosure is constituted by a vertical and fixed base plate (2) and a tubular casing (1) mounted on the horizontal base (8) of the apparatus via rollers (7).The body of stacking (4) is supported during the opening operation by holds (12) and by rolling rollers (6).The invention finds its use for the production of hydrogen and oxygen using Indus trial electroleys.
