<ABSTRACT>
Improvement with trailer coupling devices with inertia control bracket, the type of those with stabilizing bars whose tensioning generates a torque to balance the forces applied to the towing vehicle and comprising a device canceling the reaction force applied to the levels of the control slider by inertia of the brakes of the trailer by creating an ascending antagonist force applied to said bearings, characterized in that this antagonistic force is made by means of two rods (11) elastically deformable, articulated freely on a common axis (12) at the end of the arrow (F) of the trailer and whose free end of each of them, normally arched down, is in permanent elastic support on the stabilizer bar ( 3) correspondent of the hitch, means being provided to keep the rods on the bars during the traction, the turning and Braking.
