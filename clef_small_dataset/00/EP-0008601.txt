<ABSTRACT>
apparatus for converting materials such as garbage con­ tained in city waste into compost.  the apparatus includes a  rotary stirring unit (9, 39) above each one of a plurality of  horizontal floors (19, 29).  the stirring unit itself rotates while  the garbage material is discharged onto the upper horizontal  floor, and the garbage material is gradually stirred and trans­ ferred from the upper floor to the lower floor.
