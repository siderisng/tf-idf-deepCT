<ABSTRACT>
Graft mixing polymers made of a crosslinked, saturated, rubber-elastic graft base and a hard shell are prepared by incorporating the graft base during the production of the graft base, in the range of 50 to 90% thermolabile comonomers.In the subsequent grafting of the shell such conditions are used that the thermobody comonomers built up in the pinpoint basis are decomposed into radicals.This leads to graft mixing polymers with improved mechanical properties, which therefore advantageously also proven in the production of mixtures with other thermoplasts, as far as they are compatible with the graft mixing polymers.
</ABSTRACT>
