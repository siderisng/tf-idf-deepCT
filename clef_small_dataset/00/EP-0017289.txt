an optical recording device having a substrate plate  which is provided on at least one side with a first ablative  recording layer and a second ablative recording layer  provided hereon, the second ablative recording layer having  a higher surface energy than the first ablative recording  layer.  both recording layers preferably comprise the same  common metal, for example tellurium.
