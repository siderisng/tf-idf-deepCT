<ABSTRACT>
the fitting has a disc which is secured against the wheel hub, with a locking handle mounted across its diameter.  inside the disc is a spiral recess, and radial guides projecting from it carry sliders and their extension arms.  each extension arm carries a curved strap which curves around the outside of the tyre, fitting over the tread.  there are at least five equally spaced straps and respective arms.  the handle is swung downwards from a disengaged position to its clamping position across the disc over the hub. 
</ABSTRACT>
