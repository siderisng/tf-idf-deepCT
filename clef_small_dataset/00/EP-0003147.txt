<ABSTRACT>
Method for separating hydrogen fluoride from its mixtures with 1-chloro-1, 1-diflunethane such as those obtained during the manufacture of the latter.The mixture is added to a third solvent selected from 1,1-dichloro-1-fluorethane, vinylidene chloride, 1,1,1-trichlorethane, and mixtures so as to obtain two separate liquid phases containing oneHydrogen fluoride and the other 1-chloro-1,1-diflunethane.Virtually all fluoride is recovered hydrogen in anhydrous form during the manufacture of monomers such as vinylidene fluoride from chlorinated hydrocarbons such as vinylidene chloride and 1,1,1-trichlorethane.
