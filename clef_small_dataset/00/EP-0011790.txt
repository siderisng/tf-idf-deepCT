The invention relates to a method for producing nitrodiphenylamine derivatives, in which an optionally substituted nitrophenol is reacted with an optionally sub stuben aromatic isocyanate in the presence of a base in the temperature range of 100 to 250 ° C in tetramethylene sulfone.
