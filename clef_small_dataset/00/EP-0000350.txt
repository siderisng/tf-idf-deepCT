<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to polycarbodiimide modified polysiloxanes in which polysiloxane and polycarbodiimide are present as distinctive phases - with chemical and / or physical binding.The polycarbodiimide-modified polysiloxanes are prepared by mixing the organopolysiloxane with di- or polyisocyanates in the presence of a catalyst accelerating the carbodiimide formation.The polymer mixture is 3 to 80 wt.% - Related to the total mixture - from polycarbodiimide.
