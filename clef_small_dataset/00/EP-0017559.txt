<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a pressure gas phase hydrocarbon nitration reactor.The reactor is constituted by a reaction enclosure within which a tubular beam is in contact with a heat transfer fluid of high heat exchange capacity.The reactor is advantageously used in the nitration of saturated hydrocarbons lower than C₅, only or mixed.
