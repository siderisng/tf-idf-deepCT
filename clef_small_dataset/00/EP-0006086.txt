<ABSTRACT>
the air pressure tank, wherein stored air (1) is under the pressure of a water column, is used as an energy tank.  the pressure air may, for example, by means of a compressor (6), be pumped into the tank for actuating a turbine or similar, as needed.  the pressure air (1) is within a moveable tank (3) in elevation under the water, which is open at the bottom and in which, from the bottom, a body (20) engages.
