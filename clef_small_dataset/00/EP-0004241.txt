<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a junction device between a transport container provided with a plug (2) at its superior part and a horizontal wall (19) of an unloading chamber.The wall (19) of the enclosure is equipped with a horizontal orifice at its lower part located above the container.The device is characterized in that on the one hand the container is provided at its upper part of an adapter ring (15) to the wall and a second protection crown (16) of the cap (2), andIn that, on the other hand, the orifice is closed by a removable operculum (17) comprising means of prehension (22) and resting on a seat (18) at the wall of the unloading chamber.Application, including the unloading of lead containers in which nuclear fuel elements were loaded.
