<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to novel carbazolyl (4) oxpropanolamine derivatives, methods for their preparation and these compounds containing medicaments.The preparation of the compounds according to the invention is carried out either by reacting 4-propoxy carbazole derivatives with substituted alkylamines or by reaction of 4- (3-aminopropoxy) carbazole derivatives with reactive substituted ethane derivatives, aldehydes or ketones or carboxylic acid derivatives.
