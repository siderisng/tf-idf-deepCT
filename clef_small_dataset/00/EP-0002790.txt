<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a measuring device for a frequency analysis of signal levels within a large dynamic range that should be extremely fast.For this purpose, the measuring device has an intermediate frequency amplifier arrangement with two and more selective amplifiers (SV1, SV2, SV3), each equipped with an automatic reinforcing control.The broadband control loop essentially contains a controllable damping member (PR1, PR2, PR3) and a control amplifier (RV1, RV2, RV3).The bandwidth of the selective amplifiers connected in succession is gradually reduced in the direction of the signal flow.The controlled variables of the control amplifiers are summed up in an adder (AD) and supplied to a signal level evaluation and display device (AA).
