<ABSTRACT>
transducer head to data track alignment is corrected in a  helical scan rotating head magnetic tape unit by displacing  the head in a direction perpendicular to the direction of rotation  of the head under the control of an error signal indicative  of misalignment between the head and a desired data track.   the error signal is generated in response to the sensing by the  head of a longitudinal servo track record defining the locations  of oblique data tracks.  displacement of the head is  effected by mounting the head on a piezoelectric actuator  controlled by the error signal.  by successively sensing a series of longitudinal servo  track records on the tape, alignment of the head with an  oblique data track is repeatedly corrected as the head scans  the data track.  in this manner the head is caused to follow a  skewed of deformed data track.
