<ABSTRACT>
Method and apparatus for discharging molten phosphor pentasulfide via an overflow (6) from a reactor (1) in which phosphorus is reacted with sulfur at temperatures above 300 ° C.In this case, the molten reaction product is filled directly in the filling container (7) defined volume contents by dimensioning the metering vessels (3, 5) so that both the amounts of phosphorus and sulfur in each case correspond to the p: S ratio desired in the end productand their total volume content is equal to the volume contents of the filling container.Furthermore, the filling is regulated so that one empties the contents of the metering vessels in the reactor and thereby simultaneously flows a corresponding amount of the reaction product via the overflow directly into the filling container.
</ABSTRACT>
