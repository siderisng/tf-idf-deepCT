<ABSTRACT>
a method and apparatus for graphically displaying by  means of an oscilloscope the measurements obtained with  an ultrasonic inspection instrument (11) of the pulse-echo  type.  the instrument is designed to inspect tubular mem­ bars (10) for primarily internal corrosion and to display the  results on the face of a cathode ray tube (27).  the oscil­ loscope produces a pictorial representation of the tube wall  thickness.
