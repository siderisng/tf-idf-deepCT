<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for measuring alignment errors on semiconductor wafers in semiconductor production.These alignment error measurements are performed on a given semiconductor pane at selected locations where a two strip pattern is above a pattern consisting of a strip.The position of the centerline of a strip with respect to the centerline between the two strips provides a direct display of the alignment error between the two patterns.The alignment error is measured in the X and Y direction and is used to monitor the alignment error or for statistical purposes and for correlation with system parameters, so that the causes of the alignment errors are determined and eliminated or kept small in the processing of further semiconductor disks.
</ABSTRACT>
