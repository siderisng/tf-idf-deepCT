<ABSTRACT>
Power supply in the rotating cylindrical platinum typewritten machines.In order to simultaneously ensure parallelism between the axes of the feed rollers and the axis of the platinum and to evenly distribute the contact forces exerted by the rollers on the plate, the axes (26, 28) carrying the rollers ofPower supply (30, 32) are mounted on a chassis (22) coupled via a ball (36) to a support member (16).The latter is coupled to the main chassis (10) of the machine so as to exert a strength reminiscent of the roller chassis (22) towards the plate.Device usable especially in the rotating cylindrical platinum typewritten machines moving or not in translation into the machine.
