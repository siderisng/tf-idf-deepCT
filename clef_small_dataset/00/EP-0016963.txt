<ABSTRACT>
1.  clock with a housing made of an injection-mouldable plastic, which receives the clock works, the indicator elements and the glass located in front of these, and with a cover made of an injection-mouldable plastic which is articulated to the housing and which is pivotable from a position covering the glass into a position which exposes the glass and in which it can be used as a housing stand, characterised in that moulded on the cover (3) are two cheek-like extensions (4) which are arranged at a distance from one another and each of which carries in the region of its free end a bearing journal (8) parallel to the cover surface, in that for each cheek-like extension (4) there is provided a receiving slot (9) extending from the housing bottom into the housing interior and open towards the front, in the housing (2), having a hole (7) interacting with the bearing journal (8), and in that there is in each receiving slot (9) a groove (6) which runs in the direction of the hole (7) and extends into the vicinity of the latter and the depth of which decreases continuously in the vicinity of the hole (7).
