a device for heating liquid for one or several washer systems.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
device for heating a liquid for one or several washer  systems, e. g.  a wind screen washer system, a head lamp  washer system, a rear screen washer system, etc. , including a  heat exchanger preferably connexted to a return pipe of the  engine of a car to the cooling system of the engine, and  comprising a central flow-through means (1) connected to said  return pipe and consisting of a material of high thermal conductivity,  said means (1) being of short length, and of a further flow-through  means (2) connected to the piping of the washer system,  also consisting of a material of high thermal conductivity,  whereby a valve means is arranged between the heat  exchanger, the washer nozzle, the washing liquid container and  a pump of the washer system, said valve means preventing a  return flow of washing liquid to the heat exchanger.  
</ABSTRACT>
