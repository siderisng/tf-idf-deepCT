<ABSTRACT>
The application article is a method for producing a biaxially stretched polyene steaching film in which the tensile strengths are improved in the longitudinal and transverse direction compared to conventional films.The method is carried out in such a way that one in each case at least the twofoldly stretched polyester film in the longitudinal and transverse direction, wherein the longitudinal and transverse axis is in a ratio of 1: 1.05 to 1: 2.5 at a temperature betweenTg + 10 째 C (TG = glass transition temperature of the polyester) and TM - 40 째 C (TM = melting point of the polyester) and a night thermal mode performs at a temperature between Tg + 50 째 C and TM - 10 째 C.
