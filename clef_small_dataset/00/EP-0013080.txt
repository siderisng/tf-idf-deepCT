double-glazing assemblies.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
a double-glazing assembly (1) suitable for horticultural  and agricultural growing houses comprises two flexible  light-transmitting thermoplastics films (2,3) especially  polyethylene terephthalate films, separated by a spacing  framework (4).  means (6,8) are provided to evaluate the  enclosure between the sheets during the day-time thereby  improving the light transmission of the assembly.  the  assembly is also preferably provided with means for inflation  to optimise the conservation or thermal energy during the  night-time.
