<ABSTRACT>
an electronically tunable radio receiver has a common  actuator (3) for both controlling the speed at which a local  oscillator frequency is varied and selecting between dx and  local receiver modes.  preferably a digital oscillator (10) has  an output which determines the speed at which the local  oscillation frequency is swept.  an oscillation frequency con­ trolling element (7b) which forms an external oscillation fre­ quency control section of the digital oscillator (10) and a cir­ cuit constant variable upon variations in its working length, is  mounted around the shaft (4) of an operator-operable knob (3)  on the operational panel (1) of the radio receiver in the path of  a sweep speed controlling element (5) mounted coaxially  thereto.  the sweep speed controlling element (5) and the  oscillation frequency controlling element (7b) are connected  to an input section of the digital oscillator (10).  there is further  provided a dx/local receiver mode selecting element (6)  mounted coaxially on the knob shaft (4).  
</ABSTRACT>
