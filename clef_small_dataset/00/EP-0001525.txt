<ABSTRACT>
Plastic dielectric film capacitors and manufacturing process.They comprise conductive electrodes and a dielectric placed between the electrodes, said dielectric comprising a biaxially oriented film made from a mixture comprising less than 80% by weight of poly propylene isotactic and more than 20% by weight of polybu tene-1.Isotactic.Their manufacturing process consists of alternately winding on a cylindrical mandrel the electrodes and the dielectric film, thermally solder on itself the film outside the cylinder, to connect each electrode to a wire connecting and to treat thermally the cylinder.capacitor obtained before soldering the connecting wires.Their temperature coefficient allows thermal compensation of inductance variations between -55 ° C and + 100 ° C.Application to telephone channel filters.
</ABSTRACT>
