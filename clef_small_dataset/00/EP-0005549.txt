<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a method for producing polyurethane foam coated textiles, wherein in a first stage, a thermosetting foam or a foaming reaction mixture of polyurethane-forming components is applied from the back of a textile material, heated in a second stage, the coated textile is heated until a fully foamed, gelled, glue-free, bearing-resistant but still thermoplastic coating was created and finally formed in a third stage, the composite of textile substrate and foam coating in a heated shape is formed and cured.
