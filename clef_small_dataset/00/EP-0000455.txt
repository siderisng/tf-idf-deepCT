<ABSTRACT>
This device is intended for the closure of a sliding member, in particular a veil opening roof, which is integral with a rectilinear translation translation transverse rod and carrying a motor gear in socket with a fixed longitudinal rack.According to the invention, this device comprises a toothed element (5) hinged at the front end of the rack (3) and whose toothing (8) has a curved front portion (10) and a rear portion (9) rectilinear,and means (6-17) for compelling the pinion (21) to remain in connection with said curved portion (10) at the end of the closing stroke.
