<ABSTRACT>
To prevent the mouth of a rotary tubular oven from being damaged by hot and abrasive products coming out of the oven, protective rings generated from metal segments are used.The PRO invention poses a device simpler and less expensive than known devices.The object of the invention is characterized in that it is constituted by a refractory tone ring (36) which covers the inner surface of the end of the ferrule (16) of the oven and the front panel of aFlange (26) attached to the end of the ferrule, outside thereof, with the ring being attached to the ferrule and the flange by anchoring elements (38) attached to them.The invention can be used in cement and in the mineral industry.
