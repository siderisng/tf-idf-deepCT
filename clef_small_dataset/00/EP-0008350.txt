<ABSTRACT>
for video telephone conferences, a video telephone station comprising several user positions according to the invention is built as a compact terminal unit into a table having a round top (1).  the user positions are arranged in sector-shaped areas (10) of the table top, distributed uniformly over its circumference.  on top of the table top, video devices (4) associated with the user positions are located in its centre area.  the screens (5) can be subdivided into a corresponding number of equally large approximately square screen part areas for the simultaneous reproduction of several images.  the centre leg (2) of the table is constructed as a hollow body and accommodates common devices of the terminal unit.  <image>
