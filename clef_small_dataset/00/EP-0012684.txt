<ABSTRACT>
the invention relates to a process and apparatus making it possible to carry out the ensilage of fodder by means of a pick-up press with fodder winding, involving coating the pressed bales with a plastic film 4 wound on a roll 4a arranged between the pick-up head 5 and the press roller 6, the shaft 1 being connected to a driving member causing the plastic film 4 to unwind before the end of the pick-up of the fodder forming a bale, so that the end of the plastic film 4 is maintained pressed by part of the fodder and the said plastic film is entrained during the rotation of the bale while the pick-up of the fodder is interrupted, the plastic film being detached from the roll 4a when the bale is coated by means of a movable knife 11 fastened to a chain 12, the said plastic film being retained on the bale by adhesive bonding or tying.  <image>
