<TITLE>
 a method of manufacturing a laminate.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>
a betong abse<sep>a-betong ab<sep>a-betong abbox 24s-351 03 växjöse<sep>a-betong ab <sep>
</APPLICANT>
<INVENTOR>
petersson ivar<sep>petersson, ivar<sep>petersson, ivarprofossvägen 15s-240 10 dalbyse<sep>petersson, ivar<sep>petersson, ivarprofossvägen 15s-240 10 dalbyse<sep>
</INVENTOR>
<ABSTRACT>
a method of manufacturing an edge element, which is a  laminate and comprises a porous core layer (9) and a hard  surface layer (8).  a non-stretchable layer (6) is placed on a  foldable mold (1, 2, 3) on a support.  a hardenable layer (8) is  sprayed on the non-stretchable layer (6).  the porous core  layer (9) is placed on the hardenable layer (8) and the mold  with the non-stretchable and the hardenable layer are folded  around the core layer.  the non-stretchable layer contributes  to making the hardenable layer come very close to the  surface and the edges of the core layer.  crack formation,  curved rather than sharp edges and wrinkle formation are  prevented in the finished laminate.  
</ABSTRACT>
