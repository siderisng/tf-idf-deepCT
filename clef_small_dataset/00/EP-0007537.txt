<ABSTRACT>
Oxidation hair dye with a content of a 1-hydroxyalkyl-2, 5-diaminobenzene of the general formula as a developer substance, where R is a hydroxyalkyl radical having 1 to 4 carbon atoms, or their salts with inorganic or organic acids.For example, 1-hydroxymethyl-2, 5-diaminobenzene is contained in a concentration of about 0.1 to 3.0% by weight, either as a developer substance alone or in combination with conventional coupler substances, in these hair dye particles.
