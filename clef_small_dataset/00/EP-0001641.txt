<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to novel phenoxy-phenoxyalkanarboxylic acid esters of herbicide and plant growth, means which contain these esters as active ingredients, as well as their use for the selective control of weeds in crops or for regulating plant growth.The phenoxy-phenoxy-alkanecarboxylic acid esters correspond to the formula wherein R₁ is hydrogen, halogen, cyan, R₂ hydrogen, lower alkyl, R₃ a radical or₄ or SR₅, R₄ a substituted alkyl, an alkenyl, alkinyl, cycloalkyl radical, an optionally substituted phenyl or benzyl radicalor a 5-6 membered heterocycle and R₅ mean unsubstituted alkyl radical or the same as R₄.
