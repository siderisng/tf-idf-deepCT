<ABSTRACT>
an anti-aircraft projectile particularly for missile interception,  characterised by three connected zones, comprising an  ogive (7), a base (3), which has a bourrelet (12) and defines a  perforated skirt (4), and a high explosive body formed by a  pair of concentric cylinders (6) and (8), between which are  steel balls (10) filling the chamber defined between said cylinders  (6) and (8) and said cylinders being connected at their  ends to the base (3) and the ogive (7).
