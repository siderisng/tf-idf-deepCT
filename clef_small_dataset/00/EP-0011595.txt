<TITLE>
method and device for controlling the advance of a tape bearing marks at regular distances.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
In a packing machine, a tape repetitively bearing brands and marks is cut into coupons for individual packaging of items such as chocolate plates.One wants to have a correction in both directions, precise, on a large margin, avoid the restrictions on the printing of the band, and allow a continuous advance and a high rate.Circuits (100-120) validate a reference detection signal (LECTREP) by eliminating parasites and inappropriate signals, and evaluate the phase shift between this signal (LECTREP) and a synchronization signal dependent on the machine.They establish a bounded correction at a certain maximum.The correction acts on the motor (1) which causes the tape.The maximum is such that the coupon remains usable, which avoids the interruption, quitting to eliminate the packed article in this defective coupon.
</ABSTRACT>
