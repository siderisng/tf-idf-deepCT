<ABSTRACT>
Electro steels for electrolysis purposes, which are particularly suitable as anodes for chloroalkali electrolysis, contain adjacent to an alloy of niobium with the metals of the iron group tantalum, tantalum carbide or alloys of tantalum with metals of the iron group individually or in admixture.The surface of these electrodes is doped with metals of the platinum group, in particular rhodium.
