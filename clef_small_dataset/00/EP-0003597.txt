<ABSTRACT>
To control the unclouded content of closed packages for completeness, the packs (1) are brought into a slant.If the content is incomplete, he will slip through the inclination, whereby the weight distribution changes within the package, which is measured.For safety, the package (1) can then be brought into the opposite slot and the occurring weight distribution is also measured.The weight distributions thus determined are then compared.
