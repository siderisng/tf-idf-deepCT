<ABSTRACT>
Frequency reflection filter Picture An hyperfaré for transmission line of the micro-ribbon type.This filter is intended to prevent, in a hyperfree member equipped with a mixer, the transmission of a parasitic frequency signal FP = 2 FOL-FS (with FS and FOL = Frequencies respectively of the receiving signal and the signal ofThe local oscillator) returned from the mixer to the tracier of the receiver.It comprises at least one quarter wave filter (20), an adapter circuit (21) and a resonator of the odd mode (22), all placed substantially at the same point of the path of the parasitic signal.
