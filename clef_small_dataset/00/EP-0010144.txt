<ABSTRACT>
1.  link (1) for window casements, door wings or the like, especially angle link for side and top-hung doors and windows, which with an articulated sleeve (4) grips laterally near to the door or window overlap facing the indoor area of vision of the fixed frame, and connected by an angle lug (3) with the one side (2') of the corner angle (2) to be made firmly fast to the groove width surface of the door or window, but rotatable through 180 degrees and lockable on the corner angle (2) in both pivoting positions, characterized by the combination of features that a) the articulated sleeve (4) within its drill-hole socket (10) is provided with a supporting seat (11), preferably accessible from the upper end and axially adjustable, for the joint pin of the pivoting part, that b) the articulated sleeve (4) is shaped symmetrically about a plane parallel with the plane of the door or window, that c) the angle lug (3) is provided with a flange (3"') lying laterally near to the door or window overlap and aligned essentially obliquely to the plane of the door or window, and that d) the angle lug (3) ist connected by an axis (5) aligned parallel to the plane of the door or window rotatable through 180 degrees and the articulated sleeve (4) is connected in a lockable manner in both pivoting positions with the flange (3"').
