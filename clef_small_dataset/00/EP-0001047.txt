<ABSTRACT>
The permselective viscosem membrane, especially for the hemodialysis, consists of regenerated cellulose and is made of viscose.It is substantially equally oriented in a longitudinal and transverse direction and shows in a dry state a birefringence in each direction of less than 6 x 10 and a speed difference of less than 150 nm. The birefringence is the difference between the refractive indices in the longitudinal and transverse direction,The gait difference The product from birefringence and membrane thickness.The membrane is made of a flat or tubular body of cellulose hydrate gel, which is from acids.Salts and sulfur-containing products freed, brings into contact with a plasticizer solution and dries, the body of cellulose hydrate gel being constricted in the transverse direction by 40 to 120 before drying.
