<ABSTRACT>
The method of casting or injection of vehicle tires consists essentially of at least three process steps, wherein in a first method step, a tire body is poured or injected, then a reverberation is applied in the circumferential direction on this tire body, and finally a further area of ​​the tire or the tread on The wrapped tire body is sprayed or poured. A particularly simple device for carrying out this method has a rotatable Kem (5) for the tire body and a guide device (9) for the reinforcing insert 10 to be applied to the tire body. In a simple manner, the device is equipped with two pivotally mounted segment groups for the boundary of the casting or injection mold, so that the device is suitable by swinging the outer segment group for the production of the tire body, after introducing the reinforcing insert 10 and pivoting the second segment group Further area of ​​the tire, in particular the tread, can be sprayed or poured.
