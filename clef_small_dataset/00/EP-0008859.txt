<ABSTRACT>
a flame retardant blend having good impact resistance  and a high heat deflection temperature comprising (1) a pre­ dominant amount of an interpolymer comprising: (a) cross­ linked c₂-c₁₀ alkyl acrylate or c-c₂₂ alkyl methacrylate or  mixtures thereof; (b) crosslinked styrene-acryonitrile- and  (c) uncrosslinked styrene-acrylonitrile components;   2) a vinyl chloride homopolymer or vinyl chloride  copolymer with a comonomer polymerizable therewith,  wherein vinyl chloride is the predominant monomer,   (3) a chlorinated vinyl chloride or vinyl chloride  copolymer as defined in (2).  this blend is particularly useful  for use as an outdoor construction material.
