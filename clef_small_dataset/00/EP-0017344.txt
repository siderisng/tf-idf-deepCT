<ABSTRACT>
a direct drive system for a high speed ultra-centrifuge.   the drive spindle assembly (50) is removable through the  rotor chamber without disturbing any vacuum seals (46, 64)  that are present to isolate the requisite vacuum environment  in the rotor chamber for high speed rotation.  all of the high  speed bearings (36, 40) are connected to the spindle  assembly for removal as a unit.  an induction motor (72)  provides the direct drive with the motor armature mounted  on the drive spindle assembly.
