<ABSTRACT>
Remote control device for adjustable eternal mirror of the interior of the vehicle by means of a cable transmission, comprising a body in which the cables are guided parallel to the longitudinal axis of said body.The body 1 is fixed on the door of the vehicle with its longitudinal axis XX₁ parallel to the longitudinal axis of the vehicle and the hemispheric control member 8 is integral with a maneuver lever 14 perpendicular to the axis of said hemispherical member 8 andextending inside the one-pending vehicle to the longitudinal axis of the body 1 when the hemispherical member is in neutral position, said body having openings 16, 17, 17a whose edges provide guidance of the lever andof the hemispherical order member.The invention is used for the remote mirror command.
</ABSTRACT>
