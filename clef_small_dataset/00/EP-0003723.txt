<ABSTRACT>
method and apparatus for heating by means of heat  pumping.  the heat pumping is effected in two separate pump stages  (15-22-23, 16-24-25; 115-122-123, 116-124-125).  in order to  reduce the energy losses air is being circulated through a heat-insulated  space (10; 110) which has a substantially adiabatic  relationship to the surroundings thereof and includes the condenser  (23; 123) of the first stage (15-22-23; 115-122-123) and  the evaporator (24; 124) of the second stage (16-24-25; 116-124-125)  and the compressors (15, 16; 115, 116) and drive  machinery (12, 13, 14; 112, 113, 114) of the two stages.  heat  absorbed by the air from the condenser of the first stage, the  compressors and the drive machinery is supplied to the  evaporator of the second stage.  the pump stages have different  pressure and temperature gradients and are thermodynamically  dimensioned in such a way that the total of the heat absorbed  equals the heat of vaporization of the second stage.  
</ABSTRACT>
