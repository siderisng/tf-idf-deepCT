<ABSTRACT>
The switching system is used for spatial switching of information that modulate optical carrier frequencies.In the system, selectors are light deviation elements of the holographic optical network type.Each selector is associated with an input receiving an incident light wave and each holographic optical network deviates in a non-zero direction, the incident wave toward a selected output.The recordings of the holographic optical network are controlled by a control unit, using a marker.In the direction of order o of each selector, is disposed a photodetector whose output signals define the onto or not state of the associated input, ie the supervision, and are transmitted to said control unit.A subscriber optical station is also defined.Usable in multiservice switching networks.
