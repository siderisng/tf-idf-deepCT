rotor for an electric machine with salient poles.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
the invention relates to a rotor of an electrical machine with salient poles rigidly connected to the shaft of the machine.  a hoop (7) with a high mechanical strength is disposed round the poles and comprises alternatively portions (7a) of metal with a good magnetic permeability which are disposed opposite the poles (2), and portions (7b) of non-magnetic metal which are disposed opposite the interpolar spaces.  the invention renders it possible to make rotors with salient poles capable of rotating at high speed and having low losses through ventilation.  <image>
