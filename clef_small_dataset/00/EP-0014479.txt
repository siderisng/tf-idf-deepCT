<ABSTRACT>
It becomes a means for treating hair before winding on continuous wave wraps with a content of a) at least one methylpolysiloxane with a boiling point in the range of 100 to 190 ° C and / or at least one paraffin or isoparaffin with a boiling point in the range of 100 To 190 ° C, b) at least one cationactive, non-capillary compound and c) described at least one ampholytic capillary active imida zolin compound. The total amount of the methylpoly siloxanes contained, paraffins and isoparaffins is preferably 25 to 55 wt. %, while the total amount of the Catio nakthen, non-capillary compound as well as the ampho lytic, capillary active imidazoline compound preferably each, each 0.2 to 3.0 wt. % is. The described hair treatment agent facilitates the combing, compartments and wraps of the hair. It provides a uniform wool result, and after the permanent corrugation, the hair has excellent hair size feathers, especially with respect to handle, gloss and combingness of the hair.
