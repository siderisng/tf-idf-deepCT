<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to new N, N-dialkyl-o-pyrimidinylcarbamic acid esters of the formula wherein R to R³ are the same or different alkyl, R⁴ for hydrogen, alkyl, alkylthio or halogen and R⁵ for hydrogen or alkyl.These new connections are characterized by insecticidal properties.They are obtained by reacting 2-hydroxy-4-dialkylamino pyrimidines, optionally in the form of the alkali or alkaline scales or optionally in the presence of an acid acceptor with N, N-dialkyl carbamic acid halides or optionally in the presence of a diluent with phosgene and then with aAmine optionally in the presence of an acid acceptor.
