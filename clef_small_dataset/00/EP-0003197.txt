<ABSTRACT>
The machine comprises an oscillating arm (1) in a vertical plane, movable about an axis (7) attached to a support (8);displacement means (10) of the arm;a table (13) raised by hydrostatic pads (15) powered by cutting fluid;A base (12) on which the table and the bidirectional displacement means (29, 32) of the table moves.A pressure-feed hydraulic device controls the vertical movement of the arm by a double-acting cylinder (10), one of which ensures safety in case of efforts too large on the grinding wheel.
