<ABSTRACT>
the display device has an improved stability and sealing of the cells under fluctuating operating temperatures, especially in the range from 248 deg k to 353 deg k.  the electrochromic display device according to the invention has a non-transparent rear electrode (1) made of preformed metal sheets, e. g.  of titanium, nickel or fine steel, in the thickness range from 0. 3 to 0. 5 mm.  the sheets exhibit at their inner surface a tungsten trioxide layer (2) and at the same time form the rear wall of the cell.  <image>
