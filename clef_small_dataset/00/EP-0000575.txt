<TITLE>
fence made of large-panel fence elements secured to posts.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>
dethloff jens<sep>dethloff, jens<sep>dethloff, jensfeldstrasse 19d-2240 heidede<sep>dethloff, jens <sep>
</APPLICANT>
<INVENTOR>
dethloff jens<sep>dethloff, jens<sep>dethloff, jensfeldstrasse 19d-2240 heidede<sep>dethloff, jens <sep>dethloff, jensfeldstrasse 19d-2240 heidede<sep>
</INVENTOR>
<ABSTRACT>
1.  fence of large area fence elements (4) connectable to posts (1) in which each post (1) is securable in the ground and has two clamping strips (1a, 1b) extending in its longitudinal direction which are clampable to one another by a plurality of bolts (8) and nuts (9) and between which edge portions (15) of fence elements (4) adjacent the posts (1) are held, characterised in that each post (1) comprises only the clamping strips (1a, 1b) in continuous form, each large surface fence element (4) includes a fence field to be arranged as a whole between two posts (1), the edge portions (15) which are grippable between the two clamping strips (1a, 1b) are each constructed as one continuous vertical component, the bolts (8) and nuts (9) when seen in the direction of the breadth of the clamping strips (1a, 1b) are disposed centrally on the latter and fixing points are present on the portion (3) of the post (1) which is to be sunk into the ground (2) for the reception of spacers or crosspieces (6). 
</ABSTRACT>
