<ABSTRACT>
System for separating the spatial filtering and modulation functions due to mobile grids (2) from that of voidometry.It comprises an intermediate optics (15) between the grids (2) and the detector (4) of the four quadrant type.Grids produce modulation at a specified frequency.The intermediate optics is advantageously of the half-ball lens type whose center of curvature coincides substantially with the center (O) of the detector to allow a relative movement of the grids relative to the detector, such as a rotation (O) around this centercontrolled by a training device (16) annex.The invention applies in particular to achieving a passive infrared self -irect system.
