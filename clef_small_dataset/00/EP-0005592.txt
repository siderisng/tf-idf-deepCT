<ABSTRACT>
a plurality of series inductors (l₁, l₂---l₅) and shunt  capacitors (ci1---ci5---co1---co5) are provided between a radio  transmitter (10) and an antenna (70).  the inductors and  capacitors are arranged in binary increments and are  inserted in, or withdrawn from the circuit via reed relays  (31-35, 41-45; 51-55).  a micro-processor (100) activates the  relays in one of several modes until the required degree of  match is provided.  the status of the relays is stored in  provided memory (110) such that upon tuning of the  transmitter to the same channel a proper match condition  can be established without resort to the matching program.  
</ABSTRACT>
