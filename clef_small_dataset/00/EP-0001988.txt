<ABSTRACT>
A laterally on a thread air jet can carry a running thread through an opening.This "creating" of threads in nozzles failed at higher thread speeds.According to the invention, the application also succeeds even at high thread speeds when a powerful driving gas jet is divided into the nozzle in edge rays that are deflected outwardly, and a fine central beam conveying the thread through the narrow opening.
