<ABSTRACT>
The invention is a circuit which makes it possible to measure the phase jitter of an incoming little-stens proportions in digital form on the terminal of a transmission path, in particular a mobile transmit receiving station without the need for recipient-side synchronization.The scarf device consists essentially of a differentiating circuit (DS, DS ') forming the digital signals or digital signal components in drawing pulses, at least one time function transmitter (ZG), which freely generates a periodic time function and from the character change pulse at the output of the differentiating circuitis reset to a predetermined starting position and an evaluation circuit (AS) forming a mean value, which is supplied to a passing side of the instantaneous time function values upon occurrence of a drawing pulse.
