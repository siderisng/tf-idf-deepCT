<ABSTRACT>
The purpose of the invention is to produce isolated electrical wires in a particularly economical manner comparable to its properties and function with the paint insulated winding wires and are thus suitable for use in electric machine construction, transformer construction and electronics.Corresponding winding wires that meet the requirements of the German standard DIN 46435 are obtained in an economic extrusion process when using partial crystalline thermoplastic araliphatic polyamides with crystalline melting points of at least 280 Â° C and viscosity numbers of preferably at least 0.5.The thermoplastic polyamide is melted in the extruder, then pressed in the extruder head and around the wire and then forms a fixed coating without any aftertreatment.
