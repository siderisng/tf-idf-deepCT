<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for manufacturing jets shaped from compositions comprising at least 10% by weight of a polyolefin modified by polar monomers and at least 10% by weight the cellulosic fibers.The composition is kneaded at a higher temperature of at least 20 ° C at the melting temperature of the polyolefin.The process is applicable to the manufacture of plates that can be used in the automotive industry.
