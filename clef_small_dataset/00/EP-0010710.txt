<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a process for continuous production of chloramamic acid methyl ester by reacting phosgene with methanol at a tempera ture up to 50 ° C in a reactor containing chloramic acid methyl ester and dispersed in the phosgene in which gaseous rhosgene with -optionally already formed - chloramamic acidic thyl ester dispersed in the reaction room by means of a jet nozzle and at the same time methanol gives into the homogeneous gas-liquid dispersion.As a reaction room, a blown column serves expediently.If one turns the ester partially around, one chooses the proportion required for dispersing the phos gene due to the fact that the reaction mixture is heated at most by 10 ° C.
