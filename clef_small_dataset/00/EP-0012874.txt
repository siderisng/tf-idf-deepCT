<ABSTRACT>
A method for sealing a packaging container valve with a channel is prefately, after which a liquid sealant can be introduced into the channel in a simple manner.The method is carried out in such a way that the liquid sealant is arranged after connecting the channel-limiting valve parts in the channel.Preferably applied to the free channel end sealing means, which then passes through the channel by capillary effect.
