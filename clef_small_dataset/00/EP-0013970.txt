<ABSTRACT>
A windshield (1) for motor vehicles with a lower portion (15), which is disposed below the level of the bonnet (11), which serves to the storage of the windscreen wiper (10) in its rest position, is in this lower area (15)Electric heating resistors provided.In a plane between the heating and the windscreen wiper (10) lying plane, an opaque and heat rays absorber is arranged beer-end layer.This layer consists of a bonded email-like composition and is preferably arranged on the rear surface of the wind protective disc (1).Heating resistors are arranged on this layer as printed and also burned guide strips.The heating resistors are covered by another opaque layer.
