<ABSTRACT>
The folding caravan comprises a tent cloth 8 and a trailer 1 intended to contain this canvas, the latter can be deployed on the trailer 1 after opening a cover 6 articulated on one of the edges of the trailer.The tent fabric 8 is supported by a main hoop 9 hinged with trailer 2 and by two sliding secondary hoops 10 on the uprights of the main bar 9. Secondary hoops 10 are connected to the main bar. 9by rods 12 articulated with secondary arches 10 on the one hand, and at the main bar 9, on the other.Application to folding caravans in canvas whose unfolding and folding are automatic.
