<ABSTRACT>
thermoplastic elastomers with good mechanical pro­ perties are prepared by mixing a rubbery ethylen polymer  and a crystalline polyolefin.  the rubbery ethylene copoly­ mer is converted to this end with an unsaturated carboxy­ lic acid so that the gel content in benzene is in excess of  50% by weight.  at these gel contents good mechanical pro­ perties and a good processability are obtained, provided  that a limited, preferably deficient, amount of crystalline  polyolefin is present.  the mixtures are particularly suitable for applications  in which high demands are made on mechanical properties  at both high and low temperatures, for instance in the auto­ motive industry.
