<ABSTRACT>
a butterfly valve assembly to be installed between  pipeline flanges and secured by flange bolts is disclosed.  a  cylindrical valve body ring (10), having a cylindrical bore (24)  and a face portion at each end, has a resilient elastomer (12)  lining which covers the bore and extends over a portion of  the faces.  a disk (14) is attached to a shaft (16) which extends  through the body ring and liner transverse to the axis of the  body ring.  the valve assembly has at least one face plate (30)  with an opening which circumscribes the portion of the liner  extending over the face of the body ring.
