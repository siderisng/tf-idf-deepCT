<ABSTRACT>
1.  herbicidal compositions characterized in that they contain simultaneously, as active materials, 2,4-diamino-6-chloro-5-methylthio-pyrimidine or a mixture of the two isomeric compounds 2,4-diamino-6- chloro-5-methylthio-pyrimidine and 4,6-diamino-2-chloro-5-methyl-thio-pyrimidine in which the percentage of the first of these compounds is at least 80 %, and a herbicidal derivative of urea selected from the neburon of formula :  see diagramm : ep0013636,p10,f1  the isoproturon of formula  see diagramm : ep0013636,p10,f2  the chlortoluron of formula  see diagramm : ep0013636,p10,f3  the metoxuron of formula  see diagramm : ep0013636,p10,f4  the chlorbromuron of formula  see diagramm : ep0013636,p10,f5  and the methabenzthiazuron of formula  see diagramm : ep0013636,p10,f6  the ratio by weight 2,4-diamino-6-chloro-5-methylthio-pyrimidine/herbicidal derivative of urea or mixture of the two isomeric compounds/herbicidal derivative of urea in the compositions being from 1/10 to 1/1 in the case where the herbicidal derivative of urea is neburon, and from 1/10 to 10, preferably 1/4 to 4, in the case where the herbicidal derivative of urea is isoproturon, chlortoluron, metoxuron, chlorbromuron or methabenzthiazuron.
