<ABSTRACT>
In a thin-walled metal tube of a metal strip (1) curved transversely to its longitudinal axis, which is wrapped with a helical wire or tape (4), the connection between the edge regions of the curved metal strip is carried out by a connecting means (3) which in the gap (2) is arranged between the edge regions and fills the gap as a result of the gap capillarity and frozen when cooling.The connecting means, which melts under heat and incorporate in the gap, can be applied to the bent metal strip before connecting the edge regions together with the wire or bandwinder.
