<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for producing a semiconductor device, in particular for optoelectronic components, in which at least one layer of another semiconductor compound is deposited epitaxially on a substrate of a semiconductor compound.The invention is that the surface of the substrate (2) is provided with a plurality of facet structures (14) of the further semiconductor compound.Before epitaxy, the surface of the substrate is provided with a plurality of MESA mountains (10) in predetermined distribution, from which a facet structure (14) is then formed.The plane and trouble-free crystal surface of the facets retains further layers in the deposition.The method is therefore particularly suitable for the preparation of optoelectronic components and microwave components.
