<TITLE>
flat plate collector for solar radiation.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
a flat collector for collecting sunlight has an inner space filled with a heat transfer medium.  it constitutes a panel (10) made of transparent material provided with inlets and outlets (17, 18), the inner space being designed as a flow channel (11).  the heat transfer medium (12) is a liquid with suspended dusts (13), so that the sunlight is absorbed in the liquid.  in order to prevent the dusts settling, the flow channel (11) is equipped with turbulators (16).  <image>
