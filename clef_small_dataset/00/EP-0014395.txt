<ABSTRACT>
an indicator circuit has an output corresponding to the  departure of a sensed condition from a particular condition.   the circuit provides digital representation of an anaiogue  error voltage present as an output of a bridge circuit 10 where  the digital representation is independent of variation in  supply voltage v to the bridge circuit.  the circuit includes a  capacitor 29 arranged to be charged from an initial voltage  vref representative of the "zero" point of the analogue error  voltage range to a full-scale error voltage in the amount of  time that it takes for a digital counter 51 to count from zero to  full scale.  the capacitor voltage vc is compared to the error  voltage vt in a comparator 40 to stop the count upon equality.
