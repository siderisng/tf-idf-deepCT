<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to novel sulfamidic acid halides and methods for the preparation of sulfamic acid halides by reacting monosubstituted sulfamic acid halides with halogen compounds in the presence of certain amounts of basic compounds and in the presence of organic solvents and desired if necessary with subsequent water treatment at a pH of at most 7. The method of the inventionProducible, partially new compounds are valuable starting materials for the production of pesticides, dyes and pharmaceuticals.
