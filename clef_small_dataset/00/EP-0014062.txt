composition and process for the impregnation and sealing of porous articles.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
a composition useful for the impregnation and sealing  of porous articles comprises:   (a) an azonitrile;   (b) a polymerizable acrylic compound;   (c) a stabilizer which is a sterically-hindered, substi­ tuded phenol; and   (d) a metal ion chelator.  also a method for impregna­ ting and sealing said articles comprises impregnating them  with said composition and curing the composition.  the  composition may be cured by a mild heat treatment such  as by immersion of the impregnated article in hot water at  80° to 100°c.  for 1-15 minutes.
