<ABSTRACT>
a printing plate is produced by the steps of preparing  sensitive layer-forming photosensitive composition containing  an organic foaming agent and, upon drying of the composition,  thermally decompose the organic foaming agent to  roughen the surface of the photosensitive layer.  the process  in accordance with this invention requires no special mechanical  treatment for forming the photosensitive layer, eliminates  the need of rearrangement or modification of existing  coating equipments and also drastically decreases a working  time for vacuum-contact operation, thus resulting in an efficient  fabrication of printing plate and a greatly reduced production  cost.
