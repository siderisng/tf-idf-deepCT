<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for producing a short length polypropylene fibrils comprising sudden expansion of a liquid mixture of molten polypropylene and alkane, at high pressure and temperature, by ejection through a relaxation orifice.Instantly spray alkane and solidify polypropylene.The liquid mixture subjected to abruptly relaxing a non-solvent liquid of the polypropylene is dissolved, such as water.The fibrils obtained by the process of the invention can be implemented directly for the manufacture of papers by the classic paperwork.
