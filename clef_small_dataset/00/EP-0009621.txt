The present patent application claims a Ver driving for continuous production of triethylamine by catalytic hydrogenation of acetonitrile in the gas phase in which the hydrogenation in the presence of a noble metal of the VIII.Group of the Periodic Settings (MendeleJew) on a spinel as a carrier and optionally in fortified mono and diethylamine at temperatures 80 ° C to 115 ° C and pressures from 1 to 60 bar.
