<ABSTRACT>
Method for reproduction of oil paintings and the same with relief surface using an original removed relief mold (1) and a thermo plastic film (2) printed with the original color image (2) which is deformed with heating, characterized in that the film under pass alignmentis placed on the relief form and heated over its strength substantially homogeneously to its flow temperature.
