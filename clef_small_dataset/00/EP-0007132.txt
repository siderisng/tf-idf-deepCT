<ABSTRACT>
granular npk-fertilizers are prepared by granulating a  liquid mixture obtained by adding a solid, particulate potas­ sium salt to a liquid ammoniumnitrate containing phase,  which either contains ammoniumphosphate or to which this  is not added.  the potassium salt particles have an average size no  greater than 55 µ, while less than 1%wt are larger than 150 µ,  and are added at a temperature between 5 and 40°c.  the resulting granules show an excellent storage-­ stability.
