<ABSTRACT>
Cell materials from body cavities, in particular from the endometrium, are recovered with a device consisting of a guide tube and a cell bee retractable in the guide tube.The cell bee has the shape of a balloon and is circumferentially connected to the opening of the guide tube.For recording the balloon in the guide tube, a led by the guide tube is provided and connected to the inside of the balloon tension element provided.
