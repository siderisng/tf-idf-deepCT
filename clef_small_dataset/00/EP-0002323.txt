<ABSTRACT>
vesicular recording materials provided with a sensitised  layer comprising as vehicle for the sensitising agent a  polyelectrolyte, preferably a quaternised polyelectrolyte  such as a quaternised polymer of dimethylaminoethylmethacrylate.   the water sensitivity of the preferred polymers  may be reduced by copolymerisation with insolubilising  comonomers such as itaconic acid.
