<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a clamping frame for the production of rectangular frames, such as.b.Picture frame made of wood.The clamping frame has two mutually displaceably guided, a rectangle-forming frame parts (10, 16) with legs (10a, 10b; 16a, 16b) arranged perpendicular to each other.With the help of train rods (18) and forcing (22), the two frame parts (10, 16) receiving the bil frame (24) can be tightly tensioned, wherein the size of the clamping frame is automatically directed after the size of the picture frame.
