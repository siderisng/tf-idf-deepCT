<TITLE>
hf alkylation process with maximum heat utilization of isoparaffin recycle.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>
phillips petroleum cous<sep>phillips petroleum company<sep>phillips petroleum company5th and keelerbartlesville oklahoma 74004us<sep>
</APPLICANT>
<INVENTOR>

</INVENTOR>
<ABSTRACT>
in an hf alkylation process the mixed paraffin feed  fractionator is reboiled by an isoparaffin vapor stream  withdrawn from the main fractionator and steam; the total  quantity of isoparaffin vapor stream withdrawn is regulated  by a signal representative of a condition (e. g.  temperature)  in the top of the main fractionator, whereas the portion  of the isoparaffin vapor stream withdrawn that is used  for reboiling is maximized by an automatic predictive heat  balance computation and a control signal established accordingly  for regulating the flow rates of steam and isoparaffin  for reboiling the feed fractionator.  
</ABSTRACT>
