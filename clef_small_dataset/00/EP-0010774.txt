<ABSTRACT>
the method for tracking an object emitting a circu­ larly or linearly-polarized electromagnetic wave utilizes only  the te21 mode signals, extracted by means of two pairs  of slots (a, a′; b, b′) located on planes at π/4 in the  antenna feed.  the signals of a slot pair are substracted  one from the other and the resulting signals are made  proportional to the antenna displacement from nominal  object direction.  this is obtained by making an angle ,  that determines the angular position of the plane passing  through a pair, equal to half an angle α, that determines  the angular position of the polarization plane.  the system  that carries out the method is also described.
