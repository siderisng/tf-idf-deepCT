<ABSTRACT>
In order to be able to dose flowable reaction components for the purpose of mixing with a tolerance of the dosing ratio below 0.5%, a dosing unit consisting of pistons (18, 54), cylinders and drive unit is used per component, wherein the drive unit as an electrohydraulic linear amplifier (68, 69) is designed to which a control electronics associated with at least one adjustable control frequency generator (57) for one of the dosing units comprises and each has an adjustable frequency coaster (38) for each additional dosing unit.
