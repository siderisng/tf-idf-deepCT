<ABSTRACT>
A binder for inorganic-oxydic material is described, which retains its good adhesive properties over a very long period of time.Therefore, it can be stored for a long time without loss of its tongue ability than comparable well-known binding agents.The binder is preferably a known per se phenolic resin containing as adhesion intermediary alkyl-substituted ORGA noaminosilane.The binders are particularly suitable for the production of foundry forms.
