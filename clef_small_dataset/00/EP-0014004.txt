<TITLE>
process for making the luminescent stripes on a screen of a colour display tube.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

<ABSTRACT>
A layer-containing layer (2) which is curable on the front glass (1) of a color image tube is exposed in two process steps is exposed to achieve a location-dependent exposure amount for generating sharply tailored fluorescent strips with only low transverse fluctuations.In a first projection step, the main part of the amount of light required for curing is irradiated without action on the location-dependent light-quantity distribution, and in a second process step, substantially the amount of total light amount is irradiated at the required amount of light still lacking location.
