device for lifting a vehicle equipped with rubber-tired wheels, especially a motor vehicle.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
1.  apparatus for lifting a vehicle (1), especially a motor vehicle, equipped with rubber-tyred wheels (3), which apparatus consists of individual supporting frames (2) which engage on the type periphery of the wheels (3) and possess a suspension (6) becoming effective a short distance above the wheel axle (9) and which are each composed of two supporting arms (8) which are connected to one another by a joint in the wheel plane, only in the region of the suspension (6), and at the free end of which are located, on both sides of the standing face (11) of the rubber tyre (12), movable and lockable gripping devices (10) coming to rest against the tread face of said tyre, characterized in that the gripping devices (10) have two clamp parts (14, 15) which are longitudinally displaceable relative to one another parallel to the wheel axle (9) against the force of pressure accumulator elements (20) and press by means of clamping legs (16, 17) against the inner side (18) of the tyre and the outer side (19) of the tyre and which are held in engagement with the rubber tyre (12) by means of the automatically acting lock (21) to be released by hand. 
</ABSTRACT>
