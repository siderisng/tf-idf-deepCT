<ABSTRACT>
A process for the preparation of trifluoromethylphenols was found, in which one emerges from an aromatic halogen compound which, at least one trifluoromethyl group, at least one substituent;which favors the replacement of halogen against a hydroxyl group, and optionally further substituents, which converts them into alcoholic solution with aqueous alkali hydroxide in the presence of quaternary onium salts.According to the inventive method, new substituted trifluoromethylphenols can be prepared.
