<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a supplier device for packaging material, a two-reel packing machine.This device comprises two control servomotors 4, 34, reels 1, 31, a drive device 24, of the band-shaped packaging material, two coding devices 54, 56 and detection 55, 57, which detect a signal corresponding to the instantaneous diameter of the coil of packaging material into operation and provide a control signal of the flow rate of the deviiors.It also includes a press 17, 21 to automatically assemble the end of the band of the coil packaging material on the start of the strip from the next coil.
