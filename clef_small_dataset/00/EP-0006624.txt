<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for the preparation of ultrasonic heads, in particular of wall lacrimines for ultrasonic arrays or compound scanners consisting of a predeterminable number of interlocking transducer elements. Desired is a tech niche particularly simple and cost-saving manufacturing method. This is achieved by the consequences of the method steps: a) a plurality of thin films (1a to 1n; 11a to 11n) of transducer element material, in particular piezo material, is at a distance from each other by stacking. b. due to alternating between storage of spacer strips (2a to 2n; 12a to 12n) or attaching spacers (17) to the films in adhesive connection to a film stack (3; 13), b) from the film stack thus formed (3; 13) If single packages are cut off in cross sections (S1, S2 etc.), C) Each single package then forms a wall lard for itself.
