<ABSTRACT>
Checking device for the proper functioning of a Time switching bucket, and more particularly the continuity control device of the links being communicated.This device comprises several control groups I, II,..., N who work simultaneously under the commission of a microcalculator (8).Each group is connected to all incoming junctions I1 to JEN by the intermediate intermediate of a synchronization circuit and multiplexing (6) and to a part of all the outgoing junctions by the intermediate of a circuit ofmultiplexing (7).Application to telephone exchanges.
</ABSTRACT>
