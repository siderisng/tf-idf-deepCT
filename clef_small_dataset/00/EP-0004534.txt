<ABSTRACT>
The present invention is a particularly productive method for producing 4-methyl-5-hydroxymethyl imidazole by reacting 4-methyl imidazole with formaldehyde which is characterized by the reaction in alkaline milieu at a pH range of11-13, preferably 12-13, and at a relatively low temperature of about 20 to 60 ° C, preferably 30-40 ° C, conducts and no excess or excess of at most 0.5 mol formaldehyde per mol of 4-Methyl imidazole used.A surplus of formaldehyde is avoided by inflating this, for example according to the consumption, or used as a formaldehyde source of polymeric formaldehyde.
