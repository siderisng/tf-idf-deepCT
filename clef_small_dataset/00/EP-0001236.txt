<ABSTRACT>
Due to inevitable concentration fluctuations in the starting materials, the quality and yield in a continuous indirect diazotization of aromatic amines is not always satisfactory.If a partial flow of the mixture of the amine pumped from a template (7) to the diazotier container (13) with water and nitrite excess with shorter residence time is passed through an analyzer (9), where preferably potion-metrical, voltammetric or polarographically of the nitrite excess is determined, it can be usedThis measured value controls the nitrizugue into the template (7), whereby a dye is obtained with consistently high quality.
