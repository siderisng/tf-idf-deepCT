<ABSTRACT>
Method for texturing thread bundles made of synthetic high molecular weight substances with high texturing cycle speed at which the thread bundle - is guided by a inlet nozzle - then with a hot gaseous medium located in swirl storage, which by a guide in swirl sensors (3) a swirl angle ofGiven 10 to 70 °, brings together, - in a subsequent tubular chamber then the flowing medium is heated - subsequently leads to the crimping of an expansion stage and device for carrying out this method.
</ABSTRACT>
