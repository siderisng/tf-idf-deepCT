a photographic element is described which contains a  ballasted, bifunctional benzisoxazolone compound of the  formula:    wherein:   r is alkyl or aryl;   dev is a silver halide developing agent;   ball is at least one organic ballasting radical of such  molecular size and configuration as to render the compound  nondiffusible in the photographic element during develop­ ment in an alkaline processing composition; and   n is a positive integer of 1 to 3.  the compound may be employed in a negative-working,  color image transfer element to release the developer upon  processing.  the remainder of the compound functions to  scavenge oxidized developer.
