plastic resin foam articles are thermoformed by a)  preheating (1) the plastic foam, usually supplied in the  form of a continuous sheet stock, b) clamping (3), in a fixed  position, that portion of the stock material which is to be  thermoformed, c) initially deforming the preheated foam  stock with a portion of a male mold member (4) to form a  preform, d) drawing a vacuum on the interior of the female  mold member (5) to assist in completion of the thermo­ forming operation, and e) completing formation of the  desired article by deformation of the preform between a  male and female thermoforming device (4, 4′, 5).
