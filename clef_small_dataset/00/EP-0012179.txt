<ABSTRACT>
To adjust two ends to be connected to two optical fibers to be joined, is proposed to couple a light pulse at the free end of the one fiber. During passing through the light-line fibers, the light pulse sends backscatter light to the same free end, whose intensity (I) can be detected there as a function of the runtime (T). The connecting point of the two optical fibers represents a damping, so that the intensity curve shows a sudden intensity decrease in the other end of the light from one to the other end. The ends to be joined are adjusted so that this sudden intestity acceptance is virtually zero. At the free end can advantageously a signal corresponding to the intensity jump signal, z. b. A sound frequency, which is generated, which is transmitted to the assembly personnel at the connection point. The ends are then adjusted so that the signal assumes an extremal value, d. H. the extremal value belonging to the intensity jump zero is as close as possible.
