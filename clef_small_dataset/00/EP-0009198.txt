method and reagent for the detection and the determination of complement-binding antibodies.

<ABSTRACT>
to detect and determine a complement-binding antibody by reaction thereof with a binding partner and complement, one binding partner of the complement-binding antibody is rendered insoluble, the binding partner which has been rendered insoluble is reacted with the complement-binding antibody and serum complement, the reaction product is reacted with a coupling product of a reactant of serum complement with a marker which can be determined quantitatively, and finally the marker is determined.  it is possible to use for this purpose a reagent containing carrier-bound antigen, serum complement and a complement factor antibody linked to a marker. 
</ABSTRACT>
