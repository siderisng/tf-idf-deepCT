<ABSTRACT>
an aerating valve (10) for a compressor installation for the compression of a gaseous medium such as air can be connected in a pressure line (13) virtually at the outlet of the compressor (11).  an inlet chamber (31), connecting the pressure line (13), is arranged in the housing (22) of the aerating valve (10), the inlet chamber (31) opening into a cylinder chamber (34) in which a valve plate (35) is supported in a floating manner (displaceably) and can be moved by means of an electromagnet (21).  this valve plate (35) opens an outlet channel (49), arranged in the form of an annulus about the inlet chamber (31), for aeration of the pressure line (13), or closes the outlet channel (49) to increase the gas pressure.  <image>
