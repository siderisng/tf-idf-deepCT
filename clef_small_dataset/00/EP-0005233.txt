The present invention relates to a method for producing liquid, storage-stable methylene bis (phenylisocyanate) in which methylene bis (phenyl isocyanate) in the presence of 1 ppb to 10 ppm of a phospholine oxide heated to 150 to 300 ° C andAfter reaching the desired isocyanate content, the reaction mixture cools to 100 ° C or lower temperature, the methylene bis (phenylisocyanate) obtainable according to this method and its use as an isocyanate component in carrying out the isocyanate polyaddition method.
