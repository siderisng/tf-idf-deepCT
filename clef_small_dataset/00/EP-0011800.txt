<ABSTRACT>
A method for etching surfaces of copper or copper alloys is given by means of an acidic etching solution containing an oxidizing agent. The etching solution flows through after removing the copper surface for regeneration of the oxidizing agent an ELEK trolysis cell (2), at the cathode (9) the etched copper is deposited. To prevent the development of chlorine on the anode (8) of the electrolysis cell and to prevent a chemical attack of the apparatus parts, the ATZ solution chlorione is kept free. The oxidizing agent is added in the etching solution iron (III) sulfate up to a concentration of maxi times 140 gr Fe / I etching solution, wherein in the etching solution, a copper content of at least 10 gr copper / I etching solution is set and a current density of the electrolytic cell at least 2 A / DM² is maintained. Preferably, the ATZ solution is added to the anode iron (III) sulfate of the anode (III) sulfate, the amount of iron compound added is such that the ated solution after driving streams of the electrolysis cell between 10 and 140 grams / I etching solution as iron (III) - Sulfate contains.
