<ABSTRACT>
a hydrodynamic torque converter (10) mechanism having  a bladed impeller (18) a bladed turbine (20) and a bladed  stator (32) arranged in a toroidal fluid flow circuit enclosed  within a common impeller housing (12), a friction clutch in  the housing including a clutch disc (50) with a friction surface  (52) thereon situated adjacent a cooperating friction surface  (54) formed on the housing, a stator sleeve shaft (38)  supporting the stator, an impeller sleeve hub (94) journalled  on the housing, a turbine shaft (62) connected to the turbine  and extending through the stator sleeve shaft, and a double  circuit flow path for fluid circulating through the torus circuit,  one circuit being defined in part by the annular space  between the impeller hub and the stator sleeve shaft and the  other circuit being defined by the annular space between  said turbine shaft and said stator sleeve shaft and by the  annular flow space between said friction surfaces.
