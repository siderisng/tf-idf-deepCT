<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a device for maintaining the anterior portion of a pneumatic structure to avoid excessive deformation of this earlier under the effect of the waves.The device comprises a rigid support bar 11 so as to reproduce approximately the shape of the front 2 of the vention of which one of the at least ends is connected to a torsion bar fixed on the grating 6 and arranged transversely byto the boat.The folded central portion of the support bar 11 rests against a rigid support surface 16 integral with the anterior portion 2 of the boat.The support bar 11 ensures the maintenance of the anterior portion 2 of the boat thanks to the elastic force exerted by the torsion bar, the antagonist of the upper lifting forces of the venue, exerted by the waves.The invention applies mainly to the pneumatic boats used at sea.
