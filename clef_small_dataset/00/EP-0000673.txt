<ABSTRACT>
A method of producing printed circuits with metal holes made from the synthetic glass-resin laminate insulation substrates, in which the metallizing holes in the insulating support is drilled, a chemical metal metal deposit is carried out in the holes and onThe two sides are carried out by etching of the conductive metal on both sides after removing a reserve and the electrical components are positioned in the metalli holes and the tail of the components in the respective holes are silded to ensure the electrical connections..Immediately after drilling the holes (10), the support (1) is coated with a positive photosensitive resin (12) and the support coated with radiation capable of destroying the resin and then dried.
