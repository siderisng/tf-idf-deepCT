<ABSTRACT>
The thermal image contains a mosaic of pneumatic IR detectors, each preferably designed as a microgas cell 5 '.On this mosaic, the one side is projected the thermal image and leads to heat-dependent expansion of the gas in the cells.On the opposite viewing side, the cells are coated with a plastic film 11, so that a gas exchange between the cells is not possible.At a low distance from the viewing side, a reference plate 9 is arranged, which is illuminated with optical light.By providing the plastic film due to gas expansion in the cells, interference patterns resulting from which the heat image becomes visible.
