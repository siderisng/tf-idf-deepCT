<ABSTRACT>
a rotary forging or upsetting machine comprising an  upper platen(15) and a lower platen (13), the lower platen (13)  being rotatable about an axis (z₁) and the upper platen (15)  being rotatable about an axis (z₂) which intersects the axis  (z₁), the upper platen (15) being mounted on carrier (17) which  is pivotable about an axis (a-a) which passes through the  point of intersection of the axes (z₁ and z₂), means (19) being  provided for adjusting the angle (α) of the upper platen (15)  whilst the platens (15, 13) are rotating, and means (11) being  provided for applying a force (f) to the lower platen (13) to  move it towards the upper platen (15) so that a workpiece (14)  carried by the lower platen (13) is deformed by the upper  platen (15) moving around the workpiece (14).
