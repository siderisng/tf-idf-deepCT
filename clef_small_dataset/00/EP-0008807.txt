<ABSTRACT>
in an apparatus for batchwise vapour deposition ranges for the thermal deposition on articles made of plastic, metal or glass or for continuous vapour deposition ranges for coating strip material, such as plastics, paper webs or textiles, with for example aluminium, the main vacuum container holding the articles or strip material contains a second container (4) within which the vaporisers (1) are fitted and which is sealable vacuum-tight (6) independently of the main container.  this apparatus is particularly suitable for the use of vaporisers made of sinter ceramics.  <image>
