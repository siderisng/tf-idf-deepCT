<TITLE>
improved belting fabric and method of manufacture.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>
celanese corpus<sep>celanese corporation<sep>celanese corporation1211 avenue of the americasnew york new york 10036us<sep>
</APPLICANT>
<INVENTOR>
turnbull johnus<sep>turnbull, john<sep>turnbull, john5811 beckett courtcharlotte north carolinaus<sep>
</INVENTOR>
<ABSTRACT>
an improved belting fabric that is relatively stiff in the fill­ ing (weft) direction and relatively flexible in the warp direction  in which the filling yarns are resin bonded multifilament yarns,  preferably untwisted polyester containing 0. 4 to 2. 0 percent of  a polyurethane, and in which the warp yarns are unbanded.  un­ cured resin is applied to the yarn before weaving and cured  preferably after weaving.  the product has particular utility in  self retracting seat belt harnesses.  
</ABSTRACT>
