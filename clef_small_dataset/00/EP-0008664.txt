<ABSTRACT>
In the pressure or residual degree of polyvinyl chloride polymer water mixtures, the unavoidable foam is separated into separators from the vacuum pump leading gas stream.With increasing oversubs, the capacity of the separator (S) is reduced.According to the invention, the liquid or foam amount accumulated in the separator (3) is pumped back by a pump (5) arranged below the separator in the degassing (polymerization) autoclave (1).The advantages consist of a better outset of the separator capacity, better and consistent entertainment of the product and shorter degassing cycles.
