<ABSTRACT>
Cooling system for runners of electrical machines, in particular for turbogenerator runners with superconducting field winding.At the radially outer circumference (W0) of the superconducting field winding (W), a distribution channel network is provided, in which axially winding outside radial a feed channels (K30) open and from which the radial cooling channels (K32) of the winding (W) via groove base channels and the bale penetratingReturn dunes (K31) into a rotor central mixing chamber (M).In the mixed chamber (M) supplied with liquid helium, a HE two-phase mixture forms with a bonding of the liquid ring (HE1) and a concentric steam compartment (HE2).About the COMMUNIZED, appropriately heat-insulated feeding and return channels (K30, K31) are specified in the case of heat nests thermosiphon loops (S1a, S1b).
