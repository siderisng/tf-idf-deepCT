<ABSTRACT>
The coating buffer (6) is carried by a cartridge (4) containing the pasty product and rotatably driven by a motor (3) housed in the housing of the device.The cartridge (4) thus provided contains a freely movable piston (18).However, pneumatic or other discharge means (for example, a pump (20) can act at the rear of the piston (18) for the pressurization of the product contained in the cartridge so that this tendented productTo flow through the coating buffer. Moreover, the structure thereof is such that it is possible to easily fill the cartridge (4) by passing the desired product through this buffer.The present device can be used for example, for the wafer application on shoes.
