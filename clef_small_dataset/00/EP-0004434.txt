<ABSTRACT>
halomethyl substituted dialkoxypyridine compounds which  correspond to the formula:    wherein x and y represent an or′, trichloromethyl, trifluoromethyl,  dichloromethyl, dichlorofluoromethyl or chlorodifluoromethyl  group with the proviso that one of x or y must be  or′ and the other of x and y is other than or′; and r and r′  each independently represent an alkyl group containing 1 to 12  carbons or an alkenyl group containing 3 or 4 carbon atoms.   these compounds and compositions containing them have  been found to be useful as agrnomic fungicides, especially  useful and valuable for the control of soil-borne plant disease  organisms which attack the root of plants.
