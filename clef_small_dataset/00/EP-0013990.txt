<ABSTRACT>
Each binary information is transmitted as an elementary signal occupying a constant time interval called binary moment. Binary information 1 is translated alternately by a pulse or absence of impulse occupying all the corresponding binary moment; Binary information 0 is normally translated by an OC pulse one half of the corresponding binary moment, except in the case where it is the first or last of a sequence of N + 1 successive binary information 0, the first being translated according to the rule preceding as binary information 0, unless the binary information appeared since the previous N + 1 sequence of successive binary information 0 are in pair where it is translated as binary information 1 in res docking the rule of the alternation , the latter being translated according to the preceding rule as binary information 1 by violating the rule of the alternation. The invention advantageously applies to optical fiber bonds with junctions coded in HDB₃.
