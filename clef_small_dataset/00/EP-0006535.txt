Storage, solid mixture of (a) long chain, ester group-free epoxy resin with high epoxy equii Valentweight, (b) ester group-free, cyclic structure, in the molecule two to three epoxide groups have the epoxy resin with a lower epoxide equivalent weight, (c) a triphenol, (d)a curing bencher and possibly a propellant.By heating, hydrolysis-resistant, heat-shaped constant and flexible laminates, foams, pressing particles and bonds are obtained.
