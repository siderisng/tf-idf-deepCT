<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for producing an alkali metal benzoate in addition to a benzyl alcohol of an impurity benzylbenzoate which falls in the oxidation of a monoalkyl-benzene compound having a molecular oxygen-containing gas.According to the invention, the contaminated benzylbenzoate is distilled in the presence of a basic substance and hydrolyzed the resulting distillate with an alkali metal base.In this way, the benzylbenzoate to be separating away from certain tea components is placed in a pure alkali metal benzoate in addition to a pure benzyl alcohol, thereby reacting a previously unusable waste product in useful substances.
