<ABSTRACT>
the agent for melting ice and snow, in particular on traffic-carrying surfaces, contains essentially gritting salt and a corrosion inhibitor with or without other customary additives, the corrosion inhibitor being calcium cyanamide, added in an amount of 0. 1 to 10% by weight, preferably from 1 to 5% by weight.  such an agent shows a significantly better melting effect than normal gritting salt.
