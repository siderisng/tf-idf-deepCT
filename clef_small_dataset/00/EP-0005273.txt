apparatus and method for removing hydrocarbons from drill cuttings.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
a method and apparatus for removing hydrocarbons  from drill cuttings is disclosed.  the method involves heating  the contaminated drill cuttings at a pressure lower than  atmospheric pressure, milling the cuttings to expose hydrocarbons  trapped within agglomerates of the material, and  separating the vapors thereby produced from the cuttings.   the cleaned cuttings can be used or disposed of without  damage to the environment.  the vaporized hydrocarbons  may be condensed and recovered.
