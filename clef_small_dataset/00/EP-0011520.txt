<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a crosslinkable resin by electronic or photonic irradiation (gamma, x or ultra purple rays).It aims to obtain and the use of a very stable resin to the action of ambient light and heat.The resin according to the invention comprises a body whose chemical formula comprises a thietane ring: in particular a compound of the polymer type of methacry ester whose monomer has for general formula: n which can be zero or equal to an integer of 1 to 10. Application to the protection of objects against atmospheric agents and the realization of manufacturing masks in the development of semiconductor components and in particular integrated circuits.
