<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a process for the preparation of partially metallized textile structures of synthetic polymers or native fibers, wherein the side or the places of the structure which should not be metallized (en), prior to the actual metallization by printing with thermo-diffusion dyes is hydrophobicated or desak.The invention further relates to the use of such partially metallized textile structures for the production of protective clothing against the action of microwaves or for producing permanent antistatically equipped clothing.
