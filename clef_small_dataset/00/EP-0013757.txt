<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a web on which spring-driven toy cars (4) are guided.In the course of the train, absorbing ramps (1) and jump ramps (2) are provided, between which the drainage stuff (4) exerts free of charge.The ramps webs (1b, 2b) are so curved so that the driving a toy (4) runs a complete looping.Furthermore, the ramps (1, 2) are transverse to the web direction around at least one web width against each other, so that the path coming from the stepping ramp (2) web piece (3) can be passed on the jump ramp (1).
