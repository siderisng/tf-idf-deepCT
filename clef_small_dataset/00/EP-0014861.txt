<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method and a Vorrich device for non-contact distance or thickness measurement with a regulation of the deflection frequency and amplitude.When changing the light beam bundle, this control causes measurement errors.To avoid such measuring errors and thus for increasing the measurement accuracy and stability, an additional electronic control of the rich device of the light beam is provided.For this purpose, a time difference is obtained from the signals of a detector (PDB) for frequency control and another detector (PDC), which supplies a control signal for the beam deflector (UREF) after conversion into a DC voltage by either the directive with a reference voltage (UREF).
