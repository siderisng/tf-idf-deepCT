<ABSTRACT>
an improved bearing tightening device having two  tightening places in series each of which comprising a  tightening lip of an elastomeric material and both of said  tightening lips being provided on one and the same ring of  elastomeric material, all of the tightening device being  enclosed in a cover comprising two members, one of which  having a z-shaped profile and the other one having an  l-shaped profile, whereby one tightening lip contacts a  branch of the cover member of z-shaped profile and the  other one contacts a branch of the cover member of  l-shaped profile.
