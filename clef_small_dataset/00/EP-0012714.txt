<ABSTRACT>
Solid epoxy hydraulic adducts from a low molecular weight diglycidyl ether of the bisphenols and an aromatic diamine give in conjunction with a filler, a curing accelerator and an aromatic polyol as hard a molding composition, from which molded parts can be made with high cracking.
