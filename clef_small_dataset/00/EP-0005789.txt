<ABSTRACT>
Aqueous aluminosilic suspensions, which in addition finely divided aluminosilicate in amounts of 20 to 60 wt.% as stabilizing and flowability improver 0.5 to 15% of at least one hydroxyl-type dicarboxylic acid.Preferably, mixtures of adipine, glutar and succinic acid are used.These suspensions are flowing through this addition of storage-stable flowable and pumpable and can be handled without sedimentation.
