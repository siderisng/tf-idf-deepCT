<ABSTRACT>
Method for producing solar cells in which a glass (3) preferably made of glass (3) with an electrically conductive pad (5) of a cadmium sulfide layer (6) and a copper sulfide layer (7) is prepared as a prefabricated lower part (1) having oneElectrically conductive grating (12) carrying cover glass (10) is assembled into a encapsulated cell, wherein the cover glass with the grid forms a top (2).Such a solar cell is ready to use and has a significant mechanical strength through which it is made possible to interconnect a plurality of such solar cells together to a solar battery without it being necessary for example.
