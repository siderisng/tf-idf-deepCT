<ABSTRACT>
A motor vehicle roof rack for the attachment of skis and other cargo, consisting of a support tube composed of two slidable pieces (1, 1 ') composite support tube and off against each other by means of a spindle (4) sliding clamping parts (3, 6) for attachment of the roof rack on the surface of the vehicle roof. In this case, the spindle (4) moving the clamping part (6) passes through the tube (1) from above, and also an additional attachable and lockable fuse is provided with a lockable in an open end of the support tube (1), which is displaceable in it (9 ) and one with its one end (22) on the support tube (1) pivotable to be fastened to the support bar (17), the opposite end (19) has a hook (20) latched in the locking piece, which blocks through the lock (12) in closing position is, whereby the locked safety bracket (17) in turn prevents release of the clamp parts (3, 6).
