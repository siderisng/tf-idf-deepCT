<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an ultrasonic transducer consisting of a transmission layer of material with relatively high dielectric constant and high switching lining and a receiving layer of relatively low dielectric constant and low switching library material.The aim of the invention is to provide such an ultrasonic transducer, which simultaneously creates optimal results for the transmission and reception case with optimum construction.This object is achieved by the invention that the two layers (2, 3) in hybrid technology are interconnected to each other.
