<ABSTRACT>
the locking device for an arc furnace contains operating rods for at least one tilting bar and for at least one pivoting bar.  the tilting bars (16) and the pivoting bars (24) can either be connected rigidly with the aid of operating rods (15) or a system of levers (7, 23) and connecting link-type plates (11) can be used, which always lock at least one of the tilting/pivoting functions.  <image>
