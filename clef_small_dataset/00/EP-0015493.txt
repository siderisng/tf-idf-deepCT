<ABSTRACT>
1.  a band saw (1), more specially for do-it-yourself work or for use in the building trade, and able to be fixed on the frame of a main tool (2), more specially in the form of a planning machine, as a further unit, the band saw having a generally c-like frame (4) having thereon an adjustable top band wheel (5) and a lower band wheel (6) designed to be powered by the motor of the main tool (2), with the saw blade (7) trained round such wheels (5 and 6), characterized in that for driving the lower wheel (6) of the band saw (1) there is a friction wheel (23) joined up with a stem (22), in that the said stem (22) is designed so that its end, furthest from the friction wheel (23), running out past the frame (4) of the band saw (1) may be taken up and locked in a further tool support (21) of the main tool (2) which has a supporting part for supporting further tools on it one at a time, and in that the supporting part, taking up the band saw (1), and the further tool support (21) designed for use therewith, of the main tool may be moved in relation to each other upwards and downwards.
