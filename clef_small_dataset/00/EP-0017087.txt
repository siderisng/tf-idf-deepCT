<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method of optical representation of information in which ambient light in a fluorescent body is captured and decoupled again at selected locations, characterized in that by applying an electrical voltage, reflecting layers are brought into optical contact with the fluorescent body.According to the invention it is proposed to generate these reflection layers by expressing a precipitate from a liquid.This cogel is preferably obtained by an electrochemical reaction from an electrolyte and is usually either a metal such as silver or a (organic) electrochromic layer.The proposed method allows a light output of over 90 ° 0, allows a temporally unlimited, immediately erasable storage of the input information and gives no constructive problems.
