<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to azulmine acids stabilized by condensation with carbonyl compounds with a content of 0.5 to 55 percent by weight of ionic groups of the formula in which R for hydrogen, ammonium, an equivalent of a protonized or quaternized organic nitrogen base, a sulfonium cation or for equivalentof a metal cation, and with a content of 0.5 to 15% by weight of the groups of the formula resulted from the decarboxylation reaction as well as acid addition salts and complex compounds of these stabilized azulmic acids and also mixed products of these stabilized azulmic acids with additives.The invention further relates to a plurality of methods for producing said products as well as their use for different purposes.
