<ABSTRACT>
radiation curable pressure sensitive adhesive composition  comprises: a polyoxyalkylene homo- or copolymer  which is either a polyxyethylene homopolymer or a poly  (oxyethylene - oxypropylene) copolymer, or mixture thereof,  having a molecular weight of from 1,700 to 90,000, in which  at least 40 percent by weight of the oxyalkylene units are  oxyethylene units; a liquid carbamyloxy alkyl acrylate; and,  optionally, a photoinitiator.  also disclosed is a process of  preparing such a composition and the radiation cured pressure  sensitive adhesive produced therefrom.
