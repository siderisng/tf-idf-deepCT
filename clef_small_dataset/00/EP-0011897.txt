<ABSTRACT>
Method and apparatus for exploring X-ray media or gamma exploration, intended to allow the representation of slices of the medium examined by determining the electron density in each element of volume (EI) of these slices.Using a source (2) of X or gamma rays, a stationary main detector (3) and an auxiliary detector (4) aligned with the source (2), one carries out for each volume elementof each slice (SJ) two componal diffusion measurements to the main detector (3) and two source transmission measurements (2) - auxiliary detector (4), progressing from the nearest slice towards the furthest from the detectormain (3).The measurement results are progressively used to rigorously calculate the electron densities throughout the environment examined.Application: Exploration of biological tissues for medical diagnosis purposes.
</ABSTRACT>
