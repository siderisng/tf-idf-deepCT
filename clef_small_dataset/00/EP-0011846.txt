<ABSTRACT>
A scaffolding salt mixture suitable for use in detergents and cleaning agents consists of the following components: a) 45 - 75 wt.-%, preferably 50 - 75 wt.-% of a water-insoluble, finely divided, synthetically prepared, crystalline, bonded water-containing alkalialumosilicate of the formula 0.7-1.5 Me₂0.Al₂0₃.1.3-4.0 SiO₂ (based on the anhydrous active substance = AS), which has a calcium binding capacity of 50 - 200, preferably from 100 to 200 mg Ca0 / g, where Me means sodium or potassium, b) 15 - 40 wt.-%, preferably 20 - 30 wt.-% sodium or potassium carboxymethyloxysuccinate and c) 5 - 15 wt.-%, preferably 8 - 15 wt.-% sodium or potassium tripolyphosphate (based on anhydrous salt).
