<ABSTRACT>
in a shaft furnace, in particular a gas pressure operated blast furnace producing pig iron, the shell (1) of the furnace contains cooling elements (10) of circular cross-section arranged with mutual offset.  to minimise the area of the less cooled areas between the cooling elements and to maximise the uniformity of cooling them without losing excess heat, the cooling elements (10) are arranged at the nodes of a network formed from essentially equilateral triangles which enclose the shell (1).  <image>
