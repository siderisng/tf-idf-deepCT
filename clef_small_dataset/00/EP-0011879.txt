<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a field effect transistor for higher blocking voltages.Previous FET for higher blocking voltages must be provided to prevent avalanche breakage in the current-carrying channel, with one or more rewards at different distance to the channel arranged auxiliary electrodes.The invention avoids this disadvantage in that the drift line (8) lying between the control electrode (9) and the sink (5) contains an increasing number of dopant atoms for the valley (5).This can be achieved by funnel-shaped extension of the channel or the control electrode (9) or by increasing doping conentration to the sink (5).The inventive FET is easily integrated and suitable for example for liquid crystal display controls.
