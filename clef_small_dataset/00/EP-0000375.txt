<ABSTRACT>
the most different types of supporting materials are used in the cultivation of plants from shoots and seeds.  to ensure adequate stability, strength and favourable penetration behaviour and to avoid the formation of phytotoxic substances during cultivation, a cured urea/formaldehyde foam plastic whose properties such as hardness and pore volume allow the respective use conditions to be adjusted accordingly is used as the supporting material for cultivation or as a cultivation substrate.
