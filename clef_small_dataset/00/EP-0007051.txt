<ABSTRACT>
An alter-friendly process for producing Chini Zarin, an important dye intermediate is that a mixture of phthalic anhydride P-chlorophenol, sulfuric acid and boric acid anhydride is reacted at elevated temperatures in such a way that in the reaction mixture is preferably 10-20% free SO₃-containingSulfuric acid is present and the weight ratio of P-chlorophenol to total H₂SO₄ + SO₃ is 1: 1.5 to 4.0.Expensive and purity of the product are excellent despite the concentrated way of working.
