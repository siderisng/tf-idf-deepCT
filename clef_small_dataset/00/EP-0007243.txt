<ABSTRACT>
2-aryl-1,3-cyclohexanedione compounds and alkali  metal salts, ammonium salts and enol esters thereof, composition  and method of controlling acarina ectoparasites on  warmblood animals.  there is disclosed a compound for use in controlling  acarina ectoparasites on warmblood animals, which compound  comprises a 2-aryl-1; 3-cyclohexanedione compound  [e. g.  3-(2-ethylhexanoyloxy)-5, 5-dimethyl-2-(2', 4'-dimethylphenyl)-2-cyclohexenone]  of the formula    the alkali metal salts, ammonium salts and enol ester derivative  thereof are also disclosed.  there is also disclosed a composition comprising a compound  of the invention together with a carrier, and a method  of controlling acarina ectoparasites on warmblooded animals  by orally administering to the animals an ectoparasitically  effective amount of a compound or a composition of the  invention; several methods for preparing of this compounds,  salts and esters are given.
