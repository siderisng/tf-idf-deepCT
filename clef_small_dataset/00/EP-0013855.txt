<ABSTRACT>
Partitioned Case with Claire-Track, Stackable and Emboí Table, comprising an upper belt (80), a reopened background (72) and side walls as well as interest separations constituted by inclined arms (70, 71, 76, 77, 77, 81, 82, 94), projecting horizontally in the empty spaces of the bottom and forming pyramidal surfaces of maintaining and separating bottles or the like.The inclined arms (70, 71, 76, 77, 81, 82, 94) can slide on those of another crate during the discharge of several crates.An at least part of the vertices (83) may coincide with parts full of the bottom of an identical fund placed in a different orate, above the first.
