<ABSTRACT>
Tube junction device of cyclone separator of liquid mixtures.It comprises a pre-cut plate in which the hexagonal mouthpieces (17) of the stamped or molded shapes are housed, connecting by their ends opposite to the straight sections of the tubes, allowing to offer at the input of the mixture into the tubes aRight section unlikely from that of the supply duct, and a spacer plate 20 in the circular cross-section area of the tubes.
