<TITLE>
latex coated with protein material, process for the preparation of this latex, immunological reagent containing this latex, process for the preparation of this reagent, application of this reagent, testing procedure utilising this reagent and reagent kit containing this reagent.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

<ABSTRACT>
there is described an activated latex manufactured by treating discrete particles of a latex carrier, for example having a particle size of 0. 01 microns to 0. 9 microns, to which a proteinaceous material is covalently bound with an activating agent.  the activated latex may be reacted with an immunologically active material to produce a water-insoluble immunological reagent, which reagent may be used in the determination of an immunologically active material, e. g.  gamma-globulin, in a sample. 
</ABSTRACT>
