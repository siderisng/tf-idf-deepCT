<ABSTRACT>
an improved bearing tightening device having two  tightening places in series each of which comprising a  tightening lip of an elastomeric material, provided on a ring,  having a metallic liner, one of said liners having an u-shaped  profile, and the other one having a z-shaped profile, whereby  the tightening lip belonging to the liner of l-shaped profile  contacts a branch of the liner of z-shaped profile, and the  tightening lip belonging to the liner of z-shaped profile  contacts the outer branch of the liner of u-shaped profile.
