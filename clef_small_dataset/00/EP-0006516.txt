<ABSTRACT>
The present invention is a method for producing aliphatic-aromatic polycarbonates with diphenolcarbonate end groups, which is characterized in that aliphatic diols and aromatic bisphenols are reacted with diphenyl carbonate in a reaction step by cleavage of phenol to aliphatic-aromatic copolycarbonates obtained according to the invention obtained according to the invention obtainedAliphatic-aromatic polycarbonates with diphenolcarbonate end groups whose use is used for the preparation of thermoplastically processable, high molecular weight, segmented, aliphatic-aromatic polycarbonate elastomers and the elastomers obtained according to the invention.
