<ABSTRACT>
an evaporation process for the separation of a solution  into its volatile and non-volatile components by feeding the  solution to a calandria where it is heated and partially vapor­ ized and the resulting calandria vapour stream is subjected to  vapour-liquid separation for the removal of entrainment, is  improved by using an additional and intermediate step in  which the calandria vapour is contacted with the feed solu­ tion.
