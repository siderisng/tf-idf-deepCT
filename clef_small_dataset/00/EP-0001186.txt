<TITLE>
process for producing polyisobutenes.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

</INVENTOR>
<ABSTRACT>
high molecular weight polybutenes having a viscosity  ssu above 1,000,000 at 210°f are produced by using a three-component  catalyst system containing an alkyl aluminium  halide, tertiary butyl halide and a metal halide selected from  snc1₄, snbr₄, tic1₄ and tibr₄.  the reaction phase is  homogeneous and the catalyst components are added as a  solution in an inert solvent.  the polyisobutene product so  produced are useful additives for oil, adhesives and mastics.
