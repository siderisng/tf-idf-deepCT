<ABSTRACT>
A carriage with ski-like skids 12, 13 are described, which are mounted by means of oblique coating axes 29 to transverse carriers 18, 19 and can be adapted for driving on the slope or for cornering in the same direction.In each case one of the skids 12, 13, opposite the other advanced.A seat 40 mounted on the transverse carriers 18, 19 shifts and tilts likewise.To further improve the driving and curve properties, the skids in plenary position (skid distance in front be narrower than behind) and be provided with a pre-tensioning, so that the skids are running straight ahead on the inner edge.
