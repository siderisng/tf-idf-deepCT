<ABSTRACT>
a semiconductor element forming at least part of a boots­ trap circuit for use in a semiconductor device such as an  eprom or erasable and programmable read-only memory  comprises first and second mis field effect transistors (q₁, q₂)  connected in series between a power supply line (vdd) and an  earth line (vss); third and fourth mis field effect transistors  (q₄, q₅) connected in series between the power supply line  (vdd) and the earth line (vss); a capacitor (c) connected bet­ ween the gate and source of the third mis field effect transis­ tor (q₄) and; a fifth mis field effect transistor (q₃) connected  between first and second nodes, the first node connecting the  first and second mis field effect transistors (q₁, q₂) and the  second node being coupled to the gate of the third mis trans­ istor (q₄) and to the third and fourth mis field effect transistors  (q₄, q₅) and an output of the device (dout).  to reduce the  leakage current from the capacitor (c) when the element is  exposed to light a p-n junction connected to the capacitor (c)  is covered by an opaque cover which prevents light reaching  the p-n junction but permits light to reach other parts of the  device.  
</ABSTRACT>
