<ABSTRACT>
The expansion gasket comprises a first and a second member (1, 2) of the same internal dimensions as the waveguide and free to move longitudinally relative to each other, the first element being made up of atube, the second element comprising a trap (5, 6) established at least partly between said elements and outside the first element, the electrical length of said trap being equal to half the wavelength of the central frequency.Transmitted by the waveguide, said elements being separated longitudinally at the trap by a distance that varies when the waveguide expands or contracts.The trap can be folded to reduce clutter.Application to large waveguides.
