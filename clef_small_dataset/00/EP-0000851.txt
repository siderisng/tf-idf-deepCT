<ABSTRACT>
Automatic machine for cutting thermoplastic sheath and pose of sleeves around containers. The machine consists essentially, arranged on a frame: a) Plastic duct feeding means in flattened form and then conveying the continuous band under predetermined voltage conditions; b) at least at least vertical (3) of cutting the sheath and supply of sections or sleeves, this set comprising from top to bottom, arranged around a microperforcing hollow mandrel on its periphery and on which the sheath is engaged continues: - means for removing the two folds of the flat sheath; - means for cutting the sheath; Staffing means allowing both to maintain the mandrel in a vertical position and automatically adjust the height and creeper rate; means for guiding and expulsion of sleeves down the mandrel; These sleeves then automatically deposited around containers circulating on a conveyor (4) provided with means (5) of hot retraction of said sleeves, known type in itself.
