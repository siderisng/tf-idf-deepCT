<ABSTRACT>
To create a closed, but heat-shaped heating element (11) consisting of an upper and a lower part (12, 17) in the form of flat sheet metal shells, the same are soldered together or welded.In the space (22) between the sheet metal trays is a tubular shelter in the form of a flat spiral tubular heat (23) with triangular cross-section, which is with a flat side under vacuum influence in heat-conducting connection with the upper part (12) forming the cooking surface (13).It can be soldered under vacuum with the upper part (12), whereby the vacuum is also maintained during operation.However, the vacuum can also press each other without soldering upper and lower part (12, 17) so that the desired contact between the pipe heaters (23) and upper part (12) is ensured.
