<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a control device for controlling the peripheral speed of a yarn coil in a winding machine.The control device consists of two dispersed detectors (55.56) the non-contact the electrostatic charge measure on the current yarn.These measured values are processed in a correlator (61) such that in a deviation of the measured from the predetermined yarn speed this difference is extended.Here are different processing options of the measured values.
