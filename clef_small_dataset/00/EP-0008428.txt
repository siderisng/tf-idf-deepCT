<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a diagnostic agent for detecting leukocytes in body fluids consists of an absorbent support impregnated with a chromogenous azo dye ester, a suitable buffer substance and optionally with additional, commonly used auxiliaries.The invention further relates to the use of azo dye esters for the preparation of the diagnotic agent according to the invention.Furthermore, the invention relates to new, as chromogenic azo dye esters and their preparation.The preparation of the azo dye esters according to the invention takes place in that azo dyes, which have free hydroxy groups, are converted with corresponding acids or suitable reactive derivatives thereof.
