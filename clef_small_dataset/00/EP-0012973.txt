<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a novel process for the preparation of biuret and / or higher polyuret groups having polyisocyanates having the reaction of bis-secondary organic diamines with excess amounts of organic diisocyanates, wherein the reaction in the presence of catalytic amounts of strong, isocyanates mixed carbamide anhydrides Fine acids, as well as a modification of this method, which consists in the fact that one of the above-mentioned starting materials is first produced in the absence of said catalysts the corresponding urea diisocyanates and then subsequently reacted in the presence of said catalysts with further diisocyanate, which according to these methods available biuret and / or higher polyuret groups having polyisocyanates, as well as their use, optionally in the form blocked with blocking agent for isocyanate groups as a construction component in the production of pole Yurethanstoffstoffs after the isocyanate polyaddition method.
