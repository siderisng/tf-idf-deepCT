<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to contactless relays which switch in or near the zero crossing.The voltage in which the relay must no longer switch, is determined by a zener diode (15) lying in the control line of a transistor (9).The transistor (9) is thus switched on at a defined, independent of the tolerances of the construction used elements independent voltage and closes the base cathode section of a thyristor (8).This can not be ignited as long as the AC voltage to be switched exceeds the zener voltage.The invention is useful for contactless switching of alternating voltages.
