<ABSTRACT>
At least one polyphenol alkaline salt is reacted to at least one epoxy-2.3 alkane halo-1, in an anhydrous medium, aproti and heterogeneous in the presence of at least one organ chosen in the group consisting of the group.Acetonitrile, propionitrile, benzonitrile and ethylene sulphide.The method is particularly suitable for the preparation of glycidic polyethers of bisphenol-A and in particular the preparation of liquid glycid polyethers.
