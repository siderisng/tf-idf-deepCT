<ABSTRACT>
In a method of generating a thermal image, a scene is optically mapped, line by line, led over a multi-element ridge detector, and the signals of the detector of an image display device fed.To compensate the phase error, which normally occurs in the transmission of signal jumps, the signals coming from the series of individual elements of the detector are turned on two adjacent rows of light-emitting diodes, the distance between the two light-emitting diode rows be adjusted according to the phase error.The transmission chain for carrying out the method consists of a voice mirror, a multi-element ridge detector, an amplifier with low-pass filters and a picture display arrangement in which two rows of light-emitting diodes are provided for the series of detector elements whose individual elements are arranged on gap.
</ABSTRACT>
