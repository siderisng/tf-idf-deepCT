<ABSTRACT>
Production of glyoxal by oxidation of glycol in the presence of a silver catalyst of certain grain size and under certain conditions of temperature, residence time and inert gas concentration.The glyoxal producible by the method of the invention is crease-proofing means, auxiliaries to increase the tear strength and elasticity of pulps, tannin, hardener in the photographic industry and valuable starting material for the production of synthetic resins, textile aids, e.g.b.To prevent the arrival after washing, paper aid, z.b.To increase the wet strength, and plastics.
