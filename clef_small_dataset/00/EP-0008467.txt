<ABSTRACT>
For rationalization of color screech manufacturing, the automation of multiple insertion of the Schat tenmask (2) is sought into the screen tub (3).However, difficulties occur due to the undefined position of the connecting elements (detention pins (30) and sheet springs (22)).The invention solves this problem by engaging the device (1) on the connecting elements and brings them into a defined position to the device (1), which then makes it possible to automatically rest the fasteners.
