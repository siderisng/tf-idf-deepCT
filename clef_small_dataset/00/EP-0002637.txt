<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a device for the storage and setting up of floors by juxtaposed elements. The device comprises a base member 1 with at the ends of two vertical wall elements, perpendicular beams to support the floor elements, on the inner face of each vertical wall element a rail 3 support element, the floor element 5 comprising means 7 cooperating with the support member 3 for vertically suspending with the two so-called means the floor elements 5 to the two media elements facing each other, a lifting and tilting means 17, 19 oscillating mounted around the An axis 12 parallel to the longitudinal edge of the base member, this means capable of hanging a floor element 5 suspended from the support elements, to release it from it by failover above the plane of the beams. Floor, push it on said beams 9 and replace the floor elements on the support elements with an inverse maneuver. This device is used in fairway rides.
