<ABSTRACT>
Purin derivatives of general formula I in which R₁ is hydrogen or a lower alkyl group, R₂ hydrogen, an aryl or a lower alkyl group, which may optionally be substituted by hydroxyl, wherein R₁ and R₂ also together with the nitrogen atom a heterocyclicRing can form, and R₃ denote an acyl group or hydrogen, as well as their salts with pharmacologically acceptable acids, with anti-allergic, anti-inflammatory and anti-edematogenic effect, processes for producing these compounds and these compounds containing medicaments.The preparation of the compounds according to the invention is carried out by reacting purin derivatives with reactive 1,3-propane compounds and optionally acylated 4-amino piperidines.
