<ABSTRACT>
A photographic material is provided which contains a receiving element for transmitted color fabrics.The receiving element consists of a carbon polymer cast thereto made of a graft polymer which is made of gelatin, a monomerizing a water-insoluble polymer and a sulfonate group containing a sulfonate group in homopolymerization, a water-soluble polymer-supplying monomer.These receiving elements can be used to pick Catio Nische dyes in photographic dye transfer methods.
