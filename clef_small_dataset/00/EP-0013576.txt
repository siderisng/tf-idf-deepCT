<ABSTRACT>
Water-soluble products of the formula in R¹ an alkyl radical having 1 to 18 C atoms, R² hydrogen or R¹, X is an ethylene and / or propylene radical, M 6 to 100 and N 1 to 12 are suitable as a preparation agent for dispersion dyes.Dispersion dye tubes containing such attachment products show a stable fine distribution and good dyeing properties even at high dye contents.
