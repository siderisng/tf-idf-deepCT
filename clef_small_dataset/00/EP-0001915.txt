<ABSTRACT>
an improved prosthesis, in particular a hearing prosthesis,  which has a receiver located in the body for stimulating  nerve endings.  the receiver is activated by a transmitter  which is located outside the body.  in a preferred embodiment having at least two channels,  the transmitter modulates a carrier with the information carrying  signals and the receiver converts the modulated signal  from serial to parallel signals which are then used to stimulate  the nerve endings, the modulated signals preferably being in  at least two parts, including both amplitude and phase information.
