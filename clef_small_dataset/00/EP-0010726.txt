<ABSTRACT>
A multiplex spatial switch according to the invention is essentially consisting of a seri-parallel converter (1), two memories (2, 3) working alternately with writing and reading, a control memory (4), and a parallel-series converter (5).Each memory with a word capacity equal to the product of the number of incoming MULPLEX (E1, EP) by a sub-multiple of the number of lanes of a frame.When the sub-multiple is different from 1, there is possibility of a temporal brewing of the channels, with the inorganic of a sector (V1) having a number of channels equal to the sub-multiple.Application to the connection networks of a telephone switching center.
