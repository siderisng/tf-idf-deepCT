<ABSTRACT>
In the process for continuous production of azopigents by reacting a coupling component with the aqueous solution of a diazocomponent at temperatures of 10-60 ° C and residence times of 0.2 to 4 hours in a reaction zone, which is already filled with the feeder azopigment suspension, One is an aqueous suspension or solution of the coupling component in a largely laminar flow from bottom to top by the reaction zone, which has a cylindrical shape and arranged vertically. The aqueous solution of the diazocomponent is added laterally to 2-50 superimposed areas of the reaction zone, whereby one dosed from bottom to top decreasing amounts of the diazocomponent and only at the top post office sets the stoichio metric endpoint of the clutch reaction. Finally, one introduces a character characteristic of a pH pH by adding dilute alkali eyes to laterally on 2-50 superimposed sites of the reaction zone, wherein the addition points for the diazocomponent on the one hand and for the alkali eye on the other hand are arranged offset offset.
