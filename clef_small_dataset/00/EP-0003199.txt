<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to new derivatives of tetrahydro pyridinyl indole and their salts, their process for preparing, their application as drugs and pharmaceutical compositions containing them.Said derivatives respond to the formula: wherein R 'is hydrogen, halogen, 1 to 3 carbon alkoxy, R₁ and R₂ represent a hydrogen, an alkyl of 1 to 3 carbons, x' is an alkyl from 1 to 6Carbons, a cycloalcoyl of 4 to 7 carbons, alkenyl or alkynyl of 2 to 5 carbons or an aralcoyl of 7 to 12 carbons.If x 'is a methyl, at least one of the substituents R', R₁ and R₂ has more than one carbon and if x 'is a benzyl, at least one of the substituents R', R₁ and R₂ is different from hydrogen.The derivatives have antidepressant, neuroleptic and anti-emetary properties.
