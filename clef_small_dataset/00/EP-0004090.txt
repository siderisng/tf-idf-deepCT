The invention relates to cathodically deposited aqueous electric dip coating coating agents, which as by protonating with acids water-dilutable binders 55 to 95 wt.-% based on the total amount of the binder containing amino group-containing organic resin binder with a mean molecular weight (Mn) of about 500 to 10,000, a PKB value of about 3 to 7 and an amine number of about 30 to 150 and 5 to45 wt.-% crosslinking agent containing a mean molecular weight (Mn) of about 500 to 5,000, which have esterified terminal carboxyl groups.
