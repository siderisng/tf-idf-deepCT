<ABSTRACT>
In a method for applying a decorative layer to a support plate (34), it is preceded by a crosslinkable plastic foam (14) on a decorative carrier web (10) that this plastic foam is then dried to the application by a first heat treatment andIn this case, sometimes partially crosslinked and that the decorative layer thus obtained, consisting of decor carrier web (10) and partially crosslinked foam (36) is pressed under heat with the support plate, wherein the decorative carrier web is connected to the carrier plate connected and the plastic foam is further crosslinked.
