<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method and a device for continuous production of a sheet element.The sheet element 21 is caused to move continuously and passes, in an ascending part of its trajectory, between two flexible blades 38 anneted on a support 34 so that their feet are removed from the element 21. ASleeping composition 42 is injected into the corner 41 formed between element 21 and the blade.The flatness of the end zone of the blade applied parallel to the sheet element can be provided by a pneumatic tube 40, as shown, or by other means such as elastic counter-blades.The invention applies in particular to the bed of paper.
