<ABSTRACT>
The use of a ferritic-austenitic CR-Ni steel containing 30 to 70% austenite and the max. 0.1% C, max. 1.0% Si, 4.0 to 6.0% Mn, 22.0to 28.0% CR, 3.5 to 5.5% Ni, 1.0 to 3.0% MO, 0.35 to 0.6% N, remainder iron and inevitable contaminants, for forging pieces already atDouble deformation degree must have a notch toughness (according to ISO-V) of more than 35 joules at a minimum stretch limit of 600 N / mm².
