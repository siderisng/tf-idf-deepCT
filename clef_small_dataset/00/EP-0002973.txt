<ABSTRACT>
Process for the preparation of halomercurialdehydes and ketones by reacting an alkenyl ester on a mercuric halide in the presence of water and, preferably, an acid acceptor.The reaction preferably conducted between 15 and 35 ° C makes it possible to obtain very pure products with excellent yields especially when the mercuric oxide is used as the acid acceptor.
