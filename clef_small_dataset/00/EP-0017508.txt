<TITLE>
leaf spring puller for nuclear fuel rods.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

<ABSTRACT>
a fuel rod puller in the form of a collet for pulling fuel  rods from a storage area into grids of a nuclear reactor fuel  assembly has a pull rod with leaf springs grasping an end  plug in a fuel rod and drive means for pulling the rod puller  and connected fuel rod from the storage area into the fuel  assembly grids.  the rod puller includes a pull tube (22)  having leaf springs (24) at one end thereof.  the arrangement provides for a small diameter but safe  engagement mechanism easily movable through the long  fuel assemblies.
