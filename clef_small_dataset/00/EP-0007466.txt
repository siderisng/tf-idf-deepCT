<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to novel 2-cyclopropyl pyrimidine (4) yl-thionophosphonic acid esters of the formula in which R is alkyl and R¹ is alkyl or phenyl.The new active ingredients are characterized by their high effective measurement in the control of pests, in particular due to high insecticides and acaricidal efficacy.The new compounds are prepared by reacting corresponding thionophosphonic acid ester halides with 2-cyclopropyl-6-methyl-4-hydroxy-pyrimidine under hydrogen halide discharge.
