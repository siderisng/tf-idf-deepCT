<ABSTRACT>
a double walled fluid pressure line (18) for use, for  example, in a fuel injection system, including first and  second, nominally concentric tubes (30, 70) having an  annular space (76) therebetween.  both of the tubes (30, 70)  may be bendable on conventional tube bending equipment  and the innermost tube mounts fittings for connection into a  fuel injection system.  an additional fitting (56) associated  with the outermost tube establishes fluid communication to  the annular space (76) between the tubes for venting leaking  fluid.  washers (36) having passages (80) enable fluid fuel  leaking at the fittings to pass to the annular space (76) to be  vented.
