<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for the heating treatment of fine-grained good, which is preheated in a cyclone preheater (5), deaviated in a preliminal concalation zone (7) by additional lichen fuel and then fried in a rotary tube furnace (6).In this case, a Gastempe Rature (14,15) cyclone preheater (5) and the good tempera tura (16, 17) after passing the preliminal concalation zone (7) is measured, the difference of these temperatures determines and the control of a maximum or minimum value of the temperature differenceThe fuel supply to the calcination zone (7) to manual operation.In this way, a rapid adjustment of the amount of fuel to the respective ratios is achieved in this way.
