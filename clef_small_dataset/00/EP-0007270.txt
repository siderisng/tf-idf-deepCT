<ABSTRACT>
self-monitored control system, a process.The system comprises two complementary circuits (4.5) for substituting the control signals, when incorrect by security limit values calculated by separate circuits (10,11,17,18) a circuit (21) transmittingAn alarm signal when the signals, at the output of the two complementary cooked cirs (4.5), are not identical, a safety circuit (23) for selecting the signal emanating from the two complementary circuits (4.5)which is not incorrect in case of failure of one of the complementary circuits (4.5) and a monitoring circuit (26,28,29) for signaling a safety circuit failure (23).The system applies in particular to the piloting of the Aero Dynes.
