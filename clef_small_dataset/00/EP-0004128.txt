<ABSTRACT>
a novel compound frenolicin b of the formula:    is prepared by cultivating a microorganism belonging to  genus streptomyces, e. g.  streptomyces roseofulvus am-3867,  in a culture medium and isolating the compound  formed and accumulated in the medium from the cultured  product.  the compound is effective for prevention and therapy of  infectious diseases caused by eumycetes and mycoplasmas.
