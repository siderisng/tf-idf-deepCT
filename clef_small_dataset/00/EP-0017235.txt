<ABSTRACT>
The ω-¹²³J labeling of fatty acids by reacting the corresponding ω-bromo fatty acids with ¹²³J-labeled iodide is improved according to the invention by reacting the ω-bromo fatty acid in molten shape with the iodide and for this purpose in a fine layer over an inert carrier distributed is provided. Preferably, the reaction takes place near the melting point of the ω-bromo fatty acid within 2 to 1o minutes. To produce the bromostic acid raised on the carrier, a correspondingly diluted solution of the ω-bromo fatty acid is expediently mixed in a volatile solution means with the carrier and the solvent is hampered. The carrier is then brought together with a particularly reducing agent-containing ¹² iodide solution and the solvent is removed. The marking method is particularly suitable for C₁₆ to C₁₈ fatty acids. A product purified by high pressure liquid chromatography can be applied specifically in medicine.
