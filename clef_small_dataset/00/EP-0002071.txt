synthetic resin pipe joint.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
a synthetic resin pipe joint having front and rear  inclined walls (6, 7) defining a packing holding annular  groove (5) is provided with softness and elasticity sufficient  to cause flattening deformation on impingement with  a pipe (2) contained therein, and expansion of a gap (d)  between this pipe and the inner circumferential face of the  pipe joint is effectively prevented.  on overall flattening  deformation of the pipe joint, the front and rear inclined  walls are similarly deformed and a packing (4) contained  therein is deformed stably with the deformation of the  inclined walls without causing distortions in the packing.   because of such high elasticity and flexibility as not  possessed by rigid joints, a pipe line can be effectively  constructed by using this pipe joint.  
</ABSTRACT>
