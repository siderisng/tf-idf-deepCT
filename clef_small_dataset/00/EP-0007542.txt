<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for direct connection of rubber with metal surfaces by vulcanization in which to be used as adhesion promoter tellurium, clade alloys and / or tellurium compounds.These adhesives are either added to the rubber mass or applied before vulcanization to the metal surface.The invention also relates to a rubber mass for vulcanizing metal materials which are 0.01 to 5 wt.-% Tellur, clip alloy and / or tellurium compounds.The invention also relates to a cord for motor vehicle tires, in which the individual wires of the cord has been superficially coated with tellurium, a clip alloy and / or a tellurial compound during their production and or the cord. Overall prior to vulcanization.
