<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a method of preparing alumina at least partially in the form of ultra-fine beehmi.According to a particular implementation of the invention, at least partially alumina in the form of ultra-fine boehmite is obtained by treatment in an aqueous medium having a pH less than 9 of an active alumina powder obtained barely by dehydration.Fast hydrgillite in a hot gas stream.The alumina obtained can be used optionally after drying and calcinaton as a load, binder, thickened, dispersant, film-forming agent, reinforcing, membrane nes, base material for catalyst supports.
