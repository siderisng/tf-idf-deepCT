<ABSTRACT>
a combination brake and brake wear take-up device (10)  is disclosed.  the device comprises a disc brake assembly (15,  19, 21) and a link assembly (33, 35, 36) connected to actuator  discs (23, 24).  a link assembly pull rod (33) is connected to a  fluid power piston (50) slidably disposed in a cylinder (40).   one or more conical washer members (70, 71) are carried  around the piston (50) to permit the piston (50) to slide in the  cylinder (40) in one direction over a relatively extended dis­ tance, but permit the piston (50) to slide in the opposite direc­ tion over only a limited distance.  a snap ring (80) disposed in  the cylinder interior and a shoulder (84) formed in the cylinder  interior define the axial extent of that limited piston return  motion.
