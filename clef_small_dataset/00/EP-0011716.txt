<ABSTRACT>
A very sensitive and specific reagent for detecting infectious mononucleosis in body fluid thes, in particular blood serum, through agglutination consists of a suspension of mammalian (especially horse) erythrocytes treated with sulfosalicylic acid to brown coloration and optionally with z.b.Coomassie Blue or Ziehl-Neelsens-Karbolfuchsin solution solution were dyed.
