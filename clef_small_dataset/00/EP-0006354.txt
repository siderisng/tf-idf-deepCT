esters of formula     a--o-b  wherein    a-- is derived from a carboxylic acid of the  pyrethrin or pyrethroid type, and b-o- is derived from an  alcohol of the pyrethrin or pyrethroid type characterised  in that either or both of a and b comprise a fluorosubsti­ tuted methylene moiety directly bonded to the ester carbonyl  or the ester oxygen respectively.  the esters are prepared  by conventional processes from known or novel inter­ mediates, and may be formulated into compositions for  combating insect and acarine pests.
