<ABSTRACT>
Elastic, ceramic insulation spring body (1) as a connecting integration between ceramic (2) and metalli (3) components exposed to particularly high temperatures, aggressive media and strong mechanical loads. The insulating spring body (1) is formed annular in the form of a sleeve (4) or socket, wherein the surface can be smooth, roughened, profiled (6) or slotted. As preferably used materials, aluminum titanate, cordierite or silicon nitride come into question. The insulation spring body (1) must be a thermal conductivity of 0.02 to 0.2 to 1500 ° C between the ceramic and metallic component between 0.02 to 0.2 (W / EMK) and an E module from 5000 to 150 000 (N / mm²) without plastic deformability, so that in each operating state, an elastic, connection between the components (2, 3) is retained. Interesting applications for the object according to the invention result for example in turbochargers, internal combustion engines, heat exchangers, etc.
