<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to new quaternary groups containing acrylyl or methacryliclurreas and of these compounds produced homo and copolymers and the use of these homo- and copolymers as flocculation, sedimentation, drainage and retinal assistance.The compounds are prepared by reaction from the corresponding halogen-substituted ureas with trialkylamines, preferably in a solvent.Due to their double bond, they can be polymerized to homo and copolymers which are suitable as flocculation, sedimantation, drainage and retinal agents.
