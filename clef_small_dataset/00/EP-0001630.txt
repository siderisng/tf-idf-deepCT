<ABSTRACT>
In a hinge joint with a compact hinge head and porous mounting pins (1, 2), the joint heads are provided with recesses (26) and (27) and connected and guided by at least one ring (3) or (4).The ring (E) (3, 4) are in grooves (9, 10) which are arranged concentrically for the imaginary axis of rotation of the joint.For the use of the hinge joint in the medical range, it may be expedient that the fastening pins (1, 2) have a microporous structure and the rings (3, 4) are connected via webs (28) with covers (29).
