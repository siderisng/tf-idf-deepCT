<ABSTRACT>
the present invention relates to gardening implements.  fitted onto a male connector (2) of the tool (1) is a plastic connecting bush (3) having a slightly frustoconical longitudinal bore (8), a hollow tubular metal handle element (4) being fitted onto the bush (3).  the element (4) comprises in succession a first slightly frustoconical end (9), a frustoconical intermediate part (12) and a straight cylindrical main part (13).  in one embodiment, the main part is extended by a slightly frustoconical second end (11) for the purpose of receiving a wooden handle element (16) by fitting and adhesive bonding.  the invention is used for individual fixed-handle gardening implements.  <image>
