<ABSTRACT>
Device for releasably connecting two axially adjoining dental handpiece or the like parts (1, 3).The parts (1, 3) to be connected have axial, protrusion securing guide glow of the (10, 11) and contain coupling members (12) which engage with axial collapsing of the parts (1, 3).There are further means which exert in engagement with coupling members (12) to this one of the force generated by at least one spring element, which the two parts (1, 3) indepactively positively pushed together.The arrangement is thus trusted and the spring force chosen so that the connection is detachable again against the spring force by axial train.
