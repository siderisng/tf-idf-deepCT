<ABSTRACT>
an inorganic refractory insulation article comprising  randomly oriented refractory fibers retained by dried colloidal  silica uniformly distributed throughout the article  and containing from about 0. 001 to about 1. 5 percent by  weight of the article of aluminium chlorhydrate.  the invention  further comprises the process for making the article  by forming a fiber mat from a fiber slurry, impregnating the  mat with a conditioned colloidal silica composition comprising  colloidal silica, acid, aluminium chlorhydrate and water  and drying the mat to form the article.
