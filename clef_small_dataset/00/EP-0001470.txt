<ABSTRACT>
a process for the enzymatic streatment of waste water  of wheat starch plants in which the waste water is subjected  before or alternating with the first steps of the concentration  process to a process at least consisting of   a) a heat treatment followed by separation of the solid  substance, and   b) a treatment with enzyme preparations exhibiting amylase  and/or cellulase and/or hemi-cellulase activity  and optionally glucoamylase and/or β-glucanase  activity.
