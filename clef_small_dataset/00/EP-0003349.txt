<ABSTRACT>
1.  a signaling stick, made up of a handle (1), a support (2) fixed to said handle and at least one exchangeable marker (3) supported by the support (2), characterized in that the support (2) is made up of a back part (7) in the form of a plate and a front part (6) in the form of a frame and between the front and back parts at least one such marker (3) may be placed, in that the support (2) has u-like cross-section where the back part (7) and the front part (6) are joined together, in that the support (2) on the two side outer edges (15 and 16) and at the lower edge (17) nearest the handle (1) is closed in and in that the top edge (18) has a narrow opening (19) for putting in one or more markers (3).
