<ABSTRACT>
The compounds of the formula in which R is halogen, alkyl, alkoxy, alkylthio, alkynesulfonyl, haloalkyl, nitro, cyano, optionally sub stubous phenyl or optionally sub stubous phenoxy, R¹ for the groupings -O-R², -S (O) m -r³ or -O-Co-R⁴ is R² for alkyl, alkenyl, alkynyl, cycloalkyl, given if substituted aryl, optionally sub stubous arylalkyl or given of sub stubous arylalkenyl, R³ is hydrogen or R², R⁴ for R² and haloalkyl, optionally sub stubous phenoxyalkyl, amino, alkylamino, dial kylamino, alkyl alkylcarbonylamino or optionally substituted phenylamino is a for an anion of an inorganic acid and its hydrates, ME stand for a metal, n is intended for all numbers from O to 5, m For all numbers from 0 to 2, P stands for whole numbers from 1 to 6 and X stands for whole numbers from 1 to 4. When obtaining the corresponding 1-phenyl-2-triazolyl ethane derivatives in the presence of a solvent is reacted with metal salts of the formle MEA PXK H₂O. As plant protection products, the active ingredients according to the invention can be used successfully on combating venturia species, genuine mildew melts and against cereal illities.
