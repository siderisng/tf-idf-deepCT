<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for obtaining nitro-hydroxy aromatics containing wastes as they z.b.In the nitration of aromatic compounds or in the production of nitro-hydroxy aromatics.The processing of the nitro-hydroxy aromatics containing wastes takes place in such a way that the nitro-hydroxyaromatics containing wastewater is heated under air and oxygen termination to temperatures in the range of 150 ° C to 500 ° C.The wastewater pretreated according to the method according to the invention can be supplied directly to the biological wastewater treatment plant without dilution.
