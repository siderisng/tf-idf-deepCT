<ABSTRACT>
a method and apparatus for controlling radiation beam  hole drilling, especially laser hole drilling, in which radiation  passing through a drilled hole is detected and utilized to con­ tollably terminate the hole-drilling beam action.  when a  backer is used on the workpiece exit surface to improve hole  configuration and resist penetration of the beam, the backer  transmits impinged radiation from the hole location to a  detector coupled to a controller.  in a preferred embodiment, a  pulsed drilling laser beam is used with a continuous coaxial  indicator laser beam; an epoxy resin backer adhered to the  workpiece exit surface transmits indicator beam radiation to a  fiber optic receptor that guides the radiation to a photomultip­ lier which is coupled to a controller of the drilling laser.  
</ABSTRACT>
