the device for measuring the electrolyte content of air is capable of detecting even the smallest electrolyte concentrations rapidly and accurately.  the device operates using an electrical conductivity measurement circuit (20) with a sensor (2, 3), around which the air to be investigated flows and over which distilled water conducted within the closed circuit is passed, which distilled water is constantly deionised.  <image>
