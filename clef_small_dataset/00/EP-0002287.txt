<ABSTRACT>
The steel plate tape for the promotion of bulk solids has a plate division, which corresponds to a multiple of the chain pitch and on rails ongoing support plates (1), each at its rear end with roller axes (2) and truck members (3) and at their front ends with chain axes ( 5) and are articulated with a slot support tabs (7) on the chains (4) articulated. The cable chains (4) consist of uniform normal lane chain links with connecting bolts of uniform length. For the purpose of light interchangeability, the driving members (3) of the roller axes (2) are laterally on the outer members of the pulling chains (4) and the support tabs (7) of the chain axes (5) with a bolt (6) in the middle of the chain portion above the chain center axis on one other chain link releasably attached. The tooth pitch of the sprockets is formed so that a tooth gap is located on the position of the bolt (6) of the support straps (7).
