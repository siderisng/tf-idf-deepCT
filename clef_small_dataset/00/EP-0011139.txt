In a load-bearing motor vehicle strut in a wheel axle pin (1) supporting outer tube (2) a shock absorber cylinder (4) is housed, which is to be connected at the top with the chassis frame and its downwardly external piston rod (5) at the bottom (3) of the outer tube is attached. In order to improve the lubrication between the shock absorber cylinder (4) and the guide rings (8.9) and the sealing of the lubricant ring chamber (10) and at the same time a good dirt deflector effect at the top of the outer tube (2) exciting shock absorber cylinder (4) to achieve, the sealing rings are arranged above the guide rings (8.9) with axially limited motion play and as lubricating-grease-sealing felt rings (11,12) exhibition cover DET, wherein above the upper felt ring (11) at the same time its axial motion game limiting, from abrasion stem plastic plastic existing existing Dirt deflector ring existing is.
