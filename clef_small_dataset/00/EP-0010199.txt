<ABSTRACT>
Exhaust control system for imprinting in which the control of the DC motor is completely numeric.A coding wheel (26) connected to the motor shaft (25) and a detector (27.28) determine the time between two discrete positions of the motor shaft (25).The difference from a predetermined time is stored in a register and then converted into a pulse width modulated signal which controls the speed of rotation of the motor (25).
