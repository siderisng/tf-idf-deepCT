<ABSTRACT>
The fermentor consists of a pond (1) with slope walls, with a central stirrer (13, 14, 15).A tarpaulin (10) anchored at its periphery on the walls of the basin and totally immersed leads the fermentation gases into a metal bell (12) with immersed edges, to evacuate them by the tubu Lure (21).The purifying water are introduced after heating to 35 ° C in a heater (24), towards the bottom of the basin in the vicinity of the agitator (15).Application to the purification of wastewater with sugar and sugar refinery.
