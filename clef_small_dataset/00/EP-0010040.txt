<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the transmission of an analog audio-frequency signal during the time space reserved for the synchronization pulses of a television signal.The transmission method comprises a coding of the sound signal (its) of converting it into n bits (NS) to a double frequency frequency and to emit during each synchronization pulse four N bits (A, B, B,C, D) corresponding to different samples, which introduces a redundancy of two and a decorrelation between the different samples.The method comprises a decoding of comparing both the two representations of the same sample emitted during two synchronization pulses and to choose according to the result of the comparison of a representation for each sample.The invention applies in particular to storage in a video disk
