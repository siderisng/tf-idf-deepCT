<ABSTRACT>
As a new industrial product, the tris (amino-5 thia-3 pentyl) amine.This product can be obtained by action of the tris (ethanethiol) amine on the aziridine according to a molar aziridine / tris (ethanethiol) amine ratio between 3 and 3.5.This product can be used as an intermediary for the manufacture of lubricating oil additives.
