<ABSTRACT>
The device comprises two pairs of presense chambers (3, 4) whose backwalls are formed to define two pairs with respect to folding pads (1) and pressing pads (2) shaped. Externally to substantially match the shape of the balls of Bouette. The folding pads (1) have a constant sensing thickness (d) while the wise pressing pads have more important thickness (CD) side ends than the central portion (DM). The method consists of applying the folding pads first, then the pressing pads to press the capsule with formation of four protruding folds to the outside, the assembly of the pads then retracted, then the smoothing pads are Nou calf alone applied with overflow of the lateral parts thick around the collar of the bottle. Application in particular to the application of capsules on bottles of champagne or sparkling.
