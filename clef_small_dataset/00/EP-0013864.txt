<ABSTRACT>
The slide prosthesis for the knee joint is prerequisite to be attached to be cement-free, wherein the tibial parts (4), the femund particles (17) and the surface replacement for the knee disc have anchoring pins (8, 9; 21, 22) having a sawtooth-like orBone-shaped profile are provided.Preferably, the tibial and knee disk parts are made of carbon fiber reinforced plastic and the moles of aluminum oxide ceramic.In order to increase the bones to the prosthesis parts for förnn, they are coated at the corresponding places with bioactive particles such as apatite-containing glasses, apatite particles or embedded bone particles.
