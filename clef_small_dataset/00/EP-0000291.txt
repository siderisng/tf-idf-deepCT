<ABSTRACT>
a pharmaceutical composition of prolonged effect in the  treating of tropical diseases comprises an organic nitrogen  base drug which is effective against diseases such as  malaria, dispersed in a polymeric matrix of a copolymer of  the compound    and a comonorner which contains more than one functional  group selected from hydroxy and carboxy groups.
