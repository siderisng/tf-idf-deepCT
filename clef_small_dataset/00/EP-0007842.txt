<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a detecton and radiation location device.This device comprises, in a sealed enclosure (1), at least one cathode (2) carried out to a first potential by rap port at a reference potential, a plurality of anodes (3) filiforms isolated between them, and the photosensitive means;This sealed enclosure is provided with a transparent porthole (5) located opposite the cathode and anodes;This device is characterized in that it comprises an insulating support (6) presented two faces;Part of one face (7) located opposite the porthole, is covered with a layer (14) of a conductive material forming a mesh network constituting the cathode;The ends of the anodes have the shape of a tip (8) and their axes coincide with the axes of the meshes of the network;These tips are set back to the network of conductive mesh.Application to photon detection.
