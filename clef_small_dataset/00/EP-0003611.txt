<ABSTRACT>
In a spreading apparatus for treating hip dysplasia, a backrest (1) stored in the area of the cross leg is provided on the back of a patient, from which two side parts (2) extend forwards.With the side parts (2) is connected to a leaf spring-like thigh rail (5), which is connected forward to the end of the basin and thighs on the patient.The femoral rails (5) are diagonally from the top to the rear at the bottom of the insides of the thighs.At the end of the thigh rails (5), a support shell (6) is formed.The support shells (6) are to be applied to or above the knee lug at each a thigh of the patient.The expansion device is designed as a total leaf spring-like, the spreading clip-free spreading clip.
