<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to agrochemical agents based on azulminic acid stabilized by reaction with aminoplast types and carbonyl compounds or their condensa tion products, as well as the use of these agrochemical agents in agriculture. by reaction with aminoplastial artists and carbo nyl compounds or their optionally chemically modified condensation products stabilized with azul mines acids with a content of 0.5 to 55 weight percent at ionic groups of the formula in which R has the meaning given in the description, and with a content of 0 5 to 15% by weight of the groups of the formula formed by decarboxylation reactions, as well as their acid addition salts, complex compounds and their mixed products with additives can be used as agrochemical agents. The substances according to the invention can be used as such or in their formulations for supplying plants with nutrients or as a soil enhancer.
