1.  folding box consisting of a blank, especially of cardboard, with two longitudinal and two lateral walls and - provided at at least one of their free ends - first and second longitudinal and lateral gluing tabs, which are each foldable around associated longitudinal and lateral articulation lines and wherein the length of the longitudinal gluing tabs between the free end and the longitudinal articulation line of the first longitudinal gluing tab is greater than the amount of half the width of the lateral walls, characterized thereby, that the lateral gluing tabs (7, 8) each display a respective slot (14) running out from their free ends (13) and from the direction of the first longitudinal gluing tab (5) obliquely into the area of the lateral gluing tab (7, 8) that at least the first longitudinal gluing tab (5) is shortened to a length substantially equalling the spacing "a" - measured parallel to the lateral acticulation lines (12) - between the end (15) of the slot (14) and that edge (9) of the lateral wall (3, 4) which adjoins the first longitudinal gluing tab (5).
