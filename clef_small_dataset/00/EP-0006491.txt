<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a device for supporting a weapon system on a combat vehicle (100), in which vehicle the weapon system is arranged on a platform (201) and rotatably mounted about a vertical axis (X).Between the chassis frame (110) of the vehicle and the platform (210), an intermediate frame arranged over three supporting devices with the chassis frame operatively connected intermediate frame arranged, which together with the platform loading platform (210) in the direction of rotation of the vehicle (100) seen at leastOn one side with respect to the chassis frame (110) is lowered and / or liftable.The individual support devices of the three-point support are formed for receiving angular and sliding movements.
