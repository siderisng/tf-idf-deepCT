<ABSTRACT>
the saddle, completely made from rigid plastic material, comprises an inner frame 5 provided with two vertical lugs 3 and 4 which are turned downwards, in order to support, by means of a square shaft 2, a drum 1 equipped with grooves oriented parallel to its axis.  this drum 1 is held by a clamping system formed by two grooved jaws 7 and 9, one of which is provided at the end of the saddle-holder tube 8 while the other, provided as to be removable, is attached via its end 10 to the inside of a slot 11 in the first.  in the opposite direction, the two jaws are clamped against each other by a bolt 12 which passes right through.  <image>
