<ABSTRACT>
Mixtures of metal oxides and inorganic turbidity prepared by flame hydrolysis are known.For the preparation of these mixtures complex mixing methods are required to achieve intimate mixing of the individual components.According to the method according to the invention, the particular activity of metal oxides prepared by flame hydrolysis, as it consists of in the formation phase, is used to interfere in the inorganic turbid in the finest distribution in the metal oxides prepared by Flammhy Drolysis.Surely, these agglomerated mixtures provide special properties of the thermal insulation, compressive strength and durability resistance.
