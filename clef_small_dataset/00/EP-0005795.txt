<ABSTRACT>
Vinyl ester can be polymerized - optionally together with other monomers - in the presence of emulsifiers and with the help of radical-forming initiators in aqueous medium at elevated temperature.The simultaneous presence of dispersing aids is advantageous.For the use of the obtained dispersions as paints, it is expedient to add water-soluble salts of a copolymer from styrene and a maleic acid derivative after production.The thus modified plastic dispersions have a high pigment-binding capacity and are particularly suitable for the production of dispersion colors and paper coating compositions.
