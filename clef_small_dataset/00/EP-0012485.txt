<TITLE>
process for preparing a gel-forming protein product; gel-forming product and gel obtained by the process.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

<ABSTRACT>
a gel-forming protein product is prepared from a raw  material for either defatted or non-defatted oil seeds or  products derived therefrom, which contain proteins, carbo­ hydrates having a polysaccharide fraction and possibly oils.   the proteins are subjected to a thermal treatment in water.   the thermal treatment comprises heating the proteins in  one or more stages at a temperature above 50°c.  the pro­ teins need not be separated from other constituents of the  raw materials, but have to be in a state of separation from  the polysaccharide fraction during the heat treatment.  the  protein product can be formed into gels having desirable  compressibility and elasticity characteristics.  the gels are  useful in foodstuffs.  
</ABSTRACT>
