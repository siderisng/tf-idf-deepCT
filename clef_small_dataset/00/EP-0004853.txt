<TITLE>
improved apparatus for remotely repairing tubes in a steam generator.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

<ABSTRACT>
apparatus that may be assembled inside the head of a  steam generator and can perform various repair and inspection  operations on the steam generator's tubes and  tubesheet.  selective engagement of an included tool holder  (102) and associated tools with the tube (18) and tubesheet  (14) is provided by a gear train which has a portion which  houses a tool drive motor (78).  the drive motor (78) provides  power to a tool holder-mounted tool chuck (104) for driving  the various tools insertable therein, the drive motor (78)  being disposed within a hollow lead screw (96) of the tool  holder (102).  the apparatus is compact and easily insertable into, and  remotely operable in, the limited space in the steam  generator head.
