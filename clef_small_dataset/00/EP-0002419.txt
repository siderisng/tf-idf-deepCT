<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an assembly method made of aluminum and steel parts. This method is characterized by: a coating of the steel part by a metal film on the part of the surface to be assembled, the arrangement of silicon grains at the aluminum / steel interface, - a protection against the oxidation of the faces to be assembled, - a heating of the parts so as to reach the interface a temperature between 500 ° C and 650 ° C and the application of sufficient pressure to expel completely or partly the liquid eutectic thus formed, -A cooling of the whole. The assembly thus produced finds its application in particular in the so-called "clads" junction parts for igneous electrolysis tanks, in the shields requiring high mechanical strength and, in all cases where we seek to obtain links with Good electrical and mechanical properties for continuous use in a temperature range ranging from at least -200 ° C to at least + 450 ° C.
