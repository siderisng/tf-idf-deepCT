<ABSTRACT>
The present patent application claims an improved method for hydrogenation of low molecular weight hydroxydehydes, hydroxycetones and polyvalent alcohols as originating in the self-condensation of formaldehyde, characterized in that in a first stage at a pH of 7.5 to 12.5is hydrogenated, wherein the content of the reactor filling at reducible groups is the value of 2 wt.-% must not exceed, then the reaction mixture is adjusted to a pH of 3 to 7 and adhaled in a second stage at this pH at another hydrogenation catalyst than in the first stage.The patent application further claims the use of the process products according to the invention for the preparation of polyurethane imbalances, polyether and polyester polyols, and nonionic surfactants.
