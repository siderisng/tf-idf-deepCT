The invention relates to a method for producing surfaces (54, 54 ', 55) of a cutting head (53) on a cured knife steel (5, 62), as well as a device for carrying out this method. The cured and thus provided with a cutting head (53) knife steels (5) are provided for insertion in the knife head of a toothed manch. In a further step, the surfaces (63, 63 ', 54) of the pre-profile (61) to surfaces (54, 54', 55) on the previously used grinding machine are ground.
