<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for controlling the combustion of liquid fuels, as well as a burner assembly for carrying out the method.A fuel beam is detected by the side from the combustion air as a sputtering medium in a mixed channel (7).The pressure of the fuel at the input of the fuel nozzle (4) and the average relative speed of the combustion air introduced as a sputtering medium are inversely proportionally adjustable.For this purpose, the air inlet openings (10, 11) are changeable in their opening cross-section and preferably connected to a feeding amount of the fuel control valve (115).
