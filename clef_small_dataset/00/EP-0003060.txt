<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a novel method for separating known substituted vinylcyclopro pancarboxylic acids into its stereoisomers, which is characterized in that the free vinylcyclopropancar bonic acids are converted into slightly alkaline, aqueous solution with a quantity of amine salt corresponding to the CIS content, the corresponding CIS/ Trans-Isomer salts separated from each other due to their different solubility, and the CIS or trans-isomeric vinylcyclopropane carboxylic acids are released and recrystallized with the help of strong acids in freedom.
