<ABSTRACT>
1.  a process for stabilizing polyamide moulding composition against thermooxidative degradation by adding from 0. 1 to 2 % by weight, based on polyamide, of a conventional stabilizer, characterized in that the stabilizer is added in the form of a concentrate in a cooh-containing copolymer of draft copolymer of ethylene or propylene having an acid number of from 10 to 40, the stabilizer content of the concentrate being between 5 and 60 % by weight.
