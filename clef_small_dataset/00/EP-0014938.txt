<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for monitoring conductor currents on a residual current that changes the magnetization of a magnetic circuit (10).According to the invention, the magnetic core (12) is each turned alternately by follow-up of magnetization pulses (P₁ to P₀) and determined until which pulse number (P₁ to P₃) the pulse height exceeds an adjustable voltage set value.From the change of this pulse number (P₁ to P₂ and P₁ to P₄), an output signal is derived.The method has the advantage that balancing temperature and drift problems can not occur.
