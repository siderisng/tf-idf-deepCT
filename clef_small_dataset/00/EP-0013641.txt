<ABSTRACT>
This device allows the automatic testing of digital filters without disturbance of their operation.A proper detection system comprises the radar or sonar detection circuits, for example two digital filters 22,23, and a recombination circuit 24. The automatic test device comprises a circuit, 35, allowing the choice of recurrence.and the test sample connected to the detection circuits, this representation controls a circuit, 39, elaborating a binary pulse applied at the input of the preselected filter by it.The response of the tested filter is compared, 38, at the theoretical response stored permanently in a memory, 36, and in case of failure a signal is issued.The invention applies to all devices using digital filters, in particular electromagnetic detection equipment.
