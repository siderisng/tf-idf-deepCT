<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method of combustion of a liquid fuel, as well as a burner for its implementation. The burner according to the invention, which is provided with a hollow vaporization body, a combustion and heating means for booting means is remarkable in that it comprises a fixed body (18, 38, 58), provided with a channel or tubular duct of vapo rushing (12, 32, 52), in which is carried by a vector fluid, or not, a liquid fuel, said channel or conduct opening, directly or not , in a mixing chamber (111, 311, 511) separate from said channel and which also receives a combustion, the heating means (17, 37) being arranged to heat the fixed body at start-up, while the shape of said body is Such, that the combustion flame clears the latter in part, such that the liquid fuel is sprayed or gasified away from the flame, at least in part and preferably in whole, in the channel or vaporization.
