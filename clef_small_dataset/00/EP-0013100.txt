<ABSTRACT>
in the oxidation of a substituted aromatic compound such  as p-xylene using a heavy metal catalyst promoted by  bromine the effluent gases from the oxidation contain methyl  bromide and are treated for the recovery of bromine.  a variety  of methods are described whereby the bromine may be reco­ vered, preferably in a form suitable for reuse in the oxidation  process.
