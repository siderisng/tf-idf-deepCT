<ABSTRACT>
An injection device for intramuscular injection of a medicament, primarily of insulin, is equipped with such operation after a quick piercing operation, the injecting of the liquid can optionally be performed at different speed. In the injection device (1) in which a holder (4) receiving the syringe (4) receiving bracket (9) is clamped against the action of a spring and lockable in the retracted position by means of a latch (17), a manual actuator (3) for pretending the Piston (6) and the piston rod (7, 7a) of the syringe (4), with which on the one hand on the latch (17) and on the other hand on the piston rod (7, 7a) of the syringe (4) is act. A displaceably mounted push rod (23) may be provided, the front end (23a) activably acts indirectly to the latch (17) and the rear end (23b) engages directly with the end of the piston rod (7) of the syringe (4).
