<ABSTRACT>
a process for preparing nitrogenous zeolites (such as  zsm-5) in which the form of the crystals, such as their shape,  size, smoothness or discreteness.  is determined by including  in the forming solution an organic basic nitrogen compound  in addition to the organic nitrogenous template.  the forming  solution conventional for a given zeolite is supplemented by  certain amines or quaternary ammonium compounds in  order to achieve the desired effect.
