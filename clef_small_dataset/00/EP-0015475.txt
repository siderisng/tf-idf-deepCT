<ABSTRACT>
The invention is based on an X-ray diffractometer with a local detector in which in a digital adder, the sum of the digital values for the respective location of the detector and for the position of each registered X-ray quantum is formed in the detector and used for address control of a multi-channel analyzer.In order to enable the absorption of diffraction profiles as a function of a measuring parameter such as temperature or pressure, it is provided that the adder (20) additionally supplies the output of a digital area switch (13), which by a measuring parameter (14) on the sample (2)is actuated.
