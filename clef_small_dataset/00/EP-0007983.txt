<ABSTRACT>
tertiary amines containing impurities, especially primary and secondary amines, are purified according to the invention by being passed, alone or in admixture with an organic solvent, over an adsorbent for the impurities, optionally followed by isolating the pure tertiary amine from the non- adsorbed material.  the adsorbent may be an aluminium oxide having a specific surface area of 100 to 400 m<2>/g and an activity on the brockmann scale of from 1 to 3, a silica gel having a specific surface area of 500 to 700 m<2>/g, a silanised silica gel having a specific surface area of 300 to 400 m<2>/g or a calcium zeolite having a specific surface area of 500 to 600 m<2>/g. 
</ABSTRACT>
