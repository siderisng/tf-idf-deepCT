<TITLE>
process for preparing anhydrous caustic alkali melts.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

</INVENTOR>
<ABSTRACT>
the process consists in melting alkali metal hydroxide having a water content of up to 10% together with sodium monoxide and/or potassium monoxide.  heating to temperatures above the melting point is not necessary.  to prepare caustic alkali mixtures, for which the process is particularly suitable, it is sufficient to employ temperatures between 180 and 250 deg c.  the heating takes place in the absence of moisture, for example in a nitrogen atmosphere.
