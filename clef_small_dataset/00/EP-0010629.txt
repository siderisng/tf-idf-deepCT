<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for preventing the combination of free-flowing phosphor pentachloride in closed containers, which is characterized in that one in the container before or after or during the filling of the phosphor pentachloride is about 1 to 15% by weight, based on the PCL₅Amount, a PCL₅ inert, slightly disturbed organic liquid with a boiling point of about 20 to 200 ° C and closes the container airtight.
