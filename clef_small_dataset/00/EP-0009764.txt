<ABSTRACT>
A cover conveyor comprises two conveyor belts (4, 5) with two laterally patched wave edges (10, 11), which have the same height for both conveyor belts (4, 5), but different transverse distance such that they in the conveying line between the superimposed För Thicks (4, 5) are paired side by side and together with the conveyor belts (4, 5) a box shape, formed on the wave edges labyrinthlike sealed conveyor space (26). a mutual lifting of the conveyor belts (4, 5) and thus leakage of this conveyor space (26) is prevented by at least one series of lateral profiles (20, 21) on each wave edge (10, 11) which at its subpages (22, 23) with the corresponding pro len of the respectively adjacent wave edge engagement and hold the heads of the shaft edges (10, 11) to the conveyor belts (4, 5) pressed. At the beginning and at the end of the for the route, the profiles (20, 21) are brought by a lateral tilting movement of the respective outer wave edge (11) in the wave edge pairs in and out of engagement. The tilting movement can be generated by conically tapered deflection drums (6a) due to the edge regions (27). The mutual cohesion of the wave edges on their profiles makes it possible to realize a cover conveyor with you TEM conveyor space under largely renunciation of and reversing for the two conveyor belts in the course of the conveying line and thus plantally very easy to realize.
