<ABSTRACT>
the valve comprises a base body (1), into the surface (3) of which a groove-shaped channel (4) is machined.  the channel (4) is covered by an elastic diaphragm (6) which is pressed against the surface (3) of the base body (1) by an opposing body (2) at the side of the channel (4).  at least one die (9) is guided in a movable manner in the opposing body (2), with the aid of which the diaphragm (6) can be pushed, in an elastically deforming manner, into the channel (4) to block the medium flowing in the channel (4).  <image>
