<ABSTRACT>
The calling device comprises an alternative signal generator and a continuous current generator connected to an injection line of the call signal, as well as a central loop detection circuit (11) in the line (12) and a switch (7), and, in series in the transmission line of each additional extension, a local loop detection circuit (31, 32) and a bidirectional contact (41.42.).When closing a loop, the switch (7) opens the alternative signal circuit, and the curly transmission line, previously connected to the injection line (12), is connected to the connection network (13) from the installa tone before the switch (7) closes the circuit.Application: Telephony.
