<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a method for obtaining isobutene of isobutene-containing C₄-hydrocarbon mixtures by reacting the mixture with a primary alcohol in the presence of an acidic condensation agent and decomposition of the tertiary ether formed in the presence of an acidic catalyst at elevated temperatures, in which one is a primary C₃or C₄ alcohol used.
