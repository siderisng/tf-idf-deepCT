<ABSTRACT>
A device for bundling of banknote bundles is described by means of bands withdrawn by the introduced bundle of two storage rollers and extend above and below the bundle to a welding and cutting device, which forms a final weld shot for the bundle contained by a pressed punch bundleThe two bands are partitioned behind this weld and connecting them together by a second weld.Bunch of different widths and thickness can be bordered without prior adjustment, as movable stops that carry the bundle into the working position ensure that the back of the bundle is located where the weld is to be attached.
