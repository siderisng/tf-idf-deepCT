<ABSTRACT>
the present application relates to a procedure for the combustion of salt-laden residues in a fluid bed furnace at temperatures between 730 and 900 deg c, with drawing off of the fluid bed mass, in which, before a salt concentration is reached in the fluid bed mass, at which the latter would bake together at the operating temperature, a part of the fluid bed mass is drawn off and replaced by fresh fluid material.  <image>
