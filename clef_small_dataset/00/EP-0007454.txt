<ABSTRACT>
Name disc printer in which the type disk (1) is associated with a spring washer (5) of the same division.When stopping a type on the finger (2-1, 2-2, 2-3) of the type disc (1) (3-1,3-23-3), this finger becomes against the spring force of the fingers associated with it(5-1, 5-2 5-3) of the spring washer (5) moves.The fingers (5-1,5-2, 5-3) of the spring washer (5) have a different length, so that same pressure thicknesses are achieved for the different characters.
