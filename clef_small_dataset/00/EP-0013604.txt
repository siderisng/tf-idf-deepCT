<ABSTRACT>
a body implantable lead comprises a lead portion (10)  having a distal end portion (12) and a proximal terminal end  portion (13).  end portion (12) comprises a helical tissue secur­ ing member (42) adapted to be screwed into animal tissue by  the rotation of a stylet (20) via slotted screwdriver end (62) and  slot (64).  a portion of the stylet has a thinner diameter over a  portion of its length near the distal end (12) to permit smooth  transmission of torque by rotation of the stylet even in appli­ cations where the lead and stylet are tightly bent in the vicinity  of the thinned portion of the lead.
