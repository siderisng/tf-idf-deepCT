<ABSTRACT>
In a process for the preparation of semiconductor assemblies with insulated areas (30) of polycrystalline silicon, by taking the coating of phosphorus silicate glass provided on the top surfaces of these regions (30) of phosphorus silicate glass, by applying an additional, contraction of the top surface coating of phosphorus silicate glass heat treatmentlargely eliminated.This allows an approximately desired doping of the portions (30) of polycrystalline silicon from the PSG layer (40 ') provided on the top surfaces.Also treated are accordingly designed semiconductor assemblies.
