<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method and a device for boring metal or CERMET parts and BO surfaces on the surface.The pieces are available in a te 3 between 850 and 1150 ° C. and are subjected, in the presence of boron carbide, at a gas stream of trifluoruoro boroxole (Bof) ₃.Boron carbide is advantageously slowly sprayed and out of contact with parts to be borne.
