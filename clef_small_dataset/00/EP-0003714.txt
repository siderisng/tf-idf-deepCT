<ABSTRACT>
In a device for screwing fastening means, the feeding means of the fasteners and the holder of the fasteners should be improved during installation on the workpiece.Furthermore, the width of this device is supposed to be very low at least in one direction very low for the use of the device in relatively narrow shaft tunes profiled plates.For this purpose, the feed shaft (5) is carried out as part of a slide (7) relative to the screwing device.At the free end of this carriage (7), two pivotably kept baking (6) are provided for guiding the fastener when placing it.The baking (6) are blocked or released by a sliding plate (8) displaceable in response to the position of the carriage (7) within the screw device.Furthermore, the pivot plane of the baking (6) coincides with the feed plane of the fasteners.
</ABSTRACT>
