<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a contact arrangement for vacuum switches with coaxially arranged pot contacts whose slotted contact carrier is provided with a contact ring.According to the invention, the free surface of the contact ring (12) is provided radially inwardly and outwardly respectively with a bevel and the contact ring (12) and or the contact carrier (4) contain a zone (14) with respect to the material of the contact carrier (4)Essentially reduced electrical conductivity.With this design is obtained in the contact ring (12) current components, which stabilize a rotating arc between the contact support surfaces (13).
