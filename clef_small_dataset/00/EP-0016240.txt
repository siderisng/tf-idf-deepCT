<ABSTRACT>
the device for returning, to the abdominal cavity, a uterus turned inside out during delivery consists of a groove-shaped part and, adjoining this, a saucer-shaped part with a widened supporting surface.  the groove-shaped part tapers towards its free end directed towards the animal, approximately to a width which corresponds to the size of the birth canal following delivery.  straps are provided for securing to the animal. 
</ABSTRACT>
