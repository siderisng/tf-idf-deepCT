<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to radiation-curable binders consisting of a reaction product of (a) 1 grammal equivalent NCO of a polyisocyanate with 2 to 3 isocyanate groups per molecule, (b) 0 to 0.7 gramquivalent OH of a polyol with at least 2 OH groups per molecule, free of Organically bound sulfur, (C) 0.01 to 0.7 gram equivalents OH of a hydroxyalkyl acrylate having 2 to 6 C atoms in the alkyl group, characterized in that the reaction product additionally (d) 0.01 to 0.3 gram equivalents Oh of a thio or polyols containing dithio and thio- and dithiogroups containing at least 2 OH groups per molecule, the sum of the OH gram equivalents (b) to (d) is 1 to 1.2 and the content of organically bonded sulfur, Around the thio and dithiogroups, 0.01 to 10 wt. %, based on binders, is. The binders are much more reactive than comparable sulfur-free binders and yield very elastic coatings after curing.
