<ABSTRACT>
Stressed is a physiologically and food legally harmless baking racing oil component that can be used to the tale of natural, spared sperm oil in separation oils for baked goods.It consists of a synthetic wax ester from: a) a C₁₂-C₂₀ fatty acid mixture of iodine number 48-96 having a proportion of at least 90% C₁₋₋₁₈ fatty acids;b) a C₁₂-C₂₀ fatty alcohol mixture of iodine number 50-95 having a proportion of at least 90% C₁₋₋₁₈₋ fatty alcohols.The wax nest should have the following characteristics: iodine number 40 - 110;Saponification number approx. 110;Acid number approx. 1;OH number about 5;Setting range 10 - 30 ° C.
