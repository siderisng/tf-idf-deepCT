Polyglycidylamines having at least two glycidyl group pen bonded to hydrogen-free nitrogen atoms, or their salts are particularly inter esse as additives for paints based on aqueous polymer dispersions, since they improve the wet adhesion of the paints on alkyd resin-coated intubs and their block strength.
