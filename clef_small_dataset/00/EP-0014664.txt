<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method and a machine of manufacture of a perishable product conditioning receptacle.To achieve the receptacle, consisting of an expanded polystyrene bottom, coated internally with a metallic polystyrene film, the hot securing of these two elements is operated by interlaying an intermediate film 40 of extruded polystyrene.This type of receptacle can take the form of a tray for packaging foods such as meat.
