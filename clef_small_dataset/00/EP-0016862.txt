<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a control device for a hydraulic system with an adjustable, but not sweepable pump (10), which controls pressure means to at least one consumer (19,24).It is essential that a defined acceleration or delay of the consumers is sufficient by defiined increase in the pressure medium stream, wherein the device is designed by various electro niche control elements so that acceleration shocks are avoided.The pump (28) is adjustable but not reversible, by the type of controller it eliminates the character of a reversible pump.
