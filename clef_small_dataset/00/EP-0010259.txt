<ABSTRACT>
a device for supporting a spindle of an open-end spin­ ning frame, which device comprises a rotatable spindle (1)  having a rotor (2) attached at one end thereof and whorl (9),  formed at the other end thereof, to be passed by a running  belt (3), a bearing casing (4) for rotatably supporting the spin­ dle (1), a holder (5) for holding the bearing casing (4) via a pair  of resiliently yieldable member (6,7a).  at least one of the  resiliently yieldable member (6,7a) are detachably mounted  on a sleeve, (8,19) detachably secured to the bearing casing  (4).  a labyrinth seal is formed between the rotor and the  sleeve.  
</ABSTRACT>
