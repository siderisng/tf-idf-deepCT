The new isochroman derivatives in which R, R¹ and R² may be the same or different and are found for hydrogen order alkyl.They are obtained when converting benzodioxol derivatives of formula (Ia) with aldehydes of the formula (Ib).It has also been found that the new isochromic derivatives are synergistically influenced by the action of a) carbamates and / or b) carboxylic acid esters and / or c) phosphoric acid esters and / or d) haloalkanes.
