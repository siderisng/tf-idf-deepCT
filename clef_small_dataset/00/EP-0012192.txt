<ABSTRACT>
A device preferably described for granulating different materials is described, which consists of two against each other rotating drums (3, 4), into which the material to be pressed in flowed in flowed state inserted laterally and over a slot (16) in the inner drum(3) and pushed out via openings (17) in the outer rotating drum (4) in the drop shape.
