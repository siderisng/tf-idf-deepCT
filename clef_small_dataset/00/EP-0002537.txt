<TITLE>
device for the non-falsifiable marking of objects
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
a device for forgery-proof identification of objects (1) is to be created.  the identification of the object (1) is recorded in the form of a hologram recording on a carrier (2).  this carrier (2) is bonded to the object (1) to be identified.  the carrier (2) is constructed in such a manner that it can no longer be detached from the object (1) without destroying the recording of the hologram. 
</ABSTRACT>
