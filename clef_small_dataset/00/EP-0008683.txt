<ABSTRACT>
The process for producing largely monomeric, methanol ethereal methylolmelamines, which contain per mole of melamine more than 5 moles of formaldehyde and more than 4 moles of methyl ethers methylol groups and at a solids content of at least 95% at 25 ° C a viscosity of less than 20 pa. Shas, characterized in that melamine having an aqueous, between 30 and 50 wt.% formaldehyde-containing formalin solution set to pH values of 6.7 to 7.3, in molar ratio 1: 6.5 to 1: 8 for 1 1/2 to 5 hours at 50 to 85 ° C is methylated and theobtained suspension of the methylolmelamine with 15 to 30 moles of methanol per mole of melamine at temperatures of 30 to 6 ° C in the presence of a strong acid is ether.
