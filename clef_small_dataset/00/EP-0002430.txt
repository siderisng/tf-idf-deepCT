<ABSTRACT>
Mass spectrometers with an ion source, a mass filter and at least one ion detector, whereby mass filter and ion detector are not arranged laterally offset from one another.like FIG.1 shows, between mass filter (10) and ion detector (12), an elongated electrostatic, substantially cylindrical radial guide field is arranged, the ions with a one axial speed component to the guide field in elliptical screw paths around its axis (16) to the input of the detector (12).
