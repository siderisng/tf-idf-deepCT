<ABSTRACT>
The engine has a reduced cylindrical coil (8) formed of a wire that is spilled directly on the central portion (7) of the core (6).This central part is however coated with the film (9) which is formed of a compact insulating material and lasts strongly adhering to the core.To obtain a minimum thick film, and under rational conditions, electrophoresis is proceeded.The lower faces of the ears (4 and 5) are bare, so that the metal contact is made with the pole pieces (2 and 3).Before the electrophoresis operation, the faces of the ears intended to be denuded are covered with a mask of synthetic material deposited preferably in the form said in a suitable solvent.
