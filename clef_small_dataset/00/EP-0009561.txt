<ABSTRACT>
A photographic imaging unit with an integral structure consists of an image sheet (1) and a cover sheet (2) of the same size, wherein the two sheets are firmly connected to each other by a combination of individual mask strips and spacer strips (6), the combination of the mask strips from a mask strip(3) which consists of the developer container (7) on one side and a mask strip (4) folded over the trap (8) for excess development paste paste on the other side and on each of the two longitudinal sides individual mask strips (5) are attached.
