<TITLE>
support for activating plant growth
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>
inst europ d ecologiefr <sep>institut europeen d'ecologie <sep>institut europeen d'ecologie 1, rue des récolletsf-57000 metzfr<sep>
</APPLICANT>
<INVENTOR>
grosse charlesfr<sep>grosse, charles<sep>grosse, charles11, rue des carolingiensf-57100 thionvillefr<sep>
</INVENTOR>
<ABSTRACT>
a) support, storing the sun's heat which it passes to the plant and to the ground, and allowing watering at the precise point where the roots are.  applicant: institut européen d'ecologie.    b) the support is made of matt black metal tube varying in length and diameter according to the size of the plant.  this tube is provided with fins or bibs, likewise matt black, to increase its useful surface area.    c) this support is indicated for increasing the yield of a crop where the climate is too cold or the sunlight insufficient.  it also enables a considerable amount of water to be saved.  <image>
