Phenylcarbamic acid chlorides of the formula wherein X is an oxygen atom, a sulfur atom or the residue, R¹ denotes an aliphatic radical, R², R³ and R⁴ may be the same or different, and the because it is an aliphatic, cycloaliphatic, araliphatical or aromatic radical, R⁴ also for A hydrogen atom or an alkoxy group may also be a hydrogen atom, a halogen atom, a nitro group or an alkoxy group, may also be obtained by obtaining n-monosubstituted phenylenediamines of the formula in a first step with excess of phosgene at a temperature of - 30 ° C to + 200 ° C reacted and without separation in a second step thus obtaining nen isocyanatophenylcarbamic acid chlorides of the formula with compounds of the formula H - X - R³ (IV). The new phenylcarbamic acid chlorides obtained by the process of the invention are valuable intermediates for the production of pharmaceuticals, dyes, plant protection agents, plastics, art material and textile aids.
