<ABSTRACT>
A pipette consisting of a capillary and a liquid disposed therein pipette for the stroke-shaped application of a measured volume of a suspension has an extension (3) at the outlet opening (1) of the capillary, the width of which determines the line width independently of the capillary diameter.For the purpose of achieving good wettability, the extension (3) is provided with a slot-shaped recess (4) to the tip and / or coated with a hydrophilic material.
