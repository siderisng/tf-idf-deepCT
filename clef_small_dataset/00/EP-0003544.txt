<ABSTRACT>
In the field of food and in particular the pharmaceutical preparations, the use of the enzyme lactate dehydrogenase is proposed to avoid or mitigate the cariogenic attack.Particularly toothed products result in homogeneous distribution of the enzyme and at the same time presence of a hydrogen acceptor.The food or pharmaceutical preparations with addition of lactate dehydrogenase are advantageous in the water-poor or anhydrous state.At the same time, methods for producing tattershake, water-poor, sugary-containing, specially succharose-containing food and pharmaceutical preparations are proposed.
