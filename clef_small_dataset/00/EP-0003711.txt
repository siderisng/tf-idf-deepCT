<ABSTRACT>
the heating control device is embedded in the structure of a building and powered at very low voltage.  the device includes a plurality of sensors (s1, s2, s) of temperature and other meteorological factors, a means of summing the information coming from each sensor, a means of comparing (c11) the sum of this information, possibly assigned a controllable coefficient, with a reference value, furnishing a control signal used to monitor controllable semi-conductor elements (g1, g2) placed in series in the power supply for the radiating elements (3).  application to the electrical heating of domestic, industrial and commercial premises, in a very economical and very flexible way.  <image>
