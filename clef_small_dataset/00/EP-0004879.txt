<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for producing polyurethane soft foams made of urethane-modified aromatic polyisocyanates with a NCO content of 15 to 30 wt.-% and a viscosity of 100 to 2000 CPS / 20 Â° C, which are obtained by reacting a mixture of diphenylmethane diisocyanates and polyphenyl polymethylene polyisocyanates having a gas content of diphenylmethane diisocyanate isomers from 55 to 85 wt.%, based on the total weight of the polyisocyanate mixture and a hydroxy compound having 2 to 4 cerewitinous hydrogen atoms and a molecular weight of 60 to 1500 in the equivalence ratio of NCO group to active hydrogen atom of the hydroxyl compound of 2: 1 to 60: 1, polyols, catalysts,Drifting agents and optionally chain extensions, auxiliary and additives.
