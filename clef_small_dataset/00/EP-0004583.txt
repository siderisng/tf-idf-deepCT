<ABSTRACT>
By using non-hygroscopic crystal water-containing substances, which completely or partially dispensulate at mixing temperature in the heating of hot-to-processed bituminous mixing material at mixing temperature than additives to the mixing material, is significantly reduced without greater compression work of the cavity content in the finished surface, especially during road construction.Marshall stability is significantly increased and the deformation behavior significantly improved both at low and high leasing temperatures.This also applies to a filler for the bituminous mixed material to be processed, which contains such additives, for example, gypsum dihydrate (Caso₄2H₂O).
