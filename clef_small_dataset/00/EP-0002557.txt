<ABSTRACT>
the invention relates to a complex composition comprising:   (a) an ion exchange resin;   (b) a transition metal, and   (c) an organic linking compound which has at least one  moiety which is ionically bound to said ion exchange  resin and further has at least one moiety which is  coordinately bound to said metal.  said composition may be used in hydroformylation,  carbonylation, hydrogenation, isomerization, disproportionation,  oligomerization and fischer-tropsch reactions.
