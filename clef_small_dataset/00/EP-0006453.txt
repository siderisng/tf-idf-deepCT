<ABSTRACT>
In the event of increasing the life of gas loops with porous rinsing bodies made of graphite for non-Sen molten metal melts, the lances are flush through inert gas for preventing the oxidation of the graphite immediately after rinsing until the ignition temperature of the graphite is undercut.For this purpose, the lance is placed in an inertization cage, which consists of two articulated coupled half-shells (3, 4) which are lockable in the folded state (13) and surrounding the parts of the lance existing from graphite to form a narrow gap.
