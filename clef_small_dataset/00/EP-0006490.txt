<ABSTRACT>
In the recovery of the unreacted per pylene from the mother liquor incurred in the polymerization in the liquid per pylene, the larger part of the propylene in a first evaporator (2) at low temperature, the smaller part in a second evaporator (5) evaporates at high temperature.In this way, premature agglomerization of the atactic and / or low molecular suction dissolved in the mother liquor is avoided and a clogging of the heat exchange tubes and / or bonding of the column floors.
