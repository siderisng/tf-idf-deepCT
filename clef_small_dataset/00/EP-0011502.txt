<ABSTRACT>
a detergent product comprises a water-insoluble,  water-permeable bag containing a particulate detergent  composition, the bag material having a water-soluble or  removable water-insoluble layer to protect the bag material  from the composition and to reduce dusting.  the protective  layer may be coated on or impregnated into the bag material  or may form a continuous layer laminated to the inside of the  bag material or may be in the form of a separate inner bag.   the water-soluble material may be soluble polyvinyl alcohol,  polytheylene glycol, polysaccharide, alkali metal silicate,  soap, nonionic detergent, gelatine, salts or esters of alginic  acid.  the removable water-insoluble material may be a  silicone, a fatty acid, a water-insoluble cationic fabric sofÂ­ tener, polyvinylacetate, insoluble polyvinyl alcohol, clay,  zeolite, calcite, silica, titania or a wax.  the bag material may  be paper and/or plastics material such as polypropylene.   the detergent composition is a fully formulated composition  or other fabric treatment material.
