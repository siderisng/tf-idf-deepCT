<ABSTRACT>
the invention relates to a method and apparatus for  maintaining suitable velocity or velocity range in a slurry  pipeline system of the type with a water pipeline return and  slurried material pipeline output with more than one slurry  injection point.  a single set of primary pipelines, one water  (32) and one slurry (34), is able to serve multiple points of  hydraulic injection by means of slurry pipeline flowmeters  (84, 86, 88) downstream from each slurry injection water  by-pass junction (76, 74, 72), with each flowmeter actuating  water control valves (78, 80, 82) to bypass water to the slurry  pipeline 34 at the respective junction so as to maintain slurry  pipeline velocity within prescribed limits.
