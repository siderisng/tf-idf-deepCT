<ABSTRACT>
in the process, the adhesive used is a thermocurable single- component system comprising 1,2-epoxide compounds with imidazoline derivatives as curing agents.  the imidazolines used conform to the general formula <image> in which n is 1 to 2, r are identical or different radicals from the group comprising hydrogen, alkyl, aryl, aralkyl, cycloalkyl and heterocyclic radicals, and r' is an alkyl, aryl, alkylene or arylene radical, which may optionally be substituted by an alkyl, aryl, aralkyl, cycloalkyl or heterocyclic radical, and in the case where n = 1, is alternatively hydrogen, it also be possible for a plurality of radicals to be present.  examples of particularly suitable imidazolines are 2- phenylimidazoline and 1-phenyl-4-methylimidazoline; they are employed in amounts of 3-60% by weight, in particular 4-40% by weight, based on the resin/curing agent system.  the materials to be glued may be metallic or nonmetallic.
