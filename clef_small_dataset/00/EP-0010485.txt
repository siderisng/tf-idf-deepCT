<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a corrosion inhibitory composition, characterized in that it contains: a) at least one polyamine having a molecular weight greater than or equal to 320 and responding to the general formula R - [NH- (CH₂)₃] NNH₂ wherein R is a saturated or unsaturated aliphatic hydrocarbon radical in C₁₂-C₂₂;and N₁ represents an integer between 1 and 7 included, R and N₁ being such that the molecular weight of said polyamine is greater than or equal to 320;and b) at least one alkylenephosphonic acid derivative selected from the assembly consisting of aminoalkyl acid acid derivatives and polyphosphonic alkylene acid derivatives.The invention also relates to the method of preparing said composition.This composition is useful for inhibiting corrosion of metal surfaces with liquid or steam water.
