<ABSTRACT>
1.  a method of fabricating a plastics motor vehicle accessory which is tiltably fastenable to the vehicle body, which includes a comparatively hard core surrounded by padding and which is provided with at least one hinge joined to the core, characterized in that first a hollow, all-round closed core of a configuration corresponding exactly to but being dimensioned smaller than the final shape of the motor vehicle accessory is formed, the core having smooth external faces and the at least one hinge integrally formed therewith, and in that thereafter the core is externally foamed to form a foamed cover of uniform thickness and having a continuous outer skin.
