<ABSTRACT>
an instrumentation installation tool (30) for weldably at­ taching strain gauges (20) or other instrumentation to the in­ terior of a steam generator tube (42) includes a cylindrical  welding section (32).  the exterior surface of the section (32)  is formed with four longitudinal grooves (36a, 36b, 36c, and  38).  a welding slot (44) is situated in one of the grooves (38).   a ramp (46), oriented within the section (32) is in close prox­ imity to the slot (44), and an elongate multitipped electrode  (80) is slidably and coaxially disposed within the section (32).
