<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a measuring device for the simultaneous measurement of a temperature difference T₂ - T₁ and an absolutely temperature T₁ with temperature measuring resistors R1 and R2 for the temperatures T₁ and T₂ and a reference residual stand RO, which successively about current lines Li with power switches Si1, Si2, Si0 from a common concentration power source Q with the same current pulses I during the times T1, T2, T0, wherein in timefil stern F1, F2, F0, which open or close after the beginning and before the end of each current pulse, via sensing lines LS and difference switches SU1, SU2, SU0 capacitors C1, C2, C0 are charged to the pulse voltages U1, U2, U0 terminals at the resistors R1, R2, R0, U2 and in a fourth, at the last current pulse time window FD with the switches SD1, SD2 , SD0 the voltage differences (U2-U1) and (U1-U0) between the capacitors C2 and C1 and C1 and C0 on one side grounded capacitors N C21 and C1O are transmitted and reinforced with amplifiers V21 and V1o. In this way, a highly sensitive differential measurement is achieved with comparatively small technical effort, in which zero point drifts are not significant.
