<ABSTRACT>
a singly-balanced active mixer circuit is disclosed for  developing an if signal by combining a rf signal and a  local oscillator (lo) signal.  the rf and lo signals are  applied to a strip transmission line ring (100) which pro­ vides balanced components of the lo signal to a pair of  active devices (120, 121) arranged in a singly-balanced  active mixer.  the active devices (120, 121) are coupled  directly, or via a balanced pi-network (129-131) to the  primary winding of an output transformer (132) for applica­ tion to the following if stage of the radio receiver.  the  singly balanced active mixer circuit may be advantageously  utilized in a radio having a receiver with broadband front-­ end rf selectivity.
