<ABSTRACT>
a fuel injection system having a fuel injector (22) dis­ posed in a first bore (38) formed in the wall of an air intake  passage of an engine.  a porous tubular eiement(32) having a mixture passage  (44) open at both its ends is received in a second bore (36) in  close contact with the fuel injector (22).  an air passage (34) is  provided for introducing air into the mixture passage (44)  through fine passages formed in the wail of the porous tubu­ lar element (32).  this air will atomize the fuel (f) adhering to  the inner surface of the mixture passage (44) into fine parti­ cles (b) in order to form a homogeneous mixture.
