<ABSTRACT>
a novel medicament which is effective as an antihyper­ tensive agent or as an antitumour agent is proposed which  contains, as its effective ingredient, an organosilicon com­ pound represented by the general formula   r₃si(ch₂)ncooh,   where r denotes an alkyl group having from 1 to 6 carbon  atoms and n is an integer from 1 to 5 inclusive, or a thera­ peutically acceptable salt thereof such as a salt with an  alkali metal, e. g.  sodium or potassium.  the effectiveness  of the compound, for example, 3-trimethylsilyl butyric acid  as well as the sodium and potassum salts thereof is estab­ lished by the animal test untertaken with hypertensive rats  and icr(cd-1) mice to which latter two kinds of cancer  cells have been transplanted.
