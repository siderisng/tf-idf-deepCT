<ABSTRACT>
therapeutic compositions comprising certain  selenium-containing compounds which exhibit therapeutic  benefits in mammal hosts including humans are disclosed.   the selenium compounds in accordance with this invention  are preferably water soluble organic or inorganic compounds  containing selenium such as alkali metal selenites  and selenates.  among the therapeutic benefits exhibited by  these compositions are the reduction in severity of, and  improvements in recovery from, physically induced damage  to body tissue which includes damage caused by surgical  incisions, lacerations and burns.
