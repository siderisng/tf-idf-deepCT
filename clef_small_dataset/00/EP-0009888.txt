<ABSTRACT>
apparatus for forming a tube, preferably of flexible  thermally insulating material, comprises a cutting head (14)  with a pair of concentric cutting edges (16,18) which coop­ erates with a presser head (28) to cut rings (50) of material  from a sheet (48) presented to the cutting head (14).  the  rings (50) are stacked up on a guide bar (32) and sprayed  with a coating composition to consolidate them into a tube  (54) which may be collected on a take-up drum in a con­ tinuous manner.
