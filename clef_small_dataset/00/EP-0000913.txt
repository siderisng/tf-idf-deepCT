<ABSTRACT>
Reflector for solar collectors consisting of a mirrored glass plate (23).To minimize the beam energy absorbed by the glass plate, a thin glass plate is provided, whose plate thickness is below the glass thickness required for a self-supporting glass plate.This glass plate is glued with its mirrored side to a support body (22), in particular on a honeycomb-type support body.
