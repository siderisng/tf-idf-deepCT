<ABSTRACT>
a new crystalline zeolite, designated zsm-25, possessing  a silica/alumna ratio usually in the range 6 to 10, and a unique  x-ray diffraction pattern.  the zeolite can be synthesised from a  reaction mixture containing sodium and tetraethylammonium  cations and is useful in catalysis of various organic conversion  reactions.
