<ABSTRACT>
Sour galvanic nickel bath usual composition containing special gloss and depressants.These means obey of formula I in it mean a heteroaromatic nitrogen ba SE such as pyridine u. a., R, R¹ and R² same or different radicals and hydrogen or alkyl, R³ a given if substituted arylene radical, such as phenylene or thienylene, x SO₃, Y the countercut, n 1 to 3 and m 1 to 2.5.The compounds are obtained by reacting the stick of fabric bases with a corresponding arylalkyl halide, wherein heterocyclic ammonium salts arise.The latter are solded and the resulting sulfobetaine, if worked with excess sulfating agent, is neutralized with a countercatient base.With these means, it is possible to produce with in very wide highly located current-tight nickel coatings with objection free shine.
