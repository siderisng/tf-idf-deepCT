<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a process for the preparation of polyacrylonitrile or acrylonitrile copolymers, wherein the polymerization at temperatures of -40 to + 60 ° C, is under pressure, in an aliphatic chlorofluoro or fluorosubstituted hydrocarbon with up to 8 C atoms and a boiling pointBetween -60 and + 50 ° C or mixtures with boiling ranges between -60 and + 50 ° C and using a starter system from a hydroperoxide, sulfur dioxide and an acid is performed.The system may also contain aliphatic hydrocarbons in such quantities that no inflammatory overall mixture is formed.
