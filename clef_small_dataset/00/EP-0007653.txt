<ABSTRACT>
In a device for dosing and / or distributing liquid media, a distributor tube having a plurality of downwards outgoing feeding members is provided.To ensure a uniform distribution of the liquid medium to be metered over z.b.Route towers or flowing waters can be achieved, the feeding members are formed as dosing tubes fitted in the distributor pipe, which leads out of the distributor ear UN in the upper region of the manifold.Above the fitting in the manifold can be present in the dosing tube at least one side hole opening.Via the lower ends of the dosing tubes, hoses may preferably be pulled different length whose outlet openings are evenly distributed over the flow cross-section of a pouring medium.
