<ABSTRACT>
The subject of the invention relates to a method for preparing [2,3-C] (I) and [3,2-C] thienopyridines (II) carrying a hydrogen atom or the carboxy group in positions 5 and 6 respectively byreaction of a hydroxyl derivative in position 4 or 7 respectively with nitrous acid and removal of the nitroso group, either by reaction with an acid, thus obtaining the derivatives having a hydrogen in 5 or 6, either by reaction with a hydroxide of a hydroxide ofAlkali metal and neutralization, thus obtaining the corresponding carboxylated derivatives.
