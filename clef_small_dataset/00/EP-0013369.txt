<ABSTRACT>
Methods for dyeing scar leather with anionic dyes among the customized for pull-out methods, in the presence of two particular surfactants.The combined use of the two surfactants causes an improvement of the Egality and by staining in cationic (substantially mineral) tanned, anionic (synthe table or vegetable) tanned or submerged leather a deepening of the color.The two surfactants are a) a 3 to 120-fold oxethylated aliphatic alcohol with 9 to 24 carbon atoms and b) a 6-80x oxethylated aliphatic amine having 8 to 20 carbon atoms, which in weight ratio A: Blike 1: 4 to 4: 1 and a total predisfactory amount of 03 to 3%, based on fold weight, are used.
