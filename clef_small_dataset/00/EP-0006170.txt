<ABSTRACT>
Cyclohexan-1.3-dione are prepared by reacting δ-ketocarboxylic acid esters in the gas phase at temperatures of 250 ° C to 500 ° C to a catalyst.The catalyst contains at least one element of group IIIb or IVB of the periodic system in the form of a compound.Particularly preferred catalysts are thorium compounds on carbon vehicles.
