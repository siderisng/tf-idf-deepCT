<ABSTRACT>
the invention relates to a process for the manufacture of a venting or aerating conduit, applications of the process to a venting or aerating conduit of a domestic unit and a tube of self-supporting integral wall material for such conduits (figure 1).  the process is carried out by a connection of tubular conduit pieces (20, 16, 36, 34), changing the clear cross-section of the tubular conduit (2) in the region of the connection of a tube (20) of self-supporting integral wall material having a connection part (18), by deformation of the prefabricated end of one of the tubular conduit pieces, the ends of which are to be connected, into a different connection cross- section.  in this case, the end of the tube (20) to be connected to the connection part (18) is deformed into the connection cross-section.  the tube (20) has, in particular, a clear cross-section, the internal wall circumference of which is equal to that of a circular tube having a tube diameter of not less than approximately 80 and not more than approximately 150 mm.  at least one end (24) of the tube (20) has thin walls, of a flexible material, such that the wall of the tube end can be pressed and/or drawn out of the prefabricated clear cross-section into a different connection cross-section.
