The device for opening and closing an X-ray gene film cassette has a bottom part (1) and a cover part (2). A slider (3) guided on the bottom part (1) is held in the closure position by means of a spring and has a hook (16) which engages up a protrusion (18) in the lid (2) protrusion (18) in the closure position. For easy and safe closing of the cassette, an elastic, the slider holding the slide (25) is provided on the slide (3), which is actuated by the lid (2). When closing the cartridge, an element (26) formed on the lid (2) presses the pawl (25) so that the slider (3) can spray into its closure position. Thus, the operator for closing the cassette cover must be overcome essentially only the lid bias.
