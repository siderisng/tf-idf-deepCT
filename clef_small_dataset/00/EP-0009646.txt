<ABSTRACT>
A granules of a femin alloy suitable for storing hydrogen and / or interpreted rum in compressed gas cylinders is characterized by a content of oxygen to manganese in atomic ratio 1: 3 to 1: 3.5 and a titanium content that is dimensioned so that theBy metal oxides non-bonded titanium for unbanded iron in atoms such as Ti: Fe ≧ 1: 1 behaves and the feimn alloy has a FETI content of 90 to 95% by weight.Preferably, the granules (2) is used in a ultra-high vacuum-tight, in particular ultra-high-vacuum-tight compressed gas cylinder (1) in which both the activation of the granules (2) and an optionally required reactivation is guidable.The ultra-high vacuum-density execution of the compressed gas cylinder (2) allows the removal of high pure hydrogen and / or deuterium.The degree of purity of the exhausted gas is increased by a factor of 2 × 10³ over the introduced gas.
