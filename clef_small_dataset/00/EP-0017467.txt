<ABSTRACT>
a method of manufacturing an acoustic surface wave  device comprising: forming two pairs of transducers (2-1,2-2  and 3-1, 3-2), a multistrip coupler (8) and lead patterns (10)  for electrically shorting at least one pair of the transducers on  a piezoelectric material (1); mounting the piezoelectric  material (1) on a package base (11) having pins (12, 13);  bonding the electrodes (9) of the opened transducers to the  pins (12, 13) by wires (w1, w2, w3, w4); and testing the  device.  in this manufacturing method, testing is carried out  without using probing technology.
