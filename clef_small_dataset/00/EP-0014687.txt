<ABSTRACT>
This method consists in detecting piezomelectric potential characteristic of a mechanical solicitation exerted on a rigid organ elongated in an organized material than.For this purpose, potentials are measured at two points away from this member with two operating amplifiers (CL₁) and (CL₂) in order to obtain a characteristic signal of the constraint rather than its derivative and treating theseSignals using analog operators (OA₁, OA₂, OA₃) for identifying the flexural stresses (Δ F) and twisting (T) which is then compared to reference values (RT, RF) inView for example to trigger a signal.This method could in particular be used to comply with the opening of a safety ski fixation.
