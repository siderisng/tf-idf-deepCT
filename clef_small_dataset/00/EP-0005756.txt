<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to the novel 5-arylamino-benzodioxoles of general formula I in which R is hydrogen, optionally by halogen-substituted alkyl or halogen.They have excellent insecticides, scriny and fungicidal properties.They are obtained when reacting 2-chloro-3,5-dinitrobenzo trifluoride of formula II with a 5-amino benzodioxol of the formula III.
