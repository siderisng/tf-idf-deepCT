<ABSTRACT>
Polymerisat beads with a middle perl diameter of 5 to 500 μ, consisting of an inorganic finely divided filler and polymerized (meth) acrylic acid star, and a method for their preparation, in which a mixture of monomer and optionally in the monomer soluble polymer, which a viscosity of0.1 to 10 Pas, measured at the dispersing temperature, has, and filler in aqueous medium is suspended and polymerized in the aqueous medium in the presence of disperser.
