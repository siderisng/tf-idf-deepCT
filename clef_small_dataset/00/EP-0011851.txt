<ABSTRACT>
The apparatus for exposing and developing photosensitive material comprises an exposure device with a light source, a color filter, original platform, and adjustable imaging optics, a positive platform for the photosensitive material and a development station. The photosensitive material are two-blade units (15a, 15b) to be treated according to the diffusion method. A transport roller pair (34, 35) mounted on pivot levers (34, 35) may be outstanding from a receiving position next to the positive platform (13), taking the photosensitive material (15) in an input position above a developer bath (25) below the positive platform (13) Development station (8) are pivoted. A second, arranged on the output side of the developer bath (25) transport roller pair (30, 31) conveys the material from the developer bath (25) in a diffusion chamber (19) arranged below the positive platform (13). Due to the compact design and lightweight service option without electrical leads, the device is particularly suitable as a home laboratory.
