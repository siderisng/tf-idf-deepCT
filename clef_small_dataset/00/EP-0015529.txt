<ABSTRACT>
In a device for automatic labeling of objects (4) moving past the device (4), a tape (12) supported at a distance from each other is gradually from a trigger roller (14) via a dispensing edge (20) to a winding roll (23) drawn.The preferred mechanism (8) relieves a recoverable band clamping device (50).The tape feed is normally controlled by a control marks on the label tape (12) channel (18) and, in the case of a lack of individual control marks, controlled via a limit switch (54) actuated by the BE BE BE-BEABLE tape clamping device.A translation gear (63) arranged between the trigger roller (14) and WIK Kel roller (23) and a slip clutch ensure a particularly simple and effective drive of the take-up roller (23) and for generating the necessary band voltage.
