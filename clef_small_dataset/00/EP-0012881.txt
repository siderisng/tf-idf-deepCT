<ABSTRACT>
Automatic margin establishment for a sheet to be printed by a printing machine printing machine. According to the invention, the carriage (14) carrying the print element (16), also carries, on either side of the printing element, an optical unit (54) detecting the edges of the sheet. (22) By variation of lumino, and an optical unit (44) cooperating with a tracking strip (42) in order to define the position of the carriage opposite the plate (24). The positions of the left and right edges of the sheet, as detected, are stored. After detecting the right edge of the sheet, the carriage continues its scanner to the right over a specific distance in order to eliminate any errors caused by dark areas on the sheet (22). The stored positions of the edges of the sheet are used to determine the width of the sheet. After this determination, the logic of the machine automatically establishes margins equal to a proportion chosen from the width of the sheet.
