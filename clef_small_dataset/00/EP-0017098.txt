<ABSTRACT>
The differential transmission particularly for the drive axes of vehicles, differential transmissions with in the function of the ratio of the rotational speeds of the driven axle shafts variable arrangement torque is equipped with a multi-disc clutch. The part of the differential transmission housing forming outer hub (1) of the multi-disc clutch is connected to the inner hub (2) via inner (3) and outer (4) friction discs, outside which screw compression springs (5) are arranged axially. In the interior of the inner hub (2), a polygonal socket (7) is placed on the axle shaft (6) and reh, wherein between the walls and the inner cylindrical surface of the hub (2) with an axial play rollers (8) are housed, which by one Cage (9) and its centering device (10) are held in a relevant position over the walls of the polygonal socket (7). The flange of the cage (9) is in contact with a pressure disk (11), which is connected with equally distributed to the circumference plunger (12), the other ends mounted in contact with a suction bar against the housing of the drive axis (14) Gloc Kenkurve (13) stand. With the plunger (12) a disc (15) is connected, between which and the housing clamping springs (16) are arranged.
