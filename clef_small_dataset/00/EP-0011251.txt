Circuit arrangement for driving write nozzles in ink write devices with piezoelectric drive selements.The transistors switching the high voltages for the piezoelectric transducers are arranged together with a these drive transistors are arranged together with a this driving shift register in the writing head on the terminal of the write nozzle rows;For expanding the writing chambers, the individual piezoelectric transducers are supplied by individually assigned and individual ELL controllable transistors with the required chip potential;Together, the chip potentials of the piezoelectric transducers acted upon therethrough are exposed to a single electronic switch, which polls through contraction of the piezoelectric wall ler the droplet ejection.
