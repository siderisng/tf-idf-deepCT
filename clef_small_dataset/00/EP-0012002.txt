<ABSTRACT>
the compositions comprise 95 to 50% by weight of a  glass frit (preferably of a barium borate glass which advan­ tageously contains niobium oxide) and 5 to 50% by weight of a  mixture comprising molybdenum disilicide, tantalum disili­ cide, magnesium silicide and aluminium.  the amount of  aluminium is such that the molar ratio of the silicides to  aluminum is 90:10 to 50:50.  the inclusion of aluminium  increases the range of resistivity obtainable.
