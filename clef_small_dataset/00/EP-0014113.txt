<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a flexible transmission line.This line is characterized in that it comprises a duct (31) of circulation of fluids and inner reinforcements (25, 26, 27) and external to this conduit, against the forces of indoor and external pressure (33, 34, 35);The inner reinforcement comprises at least one inner cable in the conduit and which gave gentle in this duct, along the entire length of the line, of the circulating places of fluids;The exterior reinforcements are coaxial with the conduit.Application to the transport of long distance liquid or gaseous fluid samples, especially pro samples from underground or submarine gills.
