<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for the electrolytic production of hydrogen in an alkaline medium.The method includes using a cathode whose active surface consists essentially of an oxidized compound of the type of spinels.The method is suitable for electrolysis of aqueous solutions of sodium chloride in permeable diaphragm cells.
