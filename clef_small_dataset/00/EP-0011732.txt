<ABSTRACT>
1.  soil-cultivating implement with a working tool (1) which is carried, so that it can rotate, by a supporting frame and which has, on its periphery, tooth-like projections (6) which are directed outwards and the centre axis of which is inclined relative to a radial straight line (r), transversely to the axis of revolution, in the direction of rotation of the working tool (1), characterised by a helix (5) which constitutes the working tool and is formed from a piece of extruded steel (4) and which carries the projections (6) on its outside, the turns of the helix being spaced from one another.
