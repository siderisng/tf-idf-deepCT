<ABSTRACT>
Methods for influencing an internal combustion engine of a motor vehicle drive during gear change of a STU fitting and apparatus for carrying out the projection, in which a reduction of the motorcyry torque during the gear change between two engine speed values, which are determined from the engine speed when initiating the gear change.The integration of the internal combustion engine takes place depending on the switching type and the inserted gear.Furthermore, the ratios are taken into account, which occur with a highly accelerating vehicle or with strong load transactions during gear change.The two engine speed values are determined by a speed calculator stage (60.61), and with the engine speed compared to switching means (64.65, 66), which are in effect association with an influencing computer stage (67, 68), which is connected to a motor component(69.70) is connected.
