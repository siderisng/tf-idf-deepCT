<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a device for monitoring the level of a developer solution in a reservoir (1) for the development of two-component ten diazotype material.For this purpose, electrodes (14 to 17) are arranged within the supply, the lower end faces (18, 19, 20, 21) at different heights, measured from the bottom of the storage vessel (1) and which are connected to a circuit arrangement..The end face at least one of the electrodes is at a height in which the electrode triggers the connected circuit arrangement by reaching the liquid level.The circuit arrangement triggers a signal and / or control operation depending on the electrical resistance between the ELEK trodes (14 to 17).
