<TITLE>
tool for loading an electrical connector with wires.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
a tool for loading an electrical connector with wires.  the tool is for loading wires into slotted plate terminals  of an electrical connector held in a jig (6, 92), a wire inserter  (32) being movable towards the jig (6, 92) to insert the wires  which are positioned between the teeth of a wire comb (72).  for the provision of a simple and compact tool, especially  for use in wiring large upright wiring panels, the inserter  (32) is swingable about a shaft (36) by means of a handle (33)  and is indexable along the shaft (36) to load each terminal of  the connector in turn.
