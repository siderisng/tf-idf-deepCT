<ABSTRACT>
a solid pulverisable fuel of consistent calorific value  is made by mixing a thick mobile, for example an oily or  tarry waste with a finely divided aluminium-silicate-based  substance such as fly ash, cement and water.  the calorific  value of the fuel may be enhanced by using a fly ash type  material with a significant calorific value.  one such material is colliery waste.
