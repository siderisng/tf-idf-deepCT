<ABSTRACT>
1.  method of depositing a material layer in particular patterns upon a surface of a disk-shaped workpiece by vaporizing the material in a plane adjacent to the workpiece surface by means of a laser beam, characterized in that the workpiece consists of transparent material and that the laser beam moved in accordance with the pattern provided is directed to the workpiece from the opposite workpiece side.
