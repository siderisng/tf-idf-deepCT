<ABSTRACT>
the invention relates to a mold for the production of a  reinforced plastic tubular body having an annular groove  such as a pipe coupling, said mold allowing accurate molding  and comprising a mold body (1) and detachably mounted on a  predetermined position thereon, an annular groove former (2)  consisting of a surface layer (21) of an elastic material and a  core (22) covered by the surface layer.  in a method according  to the invention, a reinforced plastic tubular body is produced  by forming a layer (5) of a molding material around such a  mold, curing the layer of molding material, separating the  mold body (1) from the cured layer (5) of molding material,  and then separating the annular groove former (2) from the  cured layer (5) of molding material by removing the core (22)  and the surface layer (21) in that sequence.
