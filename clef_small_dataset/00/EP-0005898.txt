<ABSTRACT>
in a method of operating a vapour generating system  including a once-through vapour generator (10), wet vapour  is generated in the upper portion of the load range and  superheated vapour is generated in the lower portion of the  load range.  generated vapour is passed through a moisture  separator (11) external to and remote from the vapour  generator (10).  superheated vapour is desuperheated by  liquid injection via a spray device (13) as it passes via a pipe  line (46) from the vapour generator (10) to the moisture  separator (11).
