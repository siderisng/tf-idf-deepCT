<TITLE>
method for preparing an oxidation catalyst containing a molybdate anion.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

</INVENTOR>
<ABSTRACT>
a method of preparing a catalyst comprises combining in  an aqueous solution, an acid or salt of molybdenum with a  water-soluble acid or non-metallic salt of silicon, phosphorus  or arsenic, which is capable of forming a polyhedra of molyb­ denum.  an aqueous chloride ion and a compound of phos­ photungstate, silicotungstate, vanadium arsenate, silicoarse­ nate, phosphovanadate, silicovanadate or corresponding  acids thereof or mixtures thereof are then added to the mix­ ture.  the resulting combination is dried and calcined to yield  the finished catalyst.  the oxidation catalyst has the following empirical for­ mula:   ywmoxayoz   where   y is phosphorus, arsenic, silicon or mixtures thereof,   a is tungsten, vanadium, arsenic, when y is not arsenic,  or mixtures thereof,   w ranges from 0. 5 to 1. 5,   x ranges from 10 to 15,   y ranges from 0. 1 to 2. 0, and   z ranges from 1 to 42, and is an integer necessary to  satisfy the valency requirements of the formula.  
</ABSTRACT>
