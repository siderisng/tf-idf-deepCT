<ABSTRACT>
the present invention relates to a process for producing  alkylene glycol monesters from olefin oxides and carboxylic  acid using a chromium carboxylate catalyst, chromium  octanoate being most preferred.  the esters produced using  this catalyst are substantially pure and is obained in high  yield.  the esters thus produced are ideal for use as ester  hardeners for foundry cores.
