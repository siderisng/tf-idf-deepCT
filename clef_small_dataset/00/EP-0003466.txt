<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method of heat treatment of a permanent magnet fE-co-Cr alloy, consisting of 10 to 40% CO, 10 to 40% Cr, from 0 to 10% of one or more elements of theAl, NB, TA, W, MO, V, TI, SI and CU group, the rest with iron, a method comprising homogenization between 1200 and 1400 째 C, an annealing and one or more revenues between 500 and 600 째 C.According to the invention, the annealing comprises two steps, the first between 630 and 670 째 C for 5 to 30 minutes, the second, without return to the ambient, at a temperature of 40 to 70 째 C at the preceding during theLess 10 minutes.This gives isotropic magnets or anisotropic magnets whose hysteresis curve has a better rectangularity.
