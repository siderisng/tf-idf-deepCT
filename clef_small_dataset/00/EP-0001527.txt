<ABSTRACT>
Assembly device, especially between two trunns of a windshield windshield.The disposition comprises two flanges (11, 12) delimiting trunconic litters, and a collar (20) comprising clamping jaws coming into these worn and suspended from a support (30) made in two parts (32, 33), one (32) fixed and the other (33) articulated on the first.The collar can be tight or loosened by two sets with screws and nut (40.50).
