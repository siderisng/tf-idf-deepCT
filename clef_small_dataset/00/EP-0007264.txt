<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a chain that can not curl that on one side relative to the line of axes of a link, while being compatible with small diameter gears.The chain according to the invention consists of a conventional chain (11) supporting at regular intervals of the blocks (13) having coplanar faces (17) and (18) tangents at the axes (19) of the chain (11).This chain can be used in the field of the Manu Tension and the transport of people.In particular, it can constitute a variable speed handling hand.
