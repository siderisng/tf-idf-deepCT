<ABSTRACT>
the apparatus comprises a hearth open to the atmosphere, and a gas circuit (4, 5) connecting the hood (3) to an exhaust duct (20) connected to an exhaust opening to the outside through a wall of domestic premises in which the apparatus is mounted.  a heat exchanger (8) is mounted in series in the gas circuit with filters (7, 9, 10, 29), at least one of which is an electrostatic filter (10).  the apparatus can be used as an apartment fireplace without necessitating a vertical outlet duct to the top of the building.  <image>
