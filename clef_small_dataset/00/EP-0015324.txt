<ABSTRACT>
a domestic appliance comprises a programming de­ vice for receiving on-time information and for updating,  by way of repetitious signals, the ontime yet to be com­ pleted.  furthermore, these repetitious signals continuously  activate an activation signal which, however, is indepen­ dently terminated if the repetitious signals do not appear.   when the activation signal is present, first and second  switching components are activated, so that a discharge  tube can emit ultraviolet radiation.  furthermore, there is  provided a detection element which detects whether the  first and second switching components are indeed deacti­ vated outside the on-time.  if one of the two switching com­ ponents remains active also outside the on-time, the  detection element supplies a signal to prevent the starting  of a next on-time.  
</ABSTRACT>
