<ABSTRACT>
Method and apparatus for punching blanks of a uniformly moved longitudinally moved web passing between a punching blade (15) and a abutment (17), the punching blade (15) lowered to the web (25) and during the stamping operation inDevice direction of the web with this synchronous via a distance which is smaller than the length of the blank is shifted, whereupon the punching knife (15) is lifted off from the web (26) and moved back to its original position.The abutment consisting of at least one roller (17) can be moved against the direction of movement of the web (25) and the punching blade (15) during the punching operation.The web (25) can be guided about the partial circumference of a roller (17) and decreased during the punch stroke at the planar punching knife (15).
