<ABSTRACT>
a head for a razor comprises first and second arcuate  surfaces (101, 103), said surfaces being inclined with respect  to each other, said first surface (101) being a concave surface  and said second surface (103) being a convex surface, and at  least one razor cutting edge in each of said first and second  arcuate surfaces (102, 104), comforming to the arcuate shape  thereof and being located in such a manner that when either  one of said surfaces is drawn across a conforming body surface,  bodily hair growth is removed by said cutting edge.
