</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
an ultrasonic imaging system utilising a multi-element  ultrasonic transducer array for transmission and reception of  ultrasonic signals wherein a different number of elements (l)  is used in reception compared to transmission so as to  reduce sidelobes in the resultant ultrasonic beam pattern.   the elements may also be focussed to a different focal length  in reception compared to transmission so as to obtain  increased depth of field.  
</ABSTRACT>
