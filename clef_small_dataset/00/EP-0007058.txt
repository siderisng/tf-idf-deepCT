<ABSTRACT>
In a method for determining glycerol with glycerine dehydrogenase, electron transmitters, NAD and tetrazolium salt, at least 1 μg zinc is added in the form of a zncl₂ each 50 U glycerine dehydrogenase.The reagent suitable for this method contains glycerol dehydrogenase, electron transfer carrier, NAD, tetrazolium salt, buffer and at least 1 μg zinc in the form of a zinc salt per 50 U glycerine dehydrogenase.
