method and device for determining by irradiation the concentration of fat in meats.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
1.  radiation-measuring process for the non-destructive determination of the fat content of mixtures or masses of meat, characterized in that the meat mixture is exposed to the neutron radiation of an n-source and the prompt gamma-radiation emitted as a result of (n, gamma) reaction and specific for c alone or for the elements c, h and n is detected selectively, and the intensity or intensities determined for this are compared with appropriate calibration curves or are converted into a fat content. 
</ABSTRACT>
