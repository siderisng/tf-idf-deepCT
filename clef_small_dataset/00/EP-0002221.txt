<ABSTRACT>
Method for producing a mixed catalyst and its use for the polymerization of 1-olefins.The height activity of the known polymerization catalysts based on solid reaction products between a halogen-containing titanium compound and a metal alcoholate can be increased considerably by post-treating these catalysts with acid halides of the 3rd and 4th main and 4th subgroup of the periodic system.
