<TITLE>
a process and apparatus for the preparation of polymers containing hydroperoxide groups
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>
bayer agde <sep>bayer aktiengesellschaft <sep>bayer ag51368 leverkusende<sep>
</APPLICANT>
<INVENTOR>
korber helmut drde<sep>korber, helmut, dr.<sep>korber, helmut, dr.in der hildscheid 18d-5068 odenthalde<sep>
</INVENTOR>
<ABSTRACT>
a continuous process for the preparation of polymers containing hydroperoxide groups from oxidisable polymers in which the melt of the polymer is contacted for a maximum of 20 minutes under a pressure of 1-150 bar and at a temperature of 80-300 deg c with oxygen or oxygen- containing gases with vigorous mixing, and the polymer containing hydroperoxide groups is isolated after the oxidation by extrusion, and an apparatus for carrying out the process.  <image>
