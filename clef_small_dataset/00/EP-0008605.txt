<ABSTRACT>
In the intermediates of emulsifier or protective colloidal plastic dispersions, air inclusions are formed in hydraulically setting masses that lead to reducing the abrasion, pressure, bending and adhesion of the finished product.The addition of defoamers could not lower the content of air pores in the finished product not essentially, especially when the shear strain was great.Through the additive of plastic dispersion and defoamer according to the invention, it is possible to produce hydraulically lowering, plastic-containing masses which have very low air pore contents and thereby result in better strengths of the removed plastic-containing, hydraulically setting masses.
