<ABSTRACT>
Device for angularly locating a light source and identify a direction defined by a plurality of sources.It comprises a housing (1) with an optical cache (2) provided with circular transparent areas (4) so that the omnidirectional radiation transmitted by an appendix source (S1) is internal to a cone, and in a parallel planeThe cache, detective bars (5, 6, 7, 8) for determining the projected center (011) corresponding to the angular direction (Δ, θ) of the source.The invention applies in particular to headphone viewing systems.
