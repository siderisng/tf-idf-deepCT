<ABSTRACT>
The method of the present invention is mainly characterized in that it provides for the simultaneous deposition of metal and silicon on the substrate by evaporation and the heating of the substrate to form the silicide.The metal can be selected from the group comprising tungsten, molybdenum, tantalum and rhodium.In a field effect transistor (FET), the method can contribute to the formation of a door composite electrode for example a silicide layer 4 and a polycrystalline silicon layer;The silicide layer can easily be oxidized without affecting its conductivity.The present process finds in particular application in the manufacture of memory networks to a single FET.
