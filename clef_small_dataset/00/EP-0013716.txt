<ABSTRACT>
a check valve (7) for a hydraulic circuit of a positive- displacement pump (2) has a valve body (21), prestressed by a spring (19), inside a valve chamber (10) connected to a return line (12).  a valve sphere is provided as the valve body (21) whose diameter is 0. 5 to 15% greater than the diameter of the valve opening (9).  the stiffness of the spring (19) is as low as possible.  <image>
