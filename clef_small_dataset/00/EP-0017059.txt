<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to the reaction of monohydroxy fatty acids of the formula CH₃- (CH₂) M-CHOH (CH₂) N-COOH, where M represents an integer from 4 to 9 and N an integer from 6 to 11, with 12 to 20Mol ethylene oxide and the products thus obtained.The invention further relates to the use of these products as a solubilizer.
