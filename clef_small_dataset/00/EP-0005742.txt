<ABSTRACT>
In the case of a swab for medical, hygienic, cosmetic and similar purposes, the mull tissue blank is taken to a ball in which the edges of the blanks are inside.To achieve a machine-producible, homogeneous constructed and at each point equal to well sucking swab (1) without surfaces or protruding thread ends, two equal length mull tissue blanks (2, 3) are stopped, wherein the inner blank (3) a width of about two and the outer externalBlank (2) has a width of at least four-fold swab diameters.The blanks (2, 3) are struck into a bag in which the inwardly stacked blank beam are converted by a rubber ring (5).In this case, the inner blank (3) is decomposed only with its longitudinal ends through the rubber ring (5).
