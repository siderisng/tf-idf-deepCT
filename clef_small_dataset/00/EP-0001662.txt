<ABSTRACT>
1.  fastening member for fastening the hinge support of a swivelling arm or pivoting arm to a pivotable windowpane of a caravan, it being possible to screw into said fastening member the ends of at least two fastening screws to be covered by the member, characterised in that the fastening member (9) comprises a decorative cover (10) made of plastics, in which nuts (11) are inserted loosely but prevented from rotation in polygonal recesses matching the outer contour of the nuts and are retained in these recesses by a cover plate (12) secured to the decorative cover (10) and provided with holes for the passage of the screws.
