<TITLE>
aiding device for operating a zip-fastener.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

<ABSTRACT>
the aiding device (20) securely receives the pull tab (18) of a zip-fastener and, when operating the zip-fastener, holds the pull tab (18) in a position such that the pull tab (18) extends perpendicularly to the plane of extension of the zip-fastener, so that the best force can thereby be applied to the zip-fastener.  <image>
