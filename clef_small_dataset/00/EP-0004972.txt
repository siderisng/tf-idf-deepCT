<ABSTRACT>
In a process for the route measurement in electrical communication systems, the transmission side sequences are generated together as measurement signals, different individual frequencies are generated and the transmission path is supplied and the transmission side successively carries out successively measurements in the various individual frequencies.For the simplification and acceleration of this method, the transmission of the individual frequencies (F1 to FN) after a predetermined time program and the receiving side is determined for one or more of the individual frequencies (eg F1) by filtering the time of occurrence of this selected single frequency (s) (EN)Start frequency).From this point on, a received time program is started, which has the same schedule as the transmit-side timing program.The assignment of the respective measurements to the individual frequencies (F1 to Fn) is made solely after this received time program alone.
</ABSTRACT>
