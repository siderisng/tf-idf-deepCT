<ABSTRACT>
For hard contact lenses and even with soft contact lenses made of hydrophilic material, deposits of impurities on the surfaces of the contact lens are deposited during wearing of the contact lenses.To prevent this deposition of impurities.If the surface of the contact lens is exposed to a gas discharge after the contact lens has received its shape and has been polished.The gas discharge is preferably carried out in an oxygen atmosphere in an electromagnetic alternating field.
