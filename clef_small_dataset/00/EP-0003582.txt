<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for producing a stable, aqueous, electrolyte-containing cellulose ether suspension.In this case, a heat-soluble cellulose ether in solid, small parts form to an aqueous electrolyte solution is added under uniform distribution of the cellulose ether.The cellulosic ethers are nonionic type, especially methyl celluloses, alkyl-hydroxyalkyl celluloses and hydroxyalkyl celluloses.The stable suspension also contains toner, it can additionally be added to the suspension stabilizing and their viscosity-reducing organic agent.A preferred use of the suspension prepared according to the invention is the preparation of cellulosic acids containing mixtures such as those based on water-containing building materials or plastic dispersion paints, clothing or glue colors.
