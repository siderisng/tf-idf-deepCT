<ABSTRACT>
In order to be able to determine the permissible smallest insulation level between adjacent CCD channels in a planar integrated circuit in integrated storage with charge coupling memory elements and to measure the degree of charge transfer as well as the charge leak characteristic under conventional test conditions, a test integrated on the memory chip becomesControls an additional serial sliding register (18), which is arranged to the serial input slider gister (10) at different distance, wherein the isolation region is very large at the input positions of the two tabs mentioned and then progressively smaller.
