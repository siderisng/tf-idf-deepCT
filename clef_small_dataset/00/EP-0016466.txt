<ABSTRACT>
an apparatus (10) for controlling the height of one or  more cutting torches (12) above a workpiece (14).  the  apparatus (10) senses the actual height of the torch (12) above  the workpiece (14) and compares the actual height with a  desired reference height.  the apparatus (10) moves the torch  (12) up or down until the actual height and reference height  are substantially equal.  the apparatus (10) is operable in a  manual mode, a semi-automatic mode or a fully automatic  mode allowing an operator to select a desired degree of conÂ­ trol during a cutting operation.
