<ABSTRACT>
Monolithic integrated semiconductor device with at least one i²L structure containing an injection zone (P1) and an inverting transistor (T1). In this case, the injection zone (P1) and laterally, the base zone (P2) of the same first line type is arranged in a semiconductor layer (N1) of the second conductor type forming the emitter zone of the transistor. The transistor (T1) is completed by a collector zone (N2) of the second conductivity type lying in the base zone (P1). The I²l structure is at least partially surrounded by a separating zone (N⁺) introduced at a predetermined distance into the semiconductor layer. Starting from fixed minimum dimensions of the structure, a maximum injection of the injection zone (P1) in the base zone (P2) and at conductive transistor (T1) also a maximum return injection from the base zone (P2) into the injection zone (P1) by ensuring the maximum possible Length of opposing active edges of the injection zone (P1) and the base zone (P2) achieved. At the same time, it is ensured that the parasitic capacities occurring remain relatively low. These results are achieved by extending the injection zone (P1) and the base zone (P2) in the region of its two opposite edges to or in the separation zone (N⁺), while in the area of ​​its remaining edges the predetermined distance thereof exhibit.
