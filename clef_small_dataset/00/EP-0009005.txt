<ABSTRACT>
A hand-movable transportator with a loading bridge (1) for conveying loads, the staircase is gig.There are two front rollers (3) and two rear rollers (7) attached to downstream arms (7), wherein the charging surface occupies a rearwardly inclined slave when the transport device on a horizontal surface with all rollers (3.7)rests.The distance between the front and rear rollers (3.7) and the rolling diameter are chosen so that when climbing a staircase the front rollers (3) on a top and the left rolls (7) at the same time at a deeper stair stage.In the non-use state, the device can be folded together on low space.
