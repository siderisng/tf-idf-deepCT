<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a method for manufacturing polycrystalline silicon in thin layer.According to a particular implementation of the invention, the method consists in reducing a silicon compound to the gaseous state by the zinc in the liquid state in the presence of at least one metal in the liquid state selected from the group.Comprising: tin, lead, gold, silver, antimony, bismuth, the temperature of the reduction between 700 and 800 Â° C and the total pressure of 1 atmosphere.The silicon obtained by this process is applicable to photovoltaic conversion.
