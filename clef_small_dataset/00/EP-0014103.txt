<ABSTRACT>
a process for the production of carbamates is provided in  which an organic primary or secondary amino is contacted, in  the substantial absence of reactive oxygen, with a source of  carbon monoxide, an organic compound containing at least  one hydroxyl group and a source of sulfur, selenium or telleri­ um, in the presence of an effective amount of a catalyst select­ ed from nonhalide compounds and complexes of metals of  groups ivb, vb, vib, viib, viii, ib and iiia of the periodic table,  and preferably also in the presence of at least one promoter  selected from compounds of metals of group ia and iia of the  periodic table.
