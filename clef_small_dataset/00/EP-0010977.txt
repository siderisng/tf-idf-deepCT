<ABSTRACT>
a method and apparatus for applying paste to  battery grids wherein the paste is circulated in a primary  circuit (15, 28) incorporating an open reservoir (16)  wherein the paste is relieved of all pumping pressure and  a portion of the paste is diverted from the primary circuit  into a secondary circuit (25-27, 33-35) for delivery to a  nozzle (30-32) which may be selectively opened and closed  to deliver paste to the grid, the secondary circuit also  incorporates the open reservoir (16).
