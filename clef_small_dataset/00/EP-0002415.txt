<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for counting errors in a digital radio link to produce a switching request on a backup channel when the quality of the link becomes insufficient.In the process according to the invention we count each time the errors during a period T which is always at least equal to T₁ and at most equal to a value T₂, and which ends when a packet of a fixed number.N of errors has been detected, a pulse is recorded at each detection of a packet of N errors within a period T, and a switching request is emitted when N₁ Pulses have been recorded in a consecutive manner.
