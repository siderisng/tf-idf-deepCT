<ABSTRACT>
A new process for producing 2.3.5 trichloropyridine is described.The method is that trichloroacetaldehyde is attached in the presence of a catalyst to acrylinitrile and then the intermediately formed 2,4,4-trichloro -4-formyl butyronitrile under water discharge to 2,3,5-trichloropyridine cyclized.The 2,4,4-trichloro -4-formyl butyronitrile-occurring in the process according to the invention as an intermediate product is a new compound.It can be prepared by attaching trichloroacetaldehyde to acrylinitrile in the presence of a catalyst.The 2,3,5-trichloropyridine can be used as an intermediate for the production of herbicidal active substances.
