<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an electro-optical modulator based on a twisted liquid crystal cell in which two magnets are arranged parallel or perpendicular to the electrode surfaces, and the liquid crystal material has a positive dielectric and a negative diamagnetic anisotropy.With such an arrangement, the initial orientation of the liquid crystal molecules with respect to or electrode surfaces, the threshold voltage and / or the switch-off time of a display element can be influenced by electrical and / or magnetic fields.
