supporting device for the painting of vehicle body elements.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
1.  a holding device for the painting of parts of a vehicle body, comprising a circular standpipe (2) standing up vertically from a base (3), and comprising a supporting bar structure (5) lying in a horizontal plane and being adjustable in its vertical position at said standpipe, to the free arms (7) of said supporting bar structure supporting elements (8, 9) are attached for the adjsutable holding of the parts to be painted (10 to 12), characterized in that said supporting bar structure (5) has the shape of a horizontal "h" at whose cross bar (6) a cylindrical sleeve (4) is fastened centrically which surrounds said standpipe (2) in a manner vertically adjustable, and in that said supporting elements are formed as suspension holding means (8) and/or plane support holding means (9) which are adjustable in horizontal direction on said free arms (7).
