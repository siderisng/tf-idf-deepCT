<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a device for accurate and wrinkle-free envelope of sensitive cuboidal objects (10) - z. b. unpacked groups of cigarettes - in a blank (14) of a web foldable packaging material (16), wherein the blank (14) weigh at two lateral edge regions by endless conveyor (suction discs 19, 20) in a plane held transversely to the transport direction of the article and with removal of this U-shaped about the object is refinable. The cigarettes group (10) - moved by a chain conveyor - is started at the beginning of a sliding baren support device (mouthpiece 29), which takes over the blank (14) from the endless conveyor. Suction bores (22, 23a, 23b) on the peripheral surface of the suction discs (19, 20) detect the blank (14) only in certain critical areas. The wrapped group is pushed out by means of the chain conveyor into a packing track (25, 26). Further features of the invention are the structural design of the support device (29), the production of the blanks (separating device 17) and de ren delivery to the suction discs (19, 20).
