<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a thermosetting, aqueous paint coating means based on a binder mixture made by acid water-diluted binder mixture containing in combination a) 50 to 95 wt.-% tertiary amino groups include tenders and OH-group-containing polymer and / or polycondensate (component a) with the structural group and an amine number of 30 to 170, especially 60 to 120, and an OH number of 30 to 300 b) 5 to 40 wt.-% of a full-capped polyiso cyanate (component b) C) 0 to 30 wt.-% of a polymer or poly condensate with primary and / or secondary OH and / or amino groups (component C).The invention further relates to the use of the SES lacquer coating agent for electrical deposition on electrically conductive substrates and a method for the cathodic coating of an electrically conductive substrate from this paint coating agent.
