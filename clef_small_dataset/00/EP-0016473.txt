<TITLE>
a direct current motor having e-shaped interpoles and main poles with unsymmetrical pole pieces.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

</INVENTOR>
<ABSTRACT>
a direct current motor used, for example, in a machine  tool, comprising an armature (1), n main magnetic poles (3, 4)  disposed around the armature, each of the main magnetic  poles consisting of a pole core (33, 43) on which a field wind­ ing (5, 6) is provided and a pole piece (34, 44) extending along  the outer circumference of the armature, and n/2 e-shaped  interpoles (8) disposed at the intermediate position along the  outer circumference of the armature between the adjacent  main magnetic poles, and at the central axis (0) of the arma­ ture, the angle (α) spanning from the direct axis (b), which is  electrically perpendicular to the interpole axis (a), to an end  portion (31) of a pole piece (34, 44) of a main magnetic pole (3,  4) is unequal to that (β) from the direct axis (b) to the other end  portion (32) of the pole piece (34, 44) of the main magnetic  pole.  since the number of the e-shaped interpoles is decreased  by half, the direct current motor according to the present  invention has simple structure and high torque, and the heat  generated by the interpoles is relatively decreased.  
</ABSTRACT>
