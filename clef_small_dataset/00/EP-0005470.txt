<ABSTRACT>
the foams are prepared from organic polyisocyanates, polyols, catalysts, blowing agents and additives and contain, as fillers, alkali metal salts of inorganic acids, preferably in amounts of from 0. 01 to 5% by weight, based on the total weight of the organic polyisocyanate and polyol, the alkali metal salts of inorganic acids, dissolved in one or more of the abovementioned liquid starting components, preferably in ethylene glycol and/or water, in particular being introduced in the form of an aqueous solution of the foamable reaction mixture.
