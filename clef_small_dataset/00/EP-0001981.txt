<ABSTRACT>
Neue 9- (ω-heteroarylamino-alkylamino) -erythromycins of the formula R-NH (CH₂) N-NH-E (I) whose pharmacologically acceptable acid addition salts with inorganic or organic surfaces, methods for producing these compounds, the use of theConnections and these compounds-containing medicines are described.In the above general formula (I), E means the erythromycyl group, N the numbers 2 or 3 and R certain aromatic heterocycles, such as a likewise substituted 2-pyrimidinyl group, 2-quinazolinyl group or heterocyclic condensed pyrimidinyl group.The compounds of formula I and their salts are strongly effective against gram-positive and gram-negative bacteria;The medicines containing these compounds are broadband antibiotics.
</ABSTRACT>
