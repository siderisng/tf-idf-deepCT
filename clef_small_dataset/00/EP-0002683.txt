<ABSTRACT>
To improve their dispersibility in organic and aqueous media, inorganic and organic pigments and fillers are coated with β-hydroxyalkylates of formula I or their reaction products with alkylene oxides.In the formula I, the radicals R₁ and R₂ mean hydrogen or C₁-C₂₁ alkyl radicals, wherein at least one residue is an alkyl radical and the sum of the carbon atoms in R₁ and R₂ is 6 to 22.R₃ represents a C₁-C₈ alkyl radical substituted with 1 to 2 hydroxy groups and / or interrupted by 1 to 2 ether bridges.The β-hydroxyalkyl ethers are used in amounts of 0.05 to 5 weight percent, based on the pigment or filler.
