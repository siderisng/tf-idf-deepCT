<ABSTRACT>
the invention concerns a process in which a shaped  article is prepared by first coating the inside of a mould  with a coating powder, curing the coating, introducing a  thermosetting mass in the mould and curing to form an article  provided with a decorative layer.  the invention consists  in the use of a polyester-based coating powder, obtained by  absorbing a liquid catalyst in part of the unsaturated components  making up the powder, separately grinding that part  and a mixture of the remaining components to about the  same particle size and mixing the powders thus obtained.  the invention assures better curing of the coating.
