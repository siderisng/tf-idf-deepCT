<ABSTRACT>
in the case of a thread forming screw or a locking screw the screw shank (1) is subdivided into at least a straight section (5, 5a, 5b), having a constant external diameter (d0) and a gauge section (4), with a larger external diameter (d1).  the core diameter (d1) of the gauge section (4), however, is smaller than the core diameter (d0) of the straight section.  in addition, the tooth foot width (m1) of the gauge thread pitch (7) is larger than the tooth foot width (m0) of the thread pitches of the straight section.  the gauge section can be arranged at any desired point of the screw shank.  it gives the internal thread the final form.  <image>   <image>
