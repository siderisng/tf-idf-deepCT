<ABSTRACT>
In a tilting converter, the converter vessel (1) is supported by means of support forces of receiving claw pairs (5 to 11) on a support body (2) arranged around its sheath, wherein the claws (5 to 11) of a pair of claws are opposite each other on both sides of the carrier body (2).For the purpose of preventing the occurrence of bumps when tilting the converter, which shocks caused by the unavoidable game between the support body (2) and the claws (5 to 11), at least one cup (5 to 10) of each crumbs pair (5, 6; 7, 8; 9, 10) to support at least one piston cylinder unit (22) at least one piston cylinder unit (22) whose piston (30) is hydraulically adjustable in the longitudinal direction (15) of the converter vessel (1).
