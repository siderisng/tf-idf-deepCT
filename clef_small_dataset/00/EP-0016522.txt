<ABSTRACT>
a semiconductor device comprising: a semiconductor  chip; a package (1) for accommodating the chip; groups of  leads which are arranged around the perimeter of the package  (1), and; striped insulators such as plastic films (8) to each of  which one of the groups of leads (3) are adhered.  in addition, a  method for manufacturising the same comprising the steps  of: preparing a package (1) including a semiconductor chip  and having lead frames (2) each of which is composed of leads  (3) and a supporting end portion; adhering the lead frames (2)  to an insulationg sheet such as a plastic film (8), and; chipping  off the portions of the insulating sheet to which the supporting  end portions are adhered.
