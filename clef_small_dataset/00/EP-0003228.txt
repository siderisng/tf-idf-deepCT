<ABSTRACT>
1.  process for the multi-step polymerization of propene or butene-1, and for the multi-step copolymerization of propene with ethene and butene-1 using the low pressure process, with the aid of ziegler-natta mixed catalysts of ticl3  or ticl3 .  n alcl3  (n = 0. 2 to 0. 6) on the one hand, and chlorine containing aluminium-organic compounds on the other hand, whereby in the first polymerization step the polymerization is carried out in a dispersion agent of c3 - to c6 - hydrocarbons, in the final polymerization step the polymerization is carried out in the gaseous phase and between the first and the final polymerization steps the polymerization is carried out additionally if necessary in one or several further polymerization steps, characterized in that, in the first polymerization step propene or butene-1 is polymerized, and in the polymerization of butene-1, a catalytic yield of at least 1. 03 kg of polybutene-1/g ticl3  catalyst is polymerized and in the polymerization of propene, a catalytic yield of at least 6. 69 kg of polypropene/g ticl3 catalyst is polymerized, the polyolefine obtained is introduced into the next polymerization step with the complete liquid phase, and the dispersion agent is not removed until the final polymerization step.
