<ABSTRACT>
a parking apparatus in a three-wheeled vehicle having  one front wheel (9) (209) and two rear wheels (20) (220), and  a front body frame portion (2) (202) which is swingable  relative to a rear body frame portion (3) (203), comprises a  locking mechanism (31,41) (131,141) (246,254) provided  between the front and rear body portions for locking these  portions against relative swinging, a parking brake mechan­ ism (46) (145,148) (246,254) operable in cooperation with a  normal wheel brake, or independently thereof, and a com­ mon operating member (24) (224) for operating both the  locking and parking brake mechanisms simultaneously.  at  the time of parking, by simple operation of a single member,  the parking brake is applied and the front and rear body  frame portions are locked against relative rotation.  
</ABSTRACT>
