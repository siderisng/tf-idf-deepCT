<ABSTRACT>
1.  apparatus for covering plates, in particular chipboard, plywood, fibre board and the like with covering sheets of paper, felt, plastics and the like, the covering sheets being taken from piles and laid together with the plates, with a discontinuous conveyor track for the plates, a stack of covering sheets arranged above the conveyor track before the break, in the conveying direction, and a stock of covering sheets arranged below the conveyor track in the region of the break, characterized by a device (20, 21), which can be moved in a reciprocating manner in the conveying direction in the region of the break (10) and above the latter, for gripping and individual covering sheet (8 or 13) with suction nozzles (22) which are movable perpendicular to the conveying direction and are movable relative to the device (20) in a plane parallel to the plate, and by a holding device (39) in the region of the conveyor track (5) following the break (10).
