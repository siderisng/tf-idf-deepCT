<ABSTRACT>
As an adhesive interlocking intermediate layer in the preparation of polyamide coatings on metallic articles after the melt coating method, an aqueous polybutadiene dispersion is used, the liquid polybutadiene having a dynamic viscosity of 70 to 30,000 m Pa s and a configuration of 40 to 95% CIS-1.4 0 to 30% TRANS-1.4, 1 to 60% 1. 2-units and, based on the polybutadiene, contains 0.5 to 10 weight percent of an emulsifier.
