<ABSTRACT>
a tube extracting method and such a mechanism  comprising an elongated frame (4, 6), a passageway (16), a  sleeve (20) slidably supported by the frame (4, 6) for  longitudinal movement in a first direction toward a front  opening (12) and in a second direction away from the  opening, an expandable jaw (30) and a mandrel (41)  expanding the jaw when moved in the first direction.   hydraulic cylinders (40, 50) are provided for moving the  mandrel (41) and pulling the frame (4, 6).
