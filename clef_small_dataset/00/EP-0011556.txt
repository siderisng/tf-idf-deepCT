<ABSTRACT>
The filter comprises p floors, p is the number of bits on which the filter coefficients are expressed.Each of the floors comprises: -a charge transfer shift register, says Signal Registry (A₀.. AP-1), receiving the analog signal E (T) to be filtered;A load transfer shift register called register coefficients (N₀... NP-1), the same weight of the same weight of the filter coefficients (kc. KP-1).The filter further comprises an operator (1-0 1-P-1) performing the multiplication of the signal E (T) by each of the filter coefficients, then the summing of these different products to provide the output signal S (t) of the filter.
