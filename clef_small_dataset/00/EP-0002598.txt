<ABSTRACT>
alkane dicarboxylic acids of at least 4 carbon atoms  particularly the mixture of succinic, glutaric and adipic acids  obtained as byproduct in the manufacture of adipic acid are  purified by co-distillation with an alkylbenzene having an  atmospheric pressure boiling point of 300° to 350°c especially  dodecylbenzene.
