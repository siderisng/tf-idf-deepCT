<ABSTRACT>
chemically modified thickenable vinyl esters are pre­ pared by reacting an ethylenically unsaturated monocarbox­ ylic acid with a polyepoxide in the presence of a small amount  of a dicarboxylic acid.  these resins are diluted with a  copolymerizable monomer, such as styrene, blended with a  group ii metal oxide or hydroxide and other additives, and  cured with a free-radical catalyst.  the compositions can be  used for coating, laminating, moulding, or as adhesive.
