<ABSTRACT>
The resulting dispersions of the alkyd resins suitable as coating agents or paint binders which are air-drying or can be dried in the oven and crosslinking due to their double bonds contain as nonionic surfactant substances alkylphenols, which by addition of 5 to 40, preferably 5 to 25 moles of ethylene oxideto be obtained at 1 mol of alkylphenol, wherein the alkyl radical 12 to 18 has carbon atoms and 1 to 5 olefinic double bonds.The nonionic emulsifiers should be in an amount of 0.5 to 10, in particular 1 to 8 wt.%, relative to the resin content, are present.Preferred emulsifiers derive from the phenols prepared from the ingredients of the Cashew nuicer oil oil.The films prepared with the emulsions according to the invention are characterized by improved water resistance.
