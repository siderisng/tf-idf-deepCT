<ABSTRACT>
Thermo-electrical arrangement with large non-stationary temperature gradients, in which mechanical moving sheets, films or layers (1,2) are merged at one point to a non-stationary package.They form the elemental leg of a thermocouple in this non-stationary package.An electric current (6) flows perpendicular to the surfaces of sheets, films or layers through the non-stationary package in which there is a temperature difference between adjacent N / P or P / N transition lengths (3).Since relatively large amounts of heat in the time unit can be transported via the large temperature gradients in the non-stationary packet, large power densities can be achieved with simultaneously larger operating voltages.
</ABSTRACT>
