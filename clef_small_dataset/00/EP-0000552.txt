<TITLE>
process for producing a photographic print from an original in order to prepare a flat-bed printing form.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>
licht druck agch <sep>licht druck ag.<sep>licht druck ag. claridenstrasse 36 ch-8002 zürichch<sep>
</APPLICANT>
<INVENTOR>
mueller hans ernstch<sep>mueller, hans ernst<sep>müller, hans ernsthöhenstrasse 5ch-8157 dielsdorfch<sep>
</INVENTOR>
<ABSTRACT>
in a process for producing a photographic print from an original in order to prepare a screenless planographic printing plate, a positive or negative film is produced of the original.  a granulation applied to a transparency is copied over to a light-sensitive material to form a transparent copy.  this copy is then placed in the optical pathway between the positive or negative film and the photographic recording material.  the recording material is developed to form a screenless photographic print which is used as a printing plate. 
</ABSTRACT>
