<ABSTRACT>
Clamp for machine to close tubular packaging in soft material.In the rest position, one (21) of the jaws (20, 21) of this clamp receives which allows to perform on the package of other operations at the level of the clamp.The clamp will be particularly used for the closing and welding of flexible tubes (19) with plastic or metal and plastic laminate complex.
