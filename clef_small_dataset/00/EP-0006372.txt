<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a "laser" with reflector di stitribute.It has a laser diode, of which one of the semi-transparent mirrors is a cleaved face 5 of the monocrystal 1 in which is integrated an electroluminescent diode with epitaxial layers.The active zone of this diode is coupled to a waveguide 10 formed in an electrical medium 8 on one side of which is formed a network 11 forming reflector playing the role of second mirror.This reflector, when operating according to the Deu Xenth counter-reaction order emitted in order 1 diffracted perpendicular to the waveguide 10. The invention applies to fiber optic transmissions.
