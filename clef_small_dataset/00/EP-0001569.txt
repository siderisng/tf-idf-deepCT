<ABSTRACT>
In a method and apparatus for generating electrical energy by combustion of solid fuels, transmission of the combustion heat on working fluid and working relaxation of the working fluid, a part of the fuels is burned in a fluidized bed and the remainder in a vacuum firing.In this case, the heat generated in the fluidized bed firing is transmitted to the working fluid of a gas turbine corner, while the heat obtained in the vacuum firing is used to produce water vapor as a working agent of a steam turbine.The exhaust gases of the fluidized bed firing are supplied directly to the dust firing.
