<ABSTRACT>
substantially gel-free neutralised sulfonated elastomeric  polymers having both good physical and rheological properties  are made by a process wherein a sulfonated elastomer  having 10 to 50 meq.  of sulfonate groups per 100 gm  polymer dissolved in a non-reactive solvent is neutralised  with excess zinc acetate dissolved in methanol or methanol  and water, the volume % of methanol based on non-reactive  solvent being from 2 to 10, to form a zinc neutralised  sulfonated polymer.
