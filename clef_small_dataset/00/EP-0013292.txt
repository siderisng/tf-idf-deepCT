<ABSTRACT>
Printer guide screen also ensuring guidance of the print ribbon and detecting its exhaustion.The screen comprises a paper guide (10) marrying the curvature of the plate (12) to ensure the guidance of the paper.The paper guide (10) is attached to uprights (18) with each of a pair of electrically curbed rods (19-19 '), (20-20') which provide on the one hand the guidance of the ribbon DPrint (16) and on the other hand, in combination with circuits (24, 25), the detection of an electrically conductive primer (21) carried by the ribbon.The uprights (18) are pivotally mounted and adjustable with respect to a base (34) whose position is adjustable in the chassis plane (35) of the carriage carrying the character member (13).
