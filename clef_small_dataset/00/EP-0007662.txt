<ABSTRACT>
In the case of sprawling of zinc and lead of ironox ideal materials after rolling method, the material is charged to the rotary kiln with solid, carbonaceous reducing agent with a high content of volatiles, fueling compounds into the feed end.The process takes place below the softening point of the Bes Chickung.Metallic iron containing good is held at the end of the discharge, in which the combustion air is sucked.The exhaust gases with the volatile stock are deducted from the feed end.In order to increase the throughput using cheaper reducing agent, in the area of the heating zone, which starts with the occurrence of ignitable particles of the solid reduction by means and at a maximum of 50% of the furnace length ends, oxygen-containing gases by means of nozzle stones into the loaded over nozzle stones feed into regulated quantities.
