<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for producing β- (5-nitro-2-furyl) acrolein by reacting 5-nitrofurfurally in an aliphatic carboxylic acid with acetal dehyde in the presence of secondary amines.The compound serves as a starting material for producing substances active as fungicide and bactericidal, which are used in the pharmacy, textile industry and crop protection.
