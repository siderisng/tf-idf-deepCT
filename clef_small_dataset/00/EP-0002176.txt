<ABSTRACT>
Vinyl polymers are prepared by emulsion polymerization of olefinically unsaturated compounds in the presence of surfactant compounds.As emulsifiers are particularly suitable water-soluble salts of alkanephosphonic acids or alkanephosphonic acid mono-alkyl esters.These can also be used in combination with other surfactant compounds.The obtained plastic dispersion is used primarily as binders for pigments and fillers in dispersion colors.
