<ABSTRACT>
The electromagnetic overcurrent release of a low voltage circuit breaker (1) consists of a magnetic yoke (15) relative to its pole surfaces (21) an armature (17) is arranged movable.The distance of the armature (17) from the pole surfaces (21) is determined by a stop piece (22) which is supported on a stationary carrier (25).The stop piece (22) is disc-shaped and has two pins (23, 24) each, the connecting lines are perpendicular to each other.The four stop surfaces (26, 27, 28, 29) of the stoppiece (22) can be made on the one hand by rotation of the stop piece by 180 ° and on the other hand by twisting by 180 °.Both sides of the stopper (22) are provided with names for the trigger streams, of which the respective effective standing is readable.
