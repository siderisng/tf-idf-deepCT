<ABSTRACT>
The device serves to convert solar energy into electrical energy using a photovoltaic cell.A central lens (13) ensures the formation of a circular luminous spot on the receiving surface of the cell (11) and a toric mirror (14), arranged around the Len Tille, that of an annular luminous spot.Thus, the entire surface of the cell is illuminated, even in case of relinquer.The intensity distribution chosen in the light spots makes it possible to maintain a good efficiency of the cell, even at high concentration of solar energy.
