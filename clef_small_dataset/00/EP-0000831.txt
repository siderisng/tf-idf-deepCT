<ABSTRACT>
a cannula assembly for bidirectional blood flow using  co-extensive telescopic cannulae, and including a venipuncture  needle (40) which during puncturing projects forwardly  of the cannulae to facilitate venipuncture and is then displaceable  away from the venipuncture site to permit unobstructed  simultaneous fluid flow in opposite directions through the  cannulae.  the inner cannula (38) may be fixed or longitudinally  displaceable.
