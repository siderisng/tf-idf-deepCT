<ABSTRACT>
Process for the preparation of mononitrous derivatives of tetrahydro-1,2,3,4 anthraquinone from tetrahydro-1,4,4a, 9a anthraquinone characterized by the fact that one is killing a thermal pretreatment of tetrahydro-1, 4,4a, 9a anthraquinone in the presence of a hydrogenation catalyst under an inert atmosphere and in the absence of oxidants or reducers, separates the under-produced anthraquinone as well as the catalyst and submits the tetrahydro-1 mixture., 2,3,4 anthraceniol-9.10 and tetrahydro-1,2,3,4 anthraquinone obtained from a nitration reaction.
