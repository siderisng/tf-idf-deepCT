<ABSTRACT>
The counterfeiting-safe film for labeling objects, in particular high-quality brands articles, consists of at least one transparent, oriented polyester film whose mass contains organic acid, above 260 ° C thermostable dye.Preferably, a laminate is used from two films (1, 2), wherein the film z.b.A red and the other contains a yellow dye.
