<ABSTRACT>
The latent hardener for aminoplast resins consists of or contains the reaction product of diethanolamine, glycide and SO₂ in the molar ratio of 1: (1.0 to 1.2) :( 0.5 to 1.0) and is prepared by diethanolamine with glycideat 0 to 90 ° C in molar ratio 1: (1.0 to 1.2) and the resulting reaction product then reacted at 0 to 90 ° C with 0.5 to 1.0 mol of SO₂, based on diethanolamine, the hardenerAd serves to produce thermosetting aminoplast resins which consist of aminoplast precondensates, optionally modifiers, and the latent hardener.
