<TITLE>
highly rigid polyamide composition and a method for its manufacture.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

</INVENTOR>
<ABSTRACT>
a highly rigid poly-ε-caprolactam composition contain­ ing about 50 to 5000 ppm of barium and having a γ-type  crystal ratio of about 0. 3 to 0. 7 is disclosed.  the composi­ tion has improved rigidity without loss of other advanta­ geous mechanical properties of poly-ε-caprolactam.  by ad­ dition of an aluminum compound, e. g.  aluminium stearate,  the rigidity of the composition can be further improved.  a  bisamide compound, e. g.  methylene-bis-stearylamide, may  be added to provide excellent mold release without harm­ ing the mechanical properties of the composition.  the composition is made by treating poly-ε-caprolac­ tam with an aqueous solution containing ba ions and heat­ ing the treated poly-ε-caprolactam to the molten state, or  alternatively by melt-polymerizing ε-caprolactam in the pre­ sence of a suitable ba compound e. g.  barium-ε-amino  caprolate or ba(oh)₂.  if aluminium is to be included, the  barium-containing poly-ε-caprolactam can be melt-blended  with an al salt of an aliphatic carboxylic acid; alternatively  ε-caprolactam can be melt-polymerized in the presence of  suitable barium and aluminum compounds.  
</ABSTRACT>
