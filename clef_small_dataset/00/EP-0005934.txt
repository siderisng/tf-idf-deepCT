<ABSTRACT>
in applying an unthreaded metal closure to the threaded  neck of a bottle by the use of profile thread rollers to shape  the skirt of the closure shell into conformity with the bottle  thread whilst the shell is pressed down on the mouth of the  bottle, the part of the skirt in which the thread is formed is  engaged during the thread-rolling operation by at least one  plain roll angularly displaced with respect to the thread  rollers.
