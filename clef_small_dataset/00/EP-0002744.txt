<ABSTRACT>
The invention is the subject of modified poly organosiloxanguines which contain particles formed in situ, which have been produced by polymerization of aliphatic unsetted monomers in the presence of polyorgano-H-silo xanene means of free radicals under heating.These mixtures may be tabled together with Ver bonds which have at least two vinyl groups per molecule, and a catalyst which supports the addition of Si bonded hydrogen atoms to the vinyl groups, both at room temperature and in the heat to form elastomers.
