<ABSTRACT>
Device for separating a long scrolling product 8 from a liquid casing that accompanies it in the form of a coaxial jet, consisting of a subwoofer comprising a lateral opening 6 by which penetrates the jet surrounding the product, a lateral orifice, a lateral orifice. In the alignment of the opening 6, to allow the free-line passage of the product 8 in scrolling and a lower output 7 for the evacuation of the recovered liquid. The separator according to the invention is characterized in that the box 5 comprises means, such as a jet perpendicu larch 13, to cause a shear of the jet surrounding the product, preferably in association with means for weakening the jet prior to the previously its shear. The separator allows an effective interruption of the jet surrounding the product as well as the recovery and evacuation of the liquid thus collected. It finds its application as terminal element of a metal bar cooling apparatus at the end of rolling or products during continuous casting.
