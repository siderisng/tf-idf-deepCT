<ABSTRACT>
Heat evaporator exchanger of the plate type.This exchanger is characterized in that the inlet of the cold fluid is provided with a liner of a thermal insulating material intended to create, by restriction of the input diameter, an additional pressure drop for said cold fluid and to make a gradientMore important thermal between said cold fluid and hot plates in the power supply.Application to heat pumps and the recovery of solar energy.
