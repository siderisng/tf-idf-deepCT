<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a carbonaceous paste which is carried out at room temperature, without reheating.This dough contains: - at least 70% carbonaceous material, a low temperature plasticizer, such as a molasses, sugar, alginate or lignulfate, - a high temperature plasticizer, such as a dry brai.This paste applies, in particular, to the brazing of the electrolysis cells of the aluminum.
