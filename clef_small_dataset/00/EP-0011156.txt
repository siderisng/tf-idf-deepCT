<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method and apparatus for exhausting fish in which the head and entrails are torn from a piece of the fish trunk.The invention consists of noting (a) that the neck of the pea sound, then to be removed (b) the head of about 90 ° with respect to the axis of the body, finally to operate a relative displacement (C, D, e) of the head along the trunk of the fish towards the back of it.This results in a complete evisceration of the fish.The apparatus for implementing the method essentially comprises a knife (8), a guide (7) for folding the fish trunk and two devices graying one (1, 2) the head the other (11, 12) the fish trunk and animated by different movements causing the above-mentioned displacement.
