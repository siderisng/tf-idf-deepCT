<ABSTRACT>
To isolate a factor for stimulation of the proliferation rate of liver cells, the residual livers are homogenized in partial hepatectomized animals or the blood plasma partial hepatectomized animals or the livers of foetal animals or the blood plasma foetal animals.After the effect of neuraminidase or neuraminyltransferase, acidification to pH = 5.5 and heat denaturation at 95 ° C, a protein or proteid with a molecular weight of about 30,000 to 50,000 D is produced, which causes a significant increase in the mitoserates of liver cells.
