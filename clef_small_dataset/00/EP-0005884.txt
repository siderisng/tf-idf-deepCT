<TITLE>
monorail mountain slide.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>
koudelka slavos<sep>koudelka, slavos<sep>koudelka, slavoseast ridge roadwarwick, new york 10990us<sep>
</APPLICANT>
<INVENTOR>

</INVENTOR>
<ABSTRACT>
a monorail mountain slide including a mountain coaster  (101) and a monorail (110) for recreational transportation  along an inclined path (120) on a hill or mountain comprising  a channel (111) disposed on and spaced from the path (120),  a chassis frame (105) movable along the channel (111), a  coaster shell resiliently mounted on the chassis frame (105),  riding wheels (115, 117) rotatably mounted on the chassis  frame (105) and engaged with the channel (111) for movement  of the frame (105) along the channel (111), retainer  wheels rotatably mounted to the frame (105) disposed about  and engaged with the channel (111) to retain the frame (105)  on the channel (111) and a brake (112, 113, 114, 123) pivotally  mounted to the frame (105) and engagable with the channel  (111) to stop the movement of the frame (105) on the channel  (111).  the channel (111) is suspended above the inclined  path (120) by suspension brackets (125, 136, 140, 141, 142,  143, 145) which securely prevent the channel (111) from  moving.  the brackets (125, 136, 140, 141, 142, 143, 145) and  channel (111) are easily removable to change the location of  the slide run.  
</ABSTRACT>
