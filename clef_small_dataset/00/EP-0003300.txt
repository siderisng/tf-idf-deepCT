<ABSTRACT>
A catalyst containing the components a) Til₄ and / or Tior¹l₃, B) TIXCl₃ for polymerizing butadiene means a possibly substituted alkoxy group, cycloalkoxy group, phenoxy group or a carboxyl group of the formula R⁷COO, whereinR⁷ represents an alkyl radical, a cycloalkyl radical or phenyl.The preparation of the catalyst is carried out under air and moisture exclusion in hydrocarbons as solvent by reacting the above components at temperatures of -10 to 80 ° C together.
