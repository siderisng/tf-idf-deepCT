<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for preparing aryldiazonium salts in aqueous solutions.The method according to the invention is characterized in that the diazotation reaction is carried out by nitrous vapors (mixture no; NO₂, N₂O₃) rich in NO (no / no₂ molar ratio to equilibrium between 2 and 100).The yield of arydiazonium salt is quantitative with respect to the amine and relative to transformed nitrous vapors.Ayldiazonium salt aqueous solutions can be used conventionally.They open access to amino-azo-aromatic compounds.
