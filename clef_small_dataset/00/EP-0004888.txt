<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a pretension for measuring and registering the location, the location and / or the location change of a rigid body in the room using a field generator arranged on the body, preferably a magnetic field generator, with in the distance From the field generator, arranged by the body independent means for detecting the field flow or the field flow change during a measurement and with an electronic device for obtaining and evaluating in a field flow or a field flow change resulting electrical signals. The field generator (3) is formed so that it generates a defined irregular field. For detecting the field flow, several magnetic flux sensors (A, B, C, D, E, F) with magnetic flux-dependent sensor elements (17) are at a distance (A, B, C) are detected in at least two, preferably in three perpendicular to one another. arranged on a carrier (6). The arrangement is also made so that a clearance is formed by the distance (A, B, C) between the magnetic flux receivers (A to F). The device is particularly suitable for use in dentistry for the detection of lower jaw movements in a patient.
