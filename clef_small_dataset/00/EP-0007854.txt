<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a distribution device of a solid divided into a speaker.This device comprises a divided solid feeding hopper (1), an exhaust chimney (2) integral with said hopper, a rotating member disposed downstream of the output orifice of said chimney and a motor means (5)To rotate said rotating member.said tower member comprises a shaft (6) driven by said motor means and disposed substantially along the axis of the evacuation duct, and at least one deflector element (7) of low hardness worn by said shaft and articulated on that.To deviate angularly, under the effect of centrifugal force, when it is rotatably driven.Application in particular to the storage of grains in the silos.
