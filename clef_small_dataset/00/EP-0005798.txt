<ABSTRACT>
A fiber optic measuring device for measuring physical quantities, such as position, acceleration, pressure, elongation, temperature, force, has a light source and a donor acted from the measured variable, which affects the emitted light depending on the instantaneous value of the physical quantity.Furthermore, detectors for converting the light influenced by the encoder are present in electrical signals.The light influenced by the encoder is divided into at least two components, the measuring component and at least one compensation component.The meter includes electronic members to obtain a measured value from the measuring component and a control variable from the compensation component.With the latter control variable either the light source or the detector electronics are controlled for stabilizing the optoelectronics and fiber optics of the meter.
