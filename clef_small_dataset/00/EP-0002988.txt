<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a high pressure separation device for a polyphasic mixture consisting of a liquid particle gas and in particular for a mixture of ethylene and polyethylene, which comprises a cylindrical vertical enclosure (1) in which plunges a device.The arrival of the mixture and provided on its lower wall of an orifice for the evacuation duct (3) of the separate liquids, a vertical cyclone (4) in communication with the enclosure and provided on its upper wall of an orifice for an orifice.The evacuation duct (6) of the separated gases in the cyclone (4) and an ejector (8) consisting of a nozzle, a mixture zone and a diffuser capable of reducing the speed of the mixture.
