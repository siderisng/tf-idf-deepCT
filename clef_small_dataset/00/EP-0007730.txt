<ABSTRACT>
a semiconductor laser device of the double-hetero struc­ ture has a semiconductor substrate (4), a semiconductor  active layer (1) and cladding layers (2,3) sandwiching the  active layer (1).  in order to achieve a laser which can operate at a short  wavelength.  e. g.  about 0. 6 µm, the substract (4) is gaastp1-t  (0. 54 ≦ t ≦ 1. 0) and the active layer (1) is gayin1-yasxp1-x(0. 52  ≦ y ≦ 1. 0 and 0 ≦ x ≦ 1. 0).  the first cladding layer (2) is  gapal1-pasqp1-q (0 ≦ p ≦ 0. 91 and 0. 47 ≦ q ≦ 1. 0) and the  second cladding layer (3) is gap′al1-p′asq′p1-q′ (0 ≦ p′ ≦ 0. 91  and 0. 47 ≦ q′ ≦ 1. 0).  the two cladding layers (2,3) are of  opposite conductivity type.  the active layer (1) has an optical  confinement region of refractivity higher than that of the clad­ ding layers and a band gap narrower than that of the cladding  layers.  
</ABSTRACT>
