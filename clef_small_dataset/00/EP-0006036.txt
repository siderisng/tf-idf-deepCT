<ABSTRACT>
This device, very simple but effective, comprises a tube or sleeve (6), in flexible elastomer, fixed on a cup (3,4,5) integral with the rod (2) of the damper.This sleeve comprises at least one series of slots (12,13) oriented parallel to its generators and its free edge is in the vicinity of a support surface (10) so that during a relative displacement between the rod (2) and the body (1) towards the attack position, the sleeve (6) deforms in an area wherein the rod is mixture protected by the body (1) or by a conventional protective bellows (7).
