<ABSTRACT>
In the curtain rail with cord are the curtain carriers (5, 5a) on which the curtain is attached, freely movably mounted in at least one guide channel (8, 9) of the rail (1).In this channel, the cord (15) is also housed, which is guided at the ends of the rail (1) via deflection or outlet rollers.The curtain carriers (5, 5a) are frictionally engaged with the cord (15), so that they can be moved by the cord (15), but rest rest upon occurrence of a resistor, the cord (15) slows through.The curtain can be operated with both the cord and by spinning.
