<ABSTRACT>
In the core deformation of pneumatic tires (33), groups of the segmented core (1a, 1b, 1c, 1d) are moved to the tire center of the tire center and from there outside the tire plane.The core construction in the tire tool (30, 32) takes place in the opposite direction.The individual core segments (1) are connected to the tire center with a guide and adapter part (21), whereby an automatic, trouble-free core and degradation is possible.In a pureform with such a segmented core, the assembly and pressing of tires is also possible as the production of integral tires made of pourable elastomers.
