<ABSTRACT>
coffee capsules are described, comprising a hardened  shell of coffee and/or coffee substitute solids surrounding  a droplet of coffee oil containing coffee aroma.  they are made by contacting particles of an emulsion  of coffee oil and aromatic coffee distillate with particles of  instant coffee and/or coffee substitute powder.  the capsules  are added to instant coffee or an instant-coffee substitute.
