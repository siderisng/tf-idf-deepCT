<ABSTRACT>
This rolling mill comprises a rolling stand (1) with three receptacles (2) for introducing as rolling rollers of designed tools (16), which are hydraulically pressed against the tube to be rolled.In order to allow the attachment and adjustment of the tools to a reliable and easy manner, each receptacle (2) comprises a movable piston rod (3), softened with two pistons, of which the outer piston (4) with an outer cylinder (5) and the inner piston (6) operates together with an inner cylinder (7), wherein the pistons are connected by a flange (10) with a flange (13) of a slider (12), which a printing plate (14) for pressing aBracket Tum (15) has on the inner cylinder (7).
