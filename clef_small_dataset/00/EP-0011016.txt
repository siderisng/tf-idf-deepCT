<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the systems for broadcasting television audio-visual signals intended to be stored on a recording medium.The subject of the invention relates to a television audio-visual signal broadcasting system in which a pure (P) frequency pilot signal is used in a registered for video signals to reconstitute, during the reading process., the synchronization signals necessary for the reproduction of audio-visual signals.The invention also relates to the coding method implemented.The invention applies in particular to the coding of color television signals, synchronized by Frequency pilot, intended to be recorded on the recording medium of a video device.
