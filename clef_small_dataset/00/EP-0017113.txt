<ABSTRACT>
The invention includes an apparatus and method for generating a plurality of liquid threads and drops for recording information on a moving disk (9), wherein a homogeneous liquid film (5) is generated on a drainage surface (1), the film in free-falling liquid threads(6) and the threads are disassembled in droplets (7) and the drops not needed for the information are separated and collected.
