<ABSTRACT>
A workpiece (16) having an outer teeth and to be provided with an internal toothing is attached to a gearswing machine.On this workpiece are arranged via a rotary plate (36) guide rails (38), on which auxiliary carriage carriage (40) is guided so that a perpendicularly slidably mounted auxiliary stingle (44) via a sliding drive (82) and a rod (84) againstor to a stand (18) of the gearswing machine can be moved.The auxiliary steadle (44) leads to each downward stroke of a plug (26) of the gear interlocking machine, also downwardly directed worksheing.At the end of the downward stroke, the auxiliary stand (42) is moved away toward the stand (17).The device according to the invention can be easily mounted on known gear fattening machines and is particularly suitable for the production of clutch teeth of large workpieces.
