<ABSTRACT>
An information processing apparatus for receiving and further processing optical information comprises a hand reading device which is guided by hand via the information carrier for receiving the information along a first direction, wherein the information along a second direction perpendicular to the first direction, z.b.Column for column within the overlined line, is scanned.The handling device comprises means for clocking the scan along the second direction as a function of the relative speed between information carrier and hand reading device.
