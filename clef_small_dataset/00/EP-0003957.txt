<ABSTRACT>
a process for reducing the residual monomer level of a  polymer latex prepared by emulsion polymerisation comprises  exposing the latex in a separate vessel from that in  which the main polymerisation has been carried out to conditions  under which free radicals are produced by a secondary  catalyst added to the latex, the said catalyst being oil soluble  or having an oil soluble component.  in a preferred embodiment  a polymer latex prepared by the emulsion polymerisation  of styrene alone or a mixture containing styrene having a  residual styrene level not exceeding 0. 1% based on latex volume  may be obtained.
