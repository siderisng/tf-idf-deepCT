<ABSTRACT>
1.  a hardenable magnesia cement mixture consisting of magnesium oxide, magnesium chloride, water and coal waste, characterised in that crushed washing and/or local waste in an amount of 50 to 90% by weight are used as the coal waste, that the washing and/or local waste component contains at least 5% by weight of particles which are as fine as flour and have a size of less than 0. 09 mm, that the magnesium chloride solution has a density of above 1. 22 g/cm**3 , that the amount of the magnesium chloride solution lies between 5 and 25% by weight and the amount of the magnesium oxide also lies between 5 and 25% by weight.
