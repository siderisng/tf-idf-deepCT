<ABSTRACT>
In a cross-slip device for creating the cutting program for an automatic controlled model cutting machine for glass panes with X-Y web control, the signals to be stored are generated by two rotation in the pulse sensors (9,10).The rotary pulse generator (9) for the X direction is at the bridge movable in the X-direction (4), and the rotary pulse generator (10) is arranged on the carriage (5) movable in the Y direction.To drive the rotary pulse encoders (9, 10) serve driverol Len, which are driven by frictional closure with the coordinate rails.The drive rollers are formed as permanent or electromagnets.The effective diameter of the drive rollers is variable.
