<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a light collecting device with a so-called "fluorescence plate" consisting of a plastic support, containing fluorescent particles and provided with at least one light exit window. In order to turn off the disturbing self-assembly in the fluorescent plate, which result from an overlap of the EMIS sion spectrum with the absorption spectrum of the fluorine die range, the following fluorescence body composition is proposed: the fluorescent particles have a dipole moment with different sizes in the ground state or in the excited state; In the plastic carrier, an amphiphilic addition of colloidal is dissolved, such that the fluorescent particles are each enclosed by one of the colloid particles and obtained an environment with orientation polarization. In a preferred embodiment, a polar solvent for the fluorescent particles is engaged in the colloid particles. The proposed device is particularly suitable as a solar collector, for optical transmission of messages or to image brightening of passive displays.
