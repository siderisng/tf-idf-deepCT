<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a process for producing needle-shaped ferrimagnetic iron oxides by draining acicular iron (III) oxide hydroxides to acicular iron (III) oxides, subsequent reduction of these iron (III) oxides at temperatures between 300 and 650 ° C by means of organic compounds decomposing in this temperature range andOxidation of the reduction product with oxygen-containing gases at 200 to 500 ° C, wherein the needled acifle-shaped iron (III) oxide hydroxide consists of a mixture of goethit and lepidocrocitite with a lepidocontinated part of at least 60% and is dehydrated at temperatures of 250 to 700 ° C and theUse of the acicular ferrimagnetic iron oxides thus prepared for producing magnetic recording medium.
</ABSTRACT>
