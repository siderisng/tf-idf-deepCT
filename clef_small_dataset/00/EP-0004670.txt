<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for producing aluminum fluoride by reacting aluminum hydroxide with silica hydrofluoric acid in aqueous solution, wherein the AIF₃ solution obtained after filtering off the filtering of 0.1 - 1 wt.% HF Even before crystallization at product temperatures of 110-350 ° C, preferably using aluminum fluoride particles as heat transmitters, is expressed.
