<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for producing small-part polyolefinform compositions of 100 wt.parts polyolefin and 1 to 30 wt.parts of a conductivity and optionally other additives.In this method, the mixtures of polyolefin powder and carbon black are mixed in a first stage until the crystalline melting temperature of the polyolefin is reached and brought in a second stage to a temperature of the polyolefin of the polyolefin.Subsequently, the mixer is discharged within 0.5 to 30 seconds and wrapped within 120 seconds.
