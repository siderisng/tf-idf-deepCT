<ABSTRACT>
direction indicating apparatus for vehicles, for warning  the driver of a short-circuit in the direction indicating system  and protecting against damage by (e. g. ) overheating or burn­ out.  the apparatus receives both a flashing signal for energiz­ ing direction indicating lamps and a detection signal indica­ tive of the electric current flowing to the direction indicating  lamps.  the detection signal is compared with a reference  signal so that a short-circuit in the system is detected.  in the  event of a short-circuit, the direction indicating lamps are  inhibited to thereby warn the driver of a malfunction and  protect the system.  detection of a short-circuit is disabled for a  period of time in which a comparatively large surge current  flows to the direction indicating lamps.  
</ABSTRACT>
