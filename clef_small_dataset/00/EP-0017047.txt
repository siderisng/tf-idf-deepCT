<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a ratchet key which allows both the up and forth movements of the keysheet into a drive movement to the output Sachse (3).For this purpose, the a drive shaft (18) forming key shaft carries a driving bowling gear (19), with the diagonally opposite teeth two driven cone gears (7,8) comb on the output axis (3).A first derailleur (9,11) is disposed between the output axis (3) and the a driven cone gear (7) and a second derailleur (9 ', 11') is located between the output axis (3) and the other transmission nen cone gear (8), wherein each of the switching machines in a rotation of the associated cone gear in the drive nozzle to the output axis (3) locks and unlocked upon opposite rotation.
