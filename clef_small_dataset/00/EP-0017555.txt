<ABSTRACT>
Process for the preparation of high molecular weight acrylic polymers or copolymers having a very low residual monomer (s) (s) content, which is to deposit on a support a liquid layer of an aqueous solution of acrylic monomer (s)) having a pH of about 4 to about 14 and containing a polymerization photo promoter and submit the liquid layer to the action of a wave long radiation between about 300 nanometers and about 450 nanometers until obtainedof a rubbery layer, characterized in that at least one of the two faces of the rubbery layer obtained with at least one sulfite and / or at least one alkaline metabisulfite is coated with at least one, the coated rubber layer is reduced., one dries the pieces and, possibly they are grinded.The polymers or copolymers obtained are usable as flocculants for water treatment.
