<ABSTRACT>
in a drive unit for an intermittently drivable conveyor belt (2), a drive shaft (1) is influenced by two motors (3, 4).  the drive shaft is provided on both sides with in each case one lever (9, 10) which are arranged on the drive shaft by means of free wheels (7, 8).  the levers are in connection via in each case one track drive (5, 6) with in each case one of the motors.  the motors (3, 4) are alternately actuated for the movement of the conveyor belt in such a way that firstly the first motor (3) drives the conveyor belt during the forward running, while the second motor (4) is in reverse running and does not have an effect on the drive shaft (1) via the free wheel (8).  then the second motor (4) drives the conveyor belt (2) by changing over from the reverse running to the forward running position and the first motor (3) also changes its direction of rotation and does not have an effect on the drive shaft (1) via the free wheel (7).  in this way the conveyor belt is always kept in movement during the clock times corresponding to the set programme.  the conveyor belt (2) is only stopped in order to be able to transfer shaped articles (11) from the conveyor belt (12) to the conveyor belt (2).  <image>
</ABSTRACT>
