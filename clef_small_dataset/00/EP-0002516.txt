<ABSTRACT>
The vacuum head (10) for flat, thin workpieces, z.b.Plates, foil elements and the like., has a peripherally extending contact surface (24) which is connected via throttle holes with a main chamber serving as a vacuum chamber.The contact surface (24) includes a central portion which is slightly reset and thus forms a locked chamber when a flat workpiece under vacuum is applied to the contact surface (24).From the contact surface (24) protrude movable sensitivity, which are actuated only by those workpieces for identifying the workpiece, which have no hole on the associated positions.
