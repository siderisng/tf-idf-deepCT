<ABSTRACT>
Fragmented unit loading of propellant powder having the ballistic properties of granular loading.Such charging consists of: - 85 to 97% nitrocellulose propulsive powder grains, - 15 to 3% of an energy binder, thermoplastic, posing in the form of small solid grains under normal conditions and having the compositionNext: (a) 30 to 60% polyvinyl nitrate, (b) 70 to 40% nitrocellulose with low nitrogen levels, or polyvinyl acetate or a mixture of both, (c) possibly a stabilizer.The use of a solid binder makes it possible to instantly produce the homogeneous mixture of the propulsive powder with said binder, the molding of precise amounts of the mixture thus obtained, and to simplify the molding operations.
