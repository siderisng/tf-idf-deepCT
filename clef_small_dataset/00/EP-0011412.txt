<ABSTRACT>
a data processor having a microprogrammed control  store is disclosed which includes a micro control store (18)  having relatively short microwords which provide sequenc­ ing information and a nano control store (20) having relatively  long nanowords which control transfers and operations  within the execution unit (22) of the data processor.  the micro  control (18) store and nano control store (20) are addressed  simultaneously with same address.  whereas each microword  in the micro control store corresponds to a unique address, a  single nanoword may be selected by many different addres­ ses.  since each nanoword is unique, the overall size of the  control store is minimized.  
</ABSTRACT>
