<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a superimposed field effect transistor and manufacturing process.The transistor is essentially characterized in that the floating door (6) and the control door (8) are self-aligned with each other and in addition, they are self-aligned with the source regions (12) and drain (13).Preferably the material constituting doors is the polycrystalline silicon.The manufacturing process only includes four masking steps to achieve the structure represented.This transistor can be used in electrically reprogrammed memories.
