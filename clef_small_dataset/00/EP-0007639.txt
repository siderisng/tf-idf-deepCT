<ABSTRACT>
polyolefin compositions having a high level of impact  resistance and high temperature flow resistance comprising  30 to 90 weight percent resin containing greater than 50 to less  than 75 weight percent of polyolefin and greater than 25 to  less than 50 weight percent elastomer, and 10 to 70 weight  percent filler are described.  metal plastic laminates are pre­ pared by sandwiching a plastic sheet prepared from the  polyolefin compositions between two sheets of metal.  figure 2 shows the impact strength of polyolefin resin-­ filler compositions in which the resin-filler weight ratio is  constant but the elastomer content of the resin varies.
