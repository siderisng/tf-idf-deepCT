<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an aqueous lipid standard solution containing at least one lipid and a nonionic detergent and is characterized by a content of 0.5 to 10 wt.-% of an ionic detective gene.Thus, it is possible to deliver a soft, stable, aqueous standard solution with high solving capacity for lipids.
