<ABSTRACT>
It comprises inside a horizontal axis cylindrical violle, arranged parallel to said axis and symmetrically with respect to the longitudinal symmetry plane.(a) in its lower part, a pair of separators (10, 13) eliminating the water from the water and steam emulsion to overheat, b) in its upper part, a pair of removable beams of tubes constituting the superheats(14), c) channels allowing a portion of the water and steam emulsion to rise on either side separators and superheats near the internal surface of the ferrule (1), thenTo go down to each other to open at the entrance of the separators near the plane of symmetry.Application to the overheated water vapor supply of a high power turbine.
