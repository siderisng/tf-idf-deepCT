<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a circuit arrangement for encoding or decoding, in which control bits are formed by using a cyclic codes that an information polynomial is divided by a generator polynomial.The coding should be adapted to the respective requirements as simple as possible.The invention provides a device (4) for solving the binary information into several words of the same bit number, a modulo-2 adder (7) and a partial decoder.The tell decoder shares the words by the generator polynomial.The remainder determined is added to the next word in the modulo-2 adder (7).The part coder can be in particular a ROM.An inventive circuit arrangement is particularly suitable for use in telecontrol equipment.
