<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a rotary drum with loose on the drum jacket (1) provided running ring (2) and arranged underlayers (3) arranged therebetween, wherein for axial position securing of the underlayers (3) locking lines (4, 5) in the range of the same end (eg 3a)This lechic sheet is provided by arranging a locking element (5) on the drum jacket (1) and the other locking element (4) on the back panel (3).Through this design, a simple replacement of the underlayers and a change in the radial game is made possible without loosening a locking element attached to the drum jacket.
