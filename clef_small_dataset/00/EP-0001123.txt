<ABSTRACT>
In a capsule arrangement for cooling semiconductor chips (10), a heat dissipating bolt (22) is positioned on each chip (10).The bolt (22) extends within a resilient bellows (21).The chip carrier end of the bellows (21) is fixed in a recess (20) of the capsule arrangement.Between the chips (10) and the space outside the bellows (21), a coolant I and within the bellows (21) is provided another coolant II.Both coolants I and II may be the same, preferably liquid helium or unequal when the space within the bellows (21) is connected to a cooling fluid system separated from the coolant I outside the bellows.
