<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a method and a reticted product extrusion device.According to this method, a first bitch 13 receives only a crosslinkable product 11 and transmits it via a filter 14 to an injector 15 at which a crosslinking agent 16 such a peroxide is injected.Then, the mixture of the crosslinkable product and the crosslinking agent is transmitted to an extrusion head 17 to be applied for example to a cable 18. Application to the manufacture of electrical cables.
