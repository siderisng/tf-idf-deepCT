<ABSTRACT>
the invention relates to the manufacture of electronic circuits using microcomponents.  microcomponent beds are formed using matrices 10 comprising, in the upper portion, a plurality of identical housings 12, and in the lower portion, a plate 15 carrying a digital coding which defines an orientation code for the matrix and a component code.  this type of microcomponent bed can be utilised in various microcomponent manipulating machines, in automatic or semiautomatic mode.  application to the manufacture of thin or thick film circuits.  <image>
