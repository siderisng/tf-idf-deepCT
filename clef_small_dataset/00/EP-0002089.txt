<ABSTRACT>
in a device for indicating the connected-in-place condition  of a physically removable subassembly (7, 8) having  connections intended to mate and disengage with and  from fixed connections in a rack-mounting arrangement  (1) in response to corresponding translational movements,  use is made of light whose source (2) is in the rack (1).   light propagation in an optical fiber (4′) disposed in the  subassembly (7, 8) and terminating on a visible side thereof  is used to provide a visual indication.  another optical fiber (4, 5, 6) may extend from the  light source (2) in the rack (1) to the subassembly (7, 8).   the fiber end facing the subassembly is then connected to  that end of the fiber in the subassembly (7, 8) facing the  rack (1) by means of a fiber optic connector (10, 27) consisting  of two halves (10′,11).  the connector (10, 27) contains  a flexible, slit diphragm (31) which is penetrated by  the end of a fiber (4′) when the connector halves are fully  mated.
