<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for anodic oxidation of tape, film or plate-shaped aluminum material or its alloys in an aqueous electrolyte containing sulfuric acid and aluminum ions; The material can also be mechanically, chemically or electrochemically roughened before performing the anodic oxidation. The anodic oxidation of the material takes place in an electrolyte of a concentration of sulfuric acid of 25 to 100 g / l and aluminum ions of 10 to 25 g / l, at a current density of 4 to 25 A / DM² and at a temperature of 25 ° to 65 ° C. The method is used in particular in the preparation of a band, film or plate-shaped printing plate carrier material, then these carrier materials can be coated with the manufacturer of pre-sensitized printing plates or the consumer itself with a photosensitive mass. These photosensitive layers are optionally stained and preferably contain diazo compounds, diazoquinones, diazomic condensates or photopolymerizable compounds.
