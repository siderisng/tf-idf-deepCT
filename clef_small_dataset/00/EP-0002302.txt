<ABSTRACT>
Preforms with an open end (1) comprises a neck (2) molded at least partially in its final form.The molding is made by blowing in a mold (4) by means of a blowing nozzle (6, 7, 8) penetrating inside the preforms by their open end (1).When introducing the blowing nozzle (6, 7, 8) is isolated the region (1, 2) from the inside of the preforms, between the nozzle and the molded portion of the neck (2) of the preforms,The remainder of the preforms and a refrigeration of the isolated region (1, 2) is carried out by a scan by means of a refrigerant fluid.It is thus avoided to deform during the blowing the part of the already molded neck on the preform in its final form.
</ABSTRACT>
