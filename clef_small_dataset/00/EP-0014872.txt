<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to homogeneous, thermoplastic mixtures of a) 85 - 99.5 wt.-% isotactic polypro pylene and b) 15 - 0.5 wt.-% liquid, saturated digano polysiloxanes with a viscosity of 30000 to 80 000 cST at 20 ° C.The elastic ratio of moldings made of inventive polypropylene / diorganopolysiloxane mixtures is significantly improved at low diorganopolysiloxane additives, while heat-forming and stiffness remain largely preserved.
