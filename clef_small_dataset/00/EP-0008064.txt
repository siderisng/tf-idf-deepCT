<ABSTRACT>
a fluid dispenser characterized by an elongate housing  (1) adapted to envelope an end portion of a flexible fluid-­ conveying tube (4), and having an opening (3) at one end  for discharging fluid conveyed by the tube a resilient pinch  clamp (7) fitted within the housing through which the flex­ ible tube may pass and be clamped shut thereby lever means  outside of the housing and pivotally mounted through the  sides thereof, at least one tab (2a) between the clamping  edges of the pinch clamp and operatively connected to,  or forming part of, said lever means, whereby movement  of the lever means toward the housing pivots the tab and  forces the clamping edges apart and upon release of the  lever means, the clamping edges close back upon each  other.  
</ABSTRACT>
