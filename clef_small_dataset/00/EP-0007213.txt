a method and apparatus for the space dyeing of yarn  while the yarn is in the form of a loosely gathered package,  such as a conventional yarn skein (32), and which includes  injecting an atomized spray of dye and air at a plurality of  spaced apart locations in the package, and such that the dye  contacts only a portion of the yarn.  the resulting yarn is  characterized by essentially random and relatively short colÂ­ orband lengths, which avoid the formation of unsightly  streaks of "chevrons" on the face of a fabric formed from the  yarn.
