<ABSTRACT>
The plastic powder according to the invention consists of latex particles having an average particle size between 1 and 5 μm, which can be easily aggregated in part or completely larger particles.The latex particles consist of a crosslinked mixing polymer of 75 - 99 wt.-% of a monomer mixture of methyl and isobutyl methacrylate in weight ratio 1: 3 to 3: 1 and 0.5 - 10 wt.-% of a crosslinking comonomer with at least two polymeric double bonds.The plastic powder is incorporated into a coating center, further comprising a flawing, liquid component and a binder, in particular an acrylic resin, dissolved or dispersed form and for generating a non-blocking and non-adhesive coating on a solid substrate, e.g.b.Plasticizer containing PVC is used.
