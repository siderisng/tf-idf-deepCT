<ABSTRACT>
The torch comprises two sets of elements (10 to 19 and 20 to 27) with each other an annular chamber (4).Racling means (19) detach the highly adherent particles to the lower portion (4a) of the chamber and blowing means detach the weakly adherent particles and cause all the particles detached out of the chamber (4).The invention applies in particular to the MIG welding torches.
