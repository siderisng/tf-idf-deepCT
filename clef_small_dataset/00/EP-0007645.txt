<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a light collecting device with a so-called "fluorescence plate", which consists of a solid support material with a refractive index greater than 1, contains fluorescent particles and provided with at least one light exit window. In order to turn off the disturbing self-assembly in the fluorescence plate, which causes the absorption spectrum of the fluorescent material by a partial overlap of the emission spectrum, the following fluorescence body composition is proposed: the fluorescent particles have a dipole moment with different values ​​in the ground state or in excitation, and The carrier material consists of a polar, water-containing, amorphous polysilicate or polyphosphate. If the fluorescent fabric is an organic compound, then an amphiphilic polar addition of colloidal is dissolved in the carrier, such that the fluorescent particles are each enclosed by one of the colloid particles. The proposed device awards mainly as a solar collector, for the optical transmission of messages or image brightening of passive displays.
