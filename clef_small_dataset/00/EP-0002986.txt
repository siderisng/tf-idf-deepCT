<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to welding or brazing of coated sheets of a degradable protective layer at temperature, such as zinc or plastic material.According to the invention, upside down the electric torch (11) relative to the sheets (1) and (2) to be soldered along the seal (8) and provided with protective coatings (4) and (5),A cooling shoe (13) consisting of a housing (14), supplied with liquid nitrogen, the nitrogen gaseous escaping by holes (17).This arrangement makes it possible to avoid the heating of the coating without which it could degrade by fusion and / or volulation.The invention applies to the assembly by welding or brazing of coated metal parts.
