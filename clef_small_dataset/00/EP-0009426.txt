<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method of manufacturing wide distribution polyolefins of molecular masses.The method consists of polymerizing ethylene, itself accompanied by one or more other fine ololates, in contact with a catalytic system comprising: a) a catalyst consisting of a solid titanium, magnesium and halogen compound.such as chlorine or bromine b) one or more organometallic compounds of a metal of groups II and III of the periodic classification of the elements c) one or more ethylenic halogenated hydrocarbons.The resulting polyolefins are particularly able to be transformed by extrusion blowing.
