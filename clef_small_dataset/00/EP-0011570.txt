<ABSTRACT>
Two analog filters (1), of the second order, are mounted in series with a transverse filter with load transfer (2), downstream and upstream thereof.The analog filters (1) are switched capabilities comprising an amplifier (3) and a network of MOS capacities and transistors.The filtering device is fully integrated on the same semiconductor substrate.
