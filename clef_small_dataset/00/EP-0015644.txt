<ABSTRACT>
ambient or low temperature curable coating composi­ tions are disclosed which comprise aldehyde polymer and  hydrazide or hydrazone curing agent.  the polymer con­ tains 0. 25 to 10% acrolein or methacrolein, 0. 5 to 10% acid  and up to 99. 25% of other units.  the curing agent is a di­ carboxylic acid bis-hydrazide or hydrazone.  the composi­ tions may be used to coat flexible substrates such as  leather and leather substitutes.  a process is disclosed for  coating such materials with finish, plate or embossing,  color and top coats, in which at least the top coat compri­ ses such compositions.  
</ABSTRACT>
