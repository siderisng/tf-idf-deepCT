<ABSTRACT>
The oral administration of medicines in animals is, especially when these drugs have an unpleasant, mostly bitter taste often associated with great difficulties.The oral, for example, a medicament given via a medical lining is often not accepted and the desired oral treatment can either be carried out either or only in limited and mostly insufficient dimensions under forced.It has now been found that by using liver flour as a formulating auxiliary material (in mediated animal feed based on liver flour), a voluntary oral inclusion of the medicament active ingredient ("self-intake principle") is made possible by the animal to be treated.
