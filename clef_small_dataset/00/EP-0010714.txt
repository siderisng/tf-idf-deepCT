<TITLE>
intumescent flame-resistant materials, method for their preparation and their use.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>
bayer agde<sep>bayer ag<sep>bayer ag51368 leverkusende<sep>
</APPLICANT>
<INVENTOR>

</INVENTOR>
<ABSTRACT>
moulding compositions based on intumescants and spherical or almost spherical fillers exhibit much reduced dripping and fully intact foaming power at elevated temperature, for example in the event of fire.  these moulding compositions are prepared by mixing intumescants and fillers and are used as cements, filling compositions, coating agents or for producing foamed articles.
