<ABSTRACT>
Silicatic complexing agent for alkaline earth ions obtainable by loading a material with alkali by acid activation of a mineral from the Montmorillonite countertablite series.Preferred starting material is acid-activated bentonite.The loading of the acid activated material can be mixed with an alkali metal hydroxide, carbonate, phosphate or borate by dry or wet.
