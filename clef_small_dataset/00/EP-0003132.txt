<ABSTRACT>
The closure to a container, for example doses of closure cap, for powder or granular products has a lockable opening for selectively dispensing and targeted application of the container content by pressure applied to the container. Previous packaging has been equipped with closures that did not allow a targeted application of the powder. Through the invention, a closure was provided, with which a targeted directed powder beam is generated and which ensures a trouble-free removal of the container content. The opening of the closure is formed as a stepped outlet channel (7). The internal opening cross-section (8) of the outlet channel (7) is smaller than the outwardly facing (9), thereby avoiding clogging when removing. By this dimensioning of the outlet channel (7), a powder beam is caused when compressing the container, which allows the desired targeted product order. The channel opening is arranged laterally in the closure formed as a can locking cap and the slide closure is arranged vertically.
