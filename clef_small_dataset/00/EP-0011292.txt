<ABSTRACT>
1.  a cup-shaped packing container (1) injection moulded out of plastics material and tapering upwards, which has a bottom (2) which can be put on after the filling operation but which otherwise is constructed in one piece with a resealable cover (3), which is connected to the upper area of the container by means of an annular narrow region (4, 5, 6) which can be detached, wherein the detached cover can be inserted into the container opening formed by the detachment, characterised in that below the narrow region the container has an inner annular face (21) which is joined to the upper area of the container (1) and which is at least approximately cylindrical, and above the narrow region on the resealable cover there is provided an outer annular face (22) which is at least approximately cylindrical, wherein the two annular faces have essentially equal diameters and, in resealing so that the underside of the cover remains facing towards the inside of the container, interlock in a push-fit manner, and in that below a neck (18) on which is provided the annular face (21) the container (1) comprises a conical tapering portion (24) which forms a junction with the slightly conical main part (23) of the container (1). 
</ABSTRACT>
