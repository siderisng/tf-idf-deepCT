<ABSTRACT>
In a method for opening folded pressure sheet, in particular for placing on the conveyor saddle of a book threaded machine, the pressure sheets of means for spreading the individual bog parts are passed one after the other up to the opening site.In this case, the pressure sheet (1) is passed on blowing vacuum means (3) and the free area of the outer bog gap parts are sucked through the sofly of the blowing air and spread from the rest of the pressure sheet.The abge spread bodle part is directed to the suction of a subsequent bodle part from the active region of a downstream blowing air suction device.
