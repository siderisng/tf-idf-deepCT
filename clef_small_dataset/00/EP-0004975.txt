The application relates to steroid-21-halogen-17-alkyl carbonates with anti-inflammatory properties which are suitable for treating inflammatory dermatoses and a method for their preparation by introducing the halogen atom into corresponding 21-sulfonates or 17.21-dialkylcarbonates.
