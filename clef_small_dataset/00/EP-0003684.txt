<ABSTRACT>
a method of drying textile material which comprises  revolving or otherwise circulating the material in a substantially  closed container whilst simultaneously exposing  the material to radio frequency energy and evacuating  moisture from the container by suction.  apparatus for performing this method comprises  a container (1), a turntable (5) means for applying  radio frequency energy to the material while it is  circulating or revolving and a vacuum pump for evacuating  water or water vapour from the container.  
</ABSTRACT>
