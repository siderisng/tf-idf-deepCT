<ABSTRACT>
The method according to the invention relates to the preparation of acetaldehyde by reacting ethylene and oxygen in the reactor (1) in the presence of an aqueous solution of palladium and copper chloride.The gaseous mixture leaving the reap gate (1) is cooled (9), and the condensate formed is returned to the reap gate (1) (10; 7).The cooled gas mixture is washed with water (14), wherein an aqueous acetaldehyde solution is formed.This is subjected to an extractive distillation (22) with water.In the consequences of the distillation (27) of the bottom product of the extractive distillation (22), pure acetaldehyde is obtained as the top product (29), while the water formed as a swamp is attributed to the wash (14) of the gaseous reaction (46).
