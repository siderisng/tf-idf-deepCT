<ABSTRACT>
Method and apparatus for pressing and packaging thread cables in bale shape with flexible packaging material and reinforcing bands with the aid of a box press whose pressing stamp is divided into a core stamp (4) and a surrounding outer punch (5) and the position of the two stamps varies to each other during the pressing operationcan be.By holding down (10), when the outer punch (5), which forms during pressing, the collar (8) made of packaging material can be switched so that it no longer hinders a bend when lowering the external punch (5) again.
