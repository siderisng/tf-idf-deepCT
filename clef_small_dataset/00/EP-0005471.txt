<ABSTRACT>
Preparation of 3-hydroxy-2,2,4-trimethylpentylisobutyrate by reacting isobutyraldehyde in the presence of alkaline earth hydroxide with the addition of carboxylic acids or relevant salts, under certain conditions with respect to reaction time, volume ratio and concentration of hydroxide suspensions.The 3-hydroxy-2,2,4-trimethylpentylisobutyrate produced by the process of the invention is a valuable starting material for the production of dyes, pesticides, adhesives and varnishes.
