<TITLE>
a membrane securing device.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
a securing device retains a membrane in an electrolytic  cell during assembly and disassembly and allows safe,  effective storage during disassembly of the cell.  the device  comprises a hollow, cylindrical member (1) having a slit  (2) in which the membrane (4) is held.  a cap (3) is provided  at each end of the member (1) to restrict movement of  the membrane laterally within the slit (2).
