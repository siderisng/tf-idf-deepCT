The drawing device for the draw comprises a tubular body (1) of inside diameter greater than that of said articles (c), disposed vertically, means for bringing the articles one to one above the body and directing them according to the axis.of the body, a shutter means (7) to stop an article in a given position, means (5) for maintaining the article in said position by closing the body, actuated when the shutter means (7) is released, a sealing means (9) placed downstream of the shutter means, and an orifice formed in the body between the closure means and the sealing means and connected to a source ofAir under depression (11).The combined measuring apparatus comprises several measuring positions and means of transfer to pass the cigarettes into the different measuring stations.
