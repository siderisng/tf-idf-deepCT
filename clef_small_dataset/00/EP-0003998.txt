<ABSTRACT>
A machine with a circumferential welding tool 16 for machining a material web 10 from thermoplastic plastic supplied to the welded tool 16 with constant speed.The welding tool 16 is totally totally totred over a controllable ballast gear 41 with non-uniform rotary speed that it has the smallest circulation speed during welding.For the driving of the material web 10, a transmission 33 is provided with infinitely adjustable gear ratio to accommodate the speed of the material web 10 always the smallest peripheral speed of the welding tool 16.As a result, the welding can be carried out by changing the unevenness of the circulation speed of the welding tool 16 at different distances from each other without changing the speed of the drive shaft 32 of the ballast 41.
