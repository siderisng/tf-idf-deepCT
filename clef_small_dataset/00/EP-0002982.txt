<ABSTRACT>
Conical sweeping antenna system at the reception of the Cassegrain type comprising a parabolic main reflector (1) and two hyperbolic auxiliary reflectors (3, 4) with a focus of one in a focal plane on the other.One of these reflectors (3) is semi-transparent and the other (4) is animated by a rotational movement about an axis (54) inclined with respect to the focal axis (OX) of the system.Application to prosecution radar.
