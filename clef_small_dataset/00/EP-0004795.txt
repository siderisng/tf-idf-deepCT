<ABSTRACT>
vinyl chloride resin compositions containing plasticizer  have poor creep characteristics and are inferior to rubber in  recovery from stress, and cannot be used where small  compression permanent set is required.  the invention provides a method for producing a vinyl  chloride resin comprising 10 to 80 weight % of tetrahydrofuran-insoluble  vinyl chloride resin gel fraction, the balance  being tetrahydrofuran-soluble fraction with an average  polymerisation degree of 1,000 or more, wherein vinyl  chloride or a mixture thereof with a minor amount of at least  one monomer copolymerizable therewith is polymerized at  0° to 50°c in the presence of at least one polyfunctional  monomer having two or more ethylenic double bonds per  molecule until at least 60 weight % of the total monomer is  polymerized.  the resulting vinyl chloride resin has good  processability and is capable of forming a molded article,  e. g.  for packing, having small compression permanent set.  
</ABSTRACT>
