<TITLE>
flameproof styrene polymers and their use.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>
basf agde<sep>basf aktiengesellschaft<sep>basf aktiengesellschaftcarl-bosch-strasse 3867063 ludwigshafende<sep>
</APPLICANT>
<INVENTOR>
hahn klaus drde<sep>naarmann herbert drde<sep>hahn, klaus, dr.<sep>naarmann, herbert, dr.<sep>hahn, klaus, dr.burggasse 2d-6840 lampertheimde<sep>naarmann, herbert, dr.haardtblickd-6719 wattenheimde<sep>
</INVENTOR>
<ABSTRACT>
flameproof, thermoplastic moulding compounds based on styrene polymers which contain, as flameproofing agent, an organic, heterocyclic bromine compound containing the <image> group and, if desired, a blowing agent.  styrene polymers are prepared by polymerisation of styrene in an aqueous suspension in the presence of the bromine compound, the polymerisation initiator used being an organic azo compound having a half life period at 70 deg c of between 2 and 60 minutes.  the moulding compounds can be converted into mouldings, profiles or foams. 
</ABSTRACT>
