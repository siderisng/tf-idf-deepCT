In this amplitude control means means (2 to 8) are provided which increases the transmission factor in each case to the appearance of a QRS complex around a step when the amplitude of a pulse contained in the QRS complex at the output (9) of the control device between a firstand a second predetermined threshold lies and the transmission factor is reduced by one step when the amplitude of the peak value exceeds a third predetermined threshold higher than the first and second threshold.
