<ABSTRACT>
a vapor tap valve for aerosol containers is provided, for  use with flammable propellants, inhibiting delivery of  flammable liquid propellant when the valve is opened  immediately after shaking the aerosol container.  the vapor  tap valve comprises a movable delivery valve including a  valve stem (11), a delivery port (14), and a valve chamber (5);  means (spring 18) biasing the delivery valve towards a  closed position; a liquid tap orifice (5a) and a vapor tap  orifice (2) in flow communication with the valve chamber (5);  and a shut-off valve (8b) linked to the delivery valve, closing  off the vapor tap orifice (2) against entry of liquefied propellant  therethrough into the valve chamber (5), when the delivery  valve is closed, and opening the vapor tap orifice (2)  when the delivery valve is manually moved to the open position,  against the biasing force of the bias means (spring 18).
