<ABSTRACT>
in a closed receptacle (1) with built-in chambers (7 and 9) and tubes (8), a storage medium (5), for the storage and/or production of heat or cold, is accommodated.  the expansion members (6) take up the changes in volume of the storage medium (5) on heating or cooling.  by means of building in these expansion members (6), the storage medium (5) is separated from the air and separate expansion receptacles with the tubes leading to them are rendered superfluous.  <image>
</ABSTRACT>
