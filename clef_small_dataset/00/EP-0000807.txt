<ABSTRACT>
an electrical snap acting switch is calibrated by deforming  an unsupported part of a terminal strip (3) which carries a  fixed contact (12) after assembly of the switch device, to predetermine  the operating point of the switch contacts.  deformation of the fixed contact support strip (3) is facilitated  by apertures (17, 18) in the strip on opposite sides of the  fixed contact (12).  these apertures also serve to ventilate the  make and break zone of the contacts to assist in ozone dispersal  from this zone.
