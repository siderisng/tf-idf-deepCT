<ABSTRACT>
a composition is disclosed containing a polyester,  polyamide, or polyalkene and a silicone polymer.  a synthetic napped material with improved qualities is  also disclosed, in which the naps are bundles of superfine  fibres (1), bound by an organic silicone polymer (5).  the  material is made with a yarn containing the superfine fibres.   it is then treated with a shape-fixing agent (4), and if necessary  it is subjected to a process to free the superfine fibres in  the yarn e. g.  by dissolving the sea component (2) for yarn of  island-in-the-sea type fibres.  it is next treated with a curable  organic silicone polymer (7) which is at least partially cured  before the shape-fixing agent (4) is finally removed.  the  material may then be subjected to dyeing and finishing processes  as appropriate.  
</ABSTRACT>
