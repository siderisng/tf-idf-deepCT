<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a new type of fragmentable loads for small and medium weapons calibers.The method of manufacturing fragmentable nitrocellulose propellant loading according to the invention is characterized in that, in a conventional method of manufacturing propulsive powder with nitrocellulose with solvent, a smoothing operation is carried out during which one is carried out.Spraying on the powder grains A smoothing solution containing at least polyvinyl nitrate and a stabilizer, and in that, after evaporation of the solvent of said solution, comprising between about 80 and 120 ° C the grains of powder thus obtained.The invention also relates to the fragmentable loads obtained using this method.
