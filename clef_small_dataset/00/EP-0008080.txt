<ABSTRACT>
Average to oxidative coloring of hair with a content of at least one derivative of the 4, 2-methylenedioxy-benzene of the general formula in the R is an OH, NH₂, NHR 1 or NR¹R² group wherein R¹ and R² independently of each other is an alkyl- Position hydro xyalkyl or acyl radical with 1 to 4 carbon atoms, as couplers substance.The coupler substance is contained in these agents in a concentration of about 0.01 to 3.0 percent by weight, namely in admixture with conventional developer and optional couplers substances.In particular, through a mixture of 4-oxy-1,2-methylenedioxybenzene with the developing leric substance P-Aminophenol, there is the possibility of producing dark natural tones.
