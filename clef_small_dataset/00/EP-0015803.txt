<ABSTRACT>
Two mutual-fitting organs respectively male and female jointly mounted one on the pedal and the other on the shoe, are equipped with winding means capable of being implemented by simple respective mesh of the shoe compared to thepedal.According to the invention, this device is characterized in that the male piece is cylindrical core (4) and is disposed on the pedal (2) and in that the female piece consists of a bearing (5) ofFull formal form of said core (4) and is disposed in the thickness of the sole (7) of the shoe (1).
