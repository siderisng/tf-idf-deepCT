<ABSTRACT>
The application relates to a milking hose (1) for a quarter-label machine, in which at least four parallel to each other, together, held hone lines (2,3,4,5) are provided.The hose lines (2,3,4,5) are arranged such that their central longitudinal axes are substantially at the corners of a rhombus.The cohesion of the hose lines (2,3,4,5) is ensured by the fact that the facing outer surfaces of the tubing lines (2,3,4,5) are connected to each other via their entire length such that each between three hose lines a separate gap(11,12) is formed, which is suitable for the passage of a pulsation medium.
