<ABSTRACT>
For the production of ethylene oxide by oxidation of ethylene with oxygen or air, silver carrier catalysts are used, which should have the highest possible effectiveness.A method of improving the efficacy of already inserted silver carrier catalysts is described, in which one washes the used catalyst first with an inert liquid and then on the washed catalyst 1 to 1000 parts per 1 million parts of catalyst cesium, rubidium or a mixture thereofBring.The application is usually carried out by means of an impregnating solution containing cesium and / or rubidium compounds in an appropriate amount.With this method, it is possible to significantly improve silver carrier catalysts, which were already in use for the oxidation of ethylene with oxygen or air, in their effectiveness.
