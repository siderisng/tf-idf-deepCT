<ABSTRACT>
it is known that commercial acrylic paints have the defect  of having an insufficient pigment dispersibilty.  the present  invention provides modified acrylic copolymers having  improved pigment dispersibility, which are suitable as a car­ rier.  the modified acrylic copolymers may be obtained by  subjecting to polymerization double-bond containing unsatu­ rated polyesters, dialkylaminoalkyl (metha) acrylates,  methylmethaacrylates and other polymerizable monomers  respectively in predetermined weight ratio.  the products are  characterized in that the ratio of the weight average molecular  weight to the number average molecular weight is within the  range of 3 to 20.
