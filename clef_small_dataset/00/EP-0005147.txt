<ABSTRACT>
A device is proposed, which serves to wipe motor vehicle washers.The wiper device comprises a drive unit at the circumferential driven output shaft a crank is attached.On the motor vehicle disk to be wiped is a wiper blade which is connected via a wiper arm with a wiper shaft.The wiper shaft is provided with a rocker and the crank is connected via a push rod articulated with the rocker.The crank forms together with the push rod and the rocker a pendulum gear, via which the wiper blade is offset in a pendulum movement.The wiper device is equipped with means which allow pivoting of the push rod around its oscillating joint and thus a sponge of the crank even when the wiper is blocked behind the wiper shaft.
</ABSTRACT>
