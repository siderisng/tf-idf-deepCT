<ABSTRACT>
this invention relates to a swinging bucket centrifuge  rotor in which each bucket is supported by an independent  bucket hanger member (18) disposed within a cavity in the  rotor.  each hanger member (18) is spring biased in a radial  direction toward the axis of rotation, and is supported by a  guide sleeve (21) with fastening means extending through  the guide sleeve (21).  the guide sleeve (21) prevents rotation  of the bucket and hanger member (18) with respect to the  rotor body (12a).
