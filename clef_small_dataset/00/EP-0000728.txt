<ABSTRACT>
Method for removing volatiles that escape from the extrusion of plastic melts from these, wherein the volatile portions are driven immediately upon exit from the plastic melt with the surrounding air and the air containing such volatile content heated immediately after entry into the suction opening, as well as aDevice, consisting of a conical inlet 1 for the plastic melt with a heating jacket 2 of a nozzle plate 3 attached to the tip of the cone with at least one nozzle opening 4 for the outlet of the plastic melt and at least one suction opening 5, wherein the sheath 6 together with the nozzle plate 3 andThe heating jacket 2 form a heated cavity 7, which has an outlet 8.
