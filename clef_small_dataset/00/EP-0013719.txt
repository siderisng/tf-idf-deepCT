<ABSTRACT>
Languette type printing wheel structure Radial latches designed to be housed and orushed in a cartridge during storage and use in a printer.The print wheel (2) comprises a hub (26), in the form of a cup, provided with a peripheral edge (55) from the end of which the radial tabs (27) carry (27) carrying characters (28).The hub (26) has a orientation element (29), asymmetric shape, intended to accommodate in a corresponding opening (7) of a cartridge (1), in order to block the print wheel (2)in a predetermined angular position.The orientation element (29) has a depression (30) for lightening the print wheel (2), as well as a depression (43) with an opening for the rotation drive of the wheel D'Print (2).
