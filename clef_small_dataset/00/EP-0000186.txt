<ABSTRACT>
The subject of the present invention are mixtures of 60 wt.-% to 5 wt.-% of Armoatic polycarbonates with MW between 60.000 and 120,000 (component A) and 40 wt.-% to 95 wt.-% of aromatic polycarbonates with MW between 28,000 and 40,000 (component B) and traveling for their preparation and their use for the production of molded bodies.
