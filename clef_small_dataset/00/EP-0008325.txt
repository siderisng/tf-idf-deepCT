<ABSTRACT>
In a drive for cylinders of offset printing machines with a gear train, the drive wheels arranged on the axes of the cylinders are formed as obliquely toothed head teeth wheels.For synchronizing the rotation of the cylinders, clamping devices are provided, which circulating with the respective associated head gear (31) and whose tooth ring depending on the setting more or less deform.Coaxial to each head gear a clamping writing (32) is provided, on which two or more in the axial direction on the zankranz of the associated head gears are mounted tensioning devices (36).
</ABSTRACT>
