<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a message transmission system in which a digital network is superimposed on the conventional analog telecommunications network whose digi-tale terminations are connected to digital subscriber stations.Both networks are connected between the analog and digital terminations via analog digital and digital analog converters.According to the invention, in each digital subscriber station by automatic sub-channel formation by means of a multiplexer (1) and a demultiplexer (10), a data transmission is possible simultaneously.An order for data-clock recognition (7) determines which Bi Translate outputs the attached data terminal and causes that with a bit rate to be entabled to the voice channel, a reasonable sub-channel for the data is set up.
