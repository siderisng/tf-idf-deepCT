<ABSTRACT>
- Detection of magnetic or electrical fields using field effect semiconductor detectors.- the operation of the detectors according to the invention is based on the modulation of the limits of the depletion regions delimiting the conduction channel (4) which connects a source (5) to drains (6) formed in a semiconductor substrate (1).An increased output signal is achieved by reducing the width (W) of the channel (4), in order to eliminate excess load carriers that are normally found in wide channel devices and providing for depletion areasconstituting a significant fraction of the width (W).The ratio between non-impoverished width (WV) and width (W) is selected from 0 to 0.98.- Detectors applicable especially when reading magnetic tapes.
