<ABSTRACT>
Cables using optical fibers to the transmission of information.Cable element consisting of a rigid core coated with a dielectric coating in which helicoidal grooves each housing an optical fiber subjected to a twist between three and ten towers at the time of its installation, at least one ribbon beingarranged around the coating.The cable is obtained by assembling the elements with a piece of assembly between two and five times the step of the helical grooves.Cable for telecommunication.
