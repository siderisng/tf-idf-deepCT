<ABSTRACT>
A setup for holding a vacuum cassette for X-ray film on an X-ray recording apparatus, in particular special for mammography recordings, should ensure an unanswered seat of a cassette even with vertical use.For this purpose, a support plate (1) is fixedly attached to the motion plate (1) on which on two opposite sides in one of the KA settlements corresponding distance resilient retaining bracket (6) are provided.Thus, the cassette can be inserted from the BE employer with one hand and can be removed again and can also - based on the patient - by the side in the device which is inserted.
