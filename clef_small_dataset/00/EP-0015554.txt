<ABSTRACT>
a comparator circuit comprises a differential amplifier  (1) having a pair of inputs (2′, 3′) and a switch circuit (21)  arranged between the pair of inputs (2′, 3′), wherein the  switch circuit (21) is made conductive state prior to the  comparison operation to delete potential difference be­ tween the pair of inputs (2′, 3′).  the comparator circuit  which can be easily realized in the form of an integrated  circuit device can achieve a high speed operation even for  signals fed from a high impedance signal source to the  stray capacitance found at the comparator input.  the com­ parator circuit is suitably applied to an ad/converter cir­ cuit especially of sequential comparison type.  
</ABSTRACT>
