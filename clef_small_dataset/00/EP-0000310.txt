<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the distribution in the open air, in an amount assayed and automatically, from the liquid phase of a cryogenic fluid.The device comprises a retaining capacity (10) of the liquid phase, a control system (20) of the supply of said capacity, dosing means (30), forced evacuation (40), distribution (50) Cryogenic liquid and a control system (60) of said dosing member and said evacuation means.The invention applies, in particular, to the casting of the metals, to the manufacture of parts formed of different metals and the molding of parts in series.
