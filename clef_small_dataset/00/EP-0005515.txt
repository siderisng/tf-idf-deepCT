<ABSTRACT>
in a device for pulling out formwork panels from earth shafts, a crosspiece (4) reaching transversely across the earth shaft is provided.  the crosspiece can be moved in the vertical by means of pistons actuated by a pressure medium and, approximately at a distance of the width of the earth shaft, has supports (5) for the detachably fixed attachment of pulling ropes (8).
