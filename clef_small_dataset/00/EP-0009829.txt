<TITLE>
scan rate converter utilizing discrete differentially coded signals.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
a scan rate converter utilizing a discrete differential cod­ ing system is disclosed.  the scan rate converter converts  input fstv and sstv video information into discrete diffe­ rentially coded signals and stores these signals in a high  speed memory (158).  the stored video information is read  out of the high speed memory at both a fast scan and a slow  scan rate and is reconverted into respective fstv ans sstv  output signals.
