<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a light collecting device with a so-called "fluorescence plate", which consists of a solid polymerized plastic support, contains fluorescent particles and provided with at least one light exit kennel. In order to turn off the disturbing self-absorption in the fluorescent plate, which results from a partial overlap of the emission spectrum with the absorption spectrum of the fluorescent material, the following fluorescent body composition is proposed: the fluorescent particles have finite dipole moments with different values ​​in the grud state and excited state; The plastic carrier includes a polar organic additive which provides a high orientation polarization environment around the fluorescent particles; These environments can meet such rapidly umo that they can achieve virtually completely their thermodynamic equilibrium during the existence of the excitation. The addition can be dissolved or chemically bound in the tra-matrix. The present device is mainly suitable as a solar conductor, for optical transmission of messages or image brightening of passive displays
