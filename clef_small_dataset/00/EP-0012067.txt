<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a cleaning of the liquid surfaces which comprises a floating material recovery apparatus whose mouth is framed by the two wings of a towed floating dam.According to the invention the tractor means (10) carries a transverse coupling means (12) to the direction of advancement and provided with three attachment points, one (14) in the middle for a towing trailer (24)Moored at the mouth (19) of the recovery apparatus (21) and one (13a, 13b) at each of its ends for the end of the corresponding wing of the dam, each of said wings consisting of a floating rope(11a, 11b) of good resistance and weakly tense to follow the vertical movements of the liquid surface.This device is particularly effective in the fight against "black tides".
