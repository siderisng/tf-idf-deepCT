<ABSTRACT>
The invention is a sailboat mast, which consists of two mast parts (10, 12) reaching from the boat body to the mast tip (10, 12) whose front (10) has a rear, symmetrically designed to the center longitudinal plane (14) of the boat shaped hollow lug (18)and clamped by Wanten and Stage on the boat body and whose rear part (12) is unavoided, the sail (20) carries and supported with its upper end at the front part (10).According to one embodiment of the invention, a pocket (22) formed by the sail (20) surrounds the rear mast part (12).According to another embodiment of the invention, the rear mast part at which the sail is struck with its preliek (26) to rotate its longitudinal axis.
