<ABSTRACT>
For measuring the heat flow and mass flow in a fluid conveying system, a device is used, which is each of one in the feed (1,2) and a flow line to be inserted in the return line (3, 4), from a heat output line (5) betweenconsists of these two lines and from temperature sensors (11,12,13,14).The alimator circuit (5) is made of a material with low thermal conductivity, e.g.b.Glass or composite material, such as enamel or plastic-coated steel.It is designed such that the heat passage through the heat side track is lower than the heat transfer to the two interfaces between the fluid and the heat side track.At or near the ends of the two heat transfer zones in the feed and return, temperatures or temperature differences of the fluid are measured and determined therefrom the heat flow or mass flow.
</ABSTRACT>
