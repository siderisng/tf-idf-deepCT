<ABSTRACT>
a belt winch (1) for use in holding load-holding belts  (19) on vehicles (15) includes a frame (2) having vertical  legs (3) and (4) between which a belt winch roller (5) is  rotatably located.  a pawl and spigot (8) is included.  the  frame (2) has connected to it z shaped tongues (10, 11) each  having vertical portions (13) and (17) spaced by a horizontal  portion (16).  this enables the winch (1) to be hooked around  a tie rail (14) of a vehicle (15) with the portions (17) engaging  the tray of the vehicle (15) and an upward load on a belt (19)  connected to the winch (1) holds the winch (1) in place.
