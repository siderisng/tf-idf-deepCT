<ABSTRACT>
the invention discloses diagnostic compositions for use  in obtaining images of a patient's lungs.  the basic components  of the composition of the invention are sodium pertechnetate  which is radioactive and ethanol.  this composition  may be combusted and the resulting products cooled or  alternatively the composition may be inserted into a pressure  vessel with an aerosol.  in both cases a gas like mixture  results.  the patient is able to breath the mixture of the invention  in a normal way.
