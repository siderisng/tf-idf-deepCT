<ABSTRACT>
the automatic printer is able to produce any number of prints from the originals contained in a given order, and a process is provided for determining the exposure times.  the printer comprises an analysis station determining constant exposure times when the originals exhibit a density outside a density range d1, d2 and exposure times whose logarithms increase linearly between these limits.  advantageously, it is possible to vary the gradient of the straight lines representing the curve log t = f(d).  application to the construction of a photographic printer for slides.    <image>
