<ABSTRACT>
Waterproof crossing device of a single or double plastic wall by an electrical connection through an orifice of the wall, comprising a first annular seal sealing the annular region of the orifice left free by the connection, and compressed.axially.According to the invention, said axial compression is carried out by clamping means (11, 12, 14, 15) applying on either side of the wall (1) with two metal pieces (9, 10), presentingEach one face substantially parulted at said wall (1), the distance between said faces, and thus the compression of the seal (8), being defined by the presence of an interlayer metal element (5).Use: batteries of accumulators using hard metal connection parts and melting at high temperature, for example alkaline electrolyte batteries.
