<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a connector (1) for the construction of hollow tube frame racks (7) for the electrical message technology, with at least two necks (2) attached to a node point (5), which are versed with adhesives and which in the hollow pipes ( 7) be introduced and glued with them. Such connectors have adhesive gills on their surface, which should ensure the distribution of the adhesive. The inclusion of air bubbles during the adhesive process should be avoided. For this purpose, the invention provides an adhesive regulator, which consist of at least one rotary groove (3) circumferential on the surface of the nozzle (2), which leads from rod crete (4) paths. In addition, a tightly-tolerant perforated disc (6) can be pushed onto the nozzles to prevent consideration and thereby ensure further improvement of adhesive distribution. A compatible connector according to the invention is particularly suitable for use in hollow pipe frame racks for data processing systems.
