<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for producing 1-amino-8-naphthol-4,6-disulfonic acid (K-acid) as a mono alkali salt of naphthylamine-trisulfonic acid isomeric mixtures by alkaline pressure hydrolysis of naphthylamine trisulfonic acid mixtures and subsequent insulation of the K acid and Hacid by acidifying and crystallization.
