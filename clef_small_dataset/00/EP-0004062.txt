aqueous plastics dispersion based on vinyl polymers, process for its preparation and its use.

<ABSTRACT>
Aqueous plastic dispersions based on vinyl polymers are usually prepared by polymerizing the corresponding monomeric compounds in aqueous medium in the presence of radical-forming initiators and emulsifiers.The use of dispersing aids is useful.As a dispersing aid, especially water-soluble salts of multiba-like phosphonic acid derivatives, in particular alkanephosphonic acids and phosphonomethyl amines, are used.The salts are preferably alkali metal or ammonium salts.The plastic dispersions thus prepared are suitable as a coating agent and as means for impregnating, coating or bonding solid materials.
