<ABSTRACT>
Dynamic positioning device of a mobile by a magnetic field in an oxy plan.The mobile has one or more magnetometer (M₁₀₀), as well as a device (10) for identifying the angle O, what does an O'xy mobile mark with the OXY benchmark.The magnetometer (M₁₀₀) measuring the components of the HX and HY magnetic field in the O'xy reference, a circuit (20. 1) calculates the components of this HX and HY field in the fixed mark and the circuits (40 and (41)determine the position of the mobile. A circuit (30. 1) develops the motor motor control signals (90) from the values of HX and HY and XP and YP values from the position in memory. Application to theDynamic anchoring of a boat near a drilling platform on the high seas.
