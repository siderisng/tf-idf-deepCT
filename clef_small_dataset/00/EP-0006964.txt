<ABSTRACT>
the subject of the invention is a fish bait which possesses the property of floating horizontally in the water while the fishing line is held still.  the fish bait consists of three individual parts, a transparent fish-shaped sleeve, a folded, coloured and relief-embossed foil inserted therein, and a wire serving as a balance arm, which is pushed into the fish sleeve at one end and possesses at the other end a counter-weight equivalent to the weight of the sleeve with the foil.  the axis of the balance arm is the point of attachment for the fishing line.  in order to lure different fish, the foil can either be exchanged in the sleeve for another of a different colour or can be drawn through the water on its own, attached to a fishing line.  the wire balance arm can likewise be used alone, in which case any desired alternative artificial or natural dead bait can be pushed on to its free end.  <image>
