<ABSTRACT>
To align flat thin workpieces, z.b.Plates, film elements and the like. With respect to a reference surface (81), a method and apparatus for which the workpiece (212) is first brought into a layer parallel to the reference surface (81) and low distance to it.In this position, the workpiece (212) is stored on a gas pressure film "floating", and then straightening elements are effective, such.b.From the reference surface (81) extendable pencils (84), which are introduced into corresponding holes in the workpiece (212).The workpiece (212) is then placed on the reference surface (81) by shutdown of the gas pressure in the aligned position and the straightening elements are retracted.
