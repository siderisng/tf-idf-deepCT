<ABSTRACT>
in still image projectors it is possible to vary automatically both the stationary period and also, in the case of projectors provided with a dissolve facility, the dissolve period of the slides to be projected.  contacts (20) are fitted to the magazine guide (12), which contacts are short-circuited by short-circuit parts (21) on a magazine wall (31).  the stationary period and the dissolve period respectively are determined from the value of the resistance of the resistors belonging to these short-circuit parts (21) in the interior of the projector.
