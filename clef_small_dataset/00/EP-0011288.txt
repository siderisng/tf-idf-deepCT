P-tert.-Butylbenzotribromide and its at the core by halogen substituted derivatives of the formula I and p-tert.-Butylbozylbromide and its at the core by halogen substituted derivatives of formula II: wherein X = H, F, CL, BR, J, preferably H, F, CL, BR, in particular H.p.tert.Butylbenzotribromide and its at the core by halogen substituted derivatives are prepared by bromination of p.tert.-Butyltoluene and its at the core by halogen substituted derivatives under side chains of halogenating conditions.By partial saponification of the benzotribromide, the corresponding benzoyl bromide is obtained.
