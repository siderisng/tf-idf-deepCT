<ABSTRACT>
An induction welding installation for the manufacture of tubes comprises an induction coil (18) surrounding a tube blank (7) of the forging rollers (23) arranged downstream of the induction coil (18) and a concentrator(78) Internal disposed inside the tube blank (7).The invention is characterized in that the internal concentrator (78) comprises a stacking of magnetic sheets oriented parallel to the diameter of the tube blank (7) passing through a slot (10) of the blank, and of larger length.that the distance between the upstream end of the induction coil (18) and the axes of the forging rollers (23).
