<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to substituted 2-phenylamino-imidazoline (2) of the general formula I wherein Ar the residue 2,6-dichlorophenyl, 2,5-dichlorophenyl, 2 chloro-6-methylphenyl, 2-chloro-4-methylphenyl, 2,6-di chloro-4-bromophenyl, 4-cyanophenyl, 2,5-dimethoxyphenyl or 2-methyl-5-fluorophenyl and thereof addition salts.The new compounds are usable as cardiac or coronar therapeutics.
