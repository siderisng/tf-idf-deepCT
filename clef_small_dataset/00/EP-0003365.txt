<ABSTRACT>
For the manufacture of a planar or in a direction curved phosphor screen for color screens or color image display devices, a double mask raster coating method is proposed, in which the phosphors for the individual colors are separated by each one of the respective paint surfaces coating mask (3, 4, 5) and by one for allColors used base mask (2), for example, by spraying, are applied to a shield glass (1).
