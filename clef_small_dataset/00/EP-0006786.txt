<ABSTRACT>
Device for drilling a staggered hole of a pile or the like. According to the inventon, this device comprises a support frame carrying a sleeve (8) adapted to be placed relative to the frame for applying a collar (9) to support against the surface of the soil, a mandrel (16) drilling Axial, full and vertical, attached to the end of the rod of a first cylinder (12), a coaxial hollow neck (25) with a reduced height, this socket comprises ning an edge (26) expected. To cooperate with a shoulder carried by the axial mandrel at one point of its length such that this shoulder entails the socket only at the end of the tread of the mandrel, this socket then penetrated into the ground to form, at the upper end. From the hole to a recess and means for making, after simultaneous removal of the mandrel and the socket, the extraction of the planted soil portion between the sleeve and the MAN DRIN. Application to the setting up of vine stakes.
