<ABSTRACT>
The object of the invention is to ensure the pre-cis positioning, in an hermetic device, the terminal face of an optical fiber and the active face of an opto-electronic component.The fiber being immobilized in the axial recess (110) of a fiber holder (1), a block system (21, 22) provides a support plane to the support (3) of an opto-electronic component (4).The plate (12) serves as a cap at a cap (not visible, FIG. 2) with sealed travers for the electrical connection of the component, and mechanically undeed during component and fiber.Application to the emission heads, receiving or possibly at the mixed heads, alternately emitting and receiving according to the polarity of the supply voltage.
