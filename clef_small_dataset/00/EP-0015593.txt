<ABSTRACT>
Process for the preparation of storage-stable urethane acrylic from cycloaliphatic diisocyanates, in particular isophorone diisocyanate, hydroxyalkyl acrylates and bi-, tri-saturated polyols in the presence of reactive diluents and organic tin catalysts in two stages, in which diisocyanate in the first stagewith hydroxyalkylacrylic aten in the equivalent ratio NCO: OH of 1: 1 in the Tempera Turge range of 0-50 ° C is reacted and in the second stage the reaction of the NCO-containing acrylurethane formed in the first stage with multi-functional polyols or mixtures in the equivalent ratioNCO: OH of 1: 1 in the temperature range of z.b.70 ° C, with organic tin catalysts to practically NCO-free urethane acrylics.
</ABSTRACT>
