<ABSTRACT>
The invention is part of the domain of thermal regulation devices.The device of the invention consists of a temperature measuring circuit (a) comprising a resistance bridge, some (R4) are sensitive to the temperature, attacking a differential amplifier Z, and a heating circuit (b), whose calorigenic elements are a chain of transistors (t₁)...(tn) mounted in voltage stabilizers.A circuit (C) ensures a limitation of the maximum current per share based on the TC control transistor.The applications concern, in particular, the control of the temperature of the oscillating piezoelectric crystals.
