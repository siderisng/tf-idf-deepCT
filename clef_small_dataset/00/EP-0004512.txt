<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to new muramy-peptide esters.The compounds according to the invention have the chemical structure of a wall-peptide, the peptide chain containing at least two aminoacyl residues, the second residue being a glutaminyl residue.These compounds are characterized by the presence, on the γ position of the glutinyl residue, a hydrocarbon chain containing at least four carbons.These compounds are useful as immunoregulatory substances in pharmaceutical compositions or as laboratory reagents.
