<ABSTRACT>
A device for setting up a false ingot (1) for the preparation of a new casting in a continuous casting installation, comprises a basket (2 or 100) in which the false ingot (1) comes to lodge. that one separates from the casting line, a handling system for bringing the basket (2 or 100) to the position above the ingothener (33), and means for introducing the false ingot (1) from the top in said lingot. To improve the transport conditions of the false ingot (1) in its basket (2 or 100), the Manu Tention system comprises a lifting chassis (12) to which are attached to the lifting means, the basket (2 or 100) being mounted on said chassis, and a translation frame (22) whose lifting means are integral, said chassis comprising two vertical slides (24) capable to see the lifting chassis (12) and means controlling the movement of the chassis of Translation (22) along a horizontal path (23).
