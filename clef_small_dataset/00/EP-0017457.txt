<ABSTRACT>
in producing synthetic hydrocarbon oils by polymeriza­ tion of olefins using aliminum halide as the catalyst, organic  halides are produced.  these are corrosive to metal equip­ ment and are poisonous to certain hydrogenation catalysts.   this invention eliminates the problems caused by such  organic halides by reacting them with an aromatic hydrocar­ bon in a system also containing the polyolefins thus forming  an alkylation product with both reactants prior to removal of  the aluminum chloride catalyst.
