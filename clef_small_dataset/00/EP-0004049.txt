<ABSTRACT>
Potassium sorbate granulate is prepared continuously by placing dry potassium sorbate in a chamber (1) on a hole floor (3), swirls with heated air to a fluidized bed (4) and an aqueous potassium sorbate solution sprayed by nozzles (9) in or on this fluidized bed.The amount of potassium sorbate solution sprayed in the time unit is set so that the temperature of the fluidized bed 40 - 80 ° C and the relative humidity of the fluidized bed-leaving air at most 20 wt.-%, based on 60 ° C, is.The granules produced in the chamber (1) is continuously discharged via a tube (12) and classified in a sieve device (14).The granules of the desired grain size are removed (15).The too rough grain fraction is crushed in a mill (18) and returned to the combustion with the to fine grain fraction in the chamber (1).
