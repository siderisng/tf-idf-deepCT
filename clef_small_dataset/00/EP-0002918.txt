<ABSTRACT>
a process for preventing the cracking of ceramic bodies  during a hot pressing operation comprises applying a  relatively thin layer of a release material to surfaces of the  ceramic body which are to be load reaction surfaces during  the hot pressing operation.  a relatively thick layer of the  release material is applied to surfaces of the ceramic body  which are not to be load reaction surfaces during the hot  pressing operation.
