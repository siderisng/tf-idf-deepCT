<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for producing aqueous solutions of alkali organosiliconates, wherein organoalkoxypolysiloxanes of the formula wherein R¹ is an alkyl radical having 1 to 3 carbon atoms, a vinyl radical and / or phenyl radical, a alkyl radical having 1 to 4 C atoms, a a number ≦ 2and X is 1 to 1.25, with aqueous solutions of NaOH or KOH of a concentration of 0.7 to 0.9 [mol x 100 g⁻¹] with temperatures ≧ 80 ° C., optionally distilled off the releasing alcohol R²OH,whereby the addition of water can be set the desired concentration.In the process according to the invention, alkali-lorry-free alkali organosiliconate solutions, in particular high concentration, are obtained by means of avoiding filtration.
