<ABSTRACT>
a method and appartus to collect and store a sample  of an environmental gas, e. g.  air.  a sample bottle (10) is  cooled below the condensation temperature of the environ­ mental gas so that a partial vacuum is created in the con­ tainer thus permitting environmental gas to be drawn into  the bottle (10) and condensed.  included in the apparatus  are means (200) to meter the quantity of environmental  gas admitted to the sample bottle (10) at a constant rate.
