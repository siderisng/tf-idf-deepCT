<ABSTRACT>
The device comprises a swimming out of a plurality of preferably spherical swimming bodies with eccentric priorities, on the surface of the beer wort or the young beer or beer swimming de layer.These floats retain a stable floating location as a result of its eccentric focus and on these floating bodies, both the flotation foam forming during the flotation of the beer wort is stored as well as the foyer forming during fermentation and feedback of the wort or the young beer.In addition, this layer reduces the harmful contact of the liquid with the oxygen content in the airspace of the wort holder.
