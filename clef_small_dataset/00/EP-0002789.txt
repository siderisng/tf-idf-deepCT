<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a sealing body for introducing cables in cable sets.It consists of lamellar-shaped, axial direction series sealing elements (2), in which cable entry openings are cut as needed.This results in annular cavities between the individual sealing elements (2) in which semicircular slices (4) are used.The cavities are thereby filled in a large part and in this way reinforce the sealing elements (2), so that the use in mechanically heavily stressed cables is possible without dispensing with the advantages of the lamella structure of the sealing body.
