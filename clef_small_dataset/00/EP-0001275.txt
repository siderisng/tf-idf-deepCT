<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for producing sulfonic acid chlorides by reacting an aromatic compound with chlorosulfonic acid and thionyl chloride, in which one first brings about stoichiometric amounts of the aromatic compound and chlorosulfonic acid, optionally in the presence of a solvent and optionally in the presence of a sulfonation aid, and thenreactive with excess thionyl chloride.
