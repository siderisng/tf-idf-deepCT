<ABSTRACT>
the invention concerns the production of crosslinked  polyurethane, obtained by reacting one or more polyols and  at least one trifunctional crosslinking hydroxyl compound  with a polyisocyanate.  products of improved quality, especially  strength and heat resistance, are obtained by using  tris(Î²-hydroxypropyl)isocyanurate or its ethoxylated derivative  as crosslinking compound and at least one polymeric  polyol, with optionally also a low molecular weight diol, as  the other hydroxy compounds.  elastomers as well as foams  can be prepared.  the process can be carried out by reacting  all components together or by prereacting the polyisocyanate  with part of the hydroxyl compounds and next adding the  remainder.
