<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an X-ray examination device with a narrow base frame (100) and a sliding bar with the base frame (100) connected patients storage plate (27), in particular for the preparation of bucky recordings.The device has the advantage that it is largely accessible from the side without having to be provided on the wall or on the ceiling guide rails for the X-ray radiator (26) carrying tripod (16).For this purpose, a column stand (16) carrying the X-ray tube (26) is transported along a guide rail (10), which in turn movable in a table longitudinal direction to a WA gene (9), which in turn in the table longitudinal direction on a stationary frame (1) is movable.This results in a telescopic movement of the movable parts (9, 10, 16) and a displacement area, which is larger than the width of the base frame (100) and the frame (1).
