<ABSTRACT>
1.  electric switch with at least one straight inner switch partner (4) and an also straight outer switch partner, coaxially arranged to the inner switch partner and separated by tube-shaped, coaxial insulating zones, whereby each insulating zone (5) consists of a gaseous or liquid medium and, in relation to this, short insulating pieces (6), in the manner shown, that the switch partners, by a mechanical initial tension, relative to each other, can only take up two stable states ; in the unaffected state of the switch arrangement, they take up a straight course without giving contact ; when the switches are arranged in an arcuated way, the inner switch partner will bend outwards until contact is given.
