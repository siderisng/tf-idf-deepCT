<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to homogeneous, thermoplastic mixtures of a) 85 - 99.5 wt.-% Propylene ethylene copolymer with 0.5 - 15 wt.-% copolymerized ethylene nomits and with a melt index of 1.0 to 30 g / 10 min. at 230 ° C and a load of 5 kg and b) 0. 5 - 15 wt.% Liquid, saturated diorganopolysiioses with a viscosity of 30,000 to 80,000 cSt at 20 ° C.The mixtures are considered ascnders for the preparation of polypropylene-based moldings, since the elastic properties, such as permanent stretching and tear at break, are greatly improved compared to the reinn polypropylene copolymer.
