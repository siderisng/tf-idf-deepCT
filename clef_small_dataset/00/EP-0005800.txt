<ABSTRACT>
a riveting tool for riveting blind rivets having adjustable rivet stroke, consisting of a tool housing (1) with housing head (2) and formed-on lever arm (3), and a mouthpiece (5) screwed into the housing head, and a gripping and pulling part (8) displaceably mounted in the housing head, and a second lever arm (27) pivotably mounted in the housing and connected to the gripping and pulling part (8).  in this arrangement, the gripping and pulling part (8) consists of a taper sleeve (10) and a holder (16), and the holder (16) and the taper sleeve (10) are connected so as to be axially freely adjustable.  the holder (16) is screwed into the taper sleeve (10) by way of a terminal threaded portion (17) and has, at the end lying opposite the threaded portion (17) a screw head (18) which is guided in the housing head (2) open on one side.  <image>
