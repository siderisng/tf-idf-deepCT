<ABSTRACT>
An exhaust gas converter for internal combustion engines consists of a housing (1) with thermal barrier layer (6), each of a gas inlet nozzle (2) and gas outlet nozzle (3) located on the housing (1) and at least one of its top and bottom with gas passage openings, in the interior of theHouses (1) container (7).The container (7) is filled with granular catalyst to form a catalyst bed (8).The housing (1) consists of a double jacket whose outer jacket (4) and its inner jacket (5) are arranged at a distance from each other and slidably mounted against each other.In this case, the thermal insulation seal (6) is arranged between the outer shell (4) and the inner jacket (5).The containers (7) are slidably mounted.
