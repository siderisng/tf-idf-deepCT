<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a rolling film cassette consisting of a coilless storage chamber and a winding chamber connected by a web with this beaming chamber in which a coil of the exposed filmstrips and the protective paper strip are wound.To prevent the feeding of the film strip end in the take-up chamber, a resilient element is attached to the cartridge rear wall so that it engages in a film hole and blocate the film channel to the winding chamber.
