<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an in a housing (10) arranged forward and reverse switchable step switching plant for a multi-stage switch (100 and 200), with two individual by a linear work movement with the tooth pinion (56) of a drawing carrier (55) engageable Actuators (30 50 and 130, 150). Each operating element has a pushbutton (31 and 131), a scarf telement (40 and 140) and a preferably hairpin-shaped spring element (45), or is in effect with a coil spring (75) mounted in the housing. By means of the pushbutton, the respective actuation element is moved in the direction of arrow 1, whereby the switching element (40 or 140) guided with a first pin (43 and 143) along a backdrop (19) and a second pin (44 and 144 ) to engage the tooth pinion (56), so that the toothed pinion (56) rotatably mounted on an axis (20) against the restoring force of a corresponding and with a latching cam (59) in the tooth gap engaging raster spring (60) in the direction of arrow 3 is moved. The return of the actuating elements is achievable after completion of the control step by the restoring force of the spring elements (45 and 75).
