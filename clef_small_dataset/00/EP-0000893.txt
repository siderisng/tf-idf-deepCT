<ABSTRACT>
S-TRIAZIN ring having polyisocyanates of the formula in which X is for a radical, as obtained by removal of the higher-reactive isocyanate group from an organic diisocyanate of the formula OCN-X-NCO with isocyanate groups of different reactivity in the sense of the isocyanate addition reaction,and Y for a chlorine, bromine, C₁-C₈-alkyl, C₁-C₈-alkoxy, C₆-C₁₀ aryl, C₆-C₁₀ aroxy radical or a remainder of the formula -NH-Co-NHX-CNO is a process for their preparation by reacting S-triazine polyamines having diisocyanates which have isocyanate groups of different reactivity, as well as the use of the new compounds as embarrassable fillers in the production of polyurethane imsves.
