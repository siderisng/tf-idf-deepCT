<ABSTRACT>
an air nozzle and a pneumatic conveyor for particulate material provided with such an air nozzle.  the air nozzle contains a central, relatively large air-outlet zone (3), around which a plurality of smaller air-outlet zones (4) are arranged peripherally.  such a configuration of the air nozzle significantly reduces the power input of a pneumatic conveyor for particulate material equipped with such an air nozzle.  <image>
