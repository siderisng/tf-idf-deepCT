<ABSTRACT>
Hardening agent for polyepoxides with more than a 1,2-epoxy group per molecule based on cyanoethylated polyamidamines with still free secondary or primary or secondary and primary amino groups, characterized in that the cyanethylated polyamidamine a reaction product of polyadded units of a) 1 Mol of a polyamine of the formula wherein p = 0, n = 2 or 3, m = 0 to 6, x = NH₂ and p = 1, n = 2 or 3, m = 0 and x = h, e.g. b. Pentaethylenhexamine, b) 0.5-2.3 mol of a saturated lactam, z. b. Caprolactam and C) represents 0.4-2.8 moles of acrylonitrile, methacrylonitrile or whose mixture. The curing agents can additionally with 0.01-1.8 mol of a 1,2-alkylene oxide with 2-4 C atoms or with 0.001-0.3 mol of a carboxylic acid or with the 1,2-alkylene oxide and the carboxylic acid in the specified Quantities, always modified to 1 mol of the polyamine. The curing agents harden even in thin layers at room temperature to clear, glossy, stickless films with a trouble-free surface.
