<TITLE>
brake shoe.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>
sigro siewer & griesemerde <sep>sigro-siewer und griesemer gmbh metall- und kunststoffwarenfabrik kommanditgesellschaft <sep>sigro-siewer und griesemer gmbh metall- und kunststoffwarenfabrik kommanditgesellschaft in der triftd-5960 olpede<sep>
</APPLICANT>
<INVENTOR>
griesemer albertde<sep>griesemer, albert<sep>griesemer, albertahornweg 11 ad-5960 olpe/biggeseede<sep>
</INVENTOR>
<ABSTRACT>
a brake shoe for drum brakes on motor vehicles consists of an arc- shaped web (1) and a sleeve (4, 5) which covers the web approximately in the shape of a half-cylindrical shell and is used for the retention of a brake lining.  the web (1) and sleeve are formed from two sheet metal parts (2, 3), of the same surface area, which are placed on the web (1) on top of one another and are welded (10) to one another, while their outer edges in each case represent flanges (4, 5), bent outwards, in the shape of cylindrical shells, of half the width of the sleeve, which flanges together form the sleeve for retention of the brake lining.  <image>
</ABSTRACT>
