<ABSTRACT>
The outer skin (9) of reinforced earth structures is attached to bands (10), and the tensile force is removed under friction in the ground (11).The tapes (10) consist of hort-resistant, high-strength woven or braided separated organic, glass or carbon fibers or fiber mixtures and preferably impregnated with liquid reaction resins such as epoxy, polyurethane or isocyanate resins.It is advantageous to impregnate the bands and harden only after the laying.
