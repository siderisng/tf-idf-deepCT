<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a manufacturing method for copper catalysts having an average grain size between 0.1 and 100 microns.The copper (I) oxide content is 3 to 100% and is obtained by oxidation of a copper powder, wherein the oxidation is carried out at a - compared to the air-reduced oxygen partial pressure.The catalytic copper thus prepared is particularly suitable for the so-called Rochow synthesis.
