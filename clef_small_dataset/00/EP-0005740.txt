<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to an automatic packaging line in particular a line intended for packaging foods such as molded chocolate tablets or chocolate coated products. This line has power supply lines 1 arranged to bring products to be packaged to a series of packaging machines and automatic feed stations 3 arranged along said tapes to selectively distribute products to packing machines. This line is equipped with a recovery device 5 which collects products that have not been supported by stations 3, of a storage device 6, 7, and 8 to temporarily store the products collected, of a Recycling device 9 and a discharge device 10 which cooperate to reintroduce the products stored momentarily to the conveyor strips 1 and an ejection device 4 which rejects the products in excess when the storage device is saturated. This line has the advantage of being fully automatic without any manual intervention.
