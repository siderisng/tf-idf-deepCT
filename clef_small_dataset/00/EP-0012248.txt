<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a method for producing zeolite A from kaolin by transferring the kaolin in metacoolin and subsequent reaction of metacoacoline in aqueous-alkaline medium, wherein the transfer of kaolin in metacoolin at temperatures between 700 and 950 ° C in the presence of alkaline earth compounds andOptionally, in the presence of undyed halides and / or halogens, as well as the use of such prepared zeolites in washing and cleaning agents.
