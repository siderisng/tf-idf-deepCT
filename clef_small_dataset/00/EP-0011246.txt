<ABSTRACT>
Substituted pyridazines of the general formula H where a a lower alkylene group, L is a vignine or the group -NH-R¹, M a hydrogen atom or the group -C (= x) R², R¹ an optionally substituted or derivative amino group, R² an alkyl group, oneAlkoxy group, an alkyl mercapto group, an optionally substituted aryl group, a phenalkoxy group or a given if substituted amino group and X represents an oxygen atom or a sulfur atom, as well as its acid addition salts with inorganic or organic acids are new compounds.They are used in the pharmaceutical industry as intermediates and for the production of blood pressure sinks of the drugs.The invention relates to compounds of the formula H, methods for their preparation, their use and medicaments containing these compounds.
