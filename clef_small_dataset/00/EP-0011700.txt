<ABSTRACT>
Power device for a monolithi memory that reduces power dissipation.CHA that word line (WLI) is connected to a current switching circuit (14-I) comprising a transistor, whose collector is connected to an extreme voltage -V1, and the base at the output of the decoder ofAddresses, a second transistor whose collector is connected to an intermediate voltage -V2, between -V1 and -V3, -V3 being the second extreme value voltage of the cells.The transmitters are connected ENSEM BLE to the corresponding word line.Depending on the state of the output of the decoder, the first or second transistor is conductive so that the cells are powered between -V1 and -V3 or between -V2 and -V3.Application to Harper-type cell memories.
</ABSTRACT>
