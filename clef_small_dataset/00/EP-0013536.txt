device for charge control of a battery in a lighting arrangement for vehicles such as bicycles, motor vehicles and the like.

<ABSTRACT>
in the device a luminous indicator (12) is provided, the luminous indicator being extinguished after the selected execution of the circuit or lighting up when the voltage of the battery (13) with which the illumination system is operated drops below a lower threshold value which can preferably be set by means of a potentiometer (4).  the invention is preferably used in touring or racing cycles in which a battery is automatically charged during travel.  <image>
