<ABSTRACT>
thermosettable polyester resin compositions, suitable  for use in powder coating applications are derived from a  polymerization reaction mixture comprising a dicarboxylic  aromatic component (e. g. , 1,4-benzenedicarboxylic acid), a  trihydroxy alkyl component [e. g. , 2-ethyl-2-(hydroxymethyl)-1,3-propanediol],  a diol component (e. g. , 2,2-dimethyl-1,3-propanediol)  and a dihydroxy alkyl compound having 4 to 10  carbon atoms in the primary carbon chain between the  hydroxyl moieties (e. g. , 1,6-hexanediol).  polyesters of the  present invention have a number average molecular weight  of from about 2,000 to about 6,000, a combined acid and  hydroxyl number of from 35 to 70 milligrams of koh per  gram of polyester, and a glass transition temperature of from  35°c to 50°.
