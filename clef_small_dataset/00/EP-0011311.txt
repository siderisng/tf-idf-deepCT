<ABSTRACT>
novel sulfate salts of c₁₀-c₂₀ linear alkyl glyceryl ether  alcohols, useful as biodegradable surfactants in detergent  compositions, are prepared by reacting a stoichiometric  excess of c₁₀-c₂₀ linear alkyl alcohol with glycidol predis­ solved in hexyl acetate, to selectively produce c₁₀-c₂₀ linear  alkyl glyceryl ether alcohols containing 1-3 glyceryl units,  sulfating said ether alcohols, and neutralizing the sulfates  with base.
