<ABSTRACT>
In a data processing system, maintenance interface device (40) is provided to synchronize the operation of a service processor (28) to those of the central unit (20).It contains circuits for interpretence of commands of both the service processor (28) and the central unit (20) and allows the unobstructed traffic between the two.The central unit (20) includes a microprocessor (42).The maintenance interface unit (40) is constructed after a Prizip that facilitates maintenance by enabling diagnostic and test methods.
