<ABSTRACT>
In a cable channel, which consists of one-by other arranged nets and interconnected cable trays or sprays (1, 20), each cable tray or platelet is connected to one of one of one of the supporting boom (2) by means of an axial force strut (4). The one end of this axial force strut (4) is attached to the mounting side end of the casing (2) and the other end is attached to the long middle of the cable tub (1) by means of a fitting (5), wherein the axial force strut and the boom preferably an angle of 45 include.
