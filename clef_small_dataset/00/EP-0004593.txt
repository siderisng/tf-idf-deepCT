<ABSTRACT>
In a ball sleeve for linear movements, a cage (12) occupied with balls (10) occupied with balls (12) is arranged in a hollow cylindrical outer sleeve (14), which bearing the balls carrying axially parallel race portions for the balls standing with a shaft or the like in contact balls(10) having two radially upwards or descending transitions (34) of the ball lug at their two ends.In order to form the transition out of or into the beads carrying axis-parallel race portions elastically so much, so that the balls can effect frictional arm on or off, each transition (34) has a recess extending transverse to the direction of travel, e.g.b.a groove (36) or a slot (40), which forms a kind of weakness joint between the portions of the transition (34) connected via the recess.
