<ABSTRACT>
In a contactless resistance potentiometer, it is proposed according to the invention to take a photoconductive layer (1, 6) as a resistance element.On this layer is fixedly applied for outer electrodes (2,3,7,8) and for tap electrodes (4, 9) of the tracks.By moving a diaphragm under a light source along the tap electrode (4, 9) with a defined geometric course, one obtains at constant aperture and thus exposure of a partial surface of the photoconductor layer (1, 6) between the outer electrodes (2,3,7,8)A resistance division by the tap electrode (4, 9) according to their course between the outer electrodes (2,3,7,8).
