<ABSTRACT>
An amorphous metal alloy film from an amorphous alloy contains at least one transition metal from the period 4, wherein the alloy is added at least one element of the glass-forming elements from the period 2 and 3.When the film is produced by atomizing in vacuo, then the impact plate consists of the above-mentioned elements introduced into the amorphous film, wherein the process gas atmosphere contains a proportion of nitrogen greater than 2 vol.% is.The nitrogen is mixed with a noble gas, such as argon.This leads to nitrogen-containing alloys with a composition such as (CO-FE-B) N or (Fe-B) n. The resulting amorphous metal alloy films with significant nitrogen places show relatively good and adjustable magnetic, electrical and mechanical properties.
