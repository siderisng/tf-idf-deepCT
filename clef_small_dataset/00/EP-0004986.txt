<ABSTRACT>
In a device for supplying dissolved chemicals in horizontally flowing waters, a distributor tube and a plurality of feeding bodies downward from the distributor can be provided.For uniform distribution of the chemicals, a distributor tube (3) hoses (6) fixed above the water level (6) of different lengths as a feeding members whose outlet openings are distributed substantially evenly over the flow cross-section.On the feed bodies (6) float (7) are arranged at different heights, which ensure a substantially uniform distribution of the outlet openings over the flow cross-section with a fluctuating level of the water flow.Distributor tube (3) and feed members (6) are suitably made of chemical-resistant material such as Teflon, polypropylene or polyvinyl chloride.
