<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a thermometer with digital display device of the temperature value of the body temperature, which contains a temperature sensor with a thermistor and a device associated with a cable, in which the display device, the electronic circuit, the voltage source and the switches are accommodated, wherein the electronic circuitfrom a voltage multiplication (A), a control circuit (B), a sub-circuit (C) for high constant voltage, a bridge circuit (D) of four high constant resistors (8, 9, 10, 11) and the thermistor connected as a sensor (12),a sub-circuit (E) for adapting the voltage generated by the bridge circuit (D) and an integrated circuit (4) with a 3 1/2 digit digital voltmeter, which drives the display device (13) directly.
</ABSTRACT>
