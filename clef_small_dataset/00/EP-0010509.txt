<ABSTRACT>
The firing device comprises a capacitor (8) loaded using a battery (1) via a circuit comprising an integrated circuit (2), a transistor (3), a transformer (4) and a diode (5).Applying a pulse to the trigger (15) of a thyristor (8), the capacitor (8) is discharged in an electrical primer (13) of the cartridge, to cause the firing.The firearm is also equipped with a shock absorber device that can be used to cushion the relative movement of the cullassecannon assembly relative to the lacrosse.The electrical primer (13) of the cartridge is composed of a cylindrical metal body, an internal insulation and an axial electrode embedded in the insulation, a filament located near the powder being plugged between the metal body.and the front end of the axial electrode extending to the rear end of the socket.
