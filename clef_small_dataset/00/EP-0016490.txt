<ABSTRACT>
For indirect directing a protected on a vehicle gun (6) to a target (ZA) with the aid of an electronic computer according to the invention, the following verification steps are provided according to the invention, which enable it to be carried out quickly, easily and "foolproof".The gun (6) is first oriented with respect to the normal direction (s) (a).The destination zoom (Z) is stored in the computer.The computer displays the current directional deviation (β + γ) and the gun is pivoted until the display is "0".
