<ABSTRACT>
In a method for utilizing the heat content of internal combustion ingredients in sludge, especially in sewear sludge, the slurry which contains still a proportion of water is burned in a press-resistant oven.The resulting steam and exhaust gases are passed through heat exchangers and a thin film evaporator, whereby the waste heat and the heat of condensation in one or more stages cause heating and thickening of the slurry to burn.Through the overpressure of the exhaust gases, steam and liquid circles relaxation machines can be powered.
