<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a process for the preparation of ferrous alloys;Inoculant alloys comprising lanthanum and ferrous alloys obtained by this process.This method comprises incorporating at least 0.0001% to about 0.5 to 2% by weight of lanthanum with ferrous alloy during its elaboration, particularly in the form of inoculant alloys.It is thus obtained a better degassing in the case of a melting which translates into a modification of the solidification curve which is shown in FIG. 2. This method reduces or delete certain defects of ferrous alloys such as bites and cavitiesSpheroidal graph dropping fonts, carbides of the lamellar gray cast iron, to improve flowability, rolling ability and isotropy of steels, and improve the mechanical properties of ferrous alloys.
