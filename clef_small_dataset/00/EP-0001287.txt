<ABSTRACT>
the invention relates to a novel antibiotic no.  2-200 represented  by the formula    a process for producing the same by cultivating an antibiotic  no.  2-200 producing microorganism of the genus nacardia,  e. g.  nocardia sp.  no.  2-200 (atcc 31319), and recovering by  isolation the antibiotic no.  2-200 accumulated in the culture,  and pharmaceutical compositions containing said antibiotic.   the antibiotic has a broad antimicrobial spectrum, in particular  it is effective against gram negative bacteria.
