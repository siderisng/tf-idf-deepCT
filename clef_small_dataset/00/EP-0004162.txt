<ABSTRACT>
a low cost original document handling apparatus (a) for  a copying machine (6) for automatically discharging the original  document from the platen (35) after it has been copied.  it  is automatically driven for an electrically controlled time  period (13) by a helical spring drive system (b) which automatically  stores and subsequently utilizes the platen cover  (7) opening or closing force to eject documents and eliminate  the cost and power consumption of a conventional electrically  driven document drive system.  it includes automatic  over-winding protection (60).  a dual function electromechanical  release system (d) automatically unlatches the  platen cover after connecting the spring drive (b) to eject the  document.  
</ABSTRACT>
