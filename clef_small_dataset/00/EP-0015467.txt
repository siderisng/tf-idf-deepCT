<ABSTRACT>
Method for removing residual monomers from ABS polymer preparations prepared by emulsion polymerization, wherein a ABS-polymer-containing latex 0.1 to 10 wt.-% based on the solid in the latex, an electrolyte such as an acid or a salt, the mixture is simultaneously heated by removing residual monomers and a part of the water by distillation to a temperature of 55 to 120 ° C.
