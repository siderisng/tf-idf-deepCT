<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a donor for a two-component adhesive with two chambers provided for receiving each of the components with the mixing ratio of the components corresponding to the volume ratio.The dispenser consists of a straight prism shaped container (1), which is divided by an intermediate wall (2) in two chambers (11, 12), wherein the chamber floors are formed as a piston (9, 10) for simultaneous precessing of the chamber content.By moving the intermediate wall (2) by means of an external cam (6), a forced dosage is achieved, so that each desired amount of product is compressed in the same mixing ratio from the container (1).The intermediate wall (2) and the pistons (9, 10) attached to their lower ends form a single component.
