<ABSTRACT>
The positioning of the sound support is controlled, comprising an addresses track carrying coded words sharing the support of sounds in consecutive elements, so as to bring in front of a reading head (17) an element of the support of predetermined address sounds..The positioning of the image support (21) comprising images each associated with a particular address, so as to bring to a projection window (22) an image of the predetermined address image media.The simultaneous scrolling of the sound and image supports, the projection rate of the images may vary and being controlled according to pre-recorded instructions on a control information medium whose access is bound to the scrolling ofNone of the supports.This process is suitable for reproducing audio-visual sequences.
