<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a method for producing foamed plastics by reacting to high molecular weight plastics in the sense of a polymerization, condensation or polyaddition reaction of output materials in the presence of propellants or a conventional foaming of a non-foamed thermoplastic material with the aid of a propellant,wherein is the propellant 1,1,1-trifluoro-2-chloroethane, optionally in admixture with 1,1-difluoro-1,2-dichloroethane.The method is particularly suitable for the production of polyurethane foams.
