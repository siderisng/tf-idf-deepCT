<ABSTRACT>
From DE-OS 27 10 399 a method for producing ammonium sulfamate is known in which sulfur trioxide and ammonia are introduced into a pressure vessel containing a substantially from ammonium sulfamate and ammoniumimidosulfonate melt.In this case, a molar ratio NH₃ / SO₃ is adhered to from 1. 5: 1 to 1. 99: 1.It has now been found that it makes sense to relax the pressure vessel after prolonged reaction times briefly and thus split the main amount of accumulating inert gases.
