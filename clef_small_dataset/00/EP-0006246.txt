<ABSTRACT>
Abstract silencer for portable pneumatic a driving appliances, such.b.Compressed air nailer.The silencer comprises a exhaust duct, which is generally formed by the housing termination (7) and the upper cover (8) of the driving apparatus.The exhaust duct consists of two sliding chamber systems whose chambers (9, 12, 12a, 13, 16, 18) are connected to each other via wall recesses (11, 20, 21, 22, 23).The chambers are further connected via holes (10, 14, 15, 17) with the outlet side of the main control valve.
