<ABSTRACT>
In a method for increasing the graphitization degree of rods, one is based on such rods, which by thermal conversion of under normal conditions liquid or gaseous hydrocarbons in the presence of oxygen or oxygen-containing gases and optionally water vapor at temperatures of 1200 to 2000 ° C under printing of 1 to 80 bar were won. Let this carbon black in Quasifluider Phase a vertical dwell zone in the direction of gravity while driving through a residence time of 1 to 6 hours. In this case, a temperature of at least 1200 ° C is maintained in the dwell zone. For carrying through the method, a device is used, which is used from a graphite tube (,) and a concentrically arranged steel container (10) and of a litter tube (6), an exhaust pipe (7), a waterge cooled discharge member (3) and a nitrogen introduction tube (9). In this case, the space between the graphite tube (1) and the steel container (10) is filled with insulating material rial (13). The response tube (6) has a divider member (12) near its upper end. Finally, the water cooled discharge member (3) is connected to the lower end of the graphite tube (1).
