<ABSTRACT>
Quaternary stripping solution which includes ethylene-diamine, pyrocatechin, water and a zine diamond or a derivative of it and is used for stripping monocrystalline or polycrystalline silicon surfaces.The amount of diazine in the solution corresponds approximately to the molar equivalent of 5 to 10 g of ethylene-diamine pyrazine / liter.This solution can be used at temperatures and with variable pickling speeds as shown in the figure.The stripping speed can be changed by changing the water concentration and / or pyro catechin in the solution.This stripping solution does not shape residues during the stripping process and the stripping speed remains stable in the presence of oxygen.This solution is used for stripping Sili cum surfaces according to configurations determined during the manufacturing steps of semiconductor devices.
