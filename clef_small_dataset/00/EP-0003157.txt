<ABSTRACT>
a gas discharge display including a gas discharge  display panel comprising a plurality of cells arranged in a  column/row matrix.  cathodes are connected to the rows and  anodes to the columns of the matrix.  the cells are arranged  in repeating groups each comprising at least two columns.   corresponding cells in each group are primed contamporaneously  in a desired sequence whereby the last cell in the  sequence to be primed is adjacent the first cell in the  sequence to be scanned.  in priming the cells each cell is  discharged at a low level.  various types of loop sequences are disclosed together  with several techniques for isolating priming pulses from  display pulses.  
</ABSTRACT>
