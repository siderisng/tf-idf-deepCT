<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for reducing porosity and surface roughness of a ceramic surface.According to this method, the Cera mixed documents are coated with a coating assembly with a content of a non-polymeric organisy silicon compound, a non-polymeric ethylenically unsaturated silicon compound, a stabilizer and a solvent, then heated to higher temperatures for converting the silicon compounds in silicon oxides.
