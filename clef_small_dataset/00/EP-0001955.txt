<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an electrode for electrolysis cell and in particular a transparent electrode for electrolytic display cell and its manufacturing process.The electrode is constituted by a conductive layer deposited on an electrical insulating carrier and is characterized in that said said first layer layer is coated with a said second-layer conductive layer made of a material different from that of said first layer andSelected in such a way as, under the operating conditions of said electrolysis cell, it can not occur parasitic side reactions that may degrade said layers.Application to electrolytic display devices.
</ABSTRACT>
