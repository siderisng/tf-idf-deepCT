<ABSTRACT>
Partially known hydroxyphenyl ethers of the formula in which R is a radical of the formula wherein R¹, R² and R³ are independently of one another for hydrogen, halogen, haloalkyl, nitro or cyano, and R is further optionally by halogen, haloalkyl, alkoxy, nitro and/ or Cyano is substituted heterocyclic radical, which may contain optionally substituted anelated benzene ring, can be prepared when forming halogen compounds of the formula R-HAL (ii) where R is the above meaning and hal for fluorine, chromium or bromine, with dihydroxybenzene of the formula in the presence of calcium hydroxide and in the presence of a polar diluent, reacted at temperatures between 20 and 200 ° C.
