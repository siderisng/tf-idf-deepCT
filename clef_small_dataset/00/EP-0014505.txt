<ABSTRACT>
This system is intended to test for example a modem whose transmitter (4) and the receiver (10) are coupled to the transmission line (2), (3) by a hybrid junction (6) and which is provided withAn echo canceller (14), (15).The test consists in coming to a test signal applied to the transmitter to the signal returned by the receiver when the modem is curly side line.The test system comprises an adder (20) having an input (21) receives the error signal (E) of the ECHO cancelver, the other input (22) receives during the test the loopback signal (YB) and whose output is connected to the receiver.Means (24) are provided for making the signal returned by the receiver different from the test sequence, when the error signal of the echo canceller exceeds a threshold.
