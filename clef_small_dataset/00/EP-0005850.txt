<ABSTRACT>
Liquid fine washing agent with textile softening effect.It contains a combination of A) 5 - 18 wt as a surfactant component.% of an ethoxylated C₁₃-C₁₇ oxoalcohol having an ethylene oxide content of about 50-65 wt.-%, and b) 5 - 18 wt.% of an ethoxylated C₈-C₁₂-fat alcohol or oxo alcohol with an ethylene oxide content of about 55-70 wt.-%.The quantity ratio of a): b) is between 2: 1 and 1: 2. As a textile softening component, the means C) contains 2.5 - 10 wt.-% of a long-chain α-olefin-derived quarters of ammonium compound with two long 2-hydroxyalkyl radicals.The agents have good washing power and good softening effect with simultaneous favorable foaming behavior.They are stable even with long storage and have a favorable viscosity temperature behavior.
</ABSTRACT>
