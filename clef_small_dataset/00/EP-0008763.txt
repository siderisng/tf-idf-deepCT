The new compounds aminomethyl, bis (aminomethyl) and tris (aminomethyl) cyclododecane of the formula wherein n is 1, 2 or 3, in the form of the individual compounds or their mixtures, are prepared by having cyclododecria- 1.5.9with carbon monoxide and hydrogen in the presence of a rhodium-containing catalyst, e.g.b.A mixture of tris- (dibenzylsulfide) - trishlor-rhodium and dikobaltoctacarbonyl, z.b.200 bar and 110 ° C for mono-, bis- or triformyl cyclododecane of the formula wherein N has the above meaning, in the form of the individual connections or their mixtures, and subsequently hydrogenated in the presence of ammonia, e.g.b.At 120 bar and 110 ° C in the presence of Raney cobalt.The novel compounds of formula (I) are suitable as corrosion moles in heating oils, lubricants or blowing agents on the hydrocarbon basis;Furthermore, they represent crosslinkers or chain extension means for epoxy resins or NCO prepolymers.
