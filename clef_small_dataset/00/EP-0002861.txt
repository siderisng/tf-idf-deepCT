<ABSTRACT>
vinyl chloride polymers with a large bet surface area  and good plasticizer absorption are prepared by suspension  polymerization of vinyl chloride in the presence  of a primary suspension agent and as a secondary suspension  agent, a polyvinyl alcohol having a degree of  polymerization of at least 250 and the percent of solvolysis  in the range of 40 to 70 moles % which is narrowly distributed.   the vinyl chloride polymer so produced has a relatively  narrow particle size distribution and a uniformity  factor of at least 4.
