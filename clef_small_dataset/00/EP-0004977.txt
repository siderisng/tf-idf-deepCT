<ABSTRACT>
In a bottom part (1) of a plan film cassette, two slides (14, 15¹) held in the closure position (14, 15, 15)) are arranged with hooks (14a, 14a¹), which attached to the lid pin (7.7¹) in the shutter positionTick up.The two slides (14, 14¹) are opposite to each other and from about equal mass.The opposing ends of the two slides are each connected to one end (19,19¹) of a lever mounted on the bottom part lever (17).This avoids a self-effective opening of the cassette due to shock effects.
