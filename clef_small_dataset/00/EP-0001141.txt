<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a new mode for attaching a metal fret on a metal rim.It consists in providing on the metal fret 4.5 and the clamping rings 11,12 of the metal rim 2 deformable conical shoulder ments 8 and the conical bores of the same inclination 9, acting against each other.It is applicable in particular to the motor rollers for the support and the drive of cylindrical ferrules.
