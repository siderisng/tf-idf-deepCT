<ABSTRACT>
storage box for peach spoons comprising a body (7), for example parallelepipedic, comprising a series of recesses (10) formed in one of its peripheral surfaces (9) and a series of opposing holes (12) formed inAn adjacent wall and perpendicular to the above surfaces and intended to receive, respectively the shaft (3) and two branches (2) of a spoon hook and a cover (8) mounted articulated on the body, for selectively giving access to the spoons arrangedon the body.In a particular embodiment, the body is cylindrical, as well as the lid, the latter having a light for selectively giving access to a chosen angular sector of the body.
