<ABSTRACT>
In a tiltable converter with a support ring surrounding the converter vessel, which has two diametrically opposite support pin, the converter is movably connected to the support ring by train and pressure pickup connecting means.In order to allow forms of the converter vessel in each direction, the converter vessel (1) in the support ring (2) by means of three to the longitudinal axis (13) of the converter vessel of inclined lug tabs (4) is mounted, the joints (5, 6) each at the converter vessel(1) and mounted on the support ring (2), wherein the jarclothes are pivotable in the plane formed by their longitudinal or middle salmon (12) and the longitudinal axis (13) of the converter vessel.
