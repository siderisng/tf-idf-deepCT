<ABSTRACT>
in an apparatus for precisely moving a table, rectilinear  drive systems for both x- and y-axes are fixed to a rest  frame, a movable stage (7; 15) is constructed so as to have its  motion in a direction of one of both the axes constrained by a  motion of the rectilinear drive system (4, 5, 6) for the one axis  and to have its motion in a direction of the other axis  constrained by a rectilinear motion of the rectilinear drive  system (12, 13, 14) for the other axis, and means (9, 11; 22,  23) for coupling the rectilinear drive system (4, 5, 6; 12, 13,  14) for at least one of the axes and the movable stage (7; 15)  is constructed so as to have a backlash.  
</ABSTRACT>
