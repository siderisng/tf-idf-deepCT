<ABSTRACT>
1.  anchoring apparatus for floating implements, particularly for dredgers, comprising a device which serves to adjust the elevation of the hauling rope (3) of an anchor (1) and is movably mounted on the hull in a vertical guide (8) and adapted to be lowered into the region below the keel line, preferably with hinged anchor palms (11), characterized in that a funnel (6) for threading the shank (9) of the anchor is provided at the lower end of the device for adjusting the elevation of the hauling rope (3) and is adapted to be lifted to a position in front of the mouth of a hawser pipe (4) for the hauling rope (3), which hawser pipe is obliquely arranged in the hull (2), the axial direction of the threading funnel (6) agreeing with the axial direction of the hawser pipe (4). 
</ABSTRACT>
