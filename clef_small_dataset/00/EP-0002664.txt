<ABSTRACT>
Method of treatment of metal waste and device the implementation.The invention relates to a method of treating metal waste.This method consists of subjecting metal waste to physical and chemical cleaning in the towers (1) and (10) followed by an inert atmosphere grinding in the installations (12) and (26) of a reductive atmosphere treatment inThe oven (25) and a classification under an inert atmosphere (35), the passage in different treatments with vibrating devices and in a sealed atmospheric elements installation.This method makes it possible to prepare a method for the manufacture of sintered parts.
