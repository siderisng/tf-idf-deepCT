<ABSTRACT>
1.  a method of introducing solvent into the gas cylinders of a cylinder group, characterised in that the amount of solvent lacking is determined before the filling operation ; that the introduction of the solvent is carried out separately from and before the process of filling with the gas and the solvent is introduced into the group of cylinders in the form of a stream of liquid under pressure by means of a manifold which connects the cylinders of the group, the cylinders remaining open and connected to one another after the introduction of the solvent. 
</ABSTRACT>
