<ABSTRACT>
a photographic element containing a temporary barrier  layer between reactants and polymeric compositions useful  therein are disclosed.  the compositions comprise a mixture  of (1) from 5 to 95 percent by weight of a polymer comprising  from 55 to 85 percent by weight of vinylidene chloride, 5 to  35 percent by weight of an ethylenically unsaturated mono­ mer, and optionally from 0 to 20 percent by weight of an  ethylenically unsaturated carboxylic acid, and (2) from 95 to  5 percent by weight of a polymeric carboxy-ester-lactone.  a  barrier formed from such composition is particularly useful  as a process timing layer in color image transfer photo­ graphic elements.
