<ABSTRACT>
The subject of the invention relates to new fluorinated products resulting from the reaction of: (a) a molecule of one or more acids of formula: HS - A (COOH) N (I) and (b) one to five molecules one or more compounds posed at least one ethylenic bond, at least one of these compounds that meet the general formula: RF - B - = CH - R (II) and possibly neutralized or partially neutralized with a mineral base or organic. In formulas (I) and (ii), A represents an aliphatic or aromatic carbonated hydro radical, n is an integer ranging from 1 to 4, RF represents a perfluorinated chain containing 1 to 20 carbon atoms, B represents a bivalent chainage. , possibly branched that may comprise sulfur, oxygen, or nitrogen atoms, one of the symbols R represents a hydrogen atom and the other a hydrogen atom or an alkyl group containing 1 to 4 atoms of carbon. These products, applied to substrates, give them an oleofuge, water repellent effect and resistance to aggressive pro dutes or solvents.
