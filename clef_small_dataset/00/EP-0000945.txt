<ABSTRACT>
Collective pack carriers for doses with bördelrand in the form of a flat, plastic consisting of plastic and a molded carrying handle (7) supporting frame (1).The cans are held in pairs opposite each other of receiving cellar (8).The receiving plates, in turn, have elastic undercuts (12, 13) for clamping detecting the doses.The arrangement is made in such a way that no undercuts are provided in the contact area of adjacent receiving plates, so that the canmats can touch.
