<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method and a system for heat treatment of fine-grained good using a preheater, rotary kiln and radiator as well as an additional preliminary concalation zone.In this case, the cooling air stream is inserted into the preliminal concalation zone in several subflowers, and partial streams of the goods are introduced into the cooling air partial streams before entering the preliminary concalation zone.In this way, a complete and very uniform heat transfer from the fuel can be achieved with low system-technical effort for the preliminary concalation zone.
