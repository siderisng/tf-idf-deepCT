<ABSTRACT>
the invention relates to a warning device (22) which makes it possible to detect an elongate element (21) embedded or captive in a wall (20) with which this device is associated, in order to make possible the detection of the latter from the outside.  it is characterised in that it consists of a non-metallic material, into which has been incorporated a powder of a material sensitive to magnetic phenomena, which has been magnetised.  its manufacturing process is characterised in that, in the case of a plastic material, the powder of the material which is sensitive to magnetic phenomena is mixed when the plastic material is in the molten state, and in that this powder is magnetised after the extrusion of the plastic material.  the device for the detection of the warning device reacts to variations in intensity and orientation of the magnetic field which it detects.  application to the detection of pipes or tubes embedded or captive in a wall.  <image>
</ABSTRACT>
