<ABSTRACT>
Method and device for guiding a flexible wall tube between a first zone where it has an annular section and a second zone where it is flattened.According to this method, a tube section (10a) is guided for the form of a surface formed by the lateral faces of a solid (A to F) determined by a rectilinear edge EF coinciding with the rectilinear section.flattened with the tube, and by a base face (ABCD) which is located opposite this edge, which is coaxial and parallel to this edge and which has a perimeter of length equal to the length of said edge, saidSide faces being shaped so as to form a developable surface along a rectangle.The invention applies in particular to circular knitting trades.
</ABSTRACT>
