<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for anodic oxi duration of band, film or plate-shaped material made of aluminum or its alloys in an aqueous, sulfuric acid and phosphoric acid-containing electrolyte; The material can also be roughened mechanically, chemically or electrochemically before carrying out the anodis chen oxidation. The anodic oxidation of the material takes place in an electrolyte of a concentration of sulfuric acid of 25 to 150 g / l, to phosphoric acid of 10 to 50 g / l and aluminum ions of 5 to 25 g / l, at a current density of 4 to 25 A / DM² and at a temperature of 25 ° to 65 ° C. The method is used in particular in the manufacture of a tape, film or plate-shaped pressure plate carrier material, then these carrier materials can be coated with the manufacturer of pre-sensitizing th printing plates or the consumer itself with a low-tensioning mass. This light-emblem layers are optionally stained and preferably contain diazo compounds, diazoquinones, diazomic condensates or photopolymerizable verbin tions.
