<ABSTRACT>
Phthalocyanine transducers consisting of a mixture of (a) phthalocyanine and (b) 2 to 20 wt.-% based on (a), at least one urea derivative of the formula K- (-nHCO-NH-R) ₂ (I) consist, in the K 1,5-naphthylene or 4,4'-diphenylene methane, R C₁₂- to C₁₈-alkyl, C₁₂- to C₁₈ alkenyl, 3- (C₈ to C₁₈-alkoxy) -propyl, 2- (C₈- to C₁₈ alkylamino) -etherhyl or - (CH₂) ₃-O-(CH₂-CH₂O) N-R¹, R¹ C₂- to C₈-alkyl or phenyl and N 1,2,3 or 4, and wherein the mixture is obtained by milling (a) and (b) in the absence of mill aids.The preparations in printing inks such as offset printing inks color strong prints in very pure shades while at the same time very low granularity of the printing ink.
</ABSTRACT>
