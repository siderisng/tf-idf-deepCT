<ABSTRACT>
perhaloalkylvinylcyclopropanecarboxylates having the  general formula    are disclosed wherein one of y and z is a perhaloalkyl group  having 1 to 4 carbon atoms and the other is hydrogen,  halogen, lower alkyl, phenyl, phenylthio or benzyl, with the  proviso that y and z may be combined to form a perhalocyclopentylidene  group; r is halogen, hydroxy, lower alkoxy or  -or¹ in which r¹ represents an alcohol residue which forms  an insecticidal ester when combined with chrysanthemic  acid or a 3-(2,2-dihalovinyl)-2,2-dimethylcyclopropanecarboxylic  acid; r⁷ is hydrogen, lower  alkylcarbonyl, lower alkoxycarbonyl or cyano with the proviso  that when r is -or¹, r⁷ is hydrogen.  the insecticidal  efficacy and preparation of the compounds and intermediates  therefor are described and exemplified.
