<ABSTRACT>
mixtures of prostaglandin e1 and/or e2 with other active substances produce drugs which improve the utilisation of glucose in cells and reduce the blood sugar level in the body.  mixtures of these prostaglandins with glucose, fructose, xylitol and/or amino acids or with protein-free extracts of the blood of young calves produce solutions for infusion.  the combination of the blood extracts processed in gels or ointments is also suitable for use as agents for local wound treatment.  combination with oral antidiabetics or with insulin produces drugs lowering blood sugar.
