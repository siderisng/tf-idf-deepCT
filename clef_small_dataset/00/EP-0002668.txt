<ABSTRACT>
In a device for optical measurement of the lateral distance of two lines lying in different levels, e.g.b.Edges (11, 15) The diffraction pattern is evaluated, which is caused by the first edge (15) and the image (21) generated in the plane (21) of the second edge (11).From the position of the minima in the diffraction spectrum, which can be determined, for example, with a line-shaped arrangement of photodiodes (29), in a computer (32) of the edge spacing is determined.The arrangement serves for mutual alignment of two objects, for example a mask (59) and a semiconductor disk (69) on which special direct labels (57, 57 ') or (67, 67') are arranged.
