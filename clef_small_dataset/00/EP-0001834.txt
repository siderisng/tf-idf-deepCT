<ABSTRACT>
1.  a process for absorbing nitrous gases such as no and/or no2 in the presence of oxygen in aqueous phase such as water and/or nitric acid, which comprises mixing the nitrous gases with oxygen-containing gas, dispersing this mixture into the aqueous phase at the head of the column reactor, and passing it through the column reactor in parallel current with the aqueous phase towards to the buoyancy of the gases.
