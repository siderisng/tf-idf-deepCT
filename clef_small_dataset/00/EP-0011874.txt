<ABSTRACT>
In a method for producing ethylene by hydropyrolysis of a gas mixture consisting essentially of coal hydraulic materials, hydrogen, coal monoxide, coherent dioxide and water vapor, the gas mixture is heated to temperatures of over 800 ° C in a metallic walls having reaction zone.The metallic walls contain aluminum and / or copper at least at least on their upper surface.The applying of such walls, for example, applied application of these metals to metal walls takes place, for example, with the aid of halogen compounds as means of transport or by ine stirring the metal walls with a melt of aluminum and / or copper.In the case of such a method, the soot formation also periods, the end product has a high ethylene content and has no significant value in terms of the starting material.
