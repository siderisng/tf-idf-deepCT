<ABSTRACT>
Steam generator with integrated resurchaefener powered by a liquid metal.The resurchaex area consists of an annular space (16) surrounding the beat zone (s).A common manifold (26) sodium feeds the superheater and the resurpher.A valve makes it possible to act on the orifices (24) to balance the sodium flow in the superheater and in the resurpheater so that the temperature of the sodium at the output of these two zones is the same (sensors 60 and 62).Application to the realization of the secondary circuit of a cooled nuclear reactor with liquid sodium.
