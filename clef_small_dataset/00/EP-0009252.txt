<ABSTRACT>
To measure the filling level of a liquid in a fuel tank, a bracken-mounted on a carrier foil (3), as a resistive layer, is heated with a large temperature coefficient of the electrical resistance with a large temperature coefficient of the electrical resistor.In the liquid, the resistance layer (2) remains cold, outside it becomes warm.The difference of resistance to the resistance with an empty tank is proportional to the level.The resistance is measured against a non-heated comparative resistance (WII).A measurement influence through convection in the fuel tank is prevented by cover films (16) which are held by distance stripes (14, 15) at a low distance from the measurement or comparison resistor (Wi, Wii).
</ABSTRACT>
