<ABSTRACT>
a process for the recovery and purification of acrylonitrile  form an aqueous solution of acrylonitrile, acetonitrile, hcn  and impurities, comprising the steps of:   a) distilling the aqueous solution of an extractive distilla­ tion column with solvent water to produce an overhead  stream of acrylonitrile, hcn and water, and a bottoms  stream of water and impurities, and   b) distilling the overhead stream of (a) in one or more  distillation columns to recover acrylonitrile, character­ ised in that the bottoms stream of (a) is passed in indi­ rect heat exchange relationship with one or more addi­ tional columns to provide heat for distillation.  the process according to the invention provides substan­ tial savings in operating costs.
