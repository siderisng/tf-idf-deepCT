<ABSTRACT>
An electromagnet coil 29 submits to an attraction force a moving tray 30 made of magnetic material inside which is housed a ball 32 made of non-magnetic material obtaining the end of a passage 33 for the hydraulic fluid.The device further comprises electronic means for supplying the coil 29 by means of a pulsed unidirectional current whose cyclic ratio and the average value are enslaved to a set-up size represented by a control electrical signal.The device can be used as a hydraulic potentiometer for the definition of a driving pressure proportional to a control electrical signal in a hydraulic circuit for controlling an automatic transmission of industrial vehicles.
