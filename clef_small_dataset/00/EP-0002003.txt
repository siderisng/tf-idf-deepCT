<ABSTRACT>
the present invention relates to novel compositions  of matter for use in medicine, for agricultural applications,  for use in cosmetics comprising as active ingredient an  aluminum hydroxy chloride which is thixotropic, which has  an atomic ratio of aluminum to chlorine of from 5 to 1 to 6  to 1, and which contains the material in a concentration of  from about 3 to about 20 weight per cent.  for medical  topical application a suitable carrier is imbued with such  thixotropic microcrystalline aluminum chloride oxide hydrate.
