<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an arrangement for increasing interference shafts and harmonic shafts in hiking fields with a short-circuit slide (1) in the starting hollow conductor (5).With this arrangement not only the noise shafts in the limited pass range but all higher staging the broadband should be damped.The invention provides that the short-circuit slide (1) in its end face (2) (effective short circuit plane) has a recess (3) in the form of a λ / 4 or λ / 2-wave waveguide element in which a damping element (4) arrangedis.An inventive arrangement is used in hiking field tubes.
