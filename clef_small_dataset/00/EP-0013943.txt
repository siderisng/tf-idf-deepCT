<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a monolithically integrable low-pass filter circuit.It is the task of the invention to provide a circuit with the help of which one can achieve without afrage of outer circuit means Oh in the low frequency range of corner frequencies.The following circuit is used as a solution to this problem: to a circuit node is both a resistor (R) of the signal input as well as a resistor (R₂) of the noninverting input and the output of an operational amplifier (OP) over a third resistor (R₁).The non-inverting input of the operational amplifier (OP) is further connected to ground via a Kon Densator (C) and the inverting input is connected to the output of the operational amplifier (OP) at the same time the output of the filter shell device.
