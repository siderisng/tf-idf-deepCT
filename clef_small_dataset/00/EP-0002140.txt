<ABSTRACT>
an apparatus and method of testing bottles in which  water is supplied at low pressure and is then pressurized  to the test pressure.  the bottle is supported at its bottom  and the top is sealed by a chuck, (140) whereupon low  pressure water is supplied through a central feed tube (15)  and air is vented from the bottle through a venting passage  without breaking the seal.  high pressure water is then supplied through an annular  passage (130) surrounding the tube and if a bottle  breaks or leaks, any reduction in this high pressure in the  annular passage is sensed.
