<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an optical system of two patterns and a photorepéter implementing such a system for the positioning of semiconductor platelets along two coordinate axes. According to the invention, a first pattern constituted by two optical networks (41, 42) arranged according to the two axes of coordinate (x, y) is illuminated by the image of a second pattern comprising two optical networks (11, 12 ), forming reference, and produces diffraction orders. According to a variant, the optical networks consist of a sequence of parallel traits whose thickness and spatial distribution according to a direction (c) parallel to one of the coordinate Zxes (X or Y) are determined by a pseudoalized code; The targets of optical networks (41, 42) of the first pattern being further interrupted periodically according to a direction (R), paral leens at the other axis of coordinates (Y or X) to form a constant optical network. The alignet optical system comprises detection means (5) of the intensity of a predetermined portion of the diffraction orders (R₁) sui vating the direction (R).
