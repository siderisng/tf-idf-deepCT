<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to new polysaccharides ex traits of microbial bodies of Haemophilus influenzae, their process for preparation and the pharmaceutical compositions containing them.Said polysaccharides have an apparent molecular weight of upper 200.000 and consist mainly of galactose, glucose and mannose.They are endowed with antigenic and immunostimulatory properties and has a very good tolerance.
