<ABSTRACT>
For closing bottle-like containers whose neck has an external thread, a locking cap with internal thread, which has an outwardly protruding collar connected to the closure cap. The shear guts should be so stable on the one hand that a premature shearing is avoided during the production, transport and attachment on the container and on the other hand a removal of the closure cap from the container with twisting against-above the collar without excessive force on the container without excessive effective wall is. Particularly suitable for this purpose is a closure cap (10) which has several groups of shear webs. which are defined by their flexibility under divorce and arranged, so that a group (29) of shearstegs is only after a multiply angular rotation of the closure cap (10) relative to the collar (20) as another group (27) of shearsteegen .
