<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to anti-seismic constructs in particular constructions with subsoils for mant anti-atomic shelters.In this type of constructions, the underground base of the construction is constituted by a plurality of independent elements (3, 10) having a mechanical strength such that each of them is likely to resist without damage to the seismic wave ofmaximum intensity.These constructions are used as anti-atomic shelters.
