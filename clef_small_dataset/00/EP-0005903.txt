<TITLE>
process for the manufacture of polyurea foams.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

<ABSTRACT>
a process for the manufature of polyurea foams which  comprises reacting an organic polyisocyanate with at least a  chemically equivalent amount of water in the presence of a  catalyst for the reaction and a triaryl phosphate and/or an  aromatic halogen compound.  the foams are more resistant to the effects of prolonged  exposure to heat or humidity than similar foams containing  tris(halogenoalkyl)phosphates.  
</ABSTRACT>
