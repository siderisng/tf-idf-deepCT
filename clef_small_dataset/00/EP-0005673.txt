<TITLE>
metallic multilayered substrate for the absorption of solar radiation and process for realising such a substrate
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

<ABSTRACT>
the invention relates to a covering procedure which makes it possible to obtain a metal substrate which absorbs solar energy.  this procedure consists in arranging a reflecting interface between two successive layers of metal.  the spectroscopic characteristics of each of these metals are such that the first layer of metal laid is less permeable to infra-red radiation than the layer which comes to cover it, so that the internal surface of the latter constitutes a perfect reflector for the radiation reemitted by the heat-exchanging fluid circulating under such a substrate.  <image>
