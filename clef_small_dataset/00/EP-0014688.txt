<ABSTRACT>
A method for processing illuminated silver coloring bleach materials using new zumenitungen for combined color and silver bleaching is described ben. These zumenitungen contain in addition to a strong acid, a water-soluble iodide, a water-soluble oxidizing agent, an oxidation protection agent and optionally a bleach accelerator also a mixture of 1,4- or 1.2-diazins as bleach catalysts. This mixture is either from at least two bleach cata lysates with redox potentials between + 105 mV and -30 mV wherein the difference of the redox potentials of the catalysts is at least 15 mV, or from at least one bleach catalyst with a redox potential between +60 mV and -30 mV and at least a lead catalyst with a redox potential between -30 mV and -125 mV, wherein the difference of the redox potentials of the catalysts also interviewed at least 15 mV. When processing exposed silver color bleach terials with these zaßeitungen you get pictures with improved color quality.
