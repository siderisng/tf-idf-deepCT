<ABSTRACT>
For a foundation for vibration-generating machines, in particular turbomachines, with deeper from mood, the longitudinal beams (20) and cross member (22) existing made of reinforced concrete have.With which the foundation table top (18) is formed, a hollow profile.This hollow profile is dimensioned in such a way that an axial inertial momentum ment is given with respect to the horizontal bending axis (34), which is larger at least about 3-fold amount as a moment of inertia, which would be sufficient for sufficient strength.This ensures that the successive self-vibration numbers of the foundation system have a large distance that can be maintained by suitable adaptation of the cross sections a safety distance of at least 15%, based on the excitation frequency.
