<ABSTRACT>
1.  process for the preparation of 1-amino-naphthalene-3,6,8-trisulphonic acid by reduction of the alkaline-aqueous solutions of the salts of 8-nitronaphthalene-1,3,6-trisulphonic acid with hydrogen under pressure and in the presence of catalysts, characterized in that cobalt, palladium or platinum sulphides are used as the catalyst and hydrogenation is conducted at a ph value of 7. 5 to 12, at temperatures of 30 degrees to 150 degrees c and under a hydrogen pressure of 5 to 150 bars.
