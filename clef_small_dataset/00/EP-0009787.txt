<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for monoohalogenic tion of alkylbenzene in α-position, in which an excess of an alkylbenzene having at least one water in the α-position comprises with a halogen in the temperature range of about 80 ° C to the boiling point of the enclosed alkylben zol andIn the presence of UV radiation is reacted, the halogenation being carried out so far, which is the concentration of the monohalogenated alkylben zol formed in the reaction mixture withdrawn from the reaction space no more than about 33 mol% of all substances contained in reaction.Some of the substituted mono-halogenated alkylbenzoles produced by the method according to the invention are claimed as new substances.
