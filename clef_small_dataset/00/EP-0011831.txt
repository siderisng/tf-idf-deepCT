<ABSTRACT>
a position shift apparatus having an actuator section for  moving up and down a magnetic head (30) through a screw  feeding, and a control section (200) in which, when the screw  feeding is made in the up direction, it is made by a given  distance up to target position and, when in the down direcÂ­ tion, it is made beyond the target position, and then is again  made by the distance exceeding the target position in the up  direction.  even if the head (30) is positioned on either the  lower or the upper side, the head is screw-fed toward the  target position always in the up direction.
