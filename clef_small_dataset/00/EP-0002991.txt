<ABSTRACT>
Method for depositing materials on marine funds according to a given route and devices for implementing said method.The process is characterized in that: - a self-contained vehicle (16) is moved on the submarine (4) by precisely following said plot, with the vehicle with a hopper (34), - we moveon the surface of the water (8) a vessel (6) by means of an average of said trace, it is poured from said vessel (6) said materials (36) to said hopper (34) by guiding them using themsuspended piping (10) at said vessel (6) and whose lower end (14) is not integral with said hopper, it is active on the proper means of propulsion of said piping to maintain the lower end (14) of said piping above said hopper (34).Application to the installation of electric cables on the seabed.
