<ABSTRACT>
Vitamin E dry powder, which contains a protagonist a protein colloid and a disaccharide prepared by dispersing vitamin E esters in an alkaline earth-ion poor and lactose rich residual fluid of milk sugar recovery, with an addition of 2 to 30 wt.%, based on the dry matter of the residual liquid caseinat, and the dispersion spray dried.The vitamin electric dry powder thus produced is stable, easily dosed and physiologically harmless, so that it can be used arbitrarily.
