<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a holder for calotte bearings and faces the task of simplifying such a holder, which is to be arranged on the use of clamping glasses ver.According to the invention, a ring (33) is provided between the calotte bearing (32) and the bearing bearing element (30) and a ring (33) of an elastomer or felt and dome bearing (32) and bearing bearing element (30) are in the region of this ring (33) with grooves (34;35) provided.The holder according to the invention for calotte bearings is used in particular with pumps with spal tube motors for the formation of the electrolyte fluid in fuel-line aggregates.
