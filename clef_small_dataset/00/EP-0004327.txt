<ABSTRACT>
Method for pigmenting plastics, characterized in that the pigment is used as a pigment of the calcium salt of the monoazo dye of diazotized 2-nitroaniline-4-sulfonic acid and acetoacetyl-o-chloroanilide.This gives colorful green-stitch yellow dyeings of excellent light, migration and blooming fastness and good thermostability.
