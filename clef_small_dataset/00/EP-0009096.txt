<ABSTRACT>
Method for synchronizing NS emissions of NS M fields in an AMRT network using a satellite to M directive beam emission antennas on different descending frequencies.Each station successively emits m packets to the M fields on different frequencies.In the case of four A-D stations, the station 10 detects the TSA address of the transmitting station in each packet and initiates four TM1-TM4 TM1-TM4 tempo associated with A-D station.The starting moment of emission is determined by that of the templates that reaches the first the end of its time.Application to satellite communications.
