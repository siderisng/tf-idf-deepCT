<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a process for preparing compounds of formula wherein, R₁ represents hydrogen, alkyl, aryl or aralcoyl optionally substituted, R₂ and R₃ represent hydrogen or an aryl or heterocyclic alkyl radical and R₄ represents hydrogen, an alkyl radical, a cycloalcoyl, alkoxy carbonyl, carboxy, aryl or heterocyclic, by reaction of a compound of formula with a compound of formula wherein, x is halogen, optionally substituted alkoxy, optionally substituted thioalcoyl or amino possibly substituted; Y represents an optionally substituted alkoxy, possibly substituted thioalcoyl, an optionally substituted amino, or a group of formula-o-cor, where R is an alkyl or aryl radical; or the two X groups and form with the carbon atom on which they are fixed a heterocyclic hexahydro-sp- triazinic, trioxannic or trithianic core, in an inert solvent at a temperature between 0 and 150 ° C and in an anhydrous medium.
