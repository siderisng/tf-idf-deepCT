<ABSTRACT>
Method for producing adhesion reducing coatings to thin-film substrates by applying a solvent-containing mixture made of a) α, ω-polyorganosiloxandiolene, b) Si-H-group-containing organosiloxanes, c) alkyl or alkenyltriacetoxysilanes, d) organic isocyanates having more thanTwo isocyanate residues and E) curing catalysts.The mixture is the reaction product F) of a chloromethyl pololymethylsiloxane of the formula CLCH₂ (CH₃) ₂Si [OSI (CH₃) CH₂Cl] Nosi (CH₃) ₂CH₂Cl, where n can take a value of 2-200, with at most equivalent molar amounts of a primary aminewhose organic residue consists of an alkyl, alkenyl or cycloalkyl radical with 4-18 C atoms.Use of the above mixture for paper coating.
