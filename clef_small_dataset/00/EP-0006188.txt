<ABSTRACT>
In the production of hollow moldings, in particular re tires, made of polyurethane, a deformable membrane (16) is expanded to such a volume that it completely fills the fan cavity (14).When filling the polyurethane into the mold cavity (14), the diaphragm (16) pulls together again, without relevant amounts of air in the mold cavity (14) and thus can penetrate into the hardening polyurethane.
