<ABSTRACT>
method of producing a phenolated resin which can be  used to form heat stable (up to 250Â°) products.  an aromatic hydrocarbon (e. g.  toluene) formaldehyde  condensation resin is prepared.  the low molecular weight  contaminants are removed by a distillation process.  the  residue contains less than 1% low molecular weight material,  and is phenolated to give the final resin.  the resin may be  cured to give a heat stable product.  the phenolated resin may be used to form glasscloth  and asbestos laminates or as a lamp capping cement.
