<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a continuous method of manufacturing dinitroglycolurile by glycoluria nitration using absolute nitric acid.According to the invention the nitration is carried out in at least two cascade reactors, a homogeneous phase being carried out in the first reactor and a heterogeneous phase being carried out in the second reactor.The initial nitra tion ratio of between 3, 5 and 9 is preferably used and a temperature of between 30 and 40 ° C and between 45 and 70 ° C are preferably preferably between 45 and 70 ° C respectively in the first and in the second reactor.Optionally at least one third reactor can be used wherein the reaction mixture is reduced to ordinary temperature.Application: production of Dingu secondary explosive, improved stability with gross state, with excellent rendering.
