<ABSTRACT>
a vaccine composition comprising a sub-cellular anti­ genic fraction of bordetella bronchiseptica protecting swine  against the incidence and severity of atropic rhinitis.  the frac­ tion is obtained by aqueous extraction of a cell paste of a  bordetella bronchiseptica strain at elevated temperature,  digesting the supernatant fluid with rnase and dnase and  dialysing the digested fluid.
