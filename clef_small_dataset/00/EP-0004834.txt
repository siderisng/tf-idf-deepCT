<ABSTRACT>
container closure members of plastics possess certain  advantages over metal-type closures, but frequently there  are form stability problems.  this invention provides measures  of a prophylactic nature for prohibiting form instability  of plastics closures while maintaining desired characteristics  of the closures as, for example, acceptable tightness,  stacking facility, resistance against internal overpressure  and, if necessary, easy openable characteristics.  broadly, the  form stability problem has been solved by giving the plastics  closure an edge flange formable in situ to the final configuration  thereof.
