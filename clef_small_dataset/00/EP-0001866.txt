<ABSTRACT>
a separating device for separating by gravitational segregation  a mixture of a carrier liquid and substances suspended  therein comprises a separator (4) arranged in a  separator housing and having parallel, sloping plates (14), a  stilling space (9) arranged in front of said separator and having  on the top side a first collecting space (15) inteded for  light-weight substances, a feeding space (17) near the inlet  side of the separator, a second collecting space (18) near the  top wall (13) and a third collecting space (19) arranged behind  the separator.  the separator housing is constructed as a pressure  vessel and the first (15) and second (18) collecting spaces  communicate with one another through a conduit (20), the  flow resistance of which exceeds that of the separator.
