<ABSTRACT>
Polybutene-1 processing method to the solid state.The crystalline-shaped polybutene-1 is previously heated at a temperature of between 20 ° and 90 ° C and then forged therein between 8 and 100 kgf / mm2 for a duration of about 1 to 40 seconds.The proceeds according to the invention can be implemented by means of a cold tooling and with a polybutene-1 comprehension level between 65% and 85%.Application to the manufacture of finite objects in polybutene-1, of any size and geometry, such as gears, pipe flanges, fittings, pump rotors, wheels, pulleys, gears, etc...
