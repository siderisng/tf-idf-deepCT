<ABSTRACT>
Method for producing reinforced vulcanizates by vulcanization of natural or art rubber mixtures with novolak resins in the presence of an amine compound as hardener, characterized in that the vul canization in the presence of reactive melamine resins, as by reacting melamine with 0.5 to 6 molesAldehyde are obtained as hardener in a proportion of 0.5 to 120 wt.-%, based on novolak resin, and the phenolic component of the novolak resin from the group phenol, polyvalent multi-core phenols, alkylphous noles, mixtures of alkylphenols is selected with phenol, wherein it may still contain additives of resorcinol, phenylphenol or polyalkyl phenolsand rubber articles and motor vehicle tires produced from these products.
