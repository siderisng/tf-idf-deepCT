<ABSTRACT>
For the preparation of dick film varistors with zinc oxide as a main component, a screen printable varistor paste is prepared from the varistor materials and a binder, which is then applied to an insulating substrate and sintered.According to the invention, a glass-free varistor paste is used, which contains only an organic binder, but no glass frit.
