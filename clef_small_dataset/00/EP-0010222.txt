<ABSTRACT>
For the control plate of a flat plasma screen, a laying structure of the carrier plate with the sequence of glass-metal-glass is proposed.When manufacturing the control holes throughout the control plate, a downstream etching method with different etchants can be used, thereby causing only small accommodations Hen and the control plate retains sufficient mechanical stability at low thickness.When manufacturing the control solder via a laser drilling process, the metallic with telacta (1) performs the heat generated thereby, so that no thermal cracks are possible.
