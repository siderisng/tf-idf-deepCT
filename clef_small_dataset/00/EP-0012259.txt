<ABSTRACT>
For improved composite adhesion in the extrusion layer of flexible substrates, a polyethylene coating resin having a density of 0. 915 to 0. 930 g / cm³ and a melt index (190 ° C / 2. 16 Kp) is used.The coating resin is prepared in two complete back mix autoclaves, which are connected in series such that the entire reaction product of the first reactor is transferred to the two th reactor.The monomer (s) insert current to the second reactor is smaller than the monomer (s) a set stream to the first reactor.The reaction temperature in the first reactor is lower than the reaction temperature in the second reactor.
