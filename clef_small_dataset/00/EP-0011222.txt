<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a carrier catalyst of oxides of the chromium and tungsten and at least one of the oxides of the molybdenum and potassium in the atomic ratio Cr₁W0.10.50.50.0.05 k0⁻0.5 on a porous carrier material and a method for its preparation, wherein a) a dry, porous carrier material until reaching 40 to 80% of the previously determined saturation value with water; b) the pre-soaked carrier material, sequence in any row, at least once with a maximum amount of an aqueous solution of water-soluble compounds of the chromium and gives away from the potassium as well as at least once with a maximum of the potassium as well as at least once with a maximum of complete saturation. separately attached aqueous solution of a water-soluble compound of the tungsten and caused by reassembly of the molybdenum; c) the support material after each impregnation for 2 to 20 hours at 350 to 500k dries and d) finally sintering 0.5 to 12 hours at 550 to 1000 k in the airflow. The support catalyst can be used in the reactor (3) for the production of styrene by oxidative dehydrogenation of ethylbenzene with molecular oxygen in the gas phase.
