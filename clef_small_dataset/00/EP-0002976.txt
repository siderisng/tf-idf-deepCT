<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the continuous machining of helical grooves on a cylindrical piece (20) animated with a translation motion parallel to its axis made of dielectric material.The head (13) comprises a cutting tool (40) traversed by the cylindrical piece (20) resting on a heating plate (35) and animated with a rotational movement around the axis of the room;The chips (179) thus formed are removed by suction in ducts (173) and detached by knives (176).The machined part serves in particular support in cables using optical fibers.
