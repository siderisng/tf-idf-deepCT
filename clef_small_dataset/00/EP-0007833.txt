<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a new highly reactive alumina and its process for preparation.The alumina according to the invention is characterized in that it has an amorphous structure and in that its spec figure surface is greater than 600 m² / g.It is prepared by thermal decomposition of double aluminum and ammonium aluminum and ammonium aluminum (oh) ₂ at a temperature between 200 and 500 ° C.Application to the preparation of Alumi Nium hydroxychlorides of Formula Al₂ (OH) X CL6-X.
