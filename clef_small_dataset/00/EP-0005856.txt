<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the production of aliphatic and cycloaliphatic diamines through a 2-step process.Here, in the 1st stage dialdehydes with monoamines are converted to the corresponding diazomethines.In the second stage, these diazomethines are brought to the reaction with ammonia and hydrogen in the presence of a hydrogenation catalyst.
