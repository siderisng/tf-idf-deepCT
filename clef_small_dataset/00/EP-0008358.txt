<ABSTRACT>
Bis-epoxy dialkoxy alkanes of the general formula in R₁ to R₃ alkyl with 1-4 carbon atoms, as zwis chen products for the preparation of the aroma fabric 2.5-dimethyl-4-hydroxy-2,3-dihydro-Furan- 3-on.The new bispoxy dialkoxy alkanes are obtained by reacting the corresponding α, α'-dihalogen-1,2-diketone at TEM features of -20 to + 100 ° C with an alkali hydroxide or an alkali metal or alkanol.For the manufacture of the 2,5-dialkyl- 4-hydroxy-2,3-dihydro-furan -3-ONE, the bis-epoxy-ηdialkoxy alkanes are heated with strong mineral acids or strong organic acids on TEM temperatures of 60 to 120 °c.
