<ABSTRACT>
1.  corrosion-proof, double-walled contained for a flammable medium (m) consisting of an outer container wall (4) and an inner, fiberglass-reinforced plastic wall (2, 2') spaced at a distance from said outer wall and having a coating of a curing-type plastic resin (3) on the side facing the medium (m) which is at least partially covered with an electrically conductive wire mesh (1, 1') to reduce the danger of explosion and make it possible to check the seal of the plastic wall using standard measuring techniques, whereas the metallic wire mesh (1, 1') in the area of the inner wall and/or the bottom (b) of the surface facing the medium (m) is immersed and mechanically anchored into the curable-type plastic resin (3) at at least its intersection points (1a, 1a') and/or to half of the wire circumference. 
</ABSTRACT>
