<ABSTRACT>
the invention relates to an arrangement for washing cylinders on printing presses in which a washing unit with at least one washing roller can be placed against the cylinder to be washed, wherein the washing unit (6:6') is mounted on a frame (11) which is supported on a cross- beam (4) and can be swivelled in such a way that the washing unit (6:6') can be placed as desired against at least two cylinders.  <image>
