<ABSTRACT>
a miniaturized electric coil having a centre tap (10)  which is constructed from a spiral-like bottom conductor  pattern (1) provided on a substrate, an insulating inter­ mediate layer (11) and a spiral-like top conductor pattern  (16) which is connected to the bottom conductor pattern  via windows (12, 13, 14, 15 and soon) in the intermediate  layer.  a conductor path (23) which is situated in the top  conductor pattern is led out from the interior of the top  conductor pattern to form the tap (10).  at the area of said  conductor path the conductors of the top conductor patt­ ern are locally interrupted, in which connection conductors  in the bottom conductor pattern are connected parallel to  the interruptions so as to ensure an undisturbed current  flow in the top conductor pattern.  
</ABSTRACT>
