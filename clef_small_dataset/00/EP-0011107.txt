<ABSTRACT>
A method for determining an amino or aminosulfonic acid of the general formal I wherein a -Co or -SO₂ and N is 0, 1 or 2, described in urine, plasma or serum.The method essentially consists of a urine, plasma or serum sample with a connection of the type of 4-phenyl spiro [FurAN-2- (3H) 1'-phthalan] -3,3'-Dion,Connecting a combination of the type of 2-methoxy-2,4-diphenyl-3 (2H) -Furanone or with o-phthaldialdehyde and subsequently performs a photometric measurement.The reaction takes place under strong acidic conditions and optionally in the presence of an inert organic solvent.
</ABSTRACT>
