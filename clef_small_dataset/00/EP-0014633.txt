<ABSTRACT>
the invention concerns the preparation of  ultrasonographic images.  the process uses an element containing an imaging  catalyst and a diffusion barrier.  a dye-image generating  material can be included in the catalytic element in order to  obtain a dye image.  the element is placed into contact with a  solution containing a catalyst poison and is exposed to  ultrasound in an image pattern.  the catalyst is selectively  poisoned in ultrasonically exposed areas.  the remaining  unpoisoned catalyst is employed to form a viewable image.   the curves give the cyan dye density against ultrasound  exposure in mw. s/cmÂ².  curves 1 and 2 correspond to  elements placed in the catalyst poison bath respectively for 20  s and 5s before an ultrasonic exposure.  ultrasonographic process carried out under ordinary  room lighting conditions.
