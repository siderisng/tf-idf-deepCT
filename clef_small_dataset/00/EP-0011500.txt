<ABSTRACT>
a detergent-containing water-insoluble closed bag is so  constructed as to open and discharge its contents under  mechanical action in a washing machine in use.  the seams  of the bag may be heat-sealed or cold pressure sealed such  that at least one seam will burst open in use.  the bag mater­ ial may be porous with a pore size selected to minimise dust­ ing of the detergent.  the bag material may include a ther­ moplastic component to enable heat-sealing.  the bag may  contain conventional detergent compositions and or other  fabric treatment materials.  these products give consumer  benefits both by way of improved efficiency in the use of the  detergent compositions and in greater convenience in use.
