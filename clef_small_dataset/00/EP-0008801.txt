<TITLE>
daylight projector.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
a daylight projector is equipped with a fan (4) effecting the removal of the heat generated by the projection lamp from the interior of the housing, and a foil strip (3) guided over the transparent working plate.  the drive of the fan (4) and the displacement of the foil strip (3) are effected by the same motor (5), in such a manner that, selectively according to the desired direction of displacement, the axes (22; 23) of each respective one of the two spools (1; 2) carrying the foil strip (3) can be coupled to the motor shaft.  <image>
