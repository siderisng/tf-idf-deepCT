<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a supply cathode, in particular metal capillary cathode, for electrical exposure vessels with a front-side porous emission fabric carrier disc (2) made of highly melting metal.In this supply cathode, the lateral emission (Störemis Sion) should be prevented.The invention provides that the emissions carrier disc (2) has a non-porous outer shell surface (1) having a higher electron outlet work as the external active surface (3) of the EMIS sion carrier disc (2).An inventive cathode according to the invention is used as a MK cathode at hiking field.
