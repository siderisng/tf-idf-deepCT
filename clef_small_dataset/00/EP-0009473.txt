<ABSTRACT>
1.  heat-postformable, decorative synthetic resin moulded laminate which is made up of several papers impregnated with duroplastic binding agents, and pressed together, one or more of which papers constitute core layers, which are papers impregnated with phenolic resin of the post-forming quality, characterized in that the core layer papers are smooth papers of high absorbency with a tearing length of less than 4000 m.  and which, upon their impregnation, have received a sufficiently high application of resin to achieve the desired formability, in the range of 35 to 150 % wt.  solid resin in relation to the gross paper weight used.
