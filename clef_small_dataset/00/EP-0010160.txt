<ABSTRACT>
Pads for fast retrospective detection of an overdose of harmful gases are important for occupational safety in the chemical industry.Such plaqules have an indicator (4) which discolves when the gas is exposed.The visual evaluation of the indicator is substantially facilitated by the fact that the indicator surface is divided by different cover with MEM Branen (2) and (3) into several measurement fields with gradient sensitivity.Due to a separate or combined visual consideration of the individual indicator fields is then a quick assignment to predetermined doses ranges possible.
