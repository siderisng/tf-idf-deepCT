<ABSTRACT>
The invention as a new substance relates to the 4-spectinomycylamine of the formula whose physiologically acceptable acid addition salts with inorganic or organic acids and their preparation according to known methods.The Spectinomycin of Formula I and its acid addition salts act against a large number of gram-positive and gram-proof bacteria and are characterized by a large therapeutic width, they are excellent for prophylaxis and chemotherapy of local and systemic infections in human and veterinary medicine.The Specinomycin can be prepared, for example, by reducing a spectinomycin oxime, optionally provided with protective groups, whereby the protecting group are subsequently rotated again.
