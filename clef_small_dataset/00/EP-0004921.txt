<ABSTRACT>
A removal compartment, which is intended in particular for refrigerators, has a substantially horizontal storage area (2) for a plurality of same or similar designed objects and is with lateral guide walls (3) or - bars as well as the storage area beyond the level of the storage surface. Favorit the front stop wall or - strip (4) limited on the removal side. In order to achieve a fast and convenient removal and direct access to the goods to be taken, a rear stop (7) is not viewed, which is displaceable in the direction of the removal side and from this path and can be acted upon with a force acting in the direction of the removal side . Through this conscription, the objects to be taken are always tangible on the removal side and the objects behind it are pressed in the course of further removal with the help of the force-operated rear stop on the front.
