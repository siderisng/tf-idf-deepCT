<ABSTRACT>
A method of producing laminated glass, constituted by n glass plates and n-1 plastic films (N ≧ 2).The process is characterized in that a sandwich (4) is produced by alternately having a glass plate (8) and a plastic film (10), after coating the faces of the glass plates or plastic films forconstitute glass-plastic interfaces in the sandwich (4), in that it applies to said sandwich an overpressure less than or equal to 2 bar, and in that simultaneously a temperature T below the melting temperature of said plastic film ((10), for a time sufficient to obtain the prepolymerization of said adhesive material (12), and in that the said sandwich (4) at a temperature less or equal to said temperature T for a period of less than 16 hours.Application to the realization of windshield for motor vehicles.
