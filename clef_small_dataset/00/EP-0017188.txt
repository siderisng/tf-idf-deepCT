An anode for the alkali lorry electrolysis according to the amalgam method is described in which the unit of lattice or mesh-shaped planar titanium anode surface (1), current distribution rails (2), and a primary conductor rail (3) with theCopper bolts (4) can be releasably connected as a power supply, that the copper bolt (4) in its lower region has a thread (6) and below the thread (6) a cone (5), the bridge a threaded nut and a conical bore (5) for receiving the conical portion (5) of the copper pin (4), and copper bolts (4) and bridge (3) are screwed to power and positively.
