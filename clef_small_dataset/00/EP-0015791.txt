<ABSTRACT>
The method consists, for an engine whose distribution diagram comprises a cover phase (II) between the lifting of at least one escape valve (A2A, A2B curves) and at least one AD valvemission (curve B2) to close the admission valve in the vicinity or slightly before the low neutral point of the piston (PMB) and to close the exhaust valve (RFE) at the end of the fixed closure of theAdmission Valve Sion.This method makes it possible, in particular, to lower the premises at the end of compression with high loads and to increase the effective compression ratio to the low charging and start-up.The invention applies in particular to diesel engines.
