<ABSTRACT>
Cyclic method for obtaining high purity alumina by chlorical etching (b) of a silicoaluminous material, said process comprising, in a first step, the precipitation (c) of not more than 75% of the alumina present in theliquor from laattaque in the form of hexahydrate aluminum chloride, later decomposed by heating, then, in a second step, the processing (I) by HC1 gas from the liquor coming out of the first step to ensure the precipitation of A1₂O₃.solution in the form of hexahydrate aluminum chloride which is recycled in the liquor coming out of the attack, while the hydrochloric liquor (L8) coming out of the second step, impoverished in A1₂O₃ but rich in impurities, is treated with a sulfuric liquorrecycling to precipitate impurity sulphates.
