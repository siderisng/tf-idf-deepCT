<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a non-polluting oxyhydrochlorination method. According to the invention, the waste gaseous effluents resulting from an oxyhydrochlorination process are purified by catalytic oxidation in the presence of a catalyst constituted by platinum and / or iridium deposited on a particular substructure of alumina. Crystallographic structure and wash before rejection in the atmosphere. The method of the invention is more particularly applicable to a waste effluent containing mainly carbon oxide, carbon dioxide, ethylene, dichloro 1-2 ethane, and at least one chlorinated hydrocarbon included in the group consisting of by chloroform, carbon tetrachloride, ethyl chloride, dichloro 1-1 ethane, trichloro 1-1-1 and 1-1-2 ethane, tetrachloro 1-1-2-2 and 1-1 -1-2 ethane, vinyl chloride, 1-1 and 1-2 ethylene dichloro, trichloroethylene, perchlorethylene and chloral.
