<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to a fitting device of food intended in particular to grill them, frying or braising them.It consists essentially of an open container (3) of generally prismatic shape, located below a heat source (2) and animated by an alternative transport movement around a substantially horizontal axis and whose bottom (6) In the form of a dihedral.In addition, the relative layout of the container and the heat source, the particular provision thereof as well as the mode of production of the oscillating movement are characteristic of the invention.The device according to the invention makes it possible to cook foods of any kind, especially meats or vegetables, of varied sizes and shapes, with or without adding fat.It allows, in particular, the preparation of light fries apples avoiding their immersion in the oil.
