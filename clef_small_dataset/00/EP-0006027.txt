<TITLE>
container for transporting fluid materials and method of using the same.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
a method of and a means for use in packaging bulk  quantites of a fluent material for the ready transportation  thereof is proposed which involves the provision of a  cardboard container (11) having dividers (12) therein separating  the container into individual and adjacent compartments  (13) each containing a respective filled, flexible bag  (14) of said material, the bags (14) substantially filling the  respective compartments (13), and the dividers (12) and  container (11) being such as to sustain the dynamic and  static loads applied thereto during transportation.
