<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for producing an original, in which a recording layer is imped image and grid and is developed to a relief image.The original produces a metal coating used as a template for embossing duplicates of the original.The recording layer is in principle expanded by color extractor templates for a particular color, wherein first a grid exposure over the entire image area up to a uniform relief depth successes and a lattice-free exposure is followed by the different color extract templates.
