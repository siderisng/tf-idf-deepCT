<ABSTRACT>
motor-cycle of the type provided with a front engine (4)  and a rear transmission (5) on a body frame (1).  an intermediate  shaft (18) is placed between a crank shaft (8) attached to  the engine and a driving shaft (13) of the transmission (5).  the  intermediate shaft (18) is provided at its low ends with an a. c.   generator (19) and a controller (20) for an ignition circuit, e. g. ,  a pulse generator or a contact breaker.  according to this  arrangement, the width of the motor-cycle is limited with  respect to a part of its crank shaft portion, even if it includes a  multi-cylinder type engine and a relatively long crank shaft.
