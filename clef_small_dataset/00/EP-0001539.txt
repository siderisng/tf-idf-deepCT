<ABSTRACT>
a method for the production of a fibrous mat (29)  which comprises mixing artificial mineral fibres (11) and  cellulosic fibres (12) preparing an aqueous suspension  therefrom, dewatering said suspension on a wire, pressing  and drying the formed sheet, and adding kinding agents in  the form of a latex emulsion after the sheet has been dried  (39) to a dry content of between 60 and 90%.
