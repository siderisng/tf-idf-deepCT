<ABSTRACT>
This torsion shock absorber comprises two by coaxial ties, namely a hub (10) and a hub veil (11), rotary mounted relative to each other, within the limits of a determined angular travel, andagainst springs (16).According to the invention, it further comprises at least one locking member (22,40,55,66,74) which is sensitive to the centrifugal force, and which is reversibly movable between a position for which, without effect,It leaves a free action to the springs (16) and a position for which, supporting the hub veil (11) on the hub (10), it turns out the springs (16).Application including clutch friction for motor vehicles.
