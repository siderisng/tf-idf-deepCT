<ABSTRACT>
In a device for automatic unhooking of perpendicular pendant glass panes from the holding tongs (2) following the biasing treatment, the glass sheets raising the glass panes consist of grippers which grain the glass panels (1) on their surface.The grippers consist in particular of two gripping jaws (31) provided with retaining pins (35), which clamp the glass panes (1) between them, lift after grasping, and place them by pivoting into the horizontal position on a horizontal conveyor.With the device, glass slices of any shape can be automatically unhooked from the holding tongs.
