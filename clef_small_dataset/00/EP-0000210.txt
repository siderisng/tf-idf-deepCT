<ABSTRACT>
It is proposed a self-healing electrical capacitor, in particular for AC voltage, in which the problem of the field strength-dependent layer degradation is released at thin aluminum layers.For this purpose, a thin lining layer is used, which contains a small, uniformly distributed copper content.By incorporating copper into an aluminum coating layer, there is virtually completely prevented at 50 Hz and medium effective nominal frame thicknesses over 45 V / μm observed.
