<ABSTRACT>
N- (2-chloro-4-pyridyl) enhances of general formula (1) wherein R₁ is a hydrogen atom or a lower alkyl group, R₂ one, optionally substituted by a lower alkyl or alkoxy group, a hydroxy group or a halogen atom, aromatic group and xOxygen or sulfur material, as well as their acid addition salts.These compounds are suitable as plant growth regulators.They can be prepared by reacting a corresponding 2-chloro-4-amino pyridine with a corresponding phenyl isocyanate or b) reacting a corresponding 2-chlorine-4-pyridylisocyanate with a corresponding aniline or c) a corresponding 2-chlorine-isonikotinoylazide reacted with a corresponding aniline.
</ABSTRACT>
