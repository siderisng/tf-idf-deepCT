<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a diagnostic means for detecting leukocytes in body fluids consists of an absorbent carrier which is impregnated with a sulfonphthalein ester suitable as a chromogen, a suitable buffer substance and optionally with additional lichen, commonly used auxiliaries.The invention further relates to the use of sulfonphthalein esters for producing the inventive diagnostic agent.Furthermore, the invention relates to new, as chromogenic sulfonphthale nesters and their preparation.The preparation of the inventions sulfonphthalein esters is carried out by converting sulfonphthalene into a manner known per se with corresponding acids or suitable reactive derivatives thereof.
