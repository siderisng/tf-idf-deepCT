<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a device for automatic wear of wear in a brake with floating machines, acting on either side of a disc, one of which has the so-called rear cap (2) carries guides (7), parallel to the axis of the disc and, which pass through the other jacket, called the front cap (1) and a cylinder head (13).This device is characterized in that it comprises, on each guide, to limit the retreat of the cylinder head and the cap before the stop (19, 20) on which, the front cap and the cylinder head rely via the intermediarycorners (21, 22).Application to the spring and loosening brake brakes.
