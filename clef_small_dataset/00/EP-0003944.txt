<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to methods and devices for climating greenhouses by selectively absorbing solar light.A greenhouse according to the invention, having a transparent coverage (2), comprises sensors (4), placed under the cover which are part of a closed circuit (4, 7, 8, 9, 10, 11a, 11b) inwhich circulates a suspension in the water of a product P non-miscible with water and heavier than the sediment water, which is transparent to the u rays.v.and which absorbs the visible spectrum and the i.r.The reservoir (9) is a sedimentation tank equipped with several outputs (13) each provided with a circulation pump (10).A control device (37) acting on the pumps (10) makes it possible to vary the concentration of the product P in the suspension and to modulate the absorption coefficient of the visible spectrum.An application is the cultivation of plants under an artificial climate.
