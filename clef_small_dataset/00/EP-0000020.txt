<ABSTRACT>
The water content of electrically non-conductive powders, granules, pastes and fiber materials can be measured on the basis of microwave absorption.A frequency-modulated microwave oscillator feeds a closed multimode measuring resonator in a transmission arrangement, which is charged with the sample.The sample filled into a container (3) fills the multimode resonator (22) almost completely.The sample container is rotatable about its longitudinal axis.As a result, a largely homogeneity of the field distribution in the sample is achieved, so that even in bulk seal fluctuations, accurate measurements are possible.
</ABSTRACT>
