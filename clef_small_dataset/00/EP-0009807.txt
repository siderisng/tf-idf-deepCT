<ABSTRACT>
The method for the columns of heavy coal coating materials provides for hydrogenating pretreatment and subsequent thermal cleavage of the insert.The hydrogenation is carried out under conditions, in which only part of the insert is converted to easily boiling composes.The hydrogenation product is decomposed into a lightweight and a heavy fraction, wherein only the heavy fraction is thermally split for the purpose of obtaining olefins.The lighter components of the hydrogen product can be evaluated as gasoline, diesel fuel or fuel oil.
