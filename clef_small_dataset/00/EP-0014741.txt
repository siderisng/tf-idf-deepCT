<ABSTRACT>
Many machines should be switched off at the latest after a predetermined number of revolutions and not only drive against an end stop.The required switching device has two side-by-side cam discs (10,11), whose camshafts (12, 12a) are associated with each other via a translation gear.The switching contacts (15) are only operated when the switching surfaces (10a, 11a) of both cam discs (10, 11) are in the switching position and together via a rocker arm (7) and a push rod (5) the lifting of the contacts (15)cause.The button (11a) of the slowly rotating cam disk (11) has a latching point, and the button (10a) of the rapidly rotating cam (10) has such a length as required for the time for proper switching.
</ABSTRACT>
