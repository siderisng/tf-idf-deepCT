<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the field of connectors for optical monofiber.In the case where the monofiber (1) is covered with a flexible sheath or envelope (3), the attachment in a connector tip may suffer from a bad concentration.According to the invention, there is placed around the fiber a deformable cylinder (4), on which balanced forces (P) are exerted in four perpendicular directions, transmitted to the flexible sheath (3) of the fiber by four jaws (31), which cause spontaneous refocusing of the fiber by creep thereof.The cylinder is then placed in the tip.Applications include plastic sheath optic fibers for telecommunications.
