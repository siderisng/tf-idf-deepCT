<ABSTRACT>
a method of making inserts (12) suitable for tool or  drill bits which includes the step of fragmenting, e.  g.  by  means of a laser beam, an abrasive compact (10) in a  plurality of discrete, non-segmental fragments (12).  the  inserts (12) may have a variety of shapes, some of which  are new, such as conical, truncated conical or truncated  pyrimidal.  the inserts (12) are particularly useful for  wire-drawing die blanks and core or oil-well drill bits.
