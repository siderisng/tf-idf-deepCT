<ABSTRACT>
a pin seam construction joining together the ends of a  multi-layer woven papermakers dryer felt, wet felt, or for­ ming fabric comprises at each end of the felt or fabric (10),  a first row of loops (20) formed at the ends of the warp  yarns (16) of one layer (12) of the felt or fabric and a sec­ ond row of loops (26) formed at the ends of the warp yarns  (22) of a second layer (14) of the felt or fabric.  the loops  (20) of the first row at one end are interleaved or meshed  between the loops (20) of the first row at the other end of  the felt or fabric.  the loops (26) at one end are similarly  interleaved or meshed with the loops (26) at the other end  and two pins (30, 28) are threaded through the interleaved  rows of loops thus forming a double pin seam construction  which is stronger than the usual single pin construction.  
</ABSTRACT>
