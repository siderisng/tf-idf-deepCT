<ABSTRACT>
granulated welding flux is supplied under fluid pressure  from a reservoir mounted at a convenient location for reload­ ing.  the reservoir is connected to an elected vacuum tank.   recovered excess flux is also brought up to the vacuum tank,  both sources of flux maintaining a predetermined level of flux  within the vacuum tank.  a conduit from the vacuum tank pro­ vides gravity feed of flux from the tank to a lower divide box  from which a plurality of conduits deliver flux to the traveling  welding arc from behind and in front of the arc between the tip  of the welding electrode and the work piece.
