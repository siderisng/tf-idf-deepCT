<ABSTRACT>
A message cable used for emergency purposes is additionally utilized for a light signal device, with emergency call columns (NRS) to blink in the course of this cable.From central locations (eg, to) A rectifier and charging unit is fed via dining circle of the message cable in each emergency call column.About the code signals sent according to the target cable are evaluated in the emergency call columns by means of signaling devices.During the flashing operation, this signal device is separated from the rectifier and charging unit in each flashing emergency call column, and all electrical devices of the light signal device are separated from the dining circuit in each non-flashing emergency call column.This ensures that the over-loading energy during the flashing operation is exploited exclusively for feeding the lightning device and its signal lamps.
