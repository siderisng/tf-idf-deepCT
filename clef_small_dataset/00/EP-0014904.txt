<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to paper coating compositions which are 100 wt. Parts of a finely divided pigment 5 to 25 wt. Parts of a copolymer A having a glass transition temperature of to + 50 ° C in the form of aqueous dispersions, 0.5 to 10 wt. Parts of at least one copolymer B made of a high molecular weight, carboxyl group-having copoly merisate and optionally 0.1 to 2 wt. Parts of an optical brightener containing the copolymer B as characteristic monomer units a) 20 to 70 wt. % hydroxy-C₂- to C₄-alkyl acrylate or methacrylate, b) 10 to 50 wt. % Acrylic acid, methacrylic acid, malic acid and / or maleic rack best and C) 5 to 50 wt. % acrylonitrile, methacrylonitrile, esters of acrylic acid or methacrylic acid with monovalent C₁-to C₄ alcohols and vinyl esters of saturated C₁-C₄-carboxylic acids. The paper cushioning masses have a high water tension. The copolymers B cause an activation of the optical brighteners.
