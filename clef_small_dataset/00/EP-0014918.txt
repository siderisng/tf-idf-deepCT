<ABSTRACT>
Device ejecting ink drops for making a graphic design through a set of ejection holes selectively controlled by electrical or bright pulses.It comprises a first plate (1) with a large number of holes (4) disposed near the surface of the print media (5).a second plate (2) disposed at the low distance of the first plate (1) delimits with it a chamber (3) wherein the ink to be deposited is heated locally on control of the electrical or luminous means for each hole (4) or multiple holes (4) so as to make a variable (39) or fixed graphics (40).Application: Inkjet printers especially for postal postage.
