<ABSTRACT>
1.  process for the produktion of 1,1-difluoro-2-chloroethylene by dehydrohalogenating 1,1-difluoro-1,2-dichloroethane, characterised in that 1,1-difluoro-1,2-dichloroethane is reacted with basically reacting substances in the presence of water at temperatures between 50 and 130 degrees c and under elevated pressure and in the absence of surface active substances, the 1,1-difluoro-2-chloroethylene which forms being directly removed from the reaction mixture.
