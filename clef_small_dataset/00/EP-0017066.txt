<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to new N, N-dimethyl-o-pyrazolylcarbamic acid esters of the formula in which R, R¹ and R² have the meaning given in the description.It is obtained when using 5-hydroxy pyrazoles with N, N-dimethyl-carbamic acid halides 5- hydroxy-pyrazoles with phosgene and then with DIME thylamine or N, N-dimethyl-O-(3-alkylthiomethyl pyrazole (5) Yl)-Carbamic acid esters with the equimolarene of hydrogen peroxide or N, N-dimethyl-O-(3-alkylthiomethyl-pyrazolyl (5) Yl) -Carbamine acid ester with M-chloro-perbenzoic acid.The new N, N-dimethyl-o-pyrazolyl-carbamic acid esters of the formula (I) are characterized by high efficacy as pestic fighters.
