<ABSTRACT>
an improved blanker circuit for a conventional radio  receiver having a blanker gate (20) and blanking circuitry  (30) for blanking the receiver processed signal is described.   the improvement comprises:   sense means (24) for sensing the relative signal to  noise ratio of the receiver processed signal; and   inhibitor means (32, 34, 36) for inhibiting blanking  action for a predetermined time period in response to said  sense means sensing a predetermined degradation in said  signal to noise ratio.
