<ABSTRACT>
Vinylidene polyfluoride composite material and polyurethane obtained by coextrusion of a polyfluoride of vinylidene having a range of viscosity apparent to 200 ° C well defined and object of claim, and a thermoplastic polyurethane.The coextrusion is carried out by means of a conventional apparatus, the flow of polymers at the output of extruders being brought together at the latest lips in the die.It is recommended to interpose a flow distributor between the die and the extruders.
