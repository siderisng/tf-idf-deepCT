<ABSTRACT>
Simply handled microlenses are prepared by mounting rotationally symmetrical deaf gene in a glass plate, then with a deposition process zetus from the vapor phase glassy layers are deposited on the plate until the recesses are completely filled, after which the thickness of the coated glass plate on the original or onesmaller value is reduced.Two lenses produced in this way are then combined to a lens by connecting the embedded lenses together with their flat sides.
