<ABSTRACT>
photocrosslinkable, organic solvent-soluble polymers  having a tg greater than 200°c are disclosed.  the polymers  contain sufficient radiation-sensitive crosslinkable units to  render the polymers insoluble in organic solvents on exposure  to radiation to which the crosslinkable units are sensitive.   also disclosed are color imaging devices having a radiation-sensitive  surface and superposed thereon a layer containing a  color filter array prepared from the described photocrosslinkable  polymers.  the polymers are capable of receiving heat-transferable  dyes.
