<ABSTRACT>
The metal cabinet shown is constructed from a frame comprising four tubular uprights 1, square section, and two frames 2.3, of rectangular or square shape, whose sides are tubes having the same square section as the amounts.1. In the angles of each frame 2.3, the adjacent tubular elements 4, 6 are assembled by means of soldered oblique gussets, allowing the assembly with the uprights engaged in frames of the frames, by means of screws 13.The side panels 16 are locked on the roof-lid frame 23 whose folded edges 24 cover the top of the panels.
