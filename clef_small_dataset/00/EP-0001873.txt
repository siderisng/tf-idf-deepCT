<TITLE>
method of producing polyisoprenoid compounds by carbon-carbon bond formation.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>
takeda chemical industries ltd jp<sep>takeda chemical industries, ltd.  <sep>takeda chemical industries, ltd.1-1, doshomachi 4-chomechuo-ku, osakajp<sep>

<ABSTRACT>
a polyisoprenoid compound is prepared by reacting a  compound r¹ch₂y wherein y is lower alkylsulfonyl or arylsulfonyl,  and r¹ is isoprenoid or polyisoprenoid, with a xch₂r²  compound wherein x is halogen and r² has the same meaning  as r¹, in a polar solvent and in the presence of sodium  hydride, potassium hydride, sodium tertiary-alkoxide, potassium  tertiary-alkoxide, sodium amide or potassium amide, to  obtain a compound    which, in turn, is subjected to a reductive desulfonylation of  the group y, whereby a compound r¹ch₂ch₂r² is obtained.  
</ABSTRACT>
