<ABSTRACT>
in a test cycle, either at the end of a short copy  production run or during an interruption in a longer copy  production run, a xerographic copier is arranged to produce  a toned test patch (30) in an otherwise cleaned imaging area  (28) of the photoconductor (26).  the optical density of both  the cleaned and test areas are sensed by an electro-optic  system to provide output signals indicating the respective  densities.  these signals are compared in a control circuit  primarily to provide output signals indicating requirement  for replenishment of the developer mix with toner.
