<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to carrier catalysts with inert carrier material, which is first treated with a base-containing aqueous solution, and then soaked with a noble metal salt solution.The invention further relates to a method for the manufacture of such catalysts and their use, for example for hydrogenations.
