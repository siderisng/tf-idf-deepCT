<ABSTRACT>
In a key-shaped steering wheel for motor vehicles, a deformation body (4) is arranged between the steering wheel wreath (2) and that opposed to recessed steering wheel hub (1).Thus, in the case of deformation of the deformation body, the delay is approximately constant, the deformation body (4) consists of a metal wire knit, which is foamed with a homogeneous plastically deformable plastic.
