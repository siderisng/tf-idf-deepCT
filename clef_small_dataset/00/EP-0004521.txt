<ABSTRACT>
slab for the execution of various walls or surfaces removable, characterized by the fact that it comprises a plurality of clips (5) arranged on one of its faces, preferably, according to two or more more alignments, and openings(10) crossing it in its thickness and opening between the branches (5a) of said clips;An opening being disposed at the right of each clip.This slab can be instantly positioned on a removable frame formed of a tube assembly (7), simply pressing and pressure on said frame, for example for the realization of a removable floor.It can be employed in large numbers, under reduced footprint, without risk of breakage of clips.
