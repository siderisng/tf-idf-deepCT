<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a device for heating open material melt containers in tubs or basins, in which an inert gas is directed in the circulation by gas-side closed parts of the open bath in the form of shafts and a heater, the gas in the bath fluid in each longitudinal and or transverse side of the tub or the pelvis arranged vertical shafts (10) is introduced there, wherever a trigger channel (15) is peeled off above the shafts. Here, the lifetime of the inert gas into the bath liquid can be increased, for which at least one ceramic, ranging into the bath fluid wall (11) of the vertical shafts is provided with at least one bore or a slot (20) whose upper end with the Gas supply line (19) and whose lower end opens into the vertical bay or provided with means, which direct the gas in the bay.
