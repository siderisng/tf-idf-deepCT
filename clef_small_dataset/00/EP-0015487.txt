apparatus for the utilization of solar energy.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
A device for harvesting the solar heat can absorb and focus solar beams and use the focused sunbeams as heat energy for heating and cooling purposes.The device comprises a sun ray reflector (6) in the form of a para boloid and a vertical shaft (19) extending perpendicular to the center of the reflector (6).Horizontal waves (21) are mounted below the reflector (6) to keep this rotatable.A heat collecting unit (11) is located within the reflector above the vertical shaft (19), while a circulatory guide tube (12) extends for the liquid along the heat collecting unit (11).
</ABSTRACT>
