<ABSTRACT>
the hydraulic trepanning machine is for taking core samples of roadways etc. , and partic.  for drilling holes in concrete drain pipes.  it comprises a frame (1) adjustably mounted on the boom sections (2, 3) of an excavator, and with means for positioning it as desired on the pipe etc.  to be drilled (b).  a trepanning drill bit (40, 41) moves up and down in the frame, being driven by a hydraulic motor and mounted with it on a slide.  the height of the slide is positively controlled.  the frame can be mounted on the excavator by means of a ball-joint (20, 21).  the ball can be mounted on a protrusion on top of the frame, with a spindle passing through it on which a support hinges freely.  the support in turn hinges on the excavator boom.
