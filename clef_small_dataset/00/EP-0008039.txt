<ABSTRACT>
Means of oxidative dyeing of hair with a content of 6-amino-3-methyl phenol, also in the form of its salts or phenolates, as color component.The color component is contained in these agents in an amount of about 0.01 to 2.0 weight percent, alone or mixture with conventional developer and couplersub punching.If a mixture is present, the 6-amino-3-methyl phenol is preferably used in the molars under shot relative to these developer and coupler substances.The oxidation reaction of the 6-amino-3-methyl phenol associated with an intensive yellow color is generally not influenced by container developer and coupler components.
