<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to an arrangement for driving a printer carriage (2) in remote or data write machines.At the printer car (2), a flexible traction means (4) is attached, which is driven simultaneously by two drive motors (5, 6).The drive motors (5, 6) are similarly designed and driving out of the pressure area arranged deflection rollers (7, 8).As drive motors (5, 6), stepper motors are preferably used.The stepper motors can be offset by a half detent position and alternately driven, so that always one of the two stepper motors are operating in the optimum torque range.
