<ABSTRACT>
Long chain ammonium polyphosphate is a good additive in lubricants and coolants in metalworking, which are carried out under extremely high pressures and temperatures.The polyphosphate chain should contain 50 to 1000 phosphorus atoms.Up to 20% of the ammonium group Pen are replaceable by hydrogen.The additive is added to the lubrication and coolant in an amount of 0.1 to 30% by weight.
