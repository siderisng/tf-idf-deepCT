<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for removing saturated liquid residual hydrocarbons made of polyolefins by means of water and steam, wherein a) the hydrocarbon-containing polyolefin into an aqueous, emulsifier-containing solution whose surface tension is at 20 ° C against air about 50 - 68 dyn / cm,and the mixture is converted by stirring into a homogeneous dispersion, and b) initiates the dispersion into the upper part of a column equipped with about 5 - 30 sieve floors and overflow browsers and treated hot water vapor in countercurrent with about 100 - 120 ° C.
