<ABSTRACT>
an engine constructed with a hemispherically shaped  combustion chamber (40) having inlet (66) and exhaust valves  (68) flush mounted in the surface (38) of the chamber (40) with  centerlines (73) perpendicular to the chamber surface (38) and  intersecting the cylinder bore centerline (76) at a common  point (78); the valve train components for each valve all acting  in a single plane (116) and having centerlines (72, 110 and 112)  that together converge towards a common point (114) adja­ cent the cylinder bore enterline (76), the valve train planes  (116) of each pair of inlet (66) and exhaust valves (68) being  canted with respect to each other and to the camshaft axis  (118), a single overhead camshaft (104) being centrally located  between the inlet and exhaust valves 66 and 68.
