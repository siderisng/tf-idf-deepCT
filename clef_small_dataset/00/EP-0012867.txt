<ABSTRACT>
A piezoelectric strain / force transducer is described, which is fastened by preferably only a clamping screw (31) on a test body or machine part (1). The power transmission member (32, 34) of the receiver comprises two spaced bearing surfaces (36, 49) which are non-positively applied to the surface of the machine part (1) and experiencing a distance change when changing the machine part. As a result, a feather elastic member (33) tunable to a desired sensitivity of the transducer is stretched and a set in the PIE zoelectric plates (37, 38) held in the forceber (32, 34) is subjected to a corresponding thrust force. The piezoelectric plates (37, 38) are cut or polarized to respond only to shear forces pie zoelectrically. The resulting piezoelectric charge is a measure of the elongation or mechanical bean proofing of the machine part and can be used for monitoring or controlling machines or components, in particular those which are subjected to cyclic loads.
