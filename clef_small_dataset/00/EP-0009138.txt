<ABSTRACT>
A metallic insert and decaying device for bone marrow jacks consists of a guide tube (1) with detachable stop elements (11 and 7) at both ends and a stroke sleeve (6), which is arranged concentrically and slidably to guide tube (1).The stroke sleeve (6) has a specific density greater than 10 g / cm³ and is a sintered composite body, noble tungsten with bindemetal additives or consists of a steel jacket filled with metal powder or granules.The end of the guide tube (1) facing the bone marrow tail is deposited in two consecutive longitudinal portions (2 and 3), with the pipe diameter in each case decreasing.The end portion of the inner longitudinal portion (2) is provided with a thread for screwing in the bone marshaw.A stop element is formed as a one-stop sleeve (7) which rests at the stroke operation on the Kno Chenmarknagel.
</ABSTRACT>
