<ABSTRACT>
Method for producing slot pipes for a tubular welding machine with which the conversion time of the Turbiegenvorrichtung auf be shortened and the structural design can be faded.For this purpose, the entire area of the sheet curved to down to the sheet curvature of the smallest slot pipe in a series of dimensional steps (3) is subdivided and from the total number of dimensional steps (3), in accordance with the respective pipe diameter to be formed, a suitable group portion in the contour bending device is claw, whereinThis will then be carried out without adjustment by the band.The formation of the contour bending device for carrying out the method is also described and illustrated.
