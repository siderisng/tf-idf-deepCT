<ABSTRACT>
Gas discharge display device with a sheath (8) containing a front panel (7), a back plate (4) and a interior of the shelter in two spaces (1). The control plate (1) carries on its two sides each separately controllable, row or column conductor of a matrix forming electrode railways and is broken together with these paths in the crossing points of the matrix. In order to hold the front panel (7) against the control plate (1) at a low distance of approx. 1 mm, it is formed according to the web-shaped spacer elements (24) with a substantially constant wall thickness. The spacer elements extend in the plane of the control plate (1), are passed on the openings (12) of the control plate (1) and portions are alternately substantially parallel or at an angle to the matrixle tern facing the front panel (7). 4). Method for producing a spacer structure with glass, whereby suitable dimensioned glass tapes are passed into a deformable state between two gears. The sheath body may, if necessary, finally strive (19, 21), ribs (22, 23) or grooves (15) generated in a pressing process. The invention is particularly useful in a plasma duty type in which a luminous screen is excited by high-energy electrons for light generation.
