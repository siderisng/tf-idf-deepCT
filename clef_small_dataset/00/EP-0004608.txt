<TITLE>
4-acylamido-hexahydropyrimidines, and process for their preparation.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

<ABSTRACT>
novel 4-acylamidohexahydropyrimidines are prepared by reaction of hexahydropyrimidines with carboxylic acid amides at temperatures above 100 deg c.  the 5,5-disubstituted 4-ureidohexahydropyrimidines which can be prepared by the process of the invention are useful starting materials for the preparation of dyes, pesticides and auxiliaries in the textile chemistry, varnishing resin chemistry and wood materials industry.
