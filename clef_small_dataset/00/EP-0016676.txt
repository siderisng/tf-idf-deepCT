<ABSTRACT>
Process for preparing magnesium alkylphenates consisting: 1. Prepare a suspension of "active" magnesium oxide in methanol;2. To contact said suspension with a mixture containing: - an alkylphenol carrying one or more alkyl substituents in C₆-C₆₀;- a dilution oil;- and possibly a boiling point heavy alcohol between 100 and 200 ° C, the ratio number of active magnesium oxide moles / number of patterns OH phenoli being between 0.25 and 2. 3. and toCarbonate under pressure The medium obtained with carbon dioxide.Magnesium alkylphenates thus obtained can be used as detergent-dispersant additives for lubricating oils according to amounts between 1 and 8% of the weight of oils.
