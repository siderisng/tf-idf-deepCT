<ABSTRACT>
aqueous vinyl chloride copolymer dispersions;The comonomers are vinyl acetate and ethylene, and optionally olefinically unsaturated methylolamides whose production is done by polymerization in aqueous medium using a redox catalyst system, in the presence of 2 to 15% by weight.One or more polyvinyl alcohols with saponification numbers between 20 and 240 and degrees of polymerization between 300 and 2000. The dispersions thus produced are used as a binder hydraulically suspended building materials and, in combination with hardener additives, in adhesives and paints.
