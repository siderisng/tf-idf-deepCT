<TITLE>
manual device for the extraction and dosed dispensing of fruit juices
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

</INVENTOR>
<ABSTRACT>
manual device for the extraction and dosed dispensing of fruit juices, in particular of citrus fruits, consisting of a fruit boring pipe which is equipped, over part of its length, with a screwthread and oval-shaped radial juice entrance openings and is open at both ends, of a surface, arranged at the end of the boring pipe, for pressing against the fruit, and of a juice-collecting container which can be closed by a hinged lid, wherein the free end of the fruit boring pipe is equipped with axially directed serrations and the fruit boring pipe, widening conically towards the juice-collecting container, is constructed in one piece and coaxially with the essentially cylindrical juice-collecting container, and wherein an independent stop dish is provided which is equipped with a stop surface or a stop rim and receives, in the manner of an eggcup known per se, the free side of the fruit carrying the fruit boring pipe together with collecting container on the other side.  <image>
</ABSTRACT>
