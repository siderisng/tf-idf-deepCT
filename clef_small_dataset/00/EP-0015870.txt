<ABSTRACT>
An electromagnetic continuous casting coil has a support body for an induction coil and a cooling device with at least one cooling nozzle directed on the surface of the casting strand for a liquid coolant.The nozzle opening formed as a annular gap (13) is switched on down a profile (15) in the flow direction of the coolant, which injects in the web of the coolant beam (14) exiting the ring gap (14) with a groomed or the casting direction extended replace baffle surface.This surrounds the formation of the casting strand and distracts the coolant beam from the annular gap in a different direction, so that the height and angles under which the coolant struck on the casting strand are subsequently changed.
</ABSTRACT>
