<ABSTRACT>
Prepolymers of alkali and / or alkaline scales and metal oxides which are suitable as molding compositions, preferably pressed compositions for the production of pressable laminates or insulating plates, are obtained by a mixture of solid and / or water-dissolved alkali and / or alkaline scales , optionally with the addition of water, and from two to four-fat metal oxides, wherein in the mixture the ratio of metal oxide to silicate 1: 1 to 1: 9 parts by weight of dry substance and wherein the homogenized mixture is a water content of 10 to 50% by weight, based on the total amount of mixing, in the temperature range of 10 to 50 ° C can be pre-reacted until the water content of the mixture is reduced by 0.1 to 25% by weight and obtained for crushing sufficient tame-dry product, crushed and subsequently at temperatures up to 120 ° C heated until the obtained prepolymer is a waterg Events of 20 to 2% by weight.
</ABSTRACT>
