<ABSTRACT>
Chaíne for the manufacture of concrete elements (not armed, reinforced or prestressed) comprising concealing neck means, a circuit for hardening and means for the evacuation of hardening elements.It comprises a float chain (A1) to be moved in a liquid vessel and means (C, F) to place the concrete (P) elements that have just been cast on said floats, theWhat in this moving in the tank, carry the concrete elements through the curing circuit, means provided to reduce the floats to the neck means (b) of the concrete.Application to the manufacture of piles, poles for power lines, slabs, beams, panels, borders.
