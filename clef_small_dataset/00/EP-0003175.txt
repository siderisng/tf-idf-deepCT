<ABSTRACT>
the invention provides aluminium articles having  porous anodic oxide films coloured by means of an optical  interference effect.  in figure 4, the article 10 carries a first  anodic oxide film 12 with pores 14 enlarged at their inner  ends 20 and containing deposits 22.  the products may be  made by growing a second anodic oxide film 26 underneath  the deposits 22 which are preferably of acid-resistant  material.  x is at least 26 nm, y is preferably at least 60 nm, z  is preferably 15 nm to 200 nm, (y + z) is preferably 75 nm to  600 nm, and w is preferably at least 15 nm.
