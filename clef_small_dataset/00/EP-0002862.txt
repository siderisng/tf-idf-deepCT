<ABSTRACT>
binder for adhesives based on thermally prepared copolymers of cyclic diene units containing copolymers of at least 60% by weight of cyclopentadiene units, of which up to 30% by weight can be alkylated cyclopentadiene units having from 1 to 4 carbon atoms in the alkyl group, and at least 5% by weight of vinyl esters of monocarboxylic acids of from 1 to 18 carbon atoms, characterised in that the copolymer is composed of a) cyclopentadiene units to an extent of from 60 to 90% by weight, b) vinyl esters to an extent of at least 5% by weight and c) olefinically unsaturated aromatic hydrocarbons to an extent of at least 5% by weight, and is present as such or in hydrogenated form. 
</ABSTRACT>
