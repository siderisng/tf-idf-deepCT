<ABSTRACT>
The adhesive or adhesive component according to the invention, which contains a vinyl monomer, a vinylpoly mer, a free radical aroma and as adhesion improvers a acid amide orimide substituted on the nitrogen atom by a chlorosulfonated alkyl, aryl or aralkyl group, forms or gives together with an amide acceleratorAn adhesive with which heat, oil and waterproof bonds, e.g.b.of metals, polymeric materials, glass and ceramics, with very good tensile shear strength can be achieved.The adhesive can also be brought into the shape of a dried film with or without support and used in this form for gluing.
