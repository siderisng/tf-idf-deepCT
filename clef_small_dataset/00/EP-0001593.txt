<ABSTRACT>
1.  a process for the manufacture of butenediol diacetate or a butenediol diacetate isomer mixture by reacting butadiene, oxygen and acetic acid over a catalyst based on palladium or platinum, characterized in that instead of butadiene there is used a butadiene-containing mixture which is formed in the thermal cracking of higher paraffin hydrocarbons or in the dehydrogenation of n-butane and/or butene mixtures, and a catalyst is used which contains minor amounts of tellurium in addition to palladium or platinum.
