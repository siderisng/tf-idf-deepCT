<ABSTRACT>
The present invention relates to the addition salts of substituted aralkylamines of formula: A-CH₂-NH-R (I) [where A is 3,4-methylenedioxyphenyl or α-hydroxy-benzyl, and R representsCH (CH₃) ₂ or C (CH₃) ₃], with an organic acid selected from the assembly constituted by acetic, propionic, citric, fumaric, maleic, succinic and methanesulfonic acids.It also relates to the process of preparing these salts and their application in therapeutics, particularly as antidepressant agents of the CNS.
