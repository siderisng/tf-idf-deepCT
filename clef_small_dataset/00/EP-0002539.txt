<ABSTRACT>
Particle-shaped self-extinguishing thermoplastic molding compositions based on volatile propellant-containing styrene polymers, which are superficially coated with an organic bromine compound.To stabilize the bromine compound against degradation due to the expansion, they also also contain an N-hydroxyphenylbenzotriazole or a hydroxybenzophenone.The molding compounds can be used to produce foams by expanding the propellant at elevated temperatures.
