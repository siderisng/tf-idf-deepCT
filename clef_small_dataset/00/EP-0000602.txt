<TITLE>
process for the preparation of isocyanates from formamides.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>
akzo nvnl<sep>akzo n.v.<sep>akzo n.v.velperweg 76nl-6824 bm arnhemnl<sep>akzo n.v.<sep>
</APPLICANT>
<INVENTOR>
heyboer nico<sep>heyboer, nico<sep>heyboer, nicobalyeweg 2nl-6874 aj wolfhezenl<sep>heyboer, nico<sep>heyboer, nicobalyeweg 2nl-6874 aj wolfhezenl<sep>
</INVENTOR>
<ABSTRACT>
process for preparing isocyanates from formamides  wherein a formamide corresponding to the formula r  (nhcho)n, where r is an organic group and n is 1 or 2, is  oxidized with an oxygen containing gas at a temperature in  the range of 300°c to 600°c in the presence of a catalytic  amount of copper and/or one or more metals of the groups  ib and viii of the 5th and 6th period of the periodic system of  elements to yield the corresponding isocyanate r(nco)n  where r and n have the same meaning as above, and the  resultant gaseous isocyanate containing reaction mixture is  subjected to a separation process to separate the product  isocyanate from water of reaction.  
</ABSTRACT>
