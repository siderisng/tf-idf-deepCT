<ABSTRACT>
a process for producing a steroid compound having an  oxo group in the side chain, which comprises condensing an  acide halide having a steroid skeleton with an organozinc com­ pound at a halocarbononyl group of the acid halide in an inert  organic medium in the presence of catalytic amound of an  ether capable of forming a complex with the organozinc com­ pound and, if desired, hydrolyzing the product.  the process  gives the steroid compound in a high yield, and often in an  almost quantitative yield.  the steroid compound can be an  important intermediate for the production of vitamin d₃  analogs, such as active forms of vitamin d₃.  
</ABSTRACT>
