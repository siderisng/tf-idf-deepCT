<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a fully automatic device (1) for the preparation of finished developed printing platelets on electrophotographic paths using lasers (28,228; 273) for the scanning of the template (292) and for synchronous illuminating the pressure plate (3,203). For the exposure, the laser beam is modulated according to the hell or dark areas of the template. For this purpose, the template is sampled on a sampling stage (281) from a sensor element (282) which may be a fiber optic and its optical output signal controls via a photomultiplier (290) a modulator (270) in the beam path of the laser (28,228) for exposure . Charging the pressure plate is carried out by means of a corona charge device (26, 226) attached to a travelable carriage carriage (21,221) which is an optical system of beam deflection device (277) and optical elements (278,279, 227, 280) for splitting and deflecting the laser beams (229 ; 269) wears. Together with the lasers, further optical elemante (270; 271, 276, 272; 273, 275, 274) are arranged on a stationary frame (284) of the device. The device further contexts (6,7,8,9) for the supply of the printing plate to an exposure platform (30,230), elements to generation of a modulated laser beam for the image-like exposure of the printing plate and devices (40.50.60) for developing, Fix and entable the printing plate for your use when offset printing.
