<ABSTRACT>
a flow sensor is provided, responsive to fluid flows  within a range from above a predetermined  minimum to  below a predetermined maximum, to indicate that flow is  within that range and the minimum flow has been exceed­ ed, but nonresponsive to fluid flows below the predeter­ mined minimum or above the predetermined maximum, to  avoid giving a false indication of fluid flow, comprising a  flow-responsive valve (10) and a differential pressure in­ dicator (30).
