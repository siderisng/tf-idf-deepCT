<ABSTRACT>
a folding box of the type having an openable end flap  (16), the outer end of which is provided with a free holding  flap (18) which, when the end flap is closed, is put down  along the inside of the box front panel (2) opposite to the rear  panel (6), to which the end flap is hinged, the closed box  being provided with a cover portion (20) located at the outside  of the said end flap (16) and connected with opposed  edge portions of the box opening normally closed by the end  flap, such that the cover portion (20) prevents opening of the  end flap (16), said cover portion being connected with at  least one of the said edge portions across a rupture line (a)  whereby, when said rupture line (a) is broken or the cover  portion (20) is entirely torn off, the end flap (16) is freely  openable.  
</ABSTRACT>
