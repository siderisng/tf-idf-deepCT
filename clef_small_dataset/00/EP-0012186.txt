<ABSTRACT>
In a synchronous digital processing plant (eg, computers) consisting of several circuit diameters, several or all circuit diahatables (41) each have a device (T-ring 42) for generating time signals (Ti) from which the clock control signals for the logic circuitsto be derived from this platelet.All of these T-rings are synchronous because they are controlled by a common oscillator (OSC).This decentralized generation of clock control signals prevents signal distortions and runtime differences and makes it possible to increase the operating frequency of the entire system.
