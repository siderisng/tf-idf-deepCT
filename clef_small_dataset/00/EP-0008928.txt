<ABSTRACT>
in a method of smoothing the edges of a window  through a glass film (8) of a semiconductor device, a masking  film (6, 7) is provided under the glass film (8), to prevent  impurities from the glass film from penetrating into semi­ conductor substrate during the heating of the glass film (8) to  smooth its edges.  a masking film, for example, an si₃n₄ film  (7) does not, however, inhibit the penetration of hydrogen  gas and therefore still allows the device to be treated by  heating under a hydrogen atmosphere to improve the prop­ erties of the device.
