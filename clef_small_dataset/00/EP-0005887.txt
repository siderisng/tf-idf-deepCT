<ABSTRACT>
a drive, comprising a number of bodies of revolution (3)  which are pressed against at least one intermediate plate (4)  by a drivable first structural member (1) and which can roll  thereon.  the intermediate plate (4) is connected to the  outgoing part (5) of the drive and is supported by a second,  plate-shaped structural member (6).  the intermediate plate (4) is flexible in the direction  transversely of the second plate-shaped structural member  (6) and the material of the intermediate plate (4) has a  modulus of elasticity which at least equals the modulus of  elasticity of the material of the second plate-shaped structural  member (6).
