<ABSTRACT>
When using silicon for solar cells, care must be carried so that the surface intended for the light incident has the lowest possible reflectivity.For this purpose, it is expedient to recall the upper surface in an etching process.Specifically, it is intended to provide a silicon surface area using a chemical reaction process with the sputtering gas atmosphere while applying a cathode extraction process.
