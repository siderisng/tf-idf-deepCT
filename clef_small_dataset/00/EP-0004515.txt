<ABSTRACT>
1.  process for assembling a triangle rod of the brake linkage of railway vehicles, comprising a base (1) and two sides issuing from an apex (2) and formed by a single bar (3) bent in its median part corresponding to said apex, an angle piece (4), pierced with a bore (7) for receiving the bent bar and forming a head of the triangle, being fixed to the bar in its median part, in which the bent bar (3) is introduced in the receiver bore (7) which is then made to slide on the bar up to its median part, and the angle piece (4) is fixed on the bar (3), said process characterized in that the angle piece (4) is fixed by being crimped on the bar (3).
