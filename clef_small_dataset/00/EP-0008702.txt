<TITLE>
apparatus for arresting or freeing the through-flow of a flowable medium.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

<ABSTRACT>
an apparatus for arresting and releasing the through-flow of a gaseous or fluid medium by means of a valve sphere (1) which is located in a conical or spherical shaped valve seat (2)-(3) has a release bar (4) which is movable relative to the valve seat.  the longitudinal axis of the release bar is arranged at an angle in the range from 20 to 100 deg  to the direction of flow of the gaseous or fluid medium.  <image>
