<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a device for the homogeneous distribution of a pulverulent product through a longitudinal slot of evacuation, of the type in which said slot is supplied with pulverulent product suspended in a gas stream using a conduit D'food.According to the invention, the supply product is subdivided into a plurality of elementary ducts of the same length opening into regularly spaced points along the length of said slot.Application to the realization of a semi-reflective glazing, by deposit, on at least one side of this glazing, metal salts and subsequent pyrolysis of these salts.
