<ABSTRACT>
5,5-disubstituted 4-ureidohexahydropyrimidines are prepared by reaction of ureas with aldehydes at temperatures of initially below 110 deg c and then above 110 deg c.  the 5,5-disubstituted 4-ureidohexahydropyrimidines which can be prepared by the process of the invention are useful starting materials for the preparation of auxiliaries in the textile chemistry, varnishing resin chemistry and wood materials industry.
