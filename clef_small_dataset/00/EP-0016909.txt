<ABSTRACT>
Method and device for crowns, plasma, an oxide layer on semiconductor substrates (8).The substrates (8) heated by a pending independent heat source (10) are placed in an enclosure (1) comprising a plasma region (7) defined by the induction coil (3).The re-gion (7) and the substrates (8) are separated by a completed distance.Oxygen admission valves (12) and vacuum control (15) control the pressure in the enclosure.Independent control of the various parameters and the remoteness of the substrates with respect to the plasma zone make it possible to obtain a linear growth of low temperature oxide.Use for the manufacture of semiconductor devices.
