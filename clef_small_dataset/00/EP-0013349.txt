<ABSTRACT>
1.  sewing machine with switchbox (7), provided with a selector switch (12) and with switch circuits, which can be selected by said selector switch for activating the sewing instrumentalities, acting upon the sewing material and also for activating auxilliary functions of the sewing machine, whilst the control functions of the switch circuits are indicated on the control panel of the switchbox (7) and also with at least one separate switch (9) associated to the selector switch (12), which separated switch (9) is arranged in the close operating range of the head (10) of the sewing machine, characterized in that 1.  in each position of the selector switch (12) at least two switch circuits are combined.
