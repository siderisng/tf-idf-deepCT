<ABSTRACT>
a multi-contact electrical plug connector.  the plug connector (4) has latch ears (50) having a  forward ramp surface (52) and a vertical rear latching  shoulder (58), for engagement by a locking device (72) on a  receptacle connector (6).  so that the plug connector (4) can be alternatively  latched to a post header connector (86), so as to easily be  disengageable therefrom camming ramps (40) are disposed  on either side of the latch ears (50), so as to lift a flexible  latching arm (94) of the header connector (86) over the  camming ramps (50) as the plug connector (4) is disengaged  from the header connector (86).
