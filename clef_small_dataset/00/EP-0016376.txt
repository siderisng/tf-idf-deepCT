<ABSTRACT>
a thin sheet feeding apparatus has a bending mechanism  (25) to curve a pile (14) of thin sheets bundled by a belt  member (14a) and a mechanism (59) to sequentially feed at  least one thin sheet (14b) from the uppermost thin sheet (14c)  from the curved concave pile (14) of the thin sheet in the  predetermined feeding direction.
