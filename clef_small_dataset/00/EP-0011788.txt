<ABSTRACT>
In the fabric drain device (15), the deflection rod (3) upstream of the goods trigger roller (5) has counter-rotary threadbills (41, 42) and optionally at the ends of wide-stop needle rollers (61,61a) in its two halves (38.39).This ensures that the substance (1) is stretched to the width immediately when applied to the trigger roller (5).When draining roller (5) in the region (71), the fabric does not tend to be wider under the material lower tensile stress (S) of the goods tree (65).In this way, folding of the substance (1) are avoided, which could otherwise be depressed on the pressing point (72) between the goods pull-out roller (5) and downstream roller (3c) in the fabric (1).
