<ABSTRACT>
1.  device for packing various objects in fractions of a continuous tubular web which are closed transversely.    2.  the device comprises a mandrel (6) and a mechanism capable of gripping and of drawing the web (a) stored around the mandrel (6).  the mandrel (6) is dimensioned so that the height (h) measured between the top of the mandrel (6) and the gripping mechanism gives a correct useful filling volume.    3.  packing of household refuse, articles sold in shops, clean clothing, and the like.  <image>
