<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to the production of 17-hydroxypartin by oxidation of spartin by means of manganate in aqueous acid solution.The oxidation is very selective and supplies the 17-hydroxy part into high yield of over 90%.The oxidizing agent can be easily separated after the reaction as insoluble manganese ammonium phosphate from the reaction mixture.
