<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for regulating an electron beam welding generator.The method according to the invention is characterized by a suitable combination of two methods of regulating an electron beam welding bar, one consisting of varying and regulating the very high voltage while maintaining the voltage applied to theWehnelt with the potential of the cathode (operation of the "diode" type), the other of maintaining the very high voltage constant by regulating or by programming, within certain limits, the welding current by the voltage of the Wehnelt (type operation "triode").The variations and regulation of the very high voltage are provided by an induction controller, associated with a control by thyristors.Installation for regulating a welding generator applying the method.
