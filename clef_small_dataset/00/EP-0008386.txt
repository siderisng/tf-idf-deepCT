<ABSTRACT>
a probe positioner capable of being supported from a  tube sheet (38) of a nuclear steam generator for positioning a  probe (110) in relationship to the tube sheet of the nuclear  steam generator comprises a locking mechanism (56, 58) for  locking the probe positioner in the tube sheet and a plurality of  rotatable arms (62, 64, 66) for positioning the probe.  the  probe positioner also comprises quick disconnect motors (70,  72) for rotating the arms of the probe positioner relative to  each other.
