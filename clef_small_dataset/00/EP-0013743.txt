<ABSTRACT>
a cabinet for data processing apparatus has a hinged  door (35), and a flexible door extension (17) wider than  the door and made of a series of door slats hinged toge­ ther.  the door extension can be retracted into the door by  sliding it along fixed tracks (19, 20) on the cabinet hous­ ing.  during this movement that end of the door extension  which leads is guided by guides behind the door to a folded  form.  when the door extension is clear of the fixed tracks,  the door carrying the door extension can be hinged out­ wardly at the vertical corner of the cabinet to allow full  opening of the enclosure.
