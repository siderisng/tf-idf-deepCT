<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a method for recovering uranium contained in impure phosphoric acid.Impure acid is treated by means of an extractive organized phase (2) for U (VI), which is re-extracted (6) by means of a complexing aqueous phase for U (IV) in the presence of an oxidoring agent.reduced state;After electrochemical oxidation of said aqueous solution, it is extracted by a second organic phase (14) from where uranium is recovered, the exhausted aqueous solution being recycled to the reextraction after electrochemic oxidation, the process comprisingTwo organ phase cycles and a complexing aqueous solution cycle.Application to the recovery of uranium contained in a wet phosphoric acid.
