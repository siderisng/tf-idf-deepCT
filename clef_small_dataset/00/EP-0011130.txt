<ABSTRACT>
Quaternary ammonium compounds of formula I and amino ether of formula II are produced.The quaternary ammonium compounds of the formula I are as a textile softener for laundry maintenance in the washing treatment agents and for the finishing of textile fibers by impregnating in aqueous or aqueous / alcoholic fleets, for example in the foulard method or in the pull-out process.They can be used together with other active ingredients, for example fatty acid polyaminamides and / or fatty alcohol polycol ethers.The treated soft textiles also have a good absorbency.
