<ABSTRACT>
a magnet coil (7, 8) for using when welding with a magnetically moved arc, and in fact for welding with an arc moved between an auxiliary electrode (6) and a bead-like path (5) of a welding-edge seam, is improved by a coil core (7) adapted to the contours of the workpieces (3, 4) being slipped over these workpieces, a magnetic field (bl) directed perpendicularly to the welding plane being produced as a result of the applied coil winding.  the magnet coil (7, 8) serves to increase the range of application of welding with a magnetically moved arc, and in fact in particular with regard to greater wall thicknesses. 
</ABSTRACT>
