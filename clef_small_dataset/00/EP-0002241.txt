<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to magnetic recording materials of non-magnetic carriers which are provided with a magnetic layer based on the base of finely divided magnetic pigment, wherein the binder of the magnetic layer is at least 50% by weight of a particular polyurethane elastomer, which is a tetrahydrofuran-soluble, isocyanate-free thermoplastic polyurethaneis made of a) 1 mol of a polyesterol having a molecular weight of 700 to 2. 500, b) 0.3 to 3 moles of a flat chain aliphatic diol with 2 to 10 carbon atoms, c) 0.05 to 0,4 moles of a triol with 3 to 10 carbon atoms and d) 1.35 to 4.7 moles of a diisocyanate with 6 to 30 C atoms, wherein the amount of NCO groups approximately equivalent to the amount of OH groups of the sum of the components Ato C is.
