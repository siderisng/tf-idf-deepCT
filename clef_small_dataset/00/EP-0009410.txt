<ABSTRACT>
a solid, cylindrical, subcutaneous implant for im­ proving the rate of weight gain of ruminant animals which  comprises   (a) a biocompatible inert core having a diameter of from  2 to 10 mm.  and   (b) a biocompatible coating having a thickness of from  0. 2 to 1 mm. , the composition of said coating com­ prising from 5 to 40 percent by weight of estradiol and  from 95 to 60 percent by weight of a diamethylpoly­ siloxane rubber.
