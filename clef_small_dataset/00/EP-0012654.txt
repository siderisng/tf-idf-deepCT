<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a heating apparatus capable of supplying water vapor and a hot gas.It comprises a combustion chamber 22 from where the fumes which circulate in an enclosure 21 by heating at least one beam of tubes 51, 52 in which water circulates and giving steam and it is characterized byThe fact that it comprises at least one beam of tubes 71 traversed by a gas, housed in the chain 21 so as to be heated by the smoke of the said combustion chambers 22. This device applies to an installation ofrefining.
