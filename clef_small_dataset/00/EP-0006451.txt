<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to novel imidazo [1,2-A] imidazoles of the general formula wherein R¹ R² and R³ may be the same or different, a hydrogen atom or a fluorine, chlorine or bromine atom or a methyl, methoxy or trifluoromethyl group , at least one of the radicals R¹, R² or R³ must not be hydrogen and R⁴ and R⁵, which may be equal to or various, mean a hydrogen atom or a methyl group and their acid addition salts. The new compound may include, inter alia, by reacting a substitiated phenyl-imino imidazolidine of the general formula wherein R¹ to R³ have the above meanings, with an oxo compound of the general formula wherein R⁴ and R⁵ are defined as indicated above and x Chlorine, bromine or iodine atom means be prepared. The new compounds reduce the heart rate and can be used as means for the treatment of coronary diseases.
