<ABSTRACT>
Device Preserving precise positioning of the ends of the optical conductors during the relative rotation of the external bodies to which the seal is coupled.It comprises a male mouthpiece (1) and a female tip (2) arranged to form a shaft (5) rotating in a sleeve (6) according to a fixed adjustment, a stop device (8-81, 7-71) delimiting theAxial displacement of the sleeve, centering means (3-4) of the ends of the conductors (F1, F2), and fasteners to the outer bodies (A, B) respectively according to a rigid coupling (10, 12) and semi-rigid (11, 13, 14).The invention applies to achieving an optical link between two mechanical bodies rotating relative to each other about a common axis of rotation.
