<ABSTRACT>
a burner for the thermal combustion of at least periodically explosive gases is described in which the gases are introduced into a combustion chamber substantially tangentially of the burner axis through a plurality of throughflow tubes which stabilise the gas streams and are burnt in said combustion chamber with formation of a supercritical spin field having peripheral forward flow and inner axial back flow.  <image>
