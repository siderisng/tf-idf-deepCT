<ABSTRACT>
Radiant source constituted by an excited dipole by an extraplate waveguide.The waveguide (8) is of rectangular section, consti killed by a delelectric blade (7) of rectangular parallelepipedic shape, of longitudinal axis (Δ), whose four symmetrical faces relative to the axis (Δ) andopposite two to two are covered each of a metal plate.The two faces parallel to the longitudinal mediator plane are prolocated each, towards the end of the dielectric blade, by a metal tongue (11, 12) resulting in the strands (15, 16, 13, 14) of the dipole.The dipole strands may be parallel or perpendicular to the polarization direction of the electric wave radiated by the waveguide.Use in electronic scanning antennas.
