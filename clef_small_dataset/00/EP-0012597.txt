a tag for an animal's ear the tag having means for fastenÂ­ ing to the ear of the animal.  the fastening means are formed  by an opening (15) and a shaft (12) having a head portion (13)  at one end thereof, the head portion (13) in use being passed  through the ear and into the opening (15).  in order to provide an opening in the ear of the animal  which is of a sufficient size to accommodate the shaft (12),  the extremity of the head portion (13) is provided with a  punching means (14) which punches out a portion of the ear  prior to the passage of the head portion (13) therethrough.   the punching means is provided by a cutting edge (22) which  is of complementary shape to the opening (15) in the tag but  of overall larger dimensions.
