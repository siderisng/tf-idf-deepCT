<ABSTRACT>
1.  display unit for reels of web-like products such as paper, ribbons, trimmings or the like, of the type comprising spindles (9) of reels (11) supported by racks (8), integral with the uprights of two sets of frames (5, 6) supported one in front of the other by a stand (2), the set of frames (6) located at the front being supported by horizontal slides (4) making it possible to move them over a distance at least equal to the width of one frame in order to reveal at least one frame (5) of the set located at the rear, characterised in that the racks (8) are open at the rear and in that the frames (5) of the set located at the rear and pivoted about a pivot (15) passing through one of their uprights in order to be able to be pivoted through at least 90 degrees between a normal position for use in which they are parallel to the frame of the set of frames located at the front and a loading position in which, after pivoting on the front, their rear face is easily accessible from the front of the unit. 
</ABSTRACT>
