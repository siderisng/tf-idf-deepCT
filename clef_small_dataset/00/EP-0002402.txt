<ABSTRACT>
it comprises a main circuit in which air to be purified, driven by a blower 1, enters via an entrance and passes through a first filtering means constituted by a drainable filtering liquid 5, then a second filtering means, constituted by a porous body 11, before being discharged to the outside; it moreover comprises a branch circuit into which is drawn a part of the air situated in the main circuit immediately upstream of the second means of filtering in order to be reinjected at a point of the main circuit situated upstream of the first means of filtering.  the invention is applied in particular to asbestos aspirators.  <image>
