<ABSTRACT>
Preparation of aromatic and araliphatic aldehydes by oxidation of corresponding alcohols with oxygen in the presence of a silver catalyst of certain grain size and under certain conditions of the temperature.The aromatic and araliphatic aldehydes produced by the method of the invention are valuable starting materials for the production of dyes, pesticides, plastics, fragrances.
