<ABSTRACT>
The multifunction reprographic device according to the invention makes it possible to make the choice of the user, either photocopies or photographs, instantaneous or not, various formats, in black and white or in colors, as well as various types of cardsidentification.It comprises, housed in a furniture (10) having a window and a window (12), an optical system (14) disposed at the inlet of a dark chamber (15), a roller of photosensitive paper (17) guided andTrained by rollers (18), an illumination device, and a development group (20).A selector lever (24) makes it possible to modify the positions of various elements of the optical system (14) in particular to retract the mirror (23), so as to be able to make photocopies of two-dimensional subjects placed in the plane of the window orPhotographs of two or three dimensions subjects located in front of the window (12).
