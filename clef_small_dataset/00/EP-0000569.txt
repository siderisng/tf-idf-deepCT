<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a device for rewinding yarn with a stationary winding shaft (8) and a vertically movable change device (5).The diameter device (5) is always held by a pneumatic adjusting device (19) via a control device (15) at the same distance to the winding surface.For this purpose, an air gap (10) is provided between the winding surface and the edge of the diapering device, through which an air flow (18) is guided.This airflow (18) cuts a measuring air stream which flows between a suction and fishing nozzle and its strength of the control device (15) serves as the measured value.
