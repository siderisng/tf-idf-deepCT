<ABSTRACT>
Method for producing a heating gas of city gas quality with a condensing value of less than 4300 kcal / nm³ (18 000 kj / nm³) by catalytic reaction of methanol with water vapor under a pressure in the area of about 10 to 50 bar.An insert mixture containing water vapor and methanol in the weight ratio of about 0.5 to 1.5 is passed to 80 to 95% by a first reaction zone at temperatures of 300 to 700 ° C, which is a nickel catalyst with 25 to 50 wt.-% Nickel contains as an active component.The remainder of a set mixture is passed together with the product gas of the first reaction zone by a second reaction zone at temperatures of 200 to 400 ° C, which is a copper catalyst about 40 to 70 wt.-% copper entables.
</ABSTRACT>
