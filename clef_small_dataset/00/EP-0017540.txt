<ABSTRACT>
Device for increased the signal-to-noise ratio in the presence of an intense light background and significantly reduce the risk of direct detection of solar radiation.It comprises optoelectric shutter means (3) associated with the transparent areas (1) of the optical cache to pass substantially than the radiation of the source to locate (if) resulting in the associated detector (2).The shutter can be carried out using a ferroelectric cerami blade arranged to form juxtaposed elements and selectively controlled by a circuit (6) as a function of the positioning (Δ, θ) of the source in the form of data (sj) elaborated by an annexed calculator (5).The invention applies in particular to headphone viewing systems.
</ABSTRACT>
