<TITLE>
radiation-curable coating compositions and method of coating metal substrates therewith.
</TITLE>

<ABSTRACT>
radiation-curable compositions are disclosed which  comprise radiation-reactive monomer and/or oligomer.  in  order to improve their adhesion to metal substrates, such as  aluminum, steel, copper or zinc, 3-acryloxypropionic acid is  incorporated into the compositions which may be cured by  exposure to actinic radiation, such as ultraviolet, electron  beam or mercury arc radiation.
