<ABSTRACT>
The invention relates to a laminated glazing comprising electrical resistance wires (5) embedded in an intermediate layer (4) of a thermoplastic material. According to the invention, the glazing comprises an intermediate layer (4) with transparent or translucent thermoplastic material in which a network of electrical wires (5) is encrusted, the interlayer layer being in contact, on the one hand a monolithic support. (1) or laminated comprising at least one rigid sheet, and on the other hand a layer of non-rigid plastic material (3) generally less than a millimeter, the latter layer being preferably externally and preferably self-regulating properties and antiaceration. The invention also relates to a prefabricated sheet which contains the network of electrical wires and which can be used in the manufacture of glazing. Glazing can be used as heating or alarm glazing.
