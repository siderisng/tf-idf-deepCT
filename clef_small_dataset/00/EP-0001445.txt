<TITLE>
device for the dustless loading of bulk material and transparent plastic hose therefor.
</TITLE>
<APPLICANT>

</INVENTOR>
<ABSTRACT>
1.  loading device for loose dust-free loading of bulk material, preferably in or on to large-area-open platforms, wagons, containers, ships, or the like, with a flexible loading hose (11), the free end (14) of which is movable in the manner of a folding bellows, and to which spacedly arranged stiffening rings are fixed along the outer circumference, characterized in that the flexible loading hose (11) including the ships (13) surrounding the stiffening rings (12) consists as a whole of transparent, highly wear-resistant, extruded polyurethane foil whereby the connecting seam or seam of the loading hose are welded together and the ships (13) are welded to the loading hose (11). 
</ABSTRACT>
