a package 10 includes tablets 13, 14 or other substances,  which when contacted with a liquid generate hydrogen and  carbon dioxide.  a catalyst 17 for the hydrogen-oxygen  reaction, a carbon dioxide indicator 23 and anaerobic  indicators 18, 19, 21 are applied to the exterior of the  package.  the co₂-generating tablet comprises a water-­ soluble solid acid and a water-soluble solid carbonate in  proportions whereby an acidic ph is maintained upon  dissolution in the liquid.  this gas generating apparatus constitutes a disposable  unit which is intended to be employed only once for the  production of an anaerobic atmosphere.
