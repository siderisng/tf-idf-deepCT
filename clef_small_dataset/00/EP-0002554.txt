<ABSTRACT>
a device for handling objects, provided with an  attaching means for the objects in which a feeler is movable  between two positions within the region of the attaching  means, which feeler, if an object is contained in the attaching  means, cannot come in the first of the said two positions and  when in the first of the said two positions, prevents or  changes the normal working of the device.  more specifically  the invention relates to a device having two attaching means  and two feelers such that the device rejects the object, if not  both feelers are in the said second position.
