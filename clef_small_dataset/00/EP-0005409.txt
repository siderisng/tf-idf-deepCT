<ABSTRACT>
The subject of the invention relates to new piezoelectric transducers with embedded mechanical amplifier.A transducer according to the invention comprises a rigid base plate 7;two lateral plates 8a and 8b whose lower edge is connected to the plate 7 by a thinning 11;a stack 1 of piezoelectric elements 2 alternating with electrodes 3;a pavilion 23 formed of an elastic and flexible membrane which connects the upper edges of the two plates 8a and 8b;A boistier 27a, 27b and a seamlessness skin 29 transparent acoustically.An application is the emission or reception of high power acoustic waves in the very low frequencies included for example between some hertz and 500 Hz.
