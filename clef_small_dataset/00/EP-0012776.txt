<ABSTRACT>
Methods for solidifying an unbound fasculine read from natural and / or synthetic threads with a UV-curing binder.The nonwoven fabric is first compressed in a calender, smoothed and heated without fiber bonding before printing with the prepolymer binder.The binder has a viscosity of 500 to 5000 m PAS / sec during processing.and it is cured by irradiating the surface of the printed nonwoven fabric with ultraviolet light.Operating speeds of more than 50 m / min are accessible.
